====== 7. God Blessed Jacob ======
====== 7. Allah Memberkati Yakub ======

{{:en:obs:obs-07-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

As the boys grew up, Rebekah loved Jacob, but Isaac loved Esau. Jacob loved to stay at home, but Esau loved to hunt.

Ketika anak-anak itu bertumbuh, Ribka mengasihi Yakub, tetapi Ishak mencintai Esau. Yakub senang tinggal di rumah, tetapi Esau suka berburu.

{{:en:obs:obs-07-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

One day, when Esau came back from hunting, he was very hungry. Esau said to Jacob, "Please give me some of the food you have made."  Jacob responded, "First, give me your rights as the oldest son."  So Esau gave Jacob his rights as the oldest son. Then Jacob gave him some food.

Suatu hari, ketika Esau kembali dari berburu, dia sangat lapar. Kata Esau kepada Yakub, "Tolong beri aku sebagian dari makanan yang telah engkau buat itu." Yakub menjawab, "Pertama, berikan dulu hak-hakmu sebagai putra tertua." Jadi Esau memberikan hak-haknya sebagai anak tertua kepada Yakub. Lalu Yakub memberinya makanannya.

{{:en:obs:obs-07-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When Isaac was old, he wanted to give his blessing to Esau. But before he did, Rebekah and Jacob tricked him by having Jacob pretend to be Esau. Jacob put on Esaub s clothes and put goat skins on his neck and hands.

Ketika Ishak sudah tua, dia ingin memberikan berkat kepada Esau. Tapi sebelum dia melakukannya, Ribka dan Yakub menipunya dengan menyuruh Yakub berpura-pura menjadi Esau. Yakub mengenakan 
pakaian Esau dan memakai kulit kambing di leher dan tangannya.

{{:en:obs:obs-07-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Isaac could not see well. Jacob came to Isaac and said, "I am Esau, I have come to receive your blessing."  When Isaac felt the goat hair and smelled the clothes, he thought it was Esau and blessed him.

Ishak tidak bisa melihat dengan baik. Yakub datang kepada Ishak dan berkata, "Aku Esau, Aku datang untuk menerima berkat dari Bapak." Ketika Ishak merasakan bulu kambing dan mencium pakaiannya, dia pikir itu adalah Esau dan memberkatinya.

{{:en:obs:obs-07-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Esau hated Jacob because Jacob stole his rights as oldest son and also his blessing. So he planned to kill Jacob after their father died.

Esau membenci Yakub karena Yakub mencuri hak-haknya sebagai anak tertua dan juga berkat-Nya. Jadi dia berencana untuk membunuh Yakub setelah ayah mereka meninggal.

{{:en:obs:obs-07-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

But Rebekah heard of Esaub's plan. So she arranged for Jacob to be sent far away to live with her relatives.

Tapi Ribka mendengar tentang rencana Esau itu. Jadi dia mengatur supaya Yakub pergi jauh untuk tinggal bersama keluarganya.

{{:en:obs:obs-07-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

While Jacob was there, he married four women who gave birth to a total of twelve sons and a daughter. God made him very wealthy.

Sementara Yakub berada di sana, dia menikahi empat perempuan yang melahirkan dua belas putra dan seorang putri. Allah membuatnya sangat kaya.

{{:en:obs:obs-07-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

After twenty years away from his home in Canaan, Jacob returned there with his family, his servants, and all his herds of animals.

Sesudah dua puluh tahun jauh dari rumahnya di Kanaan, Yakub kembali ke sana dengan keluarganya, hamba-Nya, dan semua hewan ternaknya.

{{:en:obs:obs-07-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

He was very afraid because he thought Esau still wanted to kill him. So he sent Esau many herds of animals as a gift. The servants who brought the animals said to Esau, b These animals are a gift from your servant, Jacob. He is coming soon.b 

Dia sangat takut karena dia berpikir Esau masih ingin membunuhnya. Jadi dia mengirim Esau banyak ternak sebagai hadiah. Para pelayan yang membawa hewan berkata kepada Esau, "Hewan ini adalah pemberian dari hambamu, Yakub. Dia akan segera datang."

{{:en:obs:obs-07-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

But Esau had already forgiven Jacob, and they were happily reunited. Jacob then lived peacefully in Canaan. Then Isaac died, and Jacob and Esau buried him. The covenant promises God had promised to Abraham and then to Isaac now passed on to Jacob.

Tapi Esau sudah mengampuni Yakub, dan mereka gembira bersatu kembali. Yakub kemudian hidup damai di Kanaan. Kemudian Ishak meninggal, dan Yakub dan Esau menguburkan dia. Perjanjian yang  telah dijanjikan Allah kepada Abraham dan kemudian Ishak sekarang diteruskan kepada Yakub.
