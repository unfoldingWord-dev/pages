====== 44. Peter and John Heal a Beggar ======
====== Pinagaling nina Pedro at Juan ang Isang Pulubi ======

{{:en:obs:obs-44-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

One day Peter and John went to the Temple. On their way, they saw a crippled man begging for money.

Isang araw, pumunta sina Pedro at Juan sa Templo. Mag nadaanan silang isang pulubi na namamalimos.

{{:en:obs:obs-44-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Peter looked at him and said, “I do not have any money to give you. But what I do have I will give you. In the name of Jesus, get up and walk!”

Tiningnan siya ni Pedro at sinabi, “Wala akong pera na maibibigay sa iyo. Pero mayroong isang bagay na pwede kong ibigay. Sa pangalan ni Jesus, tumayo ka at lumakad!”

{{:en:obs:obs-44-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The man was immediately healed, and he began walking and jumping around, praising God. The people in the Temple were amazed.

Gumaling agad ang lalaki. Naglakad siya at tumalon-talon habang pinupuri niya ang Diyos. Nagtaka ang mga tao na nasa Templo.

{{:en:obs:obs-44-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

A crowd quickly formed. Peter said to them, “Why are you amazed that this man is healed? It was not through our own power or goodness that he was healed, but by faith in Jesus.”

Sinabi ni Pedro sa mga tao na nagkakatipon, “Nagtataka ba kayo dahil gumaling ang lalaking ito? Hindi siya gumaling dahil sa sarili naming kapangyarihan o kabutihan. Gumaling siya dahil sa pananampalatay kay Jesus.”

{{:en:obs:obs-44-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

“You demanded that Jesus be killed. You killed the author of life, but God raised him from the dead. This is how God fulfilled the prophecies that the Messiah would suffer and die. But now, repent and turn to God so that your sins will be washed away.”

“Pinili ninyong mamatay si Jesus. Ipinapatay ninyo ang pinagmumulan ng buhay, pero binuhay siyang muli ng Diyos. Tinupad ng Diyos ang mga inuhula tungkol sa Tagapagligtas. Siya ay maghihirap at mamamatay. Kaya nga, magsisisi na kayo at bumalik sa Diyos at lilinisin niya ang mga kasalanan ninyo.”

{{:en:obs:obs-44-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The leaders of the Temple were greatly annoyed at what Peter and John were saying. So they arrested them and threw them into prison. But many of the people believed what they had seen and heard and the Church grew to 5,000 men, plus women and children.

Nainis naman kina Pedro at Juan ang mga pinuno ng Templo. Kaya ipinahuli nila ang dalawa at ipinakulong. Pero marami sa mga nakarinig ng sinabi nila ang naniwala at umabot sa 5,000 ang nadagdag sa mga naging Cristiano nang araw na iyon. May mga babae at mga bata ding naniwala sa kanila.

{{:en:obs:obs-44-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The next day, the Jewish leaders brought Peter and John before the high priest and his family. They asked them, “By what power did you do this?”

Kinabukasan, dinala ng mga pinuno ng mga Judio sina Pedro at Juan sa bahay ng pinakapunong paring Judio. Tinanong sila ng pari, “Sino ang nagbigay sa inyo ng karapatan na gawin ang ginawa ninyo sa Templo?”

{{:en:obs:obs-44-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Peter responded, “It is by the power of Jesus the Messiah that we healed this man. You crucified him, but God raised him to life again! You rejected him, but there is no other way to be saved except through the power of Jesus!”

Sumagot si Pedro, “Si Jesus ang Tagapagligtas ang nagpagaling sa lalaking ito. Siya yung ipinapako ninyo sa krus, pero binuhay siyang muli ng Diyos! Hindi ninyo siya tinanggap pero hindi maliligtas ang kahit na sino kung hindi sa pamamagitan ni Jesus!”

{{:en:obs:obs-44-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The leaders were shocked because they could see that these men were common men who were uneducated. They also realized that these men had been with Jesus. After they had threatened Peter and John, they let them go.

Ganoon na lang ang gulat ng mga pinuno dahil nalaman nilang mga karaniwang tao lamang ang mga kausap nila at ni hindi nakapag-aral. Nalaman din nilang kasa-kasama ni Jesus ang mga taong ito. Kaya tinakot nila sina Pedro at Juan saka pinaalis.


//A Bible story from: Acts 3:1-4:22//
