====== disciple ======


===== Definition: =====

A disciple is a student who follows and learns from a teacher.

  * The people who listened to the teaching of Jesus and obeyed it were called disciples of Jesus.
  * Hundreds of disciples followed Jesus at different times during his ministry on earth.
  * The twelve apostles were chosen from among the disciples of Jesus. They continued to be called "disciples," as well as "apostles."
  * Just before Jesus went up to heaven, he commanded his disciples to teach others so that they could become his disciples, too.
  * Anyone, even in modern times, who follows the way and teachings of Jesus in obedience and faith is also considered a disciple of Jesus.

[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:apostle|apostle]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[30-08]** He (Jesus) gave the pieces to his **disciples** to give to the people. The **disciples** kept passing out the food, and it never ran out!
  ***[38-01]** Jesus knew what would happen to him and told his **disciples** he was going to Jerusalem where he would be killed.
  ***[38-11]** Then Jesus went with his **disciples** to a place called Gethsemane. Jesus told his **disciples** to pray that they would not enter into temptation.
  ***[42-10]** At the end of the forty days, Jesus said to his **disciples**, “All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. So go, make **disciples** of all people groups by baptizing them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, and by teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you.”
