===== Living among the Tombs [32-04] =====


{{:en:obs:obs-32-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The man lived **among the [[:vi:key-terms/tomb|tombs]]** in the area. This man would scream all day and all night. He did not wear clothes and cut himself repeatedly with stones.


==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **among the tombs**  - That is “near the tombs” or, “around the tombs.”
