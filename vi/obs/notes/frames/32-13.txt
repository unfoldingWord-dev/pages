===== The Sick Woman [32-13] =====


{{:en:obs:obs-32-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**[[:vi:key-terms/jesus|Jesus]]** returned to the other side of the lake. After he arrived there, a large crowd gathered around him and **pressed in on him**. In the crowd was a woman who had suffered from a bleeding problem for twelve years. She had paid all of her money to doctors so they would **[[:vi:key-terms/heal|heal]]** her, but **she only got worse**.


==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **pressed in on him**  - That is, “pushed tightly against him” or, “crowded in tightly around him.”
  * **she only got worse**  - That is, “her condition only got worse” or, ” her health got worse instead of better” or, “instead she became more sick.”
