===== Philip Baptized the Ethiopian [45-12] =====


{{:en:obs:obs-45-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


So they went down into the water, and Philip **[[:vi:key-terms/baptize|baptized]]** the Ethiopian. After they came up out of the water, the **[[:vi:key-terms/holy-spirit|Holy Spirit]]** suddenly **carried Philip away**  to another place where he continued telling people about **[[:vi:key-terms/jesus|Jesus]]**.


==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **carried Philip away**  - That is, “took Philip away” or, “snatched Philip away.”
