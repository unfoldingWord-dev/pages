===== The Deliverer [16-03] =====


{{:en:obs:obs-16-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then **[[:vi:key-terms:god|God]] provided**a **[[:vi:key-terms:deliverer|deliverer]]** who rescued them from their enemies and **brought [[:vi:key-terms:peace|peace]]** to **the land**. But then **the people forgot about God**  and started **[[:vi:key-terms:worship|worshiping]]** **[[:vi:key-terms:idol|idols]]** again. So God allowed the **[[:vi:key-terms:midian|Midianites]]**, a nearby enemy people group, to defeat them.


==== Translation Notes: ====


    * **God provided**  - This could be translated as, “God chose” or, “God appointed” or, “God raised up.”
    * **brought peace**  - This could be translated as, “allowed the people to live without fear” or, “ended the fighting” or, “stopped their enemies from attacking them.”
    * **the land**  - That refers to Canaan, the Promised Land that God had given to Abraham.
    * **the people forgot about God**  - This means, “The people stopped thinking about God and ignored what he had commanded them.”
