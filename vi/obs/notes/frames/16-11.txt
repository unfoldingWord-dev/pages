===== The Midianite's Dream [16-11] =====


{{:en:obs:obs-16-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


That night **[[:vi:key-terms:god|God]]** told **[[:vi:key-terms:gideon|Gideon]]**, “**Go down**  to the **[[:vi:key-terms:midian|Midianite]]** camp and when you hear what they say, you will **no longer be afraid**.” So that night, Gideon went down to the camp and heard a Midianite soldier telling his friend about **something he had [[:vi:key-terms:dream|dreamed]]**. The man’s friend said, “This dream means that Gideon's army will defeat the Midianite army!” When Gideon heard this, he **[[:vi:key-terms:worship|worshiped]]** God.


==== Translation Notes: ====


    * **Go down**  - The Midianite soldiers were camping out in a valley and were at a lower elevation than the Israelite soldiers.
    * **you will no longer be afraid**  – That is, “you will stop being afraid.”
    * **something he had dreamed**  – That is, “something he had seen in a dream” or, “a dream he had had.”
