===== Naaman Came to Elisha [19-14] =====


{{:en:obs:obs-19-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


After the time of **[[:vi:key-terms:elijah|Elijah]]**, **[[:vi:key-terms:god|God]]** chose a man named Elisha to be his **[[:vi:key-terms:prophet|prophet]]**. God did many **[[:vi:key-terms:miracle|miracles]]** through Elisha. One of the miracles happened to **[[:vi:key-terms:naaman|Naaman]]**, **an enemy commander**, who had a horrible skin disease. **He had heard of Elisha**  so **he went and asked Elisha**  to **[[:vi:key-terms:heal|heal]]** him. Elisha told Naaman to dip himself seven times in the **[[:vi:key-terms:jordan-river|Jordan River]]**.


==== Translation Notes: ====


    * **an enemy commander**  - Naaman was a commander in the army of one of the countries that was Israel's enemy.
    * **He had heard of Elisha**  - This means that people had told Naaman that Elisha was able to perform miracles.
    * **he went and asked Elisha**  - That is, “he went to see Elisha and asked him.” Naaman had to go into Israel to find Elisha and ask him to do this.
