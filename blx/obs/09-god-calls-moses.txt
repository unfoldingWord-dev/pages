====== 9. God Calls Moses ======

{{:en:obs:obs-09-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

After Joseph died, all of his relatives stayed in Egypt. They and their descendants continued to live there for many years and had many children. They were called the Israelites.

Pangayarin pangamati Jose, silay kaganawan kamag-anak na, nanugêl sila pun sa Egipto. Sa kabuyutan un panawun, sila buy layi la, nanugêl silayna baydu buy inlumakê nay anak la. Kabay binêg silan Israelita. 

{{:en:obs:obs-09-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Many years later, a new Pharaoh came to power in Egypt. The Egyptians forgot about Joseph and all he had done to help them. They became afraid of the Israelites because there were so many of them. So the Egyptians made the Israelites their slaves.

Sa kabuyutan un panawun, silay taga Egipto, naliwan la yinay Jose buy si paglingkud na sa mani taga Egipto. Nalimwan yina mani taga Egiptu sa mani Israelita gawan inlumakê silayna. Kabay ati ya bayun puun, in-ipus na silay Israelita. 

{{:en:obs:obs-09-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The Egyptians forced the Israelites to build many buildings and even whole cities. The hard work made their lives miserable, but God blessed them, and they had even more children.

Silay Egiptio, pinilit la silay Israelita ya manyag un matag-ay ya bali buy makwal ya balayan. Pinaubra silan masakit, nuwa pinagpala silan Bapan Namalyari buy inlumakêy nay mani anak la.

{{:en:obs:obs-09-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Pharaoh saw that the Israelites were having many babies, so he ordered all Israelite baby boys to be killed by throwing them into the Nile River. 

Nuwa nakit nan Puun Paraun ya luyang silay nan inlumakêy ya anak lan Israelita. Kabay inutus na, "Nu liyaki ya anak un Israelita, isamwag ya kulaw sa ilug ta amên yan mati." 

{{:en:obs:obs-09-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

A certain Israelite woman gave birth to a baby boy. She and her husband hid the baby for as long as they could.

Amêsên, dilag gisay Israelita ya babayi ya nanganak un gisay liyaki. Siya buy asawa na Tanda nan babayi ya utus nan Puun Paraun ya patin ya anak ya liyaki. Buy sinabi na sa sarili na ya, “Itagu ku yay kulaw ku kaban a na pun tanda un Puun Paraun.”

{{:en:obs:obs-09-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then they put him in a floating basket in the Nile River. His older sister watched to see what would happen to him. 

Kabay nanyag yan basket bayu na kinuskusan un lilin ta amên a makasun un lanêm. Pamakayari, inulin na yay kulaw sa basket buy gintan na êt sa agid lanêm ya kagilid sa ilug. Si patêl nan kulaw ya naglagyun Miriam, naku ya êt sa ilug, ta pambantayan na ya nu sisabêt malyari kana. 

{{:en:obs:obs-09-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

A daughter of Pharaoh saw the basket and looked inside. When she saw the baby, she took him as her own son. She hired the baby’s own mother to nurse him. When the child grew up, Pharaoh’s daughter named him Moses.

Amêsên, si dalaga nan Puun Paraun, nakit na yay basket sa ilug. Buy inpabêg na ya kan ipus na. Sa inlatêng na ya, binuklatan nan dalaga nan Puun ya basket. Buy nakit na yay kulaw. Ya dinyag na, kinwa na yay kulaw. Ta pag-anak na yina. Moises ya impalagyu na.

{{:en:obs:obs-09-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

One day, when Moses had grown up, he saw an Egyptian beating an Israelite slave. Moses tried to save his fellow Israelite. 

Si Moises, linumakê ya sa bali nan Puun lan Taga-egipto. Amêsên, sa bayuntawu yina, nagpasyal ya sa lugal lan Israelita. Buy nakit nay gisan Israelita ya pansakitan un gisan Taga-egipto. Gawan ta tanda nan Moises ya parisu na yan Israelita, sinawpan na ya. 

{{:en:obs:obs-09-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When he thought nobody was looking, he killed the Egyptian and buried his body. But someone saw what had happened.

Buy sa indap nan Moises ya ayin nakakit, pinati na yay Taga-egipto buy in-ilbêng na ya. Nuwa dilag awêd nakakit sa dinyag na. ati ya dinyag nan Moises, nabalitan nan Puun Paraun.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When Pharaoh found out about it, he tried to kill Moses because of what he had done. Moses ran away from Egypt to the wilderness where he would be safe.

Buy sên natandan nan Puun Paraun ya nalyari, labay na yan patin si Moses. Nuwa sa natandan nan Moises ya patin na yan Puun Paraun, tinumakas ya, ta maku ya sa marayu ya lugal. Buy baydu ya nanugêl.

{{:en:obs:obs-09-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Moses became a shepherd in the wilderness far away from Egypt. He married and had two sons.

Si Moises, magpastul yan mani ayup sa kakyangan ya marayu sa Egipto. Buy baydu yina êt nakaasawa. Buy nakaanak ya un luway liyaki.

{{:en:obs:obs-09-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

One day while Moses was taking care of his sheep, he saw a bush that was on fire. But the bush did not burn up. Moses went to take a closer look. As he approached the burning bush, the voice of God said, “Moses, take off your shoes. You are standing on holy ground.”

Dilag gisay allu ya nan magpastul un tupa. Buy nakakit yan gisay pêrad kayu ya magdêkêtdêkêt. Nuwa tanda yan a sêlbên. Kabay dinanyan na ya. Buy sa dani yina, nalêngê na yan nagsabi ya Bapan Namalyari ya anti sa pêrad kayun magdêkêtdêkêt.

{{:en:obs:obs-09-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God said, “I have seen the suffering of My people. I will send you to Pharaoh so that you can bring the Israelites out of their slavery in Egypt. I will give them the land of Canaan, the land I promised to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.”

Buy sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari kan Moses, “Moises, mag-urung ka sa Egipto. Ta sika ya gamitin kun mamiligtas sa mani Israelita ya ipus lan Taga-egipto,” sinabi na Bapan Namalyari. Sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari ati, ta labay na ya mag-urung silay Israelita baydu sa Canaan ya lugal ya inpangakun nan Bapan Namalyari kalla. Ta abituy lugal ya inpangakun na sa mani Abraham, Isaac buy Jacob. 

{{:en:obs:obs-09-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Moses said, “What if the people ask me who sent me, what should I say?” God said, “I AM WHO I AM. Tell them, ‘I AM has sent me to you.’ This is My name forever.”

Nuwa si Moses, a na labay ya maku sa Egipto. Ta malimu ya.

{{:en:obs:obs-09-15.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Moses did not want to go, so God sent Moses’ brother, Aaron, to help him. God warned Moses and Aaron that Pharaoh would be stubborn.

Buy ya dinyag nan Bapan Namalyari, in-utus na si Aarun ya patêl nan Moises. Ta sabay ya manawup kan Moises. Nuwa sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari kallan Moses buy Aarun, ya sinabi na, “Gana kaw sumuku. Mibabata kaw. Ta nakdêy ya bêkê nan Puun Paraun sa Egipto.” 

//A Bible story from: Exodus 1-4//
