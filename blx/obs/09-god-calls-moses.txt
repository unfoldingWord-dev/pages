====== 9. God Calls Moses ======


{{:en:obs:obs-09-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


After Joseph died, all of his relatives stayed in Egypt. They and their descendants continued to live there for many years and had many children. They were called the Israelites.


Pangayarin pangamatin Jose,  ya  kaganawan kamag-anak na, nanugêl sila pun sa Egipto. Sa kinakarangan panawun, sila buy layi la, nanugêl silay na baydu buy inlumakêy nay anak la. Kabay binêg silan Israelita.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Many years later, a new Pharaoh came to power in Egypt. The Egyptians forgot about Joseph and all he had done to help them. They became afraid of the Israelites because there were so many of them. So the Egyptians made the Israelites their slaves.


Sa kinakarangan na  panawun, ya mani  taga Egipto, naliwan lay  na  si  Jose buy paglingkud na sa mani taga Egipto. Nalimwan  na  mani taga Egiptu sa mani Israelita gawan inlumakê silayna. Kabay ati ya bayun puun, inipus nay mani Israelita.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The Egyptians forced the Israelites to build many buildings and even whole cities. The hard work made their lives miserable, but God blessed them, and they had even more children.


ya mani  Egiptu, pinilit lay mani  Israelita un manyag matag-ay ya bali buy makwal la balayan. Pinaubra silan masakit, nuwa pinagpala silan Bapan Namalyari buy inlumakêy nay mani anak la.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Pharaoh saw that the Israelites were having many babies, so he ordered all Israelite baby boys to be killed by throwing them into the Nile River.


Nuwa nakit nan Puun Paraun ya luyang silay nan inlumakêy ya anak lan Israelita. Kabay in-utus na, "Nu liyaki ya anak un Israelita, isamwag ya kulaw sa ilug ta amên  mati."


{{:en:obs:obs-09-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


A certain Israelite woman gave birth to a baby boy. She and her husband hid the baby for as long as they could.


Amêsên, dilag gisay Israelita ya babayi ya nanganak un gisay liyaki. Siya buy asawa na, kaylangan  lan itagu ya kulaw un makarang nga  panawun.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then they put him in a floating basket in the Nile River. His older sister watched to see what would happen to him.


Kabay nanyag yan basket bayu na kinuskusan un lilin ta amên aya masunan  lanêm. Pamakayari, indin nay kulaw sa basket buy gintan na êt  ta basket buy inbul-is  sa agid lanêm . Si patêl nan kulaw ya naglagyun Miriam, naku ya êt sa ilug, ta pambantayan na ya nu sabêt  ta malyari kana.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


A daughter of Pharaoh saw the basket and looked inside. When she saw the baby, she took him as her own son. She hired the baby’s own mother to nurse him. When the child grew up, Pharaoh’s daughter named him Moses.


Amêsên, si dalaga nan Puun Paraun, inêlêw nay lalên basket. Sên nakit nay kulaw, ya dinyag na, kinwa nay kulaw. Ta pag-anak nayna. Binayaran nay inuli amên alagaan nay sarili nan kulaw. Sên dinumagul yinay kulaw, Moises ya impalagyu nan dilaga nan Paraun kana.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


One day, when Moses had grown up, he saw an Egyptian beating an Israelite slave. Moses tried to save his fellow Israelite.


Gisay allu, nagin bayuntawu yina si Moises. Nakit nay gisay Israelita, sabay ya ipus, ya pansakitin un gisay taga-Egiptu. Sinawpan nay ati tagawan tanda na ya kaparisu na yan Israelita.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When he thought nobody was looking, he killed the Egyptian and buried his body. But someone saw what had happened.


Buy sa indap nan Moises ya ayin nakakit, pinati na yay Taga-egipto buy in-ilbêng na ya. Nuwa dilag awêd nakakit sa dinyag na.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When Pharaoh found out about it, he tried to kill Moses because of what he had done. Moses ran away from Egypt to the wilderness where he would be safe.


Buy sên natandan nan Puun Paraun ya nalyari, labay na yan patin si Moses. Nuwa sa natandan nan Moises ya patin na yan Puun Paraun, tinumakas ya sa Egiptu, ta maku ya sa marayu ya lugal sa kakyangan buy baydu ya manugêl.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Moses became a shepherd in the wilderness far away from Egypt. He married and had two sons.


Si Moises, nagin magpastul yan mani ayup sa kakyangan ya marayu sa Egipto. Buy baydu yina êt nakaasawa. Buy nakaanak yan luway liyaki.


{{:en:obs:obs-09-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


One day while Moses was taking care of his sheep, he saw a bush that was on fire. But the bush did not burn up. Moses went to take a closer look. As he approached the burning bush, the voice of God said, “Moses, take off your shoes. You are standing on holy ground.”


Gisay allu yan magpastul un tupa. Buy nakakit yan gisay kayu ya nayêpa ya magdêkêtdêkêt. Nuwa a ya masêlbên. Kabay dinanyan nay ati buy sên narani yina, nalêngê nay tunuy nan Bapan Namalyari, ya sinabi na, <<Itas muy sapatus mu, gawan anti ka sa Banal la Lugal.>>


{{:en:obs:obs-09-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


God said, “I have seen the suffering of My people. I will send you to Pharaoh so that you can bring the Israelites out of their slavery in Egypt. I will give them the land of Canaan, the land I promised to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.”


Buy sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari kan Moses, “Nakit kuy kasakitan un mani tawu ku. Kabay sika ya maku kan Paraun. Ta sika ya mamiligtas sa mani Israelita ya ipus lan taga-Egiptu. Ta idin ku kallay lugal impangaku ku kallan ninunu la ya si Abraham, Isaac, buy Jacub.”


{{:en:obs:obs-09-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Moses said, “What if the people ask me who sent me, what should I say?” God said, “I AM WHO I AM. Tell them, ‘I AM has sent me to you.’ This is My name forever.”


Sinabin Moses, <<Nu mangutang sila kangku nu sisabêt ya namiutus kangku, sabêt ta sêpat kun sabin kalla?>> Sinabin Bapan Namalyari, <<Siku si siku. Sabin mu kalla, <Siku si siku ya namiutus kangku.> Ati ya lagyu pamêg la kangku makanuman.>>


{{:en:obs:obs-09-15.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Moses did not want to go, so God sent Moses’ brother, Aaron, to help him. God warned Moses and Aaron that Pharaoh would be stubborn.


Asê kalabay maku si Moises. Kabay in-utus na si Aarun ya patêl nan Moises. Ta sabay ya manawup kan Moises. Nuwa sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari kallan Moses buy Aarun, ya sinabi na, “Gana kaw sumuku. Mibabata kaw. Ta nakdêy ya bêkê nan Puun Paraun sa Egipto.”


//A Bible story from: Exodus 1-4//
