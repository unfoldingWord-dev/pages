====== 22. The Birth of John ======

{{:en:obs:obs-22-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

More than 400 years after the last prophet spoke to the Jews, an angel appeared to an old priest named Zechariah. He was a godly man, but his wife, Elizabeth, could not have any children.

Pagkatapos ng mahigit 400 taon na hindi nakipag-usap ang Diyos sa mga tao, saka pa lang may kinausap ang Diyos - si Zacarias. Siya ay isang matandang paring Judio. Malapit siya sa Diyos, pati ang asawa niyang si Elisabet. Kaya lang, wala silang anak.

Pamakayarin apat ta ralan na tawun na ase nakisabi si Bapana Namalyari sa mani tawu, bayu pun pinakisabyan Bapan Namalyari si Zacarias. Gisa ya sa tuway parin judiu. Matinek ya sa pangelew Bapan Namalyari,buy asawa nay si Elisabet. Nuwa, ayin silan anak 

{{:en:obs:obs-22-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The angel said to Zechariah, “Your wife will have a son. You will name him John. He will be the great prophet that comes before the Messiah!” Zechariah responded, “My wife and I are too old to have children! How will I know this will happen?”

Isang araw, kinausap ng isang anghel si Zacarias. Sinabi niya, “Manganganak ng isang lalaki ang asawa mo. Juan ang ipapangalan mo sa kanya. Siya ay magiging makapangyarihang propeta at mauuna siya sa Tagapagligtas.” Sumagot si Zacarias, “Masyado na kaming matanda ng asawa ko para magkakaanak. Paano mangyayari iyang sinasabi mo?”

Gisay allu, pikisabyan gisay ang-el si Zacarrias. Sinabi na, << Manganak gisay liyaki ya asawa mu. Juan na ipalagyu mu kana. Magin makapangyarian yan propeta buy muna ya sa  Tagapagligtas.>>
Nakitbay si Zacarias, <<Tuwa kay yana buy asawa ku amen makaanak. Parasaantun malyari ya abiin na pansabin mu?

{{:en:obs:obs-22-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The angel answered, “I was sent by God to bring you this good news. Because you did not believe me, you will not be able to speak until the child is born.” Immediately, Zechariah was unable to speak. Then the angel left. Zechariah returned home, and his wife became pregnant.

Sumagot ang anghel, “Pinapunta ako dito ng Diyos para sabihin ito sa iyo. Pero dahil hindi ka naniwala, hindi ka makakapagsalita hangga’t hindi ipinapanganak si Juan.” Nang oras ding iyon, hindi na nakapagsalita si Zacarias. Nang umalis ang anghel, umuwi na si Zacarias. Pagkalipas ng ilang buwan, nagbuntis na si Elisabet.

Nakitbay ya ang-el, <<Impalaku waku din Bapan Namalyari amen sabin kamuy ati. Nuwa gawan aka maniwala, aka makapagsabi anggan ase in-anak si  Juan.>> Sa uras et ta abaytu, ase nakapagsabi si Zacarias. Sen nimitay nay ang-el, nuli ya si Zacarias. Pamakayarin dakun umnuy buwan, nabuktut ta si Elisabet.


{{:en:obs:obs-22-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When Elizabeth was six months pregnant, the same angel appeared to Elizabeth’s relative, whose name was Mary. She was a virgin and was engaged to a man named Joseph. The angel said, “You will become pregnant and give birth to a son.”

{{:en:obs:obs-22-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The angel continued, “You are to name him Jesus. He will be the Messiah!” Mary replied, “How can this be, since I am a virgin?” The angel explained, “The Holy Spirit will come over you, and you will conceive. So your son will be called the Son of the Most High God.”

{{:en:obs:obs-22-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Not long after this, Mary went and visited Elizabeth. As soon as Elizabeth heard Mary’s greeting, Elizabeth’s baby jumped inside her. The women rejoiced together about what God had done for them. After visiting for three months, Mary returned home.

{{:en:obs:obs-22-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

After Elizabeth gave birth to her baby boy, they named him John as the angel commanded. Then God allowed Zechariah to speak again. He said, “Praise God, because He has remembered His people! You, my son, will be called the prophet of the Most High God! You will prepare the way for the Messiah!”
