====== 5. Ya Anak ka inpangaku ======

{{:en:obs:obs-05-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Abram and Sarai waited over twenty years, but they still had no son. God spoke to Abram and promised again that he would have a son and as many descendants as the stars in the sky. Abram believed God’s promise.

Luwampu yinay tawun ya nililabas ya pangaku ya ubat kan Bapan Namalyari, nuwa angga amêsên, ayin sila pun anak. Amêsên, si Abram, nalêngê na yina man ya nagsabi si Bapan Namalyari, “Magkaanak kan gisay liyaki, buy lumakêy sila lanu ya mani layi mu kaparisun sa malakêy un bêtêwên sa langit.” Buy sa nalêngê nayna man Abram ya pangaku nan Bapan Namalyari, paniwalan nayna man.

{{:en:obs:obs-05-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then Abram’s wife said to him, “Since the LORD has not allowed me to have children and now I am too old to have children, here is my servant, Hagar. Marry her also so she can have a child for me.”

Sa gisay allu, si Sarai, sinabi na kan Abram ya asawa na, “Parasaantu ati? Matuwa kina nuwa ayin kita pun anak. Siguru, a na kalabayan Bapan Namalyari ya manganak kaku. Ayin bali man. Anti baydi si Hagar ya ipus ku. Taksên mu ya ta amên yan magkaanak. Buy ya anak na, ituwad ku yan sarili kun anak.” 

{{:en:obs:obs-05-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

So Abram married Hagar. Hagar had a baby boy, and Abram named him Ishmael. Abram wanted God to bless Ishmael. God said, “I will bless him, but Ishmael is not the son of promise. I will not make my covenant with him.”

Ati ya sinabi nan asawa na, buy pinêtêg na yan tinakês ya ipus na. Kabay nanganak yan liyaki. Buy ya Abram, pinaglagyunan na yan Ismael ya anak nan Hagar. Sinabi nan Abram kan Bapan Namalyari, “Bapa, nu kalabayan mu, pagpalên mu si Ismael ya anak ku.” Buy nakitbay si Bapan Namalyari, “Awu, pagpalên ku si Ismael gawan sa kalabayan mu, nuwa alwan sabay ya kalabayan ku ya pakatuparên un pangaku ku kamu. 

{{:en:obs:obs-05-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

“Your wife, Sarai, will have a son—he will be the son of promise. Name him Isaac. My covenant will be with him, and he will become a great nation.” Then God changed Abram’s name to Abraham, which means “father of many.” God also changed Sarai’s name to Sarah, which means “princess.”

Ta, si Sara ya asawa mu, manganak yan liyaki ya palagyunan mun Isaac. Buy ya anak mu kan asawa mun pêtêg ya pakatuparên un pangaku ku kamu. Kabay ubat amêsên, palagyunan katan Abraham, ya labay sabin, ‘pangubatan un malakê ya mani layin mani bansa.’”

{{:en:obs:obs-05-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Finally, when Abraham was 100 years old and Sarah was 90, Sarah gave birth to Abraham’s son. They named him Isaac as God had told them to do.

Amêsên, si Sara, dilag yina idad da siyam mapu ya tawun. Buy si Abraham, dinalan nay êt ya tawun na sa ianak na yan Sara, ya liyaki ya pangaku nan Bapan Namalyari. Buy pinaglagyunan la yan Isaac. Ta parabaydu ya inutus nan Bapan Namalyari kalla.

{{:en:obs:obs-05-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When Isaac was a young man, God tested Abraham’s faith by saying, “Take Isaac, your only son, and kill him as a sacrifice to me.” Again Abraham obeyed what God told him to do.

Sa malakas yina Isaac, sinubukan nan Bapan Namalyari ya panampalataya nan Abraham. Sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari kana, “Kuwên mu si Isaac ya bugtung anak mu kan Sara. Ta ilaku mu ya sa bung-uy un Moriah. Ta sabay ya iandug mun ulamên na bilang iandug mu kangku.” Buy sinunul nayna man Abraham ya inutus nan Bapan Namalyari kana. 

{{:en:obs:obs-05-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

He tied up his son Isaac and laid him on an altar. He was about to kill his son when God said, “Stop! Do not hurt the boy! Now I know that you fear me because you did not keep your only son from me.”

Buy amêsên, inggapus na yay anak na, ta in-andug na ya sa pangulaman ta baydu nan patin. Nuwa sa patin na yina dayi ya anak na, inangaw un ang-el nan Bapan Namalyari, ya sinabi na, “Agana! Gana mu ya patin ya anak mu! Ta nakit nay nan Bapan Namalyari ya panampalataya mu, ta dilag kan limu kana. Ta a mu ya kinawaên ya iandug kana ya anak mu!”

{{:en:obs:obs-05-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Nearby Abraham saw a ram which was stuck in a bush. God had provided the ram to be the sacrifice instead of Isaac. Abraham happily offered the ram as a sacrifice.

Sa pangayari nan inangaw un ang-el nan Bapan Namalyari, nakit na yan Abraham ya gisay tupa ya nakasaêt ya sungay na sa mani sanga un kayu. Buy ya isip nan Abraham, sinabi na, “Ah! Ati ya kasagiliyan nan Isaac ya patin kun iandug ku dayi kan Bapan Namalyari.” Buy ya tupan nakasaêt ya in-andug na kan Bapan Namalyari.

{{:en:obs:obs-05-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then God said to Abraham, “Because you were willing to give me everything, even your only son, I promise to bless you. Your descendants will be more than the stars in the sky. Because you have obeyed me, all the families of the world will be blessed through your family.”

Pamakayari, sinabin Bapan Namalyari kan Abraham ya, “Gawan ta nakit kun natiisan mu yan iandug kangku ya anak mu, ipangaku ku kamu ya pagpalên kata. Palakên ku silay mani layi mu kaparisu sa kal-atan un bêtêwên sa langit. Buy sa kapamilatan sa kangêran un mani layi mu, pagpalên ku sila êt ya kaganawan na mani pamilya baydi sa babun luta, gawan sinunul muy mani utus ku kamu.”


//A Bible story from: Genesis 16-22//
