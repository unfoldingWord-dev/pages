====== 6. God Provides for Isaac ======

{{:en:obs:obs-06-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When Abraham had grown to be very old and Isaac was a man, Abraham sent one of his servants back to the land where his relatives lived to find a wife for his son, Isaac.

Sên matuwa yinay Abraham buy bayun tawu yina êt si Isaac, si Abraham, nami-utus yan ipus na ya maku sa lugal lan mangatwa nan Abraham. Ta maku yan mamili un mani dalaga sa mani kamag-anak na, ya malyarin asawaên nan Isaac ya anak nan Abraham kan Sara.

{{:en:obs:obs-06-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

After a very long journey, God led the servant to Rebekah, who was the granddaughter of Abraham’s brother.

Pamakayari un kabuyutan ya pamita un ipus, dilag yan nakit ya balun. Buy tinunggên ya baydu. Sên dinumêglêm yina, lumatêng mani babayi maku manayêb. Nanalangin yay ipus kan Bapan Namalyari, ya sinabi na, “Bapan Namalyari, sawpan mu ku. Anti ku baydi sa dani un balun ya panayêban lan mani dalaga. Buy ya labay ku, nu sisabêt dayi mam-i kangkun inêmên ku buy mamainêm sa mani kamelyo ku, abaytuy nay palatandaan ya labay mun idin kangku ya pag-asawa nan Isaac.” Sên manalangin ya pun, dilag yinan inlumatêng baydu ya maidu ya babayi. Rebekah ya lagyu na. Bapan na yan Nahor ya patêl nan Abraham. Buy si Rebekah, pinaynêm na yay ipus buy pinaynêm na êt ya kamelyo nan ipus.

{{:en:obs:obs-06-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

She agreed to leave her family and go back with the servant. Isaac married her as soon as she arrived.

Sa pagkalipas un pamainêm nan Rebekah, nuli ya. Buy nakilaku ya êt ya ipus. Sa pamakalatêng sila sa bali, ya ipus, inyawad na sa mani pamilya nan Rebekah, “Nu malyari dayi, kilaku ya si Rebekah kangku, ta asawaên na yan Isaac ya anak nan amung ku.” 
Buy ya indu nan Rebekah, pinatang nay êt ya Rebekah, ya sinabi na, “Rebekah, kilaku ka nayi kana o asê?” 
“Kilaku aku kana,” sinabin Rebekah. Sa lumatêng sila sa bali nan Abraham, nakit na yan Isaac ya dalaga ya naglagyun Rebekah. Buy tinanggap yan Isaac ya pag-asawa na.


{{:en:obs:obs-06-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

After a long time, Abraham died and all of the promises that God had made to him in the covenant were passed on to Isaac. But Isaac’s wife, Rebekah, could not have children. Isaac could not be the father of many nations if he did not even have a son.

Amêsên, sa kabuyutan un panawun, nati si Abraham. Buy ya kangêran na inpangakun nan Bapan Namalyari kan Abraham, tanda nay nan mana un Isaac ya naku kana. Nuwa si Isaac buy Rebekah, ayin sila pun anak. Inisip nan Isaac, ya sinabi na, “Ya masakit, ayin kay pun anak. Antuy ya kangêran ku? Parasaantu akun magin bapa un malakê ya mani nasyon?”

{{:en:obs:obs-06-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Isaac prayed for Rebekah, and God allowed her to get pregnant with twins.

Buy si Isaac, nanalangin ya kan Bapan Namalyari, ya sinabi na, “Apu, nu kalabayan mu, magkaanak kay.” Buy tinupad nan Bapan Namalyari ya panalangin nan Isaac, ta nabuktut si Rebekah.

{{:en:obs:obs-06-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Before they were born, God told Rebekah that the older son would serve the younger one.

Sên nabuktut ya pun si Rebekah un kambal, sinabi nay nan Bapan Namalyari kan Rebekah, “Ya panganay ya anak mun kambal, maglingkud ya kan ali na.” Buy nanganak yan kambal ya liyaki.

{{:en:obs:obs-06-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When they were born, the older son came out red and hairy—they named him Esau. The younger son had smooth skin, and they named him Jacob.

Sên in-anak silay na, ya panganay, pinaglagyunan la yan Esau. Buy naurit ya buy masabut ya lawini na. Ya bunsu, pinaglagyunan la yan Jacob.


//A Bible story from: Genesis 24:1-25:26//
