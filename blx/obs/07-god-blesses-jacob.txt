====== 7. Pinagpalan  Bapan Namalyari  si Jacob ======

{{:en:obs:obs-07-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


As the boys grew up, Rebekah loved Jacob, but Isaac loved Esau. Jacob loved to stay at home, but Esau loved to hunt.

Habang lumalaki ang mga anak nina Isaac at Rebecca, naging paborito ni Rebecca si Jacob. Naging paborito naman ni Isaac si Esau. Mas gusto ni Jacob ang mga gawaing-bahay. Mahilig namang mangaso si Esau.

Kaban mandumagul la maanak lan Isaac buy si Rebecca, mas kakaidwan Rebecca si Jacob. Buy kakaidwan naman êt Isaac si Esau. Labay labay yaman Jacob ba ubran pagbali. Labay labay yaman Esau wa manulipêt.


{{:en:obs:obs-07-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


One day, when Esau came back from hunting, he was very hungry. Esau said to Jacob, “Please give me some of the food you have made.” Jacob responded, “First, give me your rights as the oldest son.” So Esau gave Jacob his rights as the oldest son. Then Jacob gave him some food.

Isang araw, gutom na gutom si Esau nang umuwi siya mula sa pangangaso. Kaya sinabi niya kayJacob, “Bigyan mo naman ako ng pagkaing niluluto mo.” Sumagot si Jacob, “Ibigay mo muna sa akin ang pagiging panganay mo.” Pumayag si Esau, kaya binigyan naman siya ni Jacob ng pagkain.

Gisay allu, nalayang layang nga si Esau sên nuli yan ubat sa panulipêt. Kabay sinabi na kan Jacob, “Biyan muku watin kanên na pan-ilutu mu.” Nakitbay si Jacob, “Idin mu pun kangkuy pangapanganay mu.” Nalabay yaman si Esau, kabay binyanan yan Jacob un kanên.



{{:en:obs:obs-07-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Isaac wanted to give his blessing to Esau. But before he did, Rebekah and Jacob tricked him by having Jacob pretend to be Esau. Isaac was old and could no longer see. So Jacob put on Esau’s clothes and put goat skins on his neck and hands.

Nang matanda na si Isaac, gusto niyang pagpalain si Esau dahil ito ang panganay. Pero bago niya iyon magawa, nilinlang siya nina Rebecca at Jacob. Nagkunwari si Jacob na siya si Esau. Dahil matanda na si Isaac, hindi na niya nakikita ang kausap niya. Isinuot ni Jacob ang damit ni Esau at naglagay siya ng balat ng kambing sa leeg at mga braso niya.

Sên tuway na si Isaac, labay nan pagpalên si Esau gawan siyay panganay. Nuwa bayu na madyag ga abitu, nalinlang nginan Rebecca buy si Jacob. Nagtalingkayu si Jacob ya siya si Esau. Gawan tuway na si Isaac, anay na makitan na kasabi na. Insulud Jacob ya yaming Esau buy binyanan nan balat kambing ya bêng na buy takyay na.



{{:en:obs:obs-07-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Jacob came to Isaac and said, “I am Esau, I have come to receive your blessing.” When Isaac felt the goat hair and smelled the clothes, he thought it was Esau and blessed him.

Paglapit ni Jacob kay Isaac, sinabi niya, “Ako po si Esau. Pumunta po ako dito para ako po ay pagpalain ninyo.” Akala ni Isaac ay si Esau nga ang kausap niya. Nahawakan kasi niya ang balahibo ng kambing sa braso ni Jacob. Naamoy din niya ang damit ni Esau na suot ni Jacob. Kaya pinagpala ni Isaac si Jacob.

Pamakadanin Jacob kan Isaac, sinabi na, “Siku si Esau. Naku waku di amên pagpalên mu.” Indap Isaac si Esau ya kasabi na. Gawan natalan nay sabut kambing sa takyay Jacob. Nadêp na êt ta yaming Esau ya insulud Jacob. Kabay pinagpalan Isaac si Jacob.



{{:en:obs:obs-07-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Esau hated Jacob because Jacob had taken his rights as oldest son and also his blessing. So he planned to kill Jacob after their father died.

Galit na galit si Esau sa ginawa ni Jacob. Sabi niya, “Kinuha na nga niya sa akin ang pagiging panganay, ngayon kinuha pa din niya ang pagpapalang para sa akin!” Binalak ni Esau na patayin si Jacob oras na mamatay ang kanilang ama.

Natubag tubag si Esau sa dinyag nan Jacob. Sinabi na, “Kingwa nayna bayna kangkuy pangapanganay, amêsên kingwa napun êt ta pagpapalay para kangku!” Impalanun Esau wa patin na si Jacob nu nati yay bapa la.



{{:en:obs:obs-07-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


While Jacob was there, he married four women who gave birth to a total of twelve sons and a daughter. God made him very wealthy.


Sên idi ya  baydu si  Jacob  sa lugal  kamag-anak indu na, nakaanak yan labinluway liyaki buy gisay babayi. Buy binumandi ya baydu.


{{:en:obs:obs-07-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


After twenty years away from his home in Canaan, Jacob returned there with his family, his servants, and all his herds of animals.


Sen dilag ginan luwampuy tawun baydu sa lugal  kamag-anak  Rebekah, nuli silay na sa Canaan ya lugal la inpangakun  Bapan Namalyari, kaawyun nay  pamilya na, mani ipus buy mani ayup na.


{{:en:obs:obs-07-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


He was very afraid because he thought Esau still wanted to kill him. So he sent Esau many herds of animals as a gift. The servants who brought the animals said to Esau, “These animals are a gift from your servant, Jacob. He is coming soon.”


Kaban idi  sila sa dan, malimu si Jacob. Ta maganaka nay sinabin  kaka na ya patin na ya. Si dinyag na, namipauna  yan malakêy kambing buy  tupa. Dinalan buy luwampuy bilang  kambing. Para êt bayduy  bilang  tupa. Ta ati ya ilangad na sa  kaka na.


{{:en:obs:obs-07-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


But Esau had already forgiven Jacob, and they were happily reunited. Jacob then lived peacefully in Canaan. Then Isaac died, and Jacob and Esau buried him. The covenant promises God had promised to Abraham and then to Isaac now passed on to Jacob.


Nuwa,  ase tandan Jacob ba manay nan  pinatawad kaka na. Sên  napikakit sila matula silan niawyun. Buy si Jacob, dilag ginan kapatêtbêkan na manugêl  sa lugal la inpangakun  Bapan Namalyari kalla.


//A Bible story from: Genesis 25:27-33:20//
