====== 1. Dinyag Bapan Namalyari ya langit buy luta ======


====== 1. Ya Pinangubatan  Kaganawan ======


----


{{:en:obs:obs-01-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


This is how the beginning of everything happened. God created the universe and everything in it in six days. At first, the earth was dark and empty, and nothing had been formed in it. But God's Spirit was there over the water.


Paradi ya umpisan  kaganawan, sabaytun nunan panawun, dinyag  Bapan Namalyari ya langit buy luta sa  anem ma allu sa panawun na abaytu, ayin kapun  dakun  sabet ta makitan sa   babun luta ta pawan lanêm buy nadêglêm.  Nuwa paramanbaydu ya Ispiritun Bapan Namalyari idi yina  sa babun lanêm.




{{:en:obs:obs-01-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then God spoke and said, "Let there be light!" And there was light. God saw that the light was good and called it "day." He separated it from the darkness, which He called "night." God created the light on the first day of creation.


Amêsên, sinabin Bapan Namalyari, “Mabiyan sawang.”  kabay sinumawang yabay,   Naidu sa pangelew  Bapan Namalyari ya dinyag na.  Ya sawang, pinaglagyunan nan allu. Ya nadêglêm êt, pinaglagyunan nan yabi. Abiin na dinyag na   sa nunan allu.





{{:en:obs:obs-01-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the second day of creation, God spoke and created the sky above the earth.




Sa ikaluway allu, pinis-yay Bapan Namalyari ya lanêm.  Si kapaka,  indin na sa tengel  buy si kapaka idi sa babun luta.     ya lanem ma idi  sa tengel   bineg nan langit  buy  si  lanem ma idi  sa aypa bineg nan kalawakan/ buy kalwangan/ langit.  

{{:en:obs:obs-01-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the third day, God spoke and separated the water from the dry land. He called the dry land "earth," and He called the water "seas."


Sa ikatlun allu, pinisyay  Bapan Namalyari ya lanêm ta amên mikawani ya luta sa lanêm ya anti baydi sa nayêpa.


Sên ikatluy alluy na êt taman, pinikawanin Bapan Namalyari ya lanêm ma idi sa aypa buy ya luta. Binêg nan “babun luta” ya luta. Bineg na êt taman “dagat” ya lanêm. Naidwan Bapan Namalyari sa dinyag na.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then God said, "Let the earth produce all kinds of trees and plants." And that is what happened. God saw that what He had created was good, and He was pleased with it all.


Sên nag-awas ya luta, sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari, “Ya luta, tubwanan yan lamun, kayu, buy tanaman ya managêy un malyarin pangwanan bini.” Amêsên, nalyari ya ati ya sinabin Bapan Namalyari.


Pamakayari, sinabin Bapan Namalyari, “Labay kun tumubu sa babun lutay kaganawan kalasin puun kayu buy tanaman.” Parabaydu yabay ya nalyari. Naidwan êt Bapan Namalyari sa dinyag na.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the fourth day of creation, God spoke and made the sun, the moon, and the stars. God made them to give light to the earth and to mark day and night, seasons and years.


Sa ikaapat ta allu, sinabin Bapan Namalyari ya mabiyan allu sa langit ya mam-in sawang sa babun luta. Sinabi na pun ya mabiyan buwan sa langit buy bitêwên ya mam-in sawang sa babun luta. Ati ya sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari. Kabay nadyag gina.


Sên ikaapat tay allu, dinyag ga êt Bapan Namalyari ya allu, buwan, buy mani bêtêwên. Ya kaganawan abiin sabay ya mam-in sawang sa babun luta. Ati êt ta patanda sa mani allu buy yabi, sa mani panawun buy sa mani tawun. Naidwan na êt Bapan Namalyari sa dinyag na.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the fifth day, God created everything that swims in the water and all the birds. God saw that it was good, and He blessed them.


Sa ikalimay allu, sinabin Bapan Namalyari ya mabiyan mangaragul la kêna sa lanêm buy mabiyan un sadyay malakêy kêna ya nabyay sa lanêm. Sinabi na pun Bapan Namalyari ya mabiyan un magmikakanayun ya manuk ya manlumpad. Sên nalyari ya abaytu, sinabi na kalla, “Manganak kaw ta amên kaw lumakê.”


Sên ikalimay alluy na êt, sinabin Bapan Namalyari, “Mabiyan kaganawan kalasin ayup pa idi sa dagat buy kaganawan kalasin ibun manuk.” Parabaydu yabay ya nalyari! Naidwan na êt Bapan Namalyari sa dinyag na. Indin Bapan Namalyari ya kaganawan kaylangan mani ayup pa dinyag na.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the sixth day of creation, God said, "Let there be all kinds of land animals!" And it happened just like God said. Some were farm animals, some crawled on the ground, and some were wild. And God saw that it was good.


Sa ikaanêm ma allu, sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari ya mabiyan un saysay ya kaparisun baka, dulêg, kabayu, uysa, bakulaw, babuy, utan, asu, pusa, buy kaganawan na klasin saysay ya mangakit tamu amêsên sa babun luta. Sên nakit un Bapan Namalyari ya ati ya dinyag na, natula ya.


Sên ikaanêm ma alluy na êt, sinabin Bapan Namalyari, “Mabiyan kaganawan kalasin ayup sa luta!” Parabaydu yabay ya nalyari. Dilag êt mani ayup sa panggitnan, dilag mani ayup ya mandumakap, buy dilag mani ayup pa mangasilêb (sa kakaywan). Naidwan na êt Bapan Namalyari sa dinyag na.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then God said, "Let us make human beings in our image to be like us. They will have authority over the earth and all the animals."


Amêsên, sinabin Bapan Namalyari, “Manyag gakinan tawu.” Sên nayari nan sinabi ya abaytu, nangwa yan luta ya daygên nan lawini un tawu. Ginamit nay ati ta amên madyag ya mani partin lawini ya kaparisu sa ulu, mata, têk, bêbêy, balungus, gamêt, buy bitis. Sên nalyari nan dinyag ga abaytu, pinakangêd nan kinayêp ya balungus tawu ta amên mabiyan yan biyay.


Pamakayari, sinabin Bapan Namalyari, “Manyag kitamun mani tawu. Daygên tamu silan kalupa tamu. Silay mamaala sa babun luta buy sa kaganawan mani ayup.”


{{:en:obs:obs-01-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


So God took some dirt, formed it into a man, and breathed life into him. This man's name was Adam. God planted a garden where Adam could live, and put him there to care for it.


Amêsên, pinabêbêlêw na yay tawu. Abituy tawu ya unan dinyag nan Bapan Namalyari, liyaki ya. Pinaglagyunan nan Adan. Amêsên, si Bapan Namalyari ya nanyag mangêd da lugal ya bibiyan nan Adan ta amên mabantayan na yan mangêd. Sabaytuy lugal ya pinaglagyunan nan Eden. Sabaytun nuna, silay saysay ya dinyag nan Bapan Namalyari, gintan na sila kan Adan. Ta si Adan ya mam-in lagyu sa balang gisay saysay na.


Kabay nangwan luta si Bapan Namalyari. Dinyag nan lupan liyaki ya abaytu. Pamakayari, binyanan nan inawanên na ati amên mabyay ya. Binêg nan “Adan” na liyaki. Nanyag êt si Bapan Namalyarin panamnan amên manugêl baydu si Adan. Siyay manaysay sa panamnan.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


In the middle of the garden, God planted two special trees—the tree of life and the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. God told Adam that he could eat from any tree in the garden except from the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. If he ate from this tree, he would die.


Sa libutad un abaytuy lugal, dilag luway puun kayu. Ya gisa, mam-in biyay. Ya gisa êt, mam-in kabiyasnan tungkul sa mangêd buy nadawak. Amêsên, sinabin Bapan Namalyari kalla, “Ya tagêy un kaganawan kayu baydi, malyari yun kanên, nuwa ya tagêy un kayun mam-in kabiyasnan tungkul sa mangêd buy narawak, a yu kanên. Ta nu mangan kaw tagêy un kayun habiin, mati kaw.”


Sa libutad un panamnan dilag luway puun kayuy ayin kaparisu ya intanêm Bapan Namalyari. Ya nunan puun kayu, sabay ya puun biyay. Ya ikaluway puun kayu sabay ya katandan mangêd buy nadawak. Sinabin Bapan Namalyari kan Adan, “Malyari kan mangan kaganawan tagêy puun kayu di. Alwa ta dayin tagêy un puun kayun katandan mangêd buy nadawak.” Sinabi êt Bapan Namalyari ya nu mangan yan tagêy kayun ati mati ya.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then God said, "It is not good for man to be alone." But none of the animals could be Adam's helper.


Amêsên, sinabi nan Bapan Namalyari, “Alwan mangêd da tawuy gigisa na. Kabay manyag gakun kaawyun nan Adan.”


Sinabin Bapan Namalyari, “Asê mangêd da kay gigisay tawu.” Nuwa ayin mapagkasawup si Adan sa mani ayup pa kaawyun na.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


So God made Adam fall into a deep sleep. Then God took one of Adam's ribs and made it into a woman and brought her to him.


Amêsên, si Adan, pinabêlêw na yan Bapan Namalyari un mangêd, bayu nangwa yan gisay tagyang nan Adan, ta dinyag nan babayi. Sên nimata si Adan, nakit na ya dilag ginan kaawyun.


Kabay pinabêlêw Bapan Namalyari si Adan. Kaban maêlêk yan bêlêw, kingwan Bapan Namalyari ya gisa sa but-un tagyang Adan. Dinyag nan babayi ya ati. Pamakaimatan Adan, inpakit kanan Bapan Namalyari ya babayi.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When Adam saw her, he said, "At last! This one is like me! Let her be called 'Woman,' for she was made from Man." This is why a man leaves his father and mother and becomes one with his wife.


Naidu ya babayi ya dinyag nan Bapan Namalyari. Natula si Adan ta dilag ginan kaawyun. Buy Eva ya impalagyu nan Adan sa babayin pagkaawyun na.


Sên nakitan Adan na babayi sadyay tula na. Sinabi na, “Abaytun kayêk! dilag gakinan mapagkaawyun na kaparisu ku. Bêgên kuyan ‘Babayi’ gawan naubat ya sa ‘Liyaki’.” Gawan sa nalyari ati, Pallakwanan mani lawyaki ya mani mangatwa la. Kilalaku sila sa asawa la. Buy pakadyag gigisay liyaki buy babayi.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-15.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


God made man and woman in His own image, and He saw that it was very good. He blessed them and told them, "Have many children and grandchildren and fill the earth!" This all happened on the sixth day of creation.


Amêsên, sinabin Bapan Namalyari kalla, “Magpakalakê kaw buy sikaw ya magbantay sa kaganawan na mani ayup. Biyan kataw kanên yu buy kanên un mani ayup.”


Dinyag Bapan Namalyari ya liyaki buy babayi un kalupa na. Pinagpalan Bapan Namalyari ya luwa buy sinabi na kalla, “Manganak lakê ya maging anak yu angga sa mapapnu yun tawuy babun luta.” Sadyay tulan Bapan Namalyari sa kaganawan dinyag na. Abituy nalyari sa ikaanêm ma allu.


{{:en:obs:obs-01-16.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When the seventh day came, God had already finished His work. So God rested from all He had been doing. He blessed the seventh day and made it holy, because on this day He rested from His work. This is how God created the universe and everything in it.


Sa ikapitun allu, nagpaynawa si Bapan Namalyari, ta nayari nay dinyag na.


Sên niras say ikapituy allu, nayari yan Bapan Namalyari ya kaganawan dinyag na, kabay nagpaynawa ya. Pinagpala nay ikapituy allu. Dinyag nan banal la ati, gawan nagpaynawa ya sa allun abitu. Parabaysên dinyag Bapan Namalyari ya kaganawan bagay.


//A Bible story from: Genesis 1-2//
