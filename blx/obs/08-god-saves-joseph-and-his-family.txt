====== 8. God Saves Joseph and His Family ======
Inligtas Bapan Namalyari  si  Jose buy  Pamilya na

{{:en:obs:obs-08-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Many years later, when Jacob was an old man, he sent his favorite son, Joseph, to check on his brothers who were taking care of the herds.


Sen tuway na  si Jacob,  gisay allu in-utus nay kakaidwan nay  anak  ya  naglagyun Jose. Sinabi na kana, “Anak ku, lakwên mun elewen na mapatel  muy magpastul.”


{{:en:obs:obs-08-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Joseph’s brothers hated him because their father loved him most and because Joseph had dreamed that he would be their ruler. When Joseph found his brothers, they kidnapped him and sold him to some slave traders.


Ya mapatel na, tanda lay  kakaidwan  bapa la  si Jose buy tanda la êt ta  sa  taynêp nan Jose  sabay  siyay   mag-ari kalla. Kabay masakit ta  nakêm la  kan Jose.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Before they returned home, the brothers tore Joseph’s clothes and dipped them in blood. Then they showed the clothes to their father and lied to him saying a wild animal had killed Joseph. Jacob was very sad.


Amêsên,  inlumatêng  nga si Jose sa mapatel  na, tinalan yan  mapatel na buy kinulbutan.  Dilag dinuman bayduy   manaliw, kingwa la ya.Ta insaliw la ya sa mani manaliw. Buy gintan  yan manaliw sa Egipto. Amêsên, sen nuli yay mapatel na sa bali la, inpakit lay  yaming  Jose sa  bapa la, buy sinabi la, “Bapay, nakit yan sa dan  na yaming  Jose.” Sinabin  Jacob,  “Dat  dilag gisay saysay ya matubag ga namati kana.” Buy  sadyay lungkut bapa la.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The slave traders took Joseph to Egypt. Egypt was a large, powerful country located beside the Nile River. The slave traders sold Joseph as a slave to a wealthy government official. Joseph served his master well, and God blessed Joseph.


Amêsên,   sen inlumateng nga sa Egiptuy  manaliw, insaliw la  et  si Jose sa gisay mabandi, ya naglagyun Potiphar.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


His master’s wife tried to sleep with Joseph, but Joseph refused to sin against God in this way. She became angry and had Joseph arrested and sent to prison. Even in prison, Joseph remained faithful to God, and God blessed him.


Sen idi ya si Jose sa Egipto, mapagkatiwalan yan  amung na. Nuwa ya asawan  amung na, labay na yan gitan  si  Jose. Amêsên, si Jose, a ya kalabay  mangasalanan kan Bapan Namalyari. Buy si asawan Potiphar, sinangkanan  na  si Jose sa ayin kapêtêgan. Kabay nasukul ya. Nuwa agyan idi  ya  sa sukulan, pagtiwalan ya pun  êt murin, ta pagpalên  yan Bapan Namalyari.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Years later, Joseph was still in prison, even though he had done nothing wrong. The king of Egypt, who was called the Pharaoh, had two dreams that disturbed him greatly. None of his advisors could tell him the meaning of the dreams.


Sa panawun na abaytu, ya Puun Paraun ya mag-ari  sa Egipto, nanaynêp ya buy aya mapipaldan  ta ayin mamipaintindi kana  nu sabêt ta labay sabin nataynêp na.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


God had given Joseph the ability to interpret dreams, so Pharaoh had Joseph brought to him from the prison. Joseph interpreted the dreams for him and said, “God is going to send seven years of good harvests followed by seven years of famine.”


Tandan  Jose nu sabet ta labay sabin nataynêp Paraun. Ta pan-ipatandan Bapan Namalyari kanay labay sabin abitu. Buy  nabalitan  Puun Paraun,  ya si Jose dilag yan kabiyasnan mamipatanda nu sabet ta labay sabin nataynêp na. Kabay inpaawas na si Jose sa sukulan, ta labay nan Puun Paraun ya ipatandan  Jose  kanay  labay sabin nataynêp na. Buy sên idi ya  si  Jose sa arapan na, sinabi na kan Jose, “Jose, ipatanda mu dayi kangku  nu sabet ta labay sabin  taynêp ku.” Sinabin  Jose kana, “Panggalangên na Puun Paraun, inpatandan Bapan Namalyari kangku ya labay sabin taynêp mu. Paradi ya labay sabin. Sa lalên  pituy tawun, mangêd da pupul baydi sa Egipto, nuwa dilag êt kasunul, sa lalên pituy tawun  ayin nan   mangêd da pupul.”


{{:en:obs:obs-08-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Pharaoh was so impressed with Joseph that he made him the second most powerful man in all of Egypt!


Sên natandan  Puun Paraun,  ya labay sabin nataynêp na, natula ya kan Jose. Kabay dinyag na yan ikaluwan pinakamatag-ay ya mamaala baydu sa bansan Egipto.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


During the seven years of good harvests, Joseph told the people to store up large amounts of food. Then when the seven years of famine came, Joseph sold the food to the people so they would have enough to eat.


Ya kal-atan  pupul sa Egipto sa lalên  pituy tawun, si Jose ya mamantay un natagan buy siya êt murin na mamaala sa mani nanipun un kaatag ga pupul baydu ta amên la maisimpan ya kaatag ga pupul sa Egipto. Buy sen nilateng ngay  kasunul la  pituy tawun, magkasakit  tay  mani tawu.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The famine was severe not only in Egypt, but also in Canaan where Jacob and his family lived.


Ta  sadyay layang la. Alwan kay sa Egipto. Ta agyan  sa Canaan êt  ya lugal la inpangakun  Bapan Namalyari ya bibiyan lan Jacob,  makatanam sila êt sadyay layang. Kabay  ya dinyag nan Jose, insaliw na sa mani magkasakit sa Egiptuy  tagan ta amên  dilag  silan maêkan.

So Jacob sent his sons to Egypt to buy food. When the brothers stood before Joseph to buy food, they did not recognize him. But Joseph recognized them.


Buy sên nabalitan  Jacob ba  malakêy maêkan baydu sa Egipto, in-utus nay maanak  nay  manaliw kan Jose. Kabay nakuy mapatel Jose sa  bansan Egipto. Pamakalatêng  la  baydu, a la  tanday  si Jose awêd da mamaala sa bansan Egipto. Nuwa, si Jose, tanda nay sabay silay mani mapatêl nay nisaliw kana.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


After testing them to see if they had changed, Joseph said to them, “I am your brother, Joseph! Do not be afraid. You tried to do evil when you sold me as a slave, but God used the evil for good! Come and live in Egypt so I can provide for you and your families.”


Sinubukan  Jose ya mapatêl na, nu pêtêg gan   nagbayuy ugali la. Sên  natandan nay nagbayu silay na, nagpatanda si Jose kalla. Buy sinagyat na silan manugêl sa Egipto. Ta sawpan na sila.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When the brothers returned home and told Jacob that Joseph was still alive, he was very happy.


Sên nuli ya mapatel na , inbalita la sa bapa la, ya sinabi la, “Bapay, nabyay  pun awêd si Jose. Pansagyatên na kitamun manugêl baydu sa Egipto ta amên  masawpan na kitamu.” Sen natandan  Jacob ya nabyay  pun si Jose, sadyay tula na.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Even though Jacob was an old man, he moved to Egypt with all of his family, and they all lived there. Before Jacob died, he blessed each of his sons.


Agyan tuway na si  Jacob, gawan sa tula na, naku ya sa Egipto, kaawyun nay pamilya na. Buy nanugêl sila baydu. Nuwa bayu  nati si Jacob, pinagpala na  pun na kaganawan  anak na.


{{:en:obs:obs-08-15.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The covenant promises that God gave to Abraham were passed on to Isaac, then to Jacob, and then to Jacob’s twelve sons and their families. The descendants of the twelve sons became the twelve tribes of Israel.


Amêsên, ya mani pangakun Bapan Namalyari kan Abraham, naku kan Isaac. Buy sên nati si Isaac, naku êt kan Jacob. Buy sên nati si Jacob, naku êt sa labinluway anak na buy sa  pamilya la. Ati ya labinluway anak  Jacob ya nagin labinluway  tribun pambêgên Israel. Ta mani anak  silan Jacob ya pamêgên êt Israel.
