====== ۴۳.کلیسا شروع به کار می کند ======


{{:en:obs:obs-43-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


بعد از اینکه عیسی به آسمان بازگشت،شاگردان طبق دستور عیسی در اورشلیم ماندند.پیروان عیسی با هم جمع می شدند و به طور مداوم دعا می کردند.


{{:en:obs:obs-43-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


در آن زمان عید یهودیان،پنطیکاست فرا رسید.پنطیکاست عید شکرگزاری بود.یهودیان از سرتاسر دنیا به اورشلیم می آمدند تا این جشن را با هم برگزار کنند.


{{:en:obs:obs-43-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


وقتی ایمانداران دور هم جمع شده بودند،ناگهان صدایی شبیه صدای وزش باد در هوا پیچید و خانه را پر کرد.سپس چیزی شبیه شعله های آتش بر سرشان شعله ور شد.آنگاه همه از روح القدوس پر شدند و شروع به سخن گفتن به زبانهایی کردند که با آنها آشنایی نداشتند.


{{:en:obs:obs-43-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When the people in Jerusalem heard about this, a crowd gathered, wondering what was happening. The people were astonished when they heard the believers speaking in their own native languages, proclaiming the wonderful works of God.


{{:en:obs:obs-43-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Some accused the disciples of being drunk, but Peter stood up and said, “Listen to me! These people are not drunk! This is a fulfillment of the prophecy made by Joel which says, ‘In the last days, I will pour out My Spirit.’”


{{:en:obs:obs-43-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


“Men of Israel, Jesus was a man who did many mighty signs and wonders by the power of God, as you have seen and already know. You crucified him!”


{{:en:obs:obs-43-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


“But God raised him to life again to fulfill the prophecy which says, ‘You will not let Your Holy One rot in the grave.’ God raised this Jesus from the dead. We are witnesses.”


{{:en:obs:obs-43-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


“Jesus is now exalted to the right hand of God the Father. And the Father has sent the promised Holy Spirit which you are seeing and hearing.”


{{:en:obs:obs-43-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


“Know for certain that God has made this Jesus, the one you crucified, both Master and Messiah!”


{{:en:obs:obs-43-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The people listening to Peter were deeply moved and asked, “Brothers, what should we do?”


{{:en:obs:obs-43-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Peter said, “Repent and be baptized every one of you in the name of Jesus Christ for the forgiveness of sins, and you will receive the gift of the Holy Spirit.”


{{:en:obs:obs-43-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


About 3,000 people believed what Peter said. They were baptized and were added to the people of God, which is the Church.


{{:en:obs:obs-43-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The believers devoted themselves to the disciples’ teaching, fellowship, eating together, and prayer. They all met together and shared everything they had. They praised God and were well received by those who were not Christians.


//A Bible story from: Acts 2//
