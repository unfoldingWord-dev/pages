====== demon-possessed ======


===== Definition: =====


A person who is demon-possessed has a demon or evil spirit that controls what he does and thinks.


    * Often a demon-possessed person will hurt himself or other people because the demon forces him to do that.
    * Jesus healed demon-possessed people by forcing the demons to come out of them.
    * This could also be translated as, "demon-controlled" or, “controlled by a demon” or, “having a demon.”


[See also: **[[:hi:key-terms:demon|demon]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


    * **[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:26-09|[26-09]]]** Many people who had **demons in them**  were brought to Jesus.
    * **[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:32-02|[32-02]]]** When they reached the other side of the lake, a **demon-possessed**  man came running up to Jesus.
    * **[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:32-06|[32-06]]]** The man **with the demon**  cried out in a loud voice, “What do you want with me, Jesus, Son of the Most High God? Please do not torture me!”
    * **[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:32-09|[32-09]]]** The people from the town came and saw the man who used to **have the demons**.
    * **[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:47-03|[47-03]]]** Every day as they (Paul and Silas) walked there, a slave girl **possessed by a demon** followed them.
