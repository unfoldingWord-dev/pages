====== Sabbath ======


==== (Sabbath, day of rest, Sabbath day) ====


===== Definition: =====

The Sabbath day was a special day each week that God told the Israelites to set apart as a day of rest.

  * Each Sabbath was the seventh (last) day of the week.
  * After God finished creating the world in six days, he rested on the seventh day. In the same way, God commanded the Israelites to set aside the seventh day as a special day to rest and worship him.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:13-05|[13-05]]]**  "Always be sure to keep the **Sabbath day** holy. That is, do all your work in six days, for the seventh day is a day for you to rest and to honor me."
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]**  Jesus went to the town of Nazareth where he had lived during his childhood. On the **Sabbath**, he went to the place of worship.
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:41-03|[41-03]]]**  The day after Jesus was buried was a **Sabbath** day, and the Jews were not permitted to go to the tomb on that day.
