====== prophet ======


==== (prophet, prophecy, prophesy) ====


===== Definition: =====

A prophet is a person who speaks God's messages to people. He tells them what God wants them to know. God is the one who chooses and appoints the prophet to do this.

  * "Prophet" could be translated as "God's spokesman."
  * A "prophecy" is the message that God tells the prophet to speak. 
  * To "prophesy" is the act of communicating God's message.
  * Prophets told people about things that God had said would happen in the future. 
  * Prophets also warned people to turn away from their sins and obey God. 
  * There were also false prophets who claimed to speak messages from false gods such as Baal.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:12-12|[12-12]]]** When the Israelites saw that the Egyptians were dead, they trusted in God and believed that Moses was a **prophet** of God. 
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was very angry about what David had done, so he sent the **prophet** Nathan to tell David how evil his sin was.
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:19-01|[19-01]]]** Throughout the history of the Israelites, God sent them **prophets**. The **prophets** heard messages from God and then told the people God's messages.
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:19-06|[19-06]]]** All the people of the entire kingdom of Israel, including the 450 **prophets** of Baal, came to Mount Carmel.
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:19-17|[19-17]]]** Most of the time, the people did not obey God. They often mistreated the **prophets** and sometimes even killed them.
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:21-09|[21-09]]]** The **prophet** Isaiah **prophesied** that the Messiah would be born from a virgin.
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:43-05|[43-05]]]** "This fulfills the **prophecy** made by the **prophet** Joel in which God said, ‘In the last days, I will pour out my Spirit.’” 
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:43-07|[43-07]]]** "This fulfills the **prophecy** which says, ‘You will not let your Holy One rot in the grave.’"
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:48-12|[48-12]]]** Moses was a great **prophet** who proclaimed the word of God. But Jesus is the greatest **prophet** of all. He is the Word of God.
