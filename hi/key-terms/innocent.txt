====== innocent ======


===== Definition: =====

To be innocent means to **not** be guilty of something.

  * In the case of a crime, a person is innocent of the crime if he has **not** committed that crime.
  * Jesus is the only human being who was completely innocent, because he never did anything wrong. He was sinless.

The opposite of "innocent" is "guilty."

[See also: **[[:hi:key-terms:guilt|guilt]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:08-06|[08-06]]]** After two years, Joseph was still in prison, even though he was **innocent**.
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:40-04|[40-04]]]** One of them mocked Jesus, but the other said, “Do you have no fear of God? We are guilty, but this man is **innocent**.” 
  ***[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:40-08|[40-08]]]** When the soldier guarding Jesus saw everything that happened, he said, “Certainly, this man was **innocent**. He was the Son of God.”
