====== The Religious Leaders Grumbled [35-02] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-35-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Some **[[:en:key-terms:jewish-leaders|religious leaders]]** who were also there saw **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** treating these **[[:en:key-terms:sin|sinners]]** as friends, and they began to criticize him to each other. So Jesus told them this **story**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **story** - Jesus used this story to teach truths about God's kingdom. It is not clear if the events actually happened or not. If your language has a term that includes both fictional and real stories, you should use it here.
