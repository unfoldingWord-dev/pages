====== Love God Above All [49-04] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-49-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

He also taught that you need to **[[:en:key-terms:love|love]]** **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** more than you love anything else, including **your wealth**.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your wealth** - This could also be translated as, "your money" or, "your possessions."
