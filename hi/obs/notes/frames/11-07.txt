====== Sorrow in Egypt [11-07] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-11-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Every **[[:en:key-terms:egypt|Egyptian]]** firstborn male died, **from the firstborn of the prisoner in jail, to the firstborn of [[:en:key-terms:pharaoh|Pharaoh]]**. Many people in **[[:en:key-terms:egypt|Egypt]]** were crying and wailing because of their deep sadness.
