====== Saul Became Blind [46-03] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-46-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When **[[:en:key-terms:paul|Saul]]** got up, he could not see. His friends had to lead him into Damascus. Saul did not eat or drink anything for three days.
