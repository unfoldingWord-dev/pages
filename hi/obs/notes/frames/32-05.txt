====== Jesus Commanded the Demons [32-05] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-32-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When the man came to **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**, he **fell on his knees** in front of him. Jesus said to the **[[:en:key-terms:demon|demon]]**, “Come out of this man!”


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **fell on his knees** - That is, "quickly knelt down on the ground.”
