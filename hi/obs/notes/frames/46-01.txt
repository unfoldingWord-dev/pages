====== Saul Arrested the Believers [46-01] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-46-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**[[:en:key-terms:paul|Saul]]** was the young man who **guarded the robes of the men who killed Stephen**. He did not **[[:en:key-terms:believe|believe]]** in **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**, and so he **[[:en:key-terms:persecute|persecuted]]** the **[[:en:key-terms:believer|believers]]**. He went **from house to house** in **[[:en:key-terms:jerusalem|Jerusalem]]** to arrest both men and women and to put them in prison. The **[[:en:key-terms:priest-high|high priest]]** gave Saul permission to go to the city of **Damascus** to arrest Christians there and to bring them back to Jerusalem.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **guarded the robes of the men who killed Stephen** - See **[[:hi:obs:notes:frames:45-06|[45-06]]]**.
  * **from house to house** - That is, "into many houses."
  * **Damascus** - Damascus is now the capital of the country of Syria. During Saul's time, it was a city belonging to the Roman Empire. Most of the people there were not Jewish, but there were some Jews and also some Christians living there.
