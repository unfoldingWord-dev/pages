====== Letters to the Churches [47-14] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-47-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:paul|Paul]]** and other **[[:en:key-terms:christian|Christian]]** leaders traveled to many cities, **[[:en:key-terms:preach|preaching]]** and teaching people the **[[:en:key-terms:good-news|good news]]** about **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**. They also wrote **many letters** to encourage and teach the **[[:en:key-terms:believer|believers]]** in the **[[:en:key-terms:church|churches]]**. Some of these letters became books of the Bible.
