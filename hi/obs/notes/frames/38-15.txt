====== Peter Fought for Jesus [38-15] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-38-15.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**As the soldiers arrested [[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:peter|Peter]]** **pulled out his sword** and cut off the ear of a **[[:en:key-terms:servant|servant]]** of the **[[:en:key-terms:priest-high|high priest]]**. Jesus said, “Put the sword away! I could ask the **[[:en:key-terms:god-father|Father]]** for an army of **[[:en:key-terms:angel|angels]]** to defend me. But **I must [[:en:key-terms:obey|obey]] my Father**.” Then Jesus **[[:en:key-terms:heal|healed]]** the man’s ear. After Jesus was arrested, all the **[[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciples]]** ran away.

//A Bible story from: Matthew 26:14-56; Mark 14:10-50; Luke 22:1-53; John 12:6; 18:1-11//


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As the soldiers arrested Jesus** - That is, "While the soldiers were arresting Jesus."
  * **pulled out his sword** - That is, “pulled out his sword from the place where he was carrying it.” 
  * **I must obey my Father** - That is, “I won't do that because I must obey my Father's will and allow myself to be arrested.” 
  * //A Bible story from// - These references may be slightly different in some Bible translations.
