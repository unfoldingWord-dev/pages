====== The House of the High Priest [39-01] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-39-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

It was now **the middle of the night**. The soldiers led **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** to the house of the **[[:en:key-terms:priest-high|high priest]]** in order for the high priest **to question him**. **[[:en:key-terms:peter|Peter]]** followed far behind them. When Jesus was taken into the house, Peter stayed outside and warmed himself by a fire.
