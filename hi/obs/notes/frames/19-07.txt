====== A Sacrifice without Fire [19-07] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-19-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then **[[:en:key-terms:elijah|Elijah]]** said to the **[[:en:key-terms:prophet|prophets]]** of **[[:en:key-terms:baal|Baal]]**, “Kill a bull and prepare it as a **[[:en:key-terms:sacrifice|sacrifice]]**, but do not light the fire. I will do the same. The **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] who answers with fire** is **the real God**.” So the **[[:en:key-terms:priest|priests]]** of Baal prepared a sacrifice but did not light the fire.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **who answers with fire** - That is, “who supernaturally sends fire to burn up the sacrifice.”   
  * **the real God** - This means the one and only true God.
