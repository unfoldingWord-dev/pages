====== The Miracles [49-02] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-49-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** did many **[[:en:key-terms:miracle|miracles]]** that prove he is **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**. He walked on water, calmed storms, **[[:en:key-terms:heal|healed]]** many sick people, **drove out [[:en:key-terms:demon|demons]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:raise|raised]] the [[:en:key-terms:death|dead]] to life**, and turned five loaves of bread and two small fish into enough food for over 5,000 people.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **drove out demons** - That is, "made demons come out of people."
  * **raised the dead to life** - This could also be translated as, "caused dead people to become alive again."
