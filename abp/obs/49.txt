====== ** 49. God’s New Covenant ======

{{:en:obs:obs-49-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus’ mother, Mary, was a virgin when he was born because Jesus is God’s Son. Jesus is both God and man at the same time.

Ha inanak na yan Maria ye Jesus, ahe ya po bega natakehtakeh. Olita inanak yan tao ye Jesus, ket kapadihon Dioh ye tao.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus did many miracles proving he is God. He walked on water, calmed storms, healed many sick people, drove out demons, raised the dead to life, and fed 5,000 people with five loaves of bread and two fish.

Malabong a kapagtakaan a didinyag nan Apo Jesus a pagkakitan nin kaptegan a hiya ye Dioh. Hilati ye pagkakitan. Kinomodang ya ha babe lanom, pinatgen nay makhaw a agoot, namaitaah ya met nin malabong a ampaghakit, namaalih yan maloke a ihpidito kanlan tatao, boy nami-ay ya met nin nanatey. Namakan yan ninghan nin 5,000 katao, powida ya babayi boy aanak, agya tiliman tinapay bengat boy lowan malanghit.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus was also a great teacher. He taught that you need to love your neighbor the same way you love yourself.

Magaling yan mangipapatanda. Impatanda na po kanlan tatao, a wana kanla, "No way-omen ye pangado mo ha hadili mo, wanabay met ye pangado mo kanan kapadiho mo."

{{:en:obs:obs-49-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

He also taught that you need to love God more than anything else, including your wealth.

Boy impatanda na po a adoen yan tao ye Apo Dioh a omiigit po ha kaganaan boy agya po ha kaganaan a babandi.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus taught that salvation is worth more than anything else in the world.

Boy inhabi na po nin Apo Jesus a maalaga po a makaligtah yay tao, dinan makwa nay kaganaan a babandi ihti ha matiboen logal.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

He taught that not all people will receive him and be saved. Those who are not saved will be gathered at the end of the world and will burn forever in the fires of hell.

Impatanda na met a tangalen yan kaganaan a tatao bilang Apo, boy hiya ye Mangiligtah a makaligtah. Ket hilay ahe nangibabano kana, ket nangaipilot hila angga anggan ahe maabot ye angaan nin bi-ay ihti ha babe lota.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus taught that God loves sinners very much. He is ready to forgive them and make them His children.

Hinabi nin Apo Jesus no way-omen na hila ang-adoen ye tatao agya po man malabong ye kakahalanan la. Hiyay labay habiyen nin pangado na, "Labay na hilan patawaden ta omen mag-ilyadin aanak na hila."

{{:en:obs:obs-49-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus also taught that everyone in the world has sinned and is separated from God. Because of sin, everyone has become an enemy of God. God hates sin.

Noba agya po man impatanda nan Apo Jesus a aanak hila nin Dioh, ket nakihiyay hila kana oli ha pagkahalanan. Boy oli ha kahalanan, nag-ilyadin kaaway ya nin tao ye Apo Dioh, ta adi na labay nin Dioh ye kahalanan.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

But God loved the world so much that He gave His only son, Jesus, so that whoever believes in Him will not go to hell but will live with Him forever.

Oli ha tobat a pangado nan Apo Dioh ha kaganaan a tatao ihti ha babe lota, ket in-ibyay na yay bogtong a Anak na omon na hila ahe mapadohaan nin Apo Dioh ye agya hinyaman a mamteg kanan bogtong a Anak na, no aliwan magkamain hilan bi-ay a homain anggaan.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Because of your sin, you are guilty. You deserve death and God’s anger, but God poured out His anger on Jesus instead. When Jesus died on the cross, he received your punishment.

Ket oli ha kahalanan yo, holong tanggapen yo kamateyan boy poot nin Dioh. Noba agya wanabay man, aliwan hikawoy dinektan nin poot na, ta imbohboh na kanan Apo Jesus ha hatew ha natey ya ha kodoh. Ket oli ihtew ha kodoh, hiyabay ye nananggap nin padoha, oli ha kahalanan yo.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus chose to die as a perfect, sinless sacrifice to take away your sins and the sins of the whole world. Because of Jesus, any sin can be forgiven, no matter what it is.

Hiyay Apo Jesus ye hagpa a kabayadan nin kahalanan mo. Homain yan kahalanan. Kaya-bay malyadi nan bayadan ye kahalanan mo. Ket oli kanan Apo Jesus, mapatawad anay kaganaan a kakahalanan mo.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

No one can be saved by doing good things. Nothing you do can fix your relationship with God. Your sins can only be washed away if you believe that Jesus is the Son of God, and that he died and rose again to take away your sins.

Aghe mo ampiligtah ye hadili mo oli ha dinyag mon manganged. Homain kan madyag a mangipakanged nin pamteg mo ha Dioh, ta hiyay Apo Jesus bengat ye malyadin manlinih nin kahalanan mo, no pakikwaen mo kana boy no ipataya mo kana. Boy dapot anan namteg ka a hika dayi ye natey oli ha kahalanan mo. Boba aliwaynan hika! Boy dapot anan namteg ka a hiya ye natey oli ha kahalanan, ket hiya ye nabi-ay oman.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Anyone who believes in Jesus and is baptized will be saved, but whoever does not believe will be rejected. It does not matter if you are rich or poor, man or woman, old or young, or where you are from. God loves you and wants you to believe in Jesus so He can have a relationship with you.

Kaganaan a namteg kanan Apo Jesus a bilang Apo Dioh, ket maligtah ya. Aliwan maalaga no mabandi ka, o maidap ka, o babayi ka, matowa ka, o anak ka, o no way-ihtew ye angkonaan mo. Ket ang-adoen na kan Apo Dioh, boy labay nan pamtegan mo yay Apo Jesus ta omen ka mag-ilyadin anak nin Dioh.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Do you believe that Jesus is the Messiah, the only Son of God? Do you believe that you are a sinner and deserve God’s punishment? Do you believe that Jesus died on the cross to take away your sins? Jesus invites you to come to him, believe in him, and be baptized.

Ampamtegan mo ya nayi a hiyay Apo Jesus ye mangiligtah a mamaghan Anak nin Dioh? Ampaawoen mo nayi a magkahalanan ka, boy katapolan a tanggapen mo ye ipadoha nan Apo Dioh kamo? Namteg ka nayi a hiyay Apo Jesus ye nananggap nin padoha a ipadoha dayi kamo? Kaya-bay mamteg ka kanan Apo Jesus boy magpabawtihmo ka, ta labay na a homaley ka kana.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-15.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

If you believe this, you are a Christian! God has taken you out of Satan’s kingdom of darkness and put you into God’s Kingdom of light. All the old things are gone, because God makes all things new.

Ket no pinamtegan moyna yatin babagay, magha kaynan Cristiano/mamteg! Ta inalih na kayanan Apo Dioh ha kalitehan a ampanakopan nan Hatanah. Kaya-bay haanin, ingkonin na kaynan Apo Dioh ha panakopan nin Mahnag.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-16.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

If you are a Christian, your sins are forgiven because of what Jesus did. Now, when God looks at you, He does not see you as an enemy, but He sees you as a close friend.

No magha kaynan Cristiano, ket kaganaan a kakahalanan mo, ket pinatawad ana nin Dioh, oli ha pagteeh nan Apo Jesus. Kaya-bay haanin, tepe bilewen na kan Apo Dioh, ket aliwaynan kaaway ye pamilew na kamo, ta magha kaynan gayyem na haanin.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-17.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

As a friend of God, you will want to follow Jesus’ teachings. Even though you are a Christian, you will still struggle with sin. But God says if you confess your sins, He is faithful and will forgive you and will give you strength to fight against sin.

Haanin, bilang gayyem nin Apo Dioh boy maghilbi kanan Apo Jesus a bilang Apo Dioh mo, hombongen mo hilayna haanin ye kaganaan a inadal/impatanda nan Apo Jesus. Agya po man Kristiano kayna, magkahalanan ka po ateed. Noba hinabi nan Apo Dioh a no ipatanda mo kana ye kahalanan mo, ket loboh ya po ateed a mamatawad boy biyan na kan kakhawan, ta omen mo malabanan ye kahalanan.


{{:en:obs:obs-49-18.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God tells you to pray, study His Word, worship Him with other Christians, and tell others what He has done for you. All of these things help you to grow in your relationship with Him.

Hinabi na kammon Apo Dioh a lanang kan makigwang, mag-adal nin Habi na, boy makilakew a maghimba kanlan kanayon po a aanak nin Dioh. Boy labay na a habiyen mo po ha kanayon ye didinyag nin Dioh kammo. Ket makahaglap yati nin panlomabong nin pamteg mo kana.

//A Bible story from: Romans 3:21-26, 5:1-11; John 3:16; Mark 16:16; Colossians 1:13-14; 2 Thessalonians 5:17-21; 1 John 1:5-10//
