
====== 22. The Birth of John ======

{{:en:obs:obs-22-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


More than 400 years after the last prophet spoke to the Jews, an angel appeared to an old priest named Zechariah. He was a godly man, but his wife, Elizabeth, could not have any children.


Nalabah anay 400 a taon paibat ha kapopohan a propeta nan Apo Dioh. Haanin, main maghay padi a nag-ngalan Zacarias. Matoynong ya ha pamilew nan Apo Dioh noba hiya boy ahawa na a hiyay Elisabet, ahe hila nagkaanak. Ta bawog yay Elisabet boy matoa hilayna. 


{{:en:obs:obs-22-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The angel said to Zechariah, “Your wife will have a son. You will name him John. He will be the great prophet that comes before the Messiah!” Zechariah responded, “My wife and I are too old to have children! How will I know this will happen?”


Linomateng ye mangaamot a hiyay golopo nan Zacarias ye maghilbi ha Himbaan nan Apo Dioh.  Ket kapipikhaan main maghay pānobolen nin Dioh a nagpakit kanan Zacarias. Ampideng yayna ha dapit wanan nin pangolaman nin idigalo nan Apo Dioh.  Kaya-bay, naigat yay Zacarias boy nalimwan ya kanan anghel. Noba hinabi nan pānobolen nin Dioh kana, a wana, “Adi ka angkalimo, Zacarias! Ta linnge nan Apo Dioh ye pakigwang mo. Hiyay Elisabet a ahawa mo, manganak ya lanon maghay lākin pangalanan mon Juan. Ket hiya ye maona ha Mangiligtah a lomateng."


Haanin, nanepet yay Zacarias kanan pānobolen nin Dioh, a wana, “Way-omon yan malyadi kammi yati, ket padiho kayiynan matoan ahawa ko?” 


{{:en:obs:obs-22-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The angel answered, “I was sent by God to bring you this good news. Because you did not believe me, you will not be able to speak until the child is born.” Immediately, Zechariah was unable to speak. Then the angel left. Zechariah returned home, and his wife became pregnant.


Nakibat yay pānobolen nin Dioh kanan Zacarias, a wana, “Hikoy anghel a nag-ngalan Gabriel a lanang ampaghilbi kanan Apo Dioh. Hiyabay ye nangitobol kangkon mangihabi kammo nin yatin manged a balita. Ket yatin imbalita ko kammo, mapaptegan ya lano no maabotan ye panaon. Noba oli ta ahe ka namteg ha hinabi ko, maomel ka. Ahe ka makapaghabi anggan ahe ya lomwah ye anak mo.” Ha linomwah yay Zacarias, ahe yayna makapaghabi kanlan tatao. Ahe naboyot, hiyay Elisabet a ahawa na, binomoktot ya. (Luke 1:19, 22a, 24a)


{{:en:obs:obs-22-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When Elizabeth was six months pregnant, the same angel appeared to Elizabeth’s relative, whose name was Mary. She was a virgin and was engaged to a man named Joseph. The angel said, “You will become pregnant and give birth to a son.”


Ha anem anan bowan ye boktot nan Elisabet, intobol na yan Apo Dioh ye pānobolen na a nag-ngalan Gabriel a makew ha banwan Nazaret ha plobinhyan Galilea.  Intobol na yan makew kanan Maria a maghay balatang a homain po nadihaan a kabaang nan Jose. Haanin, hiyay Gabriel, nakew ya kanan Maria ta hinabi na kana, a wana, “Minged ka! Ta in-ingalowan na kan Apo Dioh. Ket anti yayna kammo.  (Luke 1:26, 27a, 28)


{{:en:obs:obs-22-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The angel continued, “You are to name him Jesus. He will be the Messiah!” Mary replied, “How can this be, since I am a virgin?” The angel explained, “The Holy Spirit will come over you, and you will conceive. So your son will be called the Son of the Most High God.”

Haanin, nanepet yay Maria kanan pānobolen nin Dioh, a wana, “Way-omon ako manganak, ket homain po nangahawa kangko?” 


Nakibat yay pānobolen nin Dioh, a wana, “Hiyay Ihpidito nan Apo Dioh, makew ya kammo. Ket makaoli ha kapalyadiyan nan Bapa a Dioh, bomoktot ka. Kaya-bay hiyay i-anak mo, mahabtan yan Anak nin Dioh. (Luke 1:31-32a,34-35)


{{:en:obs:obs-22-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Not long after this, Mary went and visited Elizabeth. As soon as Elizabeth heard Mary’s greeting, Elizabeth’s baby jumped inside her. The women rejoiced together about what God had done for them. After visiting for three months, Mary returned home.


Ahe naboyot, ampagpatige yaynay Maria ta makew ya ha baey nan Zacarias boy Elisabet a anti ha matagay a logal ha maghay banwa ha plobinhyan Judea. Legan anhomlep ya kananyatew a baey, hinabi na kanan Elisabet, a wana, “Oh! Komohta kayna?” Ha nange nan Elisabet ye pangomohta nan Maria, ket naggalaw yay ongiꞌ ha loob nin bitoka nan Elisabet. Ket naheb yan Ihpiditon Dioh ye Elisabet. (Luke 1:39-41)


{{:en:obs:obs-22-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


After Elizabeth gave birth to her baby boy, they named him John as the angel commanded. Then God allowed Zechariah to speak again. He said, “Praise God, because He has remembered His people! You, my son, will be called the prophet of the Most High God! You will prepare the way for the Messiah!”


Haanin, naabot anay odah nan panganak nan Elisabet. Ket hiyay in-anak na, maghay lāki.​ Impangalan la yan Juan a omon ha hinabi nan panobolen nan Dioh.  Ket kapipikhaan, nakapaghabi yaynan oman ye Zacarias boy nanggalang ya kanan Apo Dioh. Hinabi na, 


“Galangen yay Apo Dioh a Dioh lan tatao ha Israel.


Ta nakew yan mangiligtah kanlan tatao na.


Ket hika, anak ko, habtan la kan propita nin Pinakamakapalyadiyan a Dioh.


Ta hikay maonan mangihadya nin tatao ha panlomateng na." (Luke 1:57, 64, 68, 76)
