====== ** 49. God’s New Covenant ======

{{:en:obs:obs-49-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus’ mother, Mary, was a virgin when he was born because Jesus is God’s Son. Jesus is both God and man at the same time.

Hiyay Maria a indo' nan Apo Dioh, homain ya po nadihaan ha nanganak ya ta naboktot ya oli ha kapalyadiyan nan Ihpiditon Dioh. Kaya-bay Anak na yan Apo Dioh boy padiho yan Dioh boy tao.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus did many miracles proving he is God. He walked on water, calmed storms, healed many sick people, drove out demons, raised the dead to life, and fed 5,000 people with five loaves of bread and two fish.

Hiyay Apo Jesus, malabong ye kapagtakaan a dinyag na ta omon mapaptegan a hiya ye Dioh. Kinomodang ya ha babe nin dagat, pinaitaah na hilay tataon main hakit, pinaalih nay maloke a ihpidito kanlan tatao, bini-ay na yan oman ye tataon natey, boy pinalabong nay tinapay boy malanghit ta omon pakanen nay limay liboy tao ha limay tinapay boy loway malanghit.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus was also a great teacher. He taught that you need to love your neighbor the same way you love yourself.

Hiyay Apo Jesus, magaling yan maihtodo. In-adal na a ang-adoen tawoy kapadiho tawo a omon ha hadili tawo.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

He also taught that you need to love God more than anything else, including your wealth.

In-adal na met a katapolan maiigit po ye pangado tawo kanan Apo Dioh dinan ha agya hinyaman a bagay, agya kabandiyan tawo.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus taught that salvation is worth more than anything else in the world.

In-adal na a hiyay kaligtahan ha kakahalanan ye maalaga po ha kaganaan a babagay ihti ha babe lota.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

He taught that not all people will receive him and be saved. Those who are not saved will be gathered at the end of the world and will burn forever in the fires of hell.

In-adal na a aliwan kaganaan a tatao ye mananggap kana boy maligtah ha kapadohaan nin kakahalanan. Ket hilay ahe maligtah ha kapadohaan, ha kapopohan nin babe lota, maitipon hila ket maolam hila ha apoy ha impilno angga-angga.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus taught that God loves sinners very much. He is ready to forgive them and make them His children.

In-adal nan Apo Jesus a tobat ye pangado nan Apo Dioh kanlan māgkahalanan. Hadya na hilaynan patawaden ta omen mag-ilyadin aanak na.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus also taught that everyone in the world has sinned and is separated from God. Because of sin, everyone has become an enemy of God. God hates sin.

In-adal nan Apo Jesus a kaganaan a tatao ket nagkahalanan boy naihyay kanan Apo Dioh. Oli ha kakahalanan nin tatao, ket kaganaan a tatao ye kabono na. Ta ampag-inakit yay Apo Dioh ha kahalanan.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

But God loved the world so much that He gave His only son, Jesus, so that whoever believes in Him will not go to hell but will live with Him forever.

Oli ha tobat a pang-ado nan Apo Dioh ha kaganaan a tatao ihti ha babe lota, ket in-ibiyay na yay bogtong a Anak na omon na hila ahe mapadohaan nin Apo Dioh ye agya hinyaman a mamteg kana, no aliwan magkamain hilan bi-ay a homain anggaan.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Because of your sin, you are guilty. You deserve death and God’s anger, but God poured out His anger on Jesus instead. When Jesus died on the cross, he received your punishment.

Oli ha kakahalanan mo kanan Apo Dioh, mapadohaan naka dayi. Hiyay hohto kammo, kamateyan boy poot nin Apo Dioh. Noba ha natey yay Apo Jesus ha kodoh, hiyabay anay impadoha nan Apo Dioh, aliwan hika.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus chose to die as a perfect, sinless sacrifice to take away your sins and the sins of the whole world. Because of Jesus, any sin can be forgiven, no matter what it is.

Inloboh nan Apo Jesus a matey ya bilang genap boy homain kahalanan a digalo kanan Apo Dioh omon alihen nay kakahalanan mo boy agya kakahalanan nin kaganaan a tatao ha babe lota. Oli ha dinyag nan Apo Jesus ha kodoh, malyadin mapatawad ye agya hinyaman a kahalanan.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

No one can be saved by doing good things. Nothing you do can fix your relationship with God. Your sins can only be washed away if you believe that Jesus is the Son of God, and that he died and rose again to take away your sins.

Homain taoy maligtah ha kapadohaan oli ha manged a didyag la. Malinihan bengat ye kakahalanan mo no mamteg ka kanan Apo Jesus a hiyabay ye Anak nin Dioh boy nabi-ay yan oman omon alihen nay kakahalanan mo.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Anyone who believes in Jesus and is baptized will be saved, but whoever does not believe will be rejected. It does not matter if you are rich or poor, man or woman, old or young, or where you are from. God loves you and wants you to believe in Jesus so He can have a relationship with you.

Agya hinyaman a mamteg kanan Apo Jesus boy ampabawtihmo, ket maligtah ya ha kapadohaan noba agya hinyaman a ahe ampamteg, ket ahe maligtah. Homain bali no mabatnang o maidap, matowa o anak, o taga way-ihtew ka ta ang-adoen na hilan Apo Dioh ye kaganaan boy kalabayan nay mamteg ka kanan Apo Jesus ta omen magkamain nin manged a pamakilamo kana.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Do you believe that Jesus is the Messiah, the only Son of God? Do you believe that you are a sinner and deserve God’s punishment? Do you believe that Jesus died on the cross to take away your sins? Jesus invites you to come to him, believe in him, and be baptized.

Ampamteg kayna laweh a hiyay Apo Jesus ye Mangiligtah a Anak nin Dioh? Ampamteg ka laweh a magkahalanan ka boy katapolan a mapadohaan kan Apo Dioh? Ampamteg ka laweh a natey yay Apo Jesus ha kodoh omon alihen nay kakahalanan mo? Anhagyaten na kan Apo Jesus a homaley kana, mamteg kana, boy magpabawtihmo.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-15.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

If you believe this, you are a Christian! God has taken you out of Satan’s kingdom of darkness and put you into God’s Kingdom of light. All the old things are gone, because God makes all things new.

No yati ye ampamtegan mo, hikaynay Kidihtiano! Kingwa na kan Apo Dioh ibat ha panakopan nan Satanas ta ingkonin na ka ha kahnagan a panakopan na. Hiyay kaganaan a babagay ket bayoyna kammo ta hiyay Apo Dioh ye ampamayo nin kaganaan.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-16.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

If you are a Christian, your sins are forgiven because of what Jesus did. Now, when God looks at you, He does not see you as an enemy, but He sees you as a close friend.

No Kidihtiano ka, pinatawad yaynay kakahalanan mo oli ha dinyag nan Apo Jesus ha kodoh. Haanin, no bilewen na kan Apo Dioh, aliwan kabono ye angkakit na, no aliwan gayyem na.

{{:en:obs:obs-49-17.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

As a friend of God, you will want to follow Jesus’ teachings. Even though you are a Christian, you will still struggle with sin. But God says if you confess your sins, He is faithful and will forgive you and will give you strength to fight against sin.

Bilang gayyem nan Apo Dioh, higodon labay moynay manombong ha pangiadal nan Apo Jesus. Agya ampamteg kayna kanan Apo Jesus, main po pamakilaban ha kahalanan ha bi-ay mo. Noba anhabiyen nan Apo Dioh a no paghehean moy kakahalanan mo kana, ket patawaden na ka boy linihan ha kakahalanan. Ta mapatayaan boy matoynong ya. 


{{:en:obs:obs-49-18.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God tells you to pray, study His Word, worship Him with other Christians, and tell others what He has done for you. All of these things help you to grow in your relationship with Him.

Hiyay labay nan Apo Dioh, makigwang kitawo kanna, mag-adal kitawo ha Habi na, manggalang kitawo kana kalamo nin kanayon a ampamteg boy habiyen kanlan kanayon ye dinyag na ha bi-ay tawo. No diyagen tawo yati, mag-ilyadin mak-haw ye pāmteg tawo.

//A Bible story from: Romans 3:21-26, 5:1-11; John 3:16; Mark 16:16; Colossians 1:13-14; 2 Thessalonians 5:17-21; 1 John 1:5-10//
