====== ** 48. Hiyay Apo Jesus a impangako a Māngiligtah! ======

{{:en:obs:obs-48-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When God created the world, everything was perfect. There was no sin. Adam and Eve loved each other, and they loved God. There was no death or sickness or pain. This was the way God wanted things to be.

Ha hatew, ha pinalhowa nan Apo Dioh ye logal, genap ye kaganaan a babagay boy homain po kahalanan. Boy no way-omen ye payado len Adan boy Eba, wanabay met ateed ye pangado la kanan Apo Dioh. Boy hiyay labay na dayin malyadi nin Apo Dioh kanlan pinalhowa na, homain dayin kamateyan boy kahalanan.


{{:en:obs:obs-48-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Satan, who was the snake in the garden, deceived Eve, and she and Adam sinned against God. Because they sinned, death, sickness, and pain all became part of life for everyone on earth.

Noba hiyay Satanas, tinalingo na yay Eba, ket natalingo na ya met nin Eba ye ahawa na. Kaya-bay nagkahalanan hilan lowa kanan Apo Dioh. Oli kanan yatin kahalanan, ket nitayak ye kamateyan, hakit, boy idap kanlan kaganaan a tatao ihti ha matiboen lotal.


{{:en:obs:obs-48-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Even worse, their relationship with God was broken. Now, everyone is born an enemy of God because of sin. But God had a plan to fix everything that had been broken by sin.

Hiyay maiidap po, nadama met ateed ye paykahondo la kanan Apo Dioh, ket naihyay hila kana. Kaya-bay hilay kaganaan a nianak haanin, ket tampol na hilaynan kaaway nin Apo Dioh, oli ha kahalanan. Noba an-ihipen nan teed Apo Dioh, a wana, <<Labay kon maiodong ye alan a pagkahondo yo kangko a dinama nin kahalanan.>>


{{:en:obs:obs-48-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God promised that one of Eve’s descendant would crush Satan’s head, and Satan would wound his heel. This meant that Satan would kill Jesus, but God would raise him to life after three days. Jesus would crush the power of Satan forever.

Impangako nan Apo Dioh hatew kanan Eba a magha kanlan aapo na ye manete/mandonot nin o nan bikat a anhabtan, <<Hatanah.>> Noba tokaen na met Hatanah ye bitih nin namete kana. Hiyay labay habiyen, mapatey na yan Hatanah ye Apo Jesus, noba mabi-ay ya met ateed ha ikatlon mangaamot. Boy oli ha pagkabi-ay nan oman, ket maanggaan anay ye kaganaan a kapalyadiyan nan Hatanah boy anti yayna ha kapalyadiyan nan Apo Jesus anggan makanoman.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When God destroyed the whole earth in the flood, He provided the boat as a way of salvation for those who believed in Him. In the same way, though everyone deserves to be destroyed for their sins, God has provided a way of salvation for everyone who believes in Jesus.

Ha hatew ha damaen nan lanab nin Apo Dioh ye babe lota, namadyag ya po kanan Noe nin mayadet a bangka. Ta yati ye hangkan a pagkakitan nin pangiligtah nan Apo Dioh hatew kanan Noe boy hilay pamilya na. Ket wanabay met a teed ye dinapat nan Apo Dioh kanlan tatao. Ta gay mangamatey hilayna dayi ye tatao, oli ha kahalanan la, ket nanyag ya po a teed ye Apo Dioh nin papadan ta omen hila makaligtah ye agya hinyaman a mamteg kanan Apo Jesus.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Melchizedek was a priest who blessed Abraham. Like Melchizedek, Jesus is a priest, but Jesus is the Great High Priest. Jesus offered only one sacrifice, himself. This was the only sacrifice needed to take away the sin of the whole world.

Hiyay Abraham, in-inged Melquizedek a maghan padi. Hiyay Melquizedek, kapadiho na yan padi ye Apo Jesus. Noba hiyay Apo Jesus ye pinakpoon nin padi. Ninghan yan bengat nangihagpa, noba bi-ay nay inhagpa na ta yatin bengat kaya-bay ye hagpa a makaalih kahalanan nin kaganaan a tatao. Kaya-bay hiyay Apo Jesus ye nananggap nin padoha ha kahalanan nin kaganaan.


{{:en:obs:obs-48-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God told Abraham, “Through you, all the people groups of the earth will be blessed.” Jesus was a descendant of Abraham. All the people groups of the earth are blessed through Jesus, because everyone who believes in him becomes one of God’s chosen people, a spiritual descendant of Abraham.

Hinabi nan Apo Dioh kanan Abraham, a wana, "Oli ha pamteg mo, ket inged ko hilan kaganaan a tatao ha babe lota. Boy oli met kanan Apo Jesus a lahi nan Abraham, ket in-inged na hilay tataon mamteg kana. Ket nag-ilyadi hilan banwa nin Dioh, a bilang espiritwal a lahi nan Abraham


{{:en:obs:obs-48-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God provided a ram for Abraham to sacrifice instead of Isaac. Jesus is like that ram because he died instead of us. Everyone deserves to die for their sins, including you! But God provided Jesus as a sacrifice to die instead of you.

Ha hatew ha inhagpa na yan Abraham ye Isaak, ket hiyay Apo Dioh met ateed ye namyay nin topa a kahagiliyan nan Isaak ha pateyen na ya dayin Abraham ye anak na. Boy namyay ya po ye Apo Dioh nin magha a ihagpa tongkol ha kahalanan nin kaganaan a tatao. Hiyay labay habiyen nin maghan topan hagpa tongkol ha kahalanan, hiyay Apo Jesus yain. Hikitawobay dayi ye katapapolan matey oli ha kahalanan tawo! Noba hiyay Apo Jesus po ye inibiyay nan Apo Dioh a ihagpa a kahagiliyan tawo.


{{:en:obs:obs-48-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When God sent the last plague on Egypt, each Israelite family needed to kill a perfect lamb and spread the blood around their doors. When the angel of God saw the blood, he passed over their houses and did not kill their firstborn sons.

Ha hatew, ha impakit nan Apo Dioh ye hoyot a pamahakit ihtew ha Egipto, hinabi na kanlan tatao a mangwa hilan maghan lakin oybon topan pateyen la. Ket hiyay daya na, iponah la ha gilid nin ilwangan la. Ta nakit nin Dioh ye daya, ket ahe na hila pinatey ye angkomonin ihtewbay a baey. Ket yabayti yay anhabtan "Pahkwa".

{{:en:obs:obs-48-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus is our Passover Lamb. He was perfect and sinless and was killed at the time of the Passover Feast. When anyone believes in Jesus, the blood of Jesus washes away that person’s sin, and God’s punishment passes over him.

Ket hiyay Apo Jesus ye Topan Pamahkwa a ampakatopad boy homain bega kahalanan. Pinatey yan maghan mangaamot nin Pahkwa. Boy nililinihan nay kahalanan nin agya hinyan taon malabay mamteg kana, boy ilabah nin Apo Dioh ye padohan kakahalanan.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God made a covenant with the Israelites who were His chosen people. In God’s New Covenant, anyone from any people group can become part of God’s chosen people by believing in Jesus.

Nakipagkahondo yay Apo Dioh kanlan taga Israel nin wanae, <<Hiyay bayoon kahondoan, agya hinyaman a kahalahin taon mamteg kanan Anak ko, mailamo ya kanlan pinili ko.>>


{{:en:obs:obs-48-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Moses was a great prophet who proclaimed the Word of God. Like Moses, Jesus is a prophet, but Jesus is the greatest prophet. He is the Word of God.

Hiyay Moises, magha yan manged a propetan ampangibalita nin Habi nin Dioh. Hiyay Moises, kapadiho na yan propeta ye Apo Jesus. Noba hiyay Apo Jesus, aliwan bengat manged a propeta ye anti kana, ta hiyaynabay met a homain kanayon ye Habi nin Dioh.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God promised David that the Messiah would be one of his descendants. Jesus, the Messiah, was that special descendant of David.

Impangako nan Apo Dioh kanan David a mangibat ya ha lahi na ye lomateng a Mangiligtah. Homain kanayon a andektan nin yatin pangako no aliwan hiyay Apo Jesus.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Like David, Jesus is a king, but Jesus is the king of the universe! He will come again and rule his Kingdom with justice and peace.

Hiyay David, kapadiho na yan Poon ye Apo Jesus. Noba hiyay Apo Jesus, Poon layan kaganaan a pinalyadi. Mag-odong yan minakop nin kangedan boy katanaan a homain anggaan.


//A Bible story from: Genesis 1-3, 6, 14, 22; Exodus 12, 20; 2 Samuel 7; Hebrew 3:1-6, 4:14-5:10, 7:1-8:13, 9:11-10:18; Revelation 21//
