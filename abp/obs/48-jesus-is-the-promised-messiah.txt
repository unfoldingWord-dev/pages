====== ** 48. Hiyay Apo Jesus ye Māngiligtah a impangako nan Apo Dioh! ======

{{:en:obs:obs-48-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When God created the world, everything was perfect. There was no sin. Adam and Eve loved each other, and they loved God. There was no death or sickness or pain. This was the way God wanted things to be.

Ha pinalhowa nin Apo Dioh ye babe lota, genap ye kaganaan a babagay. Ta homain kahalanan, ket hiyay Adan boy Eva, ampayado hila boy ampangado hila met kanan Apo Dioh. Homain kamateyan o hakit o ilab ha hatew. Kaganaan yati ye kalabayan nan Apo Dioh.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Satan, who was the snake in the garden, deceived Eve, and she and Adam sinned against God. Because they sinned, death, sickness, and pain all became part of life for everyone on earth.

Hiyay Satanas a nag-ilyadin bikat ha hardin, ket nanloko ya kanan Eva. Kaya-bay hiyay Eva boy Adan, nagkahalanan hila kanan Apo Dioh. Oli ta nagkahalanan hila, nagkamain anan kamateyan, hakit, boy ilab ha kaganaan a tatao ihti ha babe lota.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Even worse, their relationship with God was broken. Now, everyone is born an enemy of God because of sin. But God had a plan to fix everything that had been broken by sin.

Ket naihyay ye manged a paylamo kanan Apo Dioh. Haanin, kaganaan a maianak ket kabono hila kanan Apo Dioh oli ha kakahalanan la. Noba hiyay Apo Dioh, main yan manged a palano no way-omen pakangeden yatin problima nin kahalanan lan tatao.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God promised that one of Eve’s descendant would crush Satan’s head, and Satan would wound his heel. This meant that Satan would kill Jesus, but God would raise him to life after three days. Jesus would crush the power of Satan forever.

Ta impangako nan Apo Dioh a main maghay lahi nan Eva ye mamitpit nin ō nan Satanas agya manoka yay Satanas ha bitih na. Hiyay labay nan habiyen, ipapatey na yan Satanas ye Apo Jesus noba bi-ayen na yan oman ye Apo Dioh. Ha wanabay, damaen nan Apo Jesus ye kapalyadiyan nan Satanas angga makanoman.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When God destroyed the whole earth in the flood, He provided the boat as a way of salvation for those who believed in Him. In the same way, though everyone deserves to be destroyed for their sins, God has provided a way of salvation for everyone who believes in Jesus.

Ha hatew, linanab nan Apo Dioh ye babe lota noba impadyag na met ye mayadet a bangka bilang padān nin kaligtahan kanlan namteg kana. Wanabay met, agya mapadohaan kitawo dayi oli ha kakahalanan tawo, dinyag nay padān nin kaligtahan. Ta hilay kaganaan a mamteg kanan Apo Jesus, ket maligtah hilayna ha kapadohaan.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Melchizedek was a priest who blessed Abraham. Like Melchizedek, Jesus is a priest, but Jesus is the Great High Priest. Jesus offered only one sacrifice, himself. This was the only sacrifice needed to take away the sin of the whole world.

Hiyay Melkizedek ye pari nin Apo Dioh ha hatew. Nangihagpa yan digalo kanan Apo Dioh boy binindihyonan na yay Abraham. Wanabay met ateed kanan Apo Jesus a padi ya. Noba hiyay Apo Jesus ye pinakamanged a Pari ta hiyay inhagpa na a idigalo na kanan Apo Dioh, ket hiyay hadi na. Yatin digalo bengat ye katapolan omon alihen ye kakahalanan nin kaganaan a tataon namteg kana.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God told Abraham, “Through you, all the people groups of the earth will be blessed.” Jesus was a descendant of Abraham. All the people groups of the earth are blessed through Jesus, because everyone who believes in him becomes one of God’s chosen people, a spiritual descendant of Abraham.

Hinabi nan Apo Dioh kanan Abraham, “Ket makaoli ha lalahi mo a inged ko, inged ko hila met ye kaganaan a nanahyon.” Hiyay Apo Jesus ye lahi nan Abraham ket hilay tatao ha kaganaan a nahyon ye in-inged na. Ta agya hinyaman a mamteg kana, ket mag-ilyadin pinilin tao nan Apo Dioh a omon kanan Abraham ha hatew.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God provided a ram for Abraham to sacrifice instead of Isaac. Jesus is like that ram because he died instead of us. Everyone deserves to die for their sins, including you! But God provided Jesus as a sacrifice to die instead of you.

Ha hatew, hiyay Abraham, ahe na katapolan a ihagpa na yan iolam kanan Apo Dioh ye anak na ta impakit nan Apo Dioh ye topa a nag-ilyadin kahagiliyan nin anak na. Wanabay met kanan Apo Jesus ta agya katapolan a hikitawo ye idigalo kanan Apo Dioh, hiyay Apo Jesus ye nag-ilyadin kahagiliyan tawo ha natey ya ha kodoh. Ahe kitawo matey oli ha kakahalanan tawo no aliwan hiyay Apo Jesus ye natey bilang kahagiliyan tawo! 

{{:en:obs:obs-48-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

When God sent the last plague on Egypt, each Israelite family needed to kill a perfect lamb and spread the blood around their doors. When the angel of God saw the blood, he passed over their houses and did not kill their firstborn sons.

Ha hatew, ha an-omalih hilay Israelita ha Egipto, balang pamilya lan Israelita, katapolan a patyen lay topa boy iponah lay daya nin topa ha babe nin ilwangan la. Ha linomateng ye pānobolen nan Apo Dioh, nalabhan nay kaganaan a baey a main daya ha ilwangan ket ahe na pinatey ye ponganay a lāki la.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Jesus is our Passover Lamb. He was perfect and sinless and was killed at the time of the Passover Feast. When anyone believes in Jesus, the blood of Jesus washes away that person’s sin, and God’s punishment passes over him.

Hiyay Apo Jesus ye topa tawo ha Pihtan Kaligtahan ha Egipto. Ta agya genap ya a homain kahalanan, pinatey la ya ha panaon nin Pihtan Kaligtahan, ket makaoli ha daya na, patawaden nay kakahalanan tawo boy labhan na kitawon Apo Dioh no padohaan na hilay tataon nagkahalanan kana.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God made a covenant with the Israelites who were His chosen people. In God’s New Covenant, anyone from any people group can become part of God’s chosen people by believing in Jesus.

Hiyay Apo Dioh, pinili na hilay Israelites a tatao. Ket nanyag yan pangako kanla. Ha pangako na, agya hinyaman a tao ha agya hinyaman a tidibo, malyadin mag-ilyadin tatao na no mamteg hila kanan Apo Jesus.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Moses was a great prophet who proclaimed the Word of God. Like Moses, Jesus is a prophet, but Jesus is the greatest prophet. He is the Word of God.

Hiyay Moises ye manged a propita ta nangibabala ya nin Habi nin Dioh. Wanabay met ateed kanan Apo Jesus a propita ya, noba hiyay Apo Jesus ye pinakamanged a propita ha kaganaan. Ta hiyabay ye Habi nin Dioh.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

God promised David that the Messiah would be one of his descendants. Jesus, the Messiah, was that special descendant of David.

Impangako nan Apo Dioh kanan Poon David a magha ha lahi na ye mag-ilyadin Criston Māngiligtah. Hiyay Apo Jesus, hiyabay ye Criston Māngiligtah a lahi nan Poon David.

{{:en:obs:obs-48-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Like David, Jesus is a king, but Jesus is the king of the universe! He will come again and rule his Kingdom with justice and peace.

Hiyay David ye Poon ha Israel ha hatew. Wanabay met ateed kanan Apo Jesus a Poon ya, noba hiyay Apo Jesus ye Poon ha kaganaan. Mag-odong yay Apo Jesus ihti ha babe lota to mamoon ya ha panakopan na a main katoynongan boy katanaan.


//A Bible story from: Genesis 1-3, 6, 14, 22; Exodus 12, 20; 2 Samuel 7; Hebrew 3:1-6, 4:14-5:10, 7:1-8:13, 9:11-10:18; Revelation 21//
