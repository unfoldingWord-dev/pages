====== demon ======

 
===== Definition: =====

  * Demons are spirits that hate God. Though God created them, they refuse to obey him.
  * Demons also hate people and sometimes attack people or try to control people
  * Demons are powerful, but only because God gave them power.
  * God is more powerful than demons.
  * Jesus gave his apostles power over the demons.
  * When Jesus returns to earth, he will punish all the demons.
  * Demon can be translated as "evil spirit".
