====== demon ======

==== (demon, evil spirit) ====
 
===== Definition: =====

  * Demons are powerful spirit beings that opposes God's will.
  * God created demons as good and he gave them power, but they rebelled against him.
  * God is more powerful than demons.
  * Sometimes demons attack people or try to control them.
  * Jesus gave his apostles power over the demons.
  * When Jesus returns to earth, he will punish all the demons.
  * Demon can be translated as "evil spirit".

The opposite of “demon” is an “angel.”
