====== John (Baptist) ======


==== (John, John the Baptist) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * "John the Baptizer" was the son of Zechariah and Elizabeth.
  * He was the prophet whom God sent to prepare people to believe in and follow God's Messiah.
  * John prepared people to receive the Messiah by teaching them to confess their sins, turn to God, and stop sinning.
  * People went to John to be baptized in water as a sign that they were sorry for their sins and were turning away from them. 
  * John was called "John the Baptizer" because he baptized many people.
