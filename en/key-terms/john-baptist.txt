====== John (Baptist) ======


==== (John, John the Baptist) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * John was the son of Zechariah and Elizabeth.
  * John was the prophet who came to prepare the way for God's Messiah.
  * John prepared the way for the Messiah by teaching the people to turn away from their sins.
  * John baptized people in water as a sign that they were turning away from their sins.
  * John was called "John the Baptist" because he baptized many people.
