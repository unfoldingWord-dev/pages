====== Exile ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * For people to be in exile means they are forced to leave their home country in order to live somewhere else.
  * "the Exile" refers to the time in Israelite history when most of the Jews from the southern kingdom of Judah were captured and taken to Babylon to live. Many years later, thousands of these exiled captives were allowed to return and rebuild the broken down walls of Jerusalem.

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]** This period of time when God's people could not live in the Promised Land is called the **Exile**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:20-10|[20-10]]]** Even though the **Exile** was punishment for their sin, God did not forget his people or his promises.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:20-12|[20-12]]]** After seventy years in **exile**, a small group of Jews returned to Jerusalem.
