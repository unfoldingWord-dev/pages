====== Life ======


==== (life, live, living, lives, alive) ====


===== Definitions: =====

=== 1. Physical life ===

  * The presence of the spirit in the body. God breathed life into Adam's body, and he became a living being.
  * Can refer generally to people as in, “several lives were saved”.
  * Sometimes means the duration of life on earth, as in, "made their lives miserable."
  * The opposite of life is **[[:en:key-terms:death|death]]**.

=== 2. Spiritual life ===

  * The presence of the person with God. 
  * Often also called "eternal life" to indicate that it does not end. 
  * The opposite of spiritual life is spiritual death, which means being separated from God and experiencing eternal punishment.
