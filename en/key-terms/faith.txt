====== faith ======


===== Definitions: =====

  * Faith is trust or confidence in someone.
  * Faith in someone is believing that he always says what is true.
  * Faith in someone is believing that he will do what he says he will do. 
  * It is the practice of believing in, trusting in, or relying on someone.

True faith in God results in faithful obedience to Him.
