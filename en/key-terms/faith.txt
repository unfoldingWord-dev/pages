====== Faith ======

=== (faith, faithful) ===

===== Definitions: =====


    * Faith is the act of believing in someone or something.
    * When we believe in God and trust Jesus to save us, we have faith.
    * True faith or belief in Jesus will result in actions that please God and bless other people.
    * Faith means believing God and obeying Him, even when we can't see or know where He is leading us.
    * To be faithful is to continue to persevere in following God's will.
