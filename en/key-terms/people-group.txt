====== people group ======


==== (people group, nation, a people) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * A people group is a group of people who have a common ancestry, culture, or language.
  * Usually they live together in a particular land or country.
  * A people group may be made up of many tribes.
  * Another way of translating "people group" is, "nation." Occasionally, "group of people" may be used if it is clear that it refers to people of the same culture and language. 


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:14-02|[14-02]]]** God had promised Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob that he would give the Promised Land to their descendants, but now there were many **people groups** living there.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:21-02|[21-02]]]** God promised Abraham that through him all **people groups** of the world would be blessed. This blessing was the coming Messiah who would one day provide the way of salvation for all **people groups**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:42-08|[42-08]]]** “It was also written that repentance for forgiveness of sins would be proclaimed to all **people groups**, starting in Jerusalem.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:42-10|[42-10]]]** “So go, make disciples of all **people groups** by baptizing them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit and by teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:48-11|[48-11]]]** In God’s New Covenant, anyone from any **people group** can become part of God’s chosen people by believing in Jesus.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-02|[50-02]]]** When Jesus was on earth he also said, “This Good News of the Kingdom will be preached in the whole world for a testimony to all the **people groups**, and then the end will come.”
