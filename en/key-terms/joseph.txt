====== Joseph (OT) ======


===== Facts: =====


Joseph was the eleventh son of Jacob and the first son of his mother Rachel.

    * Joseph was his father's favorite son so his brothers were jealous of him; they sold him into slavery.
    * Joseph went through difficult times, including being a slave and a prisoner in Egypt, yet he remained faithful to God.
    * God brought him to the second highest place of power in Egypt and used him to save Egypt, and his father's family, from starving.

[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:jacob|Jacob]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:08-02|[08-02]]]** **Joseph's** brothers hated him because their father loved him most and because Joseph had dreamed that he would be their ruler.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:08-04|[08-04]]]** The slave traders took **Joseph** to Egypt.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:08-05|[08-05]]]** Even in prison, **Joseph** remained faithful to God, and God blessed him.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:08-07|[08-07]]]** God had given **Joseph** the ability to interpret dreams, so Pharaoh had Joseph brought to him from the prison.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:08-09|[08-09]]]** **Joseph** told the people to store up large amounts of food during the seven years of good harvests.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:09-02|[09-02]]]** The Egyptians no longer remembered **Joseph** and all he had done to help them.
