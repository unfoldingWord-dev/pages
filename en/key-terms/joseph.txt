====== Joseph ======




===== Definitions: =====


    * Joseph was the eleventh son of Jacob and the first son of his mother Rachel.
    * He was favored by his father so his brothers were jealous of him; they sold him into slavery.
    * Through many difficult times, including being a slave and prisoner in Egypt, Joseph remained faithful to God.
    * God brought him to the second highest place of power in Egypt and used him to save Egypt, and his father's family, from starving.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:08-01|[08-01]]]** **Joseph's** brothers hated him because their father loved him most and because Joseph had dreamed that he would be their ruler.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:08-04|[08-04]]]** The slave traders took **Joseph** to Egypt.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:08-05|[08-05]]]** Even in prison, **Joseph** remained faithful to God, and God blessed him.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:08-07|[08-07]]]** God had given **Joseph** the ability to interpret dreams, so Pharaoh had Joseph brought to him from the prison.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:08-09|[08-09]]]** During the seven years of good harvests, **Joseph** told the people to store up large amounts of food.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:09-02|[09-02]]]** The Egyptians forgot about **Joseph** and all he had done to help them.
