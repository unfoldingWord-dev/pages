====== Evil ======


==== (evil, wicked, bad) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * Evil is any action, thought or character that is opposed to God's holy character and will.
    * Evil people and evil behaviors can also be described as bad, wicked or immoral.
    * The results of evil are clearly shown in how people mistreat others by killing, stealing, slandering or being cruel and unkind.
    * Evil is the opposite of **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**.
    * An evil spirit is a being that obeys the devil (Satan) and opposes God.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-04|The Lie [02-04]]] "You will be like God, knowing good and **evil**."


[[:en:obs-training:frames:04-02|The Tower [04-02]]] God saw that if they all kept working together, they could do many **evil** things.


Joseph Meets His Brothers [08-12] "You tried to do **evil** when you sold me as a slave, but God used the **evil** for good!"


The Canaanites' Idols [14-02] The Canaanites did not believe in God. They worshiped false gods and did many **evil** things.


The Evil Kings of Israel [18-11] In the kingdom of Israel, all the kings were **evil**.


A Man With Evil Spirits [32-02] When they reached the other side, a man with an **evil** spirit came running up to Jesus.
