====== punish ======


==== (to punish, punishment) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * To cause someone to suffer a negative consequence for doing something wrong.
  * God punished the Israelites when they disobeyed him, especially when they worshiped false gods.
  * Punishment is intended to help the person stop doing things that hurt themselves or other people.
  * Jesus was punished for all the evil things that people have done. He himself did nothing wrong and did not deserve that punishment.
