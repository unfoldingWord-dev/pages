====== cross ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definition: =====

  * A cross is an upright wooden post stuck into the ground with a bar going across it near the top.
  * The Roman government would hang criminals on crosses in order to kill them.
  * Jesus was falsely accused and judged and killed on a cross.
  * Long before the Romans were in power, people used to hang people on trees to kill them. The scriptures say that anyone left to hang on a tree is cursed. Jesus took the curse for our sins. For this reason, the cross that He was killed on is sometimes called a tree.
