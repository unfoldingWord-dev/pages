====== Priest ======


===== Definitions: =====

  * A religious official who presented people's offerings to God.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:04-07|Melchizedek [04-07]]]  "Melchizedek, the priest of God Most High" \\ 

[[:en:obs-training:frames:21-07|Melchizedek [21-07]]] A priest is someone who makes sacrifices to God on behalf of the people to take away their sins. Priests also pray to God for the people.
