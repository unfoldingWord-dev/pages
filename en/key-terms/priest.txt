====== priest ======


===== Definitions: =====

  * A religious official who presented people's offerings to God.
  * Priests would pray to God for the people.
  * God chose Aaron and his descendants to be His priests for the people of Israel.
  * There were also other priests who presented offerings to false gods such as Baal.
  * 'Priest' could be translated as 'intermediary', 'mediator', 'middle-man' or 'sacrificer'.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:04-07|[04-07]]]** "Melchizedek, the **priest** of God Most High" \\ 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** Anyone who disobeyed God’s law could bring an animal to the Tent of Meeting to sacrifice to God. A **priest** would kill the animal and burn it on the altar. The blood of the sacrifice covered the person’s sin and made them clean in God’s sight. God chose Aaron, Moses’ brother, and his descendants to be His **priests**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-07|[19-07]]]** So the **priests** of Baal prepared a sacrifice but did not light the fire.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:21-07|[21-07]]]** A **priest** is someone who makes sacrifices to God on behalf of the people to take away their sins. **Priests** also pray to God for the people.
