====== Disobey ======


(disobey, disobedience)

===== Definitions: =====


    * To refuse or fail to do something that was commanded.
    * To do something that was prohibited or to do it differently than instructed.


[see also: **[[:en:key-terms:evil|evil]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:sin|sin]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:wicked|wicked]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-11|The Man Is Cursed [02-11]]] God said to the man, “You listened to your wife and **disobeyed** me."


The Stone Tablets [13-07] If the people obeyed these laws, God promised that He would bless and protect them. If they **disobeyed** them, God would punish them.


Working for Nothing [35-12] He said, "All these years I’ve worked so faithfully for you! I never **disobeyed** you,"


Enemies Defeat Israel [16-02] As a result of this **disobedience**, God punished the Israelites by allowing them to be defeated by their enemies.
