====== Disobey ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * To refuse or fail to do something that was commanded.
    * To do something that was prohibited or to do it differently than instructed.


[see also: **[[:en:key-terms:evil|evil]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:sin|sin]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:wicked|wicked]]**]
