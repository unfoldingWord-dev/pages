====== Jewish leaders ======


==== (Jewish leaders, religious leaders) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * The Jewish leaders were religious leaders. 
  * Some were priests, some were experts in God's laws, and some were men who copied the scriptures so that people could read them in their meetings.
  * Most of the religious leaders did not believe that Jesus is the Messiah and Son of God.
  * They did not want people to believe in Jesus and follow him.
  * They lied and told the government rulers that Jesus should be killed.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:24-03|[24-03]]]** Some people repented and were baptized when they heard John's message, but there were many **religious leaders** who came to be baptized without repenting and confessing their sins.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:37-11|[37-11]]]** But the **religious leaders of the Jews** were jealous, so they gathered together to plan how they could kill Jesus and Lazarus.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-02|[38-02]]]** The **Jewish leaders** in Jerusalem refused to believe that Jesus was the Messiah and were plotting to kill him.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-03|[38-03]]]** The **Jewish leaders**, led by the high priest, paid Judas thirty pieces of silver to betray Jesus.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:39-05|[39-05]]]** The **Jewish leaders** all answered, “He (Jesus) deserves to die!”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:39-09|[39-09]]]** Early the next morning, the **Jewish leaders** brought Jesus to the Roman governor, Pilate, hoping to have Jesus killed.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:39-11|[39-11]]]** But the **Jewish leaders** and the crowd shouted, “Crucify him!”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:40-09|[40-09]]]** Then Joseph and Nicodemus, two **Jewish leaders** who believed Jesus was the Messiah, asked Pilate for Jesus’ body.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:44-07|[44-07]]]** The next day, the **Jewish leaders** brought Peter and John before the high priest and his family.
