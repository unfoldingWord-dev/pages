====== Create (h1254) ======


<code>
  to create, shape, form
      (Qal) to shape, fashion, create (always with God as subject)
          of heaven and earth
          of individual man
          of new conditions and circumstances
          of transformations
</code>


<code>
      (Niphal) to be created
          of heaven and earth
          of birth
          of something new
          of miracles
</code>


<code>
      (Piel)
          to cut down
          to cut out
</code>


<code>
  to be fat
      (Hiphil) to make yourselves fat
</code>
