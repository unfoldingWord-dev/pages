====== Jew ======


==== (Jew, Jewish) ====


===== Facts: =====

In modern times, Jews are people who are descended from Abraham's son Isaac.

    * People began to call the Israelites "Jews" after they returned to Judah from their exile in Babylon.
    * The word 'Jew' comes from the Israelites' word for "Judah." The Israelites who had been taken to Babylon were from the southern kingdom of Judah.
    * Jesus the Messiah was Jewish. However, the Jewish religious leaders rejected Jesus and demanded that he be killed.
