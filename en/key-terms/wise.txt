====== Wise ======


=== (Wise, Wisdom, Wisely) ===

===== Definitions: =====


    * Having important knowledge and experience
    * Discerning and judging rightly
    * Being able to make good choices
    * Knowing what is right and doing it

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-05|Disobedience [02-05]]] "She also wanted to be **wise**."


[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-15|An Offering for Gideon [16-15]]] "The people wanted to make Gideon their king. Gideon **wisely** refused their offer,"


[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-01|God Makes Solomon Wise [18-01]]] "When Solomon asked for **wisdom**, God was pleased and made him the **wisest** man in the world."


[[:en:obs-training:frames:23-09|The Wise Men Visit [23-09]]] "Some time later, **wise** men from countries far to the east saw an unusual star in the sky."
