====== wise ======


=== (wise, wisdom, wisely) ===

===== Definitions: =====

A person who is "wise" is one who exercises "wisdom." To be wise is to discern or judge a matter rightly.

    * To have "wisdom" is to possess important knowledge or experience.
    * A "wise" person is able to make good choices.
    * To act "wisely" is to know what is right and to do it.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-05|[02-05]]]** She also wanted to be **wise**, so she picked some of the fruit and ate it.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-15|[16-15]]]** The people wanted to make Gideon their king. Gideon **wisely** refused their offer, but he asked them for some of the money that each of them had taken from the enemy.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-01|[18-01]]]** When Solomon asked for **wisdom**, God was pleased and made him the **wisest** man in the world.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:23-09|[23-09]]]** Some time later, **wise** men from countries far to the east saw an unusual star in the sky.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:45-01|[45-01]]]** He (Stephen) had a good reputation and was full of the Holy Spirit and of **wisdom**.
