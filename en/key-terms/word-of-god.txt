====== word of God ======


==== (word of God, Word of God, God's word, scripture) ====


===== Definition: =====

The "word of God" refers to all that God has said to people. Usually it refers to his written message, also known as the scriptures.

    * In modern times, the "word of God" refers to the Bible.
    * Jesus is called, "the Word of God," meaning that he is the very message and truth of God. He is God himself.
    * Some languages will need to translate "God's word" as, "God's message" or, "God's book" or, "God's scroll."
