====== Word of God ======


==== (Word of God, word of God, God's Word, the Word, Scripture, scripture) ====


===== Definition: =====


    * The "word (or words) of God" refers to all that God has said to people.
    * Usually it refers to his written message, also known as the scriptures.
    * In modern times, the "Word of God" refers to the Bible.
    * Jesus is called "the Word" of God, meaning that he himself is the very message and truth of God. He is God himself.
    * Some languages will need to translate "God's word" as "God's message" or "God's book" or God's scroll.
    * Referring to Jesus, "Word of God" could be translated as "Message of God."
