====== apostle ======


===== Definition: =====

  * The apostles were men sent by Jesus to preach about God, and to heal people and make demons leave people.
  * The first 12 apostles were the 12 disciples Jesus had chosen.
  * After Jesus returned to heaven, he chose more men to be apostles. Paul was one of them.

[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciple]]**]

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:26-10|[26-10]]]** Then Jesus chose twelve men who were called his **apostles**. The **apostles** traveled with Jesus and learned from him.

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-02|[38-02]]]** Judas was one of Jesus' **apostles**. He was in charge of the **apostles'** money bag, but he loved money and often stole from the bag.

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:42-06|[42-06]]]** Suddenly, Jesus appeared! The **apostles** thought he was a ghost, but Jesus said, "Peace to you! Why are you afraid and doubting?" 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:43-13|[43-13]]]** The disciples devoted themselves to the **apostles'** teaching, fellowship, eating together, and prayer.
