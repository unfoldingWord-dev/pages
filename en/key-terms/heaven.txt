====== Heaven ======


==== (heaven, sky, heavenly) ====

===== Definitions: =====


    * Sometimes "heaven" refers to the place where God lives.
    * Other times it refers to the sky: the space above the earth, including the air we breathe and and everything that is above the earth, such as the sun, moon and stars.
    * Sometimes the meaning is not clear and could refer to either heaven or the sky, or both.
