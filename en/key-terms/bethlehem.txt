====== Bethlehem ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * Bethlehem was a city in the land of Israel, located not far from the city of Jerusalem. It still exists to the present day.
  * Bethlehem is the city where King David was born. It is also the city where Jesus was born.
  * It has also been called the "city of David."
