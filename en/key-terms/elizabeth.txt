====== Elizabeth ======





===== Facts: =====


    * Elizabeth was a woman who obeyed God.
    * Elizabeth's husband, Zechariah, was a priest.
    * Elizabeth was not able to have children.
    * When Elizabeth was old, she gave birth to a baby boy and they named him John.
    * John told people to repent of their sins, so that they would be ready to believe the Messiah who would save people from their sins.
