====== Obey ======


==== (obey, obedience) ====

===== Definitions: =====

    * To do what someone else says to do.
    * To not do what someone else says not to do.
    * To do what a law says to do.
    * To not do what a law says not to do.

This usually refers to doing what someone in authority commands, such as God, a parent, or a leader. \\ 
The opposite of **obey** is **[[:en:key-terms:disobey|disobey]]**.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:03-04|Leaving the Boat [03-04]]] Noah **obeyed** God. He and his three sons built the boat just the way God had told them.\\ 

[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-06|Test of Faith [05-06]]] Again Abraham **obeyed** God and prepared to sacrifice his son.\\ 

[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-10|The Blessing [05-10]]] "Because you have **obeyed ** me, all the families of the world will be blessed through your family" \\

[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-10|Death of the Firstborn [11-06]]] But the Egyptians did not believe God or **obey** His commands.
