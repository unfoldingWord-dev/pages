====== Obey ======


==== (obey, obedience) ====

===== Definitions: =====


    * To do what someone tells you to do or what a law states you must do (or not do).
    * Usually refers to obeying what someone in authority commands, such as God or a parent.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:03-04|Leaving the Boat [03-04]]]Noah **obeyed** God. He and his three sons built the boat just the way God had told them.\\ 
[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-06|Test of Faith [05-06]]]Again Abraham **obeyed** God and prepared to sacrifice his son.\\ 
[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-10|The Blessing [05-10]]] "Because you have **obeyed ** me, all the families of the world will be blessed through your family"
