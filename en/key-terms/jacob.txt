====== Jacob ======


===== Facts: =====


    * Jacob was the younger twin of Esau, born to Isaac and Rebekah.
    * He was clever and deceptive and greatly angered his brother Esau when he tricked him out of his birthright and blessing as as the older brother and
    * Jacob was the father of twelve sons whose descendants became the twelve tribes of Israel.
    * Although Jacob had to leave his homeland because of Esau's plan to kill him, he later returned to the land of Canaan and he and his brother lived peacefully there.
    * Jacob was the one through whom God would keep His covenant to Abraham and his descendants. 

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:07-01|[07-01]]]** As the boys grew up, Rebekah loved **Jacob**, but Isaac loved Esau. Jacob loved to stay at home, but Esau loved to hunt.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:07-07|[07-07]]]**  While **Jacob** was there, he married four women who gave birth to a total of twelve sons and a daughter.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:07-08|[07-08]]]** After twenty years away from his home in Canaan, **Jacob** returned there with his family, his servants, and all his herds of animals.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:07-10|[07-10]]]** The covenant promises God had promised to Abraham and then to Isaac now passed on to **Jacob**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:08-01|[08-01]]]** Many years later, when **Jacob** was an old man, he sent his favorite son, Joseph, to check on his brothers who were taking care of the herds.
