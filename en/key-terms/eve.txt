====== Eve ======


===== Definitions: =====


  * The name of the first woman. 
  * God personally formed Eve from a rib that He took out of Adam. 
  * Eve was created to be **[[:en:key-terms:adam|Adam's]]** "helper." She came along beside Adam to assist him in the work that God gave him to do.
  * Eve was tempted by the snake (Satan) and was the first to sin by eating the fruit that God said not to eat.
