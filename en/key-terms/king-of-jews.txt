====== King of the Jews ======


===== Definition: =====


    * 'King of the Jews' is a title for Jesus, the Messiah.
    * He was called this when he was a baby.
    * This title was also written on a piece of wood and placed at the top of Jesus' cross when he was crucified.
    * Jesus is not a worldly king: he rules over people's hearts.
