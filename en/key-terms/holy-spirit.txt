====== Holy Spirit ======


==== (The Holy Spirit, God's Spirit, The Spirit of God, My Spirit) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * The Holy Spirit is the third person within the being of **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**. God exists eternally as One God, and as three Persons, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. 
    * He is equal to God the Father and God the Son in every way. 
    * He has life in Himself and is absolutely holy, infinitely pure, and morally perfect in all His nature and in everything He does. 
    * He was active, with the Father and the Son, in the creation of the world. 
    * He enables people to do what God wants them to do.

[see also: **[[:en:key-terms:holy|holy]]**]
