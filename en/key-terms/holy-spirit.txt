====== Holy Spirit ======

====  (Holy Spirit, God's Spirit, Spirit of God, My Spirit) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * The Holy Spirit is God--the third person of the one God.
  * The Spirit of God is equal to God the Father and God the Son in divinity, power, majesty, and authority.
  * He has life in Himself and is absolutely pure and morally without flaw in all His nature and every action.
  * The Holy Spirit is eternal--taking part in the creation at the beginning.
