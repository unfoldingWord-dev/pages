====== Holy Spirit ======


==== (The Holy Spirit, God's Spirit, The Spirit of God, My Spirit) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * The Holy Spirit is the third person within the being of **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**. The one God exists eternally as three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. 
    * He is spirit and does not have a body.
    * He is holy. This means that He is totally different from created things, and that He is infinitely morally pure.
    * He is equal to God the Father and God the Son in every way. 
    * He has life in Himself and is absolutely holy, infinitely pure, and morally perfect in all His nature and in everything He does. 
    * He was active, with the Father and the Son, in creating the world. 
    * Sometimes He came to people briefly to give them a special message or to enable them to do great things.
    * When God's Son, Jesus, returned to heaven, God sent the Holy Spirit to His people.
    * The Holy Spirit teaches and comforts His people and gives them the ability to do what He wants.

[see also: **[[:en:key-terms:holy|holy]]**]
