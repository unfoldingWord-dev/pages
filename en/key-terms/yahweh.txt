====== LORD ======


==== (Yahweh, LORD, I AM) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * Literally, "I AM." 
  * The proper Hebrew name for God, stressing that He is eternal and self-existent.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-01|Hagar [05-01]]]  "So Abram's wife, Sarai, said to him, "Since the LORD has not allowed me to have children and now I am too old to have children, here is my servant, Hagar. Marry her also so she can have a child for me."
