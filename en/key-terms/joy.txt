====== joy ======


===== Definition: =====


    * Joy is a feeling of deep satisfaction and delight.
    * The joy that God gives can be experienced even when very difficult things are happening.
    * A person will have great joy when he is serving God and loving other people.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:term|term]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:33-07|[33-07]]]** “The seeds on the rocky soil are like people who hear God’s Word and accept it with **joy**.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:34-04|[34-04]]]** “The Kingdom of God is also like hidden treasure in a field, which a man found and then buried again. In his **joy**, he went and sold everything he had and bought that field.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:41-07|[41-07]]]** The women were full of fear and great **joy**. They ran to tell the disciples the good news.
