====== Barnabas ======


===== Facts: =====

Barnabas was was one of the early Christians who lived during the time of the apostles.

    * When Paul/Saul became a Christian, Barnabas encouraged the other believers to accept him as a fellow believer.
    * Barnabas and Paul traveled together to preach the good news about Jesus in different cities.

[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:paul|Paul/Saul]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:christian|Christian]]**]
