====== Pilate ======


===== Facts: =====

Pilate was the Roman governor of Judea who sentenced Jesus to death.
  * Because Pilate was a governor, he had the authority to put criminals to death.
  * When the Jewish religious leaders wanted Jesus to be crucified, they lied to Pilate about him.
  * Pilate realized that  Jesus was not guilty, but he was afraid of the crowd, so he gave in and ordered his soldiers to crucify Jesus.

[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:rome|Rome]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:crucify|crucify]]**]

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:39-12|[39-12]]]** But **Pilate** became afraid that a riot would start, so he handed Jesus over to them to be crucified.
