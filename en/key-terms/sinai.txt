====== Sinai ======


==== (Sinai, Mount Sinai) ====


===== Facts: =====

Sinai is the name of a mountain that was probably located in the southern part of what is now called the Sinai Peninsula.


  * The Israelites came to Mount Sinai as they were traveling from Egypt to the Promised Land.
  * Mount Sinai is part of a large, rocky desert.
  * God gave Moses the Ten Commandments on Mount Sinai.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-01|[13-01]]]** After God led the Israelites through the Red Sea, he led them through the wilderness to a mountain called **Sinai**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-03|[13-03]]]** Three days later, after the people had prepared themselves spiritually, God came down on top of **Mount Sinai** with thunder, lightning, smoke, and a loud trumpet blast.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-11|[13-11]]]** For many days, Moses was on top of **Mount Sinai** talking with God.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** Then Joshua renewed the covenant that God had made with the Israelites at **Sinai**.
