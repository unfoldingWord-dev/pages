====== love ======


===== Definition: =====

  * To love something is to like it very much. Often it means to want to have it. 
  * To love someone is to have affection for him and to be devoted and committed to him and to what is good for him. 
  * When we love God, we admire him, we are devoted to him, and we want to obey him. 
  * When God loves us, he cares about us and is committed to our well being. 
  * These are ways some languages talk about loving someone: “He put him in his heart”, “His thoughts were towards him” “He feels good-heart toward him”.
