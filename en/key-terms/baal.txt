====== Baal ======


===== Definitions Facts: =====


    * Baal was the name of a false god that was worshiped by several people groups in the land of Canaan.
    * The Israelites sinned by also worshiping this false god.
    * Elijah set up a test for the prophets of Baal and proved that their god does not exist.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-02|[19-02]]]** Ahab was an evil man who encouraged people to worship a false god named **Baal**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-06|[19-06]]]** All of Israel, including the 450 prophets of **Baal**, came to Mount Carmel. Elijah said to the people, “How long will you keep changing your mind? If Yahweh is God, serve him! If **Baal** is God, serve him!”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-07|[19-07]]]** Then Elijah said to the prophets of **Baal**, “Kill a bull and prepare a sacrifice, but do not light the fire.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-08|[19-08]]]** Then the prophets of **Baal** prayed to **Baal**, “Hear us, O **Baal**!” All day long they prayed and shouted and even cut themselves with knives, but **Baal** did not answer.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-12|[19-12]]]** So the people captured the prophets of **Baal**. Then Elijah took them away and killed them.
