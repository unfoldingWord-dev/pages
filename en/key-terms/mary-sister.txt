====== Mary (sister of Lazarus) ======


===== Facts: =====


    * Mary was the sister of Martha and Lazarus; they were Jesus' special friends.
    * Mary seemed ot have a special heart for listening to Jesus.
    * This Mary was different from the Mary who was Jesus' mother. If this is confusing, it may be necessary to spell these women's names differently from each other.
