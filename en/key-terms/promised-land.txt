====== Promised Land ======


==== (Promised Land, Canaan) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * This was the land that God had promised to Abraham and his descendants.
  * The Promised Land was also called the land of Canaan.
  * After Abraham's descendants had lived in Canaan for many years, there was a famine and they had to move to the country of Egypt. Over 400 years later, God brought them back to the Promised Land of Canaan.
