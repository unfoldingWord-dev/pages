====== Bathsheba ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * Bathsheba was the wife of Uriah, a soldier in King David's army.
  * David committed adultery with Bathsheba and she gave birth to his child, which died several days later.
  * Because David caused Uriah to be killed in battle, Bathsheba was widowed. David then married her. Years later she gave birth to David's son, Solomon.
