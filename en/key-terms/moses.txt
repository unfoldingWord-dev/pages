====== Moses ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * Moses was chosen by God to lead the Israelites to freedom from slavery in Egypt.
    * He was a prophet and leader of the Israelite people for over 40 years.
    * Moses' name means "drawn out" because as a baby his mother had put him in a basket among the reeds of the Nile River until Pharaoh's daughter drew him out of the water and adopted him as her son.
