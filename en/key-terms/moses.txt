====== Moses ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * Moses was chosen by God to lead the Israelites to freedom from slavery in Egypt. The Passover and Red Sea crossing were important events in their escape.
    * He was a prophet and leader of the Israelite people for over 40 years.
    * Near the end of his life, Moses disobeyed God and so he didn't get to live in the Promised Land of Canaan.
