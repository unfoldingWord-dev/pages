====== Moses ======


===== Facts: =====


Moses was a prophet and leader of the Israelite people for over 40 years.  

    * God chose Moses to lead the Israelites to freedom from slavery in Egypt.
    * God gave Moses the stone tablets with his commandments for the Israelite people.
    * Near the end of his life, Moses disobeyed God so he didn't get to live in the Promised Land of Canaan.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:09-12|[09-12]]]** One day while **Moses** was taking care of his sheep, he saw a bush that was on fire.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:12-05|[12-05]]]** **Moses** told the people, "Do not be afraid! God will fight for you today and save you."
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:12-07|[12-07]]]** God told **Moses** to raise his hand over the sea and divide the waters.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:12-12|[12-12]]]** When the Israelites saw that the Egyptians were dead, they believed in God and trusted that **Moses** was a prophet of God.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** Then God wrote these Ten Commandments on two stone tablets and gave them to **Moses**.
