====== Ark ======


==== (ark, box, chest, boat) ====

===== Definitions: =====


    * One meaning of "ark" is a box or chest. It often refers to the ark of the covenant.
    * This word is most often used to refer to the large boat or barge that Noah built in order to survive the huge worldwide flood.
