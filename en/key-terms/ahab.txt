====== Ahab ======


===== Facts: =====

Ahab was a very evil king who ruled over the northern kingdom of Israel.

  * King Ahab was wicked and encouraged the people of the kingdom of Israel to worship false gods.
  * The prophet Elijah confronted Ahab, and showed him and his people that Yahweh is the only true God.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:kingdom-of-israel|kingdom of Israel]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:Elijah|Elijah]]**]
