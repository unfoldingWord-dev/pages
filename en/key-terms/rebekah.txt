====== Rebekah ======

==== (term, other) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * Rebekah was a daughter of Abraham's nephew. Her grandfather was Abraham's brother.
    * God had chosen her to be Isaac's wife so she left her home to go back with Abraham's servant.
    * For a long time Rebekah did not have any children, but finally God blessed her with twin boys, Esau and Jacob.
