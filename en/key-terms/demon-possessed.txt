====== demon-possessed ======


===== Definition: =====


    * This could also be translated as "demon-controlled."
    * A person who is demon-possessed has a demon or evil spirit that controls what he does and thinks.
    * Often a demon-possessed person will hurt himself or other people because the demon forces him to do it.
    * Jesus healed demon-possessed people by forcing the demons to come out of them.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:demon|demon]]**]

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:47-03|[47-03]]]** Every day as they (Paul and Silas) walked to the place of prayer, a slave girl **possessed by a demon** followed them.
