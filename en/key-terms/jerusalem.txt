====== Jerusalem ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * Jerusalem was a city in the land of Israel. It is still there today.
  * King David conquered Jerusalem and made it his capital.
  * The temple was built in Jerusalem by David's son, King Solomon.
  * About 1000 years later, it was in Jerusalem that Jesus the Messiah was put on trial and sentenced to die on a cross.
  * After Jesus came back to life again, Jerusalem was one of the places where he spent time with his disciples before returning to heaven.
