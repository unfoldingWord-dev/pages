====== Isaac ======

==== (term, other) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * Isaac was the son that God promised to give Abraham and Sarah even though they were very old.
    * God made his covenant with Abraham through Isaac and tested Abraham's faith by telling him to sacrifice Isaac.
    * Isaac's son Jacob became the father of twelve sons who would become the twelve tribes of the nation of Israel.
