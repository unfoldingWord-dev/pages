======  Nile River ======





===== Definitions: =====

  * The Nile is a river in northeastern Africa.
  * It flows north through Egypt into the Mediterranean Sea.
  * Crops grow well in its fertile valley.
  * Most of the people of Egypt live near the Nile River because of its water.
