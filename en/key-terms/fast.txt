====== fast ======


===== Definition: =====

  * To fast is to stop eating food for a period of time, such as for a day or more.
  * Sometimes when people fast, they also stop drinking water.
  * Sometimes people fast to show that they are very sad.
  * Sometimes people fast so they can pray without being distracted by preparing food and eating it.
  * Some people believed that they were good people because they fasted.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:25-01|[25-01]]]** Immediately after Jesus was baptized, the Holy Spirit led him out into the desert, where he **fasted** for forty days and forty nights.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:34-08|[34-08]]]** “‘I **fast** two times every week and I give you ten percent of everything I get.’”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:46-09|[46-09]]]** One day, the Christians at Antioch were **fasting** and praying and the Holy Spirit said, “Set apart for me Paul and Barnabas to do the work I have called them to do.”
