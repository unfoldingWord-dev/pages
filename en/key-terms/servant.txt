====== servant ======

==== (servant, slave, slavery) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * In the Bible, often a servant was owned by his master and was required to work without pay, but his master would take good care of him, providing for food and shelter.
    * A master would usually take good care of his servants, providing htem with food, clothing and shelter.
    * Servants were an important part of the household and were treated almost like members of the family.
    * Depending on the context, the same word will sometimes be translated as "servant" and other times as "slave."
    * For example, the same word used to refer to the Israelites as slaves in Egypt is also used to refer to Abraham's servants.
    * If a person calls himself "your servant" he is showing respect and humility even though he is not anyone's actual servant.
    * God calls his followers "servants".
