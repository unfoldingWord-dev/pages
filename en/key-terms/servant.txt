====== Servant ======

==== (servant, slave, slavery) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * In the Bible, a servant was often bought and was required to work without pay, but his master would take good care of him, providing for food and shelter.
    * A servant would usually be treated almost like a member of the family.
    * Sometimes the word for "servant" is translated as "slave," depending on the context.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|A Wife for Isaac [06-01]]] Abraham sent one of his **servants** back to the land where his relatives lived to find a wife for his son, Isaac.


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|The Prayer of Elijah [19-10]]] Then Elijah prayed, "O LORD, God of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, show us today that You are the God of Israel and that I am Your **servant**."


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|Not Able to Repay [29-03]]] Since the **servant** could not repay the debt, the king said, "Sell this man and his family as slaves to make payment on his debt."


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|The Son Repents [35-06]]] All my father's **servants** have plenty to eat, and yet here I am starving.


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|I Have Sinned [35-08]]] I am not worthy to be your son. Let me be your **servant** instead."


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|Paul Heals the Slave Girl [47-05]]]She kept yelling, "These men are **servants** of the Most High God. "


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|Remain Faithful [50-04]]]Jesus also said, "A **servant** is not greater than his master."
