====== servant ======

==== (servant, slave, slavery) ====


===== Definitions: =====



  *A servant is a person who works for another person. \\ 
  *Some servants are an important part of their master's household and are treated almost like members of the family. \\ 
  * People who follow God are often called his "servants". \\ 
  * A slave is a kind of servant who is the property of the person he works for. Just as people buy clothing and animals, in some parts of the world, people buy people. The person who buys a slave is called his 'owner' or 'master'. Masters sometimes treat their slaves very cruelly.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:06-01|[06-01]]]** Abraham sent one of his **servants** back to the land where his relatives lived to find a wife for his son, Isaac. \\ 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:09-13|[09-13]]]** I will send you to Pharaoh so that you can bring the Israelites out of their **slavery** in Egypt \\ 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-10|[19-10]]]** Then Elijah prayed, "O LORD, God of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, show us today that you are the God of Israel and that I am your **servant**." \\
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:35-06|[35-06]]]** All my father's **servants** have plenty to eat, and yet here I am starving. \\ 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:35-08|[35-08]]]** I am not worthy to be your son. Let me be your **servant** instead." \\ 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:47-05|[47-05]]]** She kept yelling, "These men are **servants** of the Most High God."  \\ 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-04|[50-04]]]** Jesus also said, "A **servant** is not greater than his master."
