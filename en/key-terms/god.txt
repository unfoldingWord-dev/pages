====== God ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * God is the almighty Creator of the heavens and the earth and all living beings.
    * He is eternal. He existed before anything else, and will continue to exist forever.
    * Refers to the one true God who exists as three persons–the Father, the Son, and the **[[:en:key-terms:holy-spirit|Holy Spirit]]**.
    * God is revealed in the Bible to be loving, just, sinless, perfect and infinitely wise.
    * God is a covenant-keeping God, who always fulfills His promises.
