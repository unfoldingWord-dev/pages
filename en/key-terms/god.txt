====== God ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * God is the eternal one. He alone existed before anything else, and He will continue to exist forever.
    * He created the heavens and earth and all living beings in the heavens and on earth.
    * He sustains the universe, and He provides for His creatures.
    * He is perfectly righteous, sinless, just, merciful, loving.
    * He is infinitely wise, infinitely powerful (almighty), and sovereign.
    * He wants people to trust, obey, love, and worship Him.
    * He punishes sin, yet in His mercy He provides salvation.
    * He is a covenant-keeping God, who always fulfills His promises.
    * He is the one true God who exists as three persons: the Father, the Son, and the **[[:en:key-terms:holy-spirit|Holy Spirit]]**.
    * He reveals Himself through His creation and through the Bible.
