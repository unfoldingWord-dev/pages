====== believer ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definitions Facts: =====

  * To 
  * This refers to the people who believed in Jesus. A few years later, people would begin to use the term, “Christians” to refer to the believers.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:christian|Christian]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciple]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:43-01|[43-01]]]** After Jesus returned to heaven, the disciples stayed in Jerusalem as Jesus had commanded. The **believers** gathered together and were praying constantly. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:43-03|[43-03]]]** While the **believers** were all together, suddenly, they heard a sound like a strong wind that filled the house. Then something like flames of fire appeared over the heads of all the **believers**.  
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:43-13|[43-13]]]** Every day, more people became **believers**.
