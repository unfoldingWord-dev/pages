====== lake of fire ======


===== Definition: =====


    * The "lake of fire" is a place of final judgment where fire burns continually.
    * "lake of fire" could be translated as "place of fire" or "pit of fire" or "expanse of fire."
    * People who do not believe in Jesus will experience the lake of fire eternally.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:hell|hell]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-13|[50-13]]]** They (Christians) will never experience the second death, which is a **lake of fire** that burns forever. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-14|[50-14]]]** But those who do not believe in Jesus will be judged by God and thrown into the **lake of fire** forever. In the **lake of fire**, people constantly weep and grind their teeth in anguish. They are burned by fire that never goes out and eaten by worms that never die.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-15|[50-15]]]** Jesus will also completely destroy Satan and his kingdom and will throw him into the **lake of fire** where he will burn forever. Death also will be thrown into the **lake of fire**.
