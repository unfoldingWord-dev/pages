====== Philippi ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Facts: =====


    * Philippi was a city located in the northern part of ancient Greece.
    * Paul and Silas traveled to Philippi to preach about Jesus to the people there.
    * The letter to the Philippians in the Bible was written by Paul to the Christians in Philippi.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:term|Paul, Silas]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:47-01|[47-01]]]** Some years later, Paul and his friend Silas went to the town of **Philippi**, proclaiming the Good News about Jesus. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:47-02|[47-02]]]** Paul and Silas often went to pray while they were in **Philippi**. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:47-13|[47-13]]]** The next day the leaders of the city freed Paul and Silas and asked them to leave **Philippi**.
