====== Rehoboam ======


===== Facts: =====

  * Rehoboam was one of King Solomon's sons.
  * When Solomon died, Rehoboam became king of Israel.
  * Because Rehoboam was too severe on his people, ten of the tribes of Israel rebelled against him.
  * Because of this, the nation of Israel became two kingdoms: the kingdom of Israel and the kingdom of Judah.
  * Rehoboam was king over the two two tribes that stayed loyal to him in the kingdom of Judah.
