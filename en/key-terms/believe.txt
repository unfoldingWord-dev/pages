====== Believe ======


===== Definitions: =====

  * to trust that something is true.
  * to trust or rely upon something or someone.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:03-04|Building the Boat [03-04]]]  "Noah warned the people about the coming flood and told them to turn to God, but they did not **believe** him."

[[:en:obs-training:frames:04-08|No Son [04-08]]] "Abram **believed** God's promise. God declared that Abram was righteous because he **believed** in God's promise."  

[[:en:obs-training:frames:11-02|The Sacrifice [11-02]]] "God provided a way to save the firstborn of anyone who believed in Him."

[[:en:obs-training:frames:11-06|The Sacrifice [11-06]]] "But the Egyptians did not believe God or obey His commands."
