====== Believe ======

=== (Believe, Believe in) ===
===== Definitions: =====

=== 1 Believe ===

  * to trust that something is true.
  * to acknowledge that what someone has said is true.

=== 2 Believe in === 
  * to trust or rely upon something or someone.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:03-04|Building the Boat [03-04]]]  "Noah warned the people about the coming flood and told them to turn to God, but they did not **believe** him."

[[:en:obs-training:frames:04-08|No Son [04-08]]] "Abram **believed** God's promise. God declared that Abram was righteous because he **believed in** God's promise."  

[[:en:obs-training:frames:11-02|The Sacrifice [11-02]]] "God provided a way to save the firstborn of anyone who **believed in** Him."

[[:en:obs-training:frames:14-02|The Canaanites' Idols [14-02]]] The Canaanites did not **believe in** God. They worshiped false gods and did many evil things.  

[[:en:obs-training:frames:37-05|I Am the Resurrection [37-05]]] Jesus replied, I am the Resurrection and the Life. Whoever **believes in** me will live, even though he dies. Everyone who **believes in** me will never die. Do you **believe** this?
