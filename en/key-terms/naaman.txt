====== Naaman ======


===== Definitions Facts: =====

  * Naaman was the commander of an army that was an enemy to Israel.
  * He had a terrible skin disease that no one knew how to cure. Elisha told Naaman to wash in the Jordan and God healed him of his disease.
  * Naaman came to believe in God because he had healed him of this disease.
