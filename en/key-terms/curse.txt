====== Curse ======


==== (curse, cursed) ====

===== Definitions: =====

  * To wish for bad or unfortunate things to happen to someone.
  * A statement of a desire for bad or unfortunate things to happen.
  * To curse is the opposite of to **[[:en:key-terms:bless|bless]]**.
