====== Curse ======


==== (curse, cursed) ====

===== Definitions: =====


    * To declare that harm will happen to someone or something.
    * To cause something bad to happen to someone or something by saying that it will happen
 
To "curse" is the opposite of to **[[:en:key-terms:bless|bless]]**.
