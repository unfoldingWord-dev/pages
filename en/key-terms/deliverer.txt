====== deliverer ======


===== Definition: =====

A deliverer is someone who rescues people or sets people free.

  * The word "deliverer" can also be translated as, "rescuer."
  * The deliverers in Israel were leaders who rescued the Israelite people from their enemies by leading them in  battle against their enemies.
  * In many cases, the "deliverer" was like a "military leader" or, an "army commander."


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-03|[16-03]]]** Then God provided a **deliverer** who rescued them from their enemies and brought peace to the land. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-16|[16-16]]]** They (Israel) finally asked God for help, and God sent them another **deliverer**.  
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-17|[16-17]]]** Over many years, God sent many **deliverers** who saved the Israelites from their enemies.
