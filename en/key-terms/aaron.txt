====== Aaron ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * Aaron was Moses' older brother.
  * He helped Moses speak to Pharaoh about letting the Israelites go free.
  * While the Israelites were traveling through the desert, Aaron sinned by making an idol for the people to worship.
  * God appointed Aaron and his descendants to be the priests for the people of Israel.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


***[[:en:obs-training:frames:09-15|[09-15]]]**  God warned Moses and **Aaron** that Pharaoh would be stubborn. 

***[[:en:obs-training:frames:10-05|[10-05]]]**  Pharaoh called Moses and **Aaron** and told them they could leave if they stopped the plague.
 
***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]**  God chose **Aaron**, Moses' brother, and his descendants to be His priests.

***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-11|[13-11]]]**  So they brought gold to **Aaron** and asked him to make an idol for them! 

***[[:en:obs-training:frames:14-07|[14-07]]]**  But the people were mad at Moses and **Aaron** and said, "Oh, why did you bring us to this horrible place?"
