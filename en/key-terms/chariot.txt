====== chariot ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definitions Facts: =====

  *  Chariots were carts with two wheels that soldiers and important officials would stand or sit in while horses pulled them.
  * 


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:term|term]]**]

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:12-10|[12-10]]]** So the Egyptians followed the Israelites onto the path through the sea, but God threw the Egyptians into a panic and caused their **chariots** to get stuck. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:45-07|[45-07]]]** As he walked along the road, Philip saw an important official from Ethiopia riding in his **chariot**.
