====== kingdom ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * A kingdom is a nation or land ruled by a king.
  * This word is often used to refer to God's people and he is their King.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-02|[13-02]]]** God said to Moses and the people of Israel, “If you will obey my voice and keep my covenant, you will be my prized possession, a **kingdom** of priests, and a holy nation.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-04|[18-04]]]** God was angry with Solomon and, as a punishment for Solomon’s unfaithfulness, he promised to divide the **kingdom** of Israel in two after Solomon’s death.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:24-02|[24-02]]]** Many people came to listen to John. He preached to them, saying, “Repent, for the **Kingdom** of God is near!”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:39-10|[39-10]]]** Jesus answered, “You have said so, but my **Kingdom** is not an earthly kingdom.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:48-14|[48-14]]]** Like David, Jesus is a king, but Jesus is the king of the universe! He will come again and rule his **Kingdom** with justice and peace.
