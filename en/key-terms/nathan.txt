====== Nathan ======


===== Facts: =====

  * Nathan was a prophet of God who lived during the time that David was king over Israel.
  * After David sinned grievously against Uriah, Nathan confronted David and rebuked him.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:term|term]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:17-07|[17-07]]]** God sent the prophet **Nathan** to David with this message, “Because you are a man of war, you will not build this house for me.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was not pleased with what David had done, so he sent the prophet **Nathan** to show David his sin.
