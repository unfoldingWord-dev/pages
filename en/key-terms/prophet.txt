====== prophet ======


==== (prophet, prophecy) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * A prophet is a person who speaks God's messages to people.
  * Prophets sometimes tell things that will happen in the future.
  * Often prophets in the Bible warned people to turn away from their sin and obey God.
  * There were also false prophets who worshiped false gods such as Baal.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was not pleased with what David had done, so he sent the **prophet** Nathan to show David his sin.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-01|[19-01]]]** Throughout the history of the Israelites, God sent them **prophets**. The **prophets** heard messages from God and then told the people God's messages.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-06|[19-06]]]** All of Israel, including the 400 **prophets** of Baal, came to Mount Carmel.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-17|[19-17]]]** They often mistreated the **prophets** and sometimes even killed them.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:40-03|[40-03]]]** When they did this, they fulfilled a **prophecy** which said, "They divided my garments among them, and gambled for my clothing."
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:43-05|[43-05]]]** This is a fulfillment of the **prophecy** made by Joel which says, "In the last days, I will pour out My Spirit."
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:43-07|[43-07]]]** "But God raised him to life again to fulfill the **prophecy** which says, 'You will not let Your Holy One rot in the grave.'"
