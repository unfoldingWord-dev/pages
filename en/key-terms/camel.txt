====== camel ======


===== Definition: =====

  * A camel is a strong animal like a tall horse or llama. 
  * People can ride on camels or put their things on camels when they travel. 
  * People can make clothes out of camels' hair, but the clothes were very coarse.
  * If camel's are unknown in your community, you can also translate it as 'animal like a llama' or 'camel-animal' or 'beast of burden'.
