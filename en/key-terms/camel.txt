====== camel ======


===== Definition: =====

  * A camel is a large, strong animal like a tall horse or llama. 
  * People can ride on camels or load their belongs on camels when they travel. 
  * Clothes that are made out of out of camels' hair are rough and scratchy.
  * If camels are unknown in your community, you could translate this as 'animal like a llama' or 'camel-animal' or 'beast of burden'.
