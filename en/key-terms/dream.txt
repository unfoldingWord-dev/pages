====== dream ======


==== (dream, to dream) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * Dreams are things people think about while they are sleeping.
  * In their dreams people may see or hear things.
  * Sometimes God comes to people in their dreams and speaks to them directly.
  * Sometimes God causes people to dream about something so they can learn from it. Then he gives that person or someone else the ability to understand the meaning of the dream.
  * To dream is to have a dream.

[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:term|term]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:08-02|[08-02]]]** Joseph’s brothers hated him because their father loved him most and because Joseph had **dreamed** that he would be their ruler.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:08-06|[08-06]]]** One night, the king of Egypt, who was called the Pharaoh, had two **dreams** that disturbed him greatly. None of his advisors could tell him the meaning of the **dreams**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:08-07|[08-07]]]** God had given Joseph the ability to interpret **dreams**, so Pharaoh had Joseph brought to him from the prison. Joseph interpreted the **dreams** for him and said, “God is going to send seven years of good harvests followed by seven years of famine.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-11|[16-11]]]** So that night, Gideon went down to the camp and heard a Midianite soldier telling his friend about a **dream**. The man’s friend said, “This **dream** means that Gideon will defeat Midian!” Gideon worshiped God.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:23-01|[23-01]]]** He (Joseph) did not want to shame her (Mary), so he planned to quietly divorce her. Before he could do that, an angel appeared to him in a **dream**.
