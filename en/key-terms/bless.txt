====== Bless ======


==== (bless, blessed) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * To state a desire for positive and beneficial things to happen.
  * To give special positive status to someone or something.
  * To cause beneficial things to happen to someone.
  * To formally pronounce positive things on one's children.
  * Blessing is the opposite of **[[:en:key-terms:curse|cursing]]**.
