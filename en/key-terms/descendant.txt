====== Descendant ======


==== (descendant, son) ====

===== Definitions: =====


    * Someone who is a direct blood relative of someone else further back in history.
    * For example, Abraham was a descendant of Noah.
    * A person's descendants would be his children, grandchildren, great-great-grandchildren, etc.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-09|The Snake is Cursed [02-09]]] The woman's descendant will crush your head, and you will wound his heel.


[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-10|The Blessing [05-10]]] Your descendants will be more than the stars in the sky.


[[:en:obs-training:frames:17-07|God's Promise to David [17-07]]] And the Messiah will be one of your descendants!


[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-13|The Divided Kingdom [18-13]]]  The kings of Judah were descendants of David
