====== descendant ======


===== Definition: =====


A descendant is someone who is a direct blood relative of someone else further back in history.

    * For example, Abraham was a descendant of Noah.
    * A person's descendants would be his children, grandchildren, great-great-grandchildren, etc.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:02-09|[02-09]]]** "The woman's **descendant** will crush your head, and you will wound his heel."
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:04-09|[04-09]]]** "I give the land of Canaan to your **descendants**."
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:05-10|[05-10]]]** "Your **descendants** will be more than the stars in the sky."
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:17-07|[17-07]]]** "Someone from your family will always rule as king over Israel, and the Messiah will be one of your **descendants**!"
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:18-13|[18-13]]]** The kings of Judah were **descendants** of David.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:21-04|[21-04]]]** God promised King David that the Messiah would be one of David's own **descendants**.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:48-13|[48-13]]]** Jesus, the Messiah, was that special **descendant** of David.
