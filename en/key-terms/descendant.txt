====== Descendant ======


==== (descendant, son) ====

===== Definitions: =====


    * A person who is the child, grandchild, great-grandchild, great-great-grandchild (etc) of someone. For example, Abraham was a descendant of Noah.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-09|The Snake is Cursed [02-09]]] The woman's descendant will crush your head, and you will wound his heel.
