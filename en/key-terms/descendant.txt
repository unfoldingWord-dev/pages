====== Descendant ======


==== (descendant, son) ====

===== Definitions: =====


    * Someone who is physically related to someone else further back in history.
    * For example, Abraham was a descendant of Noah.
    * A person's descendants would be his children, grandchildren, great-great-grandchildren, etc.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-09|The Snake is Cursed [02-09]]] The woman's descendant will crush your head, and you will wound his heel.
