====== Altar ======


==== (altar, place of sacrifice) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * A raised structure on which animals were killed and burned as a gift to God.
    * Often an altar was a packed mound of earth or a pile of carefully placed stones. Some altars were made of metal, such as gold or bronze.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-08|[05-08]]]** When they reached the place of sacrifice, Abraham tied up his son Isaac and laid him on an **altar**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** A priest would kill the animal and burn it on the **altar**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-06|[16-06]]]** He built an **altar** to God where the idol used to be and made a sacrifice to God.
