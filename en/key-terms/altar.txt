====== Altar ======


==== (altar, place of sacrifice) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * A raised structure on which animals were killed and burned as a gift to God.
    * Often an altar was a packed mound of earth or a pile of carefully placed stones. Some altars were made of metal, such as gold or bronze.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|The Altar [13-09]]] A priest would kill the animal and burn it on the **altar**.


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|Gideon Destroys the Idol [16-06]]]He built an **altar** to God where the idol used to be and made a sacrifice to God.


The Sacrifice [05-08] When they reached the place of sacrifice, Abraham tied up his son Isaac and laid him on an **altar**.
