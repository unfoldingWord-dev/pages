====== Jordan River ======


===== Facts: =====

  * The Jordan River is a deep river that forms the eastern boundary of the land that was called Canaan.
  * When Joshua led the Israelites into Canaan, they had to cross it.
  * Because it was too deep to wade across, God stopped the water from flowing so they could go across.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:15-02|[15-02]]]** The Israelites had to cross the **Jordan River** to enter into the Promised Land. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:15-03|[15-03]]]** After the people crossed the **Jordan River**, God told Joshua how to attack the powerful city of Jericho. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-14|[19-14]]]** Elisha told him to dip himself seven times in the **Jordan River**.
