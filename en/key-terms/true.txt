====== True ======

===(True, Truth) ===

===== Definitions: =====


    * matching or conforming to how things really are
    * When something someone says is true, it really did or really will happen.
    * real, genuine, actual, rightful, legitimate
    * True is the opposite of false.
    * The truth is an understanding, belief, fact, or statement that is true.
    * Truth is the opposite of a lie or deception.



===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-04|The Lie [02-04]]] The woman answered, "God told us, ‘If you eat that fruit or even touch it, you will die.’” The snake responded, “That is not **true**! You will not die."\\ 
[[:en:obs-training:frames:14-06|Joshua and Caleb [14-06]]] "It is **true ** that the people of Canaan are tall and strong, but we can certainly defeat them!"\\
