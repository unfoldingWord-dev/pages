====== Martha (sister of Lazarus) ======


===== Facts: =====


    * Martha was the sister of Mary and Lazarus; all three were good friends of Jesus.
    * When Jesus came to visit their family, Martha was very busy preparing food and other things. She was bothered that her sister Mary was sitting listening to Jesus, not helping.
    * Martha was the first to go out to greet Jesus when he came to see them after Lazarus died. 
    * Jesus declared to Martha, "I am the Resurrection and the Life."
