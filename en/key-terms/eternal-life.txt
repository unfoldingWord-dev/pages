====== eternal life ======


===== Definition: =====


    * "eternal life" refers to life with God or living forever.
    * When a person receives Jesus and trusts in him as his savior, the Holy Spirit comes to live in that person. He has received eternal life.
    * Sometimes this is translated as "everlasting life" or "living forever." 


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:life|life]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]** One day, an expert in the Jewish law came to Jesus to test him, saying, “Teacher, what must I do to inherit **eternal life**?”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:28-01|[28-01]]]** One day, a rich young ruler came up to Jesus and asked him, “Good Teacher, what must I do to have **eternal life**?” Jesus said to him, “Why do you ask me about what is good? There is only One who is good, and that is God. But if you want to have **eternal life**, obey God’s laws.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:28-10|[28-10]]]** Jesus answered, “Everyone who has left houses, brothers, sisters, father, mother, children, or property for my name’s sake, will receive 100 times more and will also receive **eternal life**.”
