====== Lamb ======

==== (lamb, Lamb of God) ====

===== Definitions: =====


    * A lamb is a young sheep, which is a four-legged animal with a thick coat, often raised for its wool or as a source of food. By nature these animals are timid and defenseless.
    * Lambs and sheep were animals used for sacrifices to God. The lamb had to be perfect, with no defects.
    * Jesus is the perfect Lamb of God who was sacrificed to pay for people's sins.
