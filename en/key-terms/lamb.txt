====== lamb ======

==== (lamb, Lamb of God) ====

===== Definitions: =====


    * A lamb is a young sheep, which is an animal that has four legs and thick woolly hair. People raise them for their wool or as a source of food. They are defenseless and timid.
    * God's people used to sacrifice sheep and lambs to Him. The ones they sacrificed had to be perfect, with no defects.
    * Jesus is the perfect Lamb of God who was sacrificed to pay for people's sins.
