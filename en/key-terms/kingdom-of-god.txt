====== kingdom of God ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definition: =====

  * The kingdom of God can refer to God's rule and authority over his people. 
  * The kingdom of God can refer to the people who live under God's authority. 
  * The prophets said that God would establish his kingdom, that he would rule with righteousness and justice, and that he would conquer his enemies. 
  * John announced that the kingdom of God was about to be started and that people should repent because God will judge the world. 
  * Jesus announced that the kingdom of God was being started and that God will save his people. 
  * Jesus started the rule of God's kingdom when he died and rose again.
  * The Kingdom of God will never end. It cannot be conquered by another.
