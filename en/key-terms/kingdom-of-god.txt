====== kingdom of God ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definition: =====


    * The kingdom of God refers to God's rule and authority over his people. It can also refer to the people who live under God's authority.
    * The prophets said that God would establish his kingdom, that he would rule with righteousness and conquer his enemies.
    * John the Baptist and Jesus both announced that the kingdom of God was near, that God was ready to rule people and that they should repent of their sins: Jesus, the Son of God and Messiah king had come to save them.
    * The rule of God's kingdom began in a spiritual sense when Jesus died and rose again.
    * The kingdom of God will begin in a physical sense when Jesus the king returns.
    * Jesus taught that the kingdom of God will conquer Satan and his forces of evil. The kingdom of God will never be conquered and it will never end.
    * Jesus' first coming to earth began the kingdom of God for all peoples and his second coming will bringhte kingdom of God to a final state of completion. We are living in the in-between tim, where the kingdom is here, but not fully.
