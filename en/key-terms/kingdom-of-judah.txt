====== Judah (kingdom) ======


==== (kingdom of Judah, Judah) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * Judah was one of the twelve sons of Jacob, and his descendants became one of the largest tribes of Israel
  * After the death of Solomon, the Israelites split into two kingdoms
  * The Kingdom of Judah was southern kingdom, composed of the tribes of Judah and Benjamin
