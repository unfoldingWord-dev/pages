====== tempt ======


==== (to tempt, temptation) ====


===== Definition: =====


    * To tempt someone is to try to get that person to do something wrong.
    * A temptation is something that causes a person to want to do something wrong.
    * Satan tempts people to disobey God and do wrong things.
    * People are also tempted by their own sinful nature and by other people.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:term|s]]in, disobey**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


**[[:en:obs-training:frames:25-01|[25-01]]]** Then Satan appeared and **tempted** him (Jesus).


**[[:en:obs-training:frames:25-08|[25-08]]]** Jesus did not give in to Satan's **temptations**, so Satan left him.


**[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-11|[38-11]]]** Jesus told his disciples to pray that they would not fall into **temptation**.
