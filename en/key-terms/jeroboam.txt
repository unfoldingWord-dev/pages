====== Jeroboam ======


===== Facts: =====


Jeroboam was the first king of the northern kingdom of Israel. He ruled over the ten tribes that rebelled and separated from the tribes of Judah and Benjamin in the south.

    * Jeroboam was a very wicked king. He set up idols and encouraged the people of the kingdom of Israel to worship them, instead of worshiping Yahweh at the Temple in Jerusalem.
    * Future kings of the kingdom of Israel followed Jeroboam's evil example.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:kingdom-of-israel|kingdom of Israel]]**]

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-08|[18-08]]]** The other ten tribes that rebelled against Rehoboam made a man named **Jeroboam** their king.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-09|[18-09]]]** **Jeroboam** rebelled against God and caused the people to sin. He built two idols for his people to worship instead of worshiping God at the Temple in the kingdom of Judah.
