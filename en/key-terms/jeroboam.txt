====== Jeroboam ======


===== Facts: =====


    * Jeroboam was a man who led the ten northern Israelite tribes to rebel and separate from the southern two tribes.
    * These ten tribes, known as the "kingdom of Israel," made Jeroboam their first king.
    * King Jeroboam set up idols and encouraged the people of the kingdom of Israel to worship them.
    * Future kings of the kingdom of Israel followed Jeroboam's evil example.


[See also: kingdom-of-Israel ]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-08|[18-08]]]** The other ten tribes that rebelled against Rehoboam made a man named **Jeroboam** their king.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-09|[18-09]]]** **Jeroboam** rebelled against God and caused the people to sin. He built two idols for his people to worship instead of worshiping God at the Temple in the kingdom of Judah.
