====== Jeroboam ======


===== Facts: =====


    * Jeroboam became the first king of the ten tribes of the northern kingdom of Israel.
    * King Jeroboam led these ten tribes to rebel and to separate from the southern two tribes.
    * He also led the tribes of northern Israel to sin terribly by setting up idols for them to worship.
    * Future kings of the northern tribes followed his evil example.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-08|[18-08]]]** The other ten tribes that rebelled against Rehoboam made a man named **Jeroboam** their king.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:18-09|[18-09]]]** **Jeroboam** rebelled against God and caused the people to sin. He built two idols for his people to worship instead of worshiping God at the Temple in the kingdom of Judah.
