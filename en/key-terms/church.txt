====== church ======


==== (church, Church) ====


===== Definition: =====

[new]

The word, "church" refers to all of the people who believe in Jesus in a local area, such as a city or a region. It can also refer to all of the believers in Jesus in every place, from the time of the apostles until the present. When it refers to this group, it is written as, "Church."

    * In the Bible, the word "church" always refers to believer, and never to a building.
    * The literal meaning of the word for "church" in the Bible is "called out (to meet together)."
    * It could be translated as "a gathering together" or "assembly" of believers. The word "congregation" has a similar meaning.
    * Christians would meet together to pray, teach God's truth and encourage each other to live in a way that is pleasing to God.
    * God also calls his people, the Church, to go out and teach the good message about Jesus.
