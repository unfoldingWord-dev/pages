====== Adam ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * The name of the first man, from whom every person descended.
    * God created Adam by forming him from the dirt of the ground and then breathing life into him.
    * Adam was made in the image of Godâ€“sharing some of God's characteristics such as spiritual life.
    * Adam, together with his wife, **[[:en:key-terms:eve|Eve]]**, disobeyed God. This caused them to become sinners, and to pass on that sin to all of their descendents.
