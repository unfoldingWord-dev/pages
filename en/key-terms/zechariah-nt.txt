====== Zechariah (NT) ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Facts: =====


    * Zechariah was the father of John the Baptist
    * He was a priest who loved God and obeyed him.
    * For many years Zechariah and his wife Elizabeth had prayed earnestly to have a child, but it wasn't until they were very old that God granted their request and gave them a son.
    * Zechariah prophesied that his son John would be the prophet who would announce and prepare the way for the Messiah's coming.
