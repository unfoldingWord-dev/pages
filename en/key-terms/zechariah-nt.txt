====== Zechariah (NT) ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Facts: =====


    * Zechariah was the father of John the Baptist
    * He was a priest who loved God and obeyed him.
    * For many years Zechariah and his wife Elizabeth could not have any children. They prayed earnestly that God would give them one. When they were very old, God finally answered their prayers and gave them a son.
    * Zechariah prophesied that his son John would be the prophet who would announce and prepare the way for the Messiah's coming.
