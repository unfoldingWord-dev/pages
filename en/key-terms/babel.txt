====== Babel ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * The ancient Middle-Eastern city where God confused the languages.
    * The word "Babel" means "confusion."

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|Many Languages [04-03]]] The city they had begun to build was called Babel, which means "confused."
