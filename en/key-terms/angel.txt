====== Angel ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * Literally means “messenger.”
    * Refers to a powerful spirit being whom God created.
    * These spirit beings serve God by giving people messages from Him, including commands and other instructions. They also protect people.
    * Some angels followed Satan and rebelled against God; they are called "demons" or "evil spirits".
