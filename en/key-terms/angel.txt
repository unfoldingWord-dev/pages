====== Angel ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * An angel is a powerful spirit being whom God created.
    * These spirit beings serve God in different ways: They give people messages from God, including commands and other instructions. They also protect and strengthen people.
    * Some angels rebelled against God and do things against His will; they are called "demons" or "evil spirits".
    * Often "the angel of God" or "the angel of the LORD" is another way of referring to God Himself.
