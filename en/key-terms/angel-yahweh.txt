====== Angel of the Yahweh ======


==== () ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * The phrase 'the angel of the Yahweh' sometimes refers to God Himself. \\ 
  * The phrase 'an angel of God' is always used to refer to an ordinary angel.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-05|[16-05]]]**  The **angel of Yahweh** came to Gideon and said, "God is with you, mighty warrior. Go and save Israel from the Midianites."
