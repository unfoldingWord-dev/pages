====== Son ======


===== Definitions: =====

  * a male offspring
  * a boy or man in relation to his parents
  * a man or boy in relation to someone who cares for him as if he were their offspring
  * a descendent of someone, even if separated by generations

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:04-08|No Son [04-08]]]  "God spoke to Abram and promised again that he would have a **son** and as many descendants as the stars in the sky. 

[[:en:obs-training:frames:04-09|God's Covenant [04-09]]] "I will give you a **son** from your own body."

[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-05|Birth of Isaac [05-05]]] "Sarah gave birth to Abraham's **son**."

[[:en:obs-training:frames:09-07|Pharaoh's Daughter [09-07]]] When she saw the baby, she took him as her own **son**. 

[[:en:obs-training:frames:22-05|The Mother of the Messiah [22-05]]] "The angel explained, 'The Holy Spirit will come over you, and you will conceive. So your **son** will be called the **Son** of the Most High God.'"

[[:en:obs-training:frames:31-08|The Son of God [31-08]]] "They worshiped Jesus, saying to him, 'Truly, you are the **Son** of God.'"

[[:en:obs-training:frames:49-09|God Gave His Son [49-0]]] "But God loved the world so much that He gave His only **son**, Jesus, so that whoever believes in Him will not go to hell but will live with Him forever."
