====== Promise ======


==== (promise, covenant) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * To state that one will definitely do something or that something will definitely happen.
    * A vow or commitment to make something happen.
    * A declaration that someone will certainly do something or that something will certainly happen.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


The Rainbow [03-16] God then made the first rainbow as a sign of His **promise**. Every time the rainbow appeared in the sky, God would remember what He **promised** and so would His people.


No Son [04-08] God declared that Abram was righteous because he believed in God's **promise**.
