====== Promise ======


==== (promise, covenant) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * To state that someone will definitely do something or that something will definitely happen.
    * A vow or commitment to make something happen.
    * Promises are important parts of formal agreements such as covenants.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

[[:en:obs-training:frames:03-15|God's Promise [03-15]]] God said, "I **promise** I will never again curse the ground because of humans or destroy the world with a flood, even though humans are sinful from their youth."

[[:en:obs-training:frames:03-16|The Rainbow [03-16]]] God then made the first rainbow as a sign of His **promise**. Every time the rainbow appeared in the sky, God would remember what He **promised** and so would His people.

[[:en:obs-training:frames:04-08|No Son [04-08]]] God spoke to Abram and **promised** again that he would have a son and as many descendants as the stars in the sky. Abram believed God's **promise**.

[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-04|Isaac Promised [05-04]]] "Your wife, Sarai, will have a son‚ he will be the son of **promise**. I will make Ishmael a great nation, too, but my covenant will be with Isaac."
