====== Silas ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * Silas was a leader among the believers in Jerusalem. \\ 
  * The church in Jerusalem appointed him to go with Paul and Barnabas to the city of Antioch. \\ 
  * Later Silas traveled with Paul to other cities to teach people about Jesus. \\
  * In the city of Philippi, Paul and Silas were put in jail. They sang praises to God while they were there and God set them free. They told the jailer about Jesus and he became a Christian.
