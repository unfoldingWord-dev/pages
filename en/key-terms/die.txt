====== Death ======


==== (death, die, dying, dead) ====


===== Definitions: =====

=== 1. Physical death ===

  * The separation of the spirit from the body
  * To stop being alive
  * To lose physical life

=== 2. Spiritual death ===

  * The separation of a person from God
  * Adam's sin immediately separated him from God, causing him to hide from God. 
  * Sin is the cause of the ultimate eternal separation of sinners from God in judgement.

Note: "death" is the opposite of "**[[:en:key-terms:life|life]]**". In the Bible, physical and spiritual death are often compared and contrasted to physical and spiritual life.
