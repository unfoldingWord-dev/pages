====== circumcision ======


==== (circumcision, to circumcise) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * To cut off the foreskin of a man or male child.
    * God had commanded that every male in Abraham's family do this as a sign of His covenant with them.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-03|Covenant Renewed [05-03]]] "Every male in your family must be circumcised."
