====== Israel ======


==== (Israel, Israelites) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * Israel was the name God gave to Jacob.
    * Jacob's descendants became known as the "Israelites"
    * God formed His covenant with the people of Israel. They were His chosen people. Now God has made a New Covenant so that anyone can become part of His chosen people by believing in Jesus.
