====== Sin ======


==== (sin, sinful, sinner) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * Anything that is opposed to the way **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** desires for us to think and act.
    * Anything that is in opposition to God's character.
    * The world was created without sin, but the first people, **[[:en:key-terms:adam|Adam ]]** and **[[:en:key-terms:eve|Eve]]**, sinned by eating the fruit that God told them not to eat.
    * Sin is passed from parent to child through the entire human race.
    * God must judge sin.

[see also: **[[:en:key-terms:evil|evil]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:wicked|wicked]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:disobey|disobey]]**]
