====== Nebuchadnezzar ======


===== Facts: =====
  * Nebuchadnezzar was king of Babylon, a powerful empire during the time of the kingdom of Israel and the kingdom of Judah.
  * Nebuchadnezzar had a powerful army that conquered many other people groups.
  * Under Nebuchadnezzar's leadership, the Babylonian army attacked the kingdom of Judah and took most of the people of Judah to Babylon as captives. This is referred to as the “Babylonian Exile.”

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:20-06|[20-06]]]** About 100 years after the Assyrians destroyed Israel, God sent **Nebuchadnezzar**, king of the Babylonians, to attack Judah.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:20-06|[20-06]]]** The king of Judah agreed to be **Nebuchadnezzar’s** servant and pay him a lot of money every year.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:20-08|[20-08]]]**  To punish Judah’s king for rebelling, **Nebuchadnezzar’s** soldiers killed the king’s sons in front of him and then made him blind.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]** **Nebuchadnezzar** and his army took all the people of Judah to Babylon, leaving only the poorest people behind to plant the fields.
