====== Grace ======


==== (grace, favor) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * The kindness and favor God gives people even though it is not deserved.
    * Also refers specifically to God's great love and mercy in forgiving our sins based on Jesus' sacrificial death.
    * “found grace” is a special expression that means he pleased God and God had mercy on him.
