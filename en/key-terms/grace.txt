====== grace ======


==== (grace, favor) ====


===== Definitions: =====


    * The kindness and favor God gives people even though they do not deserve it.
    * It also refers specifically to God's great love and mercy in forgiving our sins based on Jesus' sacrificial death.
    * “found grace” is a special expression that means God was pleased with Noah and blessed him with His favor and mercy.
