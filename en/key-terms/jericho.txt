====== Jericho ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * Jericho was a powerful city in the Promised Land of Canaan.
  * This was the first city in the land of Canaan that God told the Israelites to conquer.
  * Like all the Canaanites, the people of Jericho worshiped false idols.
  * When Joshua led the Israelites against Jericho, God did many miracles to help them defeat the city.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:15-01|[15-01]]]**  Joshua sent two spies to the Canaanite city of **Jericho**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:15-03|[15-03]]]**  After the people crossed the Jordan River, God told Joshua how to attack the powerful city of **Jericho**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:15-05|[15-05]]]**  When the rest of the Canaanites heard about what had happened to **Jericho** they were terrified.
