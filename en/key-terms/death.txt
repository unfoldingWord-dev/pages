====== Death ======


==== (death, die, dying, dead) ====


===== Definitions: =====


=== 1. Physical death ===


    * To die is to stop living. Death is the end of physical life.
    * Death is the point when the spirit of a person leaves his body.
    * Physical death is a consequence of sin. 


=== 2. Spiritual death ===


    * Spiritual death is the separation of a person from God.
    * Adam died spiritually when he disobeyed God. He became ashamed and tried to hide from God.
    * Every descendant of Adam is a **[[:en:key-terms:sin|sinner]]**, and is spiritually dead. God makes us spiritually alive again when we have faith in Jesus Christ.


Note: "death" is the opposite of "**[[:en:key-terms:life|life]]**". In the Bible, physical life and death are often compared to spiritual life and death.
