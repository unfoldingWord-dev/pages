====== Wicked ======

===== Definitions =====

  * Something that is very evil and deserves punishment. 
  * Evil acts are also often criminal.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

[03-01]  
“After a long time, many people were living in the world. They had become very **wicked** and violent.”
[03-02]  
“But Noah found grace in God’s eyes. He was the only righteous man among all the **wicked** people.”
[17-01]  
“Saul did well at first, but he became a **wicked** man who did not obey God, so God chose a different man to be king in his place.”
