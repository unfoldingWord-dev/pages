====== Noah ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * Noah was a righteous man who obeyed God. He built a huge boat and survived the worldwide flood with his family. 

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:03-02|[03-02]]]** But **Noah** found grace in God's eyes.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:03-04|[03-04]]]** **Noah** obeyed God. He and his three sons built the boat just the way God had told them.
