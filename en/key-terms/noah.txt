====== Noah ======


===== Definitions: =====


Noah, his wife, his sons, and their wives are the only people who were saved from the world-wide flood. Everyone born since then are descendants of Noah's family. 


    * Noah was a righteous man who obeyed God.
    * He built a huge boat and survived the worldwide flood with his family.
    * Noah, his wife, their three sons and their wives were saved to repopulate the earth with their children and grandchildren.
