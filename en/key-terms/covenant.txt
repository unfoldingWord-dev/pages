====== Covenant ======


==== (promise, covenant) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * A covenant, promise, or binding agreement between two or more people (also see [[:en:key-terms:promise|promise]]). 


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|Isaac Promised [05-04]]] "I will make Ishmael a great nation, too, but my **covenant** will be with Isaac."


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|Rebekah is Pregnant [06-05]]] After a long time, Abraham died and all of the promises that God had made to him in the **covenant** were passed on to Isaac.


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|The Brothers Reunited [07-10]]]The **covenant** promises God had promised to Abraham and then to Isaac. 


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|God Calls Israel  [13-02]]]<font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff></font> God said to Moses and the people of Israel, "If you will obey My voice and keep My **covenant**, you will be My prized possession, a kingdom of priests, and a holy nation.now passed on to Jacob."
