====== Esau ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * Esau was the older twin brother of Jacob, born to Isaac and Rebekah.
    * He is known for having sold his birthright to Jacob in exchange for a bowl of food when he had come home very hungry after hunting all day.
    * Jacob also stole the blessing he deserved as the firstborn, and although Esau at first was so angry he plotted to kill Jacob, he later forgave his brother.
    * Esau had many children and grandchildren, and these descendants formed a large people group living in the land of Canaan.
