====== disciple ======


===== Definition: =====

A disciple is a student who learns from a teacher, does what the teacher says, and is loyal to the teacher.

  * Jesus told 12 men to be his disciples. They travelled with Jesus and learned from him.
  * Jesus taught his disciples to teach others so that they could be disciples too.
  * The people who listened to their teaching and obeyed it also became Jesus' disciples.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:apostle|apostle]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:30-08|[30-08]]]** He (Jesus) gave the pieces to his **disciples** to give to the people. The **disciples** kept passing out the food, and it never ran out!
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-01|[38-01]]]** Jesus knew what would happen to him and told his **disciples** he was going to Jerusalem where he would be killed.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-11|[38-11]]]** Then Jesus went with his **disciples** to a place called Gethsemane. Jesus told his **disciples** to pray that they would not fall into temptation.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:42-10|[42-10]]]** At the end of the forty days, Jesus said to his **disciples**, “All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. So go, make **disciples** of all people groups by baptizing them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit and by teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you.”
