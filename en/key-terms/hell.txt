====== hell ======


===== Definition: =====

Hell is a place of unending pain and suffering that God prepared for punishing Satan and the evil spirits that follow him.

    * Because people have sinned and rebelled against God, they deserve eternal punishment in hell.
    * Hell is described as a place of fire and anguish.
    * God does not want anyone to go to hell.
    * God sent Jesus to be a sacrifice for people's sins so that if they believe in Jesus they will not go to hell.
    * Hell could be translated as "place of darkness and anguish."

[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:heaven|heaven]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:eternal-life|eternal life]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:death|death]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:49-06|[49-06]]]** Those who are not saved will be gathered at the end of the world and will burn forever in the fires of **hell**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:49-09|[49-09]]]** But God loved the world so much that he gave his only son, Jesus, so that whoever believes in him will not go to **hell**  but will live with him forever.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-13|[50-13]]]** They (Christians) will never experience **hell**, which is a place of burning fire and suffering. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-14|[50-14]]]** But those who do not believe in Jesus will be judged by God and thrown into **hell** forever. In the **hell**, people constantly weep and grind their teeth in anguish. They are burned by fire that never goes out and eaten by worms that never die.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-15|[50-15]]]** Jesus will also completely destroy Satan and his kingdom and will throw him into **hell** where he will burn forever. Death also will be thrown into the **hell**.
