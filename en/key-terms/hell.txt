====== hell ======


===== Definition: =====


    * Hell is a place of pain and suffering that God prepared for punishing Satan and the evil spirits that follow him.
    * This place is also where people will suffer eternally if they reject God and do not believe in Jesus as their Savior.
    * Hell is described as a place of fire and anguish.
    * It is sometimes called the "lake of fire."
    * Those who go to hell experience eternal death.
    * Hell could be translated as "place of darkness and anguish"
    * God does not want anyone to go to hell.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:term|heaven, eternal life, death, lake of fire]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:49-06|[49-06]]]** Those who are not saved will be gathered at the end of the world and will burn forever in the fires of **hell**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:49-09|[49-09]]]** But God loved the world so much that he gave his only son, Jesus, so that whoever believes in him will not go to **hell** but will live with him forever.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-13|[50-13]]]** Christians will be given the crown of life. They will never experience the second death, which is **hell**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-14|[50-14]]]** But those who do not believe in Jesus will be judged by God and thrown into **hell** forever. In **hell**, people constantly weep and grind their teeth in anguish. They are burned by fire that never goes out and eaten by worms that never die.
