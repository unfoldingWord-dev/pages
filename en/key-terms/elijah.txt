====== Elijah ======


===== Facts: =====

  * Elijah was one of God's prophets when Ahab was king over the kingdom of Israel.
  * Elijah confronted Ahab because Ahab encouraged the people to worship false gods.
  * God provided for Elijah in miraculous ways.
  * God did miracles through Elijah and showed Israel that Yahweh is the only true God.
