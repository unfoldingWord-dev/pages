====== Good ======


===== Definitions: =====

The word “good” has different meanings depending on the context. Some languages may use different words to translate these different meanings. In general, something is good if it is in agreement with God's character, purposes, and will.

  * suitable, pleasing, or excellent, as in "God saw that what he had created was good."
  * morally right, as in "What good thing must I do to have eternal life?"
  * doing what is morally right, as in "There is only one who is good, and that is God."
  * beneficial or helpful as in "It is not good for man to be alone."
  * productive or plentiful, as in "seven years of good harvests"
  * able to do something well, as in "a good farmer".

Good is often contrasted with **[[:en:key-terms:evil |evil]]** in the Bible.
