====== forgive ======


==== (forgive, forgiveness) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * To not make someone pay for their sins.
  * To choose not to be offended or angry about something wrong someone has done.
  * Forgiveness is the action of deciding to not punish someone and to not be angry about something wrong or hurtful that has been done.
  * Forgiveness brings reconciliation with God and other people.
  * God forgives people their sins based on the blood Jesus shed on the cross to pay for everyone's sin.
  * When people trust in Jesus and His sacrifice for their sin, God forgives them.
  * When people repent and confess their sin to God, He will forgive them and purify them from all their sin.
