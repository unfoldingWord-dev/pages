====== forgive ======


==== (forgive, forgiveness) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * To choose not to require that someone pay for something they have done wrong.
  * To decide not to resent someone or be angry at him for doing something hurtful.
  * Forgiveness is the act of forgiving.
  * God forgives people their sins because of Jesus' sacrificial death on the cross.
  
  

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:07-10|[07-10]]]** But Esau had already **forgiven** Jacob, and they were happily reunited. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:13-15|[13-15]]]** Then Moses climbed the mountain again and prayed that God would **forgive** the people. God listened to Moses and **forgave** them.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** David repented of his sin and God **forgave** him.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** In the New Covenant, God would write his law on the people's hearts, the people would know God, they would be his people, and God would **forgive** their sins. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:29-01|[29-01]]]** One day Peter said to Jesus, "Master, how many times should I **forgive** my brother when he sins against me?"
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-05|[38-05]]]** It is my blood of the New Covenant shed for the **forgiveness** of sins.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-05|[38-05]]]** In this way, Jesus said that his blood would be poured out to **forgive** their sins.
