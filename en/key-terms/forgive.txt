====== forgive ======


==== (forgive, forgiveness) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * To not make someone pay for their sins.
  * To choose not to be offended or angry about something wrong someone has done.
  * Forgiveness is the action of deciding to not punish someone and to not be angry about something wrong or hurtful that has been done.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:07-10|[07-10]]]**  But Esau had already **forgiven** Jacob, and they were happily reunited. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-33|[19-33]]]** Then Moses climbed the mountain again and prayed that God would **forgive** the people. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** In the New Covenant, God would write His law on the people's hearts, the people would know God, they would be His people, and God would **forgive** their sins. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:29-01|[29-01]]]** One day Peter said to Jesus, "Lord, how many times should I **forgive** my brother when he sins against me?"
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-05|[38-05]]]** It is my blood of the New Covenant shed for the **forgiveness** of sins.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:38-05|[38-05]]]** In this way, Jesus said that his blood would be poured out to **forgive** their sins.
