====== peace ======


==== (peace, peaceful) ====


===== Definitions: =====

Peace is the absence of any conflict, fear, or intimidation. 

  * Peace can refer to freedom from war between groups of people.
  * To make peace with a group of people is to promise not to fight against them.
  * Peace can also refer to a good relationship between people or between people and God.
  * Personal peace refers to a calm state of mind with no anxiety or fear. 


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:15-06|[15-06]]]** God had commanded the Israelites not to make **peace** with any of the people groups in Canaan.  
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:15-12|[15-12]]]** Then God gave Israel **peace** along all its borders. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-03|[16-03]]]** Then God provided a deliverer who rescued them from their enemies and brought **peace** to the land. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:21-13|[21-13]]]** His punishment would bring **peace** between God and people. For this reason, it pleased God to crush the Messiah. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:48-14|[48-14]]]** Like David, Jesus is a king, but Jesus is the king of the universe! He will come again and rule his Kingdom with justice and **peace**, forever. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:50-13|[50-13]]]** Instead, they will live forever with God in perfect **peace**.
