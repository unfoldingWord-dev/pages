====== preach ======


===== Definition: =====

To speak to a group of people, teaching them about God and urging them to obey him.

    * Usually preaching is done by one person to a large group of people.
    * "Preaching" and "teaching" are similar, but not the same. "Preaching" mainly refers to public proclamation of spiritual or moral truth, and urging a response by the audience. "Teaching" emphasizes instruction, imparting knowledge or skill to the learner. 
    * John the Baptist, Jesus and the apostles especially preached to people that they should repent and enter the kingdom of God.
