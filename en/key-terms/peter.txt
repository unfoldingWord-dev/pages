====== Peter ======


===== Facts: =====

Peter was one of Jesus' disciples, and then became one of his 12 apostles.

  * Peter was a fisherman. 
  * When Jesus invited him, 
  * After Jesus went to heaven, Peter taught many people about Jesus. 
  * Peter prayed and God did miraculous things so that people would know that his teaching about Jesus was true.
