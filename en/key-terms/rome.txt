====== Rome ======


==== (Rome, Roman) ====


===== Facts: =====


    * Rome is the capital city of the country of Italy.
    * Rome was the center of the Roman Empire during the time when Jesus and the apostles were alive.
    * The apostle Paul was taken to the city of Rome as a prisoner b<font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>ecause he preached the good news about Jesus.</font>

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:23-04|[23-04]]]** About the time Mary was to give birth, the **Roman** government told everyone to go to the town of their ancestors for a census.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:39-09|[39-09]]]** Early the next morning, the Jewish leaders brought Jesus to the **Roman** governor, Pilate, hoping to have Jesus killed.
