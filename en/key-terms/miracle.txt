====== miracle ======


==== (miracle, wonder, sign) ====

===== Definitions: =====

  * Miracles are extraordinary things that God does with great power. Sometimes they are called wonders.
  * When we say that a person did a miracle, we mean that the person prayed or said that something would happen, and God made it happen.
  * Often miracles are called signs because they show us something about God or the person who prayed for the miracle.
