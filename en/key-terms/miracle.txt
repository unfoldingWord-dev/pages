====== miracle ======


==== (miracle, sign) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * Miracles are extraordinary things that God does with great power.
  * When we say that a person did a miracle, we mean that the person prayed or said that something would happen, and God made it happen.
  * Often miracles are called signs because they show us something about God or the person God works through.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:term|term]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-08|[16-08]]]**Gideon asked God for two **signs** so he could be sure that God would use him to save Israel.

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:19-14|[19-14]]]** God did many **miracles** through Elisha. 
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:37-10|[37-10]]]** Many of the Jews believed in Jesus because of this **miracle**.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:45-10|[45-10]]]** Stephen did many **miracles** and argued strongly that people should believe in Jesus.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:49-02|[49-02]]]** Jesus did many **miracles** proving he is God. He walked on water, calmed storms, healed many sick people, drove out demons, raised the dead to life, and fed 5,000 people with five loaves of bread and two fish.
