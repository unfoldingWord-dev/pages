====== Cyrus ======


===== Facts: =====

  * Cyrus was King of the Persian empire.
  * Under Cyrus's rule, Persia conquered the Babylonian empire.
  * Cyrus allowed the Jews in Babylon to go back to Jerusalem and build Yahweh's temple.
