====== teacher ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definition: =====

  * The word 'teacher' in the Bible refers to someone who teaches about God, not a school teacher for children. Some languages have different 
  * In OBS the word 'teacher' is capitalized as 'Teacher' when it is used as a title for Jesus.
  * People called Jesus 'Teacher' because he taught them about God, and this was a way to show him respect.
  * Some cultures have other words that they use to show respect to teachers, such as "Sir" or "Rabbi" or "Preacher".
