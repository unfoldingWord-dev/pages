====== teacher ======


==== (teacher, Teacher) ====


===== Definition: =====

A teacher is a person who instructs other people. Teachers help others to obtain and use both knowledge and skills.

  * The word "teacher" is used in a special sense in the Bible to refer to someone who teaches about God, not to a school teacher for children.
  * The people that learn from a teacher are called "students." People who learn spiritual truth from a teacher are also called "disciples."
  * In these stories, the word "teacher" is capitalized as, "Teacher" when it is used as a title for Jesus.
  * People called Jesus, "Teacher" to show him respect because he taught them about God.
  * Some cultures have other words that they use to show respect to teachers, such as, "Sir" or, "Rabbi" or, "Preacher."
