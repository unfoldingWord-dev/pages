====== Abraham ======

(Abraham, Abram)


===== Facts: =====

Abraham was the person that God chose to start the family that would grow into the people known as the Israelites. 

    * God promised Abraham that he would have many descendants and that they would become a great nation.
    * God made his covenant with Abraham and promised to uphold it with his descendants forever.
    * Abraham believed God and obeyed him. 
    * Abraham's name was originally "Abram," but God changed it to Abraham.
    * Abram means "exalted father," and Abraham means "father of many."

[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:sarah|Sarah]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:isaac|Isaac]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:04-06|[04-06]]]** When **Abram** arrived in Canaan God said, "Look all around you. I will give to you and your descendants all the land that you can see as an inheritance."
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-04|[05-04]]]** Then God changed **Abram**'s name to **Abraham**, which means “father of many.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-05|[05-05]]]** About a year later, when **Abraham** was 100 years old and Sarah was 90, Sarah gave birth to Abraham's son.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:05-06|[05-06]]]** When Isaac was a young man, God tested **Abraham's** faith by saying, "Take Isaac, your only son, and kill him as a sacrifice to me."
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:06-01|[06-01]]]** When **Abraham** was very old and his son, Isaac, had grown to be a man, **Abraham** sent one of his servants back to the land where his relatives lived to find a wife for his son, Isaac.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:06-04| [06-04]]]** After a long time, **Abraham** died and all of the promises that God had made to him in the covenant were passed on to Isaac.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:21-02|[21-02]]]** God promised **Abraham** that through him all people groups of the world would be blessed.
