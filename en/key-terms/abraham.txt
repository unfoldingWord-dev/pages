====== Abraham ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * The patriarch of Israel, whom God called to become a great nation.
    * Abraham believed God and obeyed Him.
    * God made a covenant with Abraham and his descendants.
    * "Abraham" means "father of many."


(also see [[:en:key-terms:abram|Abram]])
