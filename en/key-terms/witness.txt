====== witness ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Definition: =====

  * A witness is someone who has seen or heard something happen.
  * When someone is taken to trial for doing something wrong, witnesses are asked to tell what they saw or heard. This is to give evidence about the person's guilt or innocence.
  * Witnesses are expected to tell the truth about what they saw or heard.


[See also: **[[:en:key-terms:guilt|guilt]]**]

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:39-02|[39-02]]]** Inside the house, the Jewish leaders put Jesus on trial. They brought many **witnesses** who lied about him.
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:39-04|[39-04]]]** The high priest tore his clothes in anger and shouted, “We do not need any more **witnesses**. You have heard him say that he is the Son of God. What is your judgment?”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:42-08|[42-08]]]** “It was also written that repentance for forgiveness of sins would be proclaimed to all people groups, starting in Jerusalem. You are **witnesses** of these things.”
  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:43-07|[43-07]]]** “God raised this Jesus from the dead. We are witnesses.”
