====== Ishmael ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * The son of Abram through his second wife, Hagar.
    * Ishmael was not the son through whom God would fulfill His promise to Abraham.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|Ishmael Born [05-02]]] So Abram married Hagar. Hagar had a baby boy, and Abram named him Ishmael.


[[:en:obs-training:frames:0x-0x|Isaac Promised [05-04]]] "I will make Ishmael a great nation, too, but my covenant will be with Isaac."
