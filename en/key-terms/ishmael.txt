====== Ishmael ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * Ishmael was the son of Abram through his second wife, Hagar.
    * Ishmael was not the son through whom God would fulfill His promise to Abraham.

[see also: **[[:en:key-terms:Abraham|Abraham]], [[:en:key-terms:Sarah|Sarah]]**]
