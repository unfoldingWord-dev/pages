====== Angel of the LORD ======


==== (angel of the LORD, angel of God) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * The phrase 'the angel of the LORD' and 'the angel of God' sometimes refers to God Himself. \\ 
  * The phrase 'an angel of the LORD' or 'an angel of God' is always used to refer to an ordinary angel.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


**[[:en:obs-training:frames:16-05|[16-05]]]**  The angel of God came to Gideon and said, "God is with you, mighty warrior. Go and save Israel from the Midianites."
