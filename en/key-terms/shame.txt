====== shame ======


===== Definitions: =====

[demoted from KT]

    * The feeling that comes with being embarrassed because we have done something wrong.
    * A sense that something is inappropriate, should not be done, or causes a bad reputation.
    * Before Adam and Eve sinned there was no feeling of shame about nakedness. 

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-01|[02-01]]]** Neither of them wore clothes, but this did not cause them to feel any shame, because there was no sin in the world.
