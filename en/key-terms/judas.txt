====== Judas ======


==== (term, other) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * Judas was one of Jesus' twelve apostles. 
  * He had the responsibility of holding onto the apostles' money, but he stole some of it for himself.
  * Judas betrayed Jesus by telling the religious leaders where Jesus was so they could arrest him.
  * When he saw that the religious leaders wanted to kill Jesus, Judas regretted what he did and killed himself.
