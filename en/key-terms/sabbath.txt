====== Sabbath ======


==== (Sabbath, day of rest, Sabbath day) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * The Sabbath day was a special day each week that God told the Israelites to set apart as a day of rest.
  * Each Sabbath was the seventh day of the week.
  * After God finished creating the world in six days, He rested on the seventh day. In the same way, God commanded the Israelites to set aside the seventh day as a special resting day to worship Him.

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:41-03|[41-03]]]**  The day after Jesus was buried was a **Sabbath**, a **day of rest**.
