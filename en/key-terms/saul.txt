====== Saul (OT) ======


==== () ====


===== Facts: =====

  * Saul was an Israelite man who became the first king of Israel.
  * Although he served God at first, he later became proud and disobeyed God. As a result, God appointed David to take Saul's place as king.
  * Hundreds of years later, there was a Jew named Saul whose name was changed to Paul after he came to know Jesus.

  ***[[:en:obs-training:frames:17-05|[17-05]]]** Eventually, **Saul** died in battle, and David became king of Israel.
