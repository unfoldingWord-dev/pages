====== Jeremiah ======


===== Facts: =====

  * Jeremiah was one of God's prophets in Judah.
  * Jeremiah said that the Babylonian army would capture the city of Jerusalem.
  * Some people were angry with him for saying that, so they put him in well and hoped that he would die there.
  * The king in Jerusalem told some people to pull Jeremiah out of the well so he could live.
