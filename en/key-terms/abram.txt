====== Abram ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * The original name of Abraham, the first patriarch of the Israelite people.
    * Abram means "exalted father".

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


God Chose Abram [04-04]  Hundreds of years later, God spoke to a man named **Abram**.


No Son [04-08]  Twenty years went by, but **Abram** and Sarai still had no son.


God's Covenant [04-09]  Then God made a covenant with **Abram**.


Isaac Promised [05-04]  Then God changed **Abram**'s name to Abraham, which means “father of many.”
