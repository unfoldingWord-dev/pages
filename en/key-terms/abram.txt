====== Abram ======


===== Definitions: =====


    * The original name of Abraham, the first patriarch of the Israelite people.
    * Abram means "exalted father".

04-04 Hundreds of years later, God spoke to a man named Abram.

04-08 Twenty years went by, but Abram and Sarai still had no son.


04-09 Then God made a covenant with Abram.


05-04 Then God changed Abram's name to Abraham, which means “father of many.”
