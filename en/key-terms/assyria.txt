====== Assyria ======


==== (Assyria, Assyrian, Assyrian Empire) ====


===== Facts: =====

  * Assyria was a powerful empire during the time that the Israelites were living in the land of Canaan.
  * The Assyrians fought against Israel at different times in their history. This was one way that God punished his people for rebelling against him and worshiping idols.
  * It was the Assyrians who brought in many foreigners to intermarry with the Israelites; the Samaritan people group formed from the descendants of the children from these marriages.
