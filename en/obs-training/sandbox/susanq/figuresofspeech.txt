====== Figures of Speech ======


A figure of speech is a special use of language. In many figures of speech, the words and phrases are not intended to be understood literally. The mean something different than what their individual words mean.


For example frame 02-06 it say's that Adam and Eve's eye's were opened. What this means is that they realized something they hadn't known before.


02-09 will crush his head means will destroy him


02-09 will wound his heal means will injure him, but not completely destroy him
