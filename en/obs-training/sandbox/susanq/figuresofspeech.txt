====== Figures of Speech ======


===== Definition =====


    * A figure of speech is a special way of speaking. In many figures of speech, the words and phrases are not meant to be understood literally. They mean something different than what their individual words mean.


===== Examples from the stories =====


[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-06|their eyes were opened [02-06]]]means that they realized something they hadn't known before.


02-09 will crush his head means will destroy him.


02-09 will wound his heal means will injure him, but not completely destroy him.


03-02 he found grace in God's eyes means that God chose to think kindly about him.


03-02 in God's eyes refers to the way God chose to think about him.
