====== Luke 1:11-13 Sample ======


===== TFT Text: =====

The angel was standing at the right side of the place //where the priests burned// incense. 12 When Zechariah saw the angel, he was startled and became very afraid. 13 But the angel said to him, “Zechariah, do not be afraid! ===== UFW Text (from WEB): =====

11 An angel of the Lord appeared to him, standing on the right side of the altar of incense. 12 Zacharias was troubled when he saw him, and fear fell upon him. 13 But the angel said to him, “Don’t be afraid, Zacharias, because your request has been heard, and your wife, Elizabeth, will bear you a son, and you shall call his name John.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **an angel of the Lord** - "an angel sent by the Lord" or "an angel whom the Lord sent"
  * **appeared to him** - "suddenly came to Zechariah"
  * **standing** - The angel (not Zechariah) was standing to the right of the altar.
  * **right side** - The right side is the place of honor. If someone saw a man at the king's right side he would know that the king thought highly of that man. When Zechariah saw the angel standing at the right side of God's altar of incense, he could know that God thought highly of that angel. This could also be translated as "the place of honor next to"
  * **fear fell upon him** - This is an idiom meaning that Zechariah felt very fearful.
  * **your request has been heard** - "God has heard your prayer" or "God has heard what you asked him for"\\
  * **and your wife** - The result of God's hearing Zechariah's prayer was that Zechariah's wife would bear him a son. This can also be translated as "so your wife". 
  * **will bear you a son** - "will give birth to your son"
  * **call his name John** - "name him John"
