====== 4c. God's Covenant with Abraham ======


===== Overview: =====


Sin caused everyone to turn away from God. So God divided them into different language groups to limit their ability to do evil. But He chose one man, Abram, to follow Him. He promised to bless all of the families of the earth through the Abram's belief.
