==== Frame Sample 1 ====

{{:en:obs:obs-02-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**Adam** and his wife were very happy living in the beautiful garden **God** had made for them. Neither of them wore clothes, but they did not feel any **shame**, because there was no **sin ** in the world. They often walked in the garden and talked with **God**.


=== Special Words and Phrases: ===


  * **[[:en:key-terms:adam|Adam]]** - the first man that God created
  * **garden** -  a special collection of trees and plants that God prepared for Adam and Eve
  * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** - Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
  * **[[:en:key-terms:shame|shame]]** - a sense that something is inappropriate, should not be done, or causes a bad reputation
  * **[[:en:key-terms:sin|sin]]** - anything that is opposed to the way God desires for us to think and act


=== Translation Notes and Helps: ===


  * "//very happy living//" - the mood as this story begins is relaxed, carefree, and happy. Everything is good. There are no problems.
  * "//often walked â€¦ and talked with God//" - The man and the woman habitually, regularly had close, personal fellowship with God. They walked and talked with him as one would do with a friend.
