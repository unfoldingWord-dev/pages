====== Creation Level 3======

===== "the six-day period during which God made everything that exists" =====

  * This term may be used as a proper noun referring to the period in which God made everything that exists. Another usage of the term that is seen more in the New Testament is “creation” functioning as a common noun, referring to all that God created.
    * Compare the following:
      * “Creation” as a proper noun: Genesis 2:3 “So God blessed the seventh day and made it holy, because on it God rested from all his work that he had done in creation.” (ESV, emphasis added)
      * “Creation” as a proper noun: Romans 8:38-39 “ For I am sure that neither death nor life, nor angels nor rulers, nor things present nor things to come, nor powers, nor height nor depth, nor anything else in all creation, will be able to separate us from the love of God in Christ Jesus our Lord.” (ESV, emphasis added)
