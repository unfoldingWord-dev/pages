====== Evil ======


==== (wicked, bad) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * Anything opposed to God's character and purposes.
  * The opposite of good.
  * Other words with similar meanings are "bad" or "wicked".
  * Something that causes harm or is morally wrong.
