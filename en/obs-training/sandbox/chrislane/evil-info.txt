====== Evil (draft) ======


==== (evil, wicked, bad) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * Anything opposed to God's character and purposes.
  * The opposite of good.
  * Other words with similar meanings are "bad" or "wicked".
  * Something that causes harm or is morally wrong.



===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  *Anything opposed to God's character and purposes.
  *The opposite of good.
  *Other words with similar meanings are “bad” or “wicked”.
  *Something that causes harm or is morally wrong.
  
===Examples from the Bible stories===

03-01 “They had become very wicked and violent. It became so bad that God decided to destroy the whole world with a huge flood.

03-02 “He [Noah] was the only righteous man among all the wicked people.”

04-02 “They were very proud, and they did not care about what God said…God saw that if they all kept working together, they could do many evil things.”

08-12 “You tried to do evil when you sold me as a slave, but God used the evil for good!”

14-02 “The Canaanites did not believe in God. They worshiped false gods and did many evil things.”

17-01 “Saul did well at first, but he became a wicked man who did not obey God…”

19-02 “Ahab was an evil man who encouraged people to worship a false god named Baal.”

29-08 “In his anger the king threw the wicked servant into prison until he could pay back all of his debt.”

43-02 “When they reached the other side, a man with an evil spirit came running up to Jesus.”

50-09 “The weeds represent the people who belong to the evil one.”

50-17 And Jesus will reign forever and ever. He will wipe away every tear and there will be no more death, suffering, sadness, crying, evil, or pain.
