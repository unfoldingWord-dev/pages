===Definitions===
In agreement with or reflecting God's character, purposes and will.
Having positive and beneficial characteristics or influence.
Being skilled at doing something.

===Translation tip===
After you choose a word to translate this term try to use it consistently when it appears in other Bible stories. Make sure that the translation of this term has the correct meaning in each context. Always check for any discussion of this term on the picture frame page that might point out a different meaning or use of that word in that text.
