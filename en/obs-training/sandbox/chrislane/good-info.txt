====== Good (draft) ======


===== Definitions: =====

  * In agreement with or reflecting God's character, purposes and will. 
  * Having positive and beneficial characteristics or influence. 
  * Being skilled at doing something. 


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

[[:en:obs-training:frames:01-05|Plants Created [01-05]]]  "God saw that what He had created was **good**, and He was pleased with it all."

[[:en:obs-training:frames:01-11|Two Trees [01-11]]]  "the tree of the knowledge of **good** and evil. God told Adam that he could eat from any tree in the garden except from the tree of the knowledge of **good** and evil."

[[:en:obs-training:frames:01-12|Adam Alone [01-12]]]  "It is not **good** for man to be alone" 

[[:en:obs-training:frames:02-04|The Lie [02-04]]]  "You will be like God, knowing **good** and evil."
