====== Die ======


==== (dying, death, dead) ====


===== Definitions: =====

=== Physical death ===

  * When the spirit is separated from the body
  * To stop being alive.
  * To lose physical life

=== Spiritual death ===

  * When the person is separated from God
  * Also refers to dying spiritually.

Note: "death" is the opposite of "life". In the Bible, physical and spiritual death are often compared and contrasted to physical and spiritual life.
