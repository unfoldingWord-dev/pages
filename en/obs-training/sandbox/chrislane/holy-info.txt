====== Holy (draft) ======


===== Definitions: =====

   * To be like God in character--clean, pure, sinless.
  * Set apart by God for a special purpose.
  * Relating to God's presence
  * a holy object is //not// average or common (X)



===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


[09-12]  "You are standing on **holy** ground."

[13-02]  "If you will obey My voice and keep My covenant, you will be My prized possession, a kingdom of priests, and a **holy** nation."

[13-05]  "Remember to keep the Sabbath day **holy**."

[50-02]  "Christians are to live **holy** and godly lives."
