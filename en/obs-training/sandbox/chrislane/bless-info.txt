====== Bless ======


==== (blessed) ====


===== Definitions: =====

  * to state that positive and beneficial things will happen
  * to give special positive status to something; to honor
  * to cause beneficial things to happen to people
  * to formally pronounce positive things on one's children.
  * blessing is the opposite of cursing.
