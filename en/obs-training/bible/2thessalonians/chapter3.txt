**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse12|2 Thessalonians 3:12]]**

Summary: //We command those fellow believers who are not working to support themselves by settling down and working.//


**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse13|2 Thessalonians 3:13]]**

Summary: //You others, do not stop doing what is right because you are discouraged.//

Summary: //Publicly identify any fellow believer who does not obey what we have written in this letter and do not associate with him.//


**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse16a|2 Thessalonians 3:16a]]**

Summary: //I pray that our Lord Jesus himself will give peace to you always and in every situation.//
