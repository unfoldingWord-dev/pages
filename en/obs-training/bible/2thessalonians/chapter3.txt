===== 2 Thessalonians 3 =====

**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse1-2|2 Thessalonians 3:1-2]]**

//Pray that more and more people will believe the message about our Lord Jesus.//


**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse3-5|2 Thessalonians 3:3-5]]**

//Our Lord Jesus will cause you to continue to be steadfast and he will protect you from the evil one.//


**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse6-11|2 Thessalonians 3:6-11]]**

Summary: //We command you to disassociate yourselves from every fellow believer who refuses to work.//


**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse12|2 Thessalonians 3:12]]**

Summary: //We command those fellow believers who are not working to support themselves by settling down and working.//


**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse13|2 Thessalonians 3:13]]**

Summary: //You others, do not stop doing what is right because you are discouraged.//


**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse14-15|2 Thessalonians 3:14-15]]**

Summary: //Publicly identify any fellow believer who does not obey what we have written in this letter and do not associate with him.//


**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse16a|2 Thessalonians 3:16a]]**

Summary: //I pray that our Lord Jesus himself will give peace to you always and in every situation.//


**[[en:obs-training:bible:2thessalonians:chapter3:verse16b-18|2 Thessalonians 3:16b-18]]**

Summary: //In closing, I pray that our Lord Jesus will continue to bless you all. I, Paul, am greeting you and I am writing this myself in order that you may know that it is I who have sent this letter.//
