====== 1 Thessalonians 1:1 ======


Summary: //I, Paul, greet you believers who are in Thessalonica.// 


    - **//I//**, Paul, **//am writing this letter//**. Silas and Timothy **//are with me//**. **//We (exc) are sending this letter//** to **//you who are//** the congregation of **//believers//** in Thessalonica **//city//**. **We**  (inc) all worship (OR, trust in) God our Father [MET] and our Lord Jesus Christ. We three desire/pray that God, our Father, and Jesus Christ, our Lord, will continue to act kindly toward you and will continue to cause you to have inner peace.
===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **am writing this letter**- This is suggested in the Greek.
    * **are with me**- This is suggested in the Greek.
    * **We (exc)**- this is an exclusive personal plural pronoun.
    * **you who are**- This is suggested in the Greek.
    * **believers**- This is suggested in the Greek.
    * **We (inc)**  - this is an inclusive personal plural pronoun.
    * **(Or, trust in)**- a word meaning worship or trust in can be used in this sentence.
    * **God our Father (MET)**  - this is a [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metonymy|Metonymy]] figure of speech.
