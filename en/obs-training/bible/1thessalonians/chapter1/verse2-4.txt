====== 1 Thessalonians 1:2-4 ======


==== Prayer of Thanksgiving ====


Summary: //We always thank God for you all. We continually remember the way you live as believers and we know that God chose you. // 


**2** We thank God always for all of you as we mention you constantly in our prayers, **3** because we recall in the presence of our God and Father your work of faith and labor of love and endurance of hope in our Lord Jesus Christ. **4** We know, brothers and sisters loved by God, that he has chosen you, ((NET))
