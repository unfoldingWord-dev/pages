====== 1 Thessalonians 5:16-18 ======

Summary: //Always rejoice, pray, and thank God.//

16 Be joyful ◄at all times/always►, 

17 pray continually, 

18 and thank God ◄in all circumstances/regardless of what happens►. God wants you to behave like that because of what Christ Jesus has done for you (OR, because you have a close relationship with Christ Jesus).
