===== 1 Thessalonians 5 =====


**[[:en:obs-training:bible:1thessalonians:chapter5:verse1-8|1 Thessalonians 5:1-8]]**

Summary: //We believers should be prepared for the time when the Lord Jesus returns and not be as unbelievers who will be unprepared. We must not be like them. On the contrary, we must be vigilant and self-controlled.//


**[[:en:obs-training:bible:1thessalonians:chapter5:verse9-11|1 Thessalonians 5:9-11]]**

Summary: //God has destined us believers to be saved from future punishment and to be able to live together with our Lord Jesus after he returns. Since this is true, encourage each other.//


**[[:en:obs-training:bible:1thessalonians:chapter5:verse12-13|1 Thessalonians 5:12-13]]**

Summary: //Recognize as leaders those people who care for and instruct you; highly esteem and love them. Live peacefully with each other.//


**[[:en:obs-training:bible:1thessalonians:chapter5:verse14-15|1 Thessalonians 5:14-15]]**

Summary: //Warn believers who will not work, and encourage and help those who need it. Be patient with all people. Do good deeds to them all, including those who do evil deeds to you.//

**[[:en:obs-training:bible:1thessalonians:chapter5:verse16-18|1 Thessalonians 5:16-18]]**

Summary: //Always rejoice, pray, and thank God.//


**[[:en:obs-training:bible:1thessalonians:chapter5:verse19-22|1 Thessalonians 5:19-22]]**

Summary: //Evaluate all messages that people claim that the Holy Spirit gave them. Accept authentic messages and obey them.//

**[[:en:obs-training:bible:1thessalonians:chapter5:verse23-24|1 Thessalonians 5:23-24]]**

Summary: //We pray that God will cause you to be distinct people who behave right in every way, and I am sure that he will do this.//


**[[:en:obs-training:bible:1thessalonians:chapter5:verse25-28|1 Thessalonians 5:25-28]]**

Summary: //Pray for us. Affectionately greet all your fellow believers, and make certain that someone reads this letter aloud to all of them. May the Lord bless you.//
