====== 37. Jesus Raises Lazarus from the Dead ======


===== Overview: =====


Jesus heard that His friend, Lazarus, was sick. However, Jesus waited until Lazarus died before he went to visit. Jesus told Lazarusâ€™ sisters, Mary and Martha to believe in Him. Then Jesus told them to open the tomb where Lazarus was buried. He prayed to God, and called to Lazarus to come out alive. Many people believed in Jesus because of this miracle. But the religious leaders were jealous and planned to kill Him.
