====== The People Were Angry [47-06] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-47-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The men who owned the **[[:en:key-terms:servant| slave girl]]** became very angry when they realized that **without the demon**, **the slave girl could no longer make money for them**.

[new]

The men who owned the **[[:en:key-terms:servant|slave girl]]** became very angry! They realized that **without the demon**, the slave girl could not tell people the future. **This meant that** **her owners could not use her to make money for them**.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **without the demon** - That is, "without the power from the demon" or, "now that she did not have the demon."
  * **This meant that** - This could also be translated as, “Because of this.”
  * **her owners could not use her to make money for them** – That is, “people would no longer pay her owners money to hear their future told to them.”
