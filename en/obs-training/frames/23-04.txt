====== The Trip to Bethlehem [23-04] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-23-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

About the time **[[:en:key-terms:mary|Mary]] was to give birth**, the **[[:en:key-terms:rome|Roman]] government** told everyone to go to the **town of their ancestors** for a **census**. **[[:en:key-terms:joseph-nt|Joseph]]** and Mary had to make a long journey from where they lived in **[[:en:key-terms:nazareth|Nazareth]]** to **[[:en:key-terms:bethlehem|Bethlehem]]** because their ancestor was **[[:en:key-terms:david|David]]** whose hometown was Bethlehem.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **was to give birth** - That is, it was the end of her pregnancy and she was ready to give birth. 
  * **Roman government** - Rome had conquered and ruled over Israel at this time.
  * **town of their ancestors** - That is, the town where their ancestors had lived.
  * **census** - A census was a government counting of the entire population--usually in order to plan for taxes.
