====== Sun, Moon and Stars Created [01-06] ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the fourth day of creation, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** spoke and created the sun, the moon, and the stars. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** created them to give light to the earth and to mark day and night, seasons and years. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** saw that this was **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the fourth day** - The next in the orderly series of days in which God created.
    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] spoke** - God created the sun, moon, and stars by speaking a command.
    * **light** - Shining objects in the sky now provided light for the earth.
    * **day and night, seasons and years** - God created a different light to mark each portion of time from small to large, and set them to repeat continually until the end of time.
