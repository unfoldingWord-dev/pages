====== Sun, Moon and Stars Created [01-06] ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the fourth day of creation, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** spoke and made the sun, the moon, and the stars. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** made them to give light to the earth and to mark day and night, seasons and years.


===== Emphasis: =====


God spoke again on the fourth day and created the sun, moon and stars. He made each one to give a differnt kind of light to the earth, even during the night. He also made them to provide a way for us to measure the steady, orderly passage of time. 


===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the fourth day** - the next in the orderly series of days in which God created
    * **[[:en:obs-training/sandbox/chrislane/god-info|God]] spoke** - God created the sun, moon, and stars by means of a simple verbal command.
    * **light** - shining objects in the sky now provided light for the earth, replacing the special light that God made on the first day
    * **day and night, seasons and years** - God created a different light to mark each portion of time from small to large, and set them to repeat continually until the end of time.
