====== The New Covenant [21-05] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-21-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**Through the [[:en:key-terms:prophet|prophet]] [[:en:key-terms:jeremiah|Jeremiah]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** **[[:en:key-terms:promise|promised]]** that he would make a **[[:en:key-terms:covanent-new|New Covenant]]**, but not like God’s **[[:en:key-terms:covenant|Covenant]]** with **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israel]]**. In the New Covenant, God would **write his law on the people’s hearts**, the people would **know God**, they would **be his people**, and God would **[[:en:key-terms:forgive|forgive]]** their **[[:en:key-terms:sin|sins]]**. The **[[:en:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]]** would **start the New Covenant**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Through the prophet Jeremiah** - That is, through the messages that God gave to Jeremiah.
  * **write his law on the people’s hearts** - This expression means that the the people would understand and have a deep desire to obey his law. It would no longer be something external to them.
  * **know God** - This means that they would know God on a very intense and personal level.
  * **be his people** - There will be an extremely close bond between God and the people.
  * **start the New Covenant** - That is, the Messiah will cause the New Covenant to begin to take effect.
