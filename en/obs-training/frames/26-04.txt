====== The Prophesy about Jesus [26-04] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-26-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** sat down. Everybody watched him closely. They knew this passage was talking about the **[[:en:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]]**. Jesus said, “**Today these words have been fulfilled in your hearing**.” All the people were **amazed**. “Isn’t this the son of **[[:en:key-terms:joseph-nt|Joseph]]**?” they said.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Today these words have been fulfilled in your hearing** - This may be translated as, "You are seeing the fulfillment of this prophesy right now."
  * **amazed** - Translate the word "amazed" with a term that means that they were surprised, shocked, and confused about how this could be possible.
