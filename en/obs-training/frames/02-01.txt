====== 02-01. A Perfect World ======


{{:en:obs:obs-02-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**[[:en:key-terms:adam|Adam]]** and his wife were very happy living in the beautiful garden **[[:en:key-terms:GOD|God]]** had made for them. Neither of them wore clothes, but they did not feel any **[[:en:key-terms:shame|shame]]**, because there was no **[[:en:key-terms:sin|sin]]** in the world. They often walked in the garden and talked with **[[:en:key-terms:GOD|God]]**.
