==== Frame 01-16 ====


{{:en:obs:obs-01-16.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When the seventh day came, **God** had already **finished** His **work**. So **God** **rested** from all He had been doing. He **blessed** the seventh day and made it **holy**, because on this day He **rested** from His **work**. This is how **God** **created** the universe and everything in it.


=== Key Terms: ===

  * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** - Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
  * **[[:en:key-terms:finish|finished]]** - to complete or fully accomplish
  * **[[:en:key-terms:work|work]]** - God's labor towards the task of creating the earth and everything in it
  * **[[:en:key-terms:rest|rested]]** - He ceased working on creation because it was complete
  * **[[:en:key-terms:bless|blessed]]** - God gave a special positive status to the seventh day
  * **[[:en:key-terms:holy|holy]]** - He set it apart as a special day
  * **[[:en:key-terms:create|created]]** - "created from nothing," or "caused to exist"
