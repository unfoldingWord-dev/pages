====== 04-02. The Tower ======

{{:en:obs:obs-04-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


They were very **[[:en:key-terms:proud|proud]]**, and they did not care about what **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** said. They even began building a tall tower to reach **[[:en:key-terms:heaven|heaven]]**. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** saw that if they all kept working together, they could do many **[[:en:key-terms:evil|evil]]** things.

===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **tall tower to reach heaven** - a large structure, possibly made from dirt and rock
  * **working together** - they believed that their joint effort would allow them to reach heaven where God lived
  * **many evil things** - things that people do from their own desires and not according to God's instructions are evil
