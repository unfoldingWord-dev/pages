==== Frame 03-11 ====

{{:en:obs:obs-03-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Later **Noah** sent out a bird called a **dove**. But it also could not find any dry land, so it came back to **Noah**. A week later he sent the **dove** out again, and it came back with an **olive** branch in its beak! The water was going down, and the plants were growing again!


=== Key Terms: ===

 
  * **[[:en:key-terms:noah|Noah]]** - righteous man who built the ark and survived the flood
  * **[[:en:key-terms:dove|dove]]** - a small bird known as a dove, or pigeon, often a symbol for peace
  * **[[:en:key-terms:olive|olive]]** - the edible fruit of the olive tree, also squeezed to provide oil


=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to ...


  * "//it came back//" - the first time the dove went out, it could not find a place to land. It returned to the ark.
  * "//olive branch//" - the fresh green branch of an olive tree which would be growing after the flood
  * "//water was going down//" - the olive tree could not grow unless the water went down enough for dry ground to appear
  * "//plants were growing again//" - all of the plants died during the year the flood covered the earth. Ndew plants were now growing from seeds.
