==== 03-01. Sin Increases ====

{{:en:obs:obs-03-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


After a long time, many people were living in the world. They had become very **wicked** and violent. It became so bad that **God** decided to destroy the whole world with a huge flood.


=== Key Terms: ===

 
  * **[[:en:key-terms:wicked|wicked]]** - very evil person who deserves punishment for crimes
  * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** - Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect

=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to ...


  * "//After a long time//" - this story happens many generations after creation
  * "//very wicked and violent//" - the natural result of sin entering into the world
  * "//God decided to destroy//" - God is good and cannot accept sin. He character demands that he punish sin.
  * "//huge flood//" - an unusually large flood where water covered the top of even the highest mountains
