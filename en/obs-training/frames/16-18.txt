====== Israel Wanted a King [16-18] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-16-18.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**Finally**, the people asked **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** for a **[[:en:key-terms:king|king]]** like all the other nations had. They wanted a king who was tall and strong, and who could lead them into battle. God did not like this request, but **he gave them what they wanted**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Finally** - This could be translated as, “After their enemies attacked them many times” or “After many years of being attacked by many different nations.” 
  * **like all the other nations had** - Other nations had a king. Israel wanted to be like them and have a king too. 
  * **he gave them what they wanted** – This could be translated as, “He gave them a king just as they had asked.”
