==== Frame 02-08 ====


{{:en:obs:obs-02-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then **God** asked, “Who told you that you were **naked**? Did you eat the fruit I told you not to eat?” The man answered, “You gave me this woman, and she gave me the fruit.” Then **God** asked the woman, “What have you done?” The woman replied, “The **snake** tricked me.”
=== Key Terms: ===


    * **[[:en:key-terms:beginning|sample]]**- explanation
    * God
    * naked
    * snake


=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to …


    * "////Who told you that you were naked?////" - Being naked was not a sin. God created them that way. Their knowledge of their nakedness was the problem. Their shame showed that they had sinned.
    * "//she gave me the fruit//" - The man blamed the woman rather then confessing his disobedience 
    * "//The snake tricked me//" - The woman blamed the snake rather than confessing her disobedience.
