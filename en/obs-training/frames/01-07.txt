==== 01-07. Birds and Fish Created ====


{{:en:obs:obs-01-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the fifth day, **God** **created** everything that swims in the water and all the birds. **God ** saw that it was **good**, and He **blessed** them.
=== Key Terms: ===


    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**- Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect 
    * **[[:en:key-terms:create|created]]**- "created from nothing," or "caused to exist" 
    * **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**- in agreement with God's character and purposes 
    * **[[:en:key-terms:bless|blessed]]**- God spoke to his intent to make things go well for them
