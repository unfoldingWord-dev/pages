====== The Fire of Hell [50-14] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-50-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

But **those who do not [[:en:key-terms:believe|believe]]** in **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** will be **[[:en:key-terms:judge|judged]]** by **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** and thrown into **[[:en:key-terms:hell|hell]]** forever. In the hell, people constantly **weep and grind their teeth in anguish**. They are burned by fire that never goes out and **eaten by worms** that never **[[:en:key-terms:death|die]]**.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **those who do not believe** - That is, "those people who do not believe."
  * **weep and grind their teeth in anguish** - These expressions represent extreme suffering. Translate them literally if they will be understood. If not, you may need to substitute another phrase the has the same meaning.
  * **eaten by worms** - Worms eat dead bodies. This phrase expresses that the people in hell experience this, even though they are not dead. If possible, translate this literally enough to convey that horrible situation.
