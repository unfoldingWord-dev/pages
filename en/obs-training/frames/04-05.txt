==== Frame 04-05 ====

{{:en:obs:obs-04-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

So **Abram** **obeyed**. He took his wife, **Sarai**, and all his servants and herds of animals and went to the land **God** showed him, the land of **Canaan**.


=== Key Terms: ===

 
  * **[[:en:key-terms:abram|Abram]]** - the original name of Abraham
  * **[[:en:key-terms:obey|obeyed]]** - literally "to hear," meaning to do what was commanded
  * **[[:en:key-terms:sarai|Sarai]]** - the original name of Abraham's wife, Sarah
  * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** - Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
  * **[[:en:key-terms:canaan|Canaan]]** - the area between the Jordan River and the Mediterranean Sea


=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to ...


  * "//all his servants and herds//" - Abram had become very wealthy in his own land, which made obeying God and leaving that land a risk for him
  * "//God showed him//" - Abram went to a country that he did not know only because God directed him there
