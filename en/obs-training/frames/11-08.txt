====== Go Away! [11-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-11-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

That same night, **[[:en:key-terms:pharaoh|Pharaoh]] called for [[:en:key-terms:moses|Moses]]** and **[[:en:key-terms:aaron|Aaron]] and said**, “Take the **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]]** and leave **[[:en:key-terms:egypt|Egypt]]** immediately!” The **[[:en:key-terms:egypt|Egyptian]]** people also urged the Israelites to leave **right away**.

//A Bible story from: Exodus 11:1-12:32//


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **called for** – This means that Pharaoh told his servants to tell Moses and Aaron to come to him. 
  * **and said** – Pharaoh said the following words to Moses and Aaron after they came to him. In some languages it is necessary to say, “and said to them,” or, "After they came, Pharaoh said to them."
  * **right away** - This means the same as "immediately."
  * **A Bible story from:** - If all or portions of the Bible have already been translated into your language, check to make sure that these references point to the correct chapters and verses.
