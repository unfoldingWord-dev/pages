====== The Midianite's Dream [16-11] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-16-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

That night **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** told **[[:en:key-terms:gideon|Gideon]]**, “**Go down** to the **[[:en:key-terms:midian|Midianite]]** camp and when you hear what they say, you will **no longer be afraid**.” So that night, Gideon went down to the camp and heard a Midianite soldier telling his friend about a **[[:en:key-terms:dream|dream]]** he had. The man’s friend said, “This dream means that Gideon's army will defeat the Midianite army!” When Gideon heard this, he **[[:en:key-terms:worship|worshiped]]** God.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Go down** - The Midianite soldiers were camping out in a valley and were at a lower elevation than the Israelite soldiers. 
  * **you will no longer be afraid** – That is, “you will stop being afraid.”
