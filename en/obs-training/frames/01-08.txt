==== 01-08. Animals Created ====


{{:en:obs:obs-01-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the sixth day of **creation**, **God** said, "Let there be all kinds of land animals!" And it happened just like **God** said. Some were farm animals, some crawled on the ground, and some were wild. And **God** saw that it was **good**.
=== Key Terms: ===


    * **[[:en:key-terms:creation|creation]]**- the six-day period during which God made everything that exists
    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**- Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
    * **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**- in agreement with God's character and purposes
