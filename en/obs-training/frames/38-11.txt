====== At Gethsemane [38-11] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-38-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** went with his **[[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciples]]** to a place called **Gethsemane**. Jesus told his disciples to **[[:en:key-terms:pray|pray]]** that they would not **enter into [[:en:key-terms:tempt|temptation]]**. Then Jesus went to pray by himself.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Gethsemane** - This was a small, wooded garden area just outside the walls of Jerusalem.
  * **enter into temptation** - explanation
