====== Two Trees [01-11] ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


In the middle of the garden, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** planted two special treesâ€”the tree of life and the tree of the knowledge of **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]** and **[[:en:key-terms:evil|evil]]**. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**  told **[[:en:key-terms:Adam|Adam]]**  that he could eat from any tree in the garden except from the tree of the knowledge of **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]** and **[[:en:key-terms:evil|evil]]**. If he ate from this tree, he would **[[:en:key-terms:death|die]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **in the middle** - The central location emphasizes the importance of the two trees.
  * **garden** - An area of land where plants and trees are grown for a particular purpose. God had especially prepared this garden as a place for human beings to live in and enjoy. 
  * **tree of [[:en:key-terms:life|life]]** - The fruit of this tree could give unending life to the person who eats it.
  * **tree of knowledge of [[:en:key-terms:good|good]] and [[:en:key-terms:evil|evil]]** - The fruit of this tree could enable a person to know the difference between good and evil. 
  * **knowledge** - To know or understand by personal experience
  * **from any tree except** - The tree of the knowledge of good and evil was the only forbidden tree. There were other permissible trees in addition to the tree of life.
  * **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]] and [[:en:key-terms:evil|evil]]** - These terms are extreme opposites.
  * **[[:en:key-terms:death|die]]** - In this instance, he would die both physically (separation of his spirit from his body) and spiritually (separation of the man from God).
