===== Translation Notes: =====


  * "//garden//" - a special collection of trees and plants that God prepared for Adam 
  * "//tree of life//" - a literal tree with fruit that could keep a person alive continually 
  * "//tree of knowledge of good and evil//" - a literal tree with fruit that could enable a person to know the difference between good and evil
