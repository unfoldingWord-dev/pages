==== Frame 04-08 ====

{{:en:obs:obs-05-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**Abram** and **Sarai** waited over twenty years, but they still had no **son**. **God** spoke to **Abram** and **promised** again that he would have a **son** and as many **descendants** as the stars in the sky. **Abram** **believed** **Godâ€™s** **promise**. **God** said that **Abram** was **righteous** through **believing** in **God's** **promise**.


=== Key Terms: ===

 
  * **[[:en:key-terms:abram|Abram]]** - the original name of Abraham
  * **[[:en:key-terms:sarai|Sarai]]** - the original name of Abraham's wife, Sarah
  * **[[:en:key-terms:son|son]]** - a male offspring
  * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** - Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
  * **[[:en:key-terms:promise|promise]]** - a covenant, promise, or binding agreement between two or more people
  * **[[:en:key-terms:descendant|descendants]]** - people who are physically derived from someone by birth; children, grand children, etc.
  * **[[:en:key-terms:believe|believe]]** - to trust or rely upon something or someone
  * **[[:en:key-terms:righteous|righteous]]** - just, acting in a way that is proper, proven correct


=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to ...


  * "//twenty years//" - twenty years after God promised that Abram would have a son
  * "//no son//" - Abram still did not have a son through whom Abram's descendents could possess the land
  * "//as the stars in the sky//" - a figure of speech that means they will be too many to count
  * "//righteous through believing//" - God considered Abram righteous because Abram believed God's promise
  * "//God's promise//" - that Abram's name would become great and his descendents would possess the land
