====== Enemies Defeated Israel [16-02] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-16-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**As a result of this [[:en:key-terms:disobey|disobedience]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** **[[:en:key-terms:punish|punished]] the [[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]]** by allowing their enemies to defeat them. These enemies stole things from the Israelites, destroyed their property, and killed many of them. **After many years**, the Israelites **[[:en:key-terms:repent|repented]]** and **asked God to rescue them**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **As a result of this disobedience, God punished the Israelites** - This could be translated as, “Because the Israelites kept disobeying God, He punished them...” or, “The Israelites continued to disobey God. So He punished them...” 
  * **After many years** - This could be translated as, “After many years of disobeying God and being oppressed by their enemies…” or “This happened for many years and then…” 
  * **asked God to rescue them** - That is, they asked God to help them and to set them free from their enemies.
