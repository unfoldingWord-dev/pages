====== Enemies Defeated Israel [16-02] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-16-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**As a result of this [[:en:key-terms:disobey|disobedience]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** **[[:en:key-terms:punish|punished]]** the **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]]** by allowing them to be defeated by their enemies. These enemies stole things from the Israelites, destroyed their property, and killed many of them. After many years, the Israelites **[[:en:key-terms:repent|repented]]** and asked God to rescue them.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **As a result of this disobedience, God punished the Israelites** - This could be translated as, “Because the Israelites kept disobeying God, He punished them...” or, “The Israelites continued to disobey God. So He punished them...” 
  * **by allowing them to be defeated by their enemies** - That is, “by allowing their enemies to fight against them and win.” 
  * **After many years** - That is, “After many years of disobeying God and being oppressed by their enemies…” This could also be translated as, “This happened for many years and then…”
