====== The People Attacked the Believers [45-06] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-45-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

A man named **[[:en:key-terms:paul|Saul]]** approved of **Stephen's execution** and **guarded the belongings** of those who **stoned Stephen**. That day many people started **[[:en:key-terms:persecute|persecuting]]** the **[[:en:key-terms:christian|Christians]]**, and they were scattered through **that region**. But everywhere they went they **[[:en:key-terms:preach|preached]]** about **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Stephen's execution** - That is, "the killing of Stephen." See how you translated Stephen in **[[:en:obs-training:frames:45-01|[45-01]]]**.
  * **guarded the belongings** - This could also be translated as, "watched over the possessions." This probably refers to their coats and any bags or other items they had been carrying when they joined in the attack on Stephen.
  * **stoned Stephen** - That is, "threw stones at Stephen."
  * **that region** - This is the area that is today called the Middle East.
