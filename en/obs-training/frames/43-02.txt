====== The Feast of Pentecost [43-02] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-43-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Fifty days after **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** **[[:en:key-terms:raise|rose]]** from the **[[:en:key-terms:death|dead]]**, a **[[:en:key-terms:jew|Jewish]]** feast called **Pentecost** took place. Pentecost was a celebration of thanksgiving. Jews from all over the world would come to **[[:en:key-terms:jerusalem|Jerusalem]]** to celebrate together.

[new]

About a week after **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** went back to heaven, the **[[:en:key-terms:jew|Jews]]**  celebrated **Pentecost**. Pentecost was time when the Jews **celebrated the harvest** every year 50 days after the **[[:en:key-terms:passover|Passover]]**. Jews from all over the world would come to **[[:en:key-terms:jerusalem|Jerusalem]]** to celebrate together.

[alternate]

Every year, 50 days after the **[[:en:key-terms:passover|Passover]]**, the **[[:en:key-terms:jew|Jews]]** held an important celebration called **Pentecost**. Pentecost was a time when the Jews **celebrated the harvest**. Jews from all over the world would come to **[[:en:key-terms:jerusalem|Jerusalem]]** to celebrate together. This year, the time for Pentecost came about a week after **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** had gone back to **[[:en:key-terms:heaven|heaven]]**. 


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pentecost** - "Pentecost" means "fifty." You could use the word "Pentecost" in your translation and let the text explain the meaning. Or you could use a term that means, "50 days."
  * **celebrated the harvest** - The Jews would bring offerings to God from the harvest.
