====== 01-10. Adam Created ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


So **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** took some dirt, formed it into a man, and **[[:en:key-terms:breathe|breathed]]** **[[:en:key-terms:life|life]]** into him. This man's name was **[[:en:key-terms:adam|Adam]]**. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** planted a garden where **[[:en:key-terms:adam|Adam]]** could live, and put him there to care for it.
