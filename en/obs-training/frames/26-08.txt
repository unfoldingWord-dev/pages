====== Jesus Healed the Sick [26-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-26-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** **went throughout the region of [[:en:key-terms:galilee|Galilee]]**, and **great crowds** came to him. They brought **all the sick people**, including those who could not see, walk, hear, or speak, and Jesus **[[:en:key-terms:heal|healed]]** them.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **went throughout the region of Galilee** - That is, "all around in Galilee" or, "from place to place in Galilee."
  * **great crowds** - That is, "large crowds."
  * **all the sick people** – This could also be translated as, “many sick people.” In some contexts “all” means, “a large number of” or, “many.” 
  * **the sick people** – That is, “people who were sick or handicapped.” Some languages will not be able to use “sick” to include such conditions as being blind or deaf.
