==== Frame 02-05 ====


{{:en:obs:obs-02-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


The woman saw that the fruit was beautiful and looked delicious. She also wanted to be **wise**, so she picked some of the fruit and ate it. Then she turned and gave some to her husband, who was with her, and he ate it, too.
=== Key Terms: ===


    * **[[:en:key-terms:wise|wise]]**- decisions and actions that are in accordance with the existance and nature of God


=== Translation Notes: ===

    * "//gave some to her husband//" - it was the woman, rather than the snake, who tempted the man to eat the fruit

    * "//he ate it//" - the man made his own decision to eat the fruit
