====== The Disciples Were Released [44-09] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-44-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The **[[:en:key-terms:jewish-leaders|leaders]]** were shocked because they could see that these men were **common men who were uneducated**. They also realized that these men **had been with [[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**. After they had threatened **[[:en:key-terms:peter|Peter]]** and **[[:en:key-terms:john|John]]**, they **let them go**.

//A Bible story from: Acts 3:1-4:22//


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **common men who were uneducated** - Peter and John were simple fishermen. Translate this phrase with terms that convey that they lacked any formal education.
  * **had been with Jesus** - This could also be translated as, "had spend time with Jesus."
  * **let them go** - That is, "allowed them to leave."
  * **A Bible story from:** - If all or portions of the Bible have already been translated into your language, check to make sure that these references point to the correct chapters and verses.
