====== The Disciples Met Together [43-13] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-43-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The **[[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciples]]** **devoted themselves** to the **[[:en:key-terms:apostle|apostles']]** teaching, fellowship, eating together, and **[[:en:key-terms:pray|prayer]]**. They all met together and shared everything they had. They **[[:en:key-terms:praise|praised]]** **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** and even **non-believers** thought well of them. Every day, more people became **believers**.

[new]

The **[[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciples]]** **were devoted to** each other. They continually listened to the teaching of the **[[:en:key-terms:apostle|apostles]]**, spent time together, ate together, and **[[:en:key-terms:pray|prayed]]** with each other. They would all **[[:en:key-terms:praise|praise]]** **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** and share everything they had with the other disciples. Even the **non-believers** **thought well of them**. Every day, more people became **believers**.

[alternate]

The **[[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciples]]** continually listened to the teaching of the **[[:en:key-terms:apostle|apostles]]**, spent time together, ate together, and **[[:en:key-terms:pray|prayed]]** with each other. They enjoyed **[[:en:key-terms:praise|praising]]** **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** together and they shared everything they had with each other. Everyone **thought well of them**. Every day, more people became **[[:en:key-terms:believer|believers]]**.


//A Bible story from: Acts 2//


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **were devoted to** - This could be translated as, "focused their attention on" or, "gave their time and attention to."
  * **non-believers** - That is, "people who did not believe in Jesus."
  * **thought well of them** - That is, "had a positive opinion of them."
