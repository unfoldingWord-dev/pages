====== Noah Finds Grace [03-02] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-03-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


But **[[:en:key-terms:noah|Noah]] found [[:en:key-terms:grace|grace]]** in **[[:en:key-terms:god|God's]] eyes**. He was a **[[:en:key-terms:righteous|righteous]]** man, living among **[[:en:key-terms:wicked|wicked]]** people. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** told **[[:en:key-terms:noah|Noah]]** about the flood He was **planning to send**. He told **[[:en:key-terms:noah|Noah]]** to build a huge boat called an **[[:en:key-terms:ark|ark]]**.

===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **found grace** - God was pleased with Noah because he feared and obeyed God. So even though Noah was not sinless, God was gracious to him and made a plan to rescue his family from the devastating flood. Make sure this doesn't sound like Noah was lucky or that he just happened to escape. Rather, it was God's choice. 
  * **God's eyes** - This is an idiom that means, in God's judgment or opinion.
  * **planning to send** - God planned to cause deep water to cover the earth by sending a lot of rain. That is, he planned to cause the flood by causing a lot of rain to fall.
