====== God Defeated the Midianites [16-14] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-16-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] confused the [[:en:key-terms:midian|Midianites]]**, so that they started attacking and killing each other. Immediately, **the rest of the [[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]] were called** from their homes to come help chase the Midianites. They killed many of them and chased the rest of them out of **their land**. 120,000 Midianites died that day. God had **[[:en:key-terms:save|saved]]** **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israel]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **God confused the Midianites** - God caused the Midianites to be confused. They wanted to attack the Israelites, but instead, they attacked each other. 
  * **the rest of the Israelites** - This could be translated as, “many other Israelite men.” 
  * **were called** – That is, “were called out” or, “were summoned.” This sentence could also be translated as, “Gideon sent messengers to summon many other Israelite men from their homes…” 
  * **their land** - That is, “the Israelites' land.”
