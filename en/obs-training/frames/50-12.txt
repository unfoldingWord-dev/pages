====== We Will Meet Jesus in the Sky [50-12] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-50-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The **[[:en:key-terms:christian|Christians]]** who are **still [[:en:key-terms:life|alive]]** will then rise up together with them **in the air** and all Christians will be with **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** forever. Jesus will be with his people in perfect unity forever.

[new]

Then the **[[:en:key-terms:christian|Christians]]** who are **still [[:en:key-terms:life|alive]]** will then rise up into the sky to join with the other Christians who have already gone up to be with **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** there. After that Jesus will live with his people **in perfect unity** forever.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **still alive** - That is, "still alive when Jesus returns."
  * **in perfect unity** – This could be translated as, “in perfect peace with each other.”
