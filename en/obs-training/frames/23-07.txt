====== The Angels Praised God [23-07] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-23-07.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

“**Go search for the baby**, and you will find him **wrapped in pieces of cloth** and lying in a feeding trough.” Suddenly, the skies were **filled with [[:en:key-terms:angel|angels]]** praising **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**, saying, “**[[:en:key-terms:glory|Glory]]** to God in **[[:en:key-terms:heaven|heaven]]** and **[[:en:key-terms:peace|peace]]** on earth to the **people he favors**!”


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Go search for the baby** - That is, "go and look for the baby until you find him."
  * **wrapped in pieces of cloth** - The custom of that time was to wrap babies tightly in long strips of cloth to keep them warm and secure.
  * **feeding trough** - A wooden box designed to keep animal food up off of the ground.
  * **filled with angels** - This means that there were so many angels that they seemed to fill the sky. 
  * **people he favors** - This might be translated as, "people that God looks upon with favor, or positive thoughts.
