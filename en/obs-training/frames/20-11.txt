====== Persia Defeated Babylon [20-11] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-20-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

About **seventy years later**, **[[:en:key-terms:cyrus|Cyrus]]**, the **[[:en:key-terms:king|king]]** of the **Persians**, defeated **[[:en:key-terms:babylon|Babylon]]**, so the Persian Empire replaced the Babylonian Empire. **The [[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]] were now called [[:en:key-terms:jew|Jews]]** and most of them had lived their whole lives in Babylon. Only a few very old Jews even remembered the land of **[[:en:key-terms:judah|Judah]]**.

About **seventy years later**, **[[:en:key-terms:cyrus|Cyrus]]**, the **[[:en:key-terms:king|king]]** of the **Persians**, defeated **[[:en:key-terms:babylon|Babylon]]**. The Persian Empire was strong but **[[:en:key-terms:mercy|merciful]]** to the people it conquered. **The [[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]] were now called [[:en:key-terms:jew|Jews]]** and most of them had lived their whole lives in Babylon. Only a few very old Jews even remembered the land of **[[:en:key-terms:judah|Judah]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **seventy years later** - This refers to the seventy years that had passed since the Babylonian army took the people of Jerusalem into Exile.
  * **Persians** - The Persian Empire grew to cover the area from central Asia to Egypt. Its home was located in the general area of modern-day Iran.
  * **The Israelites were now called Jews** - This could also be translated as, "People now called the Israelites by the name 'Jews.'"
