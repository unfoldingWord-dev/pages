====== Jesus in Galilee [26-01] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-26-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**After overcoming** **[[:en:key-terms:satan|Satan's]]** **[[:en:key-terms:tempt|temptations]]**, **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]] returned in the [[:en:key-terms:power-of-god|power]] of the [[:en:key-terms:holy-spirit|Holy Spirit]]**  to the region of **[[:en:key-terms:galilee|Galilee]]** where he lived. Jesus went from place to place teaching. Everyone **spoke well of him**.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After overcoming** - This may also be translated as, "After resisting."
  * **Jesus returned in the power of the Holy Spirit** - This may be translated as, "The power of the Holy Spirit worked through Jesus as he returnedâ€¦"
  * **spoke well of him** - That is, "said good things about him."
