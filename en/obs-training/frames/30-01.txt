====== A Quiet Place [30-01] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-30-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** **sent his [[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciples]] to [[:en:key-terms:preach|preach]] and teach**. When they **returned**, they told Jesus what they had taught and done. Then Jesus invited them to go with him to a **quiet place** across the lake to rest for a while. So, they got into a boat and **left**.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sent his disciples to preach and teach** -  That is, “sent out his disciples to many different villages to preach and to teach the people there” or, “sent out his disciples to preach and to teach people in the towns.”
  * **returned** - This could also  be translated as, "returned to Jesus" or, "returned to where Jesus was."
  * **quiet place** - This refers to a place with few people where they could be by themselves.
  * **left** - That is, "departed" or, "went to the other side of the lake."
