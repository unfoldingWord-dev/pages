====== A Quiet Place [30-01] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-30-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** **sent his [[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciples]] to [[:en:key-terms:preach|preach]] and teach**. When they **returned**, they told Jesus what they had taught and done. Then Jesus invited them to come with him to a **quiet place** to rest for a while. So, they got into a boat and left.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sent his disciple to preach and teach** - This could also be translated as, "sent his disciples out to preach and to teach."
  * **returned** - This could also  be translated as, "returned to Jesus" or, "returned to where Jesus was."
  * **quiet place** - This refers to a place with few people where they could be by themselves.
