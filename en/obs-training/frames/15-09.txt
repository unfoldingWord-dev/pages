====== God Fought the Canaanites [15-09] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-15-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] threw the [[:en:key-terms:canaan|Canaanites]] into confusion**. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] fought for [[:en:key-terms:israel|Israel]]** that day **by sending large hailstones that killed many of the Canaanites**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **threw the Canaanites into confusion** - This could be translated as, “God caused the Canaanites to be confused,” or “God made the Canaanites panic,” or “God caused the Canaanites to be unable to fight together well.” 
  * **God fought for Israel** - God fought on the side of Israel against Israel's enemies. 
  * **by sending large hailstones that killed many of the Canaanites** - This could be translated as, “He caused very large balls of ice to come down from the sky and that ice killed many of the Canaanite soldiers.”
