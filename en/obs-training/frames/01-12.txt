==== Frame 01-12 ====


{{:en:obs:obs-01-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then **God**  said, "It is not **good**  for man to be alone." But none of the animals could be **Adam's** (CJ not a KT) helper.


=== Key Terms: ===

  * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** - Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
  * **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]** - in agreement with God's character and purposes
  * **[[:en:key-terms:Adam|Adam]]** - the name of the first man that God created

=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to …


  * "//man//" - Adam only–emphasizing the fact that women had not yet been created (LS: moved from KT)
  * "//alone//" - by himself, with no possibility of a relationship or to reproduce (LS: moved from KT)
  * "//Adam's helper//" - a person who would be very different from Adam, but would enable him to accomplish the task that God gave to him (LS: moved from KT)
