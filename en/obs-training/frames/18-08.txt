====== Jeroboam Ruled Israel [18-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-18-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The other ten tribes of the **[[:en:key-terms:israel|nation of Israel]]** that rebelled against **[[:en:key-terms:rehoboam|Rehoboam]] appointed a man named [[:en:key-terms:jeroboam|Jeroboam]] to be their [[:en:key-terms:king|king]]**. They **set up their [[:en:key-terms:kingdom|kingdom]]** in the northern part of the land and were called the **[[:en:key-terms:kingdom-of-israel|kingdom of Israel]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **set up their kingdom** - This may be translated as, they "established" or, "built" their kingdom. This sentence may also be translated as, “They separated themselves from the other two tribes and lived in the northern part of the land, and they called their country 'Israel.'”
