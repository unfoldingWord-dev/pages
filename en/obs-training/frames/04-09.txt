====== God's Covenant [04-09] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-04-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** made a **[[:en:key-terms:covenant|covenant]]** with **[[:en:key-terms:abram|Abram]]**. A **[[:en:key-terms:covenant|covenant]]** is an agreement between **two parties**. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** said, "I will give you a **[[:en:key-terms:son|son]] from your own body**. To your **[[:en:key-terms:descendant|descendants]]** I give the land of **[[:en:key-terms:canaan|Canaan]]**.‚Äù But **[[:en:key-terms:abram|Abram]]** still had **no [[:en:key-terms:son|son]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **two parties** -  The parties could be two people, two groups of people, or a person and a group of people. In this case the agreement was between God and Abram. 
  * **from your own body** - am amazing promise, since Abram was very, very old
  * **no son** - Abram was unable to have a son even when he was a younger man
