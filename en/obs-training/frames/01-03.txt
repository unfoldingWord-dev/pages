====== 01-03. Sky Created ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the second day of creation, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** spoke and created the sky above the earth.


===== Emphasis: =====


During the second day of creation, God displayed His power by creating the sky with His spoken word. This included the air we breathe and the clouds above. The air was the next necessary step of His unfolding plan for the world He was creating.


===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the second day** - God's work of creation was orderly, purposeful, and sequential. The things He created each day built upon and depended on the acts of the previous days.
    * **[[:en:obs-training/sandbox/chrislane/god-info|God]] spoke** - God created the sky by means of a simple verbal command. 
    * **created** - here used in the sense of making the sky out of nothing
    * **sky** - the space above the earth, including the air we breathe and the heavens
