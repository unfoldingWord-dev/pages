==== 01-03. Sky Created ====


{{:en:obs:obs-01-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the second day of **creation**, **God** spoke and created the sky above the earth.
=== Key Terms: ===


    * **[[:en:key-terms:creation|creation]]**- the six-day period during which God made everything that exists
    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**- Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
