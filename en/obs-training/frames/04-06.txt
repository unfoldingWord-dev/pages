==== Frame 04-06 ====

{{:en:obs:obs-04-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


When **Abram** arrived in **Canaan** **God** said, "Look all around you. All the land that you can see I will give to you and your **descendants** as an **inheritance**. I will **bless** you and make your name great." Then **Abram** settled in the land.


=== Key Terms: ===

 
  * **[[:en:key-terms:abram|Abram]]** - the original name of Abraham
  * **[[:en:key-terms:canaan|Canaan]]** - the area between the Jordan River and the Mediterranean Sea
  * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** - Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
  * **[[:en:key-terms:descendant|descendants]]** - people who are physically derived from someone by birth; children, grand children, etc.
  * **[[:en:key-terms:inheritance|inheritance]]** - land and wealth passed from father to son
  * **[[:en:key-terms:bless|bless]]** - God spoke to his intent to make things go well for them


=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to ...


  * "//all the land that you can see//" - if Abram was standing on a mountain in Canaan, he could have seen a very large area
  * "//make your name great//" - Abram's name and reputation would be known by all and viewed positively
