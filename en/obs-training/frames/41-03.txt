====== The Women [41-03] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-41-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The day after **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** was buried was a **[[:en:key-terms:sabbath|Sabbath]]**, **a day of rest**. Very early on the morning after the Sabbath, several women went to Jesusâ€™ **[[:en:key-terms:tomb|tomb]]** to put more **burial spices** on his body.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a day of rest** - The seventh day of the week, the Sabbath, was a day when God commanded the Jews not to work in remembrance of how God rested after creation.
  * **burial spices** - This refers to spices that were placed on a dead body to help control the decomposition of the body.
