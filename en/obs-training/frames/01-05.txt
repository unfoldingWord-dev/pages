====== 01-05. Plants Created ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** said, "Let the earth produce all kinds of trees and plants." And that is what happened. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** saw that what He had created was **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**, and He was pleased with it all.


===== Emphasis: =====


After making the dry land, God proceeded to fill it with living plants and trees. He created a wide variety to continually show His power, beauty and creativity. Everything that He created on the third day was beautiful and pleasing to Him, exactly according to His plan and purpose.


===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **[[:en:obs-training/sandbox/chrislane/god-info|God]] said** - God created all vegetation by means of a simple verbal command.
    * **let the earth produce** - This is an indirect command that happened immediately because it was spoken by God.
    * **all kinds** - many different species, or types, of each plant and tree
    * **was [[:en:obs-training/sandbox/chrislane/good-info|good]]** - This phrase repeats throughout the story to show that each stage of creation fulfilled God's plan and purpose.
    * **he was pleased with it all** - God takes pleasure in all he has made. It delights him.
