====== Plants Created [01-05] ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] said**, "**Let the earth produce** **all kinds** of trees and plants." And that is what happened. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** saw that what He had created **was [[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**, and He was pleased with it all. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** saw that what He had **created** was **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**.



===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] said** - God created all vegetation by speaking a command.
    * **let the earth produce** - This is a command that happened immediately because God spoke it.
    * **all kinds** - The many different species, or types, of plants and trees.
    * **was [[:en:key-terms:good|good]]** - This phrase is often repeated through the Creation story, and emphasizes that each stage of creation was pleasing to God and accomplished His plan and purpose.
    * **created** - Here used in the sense of making something out of nothing.
