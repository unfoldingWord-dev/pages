====== Plants Created [01-05] ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** said, "Let the earth produce all kinds of trees and plants." So the earth produced all kinds of trees and plants. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** saw that this was **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**.



===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] said** - God created all vegetation by means of a simple verbal command.
    * **let the earth produce** - This is an indirect command that happened immediately because it was spoken by God.
    * **all kinds** - The many different species, or types, of plants and trees.
    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] saw that this was [[:en:key-terms:good|good]]** - This phrase repeats throughout the story to show that each stage of creation fulfilled God's plan and purpose. This also means that **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** takes pleasure in all He has made. It delights Him.
