==== 01-05. Plants Created ====


{{:en:obs:obs-01-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then **God** said, "Let the earth produce all kinds of trees and plants." And that is what happened. **God ** saw that what He had **created ** was **good**, and He was pleased with it all.
=== Key Terms: ===


    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**- Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
    * **[[:en:key-terms:create|created]]**- "created from nothing," or "caused to exist"
    * **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**- in agreement with God's character and purposes
