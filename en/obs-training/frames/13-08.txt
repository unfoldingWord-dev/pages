====== The Tent of Meeting [13-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-13-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] gave the [[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]] a detailed description of a tent he wanted them to make**. **It was called the [[:en:key-terms:tent-of-meeting|Tent of Meeting]]**, and **it had two rooms, separated by a large curtain**. **No one could enter the room behind the curtain except the high [[:en:key-terms:priest|priest]]**, because **God lived there**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **God gave the Israelites a detailed description of a tent he wanted them to make** - This could be translated as, “God told the Israelites to make a tent, and He described it in detail,” or “God told the Israelites to make a tent, and He told them exactly how He wanted them to make it.”
  * **It was called the Tent of Meeting** - This could be translated as, “They called it the Tent of Meeting” or “Moses called it the Tent of meeting.” 
  * **it had two rooms, separated by a large curtain** - In other words, the Tent of Meeting had a curtain hanging in the middle of it, which divided it into two rooms. 
  * **No one could enter … except the High Priest** - This could be translated as, “Only the High Priest was allowed to go into the room behind the curtain. Other people were not allowed to go into it."
  * **the room behind the curtain** - The High Priest would first walk into one room and then he would go into the other the room that no one else could enter. Some languages would say, “the room in front of the curtain.”
