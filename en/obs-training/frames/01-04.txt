====== Land Created [01-04] ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the third day, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** spoke and separated the water from the dry land. He called the dry land "earth," and He called the water "seas."


===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **third day** - The next in the series of orderly days in which God prepared the earth for life. 
    * **[[:en:obs-training/sandbox/chrislane/god-info|God]] spoke** - God created the dry land by means of a simple verbal command.
    * **separated the water from the dry land** - God divided the world into distinct wet areas where fish would live, and dry areas for the animals that He planned to create.
