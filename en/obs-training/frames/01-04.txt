====== Land Created [01-04] ======


{{:en:obs:obs-01-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


On the **third day**, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] said**, "Let **the waters gather together and the dry land appear**." So the waters gathered and the dry land appeared. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** called the dry land "**earth**," and He called the water "seas." **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** saw that this was good.


===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **third day** - The next in the series of orderly days in which God prepared the earth for life. 
    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] said** - God created the dry land by speaking a command.
    * **the waters gather together and the dry land appear** - God divided the world into distinct wet areas where fish would live, and dry areas for the animals that He planned to create.
    * **earth** - Here this word refers to dirt or soil, which is what dry land is made of. The word “earth” in the frame “The Beginning” refers to the whole world that people live on.
