====== Joseph Ruled Egypt [08-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-08-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:pharaoh|Pharaoh]]** was so **impressed with [[:en:key-terms:joseph|Joseph]]** that he appointed him to be the **second most powerful man** in all of **[[:en:key-terms:egypt|Egypt]]**!


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **impressed with** - Pharaoh was amazed by Joseph's wisdom and felt respect for him; he trusted Joseph to make wise decisions that would benefit the people. It may be more clear to say, “impressed with Joseph's wisdom.”
  * **second most powerful man** - Pharaoh made Joseph a very powerful and important ruler over all of Egypt. Only Pharaoh was more powerful and important than Joseph.
