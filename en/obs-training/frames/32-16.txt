====== Healed by Faith [32-16] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-32-16.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The woman **fell on her knees** **before [[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**, **shaking and very afraid**. Then she told him what she had done and how she had been healed. Jesus said to her, “**Your [[:en:key-terms:faith|faith]] has [[:en:key-terms:heal|healed]] you**. Go in **[[:en:key-terms:peace|peace]]**.”


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fell on her knees** – That is, “quickly knelt down.” 
  * **before Jesus** – That is, “in front of Jesus.” 
  * **shaking and very afraid** - That is, “shaking with fear” or, “trembling because she was afraid.” 
  * **Your faith has healed you.** - This could also be translated as, “You have been healed because of your faith.” 
  * **Go in peace.** - This was a traditional greeting that includes a blessing. Other languages may say something similar such as, “Go well”, “Go with God” or, “Peace.” Other ways of translating it could be, “May you have peace as you go” or, “Go and know that you are at peace with God.”
