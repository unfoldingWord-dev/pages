====== The Mountain at Sinai [13-01] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-13-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

After God led the Israelites through the Red Sea, He led them through the wilderness to a mountain called Sinai. This was the same mountain where Moses saw the burning bush. The people set up their tents at the base of the mountain.
