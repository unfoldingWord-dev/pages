====== Preparing the Way [24-04] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-24-04.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:john-baptist|John]]** said to the **[[:en:key-terms:jewish-leaders|religious leaders]]**, “**You bunch of snakes**! **[[:en:key-terms:repent|Repent]]** and change your behavior. **Every tree that does not bear good fruit** will be cut down and thrown into the fire.” John fulfilled what the **[[:en:key-terms:prophet|prophet]]** **[[:en:key-terms:isaiah|Isaiah]]** said, “**Behold**, I send **my messenger** ahead of you, who will **prepare your way**.”


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You bunch of snakes** - This figure of speech implies that the religious leaders were dangerous and crafty.
  * **Every tree that does not bear good fruit...** - This metaphor means that any person who does not produce good works will be judged by God."
  * **Behold** - This could be translated as "Look and see," or "Pay attention!"
  * **my messenger** - This could be translated as "God will send his messenger…"
  * **prepare your way** - This means to prepare the people to receive the Messiah.
