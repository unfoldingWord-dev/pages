====== God Fought for Israel [12-10] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-12-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

So they followed the **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]]** onto the **path through the sea**, but **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] threw the [[:en:key-terms:egypt|Egyptians]] into a panic** and caused their **chariots** to **get stuck**. They shouted, “Run away! **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** is fighting for the **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]]**!”


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **path through the sea** - This was the dry strip of land across the bottom of the sea, with a wall of water on each side. 
  * **God threw the Egyptians into a panic** – This could be translated as “God caused the Egyptians to panic” or “God caused the Egyptians to get fearful and confused.” 
  * **chariots** - Chariots were carts with two wheels that soldiers and important officials would stand or sit in while horses pulled them.
  * **get stuck** - The chariots could no longer move.
