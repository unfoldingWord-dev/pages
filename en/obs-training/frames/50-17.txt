====== Jesus Will Reign Forever [50-17] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-50-17.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** and his people will live on the new earth, and he will reign forever over everything that exists. He will **wipe away every tear** and **there will be no more [[:en:key-terms:suffer|suffering]], sadness, crying, [[:en:key-terms:evil|evil]], pain, or [[:en:key-terms:death|death]]**. Jesus will **rule his [[:en:key-terms:kingdom-of-god|kingdom]] with [[:en:key-terms:peace|peace]] and [[:en:key-terms:justice|justice]]**, and he will be with his people forever.


//A Bible story from: Matthew 24:14; 28:18; John 15:20, 16:33; Revelation 2:10; Matthew 13:24-30, 36-42; 1 Thessalonians 4:13-5:11; James 1:12; Matthew 22:13; Revelation 20:10, 21:1-22:21//


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wipe away every tear** - This could also be translated as, “bring an end to all of our grief” or, “make trouble end” or, “tenderly take away any sadness people have.”
  * **there will be no more suffering, sadness, crying, evil, pain, or death** - This could also be translated as, “people will no longer suffer, be sad, cry, do evil things, feel pain, or die.”
  * **rule his kingdom with peace and justice** – This could also be translated as, “rule over his people fairly, in a way that brings them peace."
  * //A Bible story from// - These references may be slightly different in some Bible translations.
