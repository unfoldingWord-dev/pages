====== Joseph Married Mary [23-03] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-23-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

So **[[:en:key-terms:joseph-nt|Joseph]] married her** and took her home as his wife, but **he did not sleep with her** until she had given birth.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Joseph married her** - Although Joseph was not the physical father of Jesus, he became the his legal father so that Jesus would be a legitimate descendant of David, and eligible to be the king of Israel.
  * **he did not sleep with her** - Joseph did not have sexual relations with Mary. He kept her a virgin until the birth of the baby.
