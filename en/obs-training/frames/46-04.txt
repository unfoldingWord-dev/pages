====== God Sent Ananias to Saul [46-04] ======

Then **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** appeared to a **[[:en:key-terms:christian|Christian]]** named Ananias and said, "Go to the house where **[[:en:key-terms:paul|Saul]]** is staying. Lay your hands on him so that he can see again.” But Ananias said, “**[[:en:key-terms:lord|Master]]**, I have heard how he has **[[:en:key-terms:persecute|persecuted]]** the Christians.” God said, “I have chosen him to declare my name to the **unsaved**. I will show him how much he must **[[:en:key-terms:suffer|suffer]]** **for my name**."

Then **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** appeared to a **[[:en:key-terms:disciple|disciple]]** named Ananias and said, "Go to the house where **[[:en:key-terms:paul|Saul]]** is staying. Place your hands on him so that he can see again.” **But Ananias said**, “**[[:en:key-terms:lord|Master]]**, I have heard how he has **[[:en:key-terms:persecute|persecuted]]** the believers.” **God answered him**, “I have chosen him **to declare my name** to the Jews and to people from other people groups. He will **[[:en:key-terms:suffer|suffer]]** many things **for my name**."


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But Ananias said** – If it is not clear why the word “But” is used here, this could be translated as, “But Ananias was afraid so he said.” 
  * **God answered him** – This could be translated as, “To reassure Ananias, God said” to make clear why God said this. 
  * **to declare my name** - That is, “to teach about me” or, “to make me known”. 
  * **for my name** - That is, “for me” or, “because of me” or, “because he serves me” or, “because he teaches others about me.”
