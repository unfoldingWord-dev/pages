====== The Message of the Prophets [19-18] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-19-18.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The **[[:en:key-terms:prophet|prophets]] continued to speak for [[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** even though the people hated them. They warned people that God would destroy them if they did not **[[:en:key-terms:repent|repent]]**. They also reminded people of the **[[:en:key-terms:promise|promise]]** that God’s **[[:en:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]]** would come.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **continued to speak for God** - That is, “continued to tell the people what God wanted to tell them.” 
  * **of the promise of the Messiah would come** – This could be translated as, “that God had promised that his Messiah would come to save his people from their sins.”
