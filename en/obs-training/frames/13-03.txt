====== Moses Met God [13-03] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-13-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**Three days later**, after the people had **prepared themselves spiritually**, **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** came down on top of **[[:en:key-terms:sinai|Mount Sinai]]** with thunder, lightning, smoke, and a **loud trumpet blast**. **Only [[:en:key-terms:moses|Moses]] was allowed to go up** the mountain.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Three days later** - In other words, three days after they arrived at Mount Sinai and God first spoke to them. 
  * **prepared themselves spiritually** - This refers to ceremonial cleansing in preparation to meet with God. This could be translated as, “got ready to meet with God” or “washed themselves in preparation to meet with God.” 
  * **loud trumpet blast** - This could be translated as, “and a loud sound came from a horn,” or “and a horn was blown and it made a loud sound,” or “and they heard the loud sound of a horn blowing.” Trumpets were made out of ram's horns.  
  * **Only Moses was allowed to go up** - This could be translated as, “God permitted Moses to go up, but he did not permit anyone else to go up.”
