====== The First and the Last [28-10] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-28-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** answered, “Everyone who has left houses, brothers, sisters, father, mother, children, or property **for my name’s sake**, will **[[:en:key-terms:inherit|inherit]]** 100 times more in the **next world** and also **[[:en:key-terms:eternal-life|eternal life]]**. But **many who are first will be last**, and **those who are last will be first**.”


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **for my name’s sake** - This may also be translated as, "for the sake of my name" or, "because of me (Jesus) and my reputation."
  * **next world** - This refers to the new world that God will create when this sinful one passes away.
  * **many who are first will be last** - That is, "many who are prominent in this world will not be prominent in the next world."
  * **those who are last will be first** - That is, "people who are neglected in this world will be prominent in the next world."
