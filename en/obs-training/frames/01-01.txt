==== Frame 01-01 ====


{{:en:obs:obs-01-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


This is how the beginning of everything happened. **God** **created** the universe and everything in it in six days. At first, the earth was dark and empty, and nothing had been formed in it. But **God's Spirit** was there over the water.
=== Key Terms: ===


    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**- Almighty, creator, sinless & perfect…
    * **[[:en:key-terms:create|created]]**- "created from nothing", or "to cause something to exist"…
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h7307|God's Spirit]]**- the Holy Spirit, the second person of the Trinity
=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to the name for God, and the word used for "day"…


    * "the beginning" - refers to "the beginning of all things", or "before everything else" (LS: moved from KT)
    * "day" - refers to a literal 24-hour period, which began at sundown and ended at sundown the next day (LS: moved from KT)
    * "nothing had been formed" - there were no distinguishing features–just water covering everything (LS: moved from KT)
    * note the discourse…
    * note the grammatical features…
