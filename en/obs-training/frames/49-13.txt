====== God Loves You [49-13] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-49-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Anyone who **[[:en:key-terms:believe|believes]]** in **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** and **[[:en:key-terms:receive|receives]]** him as their **[[:en:key-terms:lord|Master]]** will be **[[:en:key-terms:save|saved]]**, but whoever does not believe will be rejected. It does not matter if you are rich or poor, man or woman, old or young, or where you are from. **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** **[[:en:key-terms:love|loves]]** you and wants you to believe in Jesus so he can have a relationship with you.
