====== The Sick Woman [32-13] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-32-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** returned to the other side of the **[[:en:key-terms:galilee-sea|Sea of Galilee]]**. A large crowd gathered around him and **pressed in on him**. In the crowd was a woman who had suffered from a bleeding problem for twelve years. She had paid all of her money to doctors so they would **[[:en:key-terms:heal|heal]]** her, but **she only got worse**.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **pressed in on him** - That is, “pushed tightly against him” or, “crowded in tightly around him.” 
  * **she only got worse** - That is, “her condition only got worse” or, ” her health got worse instead of better” or, “instead she became more sick.”
