“**At the end of the world**, all **the people who belong to the [[:en:key-terms:satan|devil]]** will be gathered together and thrown into a fiery furnace, where there is weeping and grinding of teeth. Then the **[[:en:key-terms:righteous|righteous]]** ones will **shine like the sun** in the **[[:en:key-terms:kingdom-of-god|kingdom]]** of their **[[:en:key-terms:god-father|Father]]**.”


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **At the end of the world** - That is, "when the world ends."
  * **the people who belong to the devil** - This refers to those who do not believe in Jesus.
  * **a fiery furnace** - That is, "a very hot, burning furnace" or, "a furnace containing a raging fire."
  * **shine like the sun** - This image has the meaning of, "appear as bright lights."
