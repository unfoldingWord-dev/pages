====== The Walls Fell Down [15-05] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-15-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then the walls around **[[:en:key-terms:jericho|Jericho]]** fell down! The **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israelites]]** destroyed everything in the city as **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** had commanded. **They only [[:en:key-terms:save|saved]] [[:en:key-terms:rahab|Rahab]] and her family**, who **became part of** the Israelites. When **the rest of the [[:en:key-terms:canaan|Canaanites]]** heard about **what had happened to Jericho** they were **terrified**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **They only saved Rahab and her family** - This could be translated as, “They only spared Rahab and her family” or “Rahab and her family were the only people that they did not kill.” 
  * **became part of** - This can be translated as, “who then joined the Israelite community” or “who then became members of the nation of Israel.”  
  * **the rest of the Canaanites** – This refers to the people living in other Canaanite cities. This could be translated as, “the other people living in Canaan.”
  * **what had happened to Jericho** – This could be translated as, “how the Israelites had destroyed Jericho.” 
  * **terrified** – It may be necessary to add, “terrified that the Israelites would also attack them.”
