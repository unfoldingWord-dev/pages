====== Gideon Made an Idol [16-16] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-16-16.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then **[[:en:key-terms:gideon|Gideon]] used the gold to make a special garment** like the high priest used to wear. But the people started **[[:en:key-terms:worship|worshiping]]** it as if it were an **[[:en:key-terms:idol|idol]]**. So **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**  **[[:en:key-terms:punish|punished]]** **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israel]]** again **for worshiping idols**. **Their enemies defeated them**. They finally asked God for help again, and God sent them another **[[:en:key-terms:deliverer|deliverer]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Gideon used the gold to make a special garment** – This could be translated as, “Gideon melted down the gold objects the people had given him and formed a special garment out of that gold.” 
  * **turned away from God** - This could be translated as, “disobeyed God” or, “stopped worshiping God.” 
  * **for worshiping idols** - That is, “because they worshiped idols.”
  * **Their enemies defeated them** - This could be translated as, “God allowed their enemies to defeat them” to make it clear that this was how He was punishing them.
