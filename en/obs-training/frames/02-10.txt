==== 02-10. The Woman Is Cursed ====


{{:en:obs:obs-02-10.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** then said to the woman, “I will make childbirth very painful for you. You will desire your husband, and he will rule over you.”


=== Translation Notes: ===


    * "//childbirth very painful//" - pain in childbirth is God's punishment on the woman for disobeying God and eating the fruit
    * "//desire your husband//" - the woman will want her husband to do whatever she wants him to do
    * "//rule over you//" - as a result of the woman's disobedience, her husband will be giving a position of authority over her
