====== Joseph Stored Up Food [08-09] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-08-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

During the seven years of good harvests, **[[:en:key-terms:joseph|Joseph]]** told the people to **store up large amounts of food**. Then when the seven years of **famine** came, **[[:en:key-terms:joseph|Joseph]]** sold the food to the people so they would have enough to eat.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **store up large amounts of food** - They took food from the abundant harvests to the cities and stored it there. The food then belonged to Pharaoh. 
  * **famine** - The gardens and fields would produce so little food that people and animals would not have enough to eat.
