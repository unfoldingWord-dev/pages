====== Safe from the Flood [03-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-03-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**Everything that lived on the dry land [[:en:key-terms:death|died]]**, except the people and animals who were in **the boat. The boat** floated on the water and everything inside the boat was **kept safe**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Everything that lived on the dry land** - all of the people and animals that lived on land died. Only the fish and creatures in the sea survived.
  * **the boat** - the ark with Noah's family and the animals
  * **kept safe** - the ark floated on the water and protected those inside from drowning
