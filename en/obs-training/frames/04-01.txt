==== 04-01. One People ====

{{:en:obs:obs-04-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Many years after the flood, there were again many people in the world, and they all spoke the same language. But the people did not **obey** **God**. Instead of having children and filling the earth, they gathered together and built a city.


=== Key Terms: ===

 
  * **[[:en:key-terms:obey|obey]]** - literally "to hear," meaning to do what was commanded
  * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** - Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect


=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to ...


  * "//many years after the flood//" - many generations
  * "//again many people//" - Noah's family multiplied into enough people to fill a city
  * "//same language//" - all of these descendents of Noah spoke the same language as Noah
  * "//a city//" - we are not sure of the location of this city
