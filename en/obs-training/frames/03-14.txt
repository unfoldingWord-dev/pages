==== Frame 03-14 ====

{{:en:obs:obs-03-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


After he got off the boat, **Noah** built an **altar** and **sacrificed** some of each kind of animal which could be used for a **sacrifice**. **God** was happy with the **sacrifice** and **blessed** **Noah** and his family.


=== Key Terms: ===

 
  * **[[:en:key-terms:noah|Noah]]** - righteous man who built the ark and survived the flood
  * **[[:en:key-terms:altar|altar]]** - a structure, usually stone, where animals were sacrificed
  * **[[:en:key-terms:sacrifice|sacrificed]]** - an offering to God, such as an animal
  * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** - Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
  * **[[:en:key-terms:bless|blessed]]** - God spoke to his intent to make things go well for them


=== Translation Notes: ===


Pay close attention to ...


  * "//animal which could be used for a sacrifice//" - God chose to accept some animals for sacrifice, but not others
  * "//God was happy//" - God approved of and accepted Noah's offering
