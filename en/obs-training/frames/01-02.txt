==== 01-02. Light Created ====


{{:en:obs:obs-01-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


Then **God** spoke and said, "Let there be light!" And there was light. **God ** saw that the light was **good ** and called it "day." He separated it from the darkness, which He called "night." **God ** created (CL: 'created' KT in 01-01, should it be bolded here with a link below as well?) the light on the first day of **creation**.
=== Key Terms: ===


    * **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]**- Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
    * **[[:en:key-terms:good|good]]**- in agreement with God's character and purposes
    * **[[:en:key-terms:creation|creation]]**- the six-day period during which God made everything that exists
