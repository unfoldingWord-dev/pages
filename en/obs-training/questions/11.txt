====== Story #11: The Passover ======

===== Comprehensive Questions and Answers =====

**1.	What was the final plague that God would send on the Egyptians?**

//He would kill all the firstborn males of both people and animals.//

**2.	How could people avoid this plague of they believed God?**

//They had to kill a perfect lamb and put its blood around the door of their house.//

**3.	What did God tell the Israelites to eat?**

//The roasted meat from the lamb and bread without yeast.//

**4.	What did God tell them to be ready to do when they ate?**

//Be ready to leave Egypt.//

**5.	What did God do at every house that had the blood around its doors?**

//He passed over that house and everyone was safe.//

**6.	What did God do at every Egyptian house?**

//He killed the firstborn son.//

**7.	How many of the firstborn sons of the Egyptians were killed?**

//All of them died, including Pharaoh's son.//

**8.	What did Pharaoh tell Moses and Aaron after this plague?**

//“Take the Israelites and leave Egypt immediately!”//

**9.	How did the Egyptian people react the Israelites after this plague?**

//They also urged the Israelites to leave.//
