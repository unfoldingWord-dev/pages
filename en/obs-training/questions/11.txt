====== Story #11: The Passover ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

  - **What was the final plague that God would send on the Egyptians?**
    *//He would kill all the firstborn males of both people and animals.//
  - **How could people avoid this plague of they believed God?**
    *//They had to kill a perfect lamb and put its blood around the door of their house.//
  - **What did God tell the Israelites to eat?**
    *//The roasted meat from the lamb and bread without yeast.//
  - **What did God tell them to be ready to do when they ate?**
    *//Be ready to leave Egypt.//
  - **What did God do at every house that had the blood around its doors?**
    *//He passed over that house and everyone was safe.//
  - **What did God do at every Egyptian house?**
    *//He killed the firstborn son.//
  - **How many of the firstborn sons of the Egyptians were killed?**
    *//All of them died, including Pharaoh's son.//
  - **What did Pharaoh tell Moses and Aaron after this plague?**
    *//“Take the Israelites and leave Egypt immediately!”//
  - **How did the Egyptian people react the Israelites after this plague?**
    *//They also urged the Israelites to leave.//
