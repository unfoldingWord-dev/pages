====== Story #29: The Story of the Unmerciful Servant ======


===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What question did Peter ask Jesus?**
    *//"How many times should I forgive my brother when he sins against me?"//
  - **How many times did Peter think he should forgive his brother?**
    *//Seven times.//
  - **How many times did Jesus say he should forgive his brother?**
    *//Seventy times seven.//
  - **What did Jesus mean when he said, "Seventy times seven?**
    *//He meant that we should always forgive.//
  - **	Was the servant able to pay his huge debt to the king?**
    *//No.//
  - **How did the king punish the servant with the huge debt?**
    *//The king ordered that the man and his family to be sold as slaves.//
  - **What did the servant with the huge debt do when he heard the punishment?**
    *//He fell on his knees and asked the king to be patient with him until he paid.//
  - **	Then what did the king do?**
    *//He felt pity on him and cancelled the servant’s debt.//
  - **What did the servant go out and do to another servant who owed him a debt?**
    *//He grabbed him and demanded payment from him.//
  - **Then what did this other servant do?**
    *//He fell on his knees and asked the servant to be patient with him until he paid.//
  - **	Then what did the wicked servant do?**
    *//He threw him in prison.//
  - **	Seeing this, what did some other servants do?**
    *//They told the king everything.//
  - **	What did the king tell the wicked servant he should have done for the other servant who owed him money?**
    *//He should have forgiven the other servant’s debt like the king had forgiven his debt.//
  - **Then what did the king do to the wicked servant?**
    *//He threw him in prison.//
  - **Will God forgive us if we do not forgive others from our heart?**
    *//No.//
