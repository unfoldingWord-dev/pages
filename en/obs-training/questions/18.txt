====== Story #18: The Divided Kingdom ======

===== Comprehensive Questions and Answers =====

**1.	When God asked Solomon what he wanted most, for what did Solomon ask?**

//Wisdom.//

**2.	What did the people and God do at the Temple that Solomon built?**

//The people worshiped God and offered sacrifices, and God lived there with his people.//

**3.	What great sins did Solomon commit?**

//He married many foreign women and worshiped their gods in his old age.//

**4.	What was God’s punishment for Solomon’s sins?**

//God promised to divide the nation of Israel into two after Solomon’s death.//

**5.	What was Rehoboam’s foolish answer to the people?**

//I will make you work harder and punish you more than my father Solomon did.//

**6.	After the division, what were the names and locations of the two kingdoms?**

//The kingdom of Judah was in the south, and the kingdom of Israel was in the north.//

**7.	How many tribes joined the kingdom of Israel, and how many joined the kingdom of Judah?**

//Ten tribes joined the kingdom of Israel, and two joined the kingdom of Judah.//

**8.	How did Jeroboam keep his people from worshiping at the Temple in Judah?**

//He built two idols for the people to worship.//

**9.	What sins did the people of Israel commit in their idol worship?**

//Sexual immorality and child sacrifice.//

**10.	How many kings of Israel were faithful to God?**

//None.//

**11.	From which line of descendants did the kings of Judah come?**

//From the line of David.//

**12.	Were there any kings of Judah who were faithful to God?**

//Yes, some were faithful, but most were evil.//
