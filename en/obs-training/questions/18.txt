====== Story #18: The Divided Kingdom ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

**1.	What did Solomon ask for God to give him?**

//Wisdom.//

**2.	What did the people do at the Temple that Solomon built?**

//The people worshiped God and offered sacrifices.//

**3.	What did God do at the Temple?**

//God lived there with his people.//

**4.	What great sins did Solomon commit?**

//He married many foreign women and worshiped their gods in his old age.//

**5.	How did God punish Solomon’s sins?**

//God promised to divide the nation of Israel into two after Solomon’s death.//

**6.	What was the foolish answer that Rehoboam gave to the people?**

//I will make you work harder and punish you more than my father Solomon did.//

**7.	What were the two kingdoms into which the nation of Israel divided?**

//The kingdom of Judah was in the south, and the kingdom of Israel was in the north.//

**8.	How many tribes of Israel joined each of the kingdoms?**

//Ten tribes joined the kingdom of Israel, and two tribes joined the kingdom of Judah.//

**9.	How did Jeroboam keep his people from going to Judah to worship at the Temple?**

//He built two idols for the people to worship.//

**10.	What sins did the people of Israel commit as they worshiped idols?**

//Sexual immorality and child sacrifice.//

**11.	How many of the kings of Israel were faithful to God?**

//None.//

**12.	Who was the ancestor of the kings of Judah?**

//King David.//

**13.	Were any kings of Judah faithful to God?**

//Yes, some were faithful, but most were evil.//
