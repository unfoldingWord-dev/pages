====== Story #40: Jesus Is Crucified ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What was the name of the place where Jesus was crucified?**
    *//The Skull.//
  - **What did Jesus pray to the Father concerning the people who were crucifying him?**
    *//“Father, forgive them, because they do not know what they are doing.”//
  - **What did Pilate command be written above Jesus’ head?**
    *//King of the Jews.//
  - **How did the soldiers at the crucifixion then fulfill prophecy?**
    *//They gambled for Jesus’ clothing as had been prophesied.//
  - **What was the request to Jesus of the robber who feared God?**
    *//“Please remember me in your kingdom.”//
  - **What was Jesus’ response to this robber?**
    *//“Today, you will be with me in Paradise.”//
  - **What did the crowd who was mocking Jesus want him to do to prove he was the Son of God?**
    *//Come down from the cross and save himself.//
  - **What then happened in the sky, even though it was the middle of the day?**
    *//It became completely dark from noon until 3:00 in the afternoon.//
  - **What were the last words Jesus cried out from the cross?**
    *//“It is finished! Father, I give my spirit into your hands.”//
  - **What happened after Jesus cried out his last words?**
    *//There was an earthquake and the large curtain in the Temple was torn in two.//
  - **What did Jesus make possible for people through his death?**
    *//Jesus opened a way for people to come to God.//
  - **Who asked Pilate for Jesus’ body?**
    *//Joseph and Nicodemus.//
  - **What did they do with the body?**
    *//They wrapped the body in cloth and placed it in a tomb cut out of rock, and then rolled a large stone in front of the tomb.//
