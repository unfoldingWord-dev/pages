====== Story #40: Jesus Is Crucified ======

Catechism Questions and Answers

1.	What was the name of the place where Jesus was crucified?

The Skull.

2.	What did Jesus pray to the Father concerning the people who were crucifying him?

“Father, forgive them, because they do not know what they are doing.”

3.	What did Pilate command be written above Jesus’ head?

King of the Jews.

4.	How did the soldiers at the crucifixion then fulfill prophecy?

They gambled for Jesus’ clothing as had been prophesied.

5.	What was the request to Jesus of the robber who feared God?

“Please remember me in your kingdom.”

6.	What was Jesus’ response to this robber?

“Today, you will be with me in Paradise.”

7.	What did the crowd who was mocking Jesus want him to do to prove he was the Son of God?

Come down from the cross and save himself.

8.	What then happened in the sky, even though it was the middle of the day?

It became completely dark from noon until 3:00 in the afternoon.

9.	What were the last words Jesus cried out from the cross?

“It is finished! Father, I give my spirit into your hands.”

10.	What happened after Jesus cried out his last words?

There was an earthquake and the large curtain in the Temple was torn in two.

11.	What did Jesus make possible for people through his death?

Jesus opened a way for people to come to God.

12.	Who asked Pilate for Jesus’ body?

Joseph and Nicodemus.

13.	What did they do with the body?

They wrapped the body in cloth and placed it in a tomb cut out of rock, and then rolled a large stone in front of the tomb.
