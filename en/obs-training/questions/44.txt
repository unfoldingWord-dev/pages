====== Story #44: Peter and John Heal a Beggar ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What did Peter not have to give the crippled man?**
    *//Money.//
  - **What did Peter have to give to the crippled man?**
    *//The man’s healing in the name of Jesus.//
  - **What did Peter say had healed the crippled man?**
    *//The power of Jesus and the faith that Jesus gives.//
  - **What title did Peter give Jesus when he said the people had killed Jesus, but God had raised him from the dead?**
    *//The author of life.//
  - **What prophecies were fulfilled unknowingly by the people?**
    *//The prophecies that the Messiah would suffer and die.//
  - **What did Peter call on the people to do, and what would God then do for them?**
    *//Repent and turn to God, so that their sins would be washed away.//
  - **How many men believed in Jesus through Peter’s preaching?**
    *//5000.//
  - **What did Peter say was the only way to be saved?**
    *//Through the power of Jesus.//
  - **Why were the Jewish leaders surprised at the preaching of Peter and John?**
    *//Peter and John were uneducated.//
  - **What did the Jewish leaders finally do with Peter and John?**
    *//They threatened them, and let them go.//
