====== Story #38: Jesus Is Betrayed ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What festival did Jesus want to celebrate with the disciples in Jerusalem, and what is its meaning?**
    *//The Passover, which celebrates how God saved the Israelites from slavery in Egypt.//
  - **What did Judas do in Jerusalem when they arrived, and why?**
    *//Judas offered to betray Jesus because he loved money.//
  - **What was the amount Judas was paid, and why is it significant?**
    *//He was paid 30 pieces of silver, just as the prophets had predicted.//
  - **At the Passover meal, what did Jesus say when he took the bread and broke it?**
    *//“Take and eat this. This is my body, which is given for you. Do this to remember me.”//
  - **What did Jesus say when he picked up the cup?**
    *//“Drink this. It is my blood of the New Covenant that is poured out for the forgiveness of sins. Do this to remember me every time you drink it.”//
  - **When Judas took the bread from Jesus, what happened to him?**
    *//Satan entered him.//
  - **What did Jesus predict would happen to the disciples that night?**
    *//They would all abandon Jesus and scatter.//
  - **What did Jesus say Peter would also do?**
    *//Peter would deny that he knew Jesus three times before the rooster crowed.//
  - **How did the disciples respond to Jesus’ predictions about them?**
    *//They said that they would die before denying Jesus.//
  - **What did Jesus pray to the Father at Gethsemane?**
    *//If it is possible, let me not have to drink this cup of suffering, but if there is no other way for people’s sins to be forgiven, then let your will be done.//
  - **What were the disciples doing while Jesus prayed?**
    *//They were sleeping.//
  - **With what sign did Judas betray Jesus?**
    *//With a kiss.//
  - **What did Jesus say he could do if he wanted to be defended?**
    *//He could ask the Father for an army of angels.//
  - **What did the disciples do after Jesus was arrested?**
    *//They ran away.//
