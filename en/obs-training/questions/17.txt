====== Story #17: God’s Covenant with David ======

===== Comprehensive Questions and Answers =====

**1.	Was Saul, the first king of Israel, a good or wicked king?**

//He was good for the first few years, but then he became wicked.//

**2.	Where was David from, and what was his work there?**

//David came from Bethlehem, and he was a shepherd of sheep.//

**3.	Why was it a miracle that David was able to kill Goliath?**

//Goliath was a trained soldier, very strong, and almost three meters tall!//

**4.	Why did the people praise David?**

//He won many victories over Israel’s enemies.//

**5.	How did David show that he would not kill Saul in order to become king?**

//When David had a chance to kill Saul, he cut off a piece of Saul’s clothes instead.//

**6.	What city did David capture and then make the capital city of Israel?**

//Jerusalem.//

**7.	What did David want to do to replace the Tent of Meeting Moses had built?**

//David wanted to build a temple where all the Israelites could worship God and offer sacrifices.//

**8.	Why did God say David would not build the temple, and whom did God say would build it?**

//David would not build the temple because he was a man of war, but David’s son would build it.//

**9.	What great promise did God give David at this time?**

//God promised that someone from David’s family would always rule as king of Israel, and that the Messiah would be one of his descendants.//

**10.	What would the Messiah do?**

//He would save people of the world from sin.//

**11.	What great sin did David commit at the end of his life?**

//He slept with Uriah’s wife Bathsheba, and murdered Uriah.//

**12.	When Nathan told David how evil his sin was, what did David do?**

//David repented of his sin and God forgave him.//

**13.	What was the punishment for David’s sin?**

//David’s baby boy died, there was fighting in David’s family for the rest of his life, and David’s power was greatly weakened.//

**14.	Although David had been unfaithful, did God still keep his promises to David?**

//Yes.//

**15.	What was the name of David and Bathsheba’s son that was born later?**

//Solomon.//
