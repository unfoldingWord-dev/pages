====== Story #23: The Birth of Jesus ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

  - **What kind of man was Joseph?**
    *//He was a righteous man.//
  - **What was he going to do with Mary since she was pregnant?**
    *//He planned to quietly divorce her.//
  - **What caused Joseph to change his mind and marry Mary instead?**
    *//An angel came and spoke to him in a dream and said, “Do not be afraid to take Mary as your wife.”//
  - **What is the meaning of the name “Jesus”?**
    *//It means, “Yahweh saves”.//
  - **Why did Joseph and Mary make the long journey to Bethlehem?**
    *//Because the Roman government required for a census that everyone return to the town where their ancestors had lived.//
  - **In what kind of place was Jesus born?**
    *//He was born in a place where animals stayed.//
  - **What was the angel’s message to the shepherds in the field?**
    *//“Do not be afraid, because I have some good news for you. The Messiah, the Master, has been born in Bethlehem!”//
  - **What did the angels who filled the skies sing?**
    *//“Glory to God in heaven and peace on earth to the people he favors.”//
  - **Later, how did the wise men from the east know a new king of the Jews had been born?**
    *//They saw an unusual star in the sky.//
  - **What did the wise men do when they saw Jesus?**
    *//They bowed down and worshiped him, and gave Jesus expensive gifts.//
