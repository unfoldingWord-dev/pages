====== Story #26: Jesus Starts His Ministry ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

1.	Where did Jesus go after overcoming Satan’s temptations in the wilderness?

Jesus went to Galilee where he lived.

2.	What message did Jesus read to the people from the scroll of Isaiah?

“God has given me his Spirit so that I can proclaim good news to the poor, freedom to captives, recovery of sight for the blind, and release to the oppressed. This is the year of the Lord’s favor.”

3.	The people knew that this passage of Scripture referred to what?

The Messiah.

4.	When did Jesus say this Scripture was fulfilled?

It was being fulfilled right now.

5.	Did the people of his hometown accept Jesus’ words?

No.

6.	What examples did Jesus give of God blessing people from other nations?

God helping a widow from another nation in Elijah’s time during the drought, and God healing Naaman, a commander of Israel’s enemies.

7.	How did the people respond when they heard these examples?

They were furious and tried to kill Jesus.

8.	How did Jesus escape the crowd?

He walked through the crowd and left the town.

9.	How did Jesus then continue to fulfill Isaiah’s prophecy?

He went through Galilee healing many who were sick or handicapped.

10.	How did Jesus demonstrate his power over Satan?

He commanded demons to come out of the people.

11.	What did Jesus’ twelve apostles do?

They traveled with Jesus and learned from him.
