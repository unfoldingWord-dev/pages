====== Story #26: Jesus Starts His Ministry ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

  - **Where did Jesus go after he overcame Satan’s temptations?**
    *//Jesus went to the region of Galilee where he lived.//
  - **What message did Jesus read to the people from the scroll of Isaiah?**
    *//“God has given me his Spirit so that I can proclaim good news to the poor, freedom to captives, recovery of sight for the blind, and release to the oppressed. This is the year of the Lord’s favor.”//
  - **The people knew that this passage of Scripture referred to what?**
    *//The Messiah.//
  - **When did Jesus say this Scripture was fulfilled?**
    *//It was being fulfilled right now.//
  - **Did the people of his hometown accept Jesus’ words?**
    *//No.//
  - **What examples did Jesus give of God blessing people from other nations?**
    *//God helping a widow from another nation in Elijah’s time during the drought, and God healing Naaman, a commander of Israel’s enemies.//
  - **How did the people respond when they heard these examples?**
    *//They were furious and tried to kill Jesus.//
  - **How did Jesus escape the crowd?**
    *//He walked through the crowd and left the town.//
  - **How did Jesus then continue to fulfill Isaiah’s prophecy?**
    *//He went through Galilee healing many who were sick or handicapped.//
  - **How did Jesus demonstrate his power over Satan?**
    *//He commanded demons to come out of the people.//
  - **What did Jesus’ twelve apostles do?**
    *//They traveled with Jesus and learned from him.//
