======Story #4: God’s Covenant with Abraham======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

**1.	After the flood, did the people fill the earth as God had commanded?**

//No. They gathered together and built a city.//

**2.	How many different languages were there in the world at this time?**

//One.//

**3.	What evil thing did the people work together to do?**

//Build a tall tower to reach heaven.//

**4.	How did God spread the people out all over the world?**

//He changed their language into many different languages.//

**5.	What was the name of the city that they were building?**

//Babel.//

**6.	What does the name "Babel" mean?**

//Confused.//

**7.	What did God tell Abram to do?**

//God told Abram to leave his country and family and go to another land.//

**8.	What did God promise to do for Abram?**

//He promised to give Abram all the land he could see, to make his name great, to make his descendants a great nation, and to bless all the people groups of the world through him.//

**9.	Which land did Abram go to?**

//The land of Canaan.//

**10.	What did Melchizedek, priest of God Most High, do for Abram?**

//He blessed Abram.//

**11.	What did Abram then give Melchizedek?**

//A tenth of all he owned.//

**12.	Why did God say Abram was righteous?**

//Because Abram believed God’s promise.//

**13.	What is a covenant?**

//An agreement between two parties.//

**14.	What problem made the promise to Abram and his wife seem impossible?**

//They did not have a son.//
