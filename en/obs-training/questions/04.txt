======Story #4: God’s Covenant with Abraham======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

  - **After the flood, did the people fill the earth as God had commanded?**
    *//No. They gathered together and built a city.//
  - **How many different languages were there in the world at this time?**
    *//One.//
  - **What evil thing did the people work together to do?**
    *//Build a tall tower to reach heaven.//
  - **How did God spread the people out all over the world?**
    *//He changed their language into many different languages.//
  - **What was the name of the city that they were building?**
    *//Babel.//
  - **What does the name "Babel" mean?**
    *//Confused.//
  - **What did God tell Abram to do?**
    *//God told Abram to leave his country and family and go to another land.//
  - **What did God promise to do for Abram?**
    *//He promised to give Abram all the land he could see, to make his name great, to make his descendants a great nation, and to bless all the people groups of the world through him.//
  - **Which land did Abram go to?**
    *//The land of Canaan.//
  - **What did Melchizedek, priest of God Most High, do for Abram?**
    *//He blessed Abram.//
  - **What did Abram then give Melchizedek?**
    *//A tenth of all he owned.//
  - **Why did God say Abram was righteous?**
    *//Because Abram believed God’s promise.//
  - **What is a covenant?**
    *//An agreement between two parties.//
  - **What problem made the promise to Abram and his wife seem impossible?**
    *//They did not have a son.//
