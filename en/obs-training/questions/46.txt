====== Story #46: Saul Believes in Jesus ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **Why was Saul going to Damascus?**
    *//To arrest Christians and to bring them back to Jerusalem.//
  - **On Saul’s way to Damascus, what question did Jesus ask him?**
    *//“Why do you persecute me?”//
  - **When Saul got up after seeing the bright light, what had happened to him?**
    *//He could not see.//
  - **When speaking to Ananias, for what purpose did God say that he had chosen Saul?**
    *//To declare God’s name to the Jews and to people from other people groups.//
  - **What did God say would happen to Saul as he completed God’s purpose?**
    *//He would suffer many things.//
  - **What did Ananias do to Saul?**
    *//He placed his hands on Saul and then baptized him.//
  - **What message did Saul immediately begin preaching to the Jews?**
    *//“Jesus is the Son of God!”//
  - **What did the Jews finally decide to do with Saul?**
    *//They made a plan to kill Saul.//
  - **How did Saul escape from Damascus?**
    *//His friends lowered him over the city wall in a basket.//
  - **In what way was Saul accepted by the disciples in Jerusalem?**
    *//Barnabus took Saul to the apostles and told them how Saul had preached boldly in Damascus.//
  - **In Antioch, what kind of people became believers for the first time?**
    *//People who were not Jews.//
  - **What name did the believers first receive in Antioch?**
    *//Christians.//
  - **What did the church in Antioch send Paul and Barnabus out to do?**
    *//To preach the good news about Jesus in many other places.//
