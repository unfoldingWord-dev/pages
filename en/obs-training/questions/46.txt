====== Story #46: Saul Believes in Jesus ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


1.	Why was Saul going to Damascus?

To arrest Christians and to bring them back to Jerusalem.

2.	On Saul’s way to Damascus, what question did Jesus ask him?

“Why do you persecute me?”

3.	When Saul got up after seeing the bright light, what had happened to him?

He could not see.

4.	When speaking to Ananias, for what purpose did God say that he had chosen Saul?

To declare God’s name to the Jews and to people from other people groups.

5.	What did God say would happen to Saul as he completed God’s purpose?

He would suffer many things.

6.	What did Ananias do to Saul?

He placed his hands on Saul and then baptized him.

7.	What message did Saul immediately begin preaching to the Jews?

“Jesus is the Son of God!”

8.	What did the Jews finally decide to do with Saul?

They made a plan to kill Saul.

9.	How did Saul escape from Damascus?

His friends lowered him over the city wall in a basket.

10.	In what way was Saul accepted by the disciples in Jerusalem?

Barnabus took Saul to the apostles and told them how Saul had preached boldly in Damascus.

11.	In Antioch, what kind of people became believers for the first time?

People who were not Jews.

12.	What name did the believers first receive in Antioch?

Christians.

13.	What did the church in Antioch send Paul and Barnabus out to do?

To preach the good news about Jesus in many other places.
