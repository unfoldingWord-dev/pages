====== Story #13: God’s Covenant with Israel ======

===== Comprehensive Questions and Answers =====

**1.	What did God promise the Israelites if they would keep his covenant?**

//They would be his prized possession, a kingdom of priests, and a holy nation.//

**2.	How did God come down on Mount Sinai?**

//With thunder, lightning, smoke, and a loud trumpet blast.//

**3.	What were the Ten Commandments God gave them to obey?**

//Do not worship other gods, do not make idols, do not use my name in a disrespectful way, keep the Sabbath day holy, honor your father and your mother, do not murder, do not commit adultery, do not steal, do not lie, do not desire anything that belongs to your neighbor.//

**4.	What did God say he would do if they disobeyed the commandments?**

//He would punish them.//

**5.	What did God tell them to make, and why did God want it made?**

//He told them to make the Tent of Meeting so he could live there.//

**6.	Who could enter the room behind the curtain where God lived?**

//No one except the high priest.//

**7.	In this covenant, how could someone be cleansed from sin?**

//The blood of an animal sacrifice made the person clean in God’s sight.//

**8.	Who did God choose to be his priests at the Tent of Meeting?**

//Aaron and his descendants.//

**9.	What did the people do while waiting for Moses to return from Mount Sinai?**

//They asked Aaron to make a gold idol, and then they began to wildly worship it and make sacrifices to it.//

**10.	Why did God not destroy the Israelites for their disobedience?**

//Moses prayed for them.//

**11.	What did Moses do when he saw the idol?**

//He smashed the stones with the Ten Commandments, beat the idol into powder, and made the people drink the powder with water.//

**12.	How were the stones with the Ten Commandments replaced?**

//Moses wrote the Ten Commandments on new stone tablets.//

**13.	Where did the Israelites go after this?**

//God led the Israelites toward the Promised Land.//
