====== Story #36: The Transfiguration ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **Who did Jesus take up on the mountain to pray?**
    *//His disciples Peter, James, and John.//
  - **What happened to Jesus as he was praying?**
    *//His face became as bright as the sun and his clothes became as white as light.//
  - **Who then appeared with Jesus?**
    *//Moses and Elijah.//
  - **What did they discuss?**
    *//Jesus’ death.//
  - **Where was Jesus going to die?**
    *//Jerusalem.//
  - **Seeing this, what did Peter want to do?**
    *//He wanted the disciples to make three shelters, one for Jesus, one for Moses, and one for Elijah.//
  - **Why did Peter say this?**
    *//He did not know what he was saying.//
  - **How did God then speak to the disciples?**
    *//With a voice from a bright cloud.//
  - **What did God say?**
    *//“This is my Son whom I love. I am pleased with him. Listen to him.”//
  - **What instructions did Jesus give the disciples as they went back down the mountain?**
    *//Do not tell anyone about what happened on the mountain until after I die and come back to life.//
