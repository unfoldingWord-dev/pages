====== Story #30: Jesus Feeds Five Thousand People ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What did Jesus send his apostles to do in the villages?**
    *//Preach and teach.//
  - **What was Jesus’ attitude toward the crowd that was waiting for them?**
    *//He had compassion on them.//
  - **To Jesus, what did these people seem like?**
    *//Sheep without a shepherd.//
  - **What did Jesus do for the people during that day?**
    *//He taught them and healed the sick.//
  - **When the disciples said the people needed something to eat, what did Jesus say to them?**
    *//“You give them something to eat!”//
  - **Did the disciples know how to feed the people?**
    *//No.//
  - **How much food did the disciples have?**
    *//Five loaves of bread and two small fish.//
  - **What did Jesus do with the five loaves of bread and the two fish?**
    *//He looked up to heaven and thanked God for the food.//
  - **	How many people ate and were satisfied?**
    *//Five thousand men, not counting the women and children.//
  - **How much food was left over after everyone ate?**
    *//Twelve baskets full.//
