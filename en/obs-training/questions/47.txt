===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What was the Roman name that Saul began to use?**

  - **Where did Paul go in Philippi to proclaim the good news about Jesus?**

  - **What made it possible for Lydia to believe in Jesus?**
    *//God opened her heart to understand the message. [47-02]//
  - **What did Paul and Silas do to Lydia after she believed?**
    *//The baptized Lydia and her family. [47-02]//

  - **How did Paul ?**

    *//Paul cast out the slave girl’s demon, and she could no longer tell people the future. [47-0]//

    *//They sang songs of praise to God. [47-0]//

    *//There was a violent earthquake, the prison doors flew open, and the chains of the prisoners fell off. [47-0]//

  - **Why was the jailer terrified?**
    *//He thought all the prisoners had escaped. [47-1]//

  - **What question did the jailer ask Paul?**
    *//“What must I do to be saved?” [47-1]//

  - **What was Paul’s answer to the jailer’s question?**
    *//“Believe in Jesus, the Master, and you and your family will be saved.” [47-1]//

  - **How did Paul and Silas get out of prison?**
    *//The next day the leaders of the city released them and asked them to leave the city. [47-1]//

  - **What happened to some of the letters Paul and other Christian leaders wrote as they traveled and preached the good news about Jesus?**
