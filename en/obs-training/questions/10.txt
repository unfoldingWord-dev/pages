====== Story #10: The Ten Plagues ======

===== Comprehensive Questions and Answers =====

**1.	What was God's message that Moses and Aaron gave to Pharaoh?**

//“Let my people go!”//

**2.	What did Pharaoh do when he heard this command?**

//He forced the Israelites to work even harder.//

**3.	What did God do when Pharaoh refused to let the Israelites go?**

//He sent ten terrible plagues.//

**4.	What did God show Pharaoh through these plagues?**

//That he is more powerful than Pharaoh and all of Egypt’s gods.//

**5.	What were the first nine plagues?**

//Turning the Nile River into blood, frogs, gnats, flies, the death of farm animals, skin sores, hail, locusts, and darkness.//

**6.	Who did the plague of the painful skin sores affect?**

//The sores appeared on the Egyptians, but not on the Israelites.//

**7.	Did the plague of darkness affect everyone equally?**

//No. It was dark where the Egyptians lived, but there was light where the Israelites lived.//

**8.	How did Pharaoh respond to each of the first nine plagues?**

//His heart was hardened, and he did not let the people go free.//

**9.	What did God do after Pharaoh did not listen to the first nine plagues?**

//God planned to send one last plague.//

**10.	What would this last plague do that the first nine had not done?**

//It would change Pharaoh’s mind.//
