====== Story #28: The Rich Young Ruler ======


===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What did the rich young ruler ask Jesus?**
    *//What must I do to have eternal life?//
  - **Which commandments of God did Jesus tell the young man to obey?**
    *//Do not murder. Do not commit adultery. Do not steal. Do not lie. Honor your father and mother. Love your neighbor as yourself.//
  - **Q**
    *//A//
  - **When the young ruler said he had kept the commandments his whole life, what additional commandment did Jesus give him to obey?**
    *//Sell everything you own and give the money to the poor, then follow me.//
  - **What reward did Jesus promise the rich young ruler if he would do this?**
    *//Treasure in heaven.//
  - **Why did the young ruler become sad and then go away from Jesus?**
    *//He did not want to give away all the things he possessed.//
  - **How difficult did Jesus say it is for a rich man to enter the Kingdom of God?**
    *//It is more difficult than a camel going through the eye of a needle.//
  - **Is it possible for a rich man to be saved?**
    *//Yes, with God everything is possible.//
  - **What did Peter say the disciples had left behind to follow Jesus?**
    *//Everything.//
  - **What did Jesus say would be their reward?**
    *//One hundred times more in the next world and also eternal life.//
