====== Story #35: The Story of the Compassionate Father ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


1.	Why were the religious leaders criticizing Jesus?

Because Jesus treated tax collectors and sinners as friends.

2.	In Jesus’ story, what did the younger son demand from his father?

He wanted his inheritance now.

3.	What did the younger son do with his inheritance?

He wasted it on sinful living.

4.	After this, what happened in the land in which he was living?

There was a severe famine.

5.	What did the younger son then do?

He fed pigs in order to eat.

6.	Finally, what did the younger son say to himself?

My father’s servants have plenty to eat, so I will go back and ask to be one of his servants.

7.	What did the father do when he saw the younger son still far away?

He ran to him, hugged him, and kissed him.

8.	What was the younger son’s confession to his father?

“Father, I have sinned against God and against you. I am not worthy to be your son.”

9.	What did the father do after the younger son’s confession?

He put clothes, a ring, and sandals on the younger son and called for a feast and celebration.

10.	How did the older son respond when he found out?

He was very angry and refused to go into the house.

11.	What was the older son’s complaint to his father?

I worked faithfully for you and you did not give me a small goat to celebrate, but you killed the best calf for this son of yours who consumed your money.

12.	What did the father say belonged to the older son?

Everything the father had.

13.	Why did the father say it was right to celebrate the return of the younger son?

He was dead, but now is alive. He was lost, but now is found.
