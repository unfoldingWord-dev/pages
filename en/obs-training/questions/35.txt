====== Story #35: The Story of the Compassionate Father ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **Why did the religious leaders criticize Jesus?**
    *//Because Jesus treated tax collectors and sinners as his friends.//
  - **What did the younger son demand from his father?**
    *//He wanted his inheritance right away.//
  - **What did the younger son do with his inheritance?**
    *//He wasted it on sinful living.//
  - **What happened in the land in where the younger son went to live?**
    *//There was a severe famine.//
  - **What did the younger son do to survive during the famine?**
    *//He took a job feeding pigs.//
  - **Why did the younger son decide to return home?**
    *//He realized that father’s servants lived better than he did and had plenty to eat.//
  - **What did the younger son plan to ask his father to do?**
    *//He wanted his father to let him be one of his servants.//
  - **What did the father do when he saw the younger son approaching?**
    *//He ran to him, hugged him, and kissed him.//
  - **What did the younger son say to his father when they met?**
    *//“Father, I have sinned against God and against you. I am not worthy to be your son.”//
  - **How did the father respond to the younger son’s confession?**
    *//He put clothes, a ring, and sandals on the younger son and had a feast to celebration his return.//
  - **How did the older son react when he learned that his brother had come home?**
    *//He was very angry and refused to go into the house.//
  - **What was the older son’s complaint to his father?**
    *//I worked faithfully for you and you did not give me a small goat to celebrate, but you killed the best calf for this son of yours who consumed your money.//
  - **How much of the father's possessions now belonged to the older son?**
    *//Everything the father had.//
  - **Why was it reasonable for the father to celebrate the return of his younger son?**
    *//He had been dead, but now was alive. He had been lost, but now was found.//
