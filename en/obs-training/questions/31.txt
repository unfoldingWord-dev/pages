====== Story #31: Jesus Walks on Water ======


===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

  - **What did Jesus tell the disciples to do after he dismissed the crowd?**
    *//Get into a boat and sail to the other side of the lake.//
  - **What did Jesus do after he sent the disciples away in their boat?**
    *//He went up on a mountainside to pray.//
  - **	What happened during the night to the disciples in the boat?**
    *//The wind was against them, so they were only able to travel halfway across the lake.//
  - **	How did Jesus get to the boat?**
    *//He walked on top of the water.//
  - **	How did the disciples react when they first saw Jesus?**
    *//They were very afraid because they thought he was a ghost.//
  - **	What did Jesus say to them?**
    *//Do not be afraid. It is I.//
  - **	Then what did Jesus tell Peter to do?**
    *//He told Peter to come to him on the water.//
  - **Why did Jesus tell Peter to come to him on the water?**
    *//Because Peter told Jesus to command him to do that.//
  - **What happened when Peter turned his eyes away from Jesus and became afraid?**
    *//He became afraid and began to sink.//
  - **After grabbing Peter, what did Jesus say to Peter?**
    *//You man of little faith, why did you doubt?//
  - **	What happened immediately after Jesus got into the boat?**
    *//The wind stopped.//
  - **How did the disciples react to this miracle?**
    *//They worshiped Jesus and said, “Truly, you are the Son of God.”//
