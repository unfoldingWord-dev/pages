====== Story #19: The Prophets ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

**1.	Where did the prophets get the messages that they spoke to the people?**

//They heard the messages from God.//

**2.	What prophecy did Elijah speak to King Ahab?**

//“There will be no rain or dew in the kingdom of Israel until I say so.”//

**3.	How did God provide for Elijah in the wilderness where he was hiding?**

//God sent birds every morning and evening with bread and meat.//

**4.	How did God provide for Elijah when he lived with the widow and her son?**

//God caused their flour jar and bottle of oil to never become empty.//

**5.	What great evil did Elijah say that Ahab had committed?**

//Ahab had abandoned Yahweh, the true God, and had worshiped Baal.//

**6.	What choice did Elijah tell the people they must make?**

//If Yahweh is God, serve him, but if Baal is God, serve him.//

**7.	How did God demonstrate he was the real God?**

//God sent fire from the sky and burned up the meat, the wood, the rocks, the dirt, and the water around the altar.//

**8.	How did the people respond when they saw this demonstration of power?**

//They fell on the ground and said, “Yahweh is God! Yahweh is God!”//

**9.	What did Elijah and the people then do to the prophets of Baal?**

//They killed the prophets of Baal.//

**10.	What did Elisha tell Naaman to do to in order to heal his skin disease?**

//Dip himself seven times in the Jordan River.//

**11.	What did Naaman do after he heard Elisha’s instructions?**

//At first he was angry and would not do it because it seemed foolish, but later he changed his mind and did it, and was completely healed.//

**12.	What was the common message of the prophets to the people?**

//Stop worshiping idols and show justice and mercy to others; otherwise, God will punish you.//

**13.	In general, how did the people treat the prophets?**

//The people mistreated the prophets and sometimes even killed them.//

**14.	How did the people mistreat the prophet Jeremiah?**

//The people put Jeremiah in a dry well and left him there to die.//

**15.	Did Jeremiah die in the well?**

//No. The king had mercy him and had his servants pull Jeremiah out.//

**16.	Of what promise of God did the prophets also remind the people?**

//The promise that God’s Messiah would come.//
