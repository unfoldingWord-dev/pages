====== Story #34: Jesus Teaches Other Stories ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **How does a mustard seed compare to other seeds?**
    *//It is the smallest seed of all.//
  - **How is the kingdom of God like a mustard seed?**
    *//It starts very small and then grow to be very large.//
  - **What happens to yeast when it is mixed into bread dough?**
    *//It spreads throughout the dough.//
  - **How is the kingdom of God like yeast?**
    *//It will also spread everywhere.//
  - **How is the kingdom of God like a treasure hidden in a field?**
    *//It is worth selling everything else in order to have it.//
  - **How is the kingdom of God like a perfect pearl?**
    *//It is worth selling everything else in order to have it.//
  - **To whom did Jesus tell the story of the two men praying at the Temple?**
    *//People who trusted in their own good deeds and despised other people.//
  - **Why did the religious leader thank God?**
    *//He thanked God that he was not a sinner like other men.//
  - **Why did the religious leader think he was righteous?**
    *//He fasted two times every week and gave ten percent of his money and goods.//

    *//He asked God to be be merciful to him because he was a sinner.‚Äù//
  - **Which man did God declare to be righteous?**
    *//The tax collector.//
  - **What will God do to everyone who is proud?**
    *//He will humble them.//

    *//He will lift them up.//
