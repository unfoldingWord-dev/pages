====== Story #42: Jesus Returns to Heaven ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


1.	When the two disciples who were walking to a nearby town met the risen Jesus, who did they think he was?

They thought he was a visitor who did not know what happened in Jerusalem.

2.	Of what prophecies concerning the Messiah did Jesus remind them?

That the Messiah would suffer and be killed, but would rise again on the third day.

3.	What was Jesus doing when they recognized him?

He was breaking a loaf of bread and thanking God for it.

4.	What did the two disciples do after seeing Jesus?

They returned to Jerusalem and told the other disciples that Jesus was alive and that they had seen him.

5.	When Jesus appeared to the disciples, how did he prove he was not a ghost?

He took a piece of cooked fish and ate it.

6.	How were the disciples able to then understand God’s word?

Jesus opened their minds.

7.	The scriptures say that Jesus’ disciples will do what?

They will proclaim that everyone should repent in order to receive forgiveness for their sins.

8.	What did Jesus do for the next forty days?

He appeared to his disciples many times, proved he was alive, and taught them about the kingdom of God.

9.	Since all authority had been given to Jesus, what did Jesus command his disciples to do?

“Go, make disciples of all people groups by baptizing them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you.”

10.	For what did Jesus tell the disciples to wait?

They were to wait until the Father gave them power when the Holy Spirit came on them.

11.	Where did Jesus go forty days after his resurrection?

He went up to heaven.

12.	What is Jesus doing right now?

He is seated at the right hand of God and ruling over all things.
