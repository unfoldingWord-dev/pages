====== Story #48: Jesus is the Promised Messiah ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What was the world like when God first created it?**
    *//It was perfect, without sin, sickness, or death.//
  - **Why does everyone on earth now get sick and die?**
    *//Because Adam and Eve sinned against God.//
  - **What kind of nature do we have and what is our relationship to God when we are born?**
    *//We have a sinful nature and are enemies of God.//
  - **God promised that one of Eve’s descendants would do what to Satan, but Satan would do what to him?**
    *//He would crush Satan’s head, but Satan would wound his heel.//
  - **How is Jesus like the boat God provided in the flood?**
    *//Like the boat, Jesus is the way God provided for people who believe in him to be saved, even though everyone deserves to be destroyed.//
  - **How is Jesus the perfect high priest?**
    *//He offered himself as the only sacrifice that could take away the sin of all the people of the world.//
  - **How is God’s promise to Abraham fulfilled through Jesus?**
    *//Everyone, from any people group, who believes in Jesus is saved from sin.//
  - **How is Jesus like the lamb sacrificed instead of Isaac?**
    *//God provided Jesus, the Lamb of God, as a sacrifice to die in our place.//
  - **How is Jesus our Passover Lamb?**
    *//When anyone believes in Jesus, the blood of Jesus pays for that person’s sin, and God’s punishment passes over that person.//
  - **Who can be part of the New Covenant?**
    *//Anyone from any people group can be in the New Covenant by believing in Jesus.//
  - **How is Jesus the greatest prophet of all?**
    *//He is the Word of God.//
  - **How is Jesus the fulfillment of God’s promise to King David?**
    *//Jesus is the Messiah and a descendant of David.//
  - **Of what is Jesus the king?**
    *//Of the entire universe.//
  - **What will King Jesus do in the future?**
    *//He will come again and rule his kingdom with justice and peace, forever.//
