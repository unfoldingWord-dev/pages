====== Story #45: Phillip and the Ethiopian Official ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What kind of person was Stephen?**
    *//He had a good reputation and was full of the Holy Spirit and of wisdom.//
  - **What false charge did some Jews bring against Stephen?**
    *//That he had spoken evil things about Moses and God.//
  - **Of what did Stephen say the people accusing him were guilty?**
    *//Of rejecting the Holy Spirit and of killing the Messiah.//
  - **Hearing this, what did the religious leaders then do to Stephen?**
    *//They dragged Stephen out of the city and threw stones at him in order to kill him.//
  - **What was the last thing Stephen said before he died?**
    *//“Master, do not count this sin against them.”//
  - **Who was one of the men who helped kill Stephen?**
    *//Saul.//
  - **What happened because of the persecution of believers in Jerusalem?**
    *//The believers fled to other places and preached about Jesus.//
  - **Who did the Holy Spirit tell Philip to go and talk to?**
    *//An important official from Ethiopia.//
  - **What was the Ethiopian official doing as Stephen approached?**
    *//Reading from the prophet Isaiah.//
  - **In what way was the prophecy from Isaiah about Jesus?**
    *//Jesus was treated unfairly and killed, but he did not say a word.//
  - **After hearing the explanation of the prophecy, what question did the Ethiopian official ask?**
    *//“May I be baptized?”//
  - **What happened to Philip after he baptized the Ethiopian official?**
    *//The Holy Spirit carried Philip away.//
  - **What did the Ethiopian official do after being baptized?**
    *//He continued traveling toward his home, happy that he knew Jesus.//
