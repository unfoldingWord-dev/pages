====== Story #37: Jesus Raises Lazarus from the Dead ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What did Jesus say would be the end result of Lazarus’ sickness?**
    *//It would be for the glory of God.//
  - **Initially, how did the disciples think Lazarus would get better?**
    *//They thought he was just asleep and would get better by himself.//
  - **Why did Jesus say he was glad he was not there when Lazarus died?**
    *//So the disciples would believe in him when Jesus woke Lazarus from the dead.//
  - **What did Martha say to Jesus that demonstrated her faith in him?**
    *//“But I believe God will give you whatever you ask from him.”//
  - **What was Jesus’ “I am” statement to Martha?**
    *//“I am the Resurrection and the Life. Whoever believes in me will live, even though he dies. Everyone who believes in me will never die.”//
  - **Who did Martha say that Jesus was?**
    *//The Messiah, the Son of God.//
  - **How long had Lazarus been dead?**
    *//He had been dead for four days.//
  - **Why did Jesus say that he was thanking the Father out loud so the people could hear him?**
    *//So that the people would believe that the Father had sent Jesus.//
  - **What command did Jesus shout to Lazarus?**
    *//“Lazarus, come out!”//
  - **How did many of the Jews react when they saw the miracle?**
    *//Many Jews believed in Jesus.//
  - **How did the religious leaders of the Jews react when they saw the miracle?**
    *//They planned how they could kill Jesus and Lazarus.//
