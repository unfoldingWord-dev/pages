======Story #7: God Blesses Jacob======

=====Comprehensive Questions and Answers=====

**1.	Who was Rebekah’s favorite son?**

//Rebekah loved Jacob.//

**2.	Who was Isaac’s favorite son?**

//Isaac loved Esau.//

**3.	How did Jacob, the younger son, obtain the rights of the oldest son?**

//Esau gave Jacob the rights of the oldest son in exchange for some food.//

**4.	To whom did Isaac want to give his formal blessing?**

//Esau.//

**5.	How did Jacob trick Isaac into giving him the blessing?**

//He pretended to be Esau by wearing goatskins so that Isaac would think he was Esau.//

**6.	Because of this trick, what did Esau plan to do to Jacob?**

//Esau planned to kill Jacob after Isaac died.//

**7.	What did Rebekah do when she heard of Esau’s plan?**

//She sent Jacob far away to live with her relatives.//

**8.	What happened to Jacob during the next twenty years?**

//He married, had twelve sons and a daughter, and God made him wealthy.//

**9.	Why was Jacob afraid when he returned to Canaan?**

//He thought Esau would kill him.//

**10.	What did Jacob do in order to soothe Esau's anger?**

//He sent herds of animals to Esau as a gift.//

**11.	Was Esau still angry with Jacob?**

//No, he had already forgiven him.//

**12.	Where did Jacob settle to live?**

//In Canaan.//

**13.	When Isaac then died, who buried him?**

//Both Jacob and Esau buried him.//

**14.	After Isaac’s death, who received the covenant promises that God originally gave to Abraham?**

//Jacob.//
