====== Story #27: The Story of the Good Samaritan ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

  - **What question did the expert in the Jewish law ask Jesus?**
    *//“Teacher, what must I do to inherit eternal life?”//
  - **What did Jesus agree he must do in order to live?**
    *//Love the Lord your God with all your heart, soul, strength, and mind. And love your neighbor as yourself.//
  - **What question did the expert in the Jewish law then ask Jesus?**
    *//“Who is my neighbor?”//
  - **Why did the expert in the Jewish law ask this question?**
    *//He wanted to prove that he was righteous.//
  - **In Jesus’ story, what happened to the Jewish man while he was traveling?**
    *//He was attacked by robbers, who took everything he had, and beat him until he was almost dead.//
  - **Who was the first person to see the beaten man and pass by?**
    *//A Jewish priest.//
  - **Who was the second person to see the beaten man and pass by?**
    *//A Levite.//
  - **Who was the third person to see the beaten man?**
    *//A Samaritan.//
  - **What was the relationship between Jews and Samaritans?**
    *//They hated each other.//
  - **How did the Samaritan treat the Jewish man who had been beaten?**
    *//He took care of him and paid the expenses.//
  - **Who was a neighbor to the beaten man?**
    *//The Samaritan who was merciful to him.//
  - **	What was Jesus’ final statement to the Jewish law expert?**
    *//“You go and do the same.”//
