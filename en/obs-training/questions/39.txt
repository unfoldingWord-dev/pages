====== Story #39: Jesus Is Put on Trial ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **During what time of the day did the Jewish leaders put Jesus on trial?**
    *//During the middle of the night.//
  - **Why could the Jewish leaders not prove Jesus was guilty of anything?**
    *//The statements of the false witnesses did not agree with each other.//
  - **Finally, what question did the high priest ask Jesus?**
    *//“Tell us, are you the Messiah, the Son of the living God?”//
  - **What was Jesus’ answer?**
    *//“I am, and you will see me seated with God and coming from heaven.”//
  - **Of what crime did the high priest say Jesus was guilty?**
    *//Saying that he was the Son of God.//
  - **When asked the third time if he knew Jesus, how did Peter answer?**
    *//“May God curse me if I know this man!”//
  - **What happened immediately after Peter gave this answer?**
    *//A rooster crowed, and Jesus turned and looked at Peter.//
  - **When Judas saw the Jewish leaders had condemned Jesus, what did he do?**
    *//Judas became full of sorrow and went away and killed himself.//
  - **Why did the Jewish leaders take Jesus to the Roman governor Pilate?**
    *//They hoped Pilate would condemn Jesus and sentence him to be killed.//
  - **What was Pilate’s question to Jesus?**
    *//“Are you the king of the Jews?”//
  - **Why did Jesus say he had come to earth?**
    *//To tell the truth about God.//
  - **How did Pilate show that he strongly believed Jesus was not guilty?**
    *//Pilate said three times to the crowd that Jesus was not guilty.//
  - **Why then did Pilate allow Jesus to be crucified?**
    *//Pilate became afraid that the crowd would begin to riot.//
  - **What did the Roman soldiers do to Jesus?**
    *//They whipped Jesus, put a royal robe and a crown of thorns on him, and mocked him.//
