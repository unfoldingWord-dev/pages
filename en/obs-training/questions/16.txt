====== Story #16: The Deliverers ======

===== Comprehensive Questions and Answers =====

**1.	After Joshua died, what did the Israelites do wrong?**

//They disobeyed God’s laws, did not drive out the Canaanites, and worshipped the Canaanite gods instead of Yahweh, the true God.//

**2.	How did God punish the Israelites for their disobedience?**

//God allowed their enemies to defeat them.//

**3.	When the Israelites cried out for help, what did God do for them?**

//He provided a deliverer who rescued them and brought peace to the land.//

**4.	When the Midianites defeated the Israelites, how did God call Gideon to be their deliverer?**

//An angel came to Gideon and said, “God is with you, mighty warrior. Go and save Israel from the Midianites.”//

**5.	What two signs did Gideon ask for so he could be sure God would use him to save Israel?**

//He asked for the morning dew to fall only on the cloth and not the ground, and then that the dew fall only on the ground and not the cloth.//

**6.	Why did God tell Gideon to send home all but 300 soldiers?**

//So that the Israelites would know that God had won the battle for them.//

**7.	What additional sign did God give Gideon so he would not be afraid?**

//Gideon went to the Midianite camp at night and heard a soldier say he had a dream that meant Gideon’s army would defeat the Midianite army.//

**8.	What did Gideon do when he heard this sign?**

//He worshiped God.//

**9.	How did Gideon and his men attack the Midianites?**

//They surrounded the Midianite camp, broke their pots to reveal their torches, and blew their horns and shouted, “A sword for God and for Gideon.”//

**10.	What did God do to give Gideon the victory?**

//He confused the Midianites so they attacked and killed each other.//

**11.	Afterwards, how did the Israelites fall back into idol worship?**

//Gideon made a special garment of gold, but the people worshiped it as an idol.//

**12.	What pattern then repeated many times in the life of Israel?**

//The Israelites would sin, God would punish them, they would repent, and God would send a deliverer to save them.//

**13.	Finally, for what did the people ask God?**

//They asked for a king like all the other nations had.//

**14.	Why was God not pleased with this request?**

//Because the Israelites were rejecting him as their ruler.//

**15.	How did God answer their request?**

//He gave them a king just as they had asked.//
