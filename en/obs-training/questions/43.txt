====== Story #43: The Church Begins ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


1.	What festival were the Jews celebrating, and where and when was it held?

Pentecost, held in Jerusalem 50 days after the Passover.

2.	On Pentecost, what happened in the house where the believers were gathered?

There was a sound like a strong wind, something like flames of fire appeared over the heads of the believers, and they were filled with the Holy Spirit and began to speak in other languages.

3.	What was astonishing to the crowd who heard the disciples?

They heard about the wonderful works of God in their own native language.

4.	What prophecy did Peter say was being fulfilled?

Joel’s prophecy, “In the last days, I will pour out my Spirit.”

5.	Who did Peter say had responsibility for Jesus’ crucifixion?

The people in the crowd.

6.	What prophecy did Peter say was fulfilled in Jesus’ resurrection?

“You will not let your Holy One rot in the grave.”

7.	How were the disciples able to speak in other languages?

The Holy Spirit caused them to be able to speak in other languages.

8.	According to Peter, what two titles did God give Jesus?

Lord and Messiah.

9.	After hearing Peter, what question did the people ask?

“Brothers, what should we do?”

10.	What was Peter’s answer to them?

Repent and be baptized in the name of Jesus Christ so that God will forgive your sins. Then he will also give you the gift of the Holy Spirit.

11.	How many people believed and were baptized?

About 3000.

12.	What did the disciples spend their time doing?

Listening to the teaching of the apostles, spending time together, eating together, praying with each other, praising God together, and sharing everything.
