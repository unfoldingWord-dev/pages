 ====== Story #24: John Baptizes Jesus ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

  - **Where did John live when he grew up?**
    *//He lived in the wilderness.//
  - **What message did John preach to the people?**
    *//“Repent, for the kingdom of God is near!”//
  - **What did John do to people who repented of their sins?**
    *//John baptized them.//
  - **What did John tell people who did not repent?**
    *//“Every tree that does not bear good fruit will be cut down and thrown into the fire.”//
  - **What role did John fulfill that was predicted by the prophets?**
    *//John was a messenger who prepared the way for the Messiah.//
  - **What was John's answer when the Jews asked him if he was the Messiah?**
    *//He said he was not the Messiah.//
  - **When John saw Jesus coming to be baptized, what did he say?**
    *//“Look! There is the Lamb of God who will take away the sin of the world.”//
  - **Why did Jesus not need to be baptized for his own benefit?**
    *//Jesus had never sinned.//
  - **Why did Jesus say that John should baptize him anyway?**
    *//Because it was the right thing to do.//
  - **When Jesus was baptized, what appeared?**
    *//The Spirit of God appeared in the form of a dove and came down and rested on him.//
  - **When Jesus was baptized, what did God say from heaven?**
    *//“You are my Son whom I love, and I am very pleased with you.”//
  - **When John saw the Holy Spirit come down and rest on Jesus, what did he know about Jesus?**
    *//He knew that Jesus was the Son of God.//
  - **How many gods are there?**
    *//There is only one God.//
