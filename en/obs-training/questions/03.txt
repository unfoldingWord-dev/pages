======Story #3: The Flood======

=====Comprehensive Questions and Answers=====

**1.	Why did God decide to destroy the whole world with a flood?**

//People had become very wicked and violent.//

**2.	Why did Noah find grace in God’s eyes?**

//He was a righteous man//.

**3.	What did God tell Noah to do?**

//Build an ark.//

**4.	Who and what would go into the ark for safety during the flood?**

//Noah, his family, two of every kind of animal, and seven pairs of every type of animal that could be used for sacrifice.//

**5.	When Noah told the people about the coming flood, what did they say?**

//They laughed at him.//

**6.	When Noah’s family and the animals were inside the ark, who closed the door?**

//God closed the door.//

**7.	How long did it rain?**

//Forty days and forty nights.//

**8.	What was covered by the water of the flood?**

//Everything in the whole world, even the highest mountains.//

**9.	What happened to everything that lived on land?**

//They all died.//

**10.	How did Noah know the water had dried up?**

//The dove he sent out did not return.//

**11.	What did God tell Noah and his family to do after they left the ark?**

//He told them to have many children and grandchildren and to fill the earth.//

**12.	What did God promise He would never do again?**

//Curse the ground or destroy the world with a flood.//

**13.	What did God make as a sign of His promise?**

//A rainbow in the sky.//
