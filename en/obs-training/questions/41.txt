====== Story #41: God Raises Jesus from the Dead ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What prediction had the Jewish leaders heard Jesus make about his death?**
    *//That he would rise from the dead after three days.//
  - **What name did the Jewish leaders call Jesus because of his prediction?**
    *//A liar.//
  - **What were the Jewish leaders afraid would now happen?**
    *//The disciples would steal the body and claim Jesus had risen from the dead.//
  - **What did the Jewish leaders do to secure Jesus’ tomb?**
    *//They put soldiers at the tomb and a seal on the stone at the entrance of the tomb.//
  - **When did the women go to Jesus’ tomb?**
    *//Very early on the morning after the Sabbath day.//
  - **Who appeared at the tomb and what did he do?**
    *//An angel appeared, rolled away the stone and sat on it.//
  - **What did the angel say to the women?**
    *//Do not be afraid. Jesus has risen from the dead. Look in the tomb and see.//
  - **What instructions did the angel then give the women for the disciples?**
    *//Tell the disciples that Jesus has risen from the dead, and that he will go to Galilee ahead of you.//
  - **Who did the women see on their way to tell the disciples?**
    *//Jesus.//
  - **What did the women do when they saw Jesus?**
    *//They worshiped him.//
