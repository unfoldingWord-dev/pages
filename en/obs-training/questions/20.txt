====== Story #20: The Exile and Return ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====


  - **What was God’s message to the people through the prophets, and how did the people respond?**
    *//God told them to repent and worship him, but the people refused.//
  - **Who did God allow to destroy the kingdom of Israel?**
    *//The Assyrian Empire.//
  - **Which two groups of people made up the Samaritans?**
    *//The very poor Israelites left in the land, and the foreigners brought to the land by the Assyrians.//
  - **What did the people of the kingdom of Judah do when they saw how God had punished the kingdom of Israel?**
    *//They continued worshiping idols.//
  - **Who was the Babylonian king that made the king of Judah his servant?**
    *//King Nebuchadnezzar.//
  - **When the king of Judah rebelled, what did Nebuchadnezzar’s soldiers do to him?**
    *//They killed the king’s sons in front of him, made him blind, and took him away to die in prison in Babylon.//
  - **What is the time period called when God’s people were forced to leave the Promised Land?**
    *//The Exile.//
  - **What was God’s promise to the people in the Exile?**
    *//God promised that after seventy years, they would return to the Promised Land.//
  - **Which king and empire defeated the Babylonians?**
    *//King Cyrus from the Persian Empire.//
  - **What order did King Cyrus give regarding the Jews?**
    *//Any Jew who wanted to return to Judah could.//
  - **What did the Jews do who returned to Jerusalem?**
    *//They rebuilt the Temple and the wall around the city.//
