====== Story #15: The Promised Land ======

===== Comprehensive Questions and Answers =====

**1.	When it was time to enter the Promised Land, what did Joshua do first?**

//He sent two spies to Jericho.//

**2.	What agreement did the spies make with Rahab the prostitute?**

//Rahab helped the spies escape Jericho, and the spies promised to protect Rahab and her family when the Israelites destroyed the city.//

**3.	How did the Israelites cross the Jordan River to enter the Promised Land?**

//When the priests started to step into the Jordan River, the water stopped flowing.//

**4.	How did the Israelites attack Jericho, as God had told them?**

//They marched around the city once a day for six days, then on the seventh day they marched around the city seven more times. While they marched around the city for the last time, the soldiers shouted while the priests blew trumpets.//

**5.	What happened when the soldiers shouted while the priests blew trumpets?**

//The walls of Jericho fell down, and the Israelites destroyed everything in the city.//

**6.	What happened to Rahab and her family?**

//They were not killed, and became part of the Israelites.//

**7.	How did the Gibeonites trick the Israelites into making a peace treaty with them?**

//They said they were from a place far from Canaan.//

**10.	What did God do to fight for the Israelites against the Amorites?**

//He confused the Amorites, sent large hailstones on them, and caused the sun to stay in one place so the Israelites had time to completely defeat them.//

**11.	What did Joshua and the Israelites do to the many other Canaanite people groups who then attacked them?**

//Joshua and the Israelites destroyed them.//

**12.	How was the Promised Land divided among the Israelites?**

//God gave each tribe its own section.//

**13.	When Joshua was old what did he do?**

//He called the Israelites together and reminded them of their obligation to obey the covenant with God.//

**14.	What did the people promise to do?**

//They promised to remain faithful to God and follow his laws.//
