====== Story #8: God Saves Joseph and His Family ======

===== Comprehensive Questions and Answers =====

1.	Why did Joseph’s brothers hate him?

Because Joseph was Jacob’s favorite son and because Joseph had dreamed he would be their ruler.

2.	What evil thing did Joseph’s brothers do to him?

They kidnapped him and sold him to some slave traders.

3.	How did the brothers explain Joseph’s disappearance to Jacob?

They put goat’s blood on Joseph’s robe and told Jacob that a wild animal had killed him.

4.	Why was Joseph sent to prison even though he had served his master well?

His master’s wife tried to sleep with him, but Joseph refused, so she falsely accused him.

5.	What ability had God given Joseph?

The ability to interpret dreams.

6.	What was the meaning of Pharaoh’s dream that Joseph interpreted?

God was going to send seven years of plentiful harvests, followed by seven years of famine.

7.	What did Pharaoh do for Joseph when he heard the interpretation?

He appointed Joseph second most powerful man in Egypt.

8.	What was Joseph’s plan to deal with the coming famine?

Joseph told the people to store up large amounts of food during the seven good years, and then he sold the food to the people during the seven years of famine.

9.	Why did Joseph’s brothers come to Egypt?

They came to buy food, because the famine was severe in Canaan also.

10.	What did Joseph do to his brothers first to see if they had changed?

He tested them.

11.	How did God use Joseph’s slavery for good?

As a powerful ruler in Egypt, Joseph could now provide for his family during the famine.

12.	To where did Jacob and his family move?

Egypt.

13.	After Jacob died, who received the covenant promises of God given to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob?

The twelve sons of Jacob.

14.	What did the descendants of the twelve sons of Jacob become?

The twelve tribes of Israel.
