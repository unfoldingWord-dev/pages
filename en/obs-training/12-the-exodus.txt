====== 12. The Exodus ======


===== Overview: =====


The Israelites left Egypt with joy. God appeared as a pillar of cloud and fire to lead them to the Promised Land. However, Pharaoh changed his mind and pursued Israel in order to make them slaves again. But God separated the waters of the sea so that the Israelites could pass through on dry ground. When Egyptians tried to follow, they all drowned. Israel celebrates that great victory with the annual Passover Feast.
