====== 26. Jesus Starts His Ministry ======


===== Overview: =====

Jesus began to preach in Galilee. He returned to Nazareth where He grew up and went to worship. Jesus read Isaiahâ€™s prophesy about the Messiah, and said that it was talking about Him. The people did not believe Him, and tried to kill Him. So Jesus left them and went to other places to teach, to heal, and to do miracles.  He chose twelve men to be His disciples and to learn from Him.
