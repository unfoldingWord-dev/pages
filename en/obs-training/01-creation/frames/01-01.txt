===== Frame 01-01 =====

{{:en:obs:obs-01-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


This is how **the beginning**  of everything happened. **God**  **created**  the universe and everything in it in six **days**. At first, the earth was **dark**  and **empty**, and **nothing had been formed **  in it. But **God's Spirit**  was there over the water.


**Key Terms:** 


    * **[[:en:key-terms:the beginning|the beginning]]**- This refers to "the beginning of all things", or "before everything else"…
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h430|God]]**- Almighty, creator, sinless & perfect…
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h1254a|created]]**- "created from nothing", or "to cause something to exist"…
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h3117|days]]**- the term refers to a literal 24-hour period
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h2822|dark]]**- there was not yet any light from any source
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h922|empty]]**- contained absolutely nothing–no living things
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h8414|nothing had been formed]]**- there were no distinguishing features–just water covering everything
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h7307|God's Spirit]]**- the Holy Spirit, the second person of the Trinity
**Translation Notes:**
