==== Frame 01-11 ====


{{:en:obs:obs-01-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


In the middle of the **garden**, **God **  planted two special trees—the **tree of life**  and the **tree of the knowledge of good and evil**. **God **  told **Adam **  that he could eat from any tree in the **garden **  except from the **tree of the knowledge of good and evil**. If he ate from this tree, he would **die**.


=== Key Terms: ===
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h1588|garden]]**- a special collection of trees and plants that God prepared for Adam
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h430|God]]**- Almighty, creator, sinless and perfect
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h6086|tree of life]]**- a literal tree with fruit that could keep a person alive continually
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h2896a|good]]**- in agreement with God's character and purposes
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h7451b|evil]]**- opposed to God's character and purposes
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h6086|tree of knowledge of good and evil]]**- a literal tree with fruit that could enable a person to know the difference between good and evil
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h120|Adam]]**- Adam's name was literally the Hebrew word for "man"
    * **[[:en:key-terms:h4191|die]]**- to die is to be separated from God and from life on this earth
