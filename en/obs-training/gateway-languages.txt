====== Gateway Languages ======

The objective of this page is to identify the key "gateway languages" of the world, providing the fewest number of languages where a translation of a training resource would reach through second-language speakers to every living language.

<graphviz dot>
digraph languages {
	rankdir=LR;
	size="9,5"
	"source text" -> "gateway language" -> "all languages";
}
</graphviz>

===== Current List of Languages =====

  - Hindi (hi)

  - Arabic (ar)
  - Persian (fa)
