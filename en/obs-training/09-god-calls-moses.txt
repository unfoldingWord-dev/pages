====== 9. God Calls Moses ======


===== Overview: =====


After Joseph died, a new Pharaoh forced the Israelites to become slaves. He ordered them to kill all of their baby boys, but God protected Moses. Pharaohâ€™s daughter adopted him and he became a prince in Egypt. But Moses killed an Egyptian and fled to the wilderness. However, God appeared to Moses and sent him back to Egypt to free the Israelites from slavery.
