====== Open Bible Stories ======
~~NOTOC~~
{{ :en:obs-pic_360p.jpg?nolink&300|http://openbiblestories.com}} **50 key stories of the Bible, from Creation to Revelation, for evangelism & discipleship, in text, audio, and video, on any mobile phone, in any language, for free.**
