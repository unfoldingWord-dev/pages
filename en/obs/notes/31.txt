====== 31. Jesus Walks on Water ======


===== Overview: =====


One day, Jesus was praying near the lake as his disciples went across it in a boat. A storm arose and battered their boat. Then Jesus came to them walking on the water, and the disciples were afraid. But Peter got out of the boat and walked to Jesus on the water. When Peter began to sink, Jesus saved him and calmed the storm.
