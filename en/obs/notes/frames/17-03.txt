====== David Killed Goliath [17-03] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-17-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:david|David]]** became a great soldier and leader. When David was still a young man, he fought against **a giant named Goliath**. Goliath was a trained soldier, very strong, and almost three meters tall! But **[[:en:key-terms:god|God]]** helped David kill Goliath and save **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israel]]**. After that, David won many victories over Israel's enemies, for which the people **[[:en:key-terms:praise|praised]]** him.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **a giant named Goliath** - The word "giant" here describes a person who is unusually large and powerful. Goliath was a huge soldier in an army that was fighting against Israel.
