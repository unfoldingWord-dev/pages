====== The Jailer Was Baptized [47-12] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-47-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The jailer and his whole family **[[:en:key-terms:believe|believed]]** in **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**  and were **[[:en:key-terms:baptize|baptized]]**. Then the jailer gave **[[:en:key-terms:paul|Paul]]** and **[[:en:key-terms:silas|Silas]]** a meal and they **[[:en:key-terms:joy|rejoiced]]** together.
