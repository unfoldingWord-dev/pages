====== God Fought the Amorites [15-09] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-15-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:god|God]] threw the [[:en:key-terms:amorite|Amorites]] into confusion**. **God fought for [[:en:key-terms:israel|Israel]]** that day **by sending large hailstones that killed many of the Amorites**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **threw the Amorites into confusion** - This could be translated as, “God caused the Amorites to be confused,” or “God made the Amorites panic,” or “God caused the Amorites to be unable to fight together well.” 
  * **God fought for Israel** - God fought on the side of Israel against Israel's enemies. 
  * **by sending large hailstones that killed many of the Amorites** - This could be translated as, “He caused very large balls of ice to come down from the sky and that ice killed many of the Amorite soldiers.”
