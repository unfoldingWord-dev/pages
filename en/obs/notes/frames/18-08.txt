====== Jeroboam Ruled Israel [18-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-18-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The other ten tribes that rebelled against **[[:en:key-terms:rehoboam|Rehoboam]] made a man named [[:en:key-terms:jeroboam|Jeroboam]] their [[:en:key-terms:king|king]]**. They **set up their [[:en:key-terms:kingdom|kingdom]]** in the northern part of the land and were called the kingdom of **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israel]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **made a man named Jeroboam their king** – This could also be translated as, “appointed a man named Jeroboam to be their king.” 
  * **set up their kingdom** - This may be translated as, they "established" or "built" their kingdom. This sentence may also be translated as, “They separated themselves from the other two tribes and lived in the northern part of the land, and they called their country 'Israel.'”
