====== King of the Universe [48-14] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-48-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:key-terms:david|David]]** was the **[[:en:key-terms:king|king]]** of **[[:en:key-terms:israel|Israel]]**, but **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** is **the king of the entire universe**! He will come again and rule his **[[:en:key-terms:kingdom-of-god|kingdom]]** with **[[:en:key-terms:justice|justice]]** and **[[:en:key-terms:peace|peace]]**, forever.
