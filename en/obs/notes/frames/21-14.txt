====== Resurrection of the Messiah [21-14] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-21-14.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**In addition to [[:en:key-terms:prophet| prophesying]] that the [[:en:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]] would [[:en:key-terms:death|die]], the prophets foretold that [[:en:key-terms:god|God]] would [[:en:key-terms:raise|raise]] him from the dead**. **Through the Messiah’s death and [[:en:key-terms:resurrection|resurrection]], God would complete his plan of [[:en:key-terms:save|salvation]]** and **start the [[:en:key-terms:covenant-new|New Covenant]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **In addition to prophesying that the Messiah would die, the prophets foretold that God would** - This could also be translated as, “The prophets foretold that the Messiah would die and that God would also.” 
  * **raise him from the dead** - That is, “cause him to be alive again.” 
  * **Through the Messiah’s death  and resurrection, God would** - This could also be translated as, “God would use the Messiah's death and resurrection to” or “The Messiah's death and resurrection would be the way that God would."
  * **complete his plan of salvation** - That is, “accomplish his plan to save sinners.”  
  * **start the New Covenant** – That is, “put the New Covenant into effect.”
