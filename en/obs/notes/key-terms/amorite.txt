====== Amorite ======


===== Facts: =====

The Amorites were a group of people living in Canaan in the time of Joshua.

  * The Amorites lived in cities on both sides of the Jordan River. Each city was ruled by a king.
  * God had promised to give the Israelites the land of Canaan, including the land of the Amorites.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:15-07|[15-07]]]** Sometime later, the kings of another people group in Canaan, the **Amorites**, heard that the Gibeonites had made a peace treaty with the Israelites, so they combined their armies into one large army and attacked Gibeon. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:15-08|[15-08]]]** In the early morning they surprised the **Amorite** armies and attacked them. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:15-09|[15-09]]]** God fought for Israel that day. He caused the **Amorites** to be confused and he sent large hailstones that killed many of the **Amorites**.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:15-10|[15-10]]]** God also caused the sun to stay in one place in the sky so that Israel would have enough time to completely defeat the **Amorites**.
