====== believer ======


===== Definitions Facts: =====

A "believer" is a person who trusts completely in a person or an idea. 

  * In the Bible, "believer" specifically refers to someone who believes in Jesus. 
  * The term "Christian" eventually came to be the main title for believers because it indicates that they believe in Christ.
  * Other common ways to translate "believer" are, "follower of Jesus" or, "disciple."

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:43-01|[43-01]]]** After Jesus returned to heaven, the disciples stayed in Jerusalem as Jesus had commanded them to do. The **believers** there constantly gathered together to pray. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:43-03|[43-03]]]** While the **believers** were all together, suddenly the house where they were was filled with a sound like a strong wind. Then something that looked like flames of fire appeared over the heads of all the **believers**.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:43-13|[43-13]]]** Every day, more people became **believers**. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:46-06|[45-06]]]** That day many people in Jerusalem started persecuting the followers of Jesus, so the **believers** fled to other places. But in spite of this, they preached about Jesus everywhere they went. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:46-01|[46-01]]]** Saul was the young man who guarded the robes of the men who killed Stephen. He did not believe in Jesus, so he persecuted the **believers**.  
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:46-09|[46-09]]]** Some **believers** who fled from the persecution in Jerusalem went far away to the city of Antioch and preached about Jesus...It was at Antioch that **believers** in Jesus were first called "Christians." 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:47-14|[47-14]]]** They also wrote many letters to encourage and teach the **believers** in the churches.
