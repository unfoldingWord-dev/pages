====== peace ======


==== (peace, peaceful) ====


===== Definition: =====

Peace is the absence of any conflict, fear, or intimidation. 

  * Peace can refer to freedom from war between groups of people.
  * To make peace with a group of people is to stop fighting against them.
  * Peace can also refer to a good relationship between people or between people and God.
  * Personal peace refers to a calm state of mind with no anxiety or fear. 


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:15-06|[15-06]]]** God had commanded the Israelites not to make a **peace** treaty with any of the people groups in Canaan.  
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:15-12|[15-12]]]** Then God gave Israel **peace** along all its borders. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:16-03|[16-03]]]** Then God provided a deliverer who rescued them from their enemies and brought **peace** to the land. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:21-13|[21-13]]]** He (Messiah) would die to receive the punishment for other people's sin. His punishment would bring **peace** between God and people. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:48-14|[48-14]]]** David was the king of Israel, but Jesus is the king of the entire universe! He will come again and rule his kingdom with justice and **peace**, forever.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:50-17|[50-17]]]** Jesus will rule his kingdom with **peace** and justice, and he will be with his people forever.
