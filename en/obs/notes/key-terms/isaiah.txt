====== Isaiah ======


===== Facts: =====

Isaiah was a prophet of God.

  * Isaiah wrote many prophecies about Israel that came true while he was still living.
  * Isaiah is especially known for the prophecies he wrote about the Messiah that came true 700 years later when Jesus was living on earth.
  * Jesus and his disciples quoted Isaiah's prophecies to teach people about the Messiah.
  * The Book of Isaiah is one of the major books of the Bible.

[See also: **[[en:obs:notes:key-terms:prophet|prophet]], [[en:obs:notes:key-terms:Messiah|Messiah]]**]


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:21-09|[21-09]]]** The prophet **Isaiah** prophesied that the Messiah would be born from a virgin.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:21-10|[21-10]]]** The prophet **Isaiah** said the Messiah would live in Galilee, comfort broken-hearted people, and proclaim freedom to captives and release to prisoners. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:21-11|[21-11]]]** The prophet **Isaiah** also prophesied that the Messiah would be hated without reason and rejected.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:21-12|[21-12]]]** **Isaiah** prophesied that people would spit on, mock, and beat the Messiah. 
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]** They handed him (Jesus) the scroll of the prophet **Isaiah** so that he would read from it. Jesus opened up the scroll and read part of it to the people.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:45-08|[45-08]]]** When Philip approached the chariot, he heard the Ethiopian reading from what the prophet **Isaiah** wrote.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:45-10|[45-10]]]** Philip explained to the Ethiopian that **Isaiah** was writing about Jesus.
