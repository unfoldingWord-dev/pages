====== joy ======


===== Definition: =====

Joy is a feeling of deep satisfaction and delight.

    * A person can experience great joy when he is serving God and loving other people.
    * God can give joy even when very difficult things are happening.


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:33-07|[33-07]]]** "The rocky ground is a person who hears God's word and accepts it with **joy**."
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:34-04|[34-04]]]** "The kingdom of God is also like hidden treasure that someone hid in a field.. Another man found the treasure and then buried it again. He was so filled with **joy**, that he went and sold everything he had and used the money to buy that field."
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:41-07|[41-07]]]** The women were full of fear and great **joy**. They ran to tell the disciples the good news.
