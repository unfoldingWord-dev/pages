====== Rome ======


==== (Rome, Roman) ====


===== Facts: =====

In the current day, Rome is the capital city of the country of Italy.

    * During the time of Jesus and the apostles, Rome was the center of the Roman Empire.
    * The Roman Empire ruled over all of the regions around the Mediterranean Sea, including Israel.
    * The term, "Roman" refers to anything relating to the regions that the government in Rome controlled, including Roman citizens and Roman officials.
    * The Apostle Paul was taken to the city of Rome as a prisoner because he preached the good news about Jesus.

  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:23-04|[23-04]]]** When the time was near for Mary to give birth, the **Roman** government told everyone to go for a census to the town where their ancestors had lived.

  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:39-09|[39-09]]]** Early the next morning, the Jewish leaders brought Jesus to the **Roman** governor, Pilate, hoping to have Jesus killed.
  ***[[:en:obs:notes:frames:39-12|[39-12]]]** The **Roman** soldiers whipped Jesus and put a royal robe and a crown made of thorns on him. Then they mocked him by saying, "Look, the King of the Jews!"
