====== H100: אַגְמוֹן (ʼagmôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H98; a marshy **pool** (others from a different root, a **kettle**); by implication

===== Meaning =====

a **rush** (as growing there); collectively a **rope** of rushes

===== Definitions =====

  - pool
  - kettle
  - rush
  - rope

===== Usage =====

bulrush, caldron, hook, rush.
