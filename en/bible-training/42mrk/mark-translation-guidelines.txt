====== Guidelines for the Translation of Mark ======

(This is an "under construction" page. The audience is the MTT translator. Please add your comments and suggestions for content to the Discussion below.)


===== Guidelines: =====

  * Read though an least a chapter before you translate any passage.
  * Read a passage multiple times before you translate it.
  * Read a passage in as many source languages as you understand.
  * Pray and ask God for wisdom before translating.
  * Translate the passages in order, from the beginning.
