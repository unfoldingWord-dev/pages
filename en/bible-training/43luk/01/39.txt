===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in those days** - This can also be translated as, "at that time."
  * **hill country** - That is, "the mountainous region" or the "hilly area of Israel."
  * **the baby** - This refers John, who was not yet born.
  * **filled with the Holy Spirit** - That is, "controlled by the Holy Spirit."
