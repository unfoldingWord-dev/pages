====== Luke 1:8-10 ======


===== TFT Text: =====

<sup>8</sup> One day //Zechariah's// group was doing their work //in the Temple in Jerusalem//, and he was serving as a priest in God's presence. <sup>9</sup> Following their custom, //the other priests// chose him by lot to enter the Lord's temple and burn incense. <sup>10</sup> While he was burning the incense, many people were outside //in the courtyard,// praying.


===== UFW Text: =====

<sup>8</sup> Now while he executed the priest’s office before God in the order of his division, <sup>9</sup> according to the custom of the priest’s office, his lot was to enter into the temple of the Lord and burn incense. <sup>10</sup> The whole multitude of the people were praying outside at the hour of incense.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **executed the priest's office** - That is, "did the work of a priest" or "served as priest".
  * **before God** - That is, "in God's presence."
  * **according to the custom of the priests office** - That is, "in the way that the priests normally chose priests to do a certain task."
  * **in the order of his division** - That is, "with the priests in his group" or "with the priests who belonged to the group that he belonged to."
  * **his lot was** - That is, "he was chosen by lot". This could also be translated as "The priests chose him by lot." To choose by lot is to ...
  * **The whole multitude** - That is, "Many people"
  * **at the hour of incense** - That is, "at the time for burning incense" or "at the time that the chosen priest would burn incense.
