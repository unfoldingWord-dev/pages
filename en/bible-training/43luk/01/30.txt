===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **found favor with God** - This can also be translated as, "God is pleased with you."
  * **conceive in your womb** - Each language group may have a particular way of expressing that the baby was formed inside of the mother's body.
  * **call his name** - That is, "name him."
  * **will be called** - This can be translated as, "they will call" or, "people will call."
  * **give him the throne** - This expression means, "make him king."  See [MTY]
  * **his father, David** - That is, "his ancestor, David." Scripture sometimes skips generations when expressing "father" or "son."
  * **house of Jacob** - That is, "the descendants of Jacob" or, "the nation of Israel." God changed Jacob's name to Israel.
