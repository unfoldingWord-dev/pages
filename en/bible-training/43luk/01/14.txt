====== Luke 1:14-15 ======


===== TFT Text: =====

<sup>14</sup> He will cause you to be very happy, and many other people will also be happy because he is born. <sup>15</sup> God will consider him to be very important. He must never drink wine or any other alcoholic drink, //in order that he will be completely dedicated to God//. He will be controlled by the Holy Spirit {The Holy Spirit will control him} from before he is born.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **have joy and gladness** - That is, "be joyful and glad" or "be very joyful," or "will rejoice greatly""
  * **many will rejoice** - That is, "many people will be joyful."
  * **at his birth** - That is, "because he is born" or "when he is born"
  * **He will be great in the sight of the Lord** - That is, "The Lord will consider him to be very important."
  * **strong drink** - A strong drink is one that can make people drunk.
  * **He will be filled with the Holy Spirit** - That is, "He will be controlled by the Holy Spirit" or "The Holy Spirit will control him."
