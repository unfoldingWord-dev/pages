===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **called out** - This can also be translated as, "cried out."
  * **loud voice** - This can also be translated as "shout."
  * **Blessed...blessed** - This same word is used twice. The first occurrence is feminine for Mary, and the section is masculine for the baby.
  * **fruit of your womb** - This expression refers to her baby. If you do not have a similar expression in your language, you could say, "the baby that will come from your womb."
  * **mother of my Lord** - This refers to Mary. She is the mother of the baby, and the baby is the Lord of everyone, including Elizabeth.
  * **believed** - That is, "believed God" or, believed what God told her through the angel."
  * **there will be a fulfillment of the things which have been spoken to her** - This can also be translated as, "the things that were spoken to her will be fulfilled."
