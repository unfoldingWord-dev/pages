====== H2930: טָמֵא (ṭâmêʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be foul**, especially in a ceremial or moral sense (contaminated)

===== Definitions =====

  - be foul

===== Usage =====

defile (self), pollute (self), be (make, make self, pronounce) unclean, × utterly.
