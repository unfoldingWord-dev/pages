====== H6767: צְלָצַל (tsᵉlâtsal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6750|H6750]] reduplicated;

===== Meaning =====

a **clatter**, i.e. (abstractly) **whirring** (of wings); (concretely) a **cricket**; also a **harpoon** (as **rattling**),a **cymbal** (as **clanging**)

===== Definitions =====

  - clatter
  - whirring
  - cricket
  - harpoon
  - rattling
  - cymbal
  - clanging

===== Usage =====

cymbal, locust, shadowing, spear.
