====== G2341: θηριομαχέω (thēriomachéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2342|G2342]] and [[en:lexicon:g3164|G3164]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a beast-fighter (in the gladiatorial show), i.e. (figuratively) to encounter (furious men)

===== Usage =====

fight with wild beasts.
