====== G642: ἀπορφανίζω (aporphanízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3737|G3737]];

===== Meaning =====

to bereave wholly, i.e. (figuratively) separate (from intercourse)

===== Usage =====

take.
