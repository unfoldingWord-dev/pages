====== G3851: παραβουλεύομαι (parabouleúomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g1011|G1011]];

===== Meaning =====

to misconsult, i.e. disregard

===== Usage =====

not (to) regard(-ing).
