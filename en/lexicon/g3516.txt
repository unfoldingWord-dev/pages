====== G3516: νήπιος (nḗpios) ======

===== Source =====

from an obsolete particle νή- (nḗ-) (implying negation) and [[en:lexicon:g2031|G2031]];

===== Meaning =====

not speaking, i.e. an infant (minor); figuratively, a simple-minded person, an immature Christian

===== Usage =====

babe, child (+ -ish).
