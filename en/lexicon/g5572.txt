====== G5572: ψευδοδιδάσκαλος (pseudodidáskalos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5571|G5571]] and [[en:lexicon:g1320|G1320]];

===== Meaning =====

a spurious teacher, i.e. propagator of erroneous Christian doctrine

===== Usage =====

false teacher.
