====== G5316: φαίνω (phaínō) ======

===== Source =====

prolongation for the base of [[en:lexicon:g5457|G5457]];

===== Meaning =====

to lighten (shine), i.e. show (transitive or intransitive, literal or figurative)

===== Usage =====

 appear, seem, be seen, shine, X think.
