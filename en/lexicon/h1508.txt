====== H1508: גִּזְרָה (gizrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h1506|H1506]];

===== Meaning =====

the **figure** or **person** (as if cut out); also an **inclosure** (as separated)

===== Definitions =====

  - figure
  - person
  - inclosure

===== Usage =====

polishing, separate place.
