====== H323: אֲחַשְׁדַּרְפַּן (ʼăchashdarpan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

of Persian derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a **satrap** or **governor** of a main province (of Persia)

===== Definitions =====

  - satrap
  - governor

===== Usage =====

lieutenant.
