====== H5349: נֹקֵד (nôqêd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

active participle from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5348|H5348]];

===== Meaning =====

a **spotter** (of sheep or cattle), i.e. the **owner** or **tender** (who thus marks them)

===== Definitions =====

  - spotter
  - owner
  - tender

===== Usage =====

herdman, sheepmaster.
