====== G1684: ἐμβαίνω (embaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to walk on, i.e. embark (aboard a vessel), reach (a pool)

===== Usage =====

come (get) into, enter (into), go (up) into, step in, take ship.
