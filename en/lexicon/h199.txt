====== H199: אוּלָם (ʼûwlâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

apparently a variation of [[en:lexicon:h194|H194]];

===== Meaning =====

**however** or **on the contrary**

===== Definitions =====

  - however
  - on the contrary

===== Usage =====

as for, but, howbeit, in very deed, surely, truly, wherefore.
