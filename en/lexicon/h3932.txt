====== H3932: לָעַג (lâʻag) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **deride**; by implication (as if imitating a foreigner) to **speak unintelligibly**

===== Definitions =====

  - deride
  - speak unintelligibly

===== Usage =====

have in derision, laugh (to scorn), mock (on), stammering.
