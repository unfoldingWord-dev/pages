====== H6823: צָפָה (tsâphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably identical with [[en:lexicon:h6822|H6822]] through the idea of **expansion** in outlook, transferring to action);

===== Meaning =====

to **sheet** over (especially with metal)

===== Definitions =====

  - expansion
  - sheet

===== Usage =====

cover, overlay.
