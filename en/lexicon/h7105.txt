====== H7105: קָצִיר (qâtsîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7114|H7114]];

===== Meaning =====

**severed**, i.e. **harvest** (as reaped), the **crop**, the **time**, the **reaper**, or figuratively; also a **limb** (of a tree, or simply foliage)

===== Definitions =====

  - severed
  - harvest
  - crop
  - time
  - reaper
  - limb

===== Usage =====

bough, branch, harvest (man).
