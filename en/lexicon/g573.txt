====== G573: ἁπλοῦς (haploûs) ======

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a particle of union) and the base of [[en:lexicon:g4120|G4120]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, folded together, i.e. single (figuratively, clear)

===== Usage =====
