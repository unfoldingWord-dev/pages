====== G4735: στέφανος (stéphanos) ======

===== Source =====

from an apparently primary στέφω (stéphō) (to twine or wreathe);

===== Meaning =====

a chaplet (as a badge of royalty, a prize in the public games or a symbol of honor generally; but more conspicuous and elaborate than the simple fillet,

===== Usage =====

crown.
