====== H1736: דּוּדַי (dûwday) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1731|H1731]];

===== Meaning =====

a **boiler** or **basket**; also the **mandrake** (as an aphrodisiac)

===== Definitions =====

  - boiler
  - basket
  - mandrake

===== Usage =====

basket, mandrake.
