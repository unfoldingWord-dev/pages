====== H6424: פָּלַס (pâlaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **roll flat**, i.e. **prepare** (a road); also to **revolve**, i.e. **weigh** (mentally)

===== Definitions =====

  - roll flat
  - prepare
  - revolve
  - weigh

===== Usage =====

make, ponder, weigh.
