====== G4759: στρατοπεδάρχης (stratopedárchēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4760|G4760]] and [[en:lexicon:g757|G757]];

===== Meaning =====

a ruler of an army, i.e. (specially), a Prætorian præfect

===== Usage =====

captain of the guard.
