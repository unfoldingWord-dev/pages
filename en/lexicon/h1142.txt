====== H1142: בְּנֵי יַעֲקָן (Bᵉnêy Yaʻăqân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the plural of [[en:lexicon:h1121|H1121]] and [[en:lexicon:h3292|H3292]]; sons of Yaakan;

===== Meaning =====

**Bene-Jaakan**, a place in the Desert

===== Definitions =====

  - Bene-Jaakan

===== Usage =====

Bene-jaakan.
