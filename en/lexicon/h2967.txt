====== H2967: טַרְפְּלַי (Ṭarpᵉlay) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a name of foreign derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a **Tarpelite** (collectively) or inhabitants of Tarpel, a place in Assyria

===== Definitions =====

  - Tarpelite

===== Usage =====

Tarpelites. y
