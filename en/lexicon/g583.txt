====== G583: ἀπογράφω (apográphō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g1125|G1125]];

===== Meaning =====

to write off (a copy or list), i.e. enrol

===== Usage =====

tax, write.
