====== H5550: סֹלְלָה (çôlᵉlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; active participle feminine of [[en:lexicon:h5549|H5549]], but used passively;

===== Meaning =====

a military **mound**, i.e. rampart of **besiegers**

===== Definitions =====

  - mound
  - besiegers

===== Usage =====

bank, mount.
