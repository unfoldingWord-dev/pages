====== H7233: רְבָבָה (rᵉbâbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7231|H7231]];

===== Meaning =====

**abundance** (in number), i.e. (specifically) a **myriad** (whether definite or indefinite)

===== Definitions =====

  - abundance
  - myriad

===== Usage =====

many, million, × multiply, ten thousand.
