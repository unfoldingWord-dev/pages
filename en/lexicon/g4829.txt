====== G4829: συμμερίζομαι (symmerízomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3307|G3307]];

===== Meaning =====

to share jointly, i.e. participate in

===== Usage =====

be partaker with.
