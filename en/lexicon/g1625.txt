====== G1625: ἐκτρέφω (ektréphō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g5142|G5142]];

===== Meaning =====

to rear up to maturity, i.e. (genitive case) to cherish or train

===== Usage =====

bring up, nourish.
