====== H4817: מֶרְכָּב (merkâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7392|H7392]];

===== Meaning =====

a **chariot**; also a **seat** (in a vehicle)

===== Definitions =====

  - chariot
  - seat

===== Usage =====

chariot, covering, saddle.
