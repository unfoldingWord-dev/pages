====== H2738: חָרוּל (chârûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; apparently, a passive participle of an unused root probably meaning to be prickly;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **pointed**, i.e. a **bramble** or other thorny weed

===== Definitions =====

  - pointed
  - bramble

===== Usage =====

nettle.
