====== H7197: קָשַׁשׁ (qâshash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to become sapless through drought; used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7179|H7179]];

===== Meaning =====

to **forage for straw**, **stubble** or **wood**; figuratively, to **assemble**

===== Definitions =====

  - forage for straw
  - stubble
  - wood
  - assemble

===== Usage =====

gather (selves) (together).
