====== G5050: τελείωσις (teleíōsis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5448|G5448]];

===== Meaning =====

(the act) completion, i.e. (of prophecy) verification, or (of expiation) absolution

===== Usage =====

perfection, performance.
