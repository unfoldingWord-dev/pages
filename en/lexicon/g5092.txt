====== G5092: τιμή (timḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5099|G5099]];

===== Meaning =====

a value, i.e. money paid, or (concretely and collectively) valuables; by analogy, esteem (especially of the highest degree), or the dignity itself

===== Usage =====

honour, precious, price, some.
