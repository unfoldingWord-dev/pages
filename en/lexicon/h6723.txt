====== H6723: צִיָּה (tsîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **parch**;

===== Meaning =====

**aridity**; concretely, a **desert**

===== Definitions =====

  - parch
  - aridity
  - desert

===== Usage =====

barren, drought, dry (land, place), solitary place, wilderness.
