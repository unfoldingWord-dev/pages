====== H4886: מָשַׁח (mâshach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **rub with oil**, i.e. to **anoint**; by implication, to **consecrate**; also to **paint**

===== Definitions =====

  - rub with oil
  - anoint
  - consecrate
  - paint

===== Usage =====

anoint, paint.
