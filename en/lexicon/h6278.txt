====== H6278: עֵת קָצִין (ʻÊth Qâtsîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6256|H6256]] and [[en:lexicon:h7011|H7011]]; **time of a judge**;

===== Meaning =====

**Eth-Katsin**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - time of a judge
  - Eth-Katsin

===== Usage =====

Ittahkazin (by including directive enclitic).
