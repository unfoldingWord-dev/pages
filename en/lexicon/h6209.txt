====== H6209: עָרַר (ʻârar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **bare**; figuratively, to **demolish**

===== Definitions =====

  - bare
  - demolish

===== Usage =====

make bare, break, raise up (perhaps by clerical error for raze), × utterly.
