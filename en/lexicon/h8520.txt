====== H8520: תֶּלַח (Telach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from an unused root meaning to dissever; **breach**;

===== Meaning =====

**Telach**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - breach
  - Telach

===== Usage =====

Telah.
