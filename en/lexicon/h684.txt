====== H684: אֹצֶם (ʼÔtsem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to be strong; strength (i.e. strong);

===== Meaning =====

**Otsem**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Otsem

===== Usage =====

Ozem.
