====== G2507: καθαιρέω (kathairéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g138|G138]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to lower (or with violence) demolish (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

cast (pull, put, take) down, destroy.
