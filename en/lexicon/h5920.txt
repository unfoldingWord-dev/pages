====== H5920: עַל (ʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5927|H5927]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, the **top**; specifically, the **Highest** (i.e. **God**); also (adverb) **aloft**, **to Jehovah**

===== Definitions =====

  - top
  - Highest
  - God
  - aloft
  - to Jehovah

===== Usage =====

above, high, most High.
