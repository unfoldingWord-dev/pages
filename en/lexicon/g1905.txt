====== G1905: ἐπερωτάω (eperōtáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g2065|G2065]];

===== Meaning =====

to ask for, i.e. inquire, seek

===== Usage =====

ask (after, questions), demand, desire, question.
