====== G476: ἀντίδικος (antídikos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and [[en:lexicon:g1349|G1349]];

===== Meaning =====

an opponent (in a lawsuit); specially, Satan (as the arch-enemy)

===== Usage =====

adversary.
