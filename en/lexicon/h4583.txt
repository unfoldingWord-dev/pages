====== H4583: מָעוֹן (mâʻôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (1 Chronicles 4:41), from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5772|H5772]];

===== Meaning =====

an **abode**, of God (the Tabernacle or the Temple), men (their home) or animals (their lair); hence, a **retreat** (asylum)

===== Definitions =====

  - abode
  - retreat

===== Usage =====

den, dwelling((-) place), habitation.
