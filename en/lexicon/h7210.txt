====== H7210: רֳאִי (rŏʼîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7200|H7200]];

===== Meaning =====

**sight**, whether abstractly (**vision**) or concretely (a **spectacle**)

===== Definitions =====

  - sight
  - vision
  - spectacle

===== Usage =====

gazingstock, look to, (that) see(-th).
