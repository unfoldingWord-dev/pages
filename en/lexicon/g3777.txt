====== G3777: οὔτε (oúte) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3756|G3756]] and [[en:lexicon:g5037|G5037]];

===== Meaning =====

not too, i.e. neither or nor; by analogy, not even

===== Usage =====

neither, none, nor (yet), (no, yet) not, nothing.
