====== H934: בֹּהֶרֶת (bôhereth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine active participle of the same as [[en:lexicon:h925|H925]];

===== Meaning =====

a **whitish spot on the skin**

===== Definitions =====

  - whitish spot on the skin

===== Usage =====

bright spot.
