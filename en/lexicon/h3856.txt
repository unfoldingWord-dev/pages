====== H3856: לָהַהּ (lâhahh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root meaning properly, to **burn**, i.e. (by implication)

===== Meaning =====

to **be rabid** (figuratively, insane); also (from the exhaustion of frenzy) to **languish**

===== Definitions =====

  - burn
  - be rabid
  - languish

===== Usage =====

faint, mad.
