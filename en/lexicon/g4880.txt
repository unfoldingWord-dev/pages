====== G4880: συναποθνήσκω (synapothnḗskō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g599|G599]];

===== Meaning =====

to decease (literally) in company with, or (figuratively), similarly to

===== Usage =====
