====== H8486: תֵּימָן (têymân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; denominative from [[en:lexicon:h3225|H3225]];

===== Meaning =====

the **south** (as being on the **right** hand of a person facing the east)

===== Definitions =====

  - south
  - right

===== Usage =====

south (side, -ward, wind).
