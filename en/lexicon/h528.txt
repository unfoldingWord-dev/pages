====== H528: אָמוֹן (ʼÂmôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

of Egyptian derivation; used only as an adjunct of [[en:lexicon:h4996|H4996]]

===== Meaning =====

**Amon** (i.e. Ammon or Amn), a deity of Egypt

===== Definitions =====

  - Amon

===== Usage =====

multitude, populous.
