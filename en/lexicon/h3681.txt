====== H3681: כָּסוּי (kâçûwy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h3680|H3680]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **covered**, i.e. (as noun) a **covering**

===== Definitions =====

  - covered
  - covering

===== Usage =====

covering.
