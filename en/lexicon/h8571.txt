====== H8571: תְּנוּךְ (tᵉnûwk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

perhaps from the same as [[en:lexicon:h594|H594]] through the idea of **protraction**;

===== Meaning =====

a **pinnacle**, i.e. **extremity**

===== Definitions =====

  - protraction
  - pinnacle
  - extremity

===== Usage =====

tip.
