====== H7194: קָשַׁר (qâshar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tie**, physically (**gird**, **confine**, **compact**) or mentally (in **love**, **league**)

===== Definitions =====

  - tie
  - gird
  - confine
  - compact
  - love
  - league

===== Usage =====

bind (up), (make a) conspire(-acy, -ator), join together, knit, stronger, work (treason).
