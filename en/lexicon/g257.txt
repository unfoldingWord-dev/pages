====== G257: ἅλων (hálōn) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the base of [[en:lexicon:g1507|G1507]];

===== Meaning =====

a threshing-floor (as rolled hard), i.e. (figuratively) the grain (and chaff, as just threshed)

===== Usage =====

floor.
