====== G360: ἀναλύω (analýō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g3089|G3089]];

===== Meaning =====

to break up, i.e. depart (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

depart, return.
