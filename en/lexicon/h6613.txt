====== H6613: פְּתַי (pᵉthay) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h6601|H6601]];

===== Meaning =====

**open**, i.e. (as noun) **width**

===== Definitions =====

  - open
  - width

===== Usage =====

breadth.
