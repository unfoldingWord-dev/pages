====== G677: ἀπρόσκοπος (apróskopos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4350|G4350]];

===== Meaning =====

actively, inoffensive, i.e. not leading into sin; passively, faultless, i.e. not led into sin

===== Usage =====

none (void of, without) offence.
