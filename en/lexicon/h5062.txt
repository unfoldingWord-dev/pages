====== H5062: נָגַף (nâgaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **push**, **gore**, **defeat**, **stub** (the toe), **inflict** (a disease)

===== Definitions =====

  - push
  - gore
  - defeat
  - stub
  - inflict

===== Usage =====

beat, dash, hurt, plague, slay, smite (down), strike, stumble, × surely, put to the worse.
