====== G2669: καταπονέω (kataponéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4192|G4192]];

===== Meaning =====

to labor down, i.e. wear with toil (figuratively, harass)

===== Usage =====

oppress, vex.
