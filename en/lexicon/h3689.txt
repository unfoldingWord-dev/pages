====== H3689: כֶּסֶל (keçel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3688|H3688]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **fatness**, i.e. by implication (literally) the **loin** (as the seat of the leaf fat) or (generally) the **viscera**; also (figuratively) **silliness** or (in a good sense) **trust**

===== Definitions =====

  - fatness
  - loin
  - viscera
  - silliness
  - trust

===== Usage =====

confidence, flank, folly, hope, loin.
