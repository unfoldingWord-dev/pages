====== H7034: קָלָה (qâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be light** (as implied in **rapid** motion), but figuratively, only (**be** [causatively, **hold**] **in contempt**)

===== Definitions =====

  - be light
  - rapid
  - be
  - hold
  - in contempt

===== Usage =====

base, contemn, despise, lightly esteem, set light, seem vile.
