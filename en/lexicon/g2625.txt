====== G2625: κατακλίνω (kataklínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2827|G2827]];

===== Meaning =====

to recline down, i.e. (specially) to take a place at table

===== Usage =====

(make) sit down (at meat).
