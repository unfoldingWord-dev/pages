====== G4879: συναπάγω (synapágō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g520|G520]];

===== Meaning =====

to take off together, i.e. transport with (seduce, passively, yield)

===== Usage =====

carry (lead) away with, condescend.
