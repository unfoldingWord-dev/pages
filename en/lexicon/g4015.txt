====== G4015: περιαστράπτω (periastráptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g797|G797]];

===== Meaning =====

to flash all around, i.e. envelop in light

===== Usage =====

shine round (about).
