====== G4315: προσάββατον (prosábbaton) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g4521|G4521]];

===== Meaning =====

a fore-sabbath, i.e. the Sabbath-eve

===== Usage =====

day before the sabbath.
