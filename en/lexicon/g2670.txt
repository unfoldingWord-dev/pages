====== G2670: καταποντίζω (katapontízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and a derivative of the same as [[en:lexicon:g4195|G4195]];

===== Meaning =====

to plunge down, i.e. submerge

===== Usage =====

drown, sink.
