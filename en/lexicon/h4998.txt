====== H4998: נָאָה (nâʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be at home**, i.e. (by implication) to be **pleasant** (or **suitable**), i.e. **beautiful**

===== Definitions =====

  - be at home
  - pleasant
  - suitable
  - beautiful

===== Usage =====

be beautiful, become, be comely.
