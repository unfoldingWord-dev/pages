====== H6931: קַדְמוֹנִי (qadmôwnîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6930|H6930]];

===== Meaning =====

(of time) **anterior** or (of place) **oriental**

===== Definitions =====

  - anterior
  - oriental

===== Usage =====

ancient, they that went before, east, (thing of) old.
