====== G1930: ἐπιδιορθόω (epidiorthóō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3717|G3717]];

===== Meaning =====

to straighten further, i.e. (figuratively) arrange additionally

===== Usage =====

set in order.
