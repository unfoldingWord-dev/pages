====== H2748: חַרְטֹם (charṭôm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h2747|H2747]];

===== Meaning =====

a **horoscopist** (as drawing magical lines or circles)

===== Definitions =====

  - horoscopist

===== Usage =====

magician.
