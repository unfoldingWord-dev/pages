====== H7736: שׁוּד (shûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **swell up**, i.e. figuratively (by implication of insolence) to **devastate**

===== Definitions =====

  - swell up
  - devastate

===== Usage =====

waste.
