====== H2898: טוּב (ṭûwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2895|H2895]];

===== Meaning =====

**good** (as a noun), in the widest sense, especially **goodness** (superlative concretely, the **best**), **beauty**, **gladness**, **welfare**

===== Definitions =====

  - good
  - goodness
  - best
  - beauty
  - gladness
  - welfare

===== Usage =====

fair, gladness, good(-ness, thing, -s), joy, go well with.
