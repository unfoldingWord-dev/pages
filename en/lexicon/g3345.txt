====== G3345: μετασχηματίζω (metaschēmatízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4976|G4976]];

===== Meaning =====

to transfigure or disguise; figuratively, to apply (by accommodation)

===== Usage =====

transfer, transform (self).
