====== H8598: תַּפּוּחַ (tappûwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5301|H5301]];

===== Meaning =====

an **apple** (from its fragrance), i.e. the **fruit** or the **tree** (probably includ. others of the pome order, as the quince, the orange, etc.)

===== Definitions =====

  - apple
  - fruit
  - tree

===== Usage =====

apple (tree). See also
