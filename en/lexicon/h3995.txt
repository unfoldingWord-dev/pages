====== H3995: מִבְדָּלָה (mibdâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h914|H914]];

===== Meaning =====

a **separation**, i.e. (concretely) a **separate place**

===== Definitions =====

  - separation
  - separate place

===== Usage =====

separate.
