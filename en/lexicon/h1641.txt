====== H1641: גָּרַר (gârar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **drag off roughly**; by implication, to **bring up the cud** (i.e. ruminate); by analogy, to **saw**

===== Definitions =====

  - drag off roughly
  - bring up the cud
  - saw

===== Usage =====

catch, chew, × continuing, destroy, saw.
