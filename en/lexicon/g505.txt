====== G505: ἀνυπόκριτος (anypókritos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5271|G5271]];

===== Meaning =====

undissembled, i.e. sincere

===== Usage =====

without dissimulation (hypocrisy), unfeigned.
