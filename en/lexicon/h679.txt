====== H679: אַצִּיל (ʼatstsîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h680|H680]] (in its primary sense of uniting);

===== Meaning =====

a **joint of the hand** (i.e. knuckle); also (according to some) a **party-wall** (Ezekiel 41:8)

===== Definitions =====

  - joint of the hand
  - party-wall

===== Usage =====

(arm) hole, great.
