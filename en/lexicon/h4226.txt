====== H4226: מְחַבְּרָה (mᵉchabbᵉrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2266|H2266]];

===== Meaning =====

a **joiner**, i.e. **brace** or **cramp**

===== Definitions =====

  - joiner
  - brace
  - cramp

===== Usage =====

coupling, joining.
