====== H6362: פָּטַר (pâṭar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cleave** or **burst through**, i.e. (causatively) to **emit**, whether literal or figurative (gape)

===== Definitions =====

  - cleave
  - burst through
  - emit

===== Usage =====

dismiss, free, let (shoot) out, slip away.
