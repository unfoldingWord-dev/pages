====== H3971: מאוּם (mʼûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

usually [[en:lexicon:|]]; as if passive participle from an unused root probably meaning to **stain**

===== Meaning =====

a **blemish** (physically or morally)

===== Definitions =====

  - stain
  - blemish

===== Usage =====

blemish, blot, spot.
