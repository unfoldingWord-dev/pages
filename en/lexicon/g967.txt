====== G967: Βηθφαγή (Bēthphagḗ) ======

===== Source =====

of Chaldee origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h01004|H01004]] and [[en:lexicon:h06291|H06291]]);

===== Meaning =====

fig-house; Beth-phage, a place in Palestine
