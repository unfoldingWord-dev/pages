====== H306: אַחְלָמָה (ʼachlâmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

perhaps from [[en:lexicon:h2492|H2492]] (and thus **dream-stone**);

===== Meaning =====

a gem, probably the **amethyst**

===== Definitions =====

  - dream-stone
  - amethyst

===== Usage =====

amethyst.
