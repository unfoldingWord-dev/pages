====== G4757: στρατιώτης (stratiṓtēs) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed derivative of the same as [[en:lexicon:g4756|G4756]];

===== Meaning =====

a camper-out, i.e. a (common) warrior (literally or figuratively)
