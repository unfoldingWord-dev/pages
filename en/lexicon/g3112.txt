====== G3112: μακράν (makrán) ======

===== Source =====

feminine accusative case singular of [[en:lexicon:g3117|G3117]] ([[en:lexicon:g3598|G3598]] being implied);

===== Meaning =====

at a distance (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

(a-)far (off), good (great) way off.
