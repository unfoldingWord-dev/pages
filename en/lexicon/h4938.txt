====== H4938: מִשְׁעֵנָה (mishʻênâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4937|H4937]];

===== Meaning =====

**support** (abstractly), i.e. (figuratively) **sustenance** or (concretely) a **walking-stick**

===== Definitions =====

  - support
  - sustenance
  - walking-stick

===== Usage =====
