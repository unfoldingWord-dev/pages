====== G1298: διαταράσσω (diatarássō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g5015|G5015]];

===== Meaning =====

to disturb wholly, i.e. agitate (with alarm)

===== Usage =====

trouble.
