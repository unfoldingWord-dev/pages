====== H8500: תֻּכִּי (tukkîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]];

===== Meaning =====

probably of foreign derivation; some **imported creature**, probably a **peacock**

===== Definitions =====

  - imported creature
  - peacock

===== Usage =====

peacock.
