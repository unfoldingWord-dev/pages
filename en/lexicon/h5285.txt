====== H5285: נַעֲצוּץ (naʻătsûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **prick**;

===== Meaning =====

probably a **brier**; by implication, a **thicket** of thorny bushes

===== Definitions =====

  - prick
  - brier
  - thicket

===== Usage =====

thorn.
