====== H6663: צָדַק (tsâdaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **make**) **right** (in a moral or forensic sense)

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - right

===== Usage =====

cleanse, clear self, (be, do) just(-ice, -ify, -ify self), (be turn to) righteous(-ness).
