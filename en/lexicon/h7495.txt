====== H7495: רָפָא (râphâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **mend** (by stitching), i.e. (figuratively) to **cure**

===== Definitions =====

  - mend
  - cure
