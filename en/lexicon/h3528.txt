====== H3528: כְּבָר (kᵉbâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3527|H3527]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **extent of time**, i.e. a **great while**; hence, **long ago**, **formerly**, **hitherto**

===== Definitions =====

  - extent of time
  - great while
  - long ago
  - formerly
  - hitherto

===== Usage =====

already, (seeing that which), now.
