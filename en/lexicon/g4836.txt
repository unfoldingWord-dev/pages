====== G4836: συμπαραγίνομαι (symparagínomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3854|G3854]];

===== Meaning =====

to be present together, i.e. to convene; by implication, to appear in aid

===== Usage =====

come together, stand with.
