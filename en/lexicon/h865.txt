====== H865: אֶתְמוֹל (ʼethmôwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably from [[en:lexicon:h853|H853]] or [[en:lexicon:h854|H854]] and [[en:lexicon:h4136|H4136]];

===== Meaning =====

**heretofore**; definitely **yesterday**

===== Definitions =====

  - heretofore
  - yesterday

===== Usage =====

  before (that) time,   heretofore, of late (old),   times past, yester(day).
