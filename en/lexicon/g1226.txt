====== G1226: διαβεβαιόομαι (diabebaióomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g950|G950]];

===== Meaning =====

to confirm thoroughly (by words), i.e. asseverate

===== Usage =====

affirm constantly.
