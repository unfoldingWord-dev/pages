====== H835: אֶשֶׁר (ʼesher) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h833|H833]];

===== Meaning =====

**happiness**; only in masculine plural construction as interjection, how **happy!**

===== Definitions =====

  - happiness
  - happy!

===== Usage =====

blessed, happy.
