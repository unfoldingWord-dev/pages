====== H831: אַשְׁקְלוֹן (ʼAshqᵉlôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:h8254|H8254]] in the sense of weighing-place (i.e. mart);

===== Meaning =====

**Ashkelon**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Ashkelon

===== Usage =====

Ashkelon, Askalon.
