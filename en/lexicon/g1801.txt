====== G1801: ἐνωτίζομαι (enōtízomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g3775|G3775]];

===== Meaning =====

to take in one's ear, i.e. to listen

===== Usage =====

hearken.
