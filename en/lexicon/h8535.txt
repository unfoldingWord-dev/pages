====== H8535: תָּם (tâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8552|H8552]];

===== Meaning =====

**complete**; usually (morally) **pious**; specifically, **gentle**, **dear**

===== Definitions =====

  - complete
  - pious
  - gentle
  - dear

===== Usage =====

coupled together, perfect, plain, undefiled, upright.
