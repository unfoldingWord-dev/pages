====== H2300: חָדַד (châdad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **make**) **sharp** or (figuratively) **severe**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - sharp
  - severe

===== Usage =====

be fierce, sharpen.
