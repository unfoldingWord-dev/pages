====== G2141: εὐπορέω (euporéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2090|G2090]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g4197|G4197]];

===== Meaning =====

(intransitively) to be good for passing through, i.e. (figuratively) have pecuniary means

===== Usage =====

ability.
