====== G5271: ὑποκρίνομαι (hypokrínomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g2919|G2919]];

===== Meaning =====

to decide (speak or act) under a false part, i.e. (figuratively) dissemble (pretend)

===== Usage =====

feign.
