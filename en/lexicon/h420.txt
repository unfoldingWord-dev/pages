====== H420: אֶלְדָּעָה (ʼEldâʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]] and [[en:lexicon:h3045|H3045]]; **God of knowledge**;

===== Meaning =====

**Eldaah**, a son of Midian

===== Definitions =====

  - God of knowledge
  - Eldaah

===== Usage =====

Eldaah.
