====== H6074: עֳפִי (ʻŏphîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h6073|H6073]];

===== Meaning =====

a **twig**; **bough**, i.e. (collectively) **foliage**

===== Definitions =====

  - twig
  - bough
  - foliage

===== Usage =====

leaves.
