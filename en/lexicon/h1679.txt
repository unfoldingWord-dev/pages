====== H1679: דֹּבֶא (dôbeʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (compare [[en:lexicon:h1680|H1680]]) (probably meaning to be sluggish, i.e. restful);

===== Meaning =====

**quiet**

===== Definitions =====

  - quiet

===== Usage =====

strength.
