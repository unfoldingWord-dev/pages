====== H1792: דָּכָא (dâkâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h1794|H1794]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **crumble**; transitively, to **bruise** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - crumble
  - bruise

===== Usage =====

beat to pieces, break (in pieces), bruise, contrite, crush, destroy, humble, oppress, smite.
