====== G1841: ἔξοδος (éxodos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g3598|G3598]];

===== Meaning =====

an exit, i.e. (figuratively) death

===== Usage =====
