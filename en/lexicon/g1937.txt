====== G1937: ἐπιθυμέω (epithyméō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g2372|G2372]];

===== Meaning =====

to set the heart upon, i.e. long for (rightfully or otherwise)

===== Usage =====

covet, desire, would fain, lust (after).
