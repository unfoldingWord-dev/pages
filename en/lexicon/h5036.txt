====== H5036: נָבָל (nâbâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5034|H5034]];

===== Meaning =====

**stupid**; **wicked** (especially **impious**)

===== Definitions =====

  - stupid
  - wicked
  - impious

===== Usage =====

fool(-ish, -ish man, -ish woman), vile person.
