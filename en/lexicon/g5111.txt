====== G5111: τολμάω (tolmáō) ======

===== Source =====

from τόλμα (tólma) (boldness; probably itself from the base of [[en:lexicon:g5056|G5056]] through
 the idea of extreme conduct);

===== Meaning =====

to venture (objectively or in act; while

===== Usage =====

be bold, boldly, dare, durst.
