====== H4166: מוּצָקָה (mûwtsâqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h3332|H3332]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **poured** out, i.e. a **casting** (of metal); by implication, a tube (as cast)

===== Definitions =====

  - poured
  - casting

===== Usage =====

when it was cast, pipe.
