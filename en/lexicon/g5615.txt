====== G5615: ὡσαύτως (hōsaútōs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5613|G5613]] and an adverb from [[en:lexicon:g846|G846]];

===== Meaning =====

as thus, i.e. in the same way

===== Usage =====

even so, likewise, after the same (in like) manner.
