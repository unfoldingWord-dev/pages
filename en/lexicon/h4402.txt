====== H4402: מִלֵּאת (millêʼth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4390|H4390]];

===== Meaning =====

**fulness**, i.e. (concretely) a **plump socket** (of the eye)

===== Definitions =====

  - fulness
  - plump socket

===== Usage =====

× fitly.
