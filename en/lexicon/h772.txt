====== H772: אֲרַע (ʼăraʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h776|H776]];

===== Meaning =====

the **earth**; by implication (figuratively) **low**

===== Definitions =====

  - earth
  - low

===== Usage =====

earth, interior.
