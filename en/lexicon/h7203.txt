====== H7203: רֹאֶה (rôʼeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

active participle of [[en:lexicon:h7200|H7200]];

===== Meaning =====

a **seer** (as often rendered); but also (abstractly) a **vision**

===== Definitions =====

  - seer
  - vision

===== Usage =====

vision.
