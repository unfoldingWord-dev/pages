====== H1387: גֶּבַע (Gebaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1375|H1375]], a **hillock**;

===== Meaning =====

**Geba**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - hillock
  - Geba

===== Usage =====

Gaba, Geba, Gibeah.
