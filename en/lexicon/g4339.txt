====== G4339: προσήλυτος (prosḗlytos) ======

===== Source =====

from the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g4334|G4334]];

===== Meaning =====

an arriver from a foreign region, i.e. (specially), an acceder (convert) to Judaism ("proselyte")

===== Usage =====

proselyte.
