====== G1275: διαπαντός (diapantós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and the genitive case of [[en:lexicon:g3956|G3956]];

===== Meaning =====

through all time, i.e. (adverbially) constantly

===== Usage =====

alway(-s), continually.
