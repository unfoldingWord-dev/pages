====== H5605: סָפַף (çâphaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to snatch away, i.e. terminate; but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h5592|H5592]] (in the sense of a vestibule),

===== Meaning =====

to **wait at the threshold**

===== Definitions =====

  - wait at the threshold

===== Usage =====

be a doorkeeper.
