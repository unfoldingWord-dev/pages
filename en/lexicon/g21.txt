====== G21: ἀγαλλιάω (agalliáō) ======

===== Source =====

from agan (much) and [[en:lexicon:g242|G242]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to jump for joy, i.e. exult

===== Usage =====

be (exceeding) glad, with exceeding joy, rejoice (greatly).
