====== G4883: συναρμολογέω (synarmologéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and a derivative of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g719|G719]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]] (in its
 original sense of laying);

===== Meaning =====

to render close-jointed together, i.e. organize compactly

===== Usage =====

be fitly framed (joined) together.
