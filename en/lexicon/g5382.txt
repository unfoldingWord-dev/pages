====== G5382: φιλόξενος (philóxenos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5384|G5384]] and [[en:lexicon:g3581|G3581]];

===== Meaning =====

fond of guests, i.e. hospitable

===== Usage =====

given to (lover of, use) hospitality.
