====== H6085: עֶפְרוֹן (ʻEphrôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculinenoun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6081|H6081]]; **fawn-like**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ephron**, the name of a Canaanite and of two places in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - fawn-like
  - Ephron

===== Usage =====

Ephron, Ephrain (from the margin).
