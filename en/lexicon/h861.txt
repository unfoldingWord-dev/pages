====== H861: אַתּוּן (ʼattûwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) probably from the corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h784|H784]];

===== Meaning =====

probably a **fire-place**, i.e. **furnace**

===== Definitions =====

  - fire-place
  - furnace

===== Usage =====

furnace.
