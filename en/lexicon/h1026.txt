====== H1026: בֵּית הָעֲרָבָה (Bêyth hâ-ʻĂrâbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1004|H1004]] and [[en:lexicon:h6160|H6160]] with the article interposed; house of the Desert;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-ha-Arabah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-ha-Arabah

===== Usage =====

Beth-arabah.
