====== G1465: ἐγκόπτω (enkóptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g2875|G2875]];

===== Meaning =====

to cut into, i.e. (figuratively) impede, detain

===== Usage =====

hinder, be tedious unto.
