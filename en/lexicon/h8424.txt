====== H8424: תּוּגָה (tûwgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3013|H3013]];

===== Meaning =====

**depression** (of spirits); concretely a **grief**

===== Definitions =====

  - depression
  - grief

===== Usage =====

heaviness, sorrow.
