====== H605: אָנַשׁ (ʼânash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be frail**, **feeble**, or (figuratively) **melancholy**

===== Definitions =====

  - be frail
  - feeble
  - melancholy

===== Usage =====

desperate(-ly wicked), incurable, sick, woeful.
