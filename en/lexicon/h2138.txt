====== H2138: זָכוּר (zâkûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

properly, passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h2142|H2142]], but used for [[en:lexicon:h2145|H2145]];

===== Meaning =====

a **male** (of man or animals)

===== Definitions =====

  - male

===== Usage =====

males, men-children.
