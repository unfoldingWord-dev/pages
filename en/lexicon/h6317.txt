====== H6317: פּוּטִיאֵל (Pûwṭîyʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (probably meaning to disparage) and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; **contempt of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Putiel**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - contempt of God
  - Putiel

===== Usage =====

Putiel.
