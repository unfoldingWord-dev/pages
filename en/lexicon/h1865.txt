====== H1865: דְּרוֹר (dᵉrôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to **move rapidly**);

===== Meaning =====

**freedom**; hence, **spontaneity** of outflow, and so **clear**

===== Definitions =====

  - move rapidly
  - freedom
  - spontaneity
  - clear

===== Usage =====

liberty, pure.
