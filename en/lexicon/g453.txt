====== G453: ἀνόητος (anóētos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3539|G3539]];

===== Meaning =====

unintelligent; by implication, sensual

===== Usage =====

fool(-ish), unwise.
