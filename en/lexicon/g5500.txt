====== G5500: χειροτονέω (cheirotonéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a comparative of [[en:lexicon:g5495|G5495]] and τείνω (teínō) (to stretch);

===== Meaning =====

to be a hand-reacher or voter (by raising the hand), i.e. (generally) to select or appoint

===== Usage =====

choose, ordain.
