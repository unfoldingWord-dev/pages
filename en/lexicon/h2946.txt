====== H2946: טָפַח (ṭâphach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; or perhaps a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h2947|H2947]], from dandling on the palms

===== Meaning =====

to **flatten** out or **extend** (as a tent); figuratively, to **nurse** a child (as **promotive** of growth);

===== Definitions =====

  - flatten
  - extend
  - nurse
  - promotive

===== Usage =====

span, swaddle.
