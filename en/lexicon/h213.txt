====== H213: אוּץ (ʼûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **press**; (by implication) to **be close**, **hurry**, **withdraw**

===== Definitions =====

  - press
  - be close
  - hurry
  - withdraw

===== Usage =====

(make) haste(-n, -y), labor, be narrow.
