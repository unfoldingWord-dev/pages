====== H2026: הָרַג (hârag) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **smite** with deadly intent

===== Definitions =====

  - smite

===== Usage =====

destroy, out of hand, kill, murder(-er), put to (death), make (slaughter), slay(-er), × surely.
