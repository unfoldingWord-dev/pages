====== H2506: חֵלֶק (chêleq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2505|H2505]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **smoothness** (of the tongue); also an **allotment**

===== Definitions =====

  - smoothness
  - allotment

===== Usage =====

flattery, inheritance, part, × partake, portion.
