====== H4165: מוּצָק (mûwtsâq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5694|H5694]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **fusion**, i.e. literally, a **casting** (of metal); figuratively, a **mass** (of clay)

===== Definitions =====

  - fusion
  - casting
  - mass

===== Usage =====

casting, hardness.
