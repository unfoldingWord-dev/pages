====== H6555: פָּרַץ (pârats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **break** out (in many applications, direct and indirect, literal and figurative)

===== Definitions =====

  - break

===== Usage =====

× abroad, (make a) breach, break (away, down, -er, forth, in, up), burst out, come (spread) abroad, compel, disperse, grow, increase, open, press, scatter, urge.
