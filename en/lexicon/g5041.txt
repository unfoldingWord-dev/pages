====== G5041: τεκνογονέω (teknogonéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5043|G5043]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g1096|G1096]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a child-bearer, i.e. parent (mother)

===== Usage =====

bear children.
