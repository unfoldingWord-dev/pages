====== H7337: רָחַב (râchab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **broaden** (intransitive or transitive, literal or figurative)

===== Definitions =====

  - broaden

===== Usage =====

be an en-(make) large(-ing), make room, make (open) wide.
