====== G755: ἀρχιτρίκλινος (architríklinos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g746|G746]] and a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5140|G5140]] and [[en:lexicon:g2827|G2827]] (a dinner-bed, because
 composed of three couches);

===== Meaning =====

director of the entertainment

===== Usage =====

governor (ruler) of the feast.
