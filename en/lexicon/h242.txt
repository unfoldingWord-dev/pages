===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h238|H238]] and [[en:lexicon:h7609|H7609]]; plot of Sheerah (i.e. settled by him);

===== Meaning =====

**Uzzen-Sheerah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Uzzen-Sheerah

===== Usage =====

Uzzen-sherah.
