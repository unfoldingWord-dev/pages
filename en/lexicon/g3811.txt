====== G3811: παιδεύω (paideúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3816|G3816]];

===== Meaning =====

to train up a child, i.e. educate, or (by implication), discipline (by punishment)

===== Usage =====

chasten(-ise), instruct, learn, teach.
