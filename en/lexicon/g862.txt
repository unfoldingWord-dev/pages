====== G862: ἄφθαρτος (áphthartos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5351|G5351]];

===== Meaning =====

undecaying (in essence or continuance)

===== Usage =====

not (in-, un-)corruptible, immortal.
