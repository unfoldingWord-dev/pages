====== G1331: διερωτάω (dierōtáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g2065|G2065]];

===== Meaning =====

to question throughout, i.e. ascertain by interrogation

===== Usage =====

make enquiry for.
