====== H6937: קָדַר (qâdar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be ashy**, i.e. **dark**-colored; by implication, to **mourn** (in sackcloth or sordid garments)

===== Definitions =====

  - be ashy
  - dark
  - mourn

===== Usage =====

be black(-ish), be (make) dark(-en), × heavily, (cause to) mourn.
