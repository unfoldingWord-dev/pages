====== G2210: ζημιόω (zēmióō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2209|G2209]];

===== Meaning =====

to injure, i.e. (reflexively or passively) to experience detriment

===== Usage =====

be cast away, receive damage, lose, suffer loss.
