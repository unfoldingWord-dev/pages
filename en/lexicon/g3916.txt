====== G3916: παραχρῆμα (parachrēma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g5536|G5536]] (in its original sense);

===== Meaning =====

at the thing itself, i.e. instantly

===== Usage =====

forthwith, immediately, presently, straightway, soon.
