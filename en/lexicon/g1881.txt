====== G1881: ἐπανίσταμαι (epanístamai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g450|G450]];

===== Meaning =====

to stand up on, i.e. (figuratively) to attack

===== Usage =====

rise up against.
