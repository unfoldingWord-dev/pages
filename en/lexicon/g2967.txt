====== G2967: κωλύω (kōlýō) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g2849|G2849]];

===== Meaning =====

to estop, i.e. prevent (by word or act)

===== Usage =====

forbid, hinder, keep from, let, not suffer, withstand.
