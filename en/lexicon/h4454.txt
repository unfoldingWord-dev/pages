====== H4454: מָלַק (mâlaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **crack a joint**; by implication, to **wring the neck of a fowl** (without separating it)

===== Definitions =====

  - crack a joint
  - wring the neck of a fowl

===== Usage =====

wring off.
