====== H3957: לִשְׁכָּה (lishkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root of uncertain meaning;

===== Meaning =====

a **room** in a building (whether for storage, eating, or lodging)

===== Definitions =====

  - room

===== Usage =====

chamber, parlour. Compare
