====== H5358: נָקַם (nâqam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **grudge**, i.e. **avenge** or **punish**

===== Definitions =====

  - grudge
  - avenge
  - punish

===== Usage =====

avenge(-r, self), punish, revenge (self), × surely, take vengeance.
