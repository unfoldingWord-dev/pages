====== H4142: מוּסַבָּה (mûwçabbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4141|H4141]];

===== Meaning =====

a **reversal**, i.e. the **backside** (of a gem), **fold** (of a double-leaved door), **transmutation** (of a name)

===== Definitions =====

  - reversal
  - backside
  - fold
  - transmutation

===== Usage =====

being changed, inclosed, be set, turning.
