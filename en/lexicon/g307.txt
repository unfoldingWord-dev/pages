====== G307: ἀναβιβάζω (anabibázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to cause to go up, i.e. haul (a net)

===== Usage =====

draw.
