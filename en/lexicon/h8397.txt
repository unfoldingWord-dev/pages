====== H8397: תֶּבֶל (tebel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h1101|H1101]];

===== Meaning =====

**mixture**, i.e. **unnatural bestiality**

===== Definitions =====

  - mixture
  - unnatural bestiality

===== Usage =====

confusion.
