====== H7758: שׁוֹלָל (shôwlâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Micah 1:8), from [[en:lexicon:h7997|H7997]];

===== Meaning =====

**nude** (especially bare-foot); by implication, **captive**

===== Definitions =====

  - nude
  - captive

===== Usage =====

spoiled, stripped.
