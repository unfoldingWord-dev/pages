====== H7979: שֻׁלְחָן (shulchân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7971|H7971]];

===== Meaning =====

a **table** (as **spread** out); by implication, a **meal**

===== Definitions =====

  - table
  - spread
  - meal

===== Usage =====

table.
