====== H5643: סֵתֶר (çêther) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Deuteronomy 32:38), from [[en:lexicon:h5641|H5641]];

===== Meaning =====

a **cover** (in a good or a bad, a literal or a figurative sense)

===== Definitions =====

  - cover

===== Usage =====

backbiting, covering, covert, × disguise(-th), hiding place, privily, protection, secret(-ly, place).
