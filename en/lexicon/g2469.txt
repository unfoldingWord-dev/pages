====== G2469: Ἰσκαριώτης (Iskariṓtēs) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin (probably [[en:lexicon:h0377|H0377]] and [[en:lexicon:h07149|H07149]]);

===== Meaning =====

inhabitant of Kerioth; Iscariotes (i.e. Keriothite), an epithet of Judas the traitor

===== Usage =====

Iscariot.
