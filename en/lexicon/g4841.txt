====== G4841: συμπάσχω (sympáschō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3958|G3958]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to experience pain jointly or of the same kind (specially, persecution; to "sympathize")

===== Usage =====

suffer with.
