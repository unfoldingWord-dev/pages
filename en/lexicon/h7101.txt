====== H7101: קָצִין (qâtsîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7096|H7096]] in the sense of **determining**;

===== Meaning =====

a **magistrate** (as **deciding**) or other **leader**

===== Definitions =====

  - determining
  - magistrate
  - deciding
  - leader

===== Usage =====

captain, guide, prince, ruler. Compare
