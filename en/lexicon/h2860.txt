====== H2860: חָתָן (châthân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2859|H2859]];

===== Meaning =====

a **relative by marriage** (especially through the bride); figuratively, a **circumcised child** (as a species of religious espousal)

===== Definitions =====

  - relative by marriage
  - circumcised child

===== Usage =====

bridegroom, husband, son in law.
