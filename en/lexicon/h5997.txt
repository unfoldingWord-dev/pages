====== H5997: עָמִית (ʻâmîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from a primitive root meaning to **associate**;

===== Meaning =====

**companionship**; hence (concretely) a **comrade** or kindred man

===== Definitions =====

  - associate
  - companionship
  - comrade

===== Usage =====

another, fellow, neighbour.
