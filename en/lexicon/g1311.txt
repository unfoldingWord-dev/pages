====== G1311: διαφθείρω (diaphtheírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1225|G1225]] and [[en:lexicon:g5351|G5351]];

===== Meaning =====

to rot thoroughly, i.e. (by implication) to ruin (passively, decay utterly, figuratively, pervert)

===== Usage =====

corrupt, destroy, perish.
