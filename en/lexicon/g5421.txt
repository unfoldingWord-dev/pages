====== G5421: φρέαρ (phréar) ======

===== Source =====

of uncertain derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a hole in the ground (dug for obtaining or holding water or other purposes), i.e. a cistern or well; figuratively, an abyss (as a prison)

===== Usage =====

well, pit.
