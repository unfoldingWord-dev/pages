====== G5276: ὑπολήνιον (hypolḗnion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g3025|G3025]];

===== Meaning =====

vessel or receptacle under the press, i.e. lower wine-vat

===== Usage =====

winefat.
