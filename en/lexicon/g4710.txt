====== G4710: σπουδή (spoudḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4692|G4692]];

===== Meaning =====

"speed", i.e. (by implication) despatch, eagerness, earnestness

===== Usage =====

business, (earnest) care(-fulness), diligence, forwardness, haste.
