====== G4034: περιλάμπω (perilámpō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2989|G2989]];

===== Meaning =====

to illuminate all around, i.e. invest with a halo

===== Usage =====

shine round about.
