====== G3845: παραβαίνω (parabaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to go contrary to, i.e. violate a command

===== Usage =====

(by) transgress(-ion).
