====== G422: ἀνεπαίσχυντος (anepaíschyntos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of a
 compound of [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g153|G153]];

===== Meaning =====

not ashamed, i.e. irreprehensible:

===== Usage =====

--that needeth not to be ashamed.
