====== H5601: סַפִּיר (çappîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5608|H5608]];

===== Meaning =====

a **gem** (perhaps used for **scratching** other substances), probably the **sapphire**

===== Definitions =====

  - gem
  - scratching
  - sapphire

===== Usage =====

sapphire.
