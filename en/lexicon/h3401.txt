====== H3401: יָרִיב (yârîyb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7378|H7378]];

===== Meaning =====

literally **he will contend**; properly, adjective **contentious**; used as noun, an **adversary**

===== Definitions =====

  - he will contend
  - contentious
  - adversary

===== Usage =====

that content(-eth), that strive.
