====== H5085: נִדְנֶה (nidneh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5084|H5084]];

===== Meaning =====

a **sheath**; figuratively, the **body** (as the receptacle of the soul)

===== Definitions =====

  - sheath
  - body

===== Usage =====

body.
