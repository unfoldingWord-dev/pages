====== G652: ἀπόστολος (apóstolos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g649|G649]];

===== Meaning =====

a delegate; specially, an ambassador of the Gospel; officially a commissioner of Christ ("apostle") (with miraculous powers)

===== Usage =====

apostle, messenger, he that is sent.
