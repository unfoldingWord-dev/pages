====== G1075: γενεαλογέω (genealogéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1074|G1074]] and [[en:lexicon:g3056|G3056]];

===== Meaning =====

to reckon by generations, i.e. trace in genealogy

===== Usage =====

count by descent.
