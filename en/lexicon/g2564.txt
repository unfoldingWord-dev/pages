====== G2564: καλέω (kaléō) ======

===== Source =====

akin to the base of [[en:lexicon:g2753|G2753]];

===== Meaning =====

to "call" (properly, aloud, but used in a variety of applications, directly or otherwise)

===== Usage =====

bid, call (forth), (whose, whose sur-)name (was (called)).
