====== H548: אֲמָנָה (ʼămânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h543|H543]];

===== Meaning =====

something **fixed**, i.e. a **covenant**. an **allowance**

===== Definitions =====

  - fixed
  - covenant
  - allowance

===== Usage =====

certain portion, sure.
