====== G4245: πρεσβύτερος (presbýteros) ======

===== Source =====

comparative of πρέσβυς (présbys) (elderly);

===== Meaning =====

older; as noun, a senior; specially, an Israelite Sanhedrist (also figuratively, member of the celestial council) or Christian "presbyter"

===== Usage =====

 elder(-est), old.
