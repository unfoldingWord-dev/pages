====== H8160: שָׁעָה (shâʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h8159|H8159]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **look**, i.e. a **moment**

===== Definitions =====

  - look
  - moment

===== Usage =====

hour.
