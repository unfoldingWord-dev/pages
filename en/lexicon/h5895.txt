====== H5895: עַיִר (ʻayir) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5782|H5782]] in the sense of raising (i.e. bearing a burden);

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **young ass** (as just broken to a load); hence an **ass-colt**

===== Definitions =====

  - young ass
  - ass-colt

===== Usage =====

(ass) colt, foal, young ass.
