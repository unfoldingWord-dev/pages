====== H2715: חֹר (chôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (fully) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h2787|H2787]]; (compare [[en:lexicon:h2751|H2751]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, **white** or **pure** (from the **cleansing** or **shining** power of fire); hence (figuratively) **noble** (in rank)

===== Definitions =====

  - white
  - pure
  - cleansing
  - shining
  - noble

===== Usage =====

noble.
