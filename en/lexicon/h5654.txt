====== H5654: עֹבֵד אֱדוֹם (ʻÔbêd ʼĔdôwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the active participle of [[en:lexicon:h5647|H5647]] and [[en:lexicon:h123|H123]]; worker of Edom;

===== Meaning =====

**Obed-Edom**, the name of five Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Obed-Edom
