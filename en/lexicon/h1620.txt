====== H1620: גַּרְגַּר (gargar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

by reduplication from [[en:lexicon:h1641|H1641]];

===== Meaning =====

a **berry** (as if a pellet of **rumination**)

===== Definitions =====

  - berry
  - rumination

===== Usage =====

berry.
