====== H7617: שָׁבָה (shâbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **transport into captivity**

===== Definitions =====

  - transport into captivity

===== Usage =====

(bring away, carry, carry away, lead, lead away, take) captive(-s), drive (take) away.
