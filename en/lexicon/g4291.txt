====== G4291: προΐστημι (proḯstēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]];

===== Meaning =====

to stand before, i.e. (in rank) to preside, or (by implication) to practise

===== Usage =====

maintain, be over, rule.
