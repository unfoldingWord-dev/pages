====== G1245: διακαθαρίζω (diakatharízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g2511|G2511]];

===== Meaning =====

to cleanse perfectly, i.e. (specially) winnow

===== Usage =====

thoroughly purge.
