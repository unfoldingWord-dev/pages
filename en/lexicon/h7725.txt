====== H7725: שׁוּב (shûwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **turn** back (hence, away) transitively or intransitively, literally or figuratively (not necessarily with the idea of **return** to the starting point); generally to **retreat**; often adverbial, **again**

===== Definitions =====

  - turn
  - return
  - retreat
  - again

===== Usage =====
