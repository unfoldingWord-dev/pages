====== H7854: שָׂטָן (sâṭân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7853|H7853]];

===== Meaning =====

an **opponent**; especially (with the article prefixed) **Satan**, the arch-enemy of good

===== Definitions =====

  - opponent
  - Satan

===== Usage =====

adversary, Satan, withstand.
