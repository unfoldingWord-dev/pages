====== G4945: συνωμοσία (synōmosía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3660|G3660]];

===== Meaning =====

a swearing together, i.e. (by implication) a plot

===== Usage =====

comspiracy.
