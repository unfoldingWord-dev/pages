====== G4008: πέραν (péran) ======

===== Source =====

apparently accusative case of an obsolete derivative of πείρω (peírō) (to
 "pierce");

===== Meaning =====

through (as adverb or preposition), i.e. across

===== Usage =====

beyond, farther (other) side, over.
