====== G4459: πῶς (pōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4226|G4226]];

===== Meaning =====

an interrogative particle of manner; in what way? (sometimes the question is indirect, how?); also as exclamation, how much!

===== Usage =====

how, after (by) what manner (means), that.
