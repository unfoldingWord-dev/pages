====== G4874: συναναμίγνυμι (synanamígnymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and a compound of [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g3396|G3396]];

===== Meaning =====

to mix up together, i.e. (figurative) associate with

===== Usage =====

(have, keep) company (with).
