====== H4799: מָרַח (mârach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **soften** by rubbing or pressure; hence (medicinally) to **apply** as an emollient

===== Definitions =====

  - soften
  - apply

===== Usage =====

lay for a plaister.
