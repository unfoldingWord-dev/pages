====== G5419: φράζω (phrázō) ======

===== Source =====

probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g5420|G5420]] through the idea of defining;

===== Meaning =====

to indicate (by word or act), i.e. (specially), to expound

===== Usage =====

 declare.
