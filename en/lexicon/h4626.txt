====== H4626: מַעַר (maʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6168|H6168]];

===== Meaning =====

**a nude place**, i.e. (literally) **the pudenda**, or (figuratively) **a vacant space**

===== Definitions =====

  - a nude place
  - the pudenda
  - a vacant space

===== Usage =====

nakedness, proportion.
