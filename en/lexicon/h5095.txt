====== H5095: נָהַל (nâhal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to run with a **sparkle**, i.e. **flow**; hence (transitively), to **conduct**, and (by inference) to **protect**, **sustain**

===== Definitions =====

  - sparkle
  - flow
  - conduct
  - protect
  - sustain

===== Usage =====

carry, feed, guide, lead (gently, on).
