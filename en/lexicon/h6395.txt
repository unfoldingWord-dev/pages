====== H6395: פָּלָה (pâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **distinguish** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - distinguish

===== Usage =====

put a difference, show marvellous, separate, set apart, sever, make wonderfully.
