====== H6751: צָלַל (tsâlal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of **hovering** over (compare [[en:lexicon:h6754|H6754]]));

===== Meaning =====

to **shade**, as twilight or an opaque object

===== Definitions =====

  - hovering
  - shade

===== Usage =====

begin to be dark, shadowing.
