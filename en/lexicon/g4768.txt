====== G4768: στυγνάζω (stygnázō) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g4767|G4767]];

===== Meaning =====

to render gloomy, i.e. (by implication) glower (be overcast with clouds, or sombreness of speech)

===== Usage =====

lower, be sad.
