====== G2818: κληρονόμος (klēronómos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2819|G2819]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g3551|G3551]] (in its original sense of partitioning,
 i.e. (reflexively) getting by apportionment);

===== Meaning =====

a sharer by lot, i.e. inheritor (literally or figuratively); by implication, a possessor

===== Usage =====

heir.
