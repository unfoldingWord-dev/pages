====== G5240: ὑπερεκχύνω (hyperekchýnō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]] and the alternate form of [[en:lexicon:g1632|G1632]];

===== Meaning =====

to pour out over, i.e. (passively) to overflow

===== Usage =====

run over.
