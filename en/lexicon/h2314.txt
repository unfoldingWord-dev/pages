====== H2314: חָדַר (châdar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **inclose** (as a room), i.e. (by analogy,) to **beset** (as in a siege)

===== Definitions =====

  - inclose
  - beset

===== Usage =====

enter a privy chamber.
