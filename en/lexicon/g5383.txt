====== G5383: φιλοπρωτεύω (philoprōteúō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5384|G5384]] and [[en:lexicon:g4413|G4413]];

===== Meaning =====

to be fond of being first, i.e. ambitious of distinction

===== Usage =====

love to have the preeminence.
