====== H5104: נָהָר (nâhâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5102|H5102]];

===== Meaning =====

a **stream** (including the sea; expectation the Nile, Euphrates, etc.); figuratively, **prosperity**

===== Definitions =====

  - stream
  - prosperity

===== Usage =====

flood, river.
