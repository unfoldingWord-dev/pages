====== G5506: χιλίαρχος (chilíarchos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5507|G5507]] and [[en:lexicon:g757|G757]];

===== Meaning =====

the commander of a thousand soldiers ("chiliarch"; i.e. colonel

===== Usage =====
