====== G4311: προπέμπω (propémpō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g3992|G3992]];

===== Meaning =====

to send forward, i.e. escort or aid in travel

===== Usage =====

accompany, bring (forward) on journey (way), conduct forth.
