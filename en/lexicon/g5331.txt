====== G5331: φαρμακεία (pharmakeía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5332|G5332]];

===== Meaning =====

medication ("pharmacy"), i.e. (by extension) magic (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====
