====== G581: ἀπογενόμενος (apogenómenos) ======

===== Source =====

past participle of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g1096|G1096]];

===== Meaning =====

absent, i.e. deceased (figuratively, renounced)

===== Usage =====
