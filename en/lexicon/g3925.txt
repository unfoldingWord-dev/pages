====== G3925: παρεμβολή (parembolḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g1685|G1685]];

===== Meaning =====

a throwing in beside (juxtaposition), i.e. (specially), battle-array, encampment or barracks (tower Antonia)

===== Usage =====

army, camp, castle.
