====== H5982: עַמּוּד (ʻammûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h5975|H5975]];

===== Meaning =====

a **column** (as **standing**); also a **stand**, i.e. platform

===== Definitions =====

  - column
  - standing
  - stand

===== Usage =====

× apiece, pillar.
