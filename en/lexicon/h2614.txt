====== H2614: חָנַק (chânaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2596|H2596]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **be narrow**; by implication, to **throttle**, or (reflex.) to **choke oneself to death** (by a rope)

===== Definitions =====

  - be narrow
  - throttle
  - choke oneself to death

===== Usage =====

hang self, strangle.
