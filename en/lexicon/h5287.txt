====== H5287: נָעַר (nâʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably identical with [[en:lexicon:h5286|H5286]], through the idea of the **rustling** of mane, which usually accompanies the lion's roar);

===== Meaning =====

to **tumble** about

===== Definitions =====

  - rustling
  - tumble

===== Usage =====

shake (off, out, self), overthrow, toss up and down.
