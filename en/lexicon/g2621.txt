====== G2621: κατάκειμαι (katákeimai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2749|G2749]];

===== Meaning =====

to lie down, i.e. (by implication) be sick; specially, to recline at a meal

===== Usage =====

keep, lie, sit at meat (down).
