====== H4385: מִכְתָּב (miktâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3789|H3789]];

===== Meaning =====

a thing **written**, the characters, or a document (letter, copy, edict, poem)

===== Definitions =====

  - written

===== Usage =====

writing.
