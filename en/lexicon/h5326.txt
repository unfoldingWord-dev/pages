====== H5326: נִצְבָּה (nitsbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h5324|H5324]];

===== Meaning =====

**fixedness**, i.e. **firmness**

===== Definitions =====

  - fixedness
  - firmness

===== Usage =====

strength.
