====== G4691: σπερμολόγος (spermológos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4690|G4690]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]];

===== Meaning =====

a seed-picker (as the crow), i.e. (figuratively) a sponger, loafer (specially, a gossip or trifler in talk)

===== Usage =====

babbler.
