====== H516: אַל תַּשְׁחֵת (ʼAl tashchêth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h408|H408]] and [[en:lexicon:h7843|H7843]];

===== Meaning =====

**'Thou must not destroy'**; probably the opening words to a popular song

===== Definitions =====

  - 'Thou must not destroy'

===== Usage =====

Al-taschith.
