====== G255: ἀλυσιτελής (alysitelḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and the base of [[en:lexicon:g3081|G3081]];

===== Meaning =====

gainless, i.e. (by implication) pernicious

===== Usage =====

unprofitable.
