====== H5429: סְאָה (çᵉʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to define;

===== Meaning =====

a **seah**, or certain measure (as **determinative**) for grain

===== Definitions =====

  - seah
  - determinative

===== Usage =====

measure.
