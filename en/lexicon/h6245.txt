====== H6245: עָשַׁת (ʻâshath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

probably to be **sleek**, i.e. **glossy**; hence (through the idea of **polishing**) to **excogitate** (as if **forming** in the mind)

===== Definitions =====

  - sleek
  - glossy
  - polishing
  - excogitate
  - forming

===== Usage =====

shine, think.
