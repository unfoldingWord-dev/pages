====== H6890: צֶרֶת הַשַּׁחַר (Tsereth hash-Shachar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6889|H6889]] and [[en:lexicon:h7837|H7837]] with the article interposed; **splendor of the dawn**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tsereth-hash-Shachar**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - splendor of the dawn
  - Tsereth-hash-Shachar

===== Usage =====

Zareth-shahar.
