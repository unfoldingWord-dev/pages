====== H4147: מוֹסֵר (môwçêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

also (in plural) feminine [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h3256|H3256]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **chastisement**, i.e. (by implication) a **halter**; figuratively, **restraint**

===== Definitions =====

  - chastisement
  - halter
  - restraint

===== Usage =====

band, bond.
