====== H4525: מַסְגֵּר (maçgêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5462|H5462]];

===== Meaning =====

a **fastener**, i.e. (of a person) a **smith**, (of a thing) a **prison**

===== Definitions =====

  - fastener
  - smith
  - prison

===== Usage =====

prison, smith.
