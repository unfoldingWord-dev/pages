====== H2675: חָצוֹר חֲדַתָּה (Châtsôwr Chădattâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2674|H2674]] and a Aramaizing form of the feminine of [[en:lexicon:h2319|H2319]] (compare [[en:lexicon:h2323|H2323]]);

===== Meaning =====

**new Chatsor**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - new Chatsor

===== Usage =====

Hazor, Hadattah (as if two places).
