====== H6848: צֶפַע (tsephaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **extrude**;

===== Meaning =====

a **viper** (as **thrusting** out the tongue, i.e. **hissing**)

===== Definitions =====

  - extrude
  - viper
  - thrusting
  - hissing

===== Usage =====

adder, cockatrice.
