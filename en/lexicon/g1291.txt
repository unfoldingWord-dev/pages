====== G1291: διαστέλλομαι (diastéllomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g4724|G4724]];

===== Meaning =====

to set (oneself) apart (figuratively, distinguish), i.e. (by implication) to enjoin

===== Usage =====

charge, that which was (give) commanded(-ment).
