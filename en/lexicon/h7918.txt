====== H7918: שָׁכַךְ (shâkak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **weave** (i.e. lay) a **trap**; figuratively, (through the idea of secreting) to **allay** (passions; physically, abate a flood)

===== Definitions =====

  - weave
  - trap
  - allay

===== Usage =====

appease, assuage, make to cease, pacify, set.
