===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h6706|H6706]];

===== Meaning =====

a **parched region**, i.e. the **desert**

===== Definitions =====

  - parched region
  - desert

===== Usage =====

dry land.
