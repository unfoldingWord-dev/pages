====== G1821: ἐξαποστέλλω (exapostéllō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g649|G649]];

===== Meaning =====

to send away forth, i.e. (on a mission) to despatch, or (peremptorily) to dismiss

===== Usage =====

send (away, forth, out).
