====== H1466: גֵּוָה (gêvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h1465|H1465]];

===== Meaning =====

**exaltation**; (figuratively) **arrogance**

===== Definitions =====

  - exaltation
  - arrogance

===== Usage =====

lifting up, pride.
