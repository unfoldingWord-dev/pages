====== G4687: σπείρω (speírō) ======

===== Source =====

probably strengthened from [[en:lexicon:g4685|G4685]] (through the idea of extending);

===== Meaning =====

to scatter, i.e. sow (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

sow(- er), receive seed.
