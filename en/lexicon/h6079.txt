====== H6079: עַפְעַף (ʻaphʻaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5774|H5774]];

===== Meaning =====

an **eyelash** (as fluttering); figuratively, **morning ray**

===== Definitions =====

  - eyelash
  - morning ray

===== Usage =====

dawning, eye-lid.
