====== H4718: מַקֶּבֶת (maqqebeth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5344|H5344]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **perforator**, i.e. a **hammer** (as **piercing**); also (intransitively) a **perforation**, i.e. a **quarry**

===== Definitions =====

  - perforator
  - hammer
  - piercing
  - perforation
  - quarry

===== Usage =====

hammer, hole.
