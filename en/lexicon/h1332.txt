====== H1332: בִּתְיָה (Bithyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1323|H1323]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; daughter (i.e. worshipper) of Jah;

===== Meaning =====

**Bithjah**, an Egyptian woman

===== Definitions =====

  - Bithjah

===== Usage =====

Bithiah.
