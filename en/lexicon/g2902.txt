====== G2902: κρατέω (kratéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2904|G2904]];

===== Meaning =====

to use strength, i.e. seize or retain (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

hold (by, fast), keep, lay hand (hold) on, obtain, retain, take (by).
