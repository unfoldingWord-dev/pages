====== G4638: σκήνωμα (skḗnōma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4637|G4637]];

===== Meaning =====

an encampment, i.e. (figuratively) the Temple (as God's residence), the body (as a tenement for the soul)

===== Usage =====

 tabernacle.
