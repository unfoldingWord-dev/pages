====== G1286: διασείω (diaseíō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g4579|G4579]];

===== Meaning =====

to shake thoroughly, i.e. (figuratively) to intimidate

===== Usage =====

do violence to.
