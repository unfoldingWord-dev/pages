====== G410: ἀνέγκλητος (anénklētos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1458|G1458]];

===== Meaning =====

unaccused, i.e. (by implication) irreproachable

===== Usage =====

blameless.
