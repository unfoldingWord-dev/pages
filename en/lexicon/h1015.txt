====== H1015: בֵּית דִּבְלָתַיִם (Bêyth Diblâthayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1004|H1004]] and the dual of [[en:lexicon:h1690|H1690]]; house of (the) two figcakes;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-Diblathajim**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-Diblathajim

===== Usage =====

Beth-diblathaim.
