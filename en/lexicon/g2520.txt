====== G2520: καθήκω (kathḗkō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2240|G2240]];

===== Meaning =====

to reach to, i.e. (neuter of present active participle, figuratively as adjective) becoming

===== Usage =====

 convenient, fit.
