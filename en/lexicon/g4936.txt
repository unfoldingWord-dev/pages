====== G4936: συντρέχω (syntréchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g5143|G5143]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to rush together (hastily assemble) or headlong (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

run (together, with).
