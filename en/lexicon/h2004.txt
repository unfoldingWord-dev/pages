====== H2004: הֵן (hên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

personal pronoun

===== Source =====

feminine plural from [[en:lexicon:h1931|H1931]];

===== Meaning =====

**they** (only used when emphatic)

===== Definitions =====

  - they

===== Usage =====

× in, such like, (with) them, thereby, therein, (more than) they, wherein, in which, whom, withal.
