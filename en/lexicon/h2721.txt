====== H2721: חֹרֶב (chôreb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

a collaterally form of [[en:lexicon:h2719|H2719]];

===== Meaning =====

**drought** or **desolation**

===== Definitions =====

  - drought
  - desolation

===== Usage =====

desolation, drought, dry, heat, × utterly, waste.
