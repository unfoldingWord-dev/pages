====== H4171: מוּר (mûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **alter**; by implication, to **barter**, to **dispose of**

===== Definitions =====

  - alter
  - barter
  - dispose of

===== Usage =====

× at all, (ex-) change, remove.
