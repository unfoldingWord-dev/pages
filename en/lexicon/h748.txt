====== H748: אָרַךְ (ʼârak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causative, **make**) **long** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - long

===== Usage =====

defer, draw out, lengthen, (be, become, make, pro-) long,   (out-, over-) live, tarry (long).
