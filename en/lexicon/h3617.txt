====== H3617: כָּלָה (kâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3615|H3615]];

===== Meaning =====

a **completion**; adverb, **completely**; also **destruction**

===== Definitions =====

  - completion
  - completely
  - destruction

===== Usage =====

altogether, (be, utterly) consume(-d), consummation(-ption), was determined, (full, utter) end, riddance.
