====== G3574: νυχθήμερον (nychthḗmeron) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3571|G3571]] and [[en:lexicon:g2250|G2250]];

===== Meaning =====

a day-and-night, i.e. full day of twenty-four hours

===== Usage =====

night and day.
