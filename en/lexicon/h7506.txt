====== H7506: רֶפַח (Rephach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to sustain; **support**;

===== Meaning =====

**Rephach**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - support
  - Rephach

===== Usage =====

Rephah.
