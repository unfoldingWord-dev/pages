====== G1458: ἐγκαλέω (enkaléō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g2564|G2564]];

===== Meaning =====

to call in (as a debt or demand), i.e. bring to account (charge, criminate, etc.)

===== Usage =====

accuse, call in question, implead, lay to the charge.
