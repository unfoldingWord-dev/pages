====== H1639: גָּרַע (gâraʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **scrape** off; by implication, to **shave**, **remove**, **lessen**, **withhold**

===== Definitions =====

  - scrape
  - shave
  - remove
  - lessen
  - withhold

===== Usage =====

abate, clip, (di-) minish, do (take) away, keep back, restrain, make small, withdraw.
