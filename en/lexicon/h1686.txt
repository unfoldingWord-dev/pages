====== H1686: דִּבְיוֹן (dibyôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

in the margin for the textual reading, [[en:lexicon:|]]; both, (in the plural only and) of uncertain derivation,

===== Meaning =====

probably some cheap **vegetable**, perhaps a **bulbous root**

===== Definitions =====

  - vegetable
  - bulbous root

===== Usage =====

dove's dung.
