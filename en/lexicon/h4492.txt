====== H4492: מִנְהָרָה (minhârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5102|H5102]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **channel** or **fissure**, i.e. (by implication) a **cavern**

===== Definitions =====

  - channel
  - fissure
  - cavern

===== Usage =====

den.
