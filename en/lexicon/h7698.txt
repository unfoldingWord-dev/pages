====== H7698: שֶׁגֶר (sheger) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to **eject**;

===== Meaning =====

the **fœtus** (as finally **expelled**)

===== Definitions =====

  - eject
  - fœtus
  - expelled

===== Usage =====

that cometh of, increase.
