====== H7812: שָׁחָה (shâchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **depress**, i.e. **prostrate** (especially reflexive, in homage to royalty or God)

===== Definitions =====

  - depress
  - prostrate

===== Usage =====

bow (self) down, crouch, fall down (flat), humbly beseech, do (make) obeisance, do reverence, make to stoop, worship.
