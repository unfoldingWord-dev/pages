====== G4078: πήγνυμι (pḗgnymi) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of a primary verb (which in its simpler form occurs
 only as an alternate in certain tenses);

===== Meaning =====

to fix ("peg"), i.e. (specially) to set up (a tent)

===== Usage =====

pitch.
