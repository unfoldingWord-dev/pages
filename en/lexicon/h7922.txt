====== H7922: שֶׂכֶל (sekel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h7919|H7919]];

===== Meaning =====

**intelligence**; by implication, **success**

===== Definitions =====

  - intelligence
  - success

===== Usage =====

discretion, knowledge, policy, prudence, sense, understanding, wisdom, wise.
