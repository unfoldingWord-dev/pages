====== G4712: στάδιον (stádion) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]] (as fixed);

===== Meaning =====

a stade or certain measure of distance; by implication, a stadium or race-course

===== Usage =====

furlong, race.
