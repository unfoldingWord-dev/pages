====== G4014: περιαιρέω (periairéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g138|G138]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to remove all around, i.e. unveil, cast off (anchor); figuratively, to expiate

===== Usage =====

take away (up).
