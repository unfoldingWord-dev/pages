====== G447: ἀνίημι (aníēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and ἵημι (híēmi) (to send);

===== Meaning =====

to let up, i.e. (literally) slacken or (figuratively) desert, desist from

===== Usage =====

forbear, leave, loose.
