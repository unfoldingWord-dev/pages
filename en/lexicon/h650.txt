====== H650: אָפִיק (ʼâphîyq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h622|H622]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **containing**, i.e. a **tube**; also a **bed** or **valley** of a stream; also a **strong** thing or a **hero**

===== Definitions =====

  - containing
  - tube
  - bed
  - valley
  - strong
  - hero

===== Usage =====

brook, channel, mighty, river,   scale, stream, strong piece.
