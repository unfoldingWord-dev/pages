====== H1262: בָּרָה (bârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to select; also (as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h1250|H1250]]) to feed; also (as equivalent to [[en:lexicon:h1305|H1305]]) to render clear (Ecclesiastes 3:18)

===== Meaning =====

to **select**; to **feed**; to **render clear**

===== Definitions =====

  - select
  - feed
  - render clear

===== Usage =====

choose, (cause to) eat, manifest, (give) meat.
