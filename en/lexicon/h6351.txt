====== H6351: פָּחַח (pâchach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to batter out; but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h6341|H6341]],

===== Meaning =====

to **spread a net**

===== Definitions =====

  - spread a net

===== Usage =====

be snared.
