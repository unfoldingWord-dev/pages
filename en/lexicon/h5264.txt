====== H5264: נָסַס (nâçaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to gleam from afar, i.e. to be conspicuous as a signal; or rather perhaps a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h5251|H5251]] (and identical with [[en:lexicon:h5263|H5263]], through the idea of a flag as fluttering in the wind);

===== Meaning =====

to **raise a beacon**

===== Definitions =====

  - raise a beacon

===== Usage =====

lift up as an ensign.
