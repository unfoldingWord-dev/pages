====== H1713: דָּגַל (dâgal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **flaunt**, i.e. **raise a flag**; figuratively, to **be conspicuous**

===== Definitions =====

  - flaunt
  - raise a flag
  - be conspicuous

===== Usage =====

(set up, with) banners, chiefest.
