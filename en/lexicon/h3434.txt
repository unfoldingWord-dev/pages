====== H3434: יָשׇׁבְעָם (Yâshobʻâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7725|H7725]] and [[en:lexicon:h5971|H5971]]; **people will return**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jashobam**, the name of two or three Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - people will return
  - Jashobam

===== Usage =====

Jashobeam.
