====== H2310: חָדֵל (châdêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2308|H2308]];

===== Meaning =====

**vacant**, i.e. **ceasing** or **destitute**

===== Definitions =====

  - vacant
  - ceasing
  - destitute

===== Usage =====

he that forbeareth, frail, rejected.
