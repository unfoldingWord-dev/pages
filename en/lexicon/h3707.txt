====== H3707: כַּעַס (kaʻaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **trouble**; by implication, to **grieve**, **rage**, **be indignant**

===== Definitions =====

  - trouble
  - grieve
  - rage
  - be indignant

===== Usage =====

be angry, be grieved, take indignation, provoke (to anger, unto wrath), have sorrow, vex, be wroth.
