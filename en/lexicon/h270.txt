====== H270: אָחַז (ʼâchaz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **seize** (often with the accessory idea of holding in possession)

===== Definitions =====

  - seize

===== Usage =====

  be affrighted, bar, (catch, lay, take) hold (back), come upon, fasten, handle, portion, (get, have or take) possess(-ion).
