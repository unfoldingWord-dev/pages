====== H288: אֲחִימֶלֶךְ (ʼĂchîymelek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h251|H251]] and [[en:lexicon:h4428|H4428]]; brother of (the) king;

===== Meaning =====

**Achimelek**, the name of an Israelite and of a Hittite
