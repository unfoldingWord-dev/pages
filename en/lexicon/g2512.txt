====== G2512: καθαρισμός (katharismós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2511|G2511]];

===== Meaning =====

a washing off, i.e. (ceremonially) ablution, (morally) expiation

===== Usage =====

cleansing, + purge, purification(-fying).
