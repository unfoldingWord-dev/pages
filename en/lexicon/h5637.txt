====== H5637: סָרַר (çârar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **turn** away, i.e. (morally) **be refractory**

===== Definitions =====

  - turn
  - be refractory

===== Usage =====

× away, backsliding, rebellious, revolter(-ing), slide back, stubborn, withdrew.
