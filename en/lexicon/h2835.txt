====== H2835: חָשִׂף (châsiph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2834|H2834]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **drawn off**, i.e. **separated**; hence, a **small company** (as divided from the rest)

===== Definitions =====

  - drawn off
  - separated
  - small company

===== Usage =====

little flock.
