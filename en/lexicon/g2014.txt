====== G2014: ἐπιφαίνω (epiphaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]];

===== Meaning =====

to shine upon, i.e. become (literally) visible or (figuratively) known

===== Usage =====

appear, give light.
