====== H1790: דַּךְ (dak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from an unused root (compare [[en:lexicon:h1794|H1794]]);

===== Meaning =====

**crushed**, i.e. (figuratively) **injured**

===== Definitions =====

  - crushed
  - injured

===== Usage =====

afflicted, oppressed.
