====== H6735: צִיר (tsîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6696|H6696]];

===== Meaning =====

a **hinge** (as **pressed** in turning); also a **throe** (as a phys. or mental **pressure**); also a **herald** or errand-doer (as **constrained** by the principal)

===== Definitions =====

  - hinge
  - pressed
  - throe
  - pressure
  - herald
  - constrained

===== Usage =====

ambassador, hinge, messenger, pain, pang, sorrow. Compare
