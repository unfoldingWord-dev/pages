====== H7411: רָמָה (râmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **hurl**; specifically, to **shoot**; figuratively, to **delude** or **betray** (as if causing to fall)

===== Definitions =====

  - hurl
  - shoot
  - delude
  - betray

===== Usage =====

beguile, betray, (bow-) man, carry, deceive, throw.
