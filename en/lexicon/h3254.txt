====== H3254: יָסַף (yâçaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **add** or **augment** (often adverbial, to **continue** to do a thing)

===== Definitions =====

  - add
  - augment
  - continue

===== Usage =====

add, × again, × any more, × cease, × come more,   conceive again, continue, exceed, × further, × gather together, get more, give more-over, × henceforth, increase (more and more), join, × longer (bring, do, make, much, put), × (the, much, yet) more (and more), proceed (further), prolong, put, be (strong-) er, × yet, yield.
