====== H2876: טַבָּח (ṭabbâch) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2873|H2873]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **butcher**; hence, a **lifeguardsman** (because he was acting as an executioner); also a **cook** (usually slaughtering the animal for food)

===== Definitions =====

  - butcher
  - lifeguardsman
  - cook

===== Usage =====

cook, guard.
