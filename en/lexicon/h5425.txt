====== H5425: נָתַר (nâthar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **jump**, i.e. **be** violently **agitated**; causatively, to **terrify**, **shake** off, **untie**

===== Definitions =====

  - jump
  - be
  - agitated
  - terrify
  - shake
  - untie

===== Usage =====

drive asunder, leap, (let) loose, × make, move, undo.
