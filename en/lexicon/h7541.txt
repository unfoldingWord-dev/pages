====== H7541: רַקָּה (raqqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h7534|H7534]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **thinness**, i.e. the **side of the head**

===== Definitions =====

  - thinness
  - side of the head

===== Usage =====

temple.
