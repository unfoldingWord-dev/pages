====== G4360: προσοχθίζω (prosochthízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and a form of ὀχθέω (ochthéō) (to be vexed with something irksome);

===== Meaning =====

to feel indignant at

===== Usage =====

be grieved at.
