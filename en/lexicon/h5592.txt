====== H5592: סַף (çaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5605|H5605]], in its original sense of containing;

===== Meaning =====

a **vestibule** (as a limit); also a **dish** (for holding blood or wine)

===== Definitions =====

  - vestibule
  - dish

===== Usage =====

bason, bowl, cup, door (post), gate, post, threshold.
