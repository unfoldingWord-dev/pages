====== H6009: עָמַק (ʻâmaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **make**) **deep** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - deep

===== Usage =====

(be, have, make, seek) deep(-ly), depth, be profound.
