====== G3640: ὀλιγόπιστος (oligópistos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3641|G3641]] and [[en:lexicon:g4102|G4102]];

===== Meaning =====

incredulous, i.e. lacking confidence (in Christ)

===== Usage =====

of little faith.
