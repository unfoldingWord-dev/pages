====== G536: ἀπαρχή (aparchḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g756|G756]];

===== Meaning =====

a beginning of sacrifice, i.e. the (Jewish) first-fruit (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

first-fruits.
