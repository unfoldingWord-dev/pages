====== H5193: נָטַע (nâṭaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **strike** in, i.e. **fix**; specifically, to **plant** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - strike
  - fix
  - plant

===== Usage =====

fastened, plant(-er).
