====== H76: אֲבַעְבֻּעָה (ʼăbaʻbuʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(by reduplication) from an unused root (meaning to belch forth);

===== Meaning =====

an **inflammatory pustule** (as eruption)

===== Definitions =====

  - inflammatory pustule

===== Usage =====

blains.
