====== H6776: צֶמֶד (tsemed) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====



===== Meaning =====

a **yoke** or **team** (i.e. pair); hence, an **acre** (i.e. day's task for a yoke of cattle to plough)

===== Definitions =====

  - yoke
  - team
  - acre

===== Usage =====

acre, couple, × together, two (donkeys), yoke (of oxen).
