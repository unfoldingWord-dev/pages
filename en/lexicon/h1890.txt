====== H1890: הַבְהָב (habhâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

by reduplication from [[en:lexicon:h3051|H3051]];

===== Meaning =====

**gift** (in sacrifice), i.e. **holocaust**

===== Definitions =====

  - gift
  - holocaust

===== Usage =====

offering.
