====== H8473: תַּחֲרָא (tachărâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2734|H2734]] in the original sense of [[en:lexicon:h2352|H2352]] or [[en:lexicon:h2353|H2353]];

===== Meaning =====

a linen **corslet** (as **white** or **hollow**)

===== Definitions =====

  - corslet
  - white
  - hollow

===== Usage =====

habergeon.
