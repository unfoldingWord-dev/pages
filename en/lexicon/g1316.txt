====== G1316: διαχωρίζομαι (diachōrízomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g5563|G5563]];

===== Meaning =====

to remove (oneself) wholly, i.e. retire

===== Usage =====

depart.
