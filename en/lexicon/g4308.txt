====== G4308: προοράω (prooráō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g3708|G3708]];

===== Meaning =====

to behold in advance, i.e. (actively) to notice (another) previously, or (middle voice) to keep in (one's own) view

===== Usage =====

foresee, see before.
