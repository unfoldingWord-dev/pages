====== H2132: זַיִת (zayith) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from an unused root (akin to [[en:lexicon:h2099|H2099]]);

===== Meaning =====

an **olive** (as yielding illuminating oil), the **tree**, the **branch** or the **berry**

===== Definitions =====

  - olive
  - tree
  - branch
  - berry

===== Usage =====

olive (tree, -yard), Olivet.
