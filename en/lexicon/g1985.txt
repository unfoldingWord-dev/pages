====== G1985: ἐπίσκοπος (epískopos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g4649|G4649]] (in the sense of [[en:lexicon:g1983|G1983]]);

===== Meaning =====

a superintendent, i.e. Christian officer in genitive case charge of a (or the) church (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

bishop, overseer.
