====== G923: Βαρσαβᾶς (Barsabâs) ======

===== Source =====

of Chaldee origin ([[en:lexicon:h01247|H01247]] and probably [[en:lexicon:h06634|H06634]]);

===== Meaning =====

son of Sabas (or Tsaba); Bar-sabas, the name of two Israelites

===== Usage =====

Barsabas.
