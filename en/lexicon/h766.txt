====== H766: אֹרֶן (ʼôren) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h765|H765]] (in the sense of **strength**);

===== Meaning =====

the **ash** tree (from its toughness)

===== Definitions =====

  - strength
  - ash

===== Usage =====

ash.
