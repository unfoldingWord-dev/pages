====== G3983: πεινάω (peináō) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g3993|G3993]] (through the idea of pinching toil; "pine");

===== Meaning =====

to famish (absolutely or comparatively); figuratively, to crave

===== Usage =====

be an hungered.
