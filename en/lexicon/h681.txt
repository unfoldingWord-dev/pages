====== H681: אֵצֶל (ʼêtsel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h680|H680]] (in the sense of joining);

===== Meaning =====

a **side**; (as a preposition) **near**

===== Definitions =====

  - side
  - near

===== Usage =====

at, (hard) by, (from) (beside), near (unto), toward, with. See also
