====== H5172: נָחַשׁ (nâchash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **hiss**, i.e. **whisper** a (magic) spell; generally, to **prognosticate**

===== Definitions =====

  - hiss
  - whisper
  - prognosticate

===== Usage =====

× certainly, divine, enchanter, (use) × enchantment, learn by experience, × indeed, diligently observe.
