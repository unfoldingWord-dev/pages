====== G4281: προέρχομαι (proérchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2064|G2064]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to go onward, precede (in place or time)

===== Usage =====

go before (farther, forward), outgo, pass on.
