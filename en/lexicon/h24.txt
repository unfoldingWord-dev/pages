====== H24: אָבִיב (ʼâbîyb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to **be tender**);

===== Meaning =====

**green**, i.e. a young **ear** of grain; hence, the name of the month **Abib** or Nisan

===== Definitions =====

  - be tender
  - green
  - ear
  - Abib

===== Usage =====

Abib, ear, green ears of corn (not maize).
