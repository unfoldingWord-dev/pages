====== H6779: צָמַח (tsâmach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sprout** (transitive or intransitive, literal or figurative)

===== Definitions =====

  - sprout

===== Usage =====

bear, bring forth, (cause to, make to) bud (forth), (cause to, make to) grow (again, up), (cause to) spring (forth, up).
