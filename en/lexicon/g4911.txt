====== G4911: συνεφίστημι (synephístēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2186|G2186]];

===== Meaning =====

to stand up together, i.e. to resist (or assault) jointly

===== Usage =====

rise up together.
