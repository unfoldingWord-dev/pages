====== G551: ἀπείραστος (apeírastos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3987|G3987]];

===== Meaning =====

untried, i.e. not temptable

===== Usage =====

not to be tempted.
