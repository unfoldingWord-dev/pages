====== H2871: טָבוּל (ṭâbûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h2881|H2881]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **dyed**, i.e. a **turban** (probably as of colored stuff)

===== Definitions =====

  - dyed
  - turban

===== Usage =====

dyed attire.
