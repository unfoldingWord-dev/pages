====== H7898: שַׁיִת (shayith) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7896|H7896]];

===== Meaning =====

**scrub** or **trash**, i.e. wild **growth** of weeds or briers (as if **put** on the field)

===== Definitions =====

  - scrub
  - trash
  - growth
  - put

===== Usage =====

thorns.
