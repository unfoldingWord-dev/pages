====== H5462: סָגַר (çâgar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **shut** up; figuratively, to **surrender**

===== Definitions =====

  - shut
  - surrender

===== Usage =====

close up, deliver (up), give over (up), inclose, × pure, repair, shut (in, self, out, up, up together), stop, × straitly.
