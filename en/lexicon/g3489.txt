====== G3489: ναυαγέω (nauagéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3491|G3491]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to be shipwrecked (stranded, "navigate"), literally or figuratively

===== Usage =====

make (suffer) shipwreck.
