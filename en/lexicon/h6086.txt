====== H6086: עֵץ (ʻêts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6095|H6095]];

===== Meaning =====

a **tree** (from its firmness); hence, **wood** (plural sticks)

===== Definitions =====

  - tree
  - wood

===== Usage =====

  carpenter, gallows, helve,   pine, plank, staff, stalk, stick, stock, timber, tree, wood.
