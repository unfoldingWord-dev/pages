====== G2146: εὐπροσωπέω (euprosōpéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and [[en:lexicon:g4383|G4383]];

===== Meaning =====

to be of good countenance, i.e. (figuratively) to make a display

===== Usage =====

make a fair show.
