====== H7324: רוּק (rûwq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pour** out (literally or figuratively), i.e. **empty**

===== Definitions =====

  - pour
  - empty

===== Usage =====

× arm, cast out, draw (out), (make) empty, pour forth (out).
