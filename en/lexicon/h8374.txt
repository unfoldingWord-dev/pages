====== H8374: תָּאַב (tâʼab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably identical with [[en:lexicon:h8373|H8373]] through the idea of puffing disdainfully at; compare [[en:lexicon:h340|H340]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **loathe** (morally)

===== Definitions =====

  - loathe

===== Usage =====

abhor.
