====== H2296: חָגַר (châgar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **gird** on (as a belt, armor, etc.)

===== Definitions =====

  - gird

===== Usage =====

be able to put on, be afraid, appointed, gird, restrain, × on every side.
