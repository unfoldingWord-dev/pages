====== G1170: δεῖνα (deîna) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:lexicon:g1171|G1171]] (through the idea of forgetting the
 name as fearful, i.e. strange);

===== Meaning =====

so and so (when the person is not specified)

===== Usage =====

such a man.
