====== G127: αἰδώς (aidṓs) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g1492|G1492]] (through the idea of
 downcast eyes);

===== Meaning =====

bashfulness, i.e. (towards men), modesty or (towards God) awe

===== Usage =====

reverence, shamefacedness.
