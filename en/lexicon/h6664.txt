====== H6664: צֶדֶק (tsedeq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6663|H6663]];

===== Meaning =====

the **right** (natural, moral or legal); also (abstractly) **equity** or (figuratively) **prosperity**

===== Definitions =====

  - right
  - equity
  - prosperity

===== Usage =====

× even, (× that which is altogether) just(-ice), (un-)right(-eous) (cause, -ly, -ness).
