====== H2449: חָכַם (châkam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root,

===== Meaning =====

to **be wise** (in mind, word or act)

===== Definitions =====

  - be wise

===== Usage =====

× exceeding, teach wisdom, be (make self, shew self) wise, deal (never so) wisely, make wiser.
