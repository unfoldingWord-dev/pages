====== H7937: שָׁכַר (shâkar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **become tipsy**; in a qualified sense, to **satiate** with a stimulating drink or (figuratively) **influence**

===== Definitions =====

  - become tipsy
  - satiate
  - influence
