====== H5998: עָמַל (ʻâmal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **toil**, i.e. **work severely and with irksomeness**

===== Definitions =====

  - toil
  - work severely and with irksomeness

===== Usage =====

(take) labour (in).
