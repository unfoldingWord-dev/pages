====== G592: ἀποδιορίζω (apodiorízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g3724|G3724]];

===== Meaning =====

to disjoin (by a boundary, figuratively, a party)

===== Usage =====

separate.
