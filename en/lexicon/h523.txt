====== H523: אֻמַּה (ʼummah) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h517|H517]];

===== Meaning =====

a **collection**, i.e. **community** of persons

===== Definitions =====

  - collection
  - community

===== Usage =====

nation, people.
