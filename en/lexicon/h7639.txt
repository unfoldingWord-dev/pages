====== H7639: שְׂבָכָה (sᵉbâkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h7638|H7638]];

===== Meaning =====

a **net-work**, i.e. (in hunting) a **snare**, (in architecture) a **ballustrade**; also a **reticulated ornament to a pillar**

===== Definitions =====

  - net-work
  - snare
  - ballustrade
  - reticulated ornament to a pillar

===== Usage =====

checker, lattice, network, snare, wreath(-enwork).
