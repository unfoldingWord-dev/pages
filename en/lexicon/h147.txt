====== H147: אִדַּר (ʼiddar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) intensive, from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h142|H142]];

===== Meaning =====

**ample**, i.e. a **threshing-floor**

===== Definitions =====

  - ample
  - threshing-floor

===== Usage =====

threshingfloor.
