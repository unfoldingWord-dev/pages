====== G1239: διαδίδωμι (diadídōmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g1325|G1325]];

===== Meaning =====

to give throughout a crowd, i.e. deal out; also to deliver over (as to a successor)

===== Usage =====

(make) distribute(-ion), divide, give.
