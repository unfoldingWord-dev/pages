====== H8250: שִׁקּוּי (shiqqûwy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8248|H8248]];

===== Meaning =====

a **beverage**; **moisture**, i.e. (figuratively) **refreshment**

===== Definitions =====

  - beverage
  - moisture
  - refreshment

===== Usage =====

drink, marrow.
