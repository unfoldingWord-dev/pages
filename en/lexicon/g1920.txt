====== G1920: ἐπιγίνομαι (epigínomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g1096|G1096]];

===== Meaning =====

to arrive upon, i.e. spring up (as a wind)

===== Usage =====
