====== H1027: בֵּית הָרָם (Bêyth hâ-Râm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1004|H1004]] and [[en:lexicon:h7311|H7311]] with the article interposed; house of the height;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-ha-Ram**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-ha-Ram

===== Usage =====

Beth-aram.
