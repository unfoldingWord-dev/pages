====== H5961: עֲלָמוֹת (ʻĂlâmôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

plural of [[en:lexicon:h5959|H5959]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **girls**, i.e. the **soprano** or female voice, perhaps **falsetto**

===== Definitions =====

  - girls
  - soprano
  - falsetto

===== Usage =====

Alamoth.
