====== G4637: σκηνόω (skēnóō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4636|G4636]];

===== Meaning =====

to tent or encamp, i.e. (figuratively) to occupy (as a mansion) or (specially), to reside (as God did in the Tabernacle of old, a symbol of protection and communion)

===== Usage =====

 dwell.
