====== H7663: שָׂבַר (sâbar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

erroneously [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Nehemiah 2:13,15), a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **scrutinize**; by implication (of watching) to **expect** (with hope and patience)

===== Definitions =====

  - scrutinize
  - expect

===== Usage =====

hope, tarry, view, wait.
