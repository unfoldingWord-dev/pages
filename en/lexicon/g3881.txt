====== G3881: παραλέγομαι (paralégomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]] (in its original sense);

===== Meaning =====

(specially), to lay one's course near, i.e. sail past

===== Usage =====

pass, sail by.
