====== G5457: φῶς (phōs) ======

===== Source =====

from an obsolete φάω (pháō) (to shine or make manifest, especially by rays;
 compare [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]], [[en:lexicon:g5346|G5346]]);

===== Meaning =====

luminousness (in the widest application, natural or artificial, abstract or concrete, literal or figurative)

===== Usage =====

fire, light.
