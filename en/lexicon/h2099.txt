====== H2099: זִו (Ziv) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from an unused root meaning to be prominent; properly, brightness (compare [[en:lexicon:h2122|H2122]]), i.e. (figuratively) the month of flowers;

===== Meaning =====

**Ziv** (corresponding to Ijar or May)

===== Definitions =====

  - Ziv

===== Usage =====

Zif.
