====== H6969: קוּן (qûwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **strike a musical note**, i.e. **chant** or **wail** (at a funeral)

===== Definitions =====

  - strike a musical note
  - chant
  - wail

===== Usage =====

lament, mourning woman.
