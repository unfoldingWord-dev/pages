====== G1282: διαπρίω (diapríō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g4249|G4249]];

===== Meaning =====

to saw asunder, i.e. (figuratively) to exasperate

===== Usage =====

cut (to the heart).
