====== H4522: מַס (maç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h4549|H4549]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **burden** (as causing to faint), i.e. a tax in the form of forced labor

===== Definitions =====

  - burden

===== Usage =====

discomfited, levy, task(-master), tribute(-tary).
