====== G3367: μηδείς (mēdeís) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3361|G3361]] and [[en:lexicon:g1520|G1520]];

===== Meaning =====

not even one (man, woman, thing)

===== Usage =====

any (man, thing), no (man), none, not (at all, any man, a whit), nothing, + without delay.
