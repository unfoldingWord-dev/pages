====== G4332: προσεδρεύω (prosedreúō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g1476|G1476]];

===== Meaning =====

to sit near, i.e. attend as a servant

===== Usage =====
