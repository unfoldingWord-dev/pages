====== H5068: נָדַב (nâdab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

**to impel**; hence, **to volunteer** (as a soldier), **to present spontaneously**

===== Definitions =====

  - to impel
  - to volunteer
  - to present spontaneously

===== Usage =====

offer freely, be (give, make, offer self) willing(-ly).
