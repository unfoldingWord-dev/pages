====== H7080: קָסַם (qâçam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **distribute**, i.e. **determine by lot** or **magical scroll**; by implication, to **divine**

===== Definitions =====

  - distribute
  - determine by lot
  - magical scroll
  - divine

===== Usage =====

divine(-r, -ation), prudent, soothsayer, use (divination).
