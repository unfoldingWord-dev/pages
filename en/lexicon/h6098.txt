====== H6098: עֵצָה (ʻêtsâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3289|H3289]];

===== Meaning =====

**advice**; by implication, **plan**; also **prudence**

===== Definitions =====

  - advice
  - plan
  - prudence

===== Usage =====

advice, advisement, counsel([-lor]), purpose.
