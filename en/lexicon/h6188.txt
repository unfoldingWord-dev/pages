====== H6188: עָרֵל (ʻârêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to strip; but used as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h6189|H6189]];

===== Meaning =====

to **expose** or **remove the prepuce**, whether literal (to go naked) or figurative (to refrain from using)

===== Definitions =====

  - expose
  - remove the prepuce

===== Usage =====

count uncircumcised, foreskin to be uncovered.
