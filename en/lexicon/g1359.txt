====== G1359: Διόσκουροι (Dióskouroi) ======

===== Source =====

from the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g2203|G2203]] and a form of the base of [[en:lexicon:g2877|G2877]];

===== Meaning =====

sons of Jupiter, i.e. the twins Dioscuri

===== Usage =====

Castor and Pollux.
