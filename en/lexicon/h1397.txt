====== H1397: גֶּבֶר (geber) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1396|H1396]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **valiant** man or **warrior**; generally, a **person** simply

===== Definitions =====

  - valiant
  - warrior
  - person

===== Usage =====

every one, man, × mighty.
