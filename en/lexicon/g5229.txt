====== G5229: ὑπεραίρομαι (hyperaíromai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]] and [[en:lexicon:g142|G142]];

===== Meaning =====

to raise oneself over, i.e. (figuratively) to become haughty

===== Usage =====

exalt self, be exalted above measure.
