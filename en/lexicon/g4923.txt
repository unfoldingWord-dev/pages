====== G4923: συνοδία (synodía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3598|G3598]] ("synod");

===== Meaning =====

companionship on a journey, i.e. (by implication), a caravan

===== Usage =====

company.
