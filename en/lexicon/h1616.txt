====== H1616: גֵּר (gêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (fully) geyr (gare); from [[en:lexicon:h1481|H1481]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **guest**; by implication, a **foreigner**

===== Definitions =====

  - guest
  - foreigner

===== Usage =====

alien, sojourner, stranger.
