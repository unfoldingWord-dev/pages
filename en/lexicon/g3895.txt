====== G3895: παραπίπτω (parapíptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g4098|G4098]];

===== Meaning =====

to fall aside, i.e. (figuratively) to apostatize

===== Usage =====

fall away.
