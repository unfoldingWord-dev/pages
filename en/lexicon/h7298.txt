====== H7298: רַהַט (rahaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to **hollow out**;

===== Meaning =====

a **channel** or watering-box; by resemblance a **ringlet** of hair (as forming parallel lines)

===== Definitions =====

  - hollow out
  - channel
  - ringlet

===== Usage =====

gallery, gutter, trough.
