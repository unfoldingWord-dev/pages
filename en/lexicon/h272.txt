====== H272: אֲחֻזָּה (ʼăchuzzâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle from [[en:lexicon:h270|H270]];

===== Meaning =====

something **seized**, i.e. a **possession** (especially of land)

===== Definitions =====

  - seized
  - possession

===== Usage =====

possession.
