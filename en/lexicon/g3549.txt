====== G3549: νομοθετέω (nomothetéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3550|G3550]];

===== Meaning =====

to legislate, i.e. (passively) to have (the Mosaic) enactments injoined, be sanctioned (by them)

===== Usage =====

establish, receive the law.
