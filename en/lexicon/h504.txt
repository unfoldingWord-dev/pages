====== H504: אֶלֶף (ʼeleph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h502|H502]];

===== Meaning =====

a **family**; also (from the sense of yoking or taming) an **ox** or **cow**

===== Definitions =====

  - family
  - ox
  - cow

===== Usage =====

family, kine, oxen.
