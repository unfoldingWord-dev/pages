====== H1497: גָּזַל (gâzal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pluck off**; specifically to **flay**, **strip** or **rob**

===== Definitions =====

  - pluck off
  - flay
  - strip
  - rob

===== Usage =====

catch, consume, exercise (robbery), pluck (off), rob, spoil, take away (by force, violence), tear.
