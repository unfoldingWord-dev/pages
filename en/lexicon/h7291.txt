====== H7291: רָדַף (râdaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **run after** (usually with hostile intent; figuratively [of time] **gone by**)

===== Definitions =====

  - run after
  - gone by

===== Usage =====

chase, put to flight, follow (after, on), hunt, (be under) persecute(-ion, -or), pursue(-r).
