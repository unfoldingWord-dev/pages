====== H1523: גִּיל (gîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or (by permutation) [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **spin** round (under the influence of any violent emotion), i.e. usually **rejoice**, or (as **cringing**) **fear**

===== Definitions =====

  - spin
  - rejoice
  - cringing
  - fear

===== Usage =====

be glad, joy, be joyful, rejoice.
