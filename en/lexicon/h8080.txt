====== H8080: שָׁמַן (shâman) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **shine**, i.e. (by analogy) **be** (causatively, **make**) **oily** or **gross**

===== Definitions =====

  - shine
  - be
  - make
  - oily
  - gross

===== Usage =====

become (make, wax) fat.
