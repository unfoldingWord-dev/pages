====== H6693: צוּק (tsûwq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **compress**, i.e. (figuratively) **oppress**, **distress**

===== Definitions =====

  - compress
  - oppress
  - distress

===== Usage =====

constrain, distress, lie sore, (op-) press(-or), straiten.
