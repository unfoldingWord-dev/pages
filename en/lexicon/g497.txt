====== G497: ἀντιστρατεύομαι (antistrateúomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and [[en:lexicon:g4754|G4754]];

===== Meaning =====

(figuratively) to attack, i.e. (by implication) destroy

===== Usage =====
