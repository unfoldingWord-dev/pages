====== H6014: עָמַר (ʻâmar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; specifically (as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h6016|H6016]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, apparently to **heap**; figuratively, to **chastise** (as if piling blows); to **gather grain**

===== Definitions =====

  - heap
  - chastise
  - gather grain

===== Usage =====

bind sheaves, make merchandise of.
