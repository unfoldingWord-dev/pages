====== H2008: הֵנָּה (hênnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2004|H2004]];

===== Meaning =====

**hither** or **thither** (but used both of place and time)

===== Definitions =====

  - hither
  - thither

===== Usage =====

here, hither(-to), now, on this (that) side,   since, this (that) way, thitherward,   thus far, to...fro,   yet.
