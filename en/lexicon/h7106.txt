====== H7106: קָצַע (qâtsaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **strip** off, i.e. (partially) **scrape**; by implication, to **segregate** (as an angle)

===== Definitions =====

  - strip
  - scrape
  - segregate

===== Usage =====

cause to scrape, corner.
