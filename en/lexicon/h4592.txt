====== H4592: מְעַט (mᵉʻaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h4591|H4591]];

===== Meaning =====

a **little** or **few** (often adverbial or comparative)

===== Definitions =====

  - little
  - few

===== Usage =====

almost (some, very) few(-er, -est), lightly, little (while), (very) small (matter, thing), some, soon, × very.
