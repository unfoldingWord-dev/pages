====== G278: ἀμεταμέλητος (ametamélētos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3338|G3338]];

===== Meaning =====

irrevocable

===== Usage =====

without repentance, not to be repented of.
