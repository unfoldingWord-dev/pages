====== H7771: שׁוֹעַ (shôwaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7768|H7768]] in the original sense of freedom;

===== Meaning =====

a **noble**, i.e. **liberal**, **opulent**; also (as noun in the derived sense) a **halloo**

===== Definitions =====

  - noble
  - liberal
  - opulent
  - halloo

===== Usage =====

bountiful, crying, rich.
