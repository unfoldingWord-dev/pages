====== H1779: דִּין (dîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (Job 19:29) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1777|H1777]];

===== Meaning =====

**judgement** (the suit, justice, sentence or tribunal); by implication also **strife**

===== Definitions =====

  - judgement
  - strife

===== Usage =====

cause, judgement, plea, strife.
