====== H8231: שָׁפַר (shâphar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **glisten**, i.e. (figuratively) **be** (causatively, **make**) **fair**

===== Definitions =====

  - glisten
  - be
  - make
  - fair

===== Usage =====

× goodly.
