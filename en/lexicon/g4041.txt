====== G4041: περιούσιος (perioúsios) ======

===== Source =====

from the present participle feminine of a compound of 4012 and [[en:lexicon:g1510|G1510]];

===== Meaning =====

being beyond usual, i.e. special (one's own)

===== Usage =====

peculiar.
