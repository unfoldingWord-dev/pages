====== G405: ἀνδραποδιστής (andrapodistḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g435|G435]] and [[en:lexicon:g4228|G4228]];

===== Meaning =====

an enslaver (as bringing men to his feet)

===== Usage =====

menstealer.
