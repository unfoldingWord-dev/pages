====== G4299: πρόκριμα (prókrima) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2919|G2919]];

===== Meaning =====

a prejudgment (prejudice), i.e. prepossession

===== Usage =====

prefer one before another.
