====== G4092: πίμπρημι (pímprēmi) ======

===== Source =====

a reduplicated and prolonged form of a
 primary πρέω (préō) ; which occurs only as an alternate in certain
 tenses);

===== Meaning =====

to fire, i.e. burn (figuratively and passively, become inflamed with fever)

===== Usage =====

be (X should have) swollen.
