====== G4594: σήμερον (sḗmeron) ======

===== Source =====

neuter (as adverb) of a presumed compound of the article [[en:lexicon:g3588|G3588]]  (t
 changed to s) and [[en:lexicon:g2250|G2250]];

===== Meaning =====

on the (i.e. this) day (or night current or just passed); generally, now (i.e. at present, hitherto)

===== Usage =====

this (to-)day.
