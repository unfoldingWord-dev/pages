====== H6207: עָרַק (ʻâraq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **gnaw**, i.e. (figuratively) **eat** (by hyberbole); also (participle) a **pain**

===== Definitions =====

  - gnaw
  - eat
  - pain

===== Usage =====

fleeing, sinew.
