====== G2549: κακία (kakía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2556|G2556]];

===== Meaning =====

badness, i.e. (subjectively) depravity, or (actively) malignity, or (passively) trouble

===== Usage =====

evil, malice(-iousness), naughtiness, wickedness.
