====== H8105: שֶׁמֶר (shemer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8104|H8104]];

===== Meaning =====

something **preserved**, i.e. the **settlings** (plural only) of wine

===== Definitions =====

  - preserved
  - settlings

===== Usage =====

dregs, (wines on the) lees.
