====== G5587: ψιθυρισμός (psithyrismós) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of ψίθος (psíthos) (a whisper; by implication, a slander;
 probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g5574|G5574]]);

===== Meaning =====

whispering, i.e. secret detraction

===== Usage =====

whispering.
