====== G1946: Ἐπικούρειος (Epikoúreios) ======

===== Source =====

from Ἐπίκουρος (Epíkouros) (compare [[en:lexicon:g1947|G1947]]) (a noted philosopher);

===== Meaning =====

an Epicurean or follower of Epicurus

===== Usage =====

Epicurean.
