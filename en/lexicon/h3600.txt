====== H3600: כִּיר (kîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

a form for [[en:lexicon:h3564|H3564]] (only in the dual);

===== Meaning =====

a **cooking range** (consisting of two parallel stones, across which the boiler is set)

===== Definitions =====

  - cooking range

===== Usage =====

ranges for pots.
