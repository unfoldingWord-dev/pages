====== H505: אֶלֶף (ʼeleph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

prop, the same as [[en:lexicon:h504|H504]];

===== Meaning =====

hence (the ox's head being the first letter of the alphabet, and this eventually used as a numeral) a **thousand**

===== Definitions =====

  - thousand

===== Usage =====

thousand.
