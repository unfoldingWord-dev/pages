====== H3794: כִּתִּי (Kittîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; patrial from an unused name denoting Cyprus (only in the plural);

===== Meaning =====

a **Kittite** or **Cypriote**; hence, an islander in general, i.e. the Greeks or Romans on the shores opposite Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Kittite
  - Cypriote

===== Usage =====

Chittim, Kittim.
