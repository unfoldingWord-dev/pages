====== H3577: כָּזָב (kâzâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3576|H3576]];

===== Meaning =====

**falsehood**; literally (untruth) or figuratively (idol)

===== Definitions =====

  - falsehood

===== Usage =====

deceitful, false, leasing,   liar, lie, lying.
