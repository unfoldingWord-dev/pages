====== H860: אָתוֹן (ʼâthôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:lexicon:h386|H386]] (in the sense of patience);

===== Meaning =====

a **female donkey** (from its docility)

===== Definitions =====

  - female donkey

===== Usage =====

(she) ass.
