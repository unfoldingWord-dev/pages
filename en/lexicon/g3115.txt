====== G3115: μακροθυμία (makrothymía) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g3116|G3116]];

===== Meaning =====

longanimity, i.e. (objectively) forbearance or (subjectively) fortitude

===== Usage =====

longsuffering, patience.
