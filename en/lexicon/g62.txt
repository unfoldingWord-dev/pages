====== G62: ἀγράμματος (agrámmatos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g1121|G1121]];

===== Meaning =====

unlettered, i.e. illiterate

===== Usage =====
