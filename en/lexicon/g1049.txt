====== G1049: γαζοφυλάκιον (gazophylákion) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1047|G1047]] and [[en:lexicon:g5438|G5438]];

===== Meaning =====

a treasure-house, i.e. a court in the temple for the collection-boxes

===== Usage =====

treasury.
