====== H6342: פָּחַד (pâchad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be startled** (by a sudden alarm); hence, to **fear** in general

===== Definitions =====

  - be startled
  - fear

===== Usage =====

be afraid, stand in awe, (be in) fear, make to shake.
