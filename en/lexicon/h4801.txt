====== H4801: מֶרְחָק (merchâq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

; from [[en:lexicon:h7368|H7368]];

===== Meaning =====

**remoteness**, i.e. (concretely) a **distant** place; often (adverbially) **from afar**

===== Definitions =====

  - remoteness
  - distant
  - from afar

===== Usage =====

(a-, dwell in, very) far (country, off). See also
