====== G2576: καμμύω (kammýō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g3466|G3466]];

===== Meaning =====

to shut down, i.e. close the eyes

===== Usage =====
