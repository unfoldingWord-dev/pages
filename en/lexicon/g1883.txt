====== G1883: ἐπάνω (epánō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g507|G507]];

===== Meaning =====

up above, i.e. over or on (of place, amount, rank, etc.)

===== Usage =====

above, more than, (up-)on, over.
