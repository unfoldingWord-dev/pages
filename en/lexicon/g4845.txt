====== G4845: συμπληρόω (symplēróō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g4137|G4137]];

===== Meaning =====

to implenish completely, i.e. (of space) to swamp (a boat), or (of time) to accomplish (passive, be complete)

===== Usage =====

(fully) come, fill up.
