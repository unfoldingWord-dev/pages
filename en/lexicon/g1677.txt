====== G1677: ἐλλογέω (ellogéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g3056|G3056]] (in the sense of account);

===== Meaning =====

to reckon in, i.e. attribute

===== Usage =====

impute, put on account.
