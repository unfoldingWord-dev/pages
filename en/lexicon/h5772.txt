====== H5772: עוֹנָה (ʻôwnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to **dwell** together;

===== Meaning =====

(sexual) **cohabitation**

===== Definitions =====

  - dwell
  - cohabitation

===== Usage =====

duty of marriage.
