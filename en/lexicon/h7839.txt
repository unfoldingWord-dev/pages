====== H7839: שַׁחֲרוּת (shachărûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7836|H7836]];

===== Meaning =====

a **dawning**, i.e. (figuratively) **juvenescence**

===== Definitions =====

  - dawning

youth.
