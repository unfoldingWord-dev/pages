====== H3814: לָאט (lâʼṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3813|H3813]] (or perhaps for active participle of [[en:lexicon:h3874|H3874]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, **muffled**, i.e. **silently**

===== Definitions =====

  - muffled
  - silently

===== Usage =====

softly.
