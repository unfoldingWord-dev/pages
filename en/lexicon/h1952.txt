====== H1952: הוֹן (hôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1951|H1951]] in the sense of [[en:lexicon:h202|H202]];

===== Meaning =====

**wealth**; by implication, **enough**

===== Definitions =====

  - wealth
  - enough

===== Usage =====

enough,   for nought, riches, substance, wealth.
