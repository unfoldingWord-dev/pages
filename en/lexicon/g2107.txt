====== G2107: εὐδοκία (eudokía) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g1380|G1380]];

===== Meaning =====

satisfaction, i.e. (subjectively) delight, or (objectively) kindness, wish, purpose

===== Usage =====

desire, good pleasure (will), X seem good.
