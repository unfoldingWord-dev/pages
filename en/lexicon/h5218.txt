====== H5218: נָכֵא (nâkêʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h5217|H5217]];

===== Meaning =====

**smitten**, i.e. (figuratively) **afflicted**

===== Definitions =====

  - smitten
  - afflicted

===== Usage =====

broken, stricken, wounded.
