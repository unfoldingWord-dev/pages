====== G1969: ἐπιπλήσσω (epiplḗssō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g4141|G4141]];

===== Meaning =====

to chastise, i.e. (with words) to upbraid

===== Usage =====

rebuke.
