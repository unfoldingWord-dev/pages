====== H8610: תָּפַשׂ (tâphas) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **manipulate**, i.e. **seize**; chiefly to **capture**, **wield**, specifically, to **overlay**; figuratively, to **use unwarrantably**

===== Definitions =====

  - manipulate
  - seize
  - capture
  - wield
  - overlay
  - use unwarrantably

===== Usage =====

catch, handle, (lay, take) hold (on, over), stop, × surely, surprise, take.
