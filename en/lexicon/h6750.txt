====== H6750: צָלַל (tsâlal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of vibration);

===== Meaning =====

to **tinkle**, i.e. **rattle together** (as the ears in reddening with shame, or the teeth in chattering with fear)

===== Definitions =====

  - tinkle
  - rattle together

===== Usage =====

quiver, tingle.
