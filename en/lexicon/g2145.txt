====== G2145: εὐπρόσεδρος (euprósedros) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and the same as [[en:lexicon:g4332|G4332]];

===== Meaning =====

sitting well towards, i.e. (figuratively) assiduous (neuter, diligent service)

===== Usage =====

X attend upon.
