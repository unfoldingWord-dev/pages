====== H7090: קִפּוֹד (qippôwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h7088|H7088]];

===== Meaning =====

a species of bird, perhaps the **bittern** (from its **contracted** form)

===== Definitions =====

  - bittern
  - contracted

===== Usage =====

bittern.
