====== H4696: מֵצַח (mêtsach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be **clear**, i.e. **conspicuous**;

===== Meaning =====

the **forehead** (as **open** and **prominent**)

===== Definitions =====

  - clear
  - conspicuous
  - forehead
  - open
  - prominent

===== Usage =====

brow, forehead,   impudent.
