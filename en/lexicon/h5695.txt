====== H5695: עֵגֶל (ʻêgel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5696|H5696]];

===== Meaning =====

a (male) **calf** (as **frisking** round), especially one nearly grown (i.e. a **steer**)

===== Definitions =====

  - calf
  - frisking
  - steer

===== Usage =====

bullock, calf.
