====== H6299: פָּדָה (pâdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sever**, i.e. **ransom**; generally to **release**, **preserve**

===== Definitions =====

  - sever
  - ransom
  - release
  - preserve

===== Usage =====

× at all, deliver, × by any means, ransom, (that are to be, let be) redeem(-ed), rescue, × surely.
