====== G2309: θέλω (thélō) ======

===== Source =====

apparently strengthened from the alternate form of [[en:lexicon:g138|G138]];

===== Meaning =====

to determine (as an active option from subjective impulse; whereas

===== Usage =====

desire, be disposed (forward), intend, list, love, mean, please, have rather, (be) will (have, -ling, - ling(-ly)).
