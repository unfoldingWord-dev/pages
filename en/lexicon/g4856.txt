====== G4856: συμφωνέω (symphōnéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4859|G4859]];

===== Meaning =====

to be harmonious, i.e. (figuratively) to accord (be suitable, concur) or stipulate (by compact)

===== Usage =====

agree (together, with).
