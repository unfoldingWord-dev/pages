====== G5275: ὑπολείπω (hypoleípō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5295|G5295]] and [[en:lexicon:g3007|G3007]];

===== Meaning =====

to leave under (behind), i.e. (passively) to remain (survive)

===== Usage =====

be left.
