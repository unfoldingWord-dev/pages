====== G5463: χαίρω (chaírō) ======

===== Source =====

a primary verb;

===== Meaning =====

to be "cheer"ful, i.e. calmly happy or well-off; impersonally, especially as salutation (on meeting or parting), be well

===== Usage =====

farewell, be glad, God speed, greeting, hall, joy(- fully), rejoice.
