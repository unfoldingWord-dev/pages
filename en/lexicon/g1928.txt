====== G1928: ἐπιδιατάσσομαι (epidiatássomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g1299|G1299]];

===== Meaning =====

to appoint besides, i.e. supplement (as a codicil)

===== Usage =====

add to.
