====== H5064: נָגַר (nâgar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **flow**; figuratively, to **stretch out**; causatively, to **pour out** or **down**; figuratively, to **deliver over**

===== Definitions =====

  - flow
  - stretch out
  - pour out
  - down
  - deliver over

===== Usage =====

fall, flow away, pour down (out), run, shed, spilt, trickle down.
