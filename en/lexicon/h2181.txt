====== H2181: זָנָה (zânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root [highly **fed** and therefore **wanton**];

===== Meaning =====

to **commit adultery** (usually of the female, and less often of simple fornication, rarely of involuntary ravishment); figuratively, to **commit idolatry** (the Jewish people being regarded as the spouse of Jehovah)

===== Definitions =====

  - fed
  - wanton
  - commit adultery
  - commit idolatry

===== Usage =====

(cause to) commit fornication, × continually, × great, (be an, play the) harlot, (cause to be, play the) whore, (commit, fall to) whoredom, (cause to) go a-whoring, whorish.
