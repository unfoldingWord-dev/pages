====== H4528: מִסְדְּרוֹן (miçdᵉrôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5468|H5468]];

===== Meaning =====

a **colonnade** or **internal portico** (from its rows of pillars)

===== Definitions =====

  - colonnade
  - internal portico

===== Usage =====

porch.
