====== G3542: νομή (nomḗ) ======

===== Source =====

feminine from the same as [[en:lexicon:g3551|G3551]];

===== Meaning =====

pasture, i.e. (the act) feeding (figuratively, spreading of a gangrene), or (the food) pasturage

===== Usage =====

X eat, pasture.
