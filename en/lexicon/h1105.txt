====== H1105: בֶּלַע (belaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1104|H1104]];

===== Meaning =====

a **gulp**; figuratively, **destruction**

===== Definitions =====

  - gulp
  - destruction

===== Usage =====

devouring, that which he hath swallowed up.
