====== H7551: רָקַם (râqam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **variegate** color, i.e. **embroider**; by implication, to **fabricate**

===== Definitions =====

  - variegate
  - embroider
  - fabricate

===== Usage =====

embroiderer, needlework, curiously work.
