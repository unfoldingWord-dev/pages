====== H4278: מֶחְקָר (mechqâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2713|H2713]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **scrutinized**, i.e. (by implication) a **recess**

===== Definitions =====

  - scrutinized
  - recess

===== Usage =====

deep place.
