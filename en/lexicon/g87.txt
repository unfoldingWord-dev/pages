====== G87: ἀδιάκριτος (adiákritos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1252|G1252]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, undistinguished, i.e. (actively) impartial

===== Usage =====

without partiality.
