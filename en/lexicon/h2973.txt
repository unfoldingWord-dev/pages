====== H2973: יָאַל (yâʼal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be slack**, i.e. (figuratively) to **be foolish**

===== Definitions =====

  - be slack
  - be foolish

===== Usage =====

dote, be (become, do) foolish(-ly).
