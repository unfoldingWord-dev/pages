====== H8615: תִּקְוָה (tiqvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6960|H6960]]; (compare [[en:lexicon:h6961|H6961]])

===== Meaning =====

literally a **cord** (as an **attachment**); figuratively, **expectancy**

===== Definitions =====

  - cord
  - attachment
  - expectancy

===== Usage =====

expectation(-ted), hope, live, thing that I long for.
