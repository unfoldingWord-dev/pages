====== H2992: יָבַם (yâbam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root of doubtful meaning; used only as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h2993|H2993]];

===== Meaning =====

to **marry** a (deceased) brother's widow

===== Definitions =====

  - marry

===== Usage =====

perform the duty of a husband's brother, marry.
