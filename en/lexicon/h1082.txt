====== H1082: בָּלַג (bâlag) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **break off** or **loose** (in a favorable or unfavorable sense), i.e. **desist** (from grief) or **invade** (with destruction)

===== Definitions =====

  - break off
  - loose
  - desist
  - invade

===== Usage =====

comfort, (recover) strength(-en).
