====== G2159: εὐτόνως (eutónōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and a derivative of τείνω (teínō) (to stretch);

===== Meaning =====

in a well-strung manner, i.e. (figuratively) intensely (in a good sense, cogently; in a bad one, fiercely)

===== Usage =====

mightily, vehemently.
