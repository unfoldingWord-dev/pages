====== H4325: מַיִם (mayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

dual of a primitive noun (but used in a singular sense);

===== Meaning =====

**water**; figuratively, **juice**; by euphemism, **urine**, **semen**

===== Definitions =====

  - water
  - juice
  - urine
  - semen

===== Usage =====

  piss, wasting, water(-ing, (-course, -flood, -spring)).
