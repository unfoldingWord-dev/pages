====== H3381: יָרַד (yârad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **descend** (literally, to **go downwards**; or conventionally to a lower region, as the shore, a boundary, the enemy, etc.; or figuratively, to **fall**); causatively, to **bring down** (in all the above applications)

===== Definitions =====

  - descend
  - go downwards
  - fall
  - bring down

===== Usage =====

× abundantly, bring down, carry down, cast down, (cause to) come(-ing) down, fall (down), get down, go(-ing) down(-ward), hang down, × indeed, let down, light (down), put down (off), (cause to, let) run down, sink, subdue, take down.
