====== H2580: חֵן (chên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2603|H2603]];

===== Meaning =====

**graciousness**, i.e. subjective (**kindness**, **favor**) or objective (**beauty**)

===== Definitions =====

  - graciousness
  - kindness
  - favor
  - beauty

===== Usage =====

favour, grace(-ious), pleasant, precious, (well-) favoured.
