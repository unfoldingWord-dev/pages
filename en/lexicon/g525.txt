====== G525: ἀπαλλάσσω (apallássō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g236|G236]];

===== Meaning =====

to change away, i.e. release, (reflexively) remove

===== Usage =====

deliver, depart.
