====== H7730: שׂוֹבֶךְ (sôwbek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

for [[en:lexicon:h5441|H5441]];

===== Meaning =====

a **thicket**, i.e. **interlaced branches**

===== Definitions =====

  - thicket
  - interlaced branches

===== Usage =====

thick boughs.
