====== H4063: מֶדֶו (medev) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **stretch**;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **extent**, i.e. **measure**; by implication, a **dress** (as measured)

===== Definitions =====

  - stretch
  - extent
  - measure
  - dress

===== Usage =====

garment.
