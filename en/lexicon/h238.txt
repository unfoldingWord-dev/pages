====== H238: אָזַן (ʼâzan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; probably to **expand**; but used only as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h241|H241]];

===== Meaning =====

to **broaden out the ear** (with the hand), i.e. (by implication) to **listen**

===== Definitions =====

  - expand
  - broaden out the ear
  - listen

===== Usage =====

give (perceive by the) ear, hear(-ken). See
