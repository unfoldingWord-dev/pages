====== G1459: ἐγκαταλείπω (enkataleípō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g2641|G2641]];

===== Meaning =====

to leave behind in some place, i.e. (in a good sense) let remain over, or (in a bad sense) to desert

===== Usage =====

forsake, leave.
