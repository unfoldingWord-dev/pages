====== G5575: ψευδομάρτυρ (pseudomártyr) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5571|G5571]] and a kindred form of [[en:lexicon:g3144|G3144]];

===== Meaning =====

a spurious witness, i.e. bearer of untrue testimony

===== Usage =====

false witness.
