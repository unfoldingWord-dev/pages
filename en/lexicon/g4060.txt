====== G4060: περιτίθημι (peritíthēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

to place around; by implication, to present

===== Usage =====

bestow upon, hedge round about, put about (on, upon), set about.
