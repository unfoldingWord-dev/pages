====== H1544: גִּלּוּל (gillûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1556|H1556]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **log** (as round); by implication, an **idol**

===== Definitions =====

  - log
  - idol

===== Usage =====

idol.
