====== G1415: δυνατός (dynatós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1410|G1410]];

===== Meaning =====

powerful or capable (literally or figuratively); neuter possible

===== Usage =====

able, could, (that is) mighty (man), possible, power, strong.
