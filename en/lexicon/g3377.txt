====== G3377: μηνύω (mēnýō) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the same base as [[en:lexicon:g3145|G3145]] and [[en:lexicon:g3415|G3415]] (i.e. μάω (máō), to strive);

===== Meaning =====

to disclose (through the idea of mental effort and thus calling to mind), i.e. report, declare, intimate

===== Usage =====

shew, tell.
