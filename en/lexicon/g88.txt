====== G88: ἀδιάλειπτος (adiáleiptos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]]
 and [[en:lexicon:g3007|G3007]];

===== Meaning =====

unintermitted, i.e. permanent

===== Usage =====

without ceasing, continual.
