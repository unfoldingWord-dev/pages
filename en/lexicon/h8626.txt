====== H8626: תָּקַן (tâqan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **equalize**, i.e. **straighten** (intransitive or transitive); figuratively, to **compose**

===== Definitions =====

  - equalize
  - straighten
  - compose

===== Usage =====

set in order, make straight.
