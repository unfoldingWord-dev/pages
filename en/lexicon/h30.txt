====== H30: אֲבִיהוּא (ʼĂbîyhûwʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h1931|H1931]]; father (i.e. worshipper) of Him (i.e. God);

===== Meaning =====

**Abihu**, a son of Aaron

===== Definitions =====

  - Abihu

===== Usage =====

Abihu.
