====== H3753: כַּרְכָּרָה (karkârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3769|H3769]];

===== Meaning =====

a **dromedary** (from its **rapid** motion as if dancing)

===== Definitions =====

  - dromedary
  - rapid

===== Usage =====

swift beast.
