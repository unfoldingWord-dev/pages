====== H5454: סַבְתָּא (Çabtâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably of foreign derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**Sabta** or **Sabtah**, the name of a son of Cush, and the country occupied by his posterity

===== Definitions =====

  - Sabta
  - Sabtah

===== Usage =====

Sabta, Sabtah.
