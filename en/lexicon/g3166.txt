====== G3166: μεγαλαυχέω (megalauchéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3173|G3173]] and αὐχέω (auchéō) (to boast; akin to [[en:lexicon:g837|G837]] and [[en:lexicon:g2744|G2744]]);

===== Meaning =====

to talk big, i.e. be grandiloquent (arrogant, egotistic)

===== Usage =====

boast great things.
