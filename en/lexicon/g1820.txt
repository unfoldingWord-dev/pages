====== G1820: ἐξαπορέομαι (exaporéomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g639|G639]];

===== Meaning =====

to be utterly at a loss, i.e. despond

===== Usage =====

(in) despair.
