====== H8041: שָׂמַאל (sâmaʼl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (denominative from [[en:lexicon:h8040|H8040]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **use the left hand** or **pass in that direction**

===== Definitions =====

  - use the left hand
  - pass in that direction

===== Usage =====

(go, turn) (on the, to the) left.
