====== H1518: גִּיחַ (gîyach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **gush forth** (as water), generally to **issue**

===== Definitions =====

  - gush forth
  - issue

===== Usage =====

break forth, labor to bring forth, come forth, draw up, take out.
