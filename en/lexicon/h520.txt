====== H520: אַמָּה (ʼammâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

prolonged from [[en:lexicon:h517|H517]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **a mother** (i.e. unit of measure, or the fore-arm (below the elbow), i.e. **a cubit**; also **a door-base** (as a bond of the entrance)

===== Definitions =====

  - a mother
  - a cubit
  - a door-base

===== Usage =====

cubit, + hundred (by exchange for
