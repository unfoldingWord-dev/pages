====== G5218: ὑπακοή (hypakoḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5219|G5219]];

===== Meaning =====

attentive hearkening, i.e. (by implication) compliance or submission

===== Usage =====

obedience, (make) obedient, obey(-ing).
