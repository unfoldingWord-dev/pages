====== G618: ἀπολαμβάνω (apolambánō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g2983|G2983]];

===== Meaning =====

to receive (specially, in full, or as a host); also to take aside

===== Usage =====

receive, take.
