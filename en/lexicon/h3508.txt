====== H3508: יֹתֶרֶת (yôthereth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine active participle of [[en:lexicon:h3498|H3498]];

===== Meaning =====

the **lobe** or **flap** of the liver (as if redundant or outhanging)

===== Definitions =====

  - lobe
  - flap

===== Usage =====

caul.
