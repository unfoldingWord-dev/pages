====== G5187: τυφόω (typhóō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5188|G5188]];

===== Meaning =====

to envelop with smoke, i.e. (figuratively) to inflate with self-conceit

===== Usage =====

high-minded, be lifted up with pride, be proud.
