====== H4127: מוּג (mûwg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **melt**, i.e. literally (to **soften**, flow down, **disappear**), or figuratively (to **fear**, **faint**)

===== Definitions =====

  - melt
  - soften
  - disappear
  - fear
  - faint

===== Usage =====

consume, dissolve, (be) faint(-hearted), melt (away), make soft.
