====== H1107: בִּלְעֲדֵי (bilʻădêy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

particle

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; constructive plural from [[en:lexicon:h1077|H1077]] and [[en:lexicon:h5703|H5703]], not till, i.e. (as preposition or adverb)

===== Meaning =====

**except**, **without**, **besides**

===== Definitions =====

  - except
  - without
  - besides

===== Usage =====

beside, not (in), save, without.
