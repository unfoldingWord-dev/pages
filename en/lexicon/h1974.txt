====== H1974: הִלּוּל (hillûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1984|H1984]] (in the sense of rejoicing);

===== Meaning =====

a **celebration of thanksgiving for harvest**

===== Definitions =====

  - celebration of thanksgiving for harvest

===== Usage =====

merry, praise.
