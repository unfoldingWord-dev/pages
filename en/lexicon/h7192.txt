====== H7192: קְשִׂיטָה (qᵉsîyṭâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (probably meaning to weigh out);

===== Meaning =====

an **ingot** (as definitely estimated and stamped for a coin)

===== Definitions =====

  - ingot

===== Usage =====

piece of money (silver).
