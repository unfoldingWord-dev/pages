====== H7393: רֶכֶב (rekeb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7392|H7392]];

===== Meaning =====

a **vehicle**; by implication, a **team**; by extension, **cavalry**; by analogy a **rider**, i.e. **the upper millstone**

===== Definitions =====

  - vehicle
  - team
  - cavalry
  - rider
  - the upper millstone

===== Usage =====

chariot, (upper) millstone, multitude (from the margin), wagon.
