====== H361: אֵילָם (ʼêylâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]] ; or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]] ; probably from [[en:lexicon:h352|H352]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pillar-space** (or colonnade), i.e. a **pale** (or portico)

===== Definitions =====

  - pillar-space
  - pale

===== Usage =====

arch.
