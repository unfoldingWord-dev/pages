====== H3526: כָּבַס (kâbaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **trample**; hence, to **wash** (properly, by stamping with the feet), whether literal (including the **fulling** process) or figurative

===== Definitions =====

  - trample
  - wash
  - fulling

===== Usage =====

fuller, wash(-ing).
