====== G1754: ἐνεργέω (energéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1756|G1756]];

===== Meaning =====

to be active, efficient

===== Usage =====

do, (be) effectual (fervent), be mighty in, shew forth self, work (effectually in).
