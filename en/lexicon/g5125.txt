====== G5125: τούτοις (toútois) ======

===== Source =====

dative case plural masculine or neuter of [[en:lexicon:g3778|G3778]];

===== Meaning =====

to (for, in, with or by) these (persons or things)

===== Usage =====

such, them, there(-in, -with), these, this, those.
