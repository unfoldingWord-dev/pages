====== H4058: מָדַד (mâdad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **stretch**; by implication, to **measure** (as if by **stretching** a line); figuratively, to **be extended**

===== Definitions =====

  - stretch
  - measure
  - stretching
  - be extended

===== Usage =====

measure, mete, stretch self.
