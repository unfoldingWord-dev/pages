====== G1893: ἐπεί (epeí) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g1487|G1487]];

===== Meaning =====

thereupon, i.e. since (of time or cause)

===== Usage =====

because, else, for that (then, -asmuch as), otherwise, seeing that, since, when.
