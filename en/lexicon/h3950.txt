====== H3950: לָקַט (lâqaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **pick** up, i.e. (generally) to **gather**; specifically, to **glean**

===== Definitions =====

  - pick
  - gather
  - glean

===== Usage =====

gather (up), glean.
