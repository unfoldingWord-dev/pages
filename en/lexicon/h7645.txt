====== H7645: שְׁבַנְיָה (Shᵉbanyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from the same as [[en:lexicon:h7644|H7644]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; **Jah has grown** (i.e. prospered);

===== Meaning =====

**Shebanjah**, the name of three or four Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has grown
  - Shebanjah

===== Usage =====

Shebaniah.
