====== G1456: ἐγκαίνια (enkaínia) ======

===== Source =====

neuter plural of a presumed compound from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g2537|G2537]];

===== Meaning =====

innovatives, i.e. (specially) renewal (of religious services after the Antiochian interruption)

===== Usage =====

dedication.
