====== G3914: παραχειμάζω (paracheimázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g5492|G5492]];

===== Meaning =====

to winter near, i.e. stay with over the rainy season

===== Usage =====
