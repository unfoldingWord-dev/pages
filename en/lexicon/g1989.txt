====== G1989: ἐπιστέλλω (epistéllō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g4724|G4724]];

===== Meaning =====

to enjoin (by writing), i.e. (genitive case) to communicate by letter (for any purpose)

===== Usage =====

write (a letter, unto).
