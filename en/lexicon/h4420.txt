====== H4420: מְלֵחָה (mᵉlêchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4414|H4414]] (in its denominative sense); understood as land ([[en:lexicon:h776|H776]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, **salted** (i.e. land), i.e. a **desert**

===== Definitions =====

  - salted
  - desert

===== Usage =====

barren land(-ness), salt (land).
