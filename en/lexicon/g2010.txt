====== G2010: ἐπιτρέπω (epitrépō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5157|G5157]];

===== Meaning =====

to turn over (transfer), i.e. allow

===== Usage =====

give leave (liberty, license), let, permit, suffer.
