====== H6571: פָּרָשׁ (pârâsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6567|H6567]]; (compare [[en:lexicon:h5483|H5483]])

===== Meaning =====

a **steed** (as stretched out to a vehicle, not single nor for mounting ); also (by implication) a **driver** (in a chariot), i.e. (collectively) **cavalry**

===== Definitions =====

  - steed
  - driver
  - cavalry

===== Usage =====

horseman.
