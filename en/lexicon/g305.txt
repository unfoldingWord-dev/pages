====== G305: ἀναβαίνω (anabaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to go up (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

arise, ascend (up), climb (go, grow, rise, spring) up, come (up).
