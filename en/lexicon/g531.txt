====== G531: ἀπαράβατος (aparábatos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3845|G3845]];

===== Meaning =====

not passing away, i.e. untransferable (perpetual)

===== Usage =====

 unchangeable.
