====== G3511: νεωκόρος (neōkóros) ======

===== Source =====

from a form of [[en:lexicon:g3485|G3485]] and κορέω (koréō) (to sweep);

===== Meaning =====

a temple-servant, i.e. (by implication) a votary

===== Usage =====

worshipper.
