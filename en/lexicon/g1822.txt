====== G1822: ἐξαρτίζω (exartízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g739|G739]];

===== Meaning =====

to finish out (time); figuratively, to equip fully (a teacher)

===== Usage =====

accomplish, thoroughly furnish.
