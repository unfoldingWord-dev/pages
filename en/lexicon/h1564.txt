====== H1564: גֹּלֶם (gôlem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1563|H1563]];

===== Meaning =====

a **wrapped** (and unformed mass, i.e. as the embryo)

===== Definitions =====

  - wrapped

===== Usage =====

substance yet being unperfect.
