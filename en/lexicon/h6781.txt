====== H6781: צָמִיד (tsâmîyd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6775|H6775]];

===== Meaning =====

a **bracelet** or **arm-clasp**; generally, a **lid**

===== Definitions =====

  - bracelet
  - arm-clasp
  - lid

===== Usage =====

bracelet, covering.
