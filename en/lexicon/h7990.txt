====== H7990: שַׁלִּיט (shallîyṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h7989|H7989]];

===== Meaning =====

**mighty**; abstractly, **permission**; concretely, a **premier**

===== Definitions =====

  - mighty
  - permission
  - premier

===== Usage =====

captain, be lawful, rule(-r).
