====== G2839: κοινός (koinós) ======

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]];

===== Meaning =====

common, i.e. (literally) shared by all or several, or (ceremonially) profane

===== Usage =====

common, defiled, unclean, unholy.
