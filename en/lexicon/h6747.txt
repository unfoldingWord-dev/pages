====== H6747: צַלַּחַת (tsallachath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6743|H6743]];

===== Meaning =====

something **advanced** or **deep**, i.e. a **bowl**; figuratively, the **bosom**

===== Definitions =====

  - advanced
  - deep
  - bowl
  - bosom

===== Usage =====

bosom, dish.
