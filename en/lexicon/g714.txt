====== G714: ἀρκέω (arkéō) ======

===== Source =====

apparently a primary verb (but probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g142|G142]] through the idea
 of raising a barrier);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to ward off, i.e. (by implication) to avail (figuratively, be satisfactory)

===== Usage =====

be content, be enough, suffice, be sufficient.
