====== H3425: יְרֻשָּׁה (yᵉrushshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3423|H3423]];

===== Meaning =====

something **occupied**; a **conquest**; also a **patrimony**

===== Definitions =====

  - occupied
  - conquest
  - patrimony

===== Usage =====

heritage, inheritance, possession.
