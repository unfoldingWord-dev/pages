====== H7703: שָׁדַד (shâdad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be burly**, i.e. (figuratively) **powerful** (passively, **impregnable**); by implication, to **ravage**

===== Definitions =====

  - be burly
  - powerful
  - impregnable
  - ravage

===== Usage =====

dead, destroy(-er), oppress, robber, spoil(-er), × utterly, (lay) waste.
