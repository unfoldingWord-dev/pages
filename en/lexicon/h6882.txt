====== H6882: צׇרְעִי (Tsorʻîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; patrial from [[en:lexicon:h6881|H6881]];

===== Meaning =====

a **Tsorite** or **Tsorathite**, i.e. inhabitants of Tsorah

===== Definitions =====

  - Tsorite
  - Tsorathite

===== Usage =====

Zorites, Zareathites, Zorathites.
