====== G519: ἀπάγχομαι (apánchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and ἄγχω (ánchō) (to choke; akin to the base of [[en:lexicon:g43|G43]]);

===== Meaning =====

to strangle oneself off (i.e. to death)

===== Usage =====

hang himself.
