====== H7728: שׁוֹבֵב (shôwbêb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7725|H7725]];

===== Meaning =====

**apostate**, i.e. **heathenish** or (actually) **heathen**

===== Definitions =====

  - apostate
  - heathenish
  - heathen

===== Usage =====

backsliding.
