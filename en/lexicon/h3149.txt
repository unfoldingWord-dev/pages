====== H3149: יְזַוְאֵל (Yᵉzavʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to sprinkle) and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; sprinkled of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Jezavel**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Jezavel

===== Usage =====

Jeziel (from the margin).
