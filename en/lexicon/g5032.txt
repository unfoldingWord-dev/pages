====== G5032: τάχιον (táchion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter singular of the comparative of [[en:lexicon:g5036|G5036]] (as adverb);

===== Meaning =====

more swiftly, i.e. (in manner) more rapidly, or (in time) more speedily

===== Usage =====

out (run), quickly, shortly, sooner.
