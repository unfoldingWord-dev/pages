====== G924: Βαρτιμαῖος (Bartimaîos) ======

===== Source =====

of Chaldee origin ([[en:lexicon:h01247|H01247]] and [[en:lexicon:h02931|H02931]]);

===== Meaning =====

son of Timæus (or the unclean); Bar-timæus, an Israelite

===== Usage =====
