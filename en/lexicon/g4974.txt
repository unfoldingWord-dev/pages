====== G4974: σφυρόν (sphyrón) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative probably of the same as
 σφαῖρα (sphaîra) (a ball, "sphere"; compare the feminine σφῦρα (sphŷra), a
 hammer);

===== Meaning =====

the ankle (as globular)

===== Usage =====

ancle bone.
