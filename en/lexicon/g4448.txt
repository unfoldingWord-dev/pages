====== G4448: πυρόω (pyróō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4442|G4442]];

===== Meaning =====

to kindle, i.e. (passively) to be ignited, glow (literally), be refined (by implication), or (figuratively) to be inflamed (with anger, grief, lust)

===== Usage =====

burn, fiery, be on fire, try.
