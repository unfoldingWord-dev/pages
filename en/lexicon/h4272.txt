====== H4272: מָחַץ (mâchats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **dash** asunder; by implication, to **crush**, **smash** or violently **plunge**; figuratively, to **subdue** or **destroy**

===== Definitions =====

  - dash
  - crush
  - smash
  - plunge
  - subdue
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

dip, pierce (through), smite (through), strike through, wound.
