====== H7850: שֹׁטֵט (shôṭêṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

active participle of an otherwise unused root meaning (properly, to pierce; but only as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7752|H7752]])

===== Meaning =====

to **flog**; a **goad**

===== Definitions =====

  - flog
  - goad

===== Usage =====

scourge.
