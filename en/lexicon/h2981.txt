====== H2981: יְבוּל (yᵉbûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2986|H2986]];

===== Meaning =====

**produce**, i.e. a **crop** or (figuratively) **wealth**

===== Definitions =====

  - produce
  - crop
  - wealth

===== Usage =====

fruit, increase.
