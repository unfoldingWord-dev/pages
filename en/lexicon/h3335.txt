====== H3335: יָצַר (yâtsar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

probably identical with [[en:lexicon:h3334|H3334]] (through the squeezing into shape); (compare [[en:lexicon:h3331|H3331]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **mould** into a form; especially as a **potter**; figuratively, to **determine** (i.e. form a resolution)

===== Definitions =====

  - mould
  - potter
  - determine

===== Usage =====

× earthen, fashion, form, frame, make(-r), potter, purpose.
