====== G2865: κομίζω (komízō) ======

===== Source =====

from a primary κομέω (koméō) (to tend, i.e. take care of);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to provide for, i.e. (by implication) to carry off (as if from harm; genitive case obtain)

===== Usage =====

bring, receive.
