====== G2147: εὑρίσκω (heurískō) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of a primary
       
      εὕρω (heúrō)  ,      which 
                                (together with another cognate form)
       
      εὑρέω (heuréō)      is used for it in all the tenses
                                except the present and imperfect;

===== Meaning =====

to find (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

find, get, obtain, perceive, see.
