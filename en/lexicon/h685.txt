====== H685: אֶצְעָדָה (ʼetsʻâdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

a variation from [[en:lexicon:h6807|H6807]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **step-chain**; by analogy, a **bracelet**

===== Definitions =====

  - step-chain
  - bracelet

===== Usage =====

bracelet, chain.
