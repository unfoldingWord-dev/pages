====== H6470: פָּעַם (pâʻam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tap**, i.e. **beat regularly**; hence (generally) to **impel** or **agitate**

===== Definitions =====

  - tap
  - beat regularly
  - impel
  - agitate

===== Usage =====

move, trouble.
