====== G599: ἀποθνήσκω (apothnḗskō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g2348|G2348]];

===== Meaning =====

to die off (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

be dead, death, die, lie a-dying, be slain (X with).
