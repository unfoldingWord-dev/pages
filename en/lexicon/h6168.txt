====== H6168: עָרָה (ʻârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **make**) **bare**; hence, to **empty**, **pour out**, **demolish**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - bare
  - empty
  - pour out
  - demolish

===== Usage =====

leave destitute, discover, empty, make naked, pour (out), rase, spread self, uncover.
