====== H5350: נִקֻּד (niqqud) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5348|H5348]];

===== Meaning =====

a **crumb** (as broken to spots); also a **biscuit** (as pricked)

===== Definitions =====

  - crumb
  - biscuit

===== Usage =====

cracknel, mouldy.
