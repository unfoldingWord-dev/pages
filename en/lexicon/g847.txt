====== G847: αὐτοῦ (autoû) ======

===== Source =====

genitive (i.e. possessive) of [[en:lexicon:g846|G846]], used as an adverb of location;

===== Meaning =====

properly, belonging to the same spot, i.e. in this (or that) place

===== Usage =====

(t-)here.
