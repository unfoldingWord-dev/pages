====== G4392: πρόφασις (próphasis) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]];

===== Meaning =====

an outward showing, i.e. pretext

===== Usage =====

cloke, colour, pretence, show.
