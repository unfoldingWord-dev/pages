====== H7290: רָדַם (râdam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **stun**, i.e. **stupefy** (with sleep or death)

===== Definitions =====

  - stun
  - stupefy

===== Usage =====

(be fast a-, be in a deep, cast into a dead, that) sleep(-er, -eth).
