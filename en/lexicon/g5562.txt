====== G5562: χωρέω (chōréō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5561|G5561]];

===== Meaning =====

to be in (give) space, i.e. (intransitively) to pass, enter, or (transitively) to hold, admit (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

come, contain, go, have place, (can, be room to) receive.
