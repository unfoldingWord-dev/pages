====== H6921: קָדִים (qâdîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6923|H6923]];

===== Meaning =====

the **fore** or front part; hence (by orientation) the **East** (often adverbially, **eastward**, for brevity the **east wind**)

===== Definitions =====

  - fore
  - East
  - eastward
  - east wind

===== Usage =====

east(-ward, wind).
