====== H7009: קִים (qîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6965|H6965]];

===== Meaning =====

an **opponent** (as rising against one), i.e. (collectively) **enemies**

===== Definitions =====

  - opponent
  - enemies

===== Usage =====

substance.
