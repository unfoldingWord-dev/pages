====== G647: ἀποστάσιον (apostásion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a (presumed) adjective from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g868|G868]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something separative, i.e. (specially) divorce

===== Usage =====

(writing of) divorcement.
