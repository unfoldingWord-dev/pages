====== G170: ἀκαιρέομαι (akairéomai) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g2540|G2540]] (meaning
 unseasonable);

===== Meaning =====

to be inopportune (for oneself), i.e. to fail of a proper occasion

===== Usage =====

lack opportunity.
