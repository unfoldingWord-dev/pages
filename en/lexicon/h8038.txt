====== H8038: שֶׁמְאֵבֶר (Shemʼêber) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h8034|H8034]] and [[en:lexicon:h83|H83]]; name of pinion, i.e. illustrious;

===== Meaning =====

**Shemeber**, a king of Zeboim

  - Shemeber

===== Usage =====
