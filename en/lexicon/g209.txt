====== G209: ἀκωλύτως (akōlýtōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative
 of [[en:lexicon:g2967|G2967]];

===== Meaning =====

in an unhindered manner, i.e. freely

===== Usage =====

no man forbidding him.
