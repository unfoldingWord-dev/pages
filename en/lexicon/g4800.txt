====== G4800: συζάω (syzáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2198|G2198]];

===== Meaning =====

to continue to live in common with, i.e. co-survive (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

live with.
