====== G4064: περιφέρω (periphérō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]];

===== Meaning =====

to convey around, i.e. transport hither and thither

===== Usage =====

bear (carry) about.
