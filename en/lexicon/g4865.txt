====== G4865: συναγωνίζομαι (synagōnízomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g75|G75]];

===== Meaning =====

to struggle in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to be a partner (assistant)

===== Usage =====

strive together with.
