====== H8467: תְּחִנָּה (tᵉchinnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2603|H2603]];

===== Meaning =====

**graciousness**; causatively, **entreaty**

===== Definitions =====

  - graciousness
  - entreaty

===== Usage =====

favour, grace, supplication.
