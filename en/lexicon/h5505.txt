====== H5505: סָחַר (çâchar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5503|H5503]];

===== Meaning =====

an **emporium**; abstractly, **profit** (from trade)

===== Definitions =====

  - emporium
  - profit

===== Usage =====

mart, merchandise.
