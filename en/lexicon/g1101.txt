====== G1101: γλωσσόκομον (glōssókomon) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1100|G1100]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g2889|G2889]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a case (to keep mouthpieces of wind-instruments in) i.e. (by extension) a casket or (specially) purse

===== Usage =====

bag.
