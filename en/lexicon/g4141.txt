====== G4141: πλήσσω (plḗssō) ======

===== Source =====

apparently another form of [[en:lexicon:g4111|G4111]] (through the idea of flattening out);

===== Meaning =====

to pound, i.e. (figuratively) to inflict with (calamity)

===== Usage =====

smite.
