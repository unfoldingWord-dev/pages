====== H2172: זִמְרָה (zimrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2167|H2167]];

===== Meaning =====

a **musical** piece or **song** to be accompanied by an instrument

===== Definitions =====

  - musical
  - song

===== Usage =====

melody, psalm.
