====== G186: ἀκλινής (aklinḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g2827|G2827]];

===== Meaning =====

not leaning, i.e. (figuratively) firm

===== Usage =====

without wavering.
