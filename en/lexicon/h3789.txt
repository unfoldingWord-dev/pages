====== H3789: כָּתַב (kâthab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **grave**, by implication, to **write** (describe, inscribe, prescribe, subscribe)

===== Definitions =====

  - grave
  - write

===== Usage =====

describe, record, prescribe, subscribe, write(-ing, -ten).
