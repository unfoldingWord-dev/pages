====== G454: ἄνοια (ánoia) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g3563|G3563]];

===== Meaning =====

stupidity; by implication, rage

===== Usage =====

folly, madness.
