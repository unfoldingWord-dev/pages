====== H3351: יְקוּם (yᵉqûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6965|H6965]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **standing** (extant), i.e. by implication, a **living thing**

===== Definitions =====

  - standing
  - living thing

===== Usage =====

(living) substance.
