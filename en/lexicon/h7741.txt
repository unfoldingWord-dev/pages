====== H7741: שָׁוֵה קִרְיָתַיִם (Shâvêh Qiryâthayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h7740|H7740]] and the dual of [[en:lexicon:h7151|H7151]]; **plain of a double city**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shaveh-Kirjathajim**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - plain of a double city
  - Shaveh-Kirjathajim

===== Usage =====

Shaveh Kiriathaim.
