====== G2704: καταφθείρω (kataphtheírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g5351|G5351]];

===== Meaning =====

to spoil entirely, i.e. (literally) to destroy; or (figuratively) to deprave;

===== Usage =====

--corrupt, utterly perish.
