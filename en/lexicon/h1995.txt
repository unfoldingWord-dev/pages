====== H1995: הָמוֹן (hâmôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Ezekiel 5:7), from [[en:lexicon:h1993|H1993]];

===== Meaning =====

a **noise**, **tumult**, **crowd**; also **disquietude**, **wealth**

===== Definitions =====

  - noise
  - tumult
  - crowd
  - disquietude
  - wealth

===== Usage =====

abundance, company, many, multitude, multiply, noise, riches, rumbling, sounding, store, tumult.
