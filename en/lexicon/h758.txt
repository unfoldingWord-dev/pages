====== H758: אֲרָם (ʼĂrâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculinenoun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h759|H759]]; the highland;

===== Meaning =====

**Aram** or **Syria**, and its inhabitants; also the name of the son of Shem, a grandson of Nahor, and of an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Aram
  - Syria

===== Usage =====

Aram, Mesopotamia, Syria, Syrians.
