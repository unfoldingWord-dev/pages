====== G5342: φέρω (phérō) ======

===== Source =====

a primary verb (for which other, and apparently not cognate ones are
 used in certain tenses only; namely,
    
      οἴω (oíō)  ;     
  and ἐνέγκω (enénkō)  ;

===== Meaning =====

to "bear" or carry (in a very wide application, literally and figuratively, as follows)

===== Usage =====

be, bear, bring (forth), carry, come, + let her drive, be driven, endure, go on, lay, lead, move, reach, rushing, uphold.
