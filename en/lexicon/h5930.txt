====== H5930: עֹלָה (ʻôlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine active participle of [[en:lexicon:h5927|H5927]];

===== Meaning =====

a **step** or (collectively, stairs, as ascending); usually a **holocaust** (as going up in smoke)

===== Definitions =====

  - step
  - holocaust

===== Usage =====

ascent, burnt offering (sacrifice), go up to. See also
