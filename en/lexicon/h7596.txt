====== H7596: שְׁאֵלָה (shᵉʼêlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (1 Samuel 1:17), from [[en:lexicon:h7592|H7592]];

===== Meaning =====

**a petition**; by implication, **a loan**

===== Definitions =====

  - a petition
  - a loan

===== Usage =====

loan, petition, request.
