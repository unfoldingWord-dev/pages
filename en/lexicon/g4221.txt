====== G4221: ποτήριον (potḗrion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a derivative of the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g4095|G4095]];

===== Meaning =====

a drinking-vessel; by extension, the contents thereof, i.e. a cupful (draught); figuratively, a lot or fate

===== Usage =====

cup.
