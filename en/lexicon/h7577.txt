====== H7577: רְתֻקָה (rᵉthuqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h7576|H7576]];

===== Meaning =====

something **fastened**, i.e. a **chain**

===== Definitions =====

  - fastened
  - chain

===== Usage =====

chain.
