====== H1479: גּוּף (gûwph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **hollow** or **arch**, i.e. (figuratively) **close**; to **shut**

===== Definitions =====

  - hollow
  - arch
  - close
  - shut

===== Usage =====

shut.
