====== G4692: σπεύδω (speúdō) ======

===== Source =====

probably strengthened from [[en:lexicon:g4228|G4228]];

===== Meaning =====

to "speed" ("study"), i.e. urge on (diligently or earnestly); by implication, to await eagerly

===== Usage =====

(make, with) haste unto.
