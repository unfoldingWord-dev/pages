====== H235: אָזַל (ʼâzal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **go away**, hence, to **disappear**

===== Definitions =====

  - go away
  - disappear

===== Usage =====

fail, gad about, go to and fro (but in Ezekiel 27:19 the word is rendered by many 'from Uzal,' by others 'yarn'), be gone (spent).
