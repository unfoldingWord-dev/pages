====== H8403: תַּבְנִית (tabnîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1129|H1129]];

===== Meaning =====

**structure**; by implication, a **model**, **resemblance**

===== Definitions =====

  - structure
  - model
  - resemblance

===== Usage =====

figure, form, likeness, pattern, similitude.
