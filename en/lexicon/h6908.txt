====== H6908: קָבַץ (qâbats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **grasp**, i.e. **collect**

===== Definitions =====

  - grasp
  - collect

===== Usage =====

assemble (selves), gather (bring) (together, selves together, up), heap, resort, × surely, take up.
