====== G419: ἀνεξερεύνητος (anexereúnētos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1830|G1830]];

===== Meaning =====

not searched out, i.e. (by implication) inscrutable

===== Usage =====

unsearchable.
