====== H2411: חַטִּיל (Chaṭṭîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to wave; fluctuating;

===== Meaning =====

**Chattil**, one of 'Solomon's servants'

===== Definitions =====

  - Chattil

===== Usage =====

Hattil.
