====== H7941: שֵׁכָר (shêkâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7937|H7937]];

===== Meaning =====

an **intoxicant**, i.e. intensely alcoholic **liquor**

===== Definitions =====

  - intoxicant
  - liquor

===== Usage =====

strong drink,   drunkard, strong wine.
