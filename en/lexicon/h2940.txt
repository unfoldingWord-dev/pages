====== H2940: טַעַם (ṭaʻam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2938|H2938]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **taste**, i.e. (figuratively) **perception**; by implication, **intelligence**; transitively, a **mandate**

===== Definitions =====

  - taste
  - perception
  - intelligence
  - mandate

===== Usage =====

advice, behaviour, decree, discretion, judgment, reason, taste, understanding.
