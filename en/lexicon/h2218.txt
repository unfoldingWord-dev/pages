====== H2218: זֶרֶד (Zered) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be exuberant in growth; lined with shrubbery;

===== Meaning =====

**Zered**, a brook East of the Dead Sea

===== Definitions =====

  - Zered

===== Usage =====

Zared, Zered.
