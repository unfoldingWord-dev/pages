====== H8162: שַׁעַטְנֵז (shaʻaṭnêz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably of foreign derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**linsey-woolsey**, i.e. cloth of linen and wool carded and spun together

===== Definitions =====

  - linsey-woolsey

===== Usage =====

garment of divers sorts, linen and wollen.
