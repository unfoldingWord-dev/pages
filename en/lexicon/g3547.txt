====== G3547: νομοδιδάσκαλος (nomodidáskalos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3551|G3551]] and [[en:lexicon:g1320|G1320]];

===== Meaning =====

an expounder of the (Jewish) law, i.e. a Rabbi

===== Usage =====

doctor (teacher) of the law.
