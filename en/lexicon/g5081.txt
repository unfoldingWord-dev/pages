====== G5081: τηλαυγῶς (tēlaugōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a compound of a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5056|G5056]] and [[en:lexicon:g827|G827]];

===== Meaning =====

in a far-shining manner, i.e. plainly

===== Usage =====

clearly.
