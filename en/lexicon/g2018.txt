====== G2018: ἐπιφέρω (epiphérō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]];

===== Meaning =====

to bear upon (or further), i.e. adduce (personally or judicially (accuse, inflict)), superinduce

===== Usage =====

add, bring (against), take.
