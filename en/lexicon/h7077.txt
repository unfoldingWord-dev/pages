====== H7077: קָנַן (qânan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to erect; but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7064|H7064]];

===== Meaning =====

to **nestle**, i.e. **build** or **occupy as a nest**

===== Definitions =====

  - nestle
  - build
  - occupy as a nest

===== Usage =====

make...nest.
