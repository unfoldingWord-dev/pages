====== H631: אָסַר (ʼâçar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **yoke** or **hitch**; by analogy, to **fasten** in any sense, to **join** battle

===== Definitions =====

  - yoke
  - hitch
  - fasten
  - join

===== Usage =====

bind, fast, gird, harness, hold, keep, make ready, order, prepare, prison(-er), put in bonds, set in array, tie.
