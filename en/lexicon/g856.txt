====== G856: ἀφεδρών (aphedrṓn) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g1476|G1476]];

===== Meaning =====

a place of sitting apart, i.e. a privy

===== Usage =====
