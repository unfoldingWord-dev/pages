====== H1238: בָּקַק (bâqaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pour** out, i.e. to **empty**, figuratively, to **depopulate**; by analogy, to **spread** out (as a fruitful vine)

===== Definitions =====

  - pour
  - empty
  - depopulate
  - spread

===== Usage =====

(make) empty (out), fail, × utterly, make void.
