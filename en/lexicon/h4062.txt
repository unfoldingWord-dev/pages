====== H4062: מַדְהֵבָה (madhêbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

perhaps from the equivalent of [[en:lexicon:h1722|H1722]];

===== Meaning =====

**goldmaking**, i.e. **exactness**

===== Definitions =====

  - goldmaking
  - exactness

===== Usage =====

golden city.
