====== G5468: χαλιναγωγέω (chalinagōgéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5469|G5469]] and the reduplicated form of [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a bit-leader, i.e. to curb (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

bridle.
