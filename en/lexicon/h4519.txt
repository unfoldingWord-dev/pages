====== H4519: מְנַשֶּׁה (Mᵉnashsheh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5382|H5382]]; **causing to forget**;

===== Meaning =====

**Menashsheh**, a grandson of Jacob, also the tribe descended from him, and its territory

===== Definitions =====

  - causing to forget
  - Menashsheh

===== Usage =====

Manasseh.
