====== G431: ἀνεψιός (anepsiós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a particle of union) and an obsolete νέπος (népos) (a brood);

===== Meaning =====

properly, akin, i.e. (specially) a cousin

===== Usage =====

sister's son.
