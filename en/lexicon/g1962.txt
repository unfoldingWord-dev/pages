====== G1962: ἐπινεύω (epineúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g3506|G3506]];

===== Meaning =====

to nod at, i.e. (by implication) to assent

===== Usage =====
