====== H1235: בֶּקַע (beqaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1234|H1234]];

===== Meaning =====

a **section** (half) of a shekel, i.e. a **beka** (a weight and a coin)

===== Definitions =====

  - section
  - beka

===== Usage =====

bekah, half a shekel.
