====== H7493: רָעַשׁ (râʻash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **undulate** (as the earth, the sky, etc.; also a field of grain), particularly through fear; specifically, to **spring** (as a locust)

===== Definitions =====

  - undulate
  - spring

===== Usage =====

make afraid, (re-) move, quake, (make to) shake, (make to) tremble.
