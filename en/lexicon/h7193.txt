====== H7193: קַשְׂקֶשֶׂת (qasqeseth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

by reduplication from an unused root meaning to shale off as bark;

===== Meaning =====

a **scale** (of a fish); hence a **coat of mail** (as composed of or covered with jointed plates of metal)

===== Definitions =====

  - scale
  - coat of mail

===== Usage =====

mail, scale.
