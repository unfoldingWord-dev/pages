====== H7911: שָׁכַח (shâkach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **mislay**, i.e. to **be oblivious** of, from want of memory or attention

===== Definitions =====

  - mislay
  - be oblivious

===== Usage =====

× at all, (cause to) forget.
