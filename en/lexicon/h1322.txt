====== H1322: בֹּשֶׁת (bôsheth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h954|H954]];

===== Meaning =====

**shame** (the feeling and the condition, as well as its cause); by implication (specifically) an **idol**

===== Definitions =====

  - shame
  - idol

===== Usage =====

ashamed, confusion,   greatly, (put to) shame(-ful thing).
