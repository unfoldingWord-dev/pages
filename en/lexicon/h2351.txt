====== H2351: חוּץ (chûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; (both forms feminine in the plural) from an unused root meaning to sever;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **separate by a wall**, i.e. **outside**, **outdoors**

===== Definitions =====

  - separate by a wall
  - outside
  - outdoors

===== Usage =====

abroad, field, forth, highway, more, out(-side, -ward), street, without.
