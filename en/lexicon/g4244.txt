====== G4244: πρεσβυτέριον (presbytérion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4245|G4245]];

===== Meaning =====

the order of elders, i.e. (specially), Israelite Sanhedrin or Christian "presbytery"

===== Usage =====

(estate of) elder(-s), presbytery.
