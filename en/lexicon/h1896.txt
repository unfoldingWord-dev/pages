====== H1896: הֵגֵא (Hêgêʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (by permutation) [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably of Persian origin;

===== Meaning =====

**Hege** or **Hegai**, a eunuch of Xerxes

===== Definitions =====

  - Hege
  - Hegai

===== Usage =====

Hegai, Hege.
