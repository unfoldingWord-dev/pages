====== H142: אָדַר (ʼâdar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **expand**, i.e. **be great** or (figuratively) **magnificent**

===== Definitions =====

  - expand
  - be great
  - magnificent

===== Usage =====

(become) glorious, honourable.
