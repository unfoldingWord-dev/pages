====== H7997: שָׁלַל (shâlal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **drop** or **strip**; by implication, to **plunder**

===== Definitions =====

  - drop
  - strip
  - plunder

===== Usage =====

let fall, make self a prey, × of purpose, (make a, (take)) spoil.
