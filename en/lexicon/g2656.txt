====== G2656: κατανεύω (kataneúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g3506|G3506]];

===== Meaning =====

to nod down (towards), i.e. (by analogy) to make signs to

===== Usage =====

beckon.
