====== H4254: מַחֲלָצָה (machălâtsâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2502|H2502]];

===== Meaning =====

a **mantle** (as easily **drawn off**)

===== Definitions =====

  - mantle
  - drawn off

===== Usage =====

changeable suit of apparel, change of raiment.
