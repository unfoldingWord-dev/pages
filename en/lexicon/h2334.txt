===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the plural of [[en:lexicon:h2333|H2333]] and a modification of [[en:lexicon:h3265|H3265]];

===== Meaning =====

**hamlets of Jair**, a region of Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - hamlets of Jair

===== Usage =====
