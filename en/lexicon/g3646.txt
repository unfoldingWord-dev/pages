====== G3646: ὁλοκαύτωμα (holokaútōma) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3650|G3650]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2545|G2545]];

===== Meaning =====

a wholly-consumed sacrifice ("holocaust")

===== Usage =====

(whole) burnt offering.
