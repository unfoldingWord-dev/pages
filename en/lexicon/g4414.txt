====== G4414: πρωτοστάτης (prōtostátēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4413|G4413]] and [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]];

===== Meaning =====

one standing first in the ranks, i.e. a captain (champion)

===== Usage =====

ringleader.
