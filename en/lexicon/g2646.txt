====== G2646: κατάλυμα (katályma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2647|G2647]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a dissolution (breaking up of a journey), i.e. (by implication) a lodging-place

===== Usage =====

 guestchamber, inn.
