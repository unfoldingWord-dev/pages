====== G5327: φάραγξ (pháranx) ======

===== Source =====

properly, strengthened from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4008|G4008]] or rather of [[en:lexicon:g4486|G4486]];

===== Meaning =====

a gap or chasm, i.e. ravine (winter-torrent)

===== Usage =====

valley.
