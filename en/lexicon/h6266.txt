====== H6266: עָתִיק (ʻâthîyq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6275|H6275]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **antique**, i.e. **venerable** or **splendid**

===== Definitions =====

  - antique
  - venerable
  - splendid

===== Usage =====

durable.
