====== H7901: שָׁכַב (shâkab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **lie down** (for rest, sexual connection, decease or any other purpose)

===== Definitions =====

  - lie down

===== Usage =====

× at all, cast down, (lover-)lay (self) (down), (make to) lie (down, down to sleep, still with), lodge, ravish, take rest, sleep, stay.
