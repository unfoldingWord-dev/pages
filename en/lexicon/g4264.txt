====== G4264: προβιβάζω (probibázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and a reduplicated form of [[en:lexicon:g971|G971]];

===== Meaning =====

to force forward, i.e. bring to the front, instigate

===== Usage =====

draw, before instruct.
