====== G2124: εὐλάβεια (eulábeia) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2126|G2126]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, caution, i.e. (religiously) reverence (piety); by implication, dread (concretely)

===== Usage =====

fear(-ed).
