====== G1811: ἐξακολουθέω (exakolouthéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g190|G190]];

===== Meaning =====

to follow out, i.e. (figuratively) to imitate, obey, yield to

===== Usage =====

follow.
