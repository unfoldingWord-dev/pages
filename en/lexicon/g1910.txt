====== G1910: ἐπιβαίνω (epibaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to walk upon, i.e. mount, ascend, embark, arrive

===== Usage =====

come (into), enter into, go abroad, sit upon, take ship.
