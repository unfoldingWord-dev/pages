====== H491: אַלְמָנוּת (ʼalmânûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h488|H488]];

===== Meaning =====

concrete, a **widow**; abstract, **widowhood**

===== Definitions =====

  - widow
  - widowhood

===== Usage =====

widow, widowhood.
