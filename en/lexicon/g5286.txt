====== G5286: ὑποπόδιον (hypopódion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g4228|G4228]];

===== Meaning =====

something under the feet, i.e. a foot-rest (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

footstool.
