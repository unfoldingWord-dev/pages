====== H8581: תַּעָב (taʻâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **loathe**, i.e. (morally) **detest**

===== Definitions =====

  - loathe
  - detest

===== Usage =====

(make to be) abhor(-red), (be, commit more, do) abominable(-y), × utterly.
