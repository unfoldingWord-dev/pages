====== H1966: הֵילֵל (hêylêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1984|H1984]] (in the sense of **brightness**);

===== Meaning =====

the **morning-star**

===== Definitions =====

  - brightness
  - morning-star

===== Usage =====

lucifer.
