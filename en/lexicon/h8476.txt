====== H8476: תַּחַשׁ (tachash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably of foreign derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a (clean) **animal with fur**, probably a **species of antelope**

===== Definitions =====

  - animal with fur
  - species of antelope

===== Usage =====

badger.
