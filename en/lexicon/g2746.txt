====== G2746: καύχησις (kaúchēsis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2744|G2744]];

===== Meaning =====

boasting (properly, the act; by implication, the object), in a good or a bad sense

===== Usage =====

boasting, whereof I may glory, glorying, rejoicing.
