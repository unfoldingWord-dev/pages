====== G5461: φωτίζω (phōtízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5457|G5457]];

===== Meaning =====

to shed rays, i.e. to shine or (transitively) to brighten up (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

enlighten, illuminate, (bring to, give) light, make to see.
