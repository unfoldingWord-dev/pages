====== G3611: οἰκέω (oikéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3624|G3624]];

===== Meaning =====

to occupy a house, i.e. reside (figuratively, inhabit, remain, inhere); by implication, to cohabit

===== Usage =====

dwell.
