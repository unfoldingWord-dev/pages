====== G1257: διαλείπω (dialeípō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g3007|G3007]];

===== Meaning =====

to leave off in the middle, i.e. intermit

===== Usage =====
