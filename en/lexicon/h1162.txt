====== H1162: בֹּעַז (Bôʻaz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root of uncertain meaning;

===== Meaning =====

**Boaz**, the ancestor of David; also the name of a pillar in front of the temple

===== Definitions =====

  - Boaz

===== Usage =====

Boaz.
