====== H4954: מִשְׁרָעִי (Mishrâʻîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

patrial from an unused noun from an unused root; probably meaning to **stretch out**; **extension**;

===== Meaning =====

a **Mishraite**, or inhabitant (collectively) of Mishra

===== Definitions =====

  - stretch out
  - extension
  - Mishraite

===== Usage =====

Mishraites.
