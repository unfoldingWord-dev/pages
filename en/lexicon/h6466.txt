====== H6466: פָּעַל (pâʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **do** or **make** (systematically and habitually), especially to **practise**

===== Definitions =====

  - do
  - make
  - practise

===== Usage =====

commit, (evil-) do(-er), make(-r), ordain, work(-er).
