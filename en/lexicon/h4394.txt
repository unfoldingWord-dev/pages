====== H4394: מִלֻּא (milluʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4390|H4390]];

===== Meaning =====

a **fulfilling** (only in plural), i.e. (literally) a **setting** (of gems), or (technically) **consecration** (also concretely, a dedicatory sacrifice)

===== Definitions =====

  - fulfilling
  - setting
  - consecration

===== Usage =====

consecration, be set.
