====== G1813: ἐξαλείφω (exaleíphō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g218|G218]];

===== Meaning =====

to smear out, i.e. obliterate (erase tears, figuratively, pardon sin)

===== Usage =====

blot out, wipe away.
