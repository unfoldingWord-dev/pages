====== H3554: כָּוָה (kâvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **prick** or **penetrate**; hence, to **blister** (as smarting or eating into)

===== Definitions =====

  - prick
  - penetrate
  - blister

===== Usage =====

burn.
