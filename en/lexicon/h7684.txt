====== H7684: שְׁגָגָה (shᵉgâgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7683|H7683]];

===== Meaning =====

a **mistake** or inadvertent **transgression**

===== Definitions =====

  - mistake
  - transgression

===== Usage =====

error, ignorance, at unawares; unwittingly.
