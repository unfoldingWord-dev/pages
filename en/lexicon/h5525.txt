====== H5525: סֻכִּי (Çukkîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

patrial from an unknown name (perhaps [[en:lexicon:h5520|H5520]]);

===== Meaning =====

a **Sukkite**, or inhabitant of some place near Egypt (i.e. hut-dwellers)

===== Definitions =====

  - Sukkite

===== Usage =====

Sukkiims.
