====== H3907: לָחַשׁ (lâchash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **whisper**; by implication, to **mumble a spell** (as a magician)

===== Definitions =====

  - whisper
  - mumble a spell

===== Usage =====

charmer, whisper (together).
