====== H6292: פִּגּוּל (piggûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **stink**;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **fetid**, i.e. (figuratively) **unclean** (ceremonially)

===== Definitions =====

  - stink
  - fetid
  - unclean

===== Usage =====

abominable(-tion, thing).
