====== H8313: שָׂרַף (sâraph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **set**) **on fire**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - set
  - on fire

===== Usage =====

(cause to, make a) burn((-ing), up) kindle, × utterly.
