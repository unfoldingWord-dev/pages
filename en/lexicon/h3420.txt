====== H3420: יֵרָקוֹן (yêrâqôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3418|H3418]];

===== Meaning =====

**paleness**, whether of persons (from fright), or of plants (from drought)

===== Definitions =====

  - paleness

===== Usage =====

greenish, yellow.
