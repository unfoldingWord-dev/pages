====== H4383: מִכְשׁוֹל (mikshôwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; masculine from [[en:lexicon:h3782|H3782]];

===== Meaning =====

a **stumbling-block**, literally or figuratively (**obstacle**, **enticement** (specifically an idol), **scruple**)

===== Definitions =====

  - stumbling-block
  - obstacle
  - enticement
  - scruple

===== Usage =====

caused to fall, offence, × (no-) thing offered, ruin, stumbling-block.
