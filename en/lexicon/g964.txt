====== G964: Βηθεσδά (Bēthesdá) ======

===== Source =====

of Chaldee origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h01004|H01004]] and [[en:lexicon:h02617|H02617]]);

===== Meaning =====

house of kindness; Beth-esda, a pool in Jerusalem

===== Usage =====

Bethesda.
