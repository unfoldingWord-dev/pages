====== H6504: פָּרַד (pârad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **break through**, i.e. **spread** or **separate** (oneself)

===== Definitions =====

  - break through
  - spread
  - separate

===== Usage =====

disperse, divide, be out of joint, part, scatter (abroad), separate (self), sever self, stretch, sunder.
