====== G1882: ἐπανόρθωσις (epanórthōsis) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g461|G461]];

===== Meaning =====

a straightening up again, i.e. (figuratively) rectification (reformation)

===== Usage =====

 correction.
