====== G428: ἀνεύθετος (aneúthetos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g2111|G2111]];

===== Meaning =====

not well set, i.e. inconvenient

===== Usage =====

not commodious.
