====== H1851: דַּק (daq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1854|H1854]];

===== Meaning =====

**crushed**, i.e. (by implication) **small** or **thin**

===== Definitions =====

  - crushed
  - small
  - thin

===== Usage =====

dwarf, lean(-fleshed), very little thing, small, thin.
