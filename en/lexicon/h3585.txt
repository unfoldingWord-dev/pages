====== H3585: כַּחַשׁ (kachash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3584|H3584]];

===== Meaning =====

literally a **failure of flesh**, i.e. **emaciation**; figuratively, **hypocrisy**

===== Definitions =====

  - failure of flesh
  - emaciation
  - hypocrisy

===== Usage =====

leanness, lies, lying.
