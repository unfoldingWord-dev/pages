====== H2352: חוּר (chûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root probably meaning to **bore**;

===== Meaning =====

the **crevice** of a serpent; the **cell** of a prison

===== Definitions =====

  - bore
  - crevice
  - cell

===== Usage =====

hole.
