====== G4494: ῥιπίζω (rhipízō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4496|G4496]] (meaning a fan or bellows);

===== Meaning =====

to breeze up, i.e. (by analogy) to agitate (into waves)

===== Usage =====

toss.
