====== G4362: προσπήγνυμι (prospḗgnymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g4078|G4078]];

===== Meaning =====

to fasten to, i.e. (specially), to impale (on a cross)

===== Usage =====

crucify.
