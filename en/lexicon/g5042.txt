====== G5042: τεκνογονία (teknogonía) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g5041|G5041]];

===== Meaning =====

childbirth (parentage), i.e. (by implication) maternity (the performance of maternal duties)

===== Usage =====

childbearing.
