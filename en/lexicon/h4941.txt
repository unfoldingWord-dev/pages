====== H4941: מִשְׁפָּט (mishpâṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8199|H8199]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **verdict** (favorable or unfavorable) pronounced judicially, especially a **sentence** or formal decree (human or (participant's) divine **law**, individual or collective), including the act, the place, the suit, the crime, and the penalty; abstractly, **justice**, including a participant's **right** or **privilege** (statutory or customary), or even a **style**

===== Definitions =====

  - verdict
  - sentence
  - law
  - justice
  - right
  - privilege
  - style

===== Usage =====

  adversary, ceremony, charge, × crime, custom, desert, determination, discretion, disposing, due, fashion, form, to be judged, judgment, just(-ice, -ly), (manner of) law(-ful), manner, measure, (due) order, ordinance, right, sentence, usest, × worthy,   wrong.
