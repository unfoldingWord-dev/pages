====== G1525: εἰσέρχομαι (eisérchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1519|G1519]] and [[en:lexicon:g2064|G2064]];

===== Meaning =====

to enter (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

X arise, come (in, into), enter in(-to), go in (through).
