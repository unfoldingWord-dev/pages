====== G4998: σώφρων (sṓphrōn) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4982|G4982]] and that of [[en:lexicon:g5424|G5424]];

===== Meaning =====

safe (sound) in mind, i.e. self-controlled (moderate as to opinion or passion)

===== Usage =====

discreet, sober, temperate.
