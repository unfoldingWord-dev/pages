====== H1482: גּוּר (gûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; perhaps from [[en:lexicon:h1481|H1481]];

===== Meaning =====

a **cub** (as still abiding in the lair), especially of the **lion**

===== Definitions =====

  - cub
  - lion

===== Usage =====

whelp, young one.
