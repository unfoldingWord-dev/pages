====== H6222: עֲשָׂיָה (ʻĂsâyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6213|H6213]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; **Jah has made**;

===== Meaning =====

**Asajah**, the name of three or four Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has made
  - Asajah

===== Usage =====

Asaiah.
