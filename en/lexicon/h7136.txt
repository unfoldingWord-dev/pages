====== H7136: קָרָה (qârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **light upon** (chiefly by accident); causatively, to **bring about**; specifically, to **impose timbers** (for roof or floor)

===== Definitions =====

  - light upon
  - bring about
  - impose timbers

===== Usage =====

appoint, lay (make) beams, befall, bring, come (to pass unto), floor, (hap) was, happen (unto), meet, send good speed.
