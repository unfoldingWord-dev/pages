====== H818: אָשֵׁם (ʼâshêm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h816|H816]];

===== Meaning =====

**guilty**; hence, **presenting a sin-offering**

===== Definitions =====

  - guilty
  - presenting a sin-offering

===== Usage =====

one which is faulty, guilty.
