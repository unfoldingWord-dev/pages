====== G540: ἀπάτωρ (apátōr) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g3962|G3962]];

===== Meaning =====

fatherless, i.e. of unrecorded paternity

===== Usage =====

without father.
