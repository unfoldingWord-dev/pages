====== G521: ἀπαίδευτος (apaídeutos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3811|G3811]];

===== Meaning =====

uninstructed, i.e. (figuratively) stupid

===== Usage =====

unlearned.
