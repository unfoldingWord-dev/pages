====== H8145: שֵׁנִי (shênîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8138|H8138]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **double**, i.e. **second**; also adverbially, **again**

===== Definitions =====

  - double
  - second
  - again

===== Usage =====

again, either (of them), (an-) other, second (time).
