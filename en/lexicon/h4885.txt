====== H4885: מָשׂוֹשׂ (mâsôws) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7797|H7797]];

===== Meaning =====

**delight**, concretely (the cause or object) or abstractly (the feeling)

===== Definitions =====

  - delight

===== Usage =====

joy, mirth, rejoice.
