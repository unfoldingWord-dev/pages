====== H3860: לָהֵן (lâhên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

conjunction

===== Source =====

from the prepositional prefix meaning **to** or **for** and [[en:lexicon:h2005|H2005]];

===== Meaning =====

properly **for if**; hence, **therefore**

===== Definitions =====

  - to
  - for
  - for if
  - therefore

===== Usage =====

for them (by mistake for prepositional suffix).
