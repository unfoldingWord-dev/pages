====== H4616: מַעַן (maʻan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

particle

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6030|H6030]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **heed**, i.e. **purpose**; used only adverbially, **on account of** (as a motive or an aim), teleologically, **in order that**

===== Definitions =====

  - heed
  - purpose
  - on account of
  - in order that

===== Usage =====

because of, to the end (intent) that, for (to,... 's sake),   lest, that, to.
