====== H5518: סִיר (çîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Jeremiah 52:18), from a primitive root meaning to **boil** up;

===== Meaning =====

a **pot**; also a **thorn** (as springing up rapidly); by implication, a **hook**

===== Definitions =====

  - boil
  - pot
  - thorn
  - hook

===== Usage =====

caldron, fishhook, pan, (wash-)pot, thorn.
