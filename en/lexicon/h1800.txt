====== H1800: דַּל (dal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1809|H1809]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **dangling**, i.e. (by implication) **weak** or **thin**

===== Definitions =====

  - dangling
  - weak
  - thin

===== Usage =====

lean, needy, poor (man), weaker.
