====== G4895: σύνειμι (sýneimi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g1510|G1510]] (including its various inflections);

===== Meaning =====

to be in company with, i.e. present at the time

===== Usage =====
