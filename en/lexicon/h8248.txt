====== H8248: שָׁקָה (shâqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **quaff**, i.e. (causatively) to **irrigate** or **furnish a potion to**

===== Definitions =====

  - quaff
  - irrigate
  - furnish a potion to
