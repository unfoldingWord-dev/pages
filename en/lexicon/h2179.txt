====== H2179: זָנַב (zânab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root meaning to wag; used only as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h2180|H2180]];

===== Meaning =====

to **curtail**, i.e. **cut off the rear**

===== Definitions =====

  - curtail
  - cut off the rear

===== Usage =====

smite the hindmost.
