====== G450: ἀνίστημι (anístēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]];

===== Meaning =====

to stand up (literal or figurative, transitive or intransitive)

===== Usage =====

arise, lift up, raise up (again), rise (again), stand up(-right).
