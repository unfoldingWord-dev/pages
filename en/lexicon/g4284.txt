====== G4284: προέχομαι (proéchomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

to hold oneself before others, i.e. (figuratively) to excel

===== Usage =====

be better.
