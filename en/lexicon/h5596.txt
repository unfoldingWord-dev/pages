====== H5596: סָפַח (çâphach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Isaiah 3:17), a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **scrape** out, but in certain peculiar senses (of **removal** or **association**)

===== Definitions =====

  - scrape
  - removal
  - association

===== Usage =====

abiding, gather together, cleave, smite with the scab.
