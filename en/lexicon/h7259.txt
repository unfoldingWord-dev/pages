====== H7259: רִבְקָה (Ribqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to clog by tying up the fetlock; fettering (by beauty);

===== Meaning =====

**Ribkah**, the wife of Isaac

===== Definitions =====

  - Ribkah

===== Usage =====

Rebekah.
