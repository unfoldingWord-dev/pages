====== H4729: מִקְטָר (miqṭâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6999|H6999]];

===== Meaning =====

something to **fume** (incense) on i.e. a **hearth** place

===== Definitions =====

  - fume
  - hearth

===== Usage =====

to burn...upon.
