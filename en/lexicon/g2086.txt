====== G2086: ἑτεροζυγέω (heterozygéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2087|G2087]] and [[en:lexicon:g2218|G2218]];

===== Meaning =====

to yoke up differently, i.e. (figuratively) to associate discordantly

===== Usage =====

unequally yoke together with.
