====== G3814: παιδίσκη (paidískē) ======

===== Source =====

feminine diminutive of [[en:lexicon:g3816|G3816]];

===== Meaning =====

a girl, i.e. (specially), a female slave or servant

===== Usage =====

bondmaid(-woman), damsel, maid(-en).
