====== H8082: שָׁמֵן (shâmên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8080|H8080]];

===== Meaning =====

**greasy**, i.e. **gross**; figuratively, **rich**

===== Definitions =====

  - greasy
  - gross
  - rich

===== Usage =====

fat, lusty, plenteous.
