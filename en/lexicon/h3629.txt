====== H3629: כִּלְיָה (kilyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h3627|H3627]] (only in the plural);

===== Meaning =====

a **kidney** (as an essential **organ**); figuratively, the **mind** (as the interior self)

===== Definitions =====

  - kidney
  - organ
  - mind

===== Usage =====

kidneys, reins.
