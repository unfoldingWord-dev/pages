====== G363: ἀναμιμνήσκω (anamimnḗskō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g3403|G3403]];

===== Meaning =====

to remind; (reflexively) to recollect

===== Usage =====

call to mind, (bring to , call to, put in), remember(-brance).
