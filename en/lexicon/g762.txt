====== G762: ἄσβεστος (ásbestos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4570|G4570]];

===== Meaning =====

not extinguished, i.e. (by implication) perpetual

===== Usage =====

not to be quenched, unquenchable.
