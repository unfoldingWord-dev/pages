====== H174: אֲהָלִים (ʼăhâlîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]] (only used thus in the plural); of foreign origin;

===== Meaning =====

**aloe wood** (i.e. sticks)

===== Definitions =====

  - aloe wood

===== Usage =====

(tree of lign-) aloes.
