====== H7682: שָׂגַב (sâgab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **make**) **lofty**, especially **inaccessible**; by implication, **safe**, **strong**; used literally and figuratively

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - lofty
  - inaccessible
  - safe
  - strong

===== Usage =====

defend, exalt, be excellent, (be, set on) high, lofty, be safe, set up (on high), be too strong.
