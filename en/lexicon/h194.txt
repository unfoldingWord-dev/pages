====== H194: אוּלַי (ʼûwlay) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h176|H176]];

===== Meaning =====

**if not**; hence perhaps

===== Definitions =====

  - if not

===== Usage =====

if so be, may be, peradventure, unless.
