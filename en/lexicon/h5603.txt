====== H5603: סָפַן (çâphan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **hide by covering**; specifically, to **roof** (passive participle as noun, a roof) or **wainscot**; figuratively, to **reserve**

===== Definitions =====

  - hide by covering
  - roof
  - wainscot
  - reserve

===== Usage =====

cieled, cover, seated.
