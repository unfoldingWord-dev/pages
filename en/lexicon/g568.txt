====== G568: ἀπέχω (apéchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

(actively) to have out, i.e. receive in full; (intransitively) to keep (oneself) away, i.e. be distant (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

be, have, receive.
