====== G1357: διόρθωσις (diórthōsis) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3717|G3717]], meaning to
 straighten thoroughly;

===== Meaning =====

rectification, i.e. (specially) the Messianic restauration

===== Usage =====

reformation.
