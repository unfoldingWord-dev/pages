====== G293: ἀμφίβληστρον (amphíblēstron) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of the base of [[en:lexicon:g297|G297]] and [[en:lexicon:g906|G906]];

===== Meaning =====

a (fishing) net (as thrown about the fish)

===== Usage =====
