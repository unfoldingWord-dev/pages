====== H2219: זָרָה (zârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2114|H2114]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **toss** about; by implication, to **diffuse**, **winnow**

===== Definitions =====

  - toss
  - diffuse
  - winnow

===== Usage =====

cast away, compass, disperse, fan, scatter (away), spread, strew, winnow.
