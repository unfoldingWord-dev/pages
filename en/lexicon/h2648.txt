====== H2648: חָפַז (châphaz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **start up suddenly**, i.e. (by implication) to **hasten away**, to **fear**

===== Definitions =====

  - start up suddenly
  - hasten away
  - fear

===== Usage =====

(make) haste (away), tremble.
