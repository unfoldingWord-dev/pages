====== H6316: פּוּט (Pûwṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun

===== Source =====

of foreign origin;

===== Meaning =====

**Put**, a son of Ham, also the name of his descendants or their region, and of a Persian tribe

===== Definitions =====

  - Put

===== Usage =====

Phut, Put.
