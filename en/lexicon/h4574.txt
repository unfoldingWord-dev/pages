====== H4574: מַעֲדָן (maʻădân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h5727|H5727]];

===== Meaning =====

a **delicacy** or (abstractly) **pleasure** (adverbially, cheerfully)

===== Definitions =====

  - delicacy
  - pleasure

===== Usage =====

dainty, delicately, delight.
