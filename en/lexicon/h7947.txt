====== H7947: שָׁלַב (shâlab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **space** off; intensive (**evenly**) to **make equi-distant**

===== Definitions =====

  - space
  - evenly
  - make equi-distant

===== Usage =====

equally distant, set in order.
