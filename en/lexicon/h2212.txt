====== H2212: זָקַק (zâqaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **strain**, (figuratively) **extract**, **clarify**

===== Definitions =====

  - strain
  - extract
  - clarify

===== Usage =====

fine, pour down, purge, purify, refine.
