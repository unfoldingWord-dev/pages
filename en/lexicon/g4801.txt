====== G4801: συζεύγνυμι (syzeúgnymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g2201|G2201]];

===== Meaning =====

to yoke together, i.e. (figuratively) conjoin (in marriage)

===== Usage =====

join together.
