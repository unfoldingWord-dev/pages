====== H7096: קָצָה (qâtsâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cut** off; (figuratively) to **destroy**; (partially) to **scrape** off

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - destroy
  - scrape

===== Usage =====

cut off, cut short, scrape (off).
