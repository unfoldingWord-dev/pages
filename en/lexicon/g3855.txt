====== G3855: παράγω (parágō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to lead near, i.e. (reflexively or intransitively) to go along or away

===== Usage =====

depart, pass (away, by, forth).
