====== H2063: זֹאת (zôʼth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

demonstrative pronoun

===== Source =====

irregular feminine of [[en:lexicon:h2088|H2088]];

===== Meaning =====

**this** (often used adverb)

===== Definitions =====

  - this

===== Usage =====

hereby (-in, -with), it, likewise, the one (other, same), she, so (much), such (deed), that, therefore, these, this (thing), thus.
