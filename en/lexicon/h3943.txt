====== H3943: לָפַת (lâphath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **bend**, i.e. (by implication) to **clasp**; also (reflexively) to **turn** around or aside

===== Definitions =====

  - bend
  - clasp
  - turn

===== Usage =====

take hold, turn aside (self).
