====== G4795: συγκυρία (synkyría) ======

===== Source =====

from a comparative of [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and κυρέω (kyréō) (to light or happen; from the
 base of [[en:lexicon:g2962|G2962]]);

===== Meaning =====

concurrence, i.e. accident

===== Usage =====

chance.
