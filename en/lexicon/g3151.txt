====== G3151: ματαιολόγος (mataiológos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3152|G3152]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]];

===== Meaning =====

an idle (i.e. senseless or mischievous) talker, i.e. a wrangler

===== Usage =====

vain talker.
