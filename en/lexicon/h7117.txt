====== H7117: קְצָת (qᵉtsâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7096|H7096]];

===== Meaning =====

a **termination** (literally or figuratively); also (by implication) a **portion**; adverbially (with prepositional prefix) **after**

===== Definitions =====

  - termination
  - portion
  - after

===== Usage =====

end, part, × some.
