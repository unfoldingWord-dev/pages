====== H6315: פּוּחַ (pûwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **puff**, i.e. **blow with the breath** or **air**; hence, to **fan** (as a breeze), to **utter**, to **kindle** (a fire), to **scoff**

===== Definitions =====

  - puff
  - blow with the breath
  - air
  - fan
  - utter
  - kindle
  - scoff

===== Usage =====

blow (upon), break, puff, bring into a snare, speak, utter.
