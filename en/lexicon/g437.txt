====== G437: ἀνθομολογέομαι (anthomologéomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g3670|G3670]];

===== Meaning =====

to confess in turn, i.e. respond in praise

===== Usage =====

give thanks.
