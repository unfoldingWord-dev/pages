====== G2184: ἐφήμερος (ephḗmeros) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g2250|G2250]];

===== Meaning =====

for a day ("ephemeral"), i.e. diurnal

===== Usage =====
