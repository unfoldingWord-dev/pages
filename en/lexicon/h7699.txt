====== H7699: שַׁד (shad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably from [[en:lexicon:h7736|H7736]] (in its original sense) contracted;

===== Meaning =====

the **breast of a woman** or **animal** (as bulging)

===== Definitions =====

  - breast of a woman
  - animal

===== Usage =====

breast, pap, teat.
