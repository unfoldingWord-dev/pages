====== H1249: בַּר (bar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1305|H1305]] (in its various senses);

===== Meaning =====

**beloved**; also **pure**, **empty**

===== Definitions =====

  - beloved
  - pure
  - empty

===== Usage =====

choice, clean, clear, pure.
