====== G2193: ἕως (héōs) ======

===== Source =====

of uncertain affinity;

===== Meaning =====

a conjunction, preposition and adverb of continuance, until (of time and place)

===== Usage =====

even (until, unto), (as) far (as), how long, (un-)til(-l), (hither-, un-, up) to, while(-s).
