====== G433: ἀνήκω (anḗkō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g2240|G2240]];

===== Meaning =====

to attain to, i.e. (figuratively) be proper

===== Usage =====

convenient, be fit.
