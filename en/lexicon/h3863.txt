====== H3863: לוּא (lûwʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

conjunction

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]];

===== Meaning =====

a conditional particle; **if**; by implication (interj. as a wish) **would that!**

===== Definitions =====

  - if
  - would that!

===== Usage =====

if (haply), peradventure, I pray thee, though, I would, would God (that).
