====== G2681: κατασκηνόω (kataskēnóō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g4637|G4637]];

===== Meaning =====

to camp down, i.e. haunt; figuratively, to remain

===== Usage =====

lodge, rest.
