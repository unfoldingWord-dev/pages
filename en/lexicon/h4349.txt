====== H4349: מָכוֹן (mâkôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3559|H3559]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **fixture**, i.e. a basis; generally a **place**, especially as an **abode**

===== Definitions =====

  - fixture
  - place
  - abode

===== Usage =====

foundation, habitation, (dwelling-, settled) place.
