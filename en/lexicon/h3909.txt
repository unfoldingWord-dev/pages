====== H3909: לָט (lâṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

a form of [[en:lexicon:h3814|H3814]] or else participle from [[en:lexicon:h3874|H3874]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **covered**, i.e. **secret**; by implication, **incantation**; also **secrecy** or (adverb) **covertly**

===== Definitions =====

  - covered
  - secret
  - incantation
  - secrecy
  - covertly

===== Usage =====

enchantment, privily, secretly, softly.
