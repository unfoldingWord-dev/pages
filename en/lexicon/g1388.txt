====== G1388: δόλος (dólos) ======

===== Source =====

from an obsolete primary verb, δέλλω (déllō) (probably meaning to decoy;
 compare [[en:lexicon:g1185|G1185]]);

===== Meaning =====

a trick (bait), i.e. (figuratively) wile

===== Usage =====

craft, deceit, guile, subtilty.
