====== H1379: גָּבַל (gâbal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h1366|H1366]]

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **twist** as a rope; to **bound** (as by a line)

===== Definitions =====

  - twist
  - bound

===== Usage =====

be border, set (bounds about).
