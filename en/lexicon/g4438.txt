====== G4438: πυκτέω (pyktéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of the same as [[en:lexicon:g4435|G4435]];

===== Meaning =====

to box (with the fist), i.e. contend (as a boxer) at the games (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

fight.
