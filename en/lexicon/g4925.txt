====== G4925: συνοικοδομέω (synoikodoméō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3618|G3618]];

===== Meaning =====

to construct, i.e. (passively) to compose (in company with other Christians, figuratively)

===== Usage =====

build together.
