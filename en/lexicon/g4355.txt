====== G4355: προσλαμβάνω (proslambánō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g2983|G2983]];

===== Meaning =====

to take to oneself, i.e. use (food), lead (aside), admit (to friendship or hospitality)

===== Usage =====

receive, take (unto).
