====== G4346: πρόσκλισις (prósklisis) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g2827|G2827]];

===== Meaning =====

a leaning towards, i.e. (figuratively) proclivity (favoritism)

===== Usage =====

partiality.
