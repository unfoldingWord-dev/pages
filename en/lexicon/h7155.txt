====== H7155: קִרְיַת חֻצוֹת (Qiryath Chutsôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7151|H7151]] and the feminine plural of [[en:lexicon:h2351|H2351]]; **city of streets**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kirjath-Chutsoth**, a place in Moab

===== Definitions =====

  - city of streets
  - Kirjath-Chutsoth

===== Usage =====

Kirjath-huzoth.
