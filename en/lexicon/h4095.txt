====== H4095: מַדְרֵגָה (madrêgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to step;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **step**; by implication, a **steep** or **inaccessible place**

===== Definitions =====

  - step
  - steep
  - inaccessible place

===== Usage =====

stair, steep place.
