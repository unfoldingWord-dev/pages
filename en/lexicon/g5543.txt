====== G5543: χρηστός (chrēstós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5530|G5530]];

===== Meaning =====

employed, i.e. (by implication) useful (in manner or morals)

===== Usage =====

better, easy, good(-ness), gracious, kind.
