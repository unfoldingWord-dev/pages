====== G1559: ἐκδιώκω (ekdiṓkō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g1377|G1377]];

===== Meaning =====

to pursue out, i.e. expel or persecute implacably

===== Usage =====

persecute.
