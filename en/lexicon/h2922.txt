====== H2922: טְלָא (ṭᵉlâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h2921|H2921]] in the (orig.) sense of **covering** (for protection); (compare [[en:lexicon:h2924|H2924]])

===== Meaning =====

a **lamb**

===== Definitions =====

  - covering
  - lamb

===== Usage =====

lamb.
