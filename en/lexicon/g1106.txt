====== G1106: γνώμη (gnṓmē) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1097|G1097]];

===== Meaning =====

cognition, i.e. (subjectively) opinion, or (objectively) resolve (counsel, consent, etc.)

===== Usage =====

advice, + agree, judgment, mind, purpose, will.
