====== G635: ἀποπλανάω (apoplanáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g4105|G4105]];

===== Meaning =====

to lead astray (figuratively); passively, to stray (from truth)

===== Usage =====

err, seduce.
