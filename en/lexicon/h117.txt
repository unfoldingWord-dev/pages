====== H117: אַדִּיר (ʼaddîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h142|H142]];

===== Meaning =====

**wide** or (generally) **large**; figuratively, **powerful**

===== Definitions =====

  - wide
  - large
  - powerful

===== Usage =====

excellent, famous, gallant, glorious, goodly, lordly, mighty(-ier one), noble, principal, worthy.
