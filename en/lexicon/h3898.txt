====== H3898: לָחַם (lâcham) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **feed on**; figuratively, to **consume**; by implication, to **battle** (as destruction)

===== Definitions =====

  - feed on
  - consume
  - battle

===== Usage =====

devour, eat, × ever, fight(-ing), overcome, prevail, (make) war(-ring).
