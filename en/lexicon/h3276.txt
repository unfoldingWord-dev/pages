====== H3276: יָעַל (yâʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **ascend**; figuratively, to **be valuable** (objectively; **useful**, subjectively; **benefited**)

===== Definitions =====

  - ascend
  - be valuable
  - useful
  - benefited

===== Usage =====

× at all, set forward, can do good, (be, have) profit, (able).
