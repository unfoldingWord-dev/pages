====== H8055: שָׂמַח (sâmach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

probably to **brighten** up, i.e. (figuratively) **be** (causatively, make) **blithe** or **gleesome**

===== Definitions =====

  - brighten
  - be
  - blithe
  - gleesome

===== Usage =====

cheer up, be (make) glad, (have, make) joy(-ful), be (make) merry, (cause to, make to) rejoice, × very.
