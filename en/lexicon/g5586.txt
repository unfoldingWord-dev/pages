====== G5586: ψῆφος (psēphos) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g5584|G5584]];

===== Meaning =====

a pebble (as worn smooth by handling), i.e. (by implication, of use as a counter or ballot) a verdict (of acquittal) or ticket (of admission); a vote

===== Usage =====

stone, voice.
