====== H2233: זֶרַע (zeraʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2232|H2232]];

===== Meaning =====

**seed**; figuratively, **fruit**, **plant**, **sowing-time**, **posterity**

===== Definitions =====

  - seed
  - fruit
  - plant
  - sowing-time
  - posterity

===== Usage =====

× carnally, child, fruitful, seed(-time), sowing-time.
