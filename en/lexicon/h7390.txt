====== H7390: רַךְ (rak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7401|H7401]];

===== Meaning =====

**tender** (literally or figuratively); by implication, **weak**

===== Definitions =====

  - tender
  - weak

===== Usage =====

faint((-hearted), soft, tender ((-hearted), one), weak.
