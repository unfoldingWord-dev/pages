====== H1626: גֵּרָה (gêrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1641|H1641]] (as in [[en:lexicon:h1625|H1625]]); properly, (like [[en:lexicon:h1620|H1620]]) a **kernel** (round as if **scraped**), i.e.

===== Meaning =====

a **gerah** or small weight (and coin)

===== Definitions =====

  - kernel
  - scraped
  - gerah

===== Usage =====

gerah.
