====== H4705: מִצְעָר (mitsʻâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6819|H6819]];

===== Meaning =====

**petty** (in size or number); adverbially, a **short** (time)

===== Definitions =====

  - petty
  - short

===== Usage =====

little one (while), small.
