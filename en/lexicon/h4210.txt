====== H4210: מִזְמוֹר (mizmôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2167|H2167]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, instrumental **music**; by implication, a **poem** set to notes

===== Definitions =====

  - music
  - poem

===== Usage =====

psalm.
