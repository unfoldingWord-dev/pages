====== G5294: ὑποτίθημι (hypotíthēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

to place underneath, i.e. (figuratively) to hazard, (reflexively) to suggest

===== Usage =====

lay down, put in remembrance.
