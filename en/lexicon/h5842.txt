====== H5842: עֵט (ʻêṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5860|H5860]] (contracted) in the sense of swooping, i.e. side-long stroke;

===== Meaning =====

a **stylus** or **marking stick**

===== Definitions =====

  - stylus
  - marking stick

===== Usage =====

pen.
