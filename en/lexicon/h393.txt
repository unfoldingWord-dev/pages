====== H393: אַכְזָר (ʼakzâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from an unused root (apparently meaning to act harshly);

===== Meaning =====

**violent**; by implication **deadly**; also (in a good sense) **brave**

===== Definitions =====

  - violent
  - deadly
  - brave

===== Usage =====

cruel, fierce.
