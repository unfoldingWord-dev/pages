====== G5378: Φιλόλογος (Philólogos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5384|G5384]] and [[en:lexicon:g3056|G3056]];

===== Meaning =====

fond of words, i.e. talkative (argumentative, learned, "philological"); Philologus, a Christian

===== Usage =====

Philologus.
