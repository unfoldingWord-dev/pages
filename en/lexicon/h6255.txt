====== H6255: עַשְׁתְּרֹת קַרְנַיִם (ʻAshtᵉrôth Qarnayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6252|H6252]] and the dual of [[en:lexicon:h7161|H7161]]; Ashtaroth of (the) double horns (a symbol of the deity);

===== Meaning =====

**Ashteroth-Karnaim**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Ashteroth-Karnaim

===== Usage =====

Ashtoreth Karnaim.
