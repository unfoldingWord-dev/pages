====== H4917: מִשְׁלַחַת (mishlachath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4916|H4916]];

===== Meaning =====

a **mission**, i.e. (abstractly and favorable) **release**, or (concretely and unfavorable) an **army**

===== Definitions =====

  - mission
  - release
  - army

===== Usage =====

discharge, sending.
