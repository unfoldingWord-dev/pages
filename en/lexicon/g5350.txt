====== G5350: φθέγγομαι (phthéngomai) ======

===== Source =====

probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g5338|G5338]] and thus to [[en:lexicon:g5346|G5346]];

===== Meaning =====

to utter a clear sound, i.e. (generally) to proclaim

===== Usage =====

speak.
