====== G4843: συμπεριλαμβάνω (symperilambánō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2983|G2983]];

===== Meaning =====

to take by enclosing altogether, i.e. earnestly throw the arms about one

===== Usage =====

embrace.
