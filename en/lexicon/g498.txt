====== G498: ἀντιτάσσομαι (antitássomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g5021|G5021]];

===== Meaning =====

to range oneself against, i.e. oppose

===== Usage =====

oppose themselves, resist.
