====== G2526: καθό (kathó) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g3739|G3739]];

===== Meaning =====

according to which thing, i.e. precisely as, in proportion as

===== Usage =====

according to that, (inasmuch) as.
