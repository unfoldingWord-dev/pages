====== G5221: ὑπαντάω (hypantáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]];

===== Meaning =====

to go opposite (meet) under (quietly), i.e. to encounter, fall in with

===== Usage =====

(go to) meet.
