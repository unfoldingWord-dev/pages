====== H3152: יִזְלִיאָה (Yizlîyʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

perhaps from an unused root (meaning to draw up); he will draw out;

===== Meaning =====

**Jizliah**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Jizliah

===== Usage =====

Jezliah.
