====== H7277: רִגְמָה (rigmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of the same as [[en:lexicon:h7276|H7276]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pile** (of stones), i.e. (figuratively) a **throng**

===== Definitions =====

  - pile
  - throng

===== Usage =====

council.
