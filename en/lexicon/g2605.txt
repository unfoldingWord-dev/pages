====== G2605: καταγγέλλω (katangéllō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g32|G32]];

===== Meaning =====

to proclaim, promulgate

===== Usage =====

declare, preach, shew, speak of, teach.
