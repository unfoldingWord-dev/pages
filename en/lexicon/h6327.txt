====== H6327: פּוּץ (pûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **dash** in pieces, literally or figuratively (especially to **disperse**)

===== Definitions =====

  - dash
  - disperse

===== Usage =====

break (dash, shake) in (to) pieces, cast (abroad), disperse (selves), drive, retire, scatter (abroad), spread abroad.
