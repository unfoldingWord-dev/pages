====== H7267: רֹגֶז (rôgez) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7264|H7264]];

===== Meaning =====

**commotion**, **restlessness** (of a horse), **crash** (of thunder), **disquiet**, **anger**

===== Definitions =====

  - commotion
  - restlessness
  - crash
  - disquiet
  - anger

===== Usage =====

fear, noise, rage, trouble(-ing), wrath.
