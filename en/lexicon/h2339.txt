====== H2339: חוּט (chûwṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to sew;

===== Meaning =====

a **string**; by implication, a **measuring tape**

===== Definitions =====

  - string
  - measuring tape

===== Usage =====

cord, fillet, line, thread.
