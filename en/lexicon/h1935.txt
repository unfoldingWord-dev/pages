====== H1935: הוֹד (hôwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root;

===== Meaning =====

**grandeur** (i.e. an imposing form and appearance)

===== Definitions =====

  - grandeur

===== Usage =====

beauty, comeliness, excellency, glorious, glory, goodly, honour, majesty.
