====== G1828: ἐξέλκω (exélkō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g1670|G1670]];

===== Meaning =====

to drag forth, i.e. (figuratively) to entice (to sin)

===== Usage =====

draw away.
