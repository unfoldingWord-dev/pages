====== H3430: יִשְׁבּוֹ בְּנֹב (Yishbôw bᵉ-Nôb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3427|H3427]] and [[en:lexicon:h5011|H5011]], with a pronominal suffix and a preposition interposed; his dwelling (is) in Nob;

===== Meaning =====

**Jishbo-be-Nob**, a Philistine

===== Definitions =====

  - Jishbo-be-Nob

===== Usage =====

Ishbi-benob (from the margin).
