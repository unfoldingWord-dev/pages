====== H4841: מֶרְקָחָה (merqâchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4840|H4840]];

===== Meaning =====

abstractly, a **seasoning** (with spicery); concretely, an **unguentkettle** (for preparing spiced oil)

===== Definitions =====

  - seasoning
  - unguentkettle

===== Usage =====

pot of ointment, × well.
