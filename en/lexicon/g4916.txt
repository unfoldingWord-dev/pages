====== G4916: συνθάπτω (syntháptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2290|G2290]];

===== Meaning =====

to inter in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to assimilate spiritually (to Christ by a sepulture as to sin)

===== Usage =====

bury with.
