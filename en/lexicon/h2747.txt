====== H2747: חֶרֶט (chereṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from a primitive root meaning to **engrave**;

===== Meaning =====

a **chisel** or **graver**; also a **style** for writing

===== Definitions =====

  - engrave
  - chisel
  - graver
  - style

===== Usage =====

graving tool, pen.
