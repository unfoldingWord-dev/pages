====== H1293: בְּרָכָה (Bᵉrâkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1288|H1288]];

===== Meaning =====

**benediction**; by implication **prosperity**

===== Definitions =====

  - benediction
  - prosperity

===== Usage =====

blessing, liberal, pool, present.
