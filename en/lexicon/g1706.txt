====== G1706: ἐμπίπτω (empíptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g4098|G4098]];

===== Meaning =====

to fall on, i.e. (literally) to be entrapped by, or (figuratively) be overwhelmed with

===== Usage =====

fall among (into).
