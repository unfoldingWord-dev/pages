====== H8071: שִׂמְלָה (simlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

perhaps by permutation for the feminine of [[en:lexicon:h5566|H5566]] (through the idea of a **cover** assuming the shape of the object beneath);

===== Meaning =====

a **dress**, especially a **mantle**

===== Definitions =====

  - cover
  - dress
  - mantle

===== Usage =====

apparel, cloth(-es, -ing), garment, raiment. Compare
