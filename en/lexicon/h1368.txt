====== H1368: גִּבּוֹר (gibbôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (shortened) intensive from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1397|H1397]];

===== Meaning =====

**powerful**; by implication, **warrior**, **tyrant**

===== Definitions =====

  - powerful
  - warrior
  - tyrant

===== Usage =====

champion, chief, × excel, giant, man, mighty (man, one), strong (man), valiant man.
