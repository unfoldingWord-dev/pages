====== G482: ἀντιλαμβάνομαι (antilambánomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g2983|G2983]];

===== Meaning =====

to take hold of in turn, i.e. succor; also to participate

===== Usage =====

help, partaker, support.
