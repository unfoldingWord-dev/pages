====== H2947: טֵפַח (ṭêphach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2946|H2946]];

===== Meaning =====

a **spread of the hand**, i.e. a **palm-breadth** (not 'span' of the fingers); architecturally, a **corbel** (as a supporting palm)

===== Definitions =====

  - spread of the hand
  - palm-breadth
  - corbel

===== Usage =====

coping, hand-breadth.
