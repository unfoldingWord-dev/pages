====== H7832: שָׂחַק (sâchaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **laugh** (in pleasure or detraction); by implication, to **play**

===== Definitions =====

  - laugh
  - play

===== Usage =====

deride, have in derision, laugh, make merry, mock(-er), play, rejoice, (laugh to) scorn, be in (make) sport.
