====== G5551: χρονοτριβέω (chronotribéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g5550|G5550]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5147|G5147]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a time-wearer, i.e. to procrastinate (linger)

===== Usage =====

spend time.
