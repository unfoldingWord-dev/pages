====== H7561: רָשַׁע (râshaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **do** or **declare**) **wrong**; by implication, to **disturb**, **violate**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - do
  - declare
  - wrong
  - disturb
  - violate

===== Usage =====

condemn, make trouble, vex, be (commit, deal, depart, do) wicked(-ly, -ness).
