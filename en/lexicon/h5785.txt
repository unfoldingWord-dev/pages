====== H5785: עוֹר (ʻôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5783|H5783]];

===== Meaning =====

**skin** (as naked); by implication, **hide**, **leather**

===== Definitions =====

  - skin
  - hide
  - leather

===== Usage =====

hide, leather, skin.
