====== H3576: כָּזַב (kâzab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **lie** (i.e. **deceive**), literally or figuratively

===== Definitions =====

  - lie
  - deceive

===== Usage =====

fail, (be found a, make a) liar, lie, lying, be in vain.
