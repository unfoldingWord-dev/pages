====== G1686: ἐμβάπτω (embáptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g911|G911]];

===== Meaning =====

to whelm on, i.e. wet (a part of the person, etc.) by contact with a fluid

===== Usage =====

dip.
