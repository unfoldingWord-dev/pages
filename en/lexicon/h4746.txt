====== H4746: מְקָרֶה (mᵉqâreh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7136|H7136]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **meeting**, i.e. a **frame** (of timbers)

===== Definitions =====

  - meeting
  - frame

===== Usage =====

building.
