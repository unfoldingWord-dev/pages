====== G5272: ὑπόκρισις (hypókrisis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5271|G5271]];

===== Meaning =====

acting under a feigned part, i.e. (figuratively) deceit ("hypocrisy")

===== Usage =====

condemnation, dissimulation, hypocrisy.
