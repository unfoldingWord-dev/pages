====== G1326: διεγείρω (diegeírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g1453|G1453]];

===== Meaning =====

to wake fully; i.e. arouse (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

arise, awake, raise, stir up.
