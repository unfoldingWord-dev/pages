====== G3614: οἰκία (oikía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3624|G3624]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, residence (abstractly), but usually (concretely) an abode (literally or figuratively); by implication, a family (especially domestics)

===== Usage =====

home, house(-hold).
