====== H5344: נָקַב (nâqab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **puncture**, literally (to **perforate**, with more or less violence) or figuratively (to **specify**, **designate**, **libel**)

===== Definitions =====

  - puncture
  - perforate
  - specify
  - designate
  - libel

===== Usage =====

appoint, blaspheme, bore, curse, express, with holes, name, pierce, strike through.
