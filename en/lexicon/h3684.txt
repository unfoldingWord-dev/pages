====== H3684: כְּסִיל (kᵉçîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3688|H3688]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **fat**, i.e. (figuratively) **stupid** or **silly**

===== Definitions =====

  - fat
  - stupid
  - silly

===== Usage =====

fool(-ish).
