====== G2113: εὐθυδρομέω (euthydroméō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2117|G2117]] and [[en:lexicon:g1408|G1408]];

===== Meaning =====

to lay a straight course, i.e. sail direct

===== Usage =====

(come) with a straight course.
