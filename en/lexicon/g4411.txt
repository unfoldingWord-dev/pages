====== G4411: πρωτοκλισία (prōtoklisía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4413|G4413]] and [[en:lexicon:g2828|G2828]];

===== Meaning =====

a reclining first (in the place of honor) at the dinner-bed, i.e. preeminence at meals

===== Usage =====

chief (highest, uppermost) room.
