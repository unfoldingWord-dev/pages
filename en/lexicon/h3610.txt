====== H3610: כִּלְאַיִם (kilʼayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

dual of [[en:lexicon:h3608|H3608]] in the original sense of separation;

===== Meaning =====

**two heterogeneities**

===== Definitions =====

  - two heterogeneities

===== Usage =====

divers seeds (-e kinds), mingled (seed).
