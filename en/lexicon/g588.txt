====== G588: ἀποδέχομαι (apodéchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g1209|G1209]];

===== Meaning =====

to take fully, i.e. welcome (persons), approve (things)

===== Usage =====

accept, receive (gladly).
