====== G3735: ὄρος (óros) ======

===== Source =====

probably from an obsolete ὄρω (órō) (to rise or "rear"; perhaps akin to [[en:lexicon:g142|G142]];
 compare [[en:lexicon:g3733|G3733]]);

===== Meaning =====

a mountain (as lifting itself above the plain)

===== Usage =====

hill, mount(-ain).
