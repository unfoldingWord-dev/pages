====== H8029: שִׁלֵּשׁ (shillêsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8027|H8027]];

===== Meaning =====

a descendant of the **third** degree, i.e. **great grandchild**

===== Definitions =====

  - third
  - great grandchild

===== Usage =====

third (generation).
