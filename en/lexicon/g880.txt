====== G880: ἄφωνος (áphōnos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5456|G5456]];

===== Meaning =====

voiceless, i.e. mute (by nature or choice); figuratively, unmeaning

===== Usage =====

dumb, without signification.
