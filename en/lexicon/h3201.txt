====== H3201: יָכֹל (yâkôl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or (fuller) [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be able**, literally (**can**, **could**) or morally (**may**, **might**)

===== Definitions =====

  - be able
  - can
  - could
  - may
  - might

===== Usage =====

be able, any at all (ways), attain, can (away with, (-not)), could, endure, might, overcome, have power, prevail, still, suffer.
