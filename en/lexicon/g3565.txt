====== G3565: νύμφη (nýmphē) ======

===== Source =====

from a primary but obsolete verb νύπτω (nýptō) (to veil as a bride; compare
 Latin "nupto," to marry);

===== Meaning =====

a young married woman (as veiled), including a betrothed girl; by implication, a son's wife

===== Usage =====

bride, daughter in law.
