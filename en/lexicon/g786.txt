====== G786: ἄσπονδος (áspondos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4689|G4689]];

===== Meaning =====

literally, without libation (which usually accompanied a treaty), i.e. (by implication) truceless

===== Usage =====

implacable, truce-breaker.
