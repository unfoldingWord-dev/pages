====== H2003: הָמָס (hâmâç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to **crackle**;

===== Meaning =====

a dry **twig** or **brushwood**

===== Definitions =====

  - crackle
  - twig
  - brushwood

===== Usage =====

melting.
