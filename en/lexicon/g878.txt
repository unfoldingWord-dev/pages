====== G878: ἄφρων (áphrōn) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5424|G5424]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, mindless, i.e. stupid, (by implication) ignorant, (specially) egotistic, (practically) rash, or (morally) unbelieving

===== Usage =====

fool(-ish), unwise.
