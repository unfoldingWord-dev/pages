====== H5496: סוּת (çûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

perhaps denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7898|H7898]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **prick**, i.e. (figuratively) **stimulate**; by implication, to **seduce**

===== Definitions =====

  - prick
  - stimulate
  - seduce

===== Usage =====

entice, move, persuade, provoke, remove, set on, stir up, take away.
