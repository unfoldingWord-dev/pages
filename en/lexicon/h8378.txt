====== H8378: תַּאֲוָה (taʼăvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h183|H183]] (abbreviated);

===== Meaning =====

a **longing**; by implication, a **delight** (subjectively, satisfaction, objectively, a charm)

===== Definitions =====

  - longing
  - delight

===== Usage =====

dainty, desire, × exceedingly, × greedily, lust(ing), pleasant. See also
