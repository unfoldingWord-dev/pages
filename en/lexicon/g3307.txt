====== G3307: μερίζω (merízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3313|G3313]];

===== Meaning =====

to part, i.e. (literally) to apportion, bestow, share, or (figuratively) to disunite, differ

===== Usage =====

deal, be difference between, distribute, divide, give participle
