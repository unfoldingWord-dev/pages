====== G4790: συγκοινωνέω (synkoinōnéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2841|G2841]];

===== Meaning =====

to share in company with, i.e. co-participate in

===== Usage =====

communicate (have fellowship) with, be partaker of.
