====== H8389: תֹּאַר (tôʼar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8388|H8388]];

===== Meaning =====

**outline**, i.e. **figure** or **appearance**

===== Definitions =====

  - outline
  - figure
  - appearance

===== Usage =====

  beautiful, × comely, countenance,   fair, × favoured, form, × goodly, × resemble, visage.
