====== G578: ἀποβλέπω (apoblépō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g991|G991]];

===== Meaning =====

to look away from everything else, i.e. (figuratively) intently regard

===== Usage =====

have respect.
