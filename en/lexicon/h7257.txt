====== H7257: רָבַץ (râbats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **crouch** (on all four legs folded, like a recumbent animal); by implication, to **recline**, **repose**, **brood**, **lurk**, **imbed**

===== Definitions =====

  - crouch
  - recline
  - repose
  - brood
  - lurk
  - imbed

===== Usage =====

crouch (down), fall down, make a fold, lay, (cause to, make to) lie (down), make to rest, sit.
