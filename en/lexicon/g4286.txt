====== G4286: πρόθεσις (próthesis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4388|G4388]];

===== Meaning =====

a setting forth, i.e. (figuratively) proposal (intention); specially, the show-bread (in the Temple) as exposed before God

===== Usage =====

purpose, shew(-bread).
