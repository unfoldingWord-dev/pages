====== H4601: מַעֲכָה (Maʻăkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Joshua 13:13), from [[en:lexicon:h4600|H4600]]; depression;

===== Meaning =====

**Maakah** (or Maakath), the name of a place in Syria, also of a Mesopotamian, of three Israelites, and of four Israelitesses and one Syrian woman

===== Definitions =====

  - Maakah

===== Usage =====

Maachah, Maachathites. See also
