====== G1259: διαλλάσσω (diallássō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g236|G236]];

===== Meaning =====

to change thoroughly, i.e. (mentally) to conciliate

===== Usage =====
