====== G4225: πού (poú) ======

===== Source =====

genitive case of an indefinite pronoun πός (pós) (some) otherwise obsolete
 (compare [[en:lexicon:g4214|G4214]]);

===== Meaning =====

as adverb of place, somewhere, i.e. nearly

===== Usage =====

about, a certain place.
