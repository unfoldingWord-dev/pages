====== H2399: חֵטְא (chêṭᵉʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2398|H2398]];

===== Meaning =====

a **crime** or its **penalty**

===== Definitions =====

  - crime
  - penalty

===== Usage =====

fault, × grievously, offence, (punishment of) sin.
