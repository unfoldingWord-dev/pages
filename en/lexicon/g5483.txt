====== G5483: χαρίζομαι (charízomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g5485|G5485]];

===== Meaning =====

to grant as a favor, i.e. gratuitously, in kindness, pardon or rescue

===== Usage =====

deliver, (frankly) forgive, (freely) give, grant.
