====== H7169: קָרַץ (qârats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pinch**, i.e. (partially) to **bite the lips**, **blink the eyes** (as a gesture of malice), or (fully) to **squeeze off** (a piece of clay in order to mould a vessel from it)

===== Definitions =====

  - pinch
  - bite the lips
  - blink the eyes
  - squeeze off

===== Usage =====

form, move, wink.
