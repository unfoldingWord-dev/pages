====== H7523: רָצַח (râtsach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **dash** in pieces, i.e. **kill** (a human being), especially to **murder**

===== Definitions =====

  - dash
  - kill
  - murder

===== Usage =====

put to death, kill, (man-) slay(-er), murder(-er).
