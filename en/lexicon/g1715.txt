====== G1715: ἔμπροσθεν (émprosthen) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]];

===== Meaning =====

in front of (in place (literally or figuratively) or time)

===== Usage =====

against, at, before, (in presence, sight) of.
