====== G4647: σκόλοψ (skólops) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4628|G4628]] and [[en:lexicon:g3700|G3700]];

===== Meaning =====

withered at the front, i.e. a point or prickle (figuratively, a bodily annoyance or disability)

===== Usage =====

thorn.
