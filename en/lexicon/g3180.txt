====== G3180: μεθοδεία (methodeía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g3593|G3593]] (compare "method");

===== Meaning =====

travelling over, i.e. travesty (trickery)

===== Usage =====

wile, lie in wait.
