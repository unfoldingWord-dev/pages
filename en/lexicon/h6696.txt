====== H6696: צוּר (tsûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cramp**, i.e. **confine** (in many applications, literally and figuratively, formative or hostile)

===== Definitions =====

  - cramp
  - confine

===== Usage =====

adversary, assault, beset, besiege, bind (up), cast, distress, fashion, fortify, inclose, lay siege, put up in bags.
