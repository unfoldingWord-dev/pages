====== H113: אָדוֹן (ʼâdôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root (meaning to **rule**);

===== Meaning =====

**sovereign**, i.e. **controller** (human or divine)

===== Definitions =====

  - rule
  - sovereign
  - controller

===== Usage =====

lord, master, owner. Compare also names beginning with 'Adoni-'.
