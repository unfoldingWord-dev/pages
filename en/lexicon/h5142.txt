====== H5142: נְזַק (nᵉzaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to the root of [[en:lexicon:h5143|H5143]];

===== Meaning =====

to **suffer** (causatively, **inflict**) **loss**

===== Definitions =====

  - suffer
  - inflict
  - loss

===== Usage =====

have (en-) damage, hurt(-ful).
