====== H22: אֲבִיאֵל (ʼĂbîyʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; father (i.e. possessor) of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Abiel**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Abiel

===== Usage =====

Abiel.
