====== G3949: παροργίζω (parorgízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g3710|G3710]];

===== Meaning =====

to anger alongside, i.e. enrage

===== Usage =====
