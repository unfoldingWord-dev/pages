====== H1755: דּוֹר (dôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1752|H1752]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **revolution** of time, i.e. an **age** or generation; also a **dwelling**

===== Definitions =====

  - revolution
  - age
  - dwelling

===== Usage =====

age, × evermore, generation, (n-) ever, posterity.
