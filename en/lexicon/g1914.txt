====== G1914: ἐπιβλέπω (epiblépō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g991|G991]];

===== Meaning =====

to gaze at (with favor, pity or partiality)

===== Usage =====

look upon, regard, have respect to.
