====== G3942: παροιμία (paroimía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and perhaps a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3633|G3633]];

===== Meaning =====

apparently a state alongside of supposition, i.e. (concretely) an adage; specially, an enigmatical or fictitious illustration

===== Usage =====

parable, proverb.
