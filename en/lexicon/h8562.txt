====== H8562: תַּמְרוּק (tamrûwq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h4838|H4838]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **scouring**, i.e. **soap** or **perfumery** for the bath; figuratively, a **detergent**

===== Definitions =====

  - scouring
  - soap
  - perfumery
  - detergent

===== Usage =====

× cleanse, (thing for) purification(-fying).
