====== G4683: σπαργανόω (sparganóō) ======

===== Source =====

from σπάργανον (spárganon) (a strip; from a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g4682|G4682]] meaning
 to strap or wrap with strips);

===== Meaning =====

to swathe (an infant after the Oriental custom)

===== Usage =====

wrap in swaddling clothes.
