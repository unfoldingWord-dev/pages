====== G989: βλάσφημος (blásphēmos) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g984|G984]] and [[en:lexicon:g5345|G5345]];

===== Meaning =====

scurrilious, i.e. calumnious (against men), or (specially) impious (against God)

===== Usage =====

blasphemer(-mous), railing.
