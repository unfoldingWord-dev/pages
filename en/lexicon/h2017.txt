====== H2017: הֹפֶךְ (hôphek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2015|H2015]];

===== Meaning =====

an **upset**, i.e. (abstract) **perversity**

===== Definitions =====

  - upset
  - perversity

===== Usage =====

turning of things upside down.
