====== H7211: רְאָיָה (Rᵉʼâyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7200|H7200]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; **Jah has seen**;

===== Meaning =====

**Reajah**, the name of three Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has seen
  - Reajah

===== Usage =====

Reaia, Reaiah.
