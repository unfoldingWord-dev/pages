====== H8033: שָׁם (shâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

a primitive particle (rather from the relative pronoun, [[en:lexicon:h834|H834]]);

===== Meaning =====

**there** (transferring to time) **then**; often **thither**, or **thence**

===== Definitions =====

  - there
  - then
  - thither
  - thence

===== Usage =====

in it,   thence, there (-in,   of,   out),   thither,   whither.
