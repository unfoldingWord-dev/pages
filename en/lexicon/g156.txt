====== G156: αἰτία (aitía) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g154|G154]];

===== Meaning =====

a cause (as if asked for), i.e. (logical) reason (motive, matter), (legal) crime (alleged or proved)

===== Usage =====

accusation, case, cause, crime, fault, (wh-)ere(-fore).
