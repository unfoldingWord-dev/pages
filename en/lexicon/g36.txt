====== G36: ἀγενής (agenḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g1085|G1085]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, without kin, i.e. (of unknown descent, and by implication) ignoble

===== Usage =====

base things.
