====== G2379: θυσιαστήριον (thysiastḗrion) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2378|G2378]];

===== Meaning =====

a place of sacrifice, i.e. an altar (special or genitive case, literal or figurative)

===== Usage =====

altar.
