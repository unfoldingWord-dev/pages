====== H4712: מֵצַר (mêtsar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6869|H6869]];

===== Meaning =====

something **tight**, i.e. (figuratively) **trouble**

===== Definitions =====

  - tight
  - trouble

===== Usage =====

distress, pain, strait.
