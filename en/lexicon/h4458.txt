====== H4458: מֶלְתָּחָה (meltâchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **spread out**;

===== Meaning =====

a **wardrobe** (i.e. room where clothing is spread)

===== Definitions =====

  - spread out
  - wardrobe

===== Usage =====

vestry.
