====== H8273: שָׁרָב (shârâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **glare**;

===== Meaning =====

quivering **glow** (of the air), especially the **mirage**

===== Definitions =====

  - glare
  - glow
  - mirage

===== Usage =====

heat, parched ground.
