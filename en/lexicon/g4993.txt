====== G4993: σωφρονέω (sōphronéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4998|G4998]];

===== Meaning =====

to be of sound mind, i.e. sane, (figuratively) moderate

===== Usage =====

be in right mind, be sober (minded), soberly.
