====== H1415: גָּדָה (gâdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to **cut** off);

===== Meaning =====

a **border** of a river (as **cut** into by the stream)

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - border
  - cut

===== Usage =====

bank.
