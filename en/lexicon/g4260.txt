====== G4260: προβαίνω (probaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to walk forward, i.e. advance (literally, or in years)

===== Usage =====

+ be of a great age, go farther (on), be well stricken.
