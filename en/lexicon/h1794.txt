====== H1794: דָּכָה (dâkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h1790|H1790]], [[en:lexicon:h1792|H1792]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **collapse** (phys. or mentally)

===== Definitions =====

  - collapse

===== Usage =====

break (sore), contrite, crouch.
