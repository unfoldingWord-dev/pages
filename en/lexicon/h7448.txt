====== H7448: רֶסֶן (reçen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **curb**;

===== Meaning =====

a **halter** (as **restraining**); by implication, the **jaw**

===== Definitions =====

  - curb
  - halter
  - restraining
  - jaw

===== Usage =====

bridle.
