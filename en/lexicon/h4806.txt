====== H4806: מְרִיא (mᵉrîyʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4754|H4754]] in the sense of **grossness**, through the idea of **domineering** (compare [[en:lexicon:h4756|H4756]]);

===== Meaning =====

**stall-fed**; often (as noun) a **beeve**

===== Definitions =====

  - grossness
  - domineering
  - stall-fed
  - beeve

===== Usage =====

fat (fed) beast (cattle, -ling).
