====== G654: ἀποστρέφω (apostréphō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g4762|G4762]];

===== Meaning =====

to turn away or back (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

bring again, pervert, turn away (from).
