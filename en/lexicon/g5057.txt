====== G5057: τελώνης (telṓnēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5056|G5056]] and [[en:lexicon:g5608|G5608]];

===== Meaning =====

a tax-farmer, i.e. collector of public revenue

===== Usage =====

publican.
