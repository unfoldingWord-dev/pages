====== H815: אֵשֶׁל (ʼêshel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from a root of uncertain signification;

===== Meaning =====

a **tamarisk tree**; by extension, a **grove** of any kind

===== Definitions =====

  - tamarisk tree
  - grove

===== Usage =====

grove, tree.
