====== H4999: נָאָה (nâʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4998|H4998]];

===== Meaning =====

a **home**; figuratively, a **pasture**

===== Definitions =====

  - home
  - pasture

===== Usage =====

habitation, house, pasture, pleasant place.
