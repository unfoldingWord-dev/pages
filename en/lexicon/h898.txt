====== H898: בָּגַד (bâgad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cover** (with a garment); figuratively, to **act covertly**; by implication, to **pillage**

===== Definitions =====

  - cover
  - act covertly
  - pillage

===== Usage =====

deal deceitfully (treacherously, unfaithfully), offend, transgress(-or), (depart), treacherous (dealer, -ly, man), unfaithful(-ly, man), × very.
