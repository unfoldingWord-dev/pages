====== H4855: מַשָּׁא (mashshâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5383|H5383]];

===== Meaning =====

a **loan**; by implication, **interest on a debt**

===== Definitions =====

  - loan
  - interest on a debt

===== Usage =====

exaction, usury.
