====== G3734: ὁροθεσία (horothesía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3725|G3725]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

a limit-placing, i.e. (concretely) boundary-line

===== Usage =====
