====== G4959: συστενάζω (systenázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g4727|G4727]];

===== Meaning =====

to moan jointly, i.e. (figuratively) experience a common calamity

===== Usage =====

groan together.
