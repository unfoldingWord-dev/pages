====== H168: אֹהֶל (ʼôhel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h166|H166]];

===== Meaning =====

a **tent** (as clearly conspicuous from a distance)

===== Definitions =====

  - tent

===== Usage =====

covering, (dwelling) (place), home, tabernacle, tent.
