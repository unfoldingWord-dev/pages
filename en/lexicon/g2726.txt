====== G2726: κατήφεια (katḗpheia) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and perhaps a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]]
 (meaning downcast in look);

===== Meaning =====

demureness, i.e. (by implication) sadness

===== Usage =====

heaviness.
