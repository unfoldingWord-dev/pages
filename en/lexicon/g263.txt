====== G263: ἀμάραντος (amárantos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3133|G3133]];

===== Meaning =====

unfading, i.e. (by implication) perpetual

===== Usage =====

that fadeth not away.
