====== H7621: שְׁבוּעָה (shᵉbûwʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h7650|H7650]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **sworn**, i.e. an **oath**

===== Definitions =====

  - sworn
  - oath

===== Usage =====

curse, oath, × sworn.
