====== G1987: ἐπίσταμαι (epístamai) ======

===== Source =====

apparently a middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g2186|G2186]] (with [[en:lexicon:g3563|G3563]] implied);

===== Meaning =====

to put the mind upon, i.e. comprehend, or be acquainted with

===== Usage =====

know, understand.
