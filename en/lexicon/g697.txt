====== G697: Ἄρειος Πάγος (Áreios Págos) ======

===== Source =====

from Ἄρης (Árēs) (the name of the Greek deity of war) and a derivative of
 [[en:lexicon:g4078|G4078]];

===== Meaning =====

rock of Ares, a place in Athens

===== Usage =====

Areopagus, Mars' Hill.
