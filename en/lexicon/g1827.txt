====== G1827: ἐξελέγχω (exelénchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g1651|G1651]];

===== Meaning =====

to convict fully, i.e. (by implication) to punish

===== Usage =====

convince.
