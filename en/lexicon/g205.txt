====== G205: ἀκροθίνιον (akrothínion) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g206|G206]] and θίς (thís) (a heap);

===== Meaning =====

properly (in the plural) the top of the heap, i.e. (by implication) best of the booty

===== Usage =====

spoils.
