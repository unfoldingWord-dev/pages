====== H41: אֲבִינָדָב (ʼĂbîynâdâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h5068|H5068]]; father of generosity (i.e. liberal);

===== Meaning =====

**Abinadab**, the name of four Israelites

  - Abinadab

===== Usage =====
