====== H8056: שָׂמֵחַ (sâmêach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8055|H8055]];

===== Meaning =====

**blithe** or **gleeful**

===== Definitions =====

  - blithe
  - gleeful

===== Usage =====

(be) glad, joyful, (making) merry((-hearted), -ily), rejoice(-ing).
