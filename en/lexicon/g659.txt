====== G659: ἀποτίθημι (apotíthēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

to put away (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

cast off, lay apart (aside, down), put away (off).
