====== H3348: יָקֶה (Yâqeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to obey; obedient;

===== Meaning =====

**Jakeh**, a symbolical name (for Solomon)

===== Definitions =====

  - Jakeh

===== Usage =====

Jakeh.
