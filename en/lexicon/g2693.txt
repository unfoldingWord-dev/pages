====== G2693: καταστρώννυμι (katastrṓnnymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g4766|G4766]];

===== Meaning =====

to strew down, i.e. (by implication) to prostrate (slay)

===== Usage =====

overthrow.
