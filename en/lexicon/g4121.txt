====== G4121: πλεονάζω (pleonázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4119|G4119]];

===== Meaning =====

to do, make or be more, i.e. increase (transitively or intransitively); by extension, to superabound

===== Usage =====

abound, abundant, make to increase, have over.
