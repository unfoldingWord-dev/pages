====== H5693: עָגוּר (ʻâgûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive part (but with active sense) of an unused root meaning to **twitter**;

===== Meaning =====

probably the **swallow**

===== Definitions =====

  - twitter
  - swallow

===== Usage =====

swallow.
