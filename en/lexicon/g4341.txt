====== G4341: προσκαλέομαι (proskaléomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g2564|G2564]];

===== Meaning =====

to call toward oneself, i.e. summon, invite

===== Usage =====

call (for, to, unto).
