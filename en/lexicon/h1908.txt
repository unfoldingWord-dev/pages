====== H1908: הֲדַד (Hădad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably of foreign origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h111|H111]]);

===== Meaning =====

**Hadad**, the name of an idol, and of several kings of Edom
