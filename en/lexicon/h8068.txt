====== H8068: שָׁמִיר (shâmîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8104|H8104]] in the original sense of **pricking**;

===== Meaning =====

a **thorn**; also (from its **keenness** for scratching) a gem, probably the **diamond**

===== Definitions =====

  - pricking
  - thorn
  - keenness
  - diamond

===== Usage =====

adamant (stone), brier, diamond.
