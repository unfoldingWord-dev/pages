====== H8121: שֶׁמֶשׁ (shemesh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be **brilliant**;

===== Meaning =====

the **sun**; by implication, the **east**; figuratively, a **ray**, i.e. (architectural) a notched **battlement**

===== Definitions =====

  - brilliant
  - sun
  - east
  - ray
  - battlement

===== Usage =====

  east side(-ward), sun (rising),   west(-ward), window. See also
