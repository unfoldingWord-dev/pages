====== G4139: πλησίον (plēsíon) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a derivative of πέλας (pélas) (near);

===== Meaning =====

(adverbially) close by; as noun, a neighbor, i.e. fellow (as man, countryman, Christian or friend)

===== Usage =====

near, neighbour.
