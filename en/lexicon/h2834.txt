====== H2834: חָשַׂף (châsaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **strip** off, i.e. generally to **make naked** (for exertion or in disgrace), to **drain** away or **bail** up (a liquid)

===== Definitions =====

  - strip
  - make naked
  - drain
  - bail

===== Usage =====

make bare, clean, discover, draw out, take, uncover.
