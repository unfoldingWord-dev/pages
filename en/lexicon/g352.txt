====== G352: ἀνακύπτω (anakýptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] (in the sense of reversal) and [[en:lexicon:g2955|G2955]];

===== Meaning =====

to unbend, i.e. rise; figuratively, be elated

===== Usage =====

lift up, look up.
