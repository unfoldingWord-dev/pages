====== H642: אֵפֻדָּה (ʼêphuddâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h646|H646]];

===== Meaning =====

a **girding** on (of the ephod); hence, generally, a **plating** (of metal)

===== Definitions =====

  - girding
  - plating

===== Usage =====

ephod, ornament.
