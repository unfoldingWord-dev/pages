====== H1516: גַּיְא (gayʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably (by transmutation) from the same root as [[en:lexicon:h1466|H1466]] (abbreviated);

===== Meaning =====

a **gorge** (from its **lofty** sides; hence, narrow, but not a gully or winter-torrent)

===== Definitions =====

  - gorge
  - lofty

===== Usage =====

valley.
