====== H1613: גֹּפֶר (gôpher) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root, probably meaning to house in;

===== Meaning =====

a kind of **tree** or **wood** (as used for building), apparently the **cypress**

===== Definitions =====

  - tree
  - wood
  - cypress

===== Usage =====

gopher.
