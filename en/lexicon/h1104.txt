====== H1104: בָּלַע (bâlaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **make away with** (specifically by **swallowing**); generally, to **destroy**

===== Definitions =====

  - make away with
  - swallowing
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

cover, destroy, devour, eat up, be at end, spend up, swallow down (up).
