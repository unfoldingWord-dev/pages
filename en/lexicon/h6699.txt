====== H6699: צוּרָה (tsûwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h6697|H6697]];

===== Meaning =====

a **rock** (Job 28:10); also a **form** (as if pressed out)

===== Definitions =====

  - rock
  - form

===== Usage =====

form, rock.
