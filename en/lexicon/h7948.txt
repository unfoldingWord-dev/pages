====== H7948: שָׁלָב (shâlâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7947|H7947]];

===== Meaning =====

a **spacer** or **raised interval**, i.e. the **stile in a frame** or **panel**

===== Definitions =====

  - spacer
  - raised interval
  - stile in a frame
  - panel

===== Usage =====

ledge.
