====== H4376: מָכַר (mâkar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sell**, literally (as merchandise, a daughter in marriage, into slavery), or figuratively (to **surrender**)

===== Definitions =====

  - sell
  - surrender

===== Usage =====

× at all, sell (away, -er, self).
