====== H8446: תּוּר (tûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **meander** (causatively, **guide**) **about**, especially for **trade** or **reconnoitring**

===== Definitions =====

  - meander
  - guide
  - about
  - trade
  - reconnoitring

===== Usage =====

chap(-man), sent to descry, be excellent, merchant(-man), search (out), seek, (e-) spy (out).
