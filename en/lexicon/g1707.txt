====== G1707: ἐμπλέκω (emplékō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g4120|G4120]];

===== Meaning =====

to entwine, i.e. (figuratively) involve with

===== Usage =====

entangle (in, self with).
