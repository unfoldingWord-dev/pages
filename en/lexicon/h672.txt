====== H672: אֶפְרָת (ʼEphrâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6509|H6509]]; fruitfulness;

===== Meaning =====

**Ephrath**, another name for **Bethlehem**;

===== Definitions =====

  - Ephrath
  - Bethlehem

===== Usage =====

once (Psalm 132:6) perhaps for Ephraim; also of an Israelitish woman; Ephrath, Ephratah.
