====== H887: בָּאַשׁ (bâʼash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **smell** bad; figuratively, to **be offensive** morally

===== Definitions =====

  - smell
  - be offensive

===== Usage =====

(make to) be abhorred (had in abomination, loathsome, odious), (cause a, make to) stink(-ing savour), × utterly.
