====== H4889: מַשְׁחִית (mashchîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7843|H7843]];

===== Meaning =====

**destructive**, i.e. (as noun) **destruction**, literally (specifically a snare) or figuratively (corruption)

===== Definitions =====

  - destructive
  - destruction

===== Usage =====

corruption, (to) destroy(-ing), destruction, trap, × utterly.
