====== G653: ἀποστοματίζω (apostomatízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and a (presumed) derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4750|G4750]];

===== Meaning =====

to speak off-hand (properly, dictate), i.e. to catechize (in an invidious manner)

===== Usage =====

provoke to speak.
