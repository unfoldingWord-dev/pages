====== H2340: חִוִּי (Chivvîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

perhaps from [[en:lexicon:h2333|H2333]]; a **villager**;

===== Meaning =====

a **Chivvite**, one of the aboriginal tribes of Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - villager
  - Chivvite

===== Usage =====

Hivite.
