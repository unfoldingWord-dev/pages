====== G5069: τετράδιον (tetrádion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative of τέτρας (tétras) (a tetrad; from [[en:lexicon:g5064|G5064]]);

===== Meaning =====

a quaternion or squad (picket) of four Roman soldiers

===== Usage =====

quaternion.
