====== H3126: יוֹנֵק (yôwnêq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

active participle of [[en:lexicon:h3243|H3243]];

===== Meaning =====

a **sucker**; hence, a **twig** (of a tree felled and sprouting)

===== Definitions =====

  - sucker
  - twig

===== Usage =====

tender plant.
