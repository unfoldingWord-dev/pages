====== H8277: שָׂרַד (sârad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare [[en:lexicon:h8279|H8279]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **puncture**, i.e. (figuratively through the idea of slipping out) to **escape** or **survive**

===== Definitions =====

  - puncture
  - escape
  - survive

===== Usage =====

remain.
