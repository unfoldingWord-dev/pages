====== G1746: ἐνδύω (endýō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g1416|G1416]] (in the sense of sinking into a garment);

===== Meaning =====

to invest with clothing (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

array, clothe (with), endue, have (put) on.
