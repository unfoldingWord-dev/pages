====== H3918: לַיִשׁ (layish) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3888|H3888]] in the sense of **crushing**;

===== Meaning =====

a **lion** (from his destructive **blows**)

===== Definitions =====

  - crushing
  - lion
  - blows

===== Usage =====

(old) lion.
