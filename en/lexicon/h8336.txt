====== H8336: שֵׁשׁ (shêsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (for alliteration with [[en:lexicon:h4897|H4897]]) [[en:lexicon:|]]; for [[en:lexicon:h7893|H7893]];

===== Meaning =====

**bleached** stuff, i.e. **white** linen or (by analogy) marble

===== Definitions =====

  - bleached
  - white

===== Usage =====

× blue, fine (twined) linen, marble, silk.
