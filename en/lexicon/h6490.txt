====== H6490: פִּקּוּד (piqqûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6485|H6485]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **appointed**, i.e. a **mandate** (of God; plural only, collectively, for the Law)

===== Definitions =====

  - appointed
  - mandate

===== Usage =====

commandment, precept, statute.
