====== H2015: הָפַךְ (hâphak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **turn** about or over; by implication, to **change**, **overturn**, **return**, **pervert**

===== Definitions =====

  - turn
  - change
  - overturn
  - return
  - pervert

===== Usage =====

× become, change, come, be converted, give, make (a bed), overthrow (-turn), perverse, retire, tumble, turn (again, aside, back, to the contrary, every way).
