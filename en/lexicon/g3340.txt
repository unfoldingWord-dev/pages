====== G3340: μετανοέω (metanoéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g3539|G3539]];

===== Meaning =====

to think differently or afterwards, i.e. reconsider (morally, feel compunction)

===== Usage =====

repent.
