====== H469: אֱלִיצָפָן (ʼĔlîytsâphân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

===== Meaning =====

**Elitsaphan** or **Eltsaphan**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Elitsaphan
  - Eltsaphan

===== Usage =====
