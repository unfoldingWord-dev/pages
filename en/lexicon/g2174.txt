====== G2174: εὐψυχέω (eupsychéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and [[en:lexicon:g5590|G5590]];

===== Meaning =====

to be in good spirits, i.e. feel encouraged

===== Usage =====

be of good comfort.
