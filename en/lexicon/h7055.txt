====== H7055: קְמוּאֵל (Qᵉmûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6965|H6965]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; **raised of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kemuel**, the name of a relative of Abraham, and of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - raised of God
  - Kemuel

===== Usage =====

Kemuel.
