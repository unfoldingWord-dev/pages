====== G4866: συναθλέω (synathléō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g118|G118]];

===== Meaning =====

to wrestle in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to seek jointly

===== Usage =====

labour with, strive together for.
