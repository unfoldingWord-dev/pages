====== G526: ἀπαλλοτριόω (apallotrióō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g245|G245]];

===== Meaning =====

to estrange away, i.e. (passively and figuratively) to be non-participant

===== Usage =====

 alienate, be alien.
