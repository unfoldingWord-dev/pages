====== G1890: ἐπαφρίζω (epaphrízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g875|G875]];

===== Meaning =====

to foam upon, i.e. (figuratively) to exhibit (a vile passion)

===== Usage =====

foam out.
