====== H6561: פָּרַק (pâraq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **break off** or **crunch**; figuratively, to **deliver**

===== Definitions =====

  - break off
  - crunch
  - deliver

===== Usage =====

break (off), deliver, redeem, rend (in pieces), tear in pieces.
