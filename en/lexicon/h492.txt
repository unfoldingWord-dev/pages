====== H492: אַלְמֹנִי (ʼalmônîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h489|H489]] in the sense of **concealment**;

===== Meaning =====

**some** one (i.e. **so and so**, without giving the name of the person or place)

===== Definitions =====

  - concealment
  - some
  - so and so

===== Usage =====

one, and such.
