====== H4290: מַחְתֶּרֶת (machtereth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2864|H2864]];

===== Meaning =====

a **burglary**; figuratively, **unexpected examination**

===== Definitions =====

  - burglary
  - unexpected examination

===== Usage =====

breaking up, secret search.
