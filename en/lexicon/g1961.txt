====== G1961: ἐπιμένω (epiménō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g3306|G3306]];

===== Meaning =====

to stay over, i.e. remain (figuratively, persevere)

===== Usage =====

abide (in), continue (in), tarry.
