====== H2759: חֲרִישִׁי (chărîyshîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2790|H2790]] in the sense of silence;

===== Meaning =====

**quiet**, i.e. **sultry** (as feminine noun, the sirocco or hot east wind)

===== Definitions =====

  - quiet
  - sultry

===== Usage =====

vehement.
