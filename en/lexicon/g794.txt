====== G794: ἄστοργος (ástorgos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of
 στέργω (stérgō) (to cherish affectionately);

===== Meaning =====

hard-hearted towards kindred

===== Usage =====

without natural affection.
