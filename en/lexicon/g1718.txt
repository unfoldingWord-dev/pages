====== G1718: ἐμφανίζω (emphanízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1717|G1717]];

===== Meaning =====

to exhibit (in person) or disclose (by words)

===== Usage =====

appear, declare (plainly), inform, (will) manifest, shew, signify.
