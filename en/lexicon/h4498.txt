====== H4498: מָנוֹס (mânôwç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5127|H5127]];

===== Meaning =====

a **retreat** (literally or figuratively); abstractly, a **fleeing**

===== Definitions =====

  - retreat
  - fleeing

===== Usage =====

× apace, escape, way to flee, flight, refuge.
