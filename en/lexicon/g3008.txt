====== G3008: λειτουργέω (leitourgéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3011|G3011]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a public servant, i.e. (by analogy) to perform religious or charitable functions (worship, obey, relieve)

===== Usage =====

minister.
