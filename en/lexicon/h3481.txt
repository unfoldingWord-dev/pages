===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

patronymically from [[en:lexicon:h3478|H3478]];

===== Meaning =====

**a Jisreelite** or descendant of Jisrael

===== Definitions =====

  - a Jisreelite

===== Usage =====

of Israel, Israelite.
