====== H2864: חָתַר (châthar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **force a passage**, as by burglary; figuratively, **with oars**

===== Definitions =====

  - force a passage
  - with oars

===== Usage =====

dig (through), row.
