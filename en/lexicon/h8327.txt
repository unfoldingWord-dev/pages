====== H8327: שָׁרַשׁ (shârash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **root**, i.e. **strike into the soil**, or (by implication) to **pluck from it**

===== Definitions =====

  - root
  - strike into the soil
  - pluck from it

===== Usage =====

(take, cause to take) root (out).
