====== G3319: μέσος (mésos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]];

===== Meaning =====

middle (as an adjective or (neuter) noun)

===== Usage =====

among, X before them, between, + forth, mid(-day, -night), midst, way.
