====== H85: אַבְרָהָם (ʼAbrâhâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

contracted from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and an unused root (probably meaning to be populous); father of a multitude;

===== Meaning =====

**Abraham**, the later name of Abram

===== Definitions =====

  - Abraham

===== Usage =====

Abraham.
