====== G4046: περιποιέομαι (peripoiéomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g4160|G4160]];

===== Meaning =====

to make around oneself, i.e. acquire (buy)

===== Usage =====

purchase.
