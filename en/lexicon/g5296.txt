====== G5296: ὑποτύπωσις (hypotýpōsis) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5179|G5179]];

===== Meaning =====

typification under (after), i.e. (concretely) a sketch (figuratively) for imitation

===== Usage =====

form, pattern.
