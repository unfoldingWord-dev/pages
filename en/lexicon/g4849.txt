====== G4849: συμπόσιον (sympósion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a derivative of the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g4844|G4844]];

===== Meaning =====

a drinking-party ("symposium"), i.e. (by extension) a room of guests

===== Usage =====

company.
