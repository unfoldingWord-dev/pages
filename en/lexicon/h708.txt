====== H708: אֶרֶג (ʼereg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h707|H707]];

===== Meaning =====

a **weaving**; a **braid**; also a **shuttle**

===== Definitions =====

  - weaving
  - braid
  - shuttle

===== Usage =====

beam, weaver's shuttle.
