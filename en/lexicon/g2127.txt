====== G2127: εὐλογέω (eulogéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and [[en:lexicon:g3056|G3056]];

===== Meaning =====

to speak well of, i.e. (religiously) to bless (thank or invoke a benediction upon, prosper)

===== Usage =====

bless, praise.
