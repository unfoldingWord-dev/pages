====== H588: אֲנָחֲרָת (ʼĂnâchărâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

probably from the same root as [[en:lexicon:h5170|H5170]]; a gorge or narrow pass;

===== Meaning =====

**Anacharath**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Anacharath

===== Usage =====

Anaharath.
