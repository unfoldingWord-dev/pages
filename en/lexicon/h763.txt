====== H763: אֲרַם נַהֲרַיִם (ʼĂram Nahărayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h758|H758]] and the dual of [[en:lexicon:h5104|H5104]];

===== Meaning =====

**Aram** of (the) two rivers (Euphrates and Tigris) or Mesopotamia

===== Definitions =====

  - Aram

===== Usage =====

Aram-naharaim, Mesopotamia.
