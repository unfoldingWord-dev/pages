====== H1979: הֲלִיכָה (hălîykâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h1978|H1978]];

===== Meaning =====

a **walking**; by implication, a **procession** or **march**, a **caravan**

===== Definitions =====

  - walking
  - procession
  - march
  - caravan

===== Usage =====

company, going, walk, way.
