====== G865: ἀφιλάγαθος (aphilágathos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5358|G5358]];

===== Meaning =====

hostile to virtue

===== Usage =====

despiser of those that are good.
