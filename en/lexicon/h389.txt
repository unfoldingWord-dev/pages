====== H389: אַךְ (ʼak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

akin to [[en:lexicon:h403|H403]];

===== Meaning =====

a particle of **affirmation**, **surely**; hence (by limitation) **only**

===== Definitions =====

  - affirmation
  - surely
  - only

===== Usage =====

also, in any wise, at least, but, certainly, even, howbeit, nevertheless, notwithstanding, only, save, surely, of a surety, truly, verily,   wherefore, yet (but).
