====== H4530: מִסָּה (miççâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4549|H4549]] (in the sense of flowing);

===== Meaning =====

**abundance**, i.e. (adverbially) **liberally**

===== Definitions =====

  - abundance
  - liberally

===== Usage =====

tribute.
