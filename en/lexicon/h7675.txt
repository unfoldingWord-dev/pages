====== H7675: שֶׁבֶת (shebeth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

infinitive of [[en:lexicon:h3427|H3427]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **session**; but used also concretely, an **abode** or **locality**

===== Definitions =====

  - session
  - abode
  - locality

===== Usage =====

place, seat. Compare
