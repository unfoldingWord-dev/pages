====== H8323: שָׂרַר (sârar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **have** (transitively, **exercise**; reflexively, **get**) **dominion**

===== Definitions =====

  - have
  - exercise
  - get
  - dominion

===== Usage =====

× altogether, make self a prince, (bear) rule.
