====== H6431: פֶּלֶת (Peleth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **flee**; **swiftness**;

===== Meaning =====

**Peleth**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - flee
  - swiftness
  - Peleth

===== Usage =====

Peleth.
