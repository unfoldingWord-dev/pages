====== H4955: מִשְׂרָפָה (misrâphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8313|H8313]];

===== Meaning =====

**combustion**, i.e. **cremation** (of a corpse), or **calcination** (of lime)

===== Definitions =====

  - combustion
  - cremation
  - calcination

===== Usage =====

burning.
