====== G1965: ἐπίορκος (epíorkos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g3727|G3727]];

===== Meaning =====

on oath, i.e. (falsely) a forswearer

===== Usage =====
