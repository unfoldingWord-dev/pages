====== H3148: יוֹתֵר (yôwthêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

active participle of [[en:lexicon:h3498|H3498]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **redundant**; hence, **over and above**, as adjective, noun, adverb or conjunction

===== Definitions =====

  - redundant
  - over and above

===== Usage =====

better, more(-over), over, profit.
