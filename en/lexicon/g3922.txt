====== G3922: παρεισέρχομαι (pareisérchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g1525|G1525]];

===== Meaning =====

to come in alongside, i.e. supervene additionally or steathily

===== Usage =====

come in privily, enter.
