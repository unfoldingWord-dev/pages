====== G3912: παραφρονέω (paraphronéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g5426|G5426]];

===== Meaning =====

to misthink, i.e. be insane (silly)

===== Usage =====

as a fool.
