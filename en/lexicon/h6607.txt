====== H6607: פֶּתַח (pethach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6605|H6605]];

===== Meaning =====

an **opening** (literally), i.e. **door** (gate) or **entrance way**

===== Definitions =====

  - opening
  - door
  - entrance way

===== Usage =====

door, entering (in), entrance (-ry), gate, opening, place.
