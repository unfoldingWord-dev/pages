====== H6173: עַרְוָה (ʻarvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h6172|H6172]];

===== Meaning =====

**nakedness**, i.e. (figuratively) **impoverishment**

===== Definitions =====

  - nakedness
  - impoverishment

===== Usage =====

dishonor.
