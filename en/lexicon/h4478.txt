====== H4478: מָן (mân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4100|H4100]];

===== Meaning =====

literally a **whatness** (so to speak), i.e. **manna** (so called from the question about it)

===== Definitions =====

  - whatness
  - manna

===== Usage =====

manna.
