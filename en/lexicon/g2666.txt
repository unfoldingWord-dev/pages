====== G2666: καταπίνω (katapínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g4095|G4095]];

===== Meaning =====

to drink down, i.e. gulp entire (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

devour, drown, swallow (up).
