====== H6093: עִצָּבוֹן (ʻitstsâbôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6087|H6087]];

===== Meaning =====

**worrisomeness**, i.e. **labor** or **pain**

===== Definitions =====

  - worrisomeness
  - labor
  - pain

===== Usage =====

sorrow, toil.
