====== H3677: כֶּסֶא (keçeʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; apparently from [[en:lexicon:h3680|H3680]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **fulness** or the **full moon**, i.e. its **festival**

===== Definitions =====

  - fulness
  - full moon
  - festival

===== Usage =====

(time) appointed.
