====== H216: אוֹר (ʼôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h215|H215]];

===== Meaning =====

**illumination** or (concrete) **luminary** (in every sense, including lightning, happiness, etc.)

===== Definitions =====

  - illumination
  - luminary

===== Usage =====

bright, clear,   day, light (-ning), morning, sun.
