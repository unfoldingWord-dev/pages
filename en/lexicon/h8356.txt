====== H8356: שָׁתָה (shâthâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7896|H7896]];

===== Meaning =====

a **basis**, i.e. (figuratively) political or moral **support**

===== Definitions =====

  - basis
  - support

===== Usage =====

foundation, purpose.
