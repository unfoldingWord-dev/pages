====== G3123: μᾶλλον (mâllon) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of the comparative of the same as [[en:lexicon:g3122|G3122]];

===== Meaning =====

(adverbially) more (in a greater degree)) or rather

===== Usage =====

+ better, X far, (the) more (and more), (so) much (the more), rather.
