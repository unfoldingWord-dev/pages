====== H1706: דְּבַשׁ (dᵉbash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **be gummy**;

===== Meaning =====

**honey** (from its **stickiness**); by analogy, **syrup**

===== Definitions =====

  - be gummy
  - honey
  - stickiness
  - syrup

===== Usage =====

honey(-comb).
