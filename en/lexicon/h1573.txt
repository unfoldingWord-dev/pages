====== H1573: גֹּמֶא (gômeʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1572|H1572]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, an **absorbent**, i.e. the **bulrush** (from its **porosity**); specifically the **papyrus**

===== Definitions =====

  - absorbent
  - bulrush
  - porosity
  - papyrus

===== Usage =====

(bul-) rush.
