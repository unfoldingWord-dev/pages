====== H8564: תַּמְרוּר (tamrûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same root as [[en:lexicon:h8558|H8558]];

===== Meaning =====

an **erection**, i.e. **pillar** (probably for a guide-board)

===== Definitions =====

  - erection
  - pillar

===== Usage =====

high heap.
