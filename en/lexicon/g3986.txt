====== G3986: πειρασμός (peirasmós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3985|G3985]];

===== Meaning =====

a putting to proof (by experiment (of good), experience (of evil), solicitation, discipline or provocation); by implication, adversity

===== Usage =====

temptation, X try.
