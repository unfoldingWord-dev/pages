====== G3333: μετακαλέω (metakaléō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g2564|G2564]];

===== Meaning =====

to call elsewhere, i.e. summon

===== Usage =====

call (for, hither).
