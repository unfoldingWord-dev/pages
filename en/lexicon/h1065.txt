====== H1065: בְּכִי (Bᵉkîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1058|H1058]];

===== Meaning =====

a **weeping**; by analogy, a **dripping**

===== Definitions =====

  - weeping
  - dripping

===== Usage =====

overflowing, × sore, (continual) weeping, wept.
