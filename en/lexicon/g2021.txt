====== G2021: ἐπιχειρέω (epicheiréō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g5495|G5495]];

===== Meaning =====

to put the hand upon, i.e. undertake

===== Usage =====

go about, take in hand (upon).
