====== H1852: דֹּק (dôq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1854|H1854]];

===== Meaning =====

something **crumbling**, i.e. **fine** (as a **thin** cloth)

===== Definitions =====

  - crumbling
  - fine
  - thin

===== Usage =====

curtain.
