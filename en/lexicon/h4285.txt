===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2821|H2821]];

===== Meaning =====

**darkness**; concretely, a **dark place**

===== Definitions =====

  - darkness
  - dark place

===== Usage =====
