====== G3328: μεταβάλλω (metabállō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g906|G906]];

===== Meaning =====

to throw over, i.e. (middle voice figuratively) to turn about in opinion

===== Usage =====

change mind.
