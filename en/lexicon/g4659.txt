====== G4659: σκυθρωπός (skythrōpós) ======

===== Source =====

from σκυθρός (skythrós) (sullen) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3700|G3700]];

===== Meaning =====

angry-visaged, i.e. gloomy or affecting a mournful appearance

===== Usage =====

of a sad countenance.
