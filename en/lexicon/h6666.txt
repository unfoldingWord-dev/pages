====== H6666: צְדָקָה (tsᵉdâqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6663|H6663]];

===== Meaning =====

**rightness** (abstractly), subjectively (**rectitude**), objectively (**justice**), morally (**virtue**) or figuratively (**prosperity**)

===== Definitions =====

  - rightness
  - rectitude
  - justice
  - virtue
  - prosperity

===== Usage =====

justice, moderately, right(-eous) (act, -ly, -ness).
