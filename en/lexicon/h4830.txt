====== H4830: מִרְעִית (mirʻîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7462|H7462]] in the sense of **feeding**;

===== Meaning =====

**pasturage**; concretely, a **flock**

===== Definitions =====

  - feeding
  - pasturage
  - flock

===== Usage =====

flock, pasture.
