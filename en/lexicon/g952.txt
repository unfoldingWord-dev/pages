====== G952: βέβηλος (bébēlos) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]] and βηλός (bēlós) (a threshold);

===== Meaning =====

accessible (as by crossing the door-way), i.e. (by implication, of Jewish notions) heathenish, wicked

===== Usage =====

profane (person).
