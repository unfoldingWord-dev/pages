====== G737: ἄρτι (árti) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g142|G142]] (compare [[en:lexicon:g740|G740]]) through the idea of
 suspension;

===== Meaning =====

just now

===== Usage =====

this day (hour), hence(-forth), here(-after), hither(-to), (even) now, (this) present.
