====== H7145: קׇרְחִי (Qorchîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

patronymic from [[en:lexicon:h7141|H7141]];

===== Meaning =====

**a Korchite** (collectively) or descendants of Korach

===== Definitions =====

  - a Korchite

===== Usage =====

Korahite, Korathite, sons of Kore, Korhite.
