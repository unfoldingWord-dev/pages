====== H193: אוּל (ʼûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to twist, i.e. (by implication) be strong;

===== Meaning =====

**the body** (as being rolled together); also **powerful**

===== Definitions =====

  - the body
  - powerful

===== Usage =====

mighty, strength.
