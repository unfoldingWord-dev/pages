====== H513: אֶלְתּוֹלַד (ʼEltôwlad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]] and a masculine form of [[en:lexicon:h8435|H8435]] (compare [[en:lexicon:h8434|H8434]]); God (is) generator;

===== Meaning =====

**Eltolad**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Eltolad

===== Usage =====

Eltolad.
