====== G2403: Ἰεζαβήλ (Iezabḗl) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin ([[en:lexicon:h0348|H0348]]);

===== Meaning =====

Jezabel (i.e. Jezebel), a Tyrian woman (used as a synonym of a termagant or false teacher)

===== Usage =====

Jezabel.
