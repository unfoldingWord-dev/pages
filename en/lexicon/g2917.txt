====== G2917: κρίμα (kríma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2919|G2919]];

===== Meaning =====

a decision (the function or the effect, for or against ("crime"))

===== Usage =====

avenge, condemned, condemnation, damnation, + go to law, judgment.
