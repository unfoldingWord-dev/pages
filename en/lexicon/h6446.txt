====== H6446: פַּס (paç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6461|H6461]]; properly, the palm (of the hand) or sole (of the foot) (compare [[en:lexicon:h6447|H6447]]); by implication (plural)

===== Meaning =====

a **long and sleeved tunic** (perhaps simply a wide one; from the original sense of the root, i.e. of many breadths)

===== Definitions =====

  - long and sleeved tunic

===== Usage =====

(divers) colours.
