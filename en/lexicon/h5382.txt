====== H5382: נָשָׁה (nâshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **forget**; figuratively, to **neglect**; causatively, to **remit**, **remove**

===== Definitions =====

  - forget
  - neglect
  - remit
  - remove

===== Usage =====

forget, deprive, exact.
