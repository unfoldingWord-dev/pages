====== H3409: יָרֵךְ (yârêk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be soft;

===== Meaning =====

the **thigh** (from its fleshy softness); by euphemistically the **generative parts**; figuratively, a **shank**, **flank**, **side**

===== Definitions =====

  - thigh
  - generative parts
  - shank
  - flank
  - side

===== Usage =====

× body, loins, shaft, side, thigh.
