====== G5330: Φαρισαῖος (Pharisaîos) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h06567|H06567]]);

===== Meaning =====

a separatist, i.e. exclusively religious; a Pharisean, i.e. Jewish sectary

===== Usage =====

Pharisee.
