====== G4679: σοφίζω (sophízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4680|G4680]];

===== Meaning =====

to render wise; in a sinister acceptation, to form "sophisms", i.e. continue plausible error

===== Usage =====

cunningly devised, make wise.
