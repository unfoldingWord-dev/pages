====== H8311: שָׂרַע (sâraʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **prolong**, i.e. (reflex) **be deformed** by excess of members

===== Definitions =====

  - prolong
  - be deformed

===== Usage =====

stretch out self, (have any) superfluous thing.
