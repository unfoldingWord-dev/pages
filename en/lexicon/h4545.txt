====== H4545: מַסֶּכֶת (maççeketh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5259|H5259]] in the sense of spreading out;

===== Meaning =====

something **expanded**, i.e. the **warp** in a loom (as stretched out to receive the woof)

===== Definitions =====

  - expanded
  - warp

===== Usage =====

web.
