====== G4159: πόθεν (póthen) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4213|G4213]] with enclitic adverb of origin;

===== Meaning =====

from which (as interrogative) or what (as relative) place, state, source or cause

===== Usage =====

whence.
