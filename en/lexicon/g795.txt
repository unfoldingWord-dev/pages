====== G795: ἀστοχέω (astochéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and στοίχος (stoíchos) (an aim);

===== Meaning =====

to miss the mark, i.e. (figuratively) deviate from truth

===== Usage =====

err, swerve.
