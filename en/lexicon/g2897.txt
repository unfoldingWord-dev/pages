====== G2897: κραιπάλη (kraipálē) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:lexicon:g726|G726]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a headache (as a seizure of pain) from drunkenness, i.e. (by implication) a debauch (by analogy, a glut)

===== Usage =====

surfeiting.
