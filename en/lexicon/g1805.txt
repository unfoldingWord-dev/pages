====== G1805: ἐξαγοράζω (exagorázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g59|G59]];

===== Meaning =====

to buy up, i.e. ransom; figuratively, to rescue from loss (improve opportunity)

===== Usage =====

redeem.
