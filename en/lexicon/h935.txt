====== H935: בּוֹא (bôwʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **go** or **come** (in a wide variety of applications)

===== Definitions =====

  - go
  - come

===== Usage =====

abide, apply, attain, × be, befall,   besiege, bring (forth, in, into, to pass), call, carry, × certainly, (cause, let, thing for) to come (against, in, out, upon, to pass), depart, × doubtless again,   eat,   employ, (cause to) enter (in, into, -tering, -trance, -try), be fallen, fetch,   follow, get, give, go (down, in, to war), grant,   have, × indeed, (in-) vade, lead, lift (up), mention, pull in, put, resort, run (down), send, set, × (well) stricken (in age), × surely, take (in), way.
