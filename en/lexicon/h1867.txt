====== H1867: דָּֽרְיָוֵשׁ (Dârᵉyâvêsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

of Persian origin;

===== Meaning =====

**Darejavesh**, a title (rather than name) of several Persian kings

===== Definitions =====

  - Darejavesh

===== Usage =====

Darius.
