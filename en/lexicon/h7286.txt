====== H7286: רָדַד (râdad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tread** in pieces, i.e. (figuratively) to **conquer**, or (specifically) to **overlay**

===== Definitions =====

  - tread
  - conquer
  - overlay

===== Usage =====

spend, spread, subdue.
