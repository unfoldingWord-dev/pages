====== H7670: שִׁבְרוֹן (shibrôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7665|H7665]];

===== Meaning =====

**rupture**, i.e. a **pang**; figuratively, **ruin**

===== Definitions =====

  - rupture
  - pang
  - ruin

===== Usage =====

breaking, destruction.
