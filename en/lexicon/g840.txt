====== G840: αὐστηρός (austērós) ======

===== Source =====

from a (presumed) derivative of the same as [[en:lexicon:g109|G109]] (meaning blown);

===== Meaning =====

rough (properly as a gale), i.e. (figuratively) severe

===== Usage =====

austere.
