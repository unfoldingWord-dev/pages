====== G423: ἀνεπίληπτος (anepílēptos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1949|G1949]];

===== Meaning =====

not arrested, i.e. (by implication) inculpable

===== Usage =====

blameless, unrebukeable.
