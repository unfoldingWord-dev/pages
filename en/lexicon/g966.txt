====== G966: Βηθσαϊδά (Bēthsaïdá) ======

===== Source =====

of Chaldee origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h01004|H01004]] and [[en:lexicon:h06719|H06719]]);

===== Meaning =====

fishing-house; Bethsaida, a place in Palestine

===== Usage =====

Bethsaida.
