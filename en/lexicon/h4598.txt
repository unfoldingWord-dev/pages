====== H4598: מְעִיל (mᵉʻîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4603|H4603]] in the sense of covering;

===== Meaning =====

a **robe** (i.e. upper and outer garment)

===== Definitions =====

  - robe

===== Usage =====

cloke, coat, mantle, robe.
