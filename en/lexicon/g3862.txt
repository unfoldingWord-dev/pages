====== G3862: παράδοσις (parádosis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3860|G3860]];

===== Meaning =====

transmission, i.e. (concretely) a precept; specially, the Jewish traditionary law

===== Usage =====

ordinance, tradition.
