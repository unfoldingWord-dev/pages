====== H4220: מֵחַ (mêach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4229|H4229]] in the sense of **greasing**;

===== Meaning =====

**fat**; figuratively, **rich**

===== Definitions =====

  - greasing
  - fat
  - rich

===== Usage =====

fatling (one).
