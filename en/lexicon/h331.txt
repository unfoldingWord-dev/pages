====== H331: אָטַם (ʼâṭam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **close** (the lips or ears); by analology to **contract** (a window by bevelled jambs)

===== Definitions =====

  - close
  - contract

===== Usage =====

narrow, shut, stop.
