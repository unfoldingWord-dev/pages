====== G4904: συνεργός (synergós) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g2041|G2041]];

===== Meaning =====

a co-laborer, i.e. coadjutor

===== Usage =====

companion in labour, (fellow-)helper(-labourer, -worker), labourer together with, workfellow.
