====== H2131: זִיקָה (zîyqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Isaiah 50:11) (feminine); and [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h2187|H2187]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, what **leaps** forth, i.e. **flash** of fire, or a burning **arrow**; also (from the original sense of the root) a **bond**

===== Definitions =====

  - leaps
  - flash
  - arrow
  - bond

===== Usage =====

chain, fetter, firebrand, spark.
