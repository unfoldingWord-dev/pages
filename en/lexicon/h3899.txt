====== H3899: לֶחֶם (lechem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3898|H3898]]; See also [[en:lexicon:h1036|H1036]]

===== Meaning =====

**food** (for man or beast), especially **bread**, or **grain** (for making it)

===== Definitions =====

  - food
  - bread
  - grain

===== Usage =====

(shew-) bread, × eat, food, fruit, loaf, meat, victuals.
