====== H7779: שׁוּף (shûwph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **gape**, i.e. **snap at**; figuratively, to **overwhelm**

===== Definitions =====

  - gape
  - snap at
  - overwhelm

===== Usage =====

break, bruise, cover.
