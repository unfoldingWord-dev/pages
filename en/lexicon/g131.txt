====== G131: αἱμοῤῥέω (haimorrhéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g129|G129]] and [[en:lexicon:g4482|G4482]];

===== Meaning =====

to flow blood, i.e. have a hæmorrhage

===== Usage =====

diseased with an issue of blood.
