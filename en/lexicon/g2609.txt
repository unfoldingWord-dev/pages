====== G2609: κατάγω (katágō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to lead down; specially, to moor a vessel

===== Usage =====

bring (down, forth), (bring to) land, touch.
