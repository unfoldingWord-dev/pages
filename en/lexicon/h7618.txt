====== H7618: שְׁבוּ (shᵉbûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (probably identical with that of [[en:lexicon:h7617|H7617]] through the idea of **subdivision** into flashes or streamers (compare [[en:lexicon:h7632|H7632]]) meaning to **flame**;

===== Meaning =====

a **gem** (from its sparkle), probably the **agate**

===== Definitions =====

  - subdivision
  - flame
  - gem
  - agate

===== Usage =====

agate.
