====== G3947: παροξύνω (paroxýnō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3691|G3691]];

===== Meaning =====

to sharpen alongside, i.e. (figuratively) to exasperate

===== Usage =====

easily provoke, stir.
