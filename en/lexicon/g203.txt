====== G203: ἀκροβυστία (akrobystía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g206|G206]] and probably a modified form of πόσθη (pósthē) (the penis or male
 sexual organ);

===== Meaning =====

the prepuce; by implication, an uncircumcised (i.e. gentile, figuratively, unregenerate) state or person

===== Usage =====

not circumcised, uncircumcised (with
