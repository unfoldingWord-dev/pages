====== H8168: שֹׁעַל (shôʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **hollow** out;

===== Meaning =====

the **palm**; by extension, a **handful**

===== Definitions =====

  - hollow
  - palm
  - handful

===== Usage =====

handful, hollow of the hand.
