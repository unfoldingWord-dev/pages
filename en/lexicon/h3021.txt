====== H3021: יָגַע (yâgaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **gasp**; hence, to **be exhausted**, to **tire**, to **toil**

===== Definitions =====

  - gasp
  - be exhausted
  - tire
  - toil

===== Usage =====

faint, (make to) labour, (be) weary.
