====== G1833: ἐξετάζω (exetázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and ἐτάζω (etázō) (to examine);

===== Meaning =====

to test thoroughly (by questions), i.e. ascertain or interrogate

===== Usage =====

ask, enquire, search.
