====== G5380: φιλόνεικος (philóneikos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5384|G5384]] and νεῖκος (neîkos) (a quarrel; probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g3534|G3534]]);

===== Meaning =====

fond of strife, i.e. disputatious

===== Usage =====

contentious.
