====== G5475: χαλκός (chalkós) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from [[en:lexicon:g5465|G5465]] through the idea of hollowing out as a vessel (this
 metal being chiefly used for that purpose);

===== Meaning =====

copper (the substance, or some implement or coin made of it)

===== Usage =====

brass, money.
