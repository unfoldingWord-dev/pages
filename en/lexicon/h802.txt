====== H802: אִשָּׁה (ʼishshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h376|H376]] or [[en:lexicon:h582|H582]]; irregular plural, [[en:lexicon:|]];(used in the same wide sense as [[en:lexicon:h582|H582]])

===== Meaning =====

a **woman**

===== Definitions =====

  - woman

===== Usage =====

(adulter) ess, each, every, female, × many,   none, one,   together, wife, woman. Often unexpressed in English.
