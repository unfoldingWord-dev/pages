====== H5791: עָוַת (ʻâvath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **wrest**

===== Definitions =====

  - wrest

===== Usage =====

bow self, (make) crooked., falsifying, overthrow, deal perversely, pervert, subvert, turn upside down.
