====== H1253: בֹּר (bôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h1252|H1252]];

===== Meaning =====

vegetable **lye** (from its **cleansing**); used as a **soap** for washing, or a **flux** for metals

===== Definitions =====

  - lye
  - cleansing
  - soap
  - flux

===== Usage =====

× never so, purely.
