====== H2331: חָוָה (châvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare [[en:lexicon:h2324|H2324]], [[en:lexicon:h2421|H2421]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **live**; by implication (intensively) to **declare** or **show**

===== Definitions =====

  - live
  - declare
  - show

===== Usage =====

show.
