====== H5858: עֵיבָל (ʻÊybâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

perhaps from an unused root probably meaning to **be bald**; **bare**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ebal**, a mountain of Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - be bald
  - bare
  - Ebal

===== Usage =====

Ebal.
