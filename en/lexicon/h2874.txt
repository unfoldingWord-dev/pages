====== H2874: טֶבַח (ṭebach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2873|H2873]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **slaughtered**; hence, a **beast** (or **meat**, as butchered); abstractly **butchery** (or concretely, a place of slaughter)

===== Definitions =====

  - slaughtered
  - beast
  - meat
  - butchery

===== Usage =====

× beast, slaughter, × slay, × sore.
