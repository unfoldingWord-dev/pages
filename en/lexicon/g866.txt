====== G866: ἀφιλάργυρος (aphilárgyros) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5366|G5366]];

===== Meaning =====

unavaricious

===== Usage =====

without covetousness, not greedy of filthy lucre.
