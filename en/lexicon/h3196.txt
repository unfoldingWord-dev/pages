====== H3196: יַיִן (yayin) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to effervesce;

===== Meaning =====

**wine** (as fermented); by implication, **intoxication**

===== Definitions =====

  - wine
  - intoxication

===== Usage =====

banqueting, wine, wine(-bibber).
