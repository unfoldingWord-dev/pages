====== H4279: מָחָר (mâchâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:h309|H309]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **deferred**, i.e. the **morrow**; usually (adverbially) **tomorrow**; indefinitely, **hereafter**

===== Definitions =====

  - deferred
  - morrow
  - tomorrow
  - hereafter

===== Usage =====

time to come, tomorrow.
