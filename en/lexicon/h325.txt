====== H325: אֲחַשְׁוֵרוֹשׁ (ʼĂchashvêrôwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]] (Esther 10:1); of Persian origin;

===== Meaning =====

**Achashverosh** (i.e. Ahasuerus or Artaxerxes, but in this case Xerxes), the title (rather than name) of a Persian king

===== Definitions =====

  - Achashverosh

===== Usage =====

Ahasuerus.
