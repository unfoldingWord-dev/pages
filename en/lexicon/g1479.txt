====== G1479: ἐθελοθρησκεία (ethelothrēskeía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2309|G2309]] and [[en:lexicon:g2356|G2356]];

===== Meaning =====

voluntary (arbitrary and unwarranted) piety, i.e. sanctimony

===== Usage =====

will worship.
