====== G968: βῆμα (bēma) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

a step, i.e. foot-breath; by implication, a rostrum, i.e. a tribunal

===== Usage =====

judgment-seat, set (foot) on, throne.
