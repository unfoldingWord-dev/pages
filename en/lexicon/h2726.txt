====== H2726: חַרְבוֹנָא (Charbôwnâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; of Persian origin;

===== Meaning =====

**Charbona** or **Charbonah**, a eunuch of Xerxes

===== Definitions =====

  - Charbona
  - Charbonah

===== Usage =====

Harbona, Harbonah.
