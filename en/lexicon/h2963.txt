====== H2963: טָרַף (ṭâraph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pluck off** or **pull to pieces**; causatively to **supply** with food (as in morsels)

===== Definitions =====

  - pluck off
  - pull to pieces
  - supply

===== Usage =====

catch, × without doubt, feed, ravin, rend in pieces, × surely, tear (in pieces).
