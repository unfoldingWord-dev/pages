====== H4591: מָעַט (mâʻaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **pare** off, i.e. **lessen**; intransitively, to **be** (or causatively, to **make**) **small** or **few** (or figuratively, **ineffective**)

===== Definitions =====

  - pare
  - lessen
  - be
  - make
  - small
  - few
  - ineffective

===== Usage =====

suffer to decrease, diminish, (be, × borrow a, give, make) few (in number, -ness), gather least (little), be (seem) little, (× give the) less, be minished, bring to nothing.
