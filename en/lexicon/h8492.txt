====== H8492: תִּירוֹשׁ (tîyrôwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h3423|H3423]] in the sense of expulsion;

===== Meaning =====

**must** or **fresh grape-juice** (as just squeezed out); by implication (rarely) **fermented wine**

===== Definitions =====

  - must
  - fresh grape-juice
  - fermented wine

===== Usage =====

(new, sweet) wine.
