====== H5958: עֶלֶם (ʻelem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5956|H5956]]; (compare [[en:lexicon:h5959|H5959]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **kept out of sight**, i.e. a **lad**

===== Definitions =====

  - kept out of sight
  - lad

===== Usage =====

young man, stripling.
