====== H1717: דַּד (dad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1730|H1730]];

===== Meaning =====

the **breast** (as the seat of love, or from its shape)

===== Definitions =====

  - breast

===== Usage =====

breast, teat.
