====== G3075: λυμαίνομαι (lymaínomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from a probably derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3089|G3089]] (meaning filth);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to soil, i.e. (figuratively) insult (maltreat)

===== Usage =====

make havock of.
