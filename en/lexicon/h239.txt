====== H239: אָזַן (ʼâzan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather identical with [[en:lexicon:h238|H238]] through the idea of scales as if two ears);

===== Meaning =====

to **weigh**, i.e. (figuratively) **ponder**

===== Definitions =====

  - weigh
  - ponder

===== Usage =====

give good heed.
