====== H3975: מְאוּרָה (mᵉʼûwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h215|H215]];

===== Meaning =====

something **lighted**, i.e. an **aperture**; by implication, a **crevice** or **hole** (of a serpent)

===== Definitions =====

  - lighted
  - aperture
  - crevice
  - hole

===== Usage =====

den.
