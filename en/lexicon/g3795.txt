====== G3795: ὀψάριον (opsárion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3702|G3702]];

===== Meaning =====

a relish to other food (as if cooked sauce), i.e. (specially), fish (presumably salted and dried as a condiment)

===== Usage =====

fish.
