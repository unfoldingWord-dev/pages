====== H8419: תַּהְפֻּכָה (tahpukâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2015|H2015]];

===== Meaning =====

a **perversity** or **fraud**

===== Definitions =====

  - perversity
  - fraud

===== Usage =====

(very) froward(-ness, thing), perverse thing.
