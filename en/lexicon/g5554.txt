====== G5554: χρυσοδακτύλιος (chrysodaktýlios) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5557|G5557]] and [[en:lexicon:g1146|G1146]];

===== Meaning =====

gold-ringed, i.e. wearing a golden finger-ring or similar jewelry

===== Usage =====

with a gold ring.
