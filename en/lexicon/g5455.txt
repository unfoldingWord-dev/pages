====== G5455: φωνέω (phōnéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5456|G5456]];

===== Meaning =====

to emit a sound (animal, human or instrumental); by implication, to address in words or by name, also in imitation

===== Usage =====

call (for), crow, cry.
