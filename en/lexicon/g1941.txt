====== G1941: ἐπικαλέομαι (epikaléomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g2564|G2564]];

===== Meaning =====

to entitle; by implication, to invoke (for aid, worship, testimony, decision, etc.)

===== Usage =====

appeal (unto), call (on, upon), surname.
