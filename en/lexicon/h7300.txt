====== H7300: רוּד (rûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tramp about**, i.e. **ramble** (free or disconsolate)

===== Definitions =====

  - tramp about
  - ramble

===== Usage =====

have the dominion, be lord, mourn, rule.
