====== H4759: מַרְאָה (marʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4758|H4758]];

===== Meaning =====

a **vision**; also (causatively) a **mirror**

===== Definitions =====

  - vision
  - mirror

===== Usage =====

looking glass, vision.
