====== H7189: קֹשֶׁט (qôsheṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to balance;

===== Meaning =====

**equity** (as evenly weighed), i.e. **reality**

===== Definitions =====

  - equity
  - reality

===== Usage =====

truth.
