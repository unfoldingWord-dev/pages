====== G5251: ὑπερυψόω (hyperypsóō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]] and [[en:lexicon:g5312|G5312]];

===== Meaning =====

to elevate above others, i.e. raise to the highest position

===== Usage =====

highly exalt.
