====== H4535: מַסָּח (maççâch) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5255|H5255]] in the sense of **staving off**;

===== Meaning =====

a **cordon**, (adverbially) or (as a) **military barrier**

===== Definitions =====

  - staving off
  - cordon
  - military barrier

===== Usage =====

broken down.
