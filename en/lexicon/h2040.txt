====== H2040: הָרַס (hâraç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pull** down or in pieces, **break**, **destroy**

===== Definitions =====

  - pull
  - break
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

beat down, break (down, through), destroy, overthrow, pluck down, pull down, ruin, throw down, × utterly.
