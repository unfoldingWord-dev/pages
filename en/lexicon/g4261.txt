====== G4261: προβάλλω (probállō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g906|G906]];

===== Meaning =====

to throw forward, i.e. push to the front, germinate

===== Usage =====

put forward, shoot forth.
