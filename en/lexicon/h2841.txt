====== H2841: חַשְׁרָה (chashrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h2840|H2840]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **combination** or **gathering**, i.e. of watery clouds

===== Definitions =====

  - combination
  - gathering

===== Usage =====

dark.
