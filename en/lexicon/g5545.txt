====== G5545: χρῖσμα (chrîsma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5548|G5548]];

===== Meaning =====

an unguent or smearing, i.e. (figuratively) the special endowment ("chrism") of the Holy Spirit

===== Usage =====

anointing, unction.
