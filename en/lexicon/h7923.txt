====== H7923: שִׁכֻּלִים (shikkulîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

plural from [[en:lexicon:h7921|H7921]];

===== Meaning =====

**childlessness** (by continued bereavements)

===== Definitions =====

  - childlessness

===== Usage =====

to have after loss of others.
