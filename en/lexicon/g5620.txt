====== G5620: ὥστε (hṓste) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5613|G5613]] and [[en:lexicon:g5037|G5037]];

===== Meaning =====

so too, i.e. thus therefore (in various relations of consecution, as follow)

===== Usage =====

(insomuch) as, so that (then), (insomuch) that, therefore, to, wherefore.
