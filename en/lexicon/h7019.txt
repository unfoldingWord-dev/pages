====== H7019: קַיִץ (qayits) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6972|H6972]];

===== Meaning =====

**harvest** (as the crop), whether the product (grain or fruit) or the (dry) season

===== Definitions =====

  - harvest

===== Usage =====

summer (fruit, house).
