====== H5786: עָוַר (ʻâvar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather denominatively from [[en:lexicon:h5785|H5785]] through the idea of a film over the eyes);

===== Meaning =====

to **blind**

===== Definitions =====

  - blind

===== Usage =====

blind, put out. See also
