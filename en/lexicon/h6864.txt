====== H6864: צֹר (tsôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6696|H6696]];

===== Meaning =====

a **stone** (as if pressed hard or to a point); (by implication, of use) a **knife**

===== Definitions =====

  - stone
  - knife

===== Usage =====

flint, sharp stone.
