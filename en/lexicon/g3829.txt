====== G3829: πανδοχεῖον (pandocheîon) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g3956|G3956]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1209|G1209]];

===== Meaning =====

all-receptive, i.e. a public lodging-place (caravanserai or khan)

===== Usage =====

inn.
