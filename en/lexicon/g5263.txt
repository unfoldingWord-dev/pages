====== G5263: ὑποδείκνυμι (hypodeíknymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g1166|G1166]];

===== Meaning =====

to exhibit under the eyes, i.e. (figuratively) to exemplify (instruct, admonish)

===== Usage =====

show, (fore-)warn.
