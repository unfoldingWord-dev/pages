====== G2686: κατασοφίζομαι (katasophízomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g4679|G4679]];

===== Meaning =====

to be crafty against, i.e. circumvent

===== Usage =====

deal subtilly with.
