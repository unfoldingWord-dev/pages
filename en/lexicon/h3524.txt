====== H3524: כַּבִּיר (kabbîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3527|H3527]];

===== Meaning =====

**vast**, whether in extent (figuratively, of power, **mighty**; of time, **aged**), or in number, **many**

===== Definitions =====

  - vast
  - mighty
  - aged
  - many

===== Usage =====

  feeble, mighty, most, much, strong, valiant.
