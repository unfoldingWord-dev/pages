====== H256: אַחְאָב (ʼAchʼâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

once (by contraction) [[en:lexicon:|]] (Jeremiah 29:22); from [[en:lexicon:h251|H251]] and [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]]; brother (i.e. friend) of (his) father;

===== Meaning =====

**Achab**, the name of a king of Israel and of a prophet at Babylon

===== Definitions =====

  - Achab

===== Usage =====

Ahab.
