====== H7939: שָׂכָר (sâkâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7936|H7936]];

===== Meaning =====

**payment of contract**; concretely, **salary**, **fare**, **maintenance**; by implication, **compensation**, **benefit**

===== Definitions =====

  - payment of contract
  - salary
  - fare
  - maintenance
  - compensation
  - benefit

===== Usage =====

hire, price, reward(-ed), wages, worth.
