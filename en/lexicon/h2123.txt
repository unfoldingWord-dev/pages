====== H2123: זִיז (zîyz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to **be conspicuous**;

===== Meaning =====

**fulness** of the breast; also a moving **creature**

===== Definitions =====

  - be conspicuous
  - fulness
  - creature

===== Usage =====

abundance, wild beast.
