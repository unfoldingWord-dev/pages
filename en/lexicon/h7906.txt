====== H7906: שֵׂכוּ (Sêkûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to surmount; an observatory (with the article);

===== Meaning =====

**Seku**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Seku

===== Usage =====

Sechu.
