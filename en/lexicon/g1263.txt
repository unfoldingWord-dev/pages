====== G1263: διαμαρτύρομαι (diamartýromai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g3140|G3140]];

===== Meaning =====

to attest or protest earnestly, or (by implication) hortatively

===== Usage =====

charge, testify (unto), witness.
