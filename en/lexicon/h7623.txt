====== H7623: שָׁבַח (shâbach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **address in a loud tone**, i.e. (specifically) **loud**; figuratively, to **pacify** (as if by words)

===== Definitions =====

  - address in a loud tone
  - loud
  - pacify

===== Usage =====

commend, glory, keep in, praise, still, triumph.
