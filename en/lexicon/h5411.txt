====== H5411: נָתִין (Nâthîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]] (Ezra 8:17), (the proper form as passive participle), from [[en:lexicon:h5414|H5414]]; **one given**, i.e. (in the plural only)

===== Meaning =====

the **Nethinim**, or **Temple-servants** (as given to that duty)

===== Definitions =====

  - one given
  - Nethinim
  - Temple-servants

===== Usage =====

Nethinims.
