====== H729: אָרַז (ʼâraz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to be firm; used only in the passive participle as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h730|H730]];

===== Meaning =====

**of cedar**

===== Definitions =====

  - of cedar

===== Usage =====

made of cedar.
