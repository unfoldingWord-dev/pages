====== G73: ἀγών (agṓn) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a place of assembly (as if led), i.e. (by implication) a contest (held there); figuratively, an effort or anxiety

===== Usage =====

conflict, contention, fight, race.
