====== G3854: παραγίνομαι (paragínomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g1096|G1096]];

===== Meaning =====

to become near, i.e. approach (have arrived); by implication, to appear publicly

===== Usage =====

come, go, be present.
