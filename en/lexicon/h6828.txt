====== H6828: צָפוֹן (tsâphôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6845|H6845]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **hidden**, i.e. **dark**; used only of the **north** as a quarter (gloomy and unknown)

===== Definitions =====

  - hidden
  - dark
  - north

===== Usage =====

north(-ern, side, -ward, wind).
