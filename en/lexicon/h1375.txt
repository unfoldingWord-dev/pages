====== H1375: גְּבִיעַ (gᵉbîyaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to be convex);

===== Meaning =====

a **goblet**; by analogy, the **calyx** of a flower

===== Definitions =====

  - goblet
  - calyx

===== Usage =====

house, cup, pot.
