====== G5311: ὕψος (hýpsos) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]];

===== Meaning =====

elevation, i.e. (abstractly) altitude, (specially), the sky, or (figuratively) dignity

===== Usage =====

be exalted, height, (on) high.
