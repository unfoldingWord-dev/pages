====== H1324: בַּת (bath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1327|H1327]];

===== Meaning =====

a **bath** or Hebrew measure (as a means of **division**) of liquids

===== Definitions =====

  - bath
  - division

===== Usage =====

bath.
