====== G4317: προσάγω (proságō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to lead towards, i.e. (transitively) to conduct near (summon, present), or (intransitively) to approach

===== Usage =====

bring, draw near.
