====== G1242: διαθήκη (diathḗkē) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1303|G1303]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a disposition, i.e. (specially) a contract (especially a devisory will)

===== Usage =====

covenant, testament.
