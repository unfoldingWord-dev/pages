====== H3655: כָּנָה (kânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **address** by an additional name; hence, to **eulogize**

===== Definitions =====

  - address
  - eulogize

===== Usage =====

give flattering titles, surname (himself).
