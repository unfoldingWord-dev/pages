====== H6760: צָלַע (tsâlaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; probably to curve; used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h6763|H6763]],

===== Meaning =====

to **limp** (as if one-sided)

===== Definitions =====

  - limp

===== Usage =====

halt.
