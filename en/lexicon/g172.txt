====== G172: ἄκακος (ákakos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g2556|G2556]];

===== Meaning =====

not bad, i.e. (objectively) innocent or (subjectively) unsuspecting

===== Usage =====

harmless, simple.
