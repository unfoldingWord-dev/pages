====== G802: ἀσύνθετος (asýnthetos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4934|G4934]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, not agreed, i.e. treacherous to compacts

===== Usage =====

 covenant-breaker.
