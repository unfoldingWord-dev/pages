====== G922: βάρος (báros) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]] (through the notion of going down;
 compare [[en:lexicon:g899|G899]]);

===== Meaning =====

weight; in the New Testament only, figuratively, a load, abundance, authority

===== Usage =====

burden(-some), weight.
