====== H1580: גָּמַל (gâmal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **treat** a person (well or ill), i.e. **benefit** or **requite**; by implication (of toil), to **ripen**, i.e. (specifically) to **wean**

===== Definitions =====

  - treat
  - benefit
  - requite
  - ripen
  - wean

===== Usage =====

bestow on, deal bountifully, do (good), recompense, requite, reward, ripen,   serve, mean, yield.
