====== H6238: עָשַׁר (ʻâshar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **accumulate**; chiefly (specifically) to **grow** (causatively, **make**) **rich**

===== Definitions =====

  - accumulate
  - grow
  - make
  - rich
