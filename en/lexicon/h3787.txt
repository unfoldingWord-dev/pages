====== H3787: כָּשֵׁר (kâshêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root properly, to be straight or right;

===== Meaning =====

by implication, to **be acceptable**; also to **succeed** or **prosper**

===== Definitions =====

  - be acceptable
  - succeed
  - prosper

===== Usage =====

direct, be right, prosper.
