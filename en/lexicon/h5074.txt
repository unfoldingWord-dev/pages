====== H5074: נָדַד (nâdad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **wave** to and fro (rarely to flap up and down); figuratively, to **rove**, **flee**, or (causatively) to **drive away**

===== Definitions =====

  - wave
  - rove
  - flee
  - drive away

===== Usage =====

chase (away), × could not, depart, flee (× apace, away), (re-) move, thrust away, wander (abroad, -er, -ing).
