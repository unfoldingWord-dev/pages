====== H1612: גֶּפֶן (gephen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **bend**;

===== Meaning =====

a **vine** (as **twining**), especially the grape

===== Definitions =====

  - bend
  - vine
  - twining

===== Usage =====

vine, tree.
