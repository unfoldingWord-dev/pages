====== H5671: עַבְטִיט (ʻabṭîyṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5670|H5670]];

===== Meaning =====

something **pledged**, i.e. (collectively) **pawned goods**

===== Definitions =====

  - pledged
  - pawned goods

===== Usage =====

thick clay (by a false etym.).
