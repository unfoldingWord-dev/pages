====== G2517: καθεξῆς (kathexēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g1836|G1836]];

===== Meaning =====

thereafter, i.e. consecutively; as a noun (by ellipsis of noun) a subsequent person or time

===== Usage =====

after(-ward), by (in) order.
