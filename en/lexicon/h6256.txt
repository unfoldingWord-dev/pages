====== H6256: עֵת (ʻêth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5703|H5703]];

===== Meaning =====

**time**, especially (adverb with preposition) **now**, **when**, etc.

===== Definitions =====

  - time
  - now
  - when

===== Usage =====

  after, (al-) ways, × certain,   continually,   evening, long, (due) season, so (long) as, (even-, evening-, noon-) tide, (meal-), what) time, when.
