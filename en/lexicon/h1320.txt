====== H1320: בָּשָׂר (bâsâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1319|H1319]];

===== Meaning =====

**flesh** (from its **freshness**); by extension, **body**, **person**; also (by euphemistically) the **pudenda** of a man

===== Definitions =====

  - flesh
  - freshness
  - body
  - person
  - pudenda

===== Usage =====

body, (fat, lean) flesh(-ed), kin, (man-) kind,   nakedness, self, skin.
