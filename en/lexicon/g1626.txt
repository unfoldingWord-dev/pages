====== G1626: ἔκτρωμα (éktrōma) ======

===== Source =====

from a comparative of [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and τιτρώσκω (titrṓskō) (to wound);

===== Meaning =====

a miscarriage (abortion), i.e. (by analogy) untimely birth

===== Usage =====

born out of due time.
