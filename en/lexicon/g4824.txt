====== G4824: συμβούλιον (symboúlion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4825|G4825]];

===== Meaning =====

advisement; specially, a deliberative body, i.e. the provincial assessors or lay-court

===== Usage =====

consultation, counsel, council.
