====== H8521: תֵּל חַרְשָׁא (Têl Charshâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8510|H8510]] and the feminine of [[en:lexicon:h2798|H2798]]; **mound of workmanship**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tel-Charsha**, a place in Babylonia

===== Definitions =====

  - mound of workmanship
  - Tel-Charsha

===== Usage =====

Tel-haresha, Tel-harsa.
