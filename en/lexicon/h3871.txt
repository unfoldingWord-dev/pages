====== H3871: לוּחַ (lûwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

probably meaning to **glisten**; a **tablet** (as polished), of stone, wood or metal

===== Definitions =====

  - glisten
  - tablet

===== Usage =====

board, plate, table.
