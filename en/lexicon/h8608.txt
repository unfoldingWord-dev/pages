====== H8608: תָּפַף (tâphaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **drum**, i.e. **play** (as) **on the tambourine**

===== Definitions =====

  - drum
  - play
  - on the tambourine

===== Usage =====

taber, play with timbrels.
