====== H890: בׇּאְשָׁה (boʼshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h889|H889]];

===== Meaning =====

**stink-weed** or any other **noxious** or **useless** plant

===== Definitions =====

  - stink-weed
  - noxious
  - useless

===== Usage =====

cockle.
