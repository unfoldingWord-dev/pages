====== H500: אֶלְעָלֵא (ʼElʻâlêʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

or (more properly) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]] and [[en:lexicon:h5927|H5927]]; **God** (is) **going up**;

===== Meaning =====

**Elale** or **Elaleh**, a place east of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - God
  - going up
  - Elale
  - Elaleh

===== Usage =====

Elealeh.
