====== G4859: σύμφωνος (sýmphōnos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g5456|G5456]];

===== Meaning =====

sounding together (alike), i.e. (figuratively) accordant (neuter as noun, agreement)

===== Usage =====

 consent.
