====== G5290: ὑποστρέφω (hypostréphō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g4762|G4762]];

===== Meaning =====

to turn under (behind), i.e. to return (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

come again, return (again, back again), turn back (again).
