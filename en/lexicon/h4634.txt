====== H4634: מַעֲרָכָה (maʻărâkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4633|H4633]];

===== Meaning =====

an **arrangement**; concretely, a **pile**; specifically a military **array**

===== Definitions =====

  - arrangement
  - pile
  - array

===== Usage =====

army, fight, be set in order, ordered place, rank, row.
