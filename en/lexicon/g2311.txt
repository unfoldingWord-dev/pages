====== G2311: θεμελιόω (themelióō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2310|G2310]];

===== Meaning =====

to lay a basis for, i.e. (literally) erect, or (figuratively) consolidate

===== Usage =====

(lay the) found(- ation), ground, settle.
