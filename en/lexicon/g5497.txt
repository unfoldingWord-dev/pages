====== G5497: χειραγωγός (cheiragōgós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5495|G5495]] and a reduplicated form of [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

a hand-leader, i.e. personal conductor (of a blind person)

===== Usage =====

some to lead by the hand.
