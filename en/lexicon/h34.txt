====== H34: אֶבְיוֹן (ʼebyôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h14|H14]], in the sense of **want** (especially in feeling);

===== Meaning =====

**destitute**

===== Definitions =====

  - want
  - destitute

===== Usage =====

beggar, needy, poor (man).
