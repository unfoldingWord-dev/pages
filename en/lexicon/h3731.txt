====== H3731: כַּפְתֹּר (Kaphtôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

or (Amos 9:7) [[en:lexicon:|]]; apparently the same as [[en:lexicon:h3730|H3730]];

===== Meaning =====

**Caphtor** (i.e. a wreath-shaped island), the original seat of the Philistines

===== Definitions =====

  - Caphtor

===== Usage =====

Caphtor.
