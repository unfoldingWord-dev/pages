====== H7356: רַחַם (racham) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7355|H7355]];

===== Meaning =====

**compassion** (in the plural); by extension, the **womb** (as **cherishing** the fetus); by implication, a **maiden**

===== Definitions =====

  - compassion
  - womb
  - cherishing
  - maiden

===== Usage =====

bowels, compassion, damsel, tender love, (great, tender) mercy, pity, womb.
