====== H5226: נֵכַח (nêkach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **be straightforward**;

===== Meaning =====

properly, the **fore** part; used adverbially, **opposite**

===== Definitions =====

  - be straightforward
  - fore
  - opposite

===== Usage =====

before, over against.
