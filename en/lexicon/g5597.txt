====== G5597: ψώχω (psṓchō) ======

===== Source =====

prolongation from the same base as [[en:lexicon:g5567|G5567]];

===== Meaning =====

to triturate, i.e. (by analogy) to rub out (kernels from husks with the fingers or hand)

===== Usage =====

rub.
