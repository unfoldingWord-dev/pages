====== G765: ἀσεβής (asebḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4576|G4576]];

===== Meaning =====

irreverent, i.e. (by extension) impious or wicked

===== Usage =====

 ungodly (man).
