====== H79: אָבַק (ʼâbaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root, probably to **float** away (as vapor), but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h80|H80]];

===== Meaning =====

to **bedust**, i.e. **grapple**

===== Definitions =====

  - float
  - bedust
  - grapple

===== Usage =====

wrestle.
