====== H6339: פָּזַז (pâzaz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with [[en:lexicon:h6338|H6338]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **solidify** (as if by refining); also to **spring** (as if separating the limbs)

===== Definitions =====

  - solidify
  - spring

===== Usage =====

leap, be made strong.
