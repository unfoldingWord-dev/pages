====== G669: ἀποφθέγγομαι (apophthéngomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g5350|G5350]];

===== Meaning =====

to enunciate plainly, i.e. declare

===== Usage =====

say, speak forth, utterance.
