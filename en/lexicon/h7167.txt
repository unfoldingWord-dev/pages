====== H7167: קָרַע (qâraʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **rend**, literally or figuratively (revile, paint the eyes, as if enlarging them)

===== Definitions =====

  - rend

===== Usage =====

cut out, rend, × surely, tear.
