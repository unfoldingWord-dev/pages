====== G2105: εὐδία (eudía) ======

===== Source =====

feminine from [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g2203|G2203]] (as the god of the
 weather);

===== Meaning =====

a clear sky, i.e. fine weather

===== Usage =====

fair weather.
