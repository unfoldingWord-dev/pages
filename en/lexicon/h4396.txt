====== H4396: מִלֻּאָה (milluʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4394|H4394]];

===== Meaning =====

a **filling**, i.e. **setting** (of gems)

===== Definitions =====

  - filling
  - setting

===== Usage =====

enclosing, setting.
