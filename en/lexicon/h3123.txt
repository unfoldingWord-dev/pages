====== H3123: יוֹנָה (yôwnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:lexicon:h3196|H3196]];

===== Meaning =====

a **dove** (apparently from the warmth of their mating)

===== Definitions =====

  - dove

===== Usage =====

dove, pigeon.
