====== H309: אָחַר (ʼâchar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **loiter** (i.e. be behind); by implication to **procrastinate**

===== Definitions =====

  - loiter
  - procrastinate

===== Usage =====

continue, defer, delay, hinder, be late (slack), stay (there), tarry (longer).
