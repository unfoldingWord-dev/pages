====== G945: βαττολογέω (battologéō) ======

===== Source =====

from Βάττος (Báttos) (a proverbial stammerer) and [[en:lexicon:g3056|G3056]];

===== Meaning =====

to stutter, i.e. (by implication) to prate tediously

===== Usage =====

use vain repetitions.
