====== G576: ἀποβαίνω (apobaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

literally, to disembark; figuratively, to eventuate

===== Usage =====

become, go out, turn.
