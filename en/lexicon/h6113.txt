====== H6113: עָצָר (ʻâtsâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **inclose**; by analogy, to **hold back**; also to **maintain**, **rule**, **assemble**

===== Definitions =====

  - inclose
  - hold back
  - maintain
  - rule
  - assemble

===== Usage =====

× be able, close up, detain, fast, keep (self close, still), prevail, recover, refrain, × reign, restrain, retain, shut (up), slack, stay, stop, withhold (self).
