====== H2986: יָבַל (yâbal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **flow**; causatively, to **bring** (especially with pomp)

===== Definitions =====

  - flow
  - bring

===== Usage =====

bring (forth), carry, lead (forth).
