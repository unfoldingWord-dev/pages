====== H822: אֶשְׁנָב (ʼeshnâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from an unused root (probably meaning to leave interstices);

===== Meaning =====

a **latticed window**

===== Definitions =====

  - latticed window

===== Usage =====

casement, lattice.
