====== G506: ἀνυπότακτος (anypótaktos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5293|G5293]];

===== Meaning =====

unsubdued, i.e. insubordinate (in fact or temper)

===== Usage =====

disobedient, that is not put under, unruly.
