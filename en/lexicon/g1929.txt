====== G1929: ἐπιδίδωμι (epidídōmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g1325|G1325]];

===== Meaning =====

to give over (by hand or surrender)

===== Usage =====

deliver unto, give, let (+ (her drive)), offer.
