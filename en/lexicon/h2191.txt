====== H2191: זְעֵיר (zᵉʻêyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (akin (by permutation) to [[en:lexicon:h6819|H6819]]), meaning to **dwindle**;

===== Meaning =====

**small**

===== Definitions =====

  - dwindle
  - small

===== Usage =====

little.
