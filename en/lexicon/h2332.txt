====== H2332: חַוָּה (Chavvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

causatively from [[en:lexicon:h2331|H2331]]; life-giver;

===== Meaning =====

**Chavvah** (or **Eve**), the first woman

===== Definitions =====

  - Chavvah
  - Eve

===== Usage =====

Eve.
