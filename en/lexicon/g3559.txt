====== G3559: νουθεσία (nouthesía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3563|G3563]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

calling attention to, i.e. (by implication) mild rebuke or warning

===== Usage =====

admonition.
