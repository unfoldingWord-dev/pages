====== G844: αὐτόματος (autómatos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g846|G846]] and the same as [[en:lexicon:g3155|G3155]];

===== Meaning =====

self-moved ("automatic"), i.e. spontaneous

===== Usage =====
