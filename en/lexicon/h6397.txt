====== H6397: פְּלוֹנִי (Pᵉlôwnîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

patronymically from an unused name (from [[en:lexicon:h6395|H6395]]) meaning **separate**;

===== Meaning =====

a **Pelonite** or inhabitant of an unknown Palon

===== Definitions =====

  - separate
  - Pelonite

===== Usage =====

Pelonite.
