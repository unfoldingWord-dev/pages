====== G4931: συντελέω (synteléō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g5055|G5055]];

===== Meaning =====

to complete entirely; generally, to execute (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

end, finish, fulfil, make.
