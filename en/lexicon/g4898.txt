====== G4898: συνέκδημος (synékdēmos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g1553|G1553]];

===== Meaning =====

a co-absentee from home, i.e. fellow-traveller

===== Usage =====

companion in travel, travel with.
