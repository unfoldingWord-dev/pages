====== G3863: παραζηλόω (parazēlóō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g2206|G2206]];

===== Meaning =====

to stimulate alongside, i.e. excite to rivalry

===== Usage =====

provoke to emulation (jealousy).
