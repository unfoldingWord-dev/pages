====== G3888: παραμυθέομαι (paramythéomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and the middle voice of a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3454|G3454]];

===== Meaning =====

to relate near, i.e. (by implication) encourage, console

===== Usage =====

comfort.
