====== G2618: κατακαίω (katakaíō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2545|G2545]];

===== Meaning =====

to burn down (to the ground), i.e. consume wholly

===== Usage =====

burn (up, utterly).
