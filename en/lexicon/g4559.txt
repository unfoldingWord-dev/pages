====== G4559: σαρκικός (sarkikós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4561|G4561]];

===== Meaning =====

pertaining to flesh, i.e. (by extension) bodily, temporal, or (by implication) animal, unregenerate

===== Usage =====

carnal, fleshly.
