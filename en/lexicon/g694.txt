====== G694: ἀργύριον (argýrion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g696|G696]];

===== Meaning =====

silvery, i.e. (by implication) cash; specially, a silverling (i.e. drachma or shekel)

===== Usage =====

money, (piece of) silver (piece).
