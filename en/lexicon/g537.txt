====== G537: ἅπας (hápas) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a particle of union) and [[en:lexicon:g3956|G3956]];

===== Meaning =====

absolutely all or (singular) every one

===== Usage =====

all (things), every (one), whole.
