====== G1289: διασπείρω (diaspeírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g4687|G4687]];

===== Meaning =====

to sow throughout, i.e. (figuratively) distribute in foreign lands

===== Usage =====

scatter abroad.
