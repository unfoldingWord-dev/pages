====== G628: ἀπολούω (apoloúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g3068|G3068]];

===== Meaning =====

to wash fully, i.e. (figuratively) have remitted (reflexively)

===== Usage =====

wash (away).
