====== G858: ἀφελότης (aphelótēs) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and φέλλος (phéllos) (in the
 sense of a stone as stubbing the foot);

===== Meaning =====

smoothness, i.e. (figuratively) simplicity

===== Usage =====

singleness.
