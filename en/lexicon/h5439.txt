====== H5439: סָבִיב (çâbîyb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h5437|H5437]];

===== Meaning =====

(as noun) a **circle**, **neighbour**, or **environs**; but chiefly (as adverb, with or without preposition) **around**

===== Definitions =====

  - circle
  - neighbour
  - environs
  - around

===== Usage =====

(place, round) about, circuit, compass, on every side.
