====== G4798: συγχράομαι (synchráomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g5530|G5530]];

===== Meaning =====

to use jointly, i.e. (by implication) to hold intercourse in common

===== Usage =====

have dealings with.
