====== H7287: רָדָה (râdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tread** down, i.e. **subjugate**; specifically, to **crumble** off

===== Definitions =====

  - tread
  - subjugate
  - crumble

===== Usage =====

(come to, make to) have dominion, prevail against, reign, (bear, make to) rule,(-r, over), take.
