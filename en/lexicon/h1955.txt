====== H1955: הוֹשַׁעְיָה (Hôwshaʻyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3467|H3467]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; **Jah has saved**;

===== Meaning =====

**Hoshajah**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has saved
  - Hoshajah

===== Usage =====

Hoshaiah.
