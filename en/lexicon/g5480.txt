====== G5480: χάραγμα (cháragma) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g5482|G5482]];

===== Meaning =====

a scratch or etching, i.e. stamp (as a badge of servitude), or sculptured figure (statue)

===== Usage =====

graven, mark.
