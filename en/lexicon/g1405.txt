====== G1405: δράσσομαι (drássomai) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps akin to the base of [[en:lexicon:g1404|G1404]] (through the idea of capturing);

===== Meaning =====

to grasp, i.e. (figuratively) entrap

===== Usage =====

take.
