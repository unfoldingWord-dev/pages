====== G4018: περιβόλαιον (peribólaion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4016|G4016]];

===== Meaning =====

something thrown around one, i.e. a mantle, veil

===== Usage =====

covering, vesture.
