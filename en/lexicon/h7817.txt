====== H7817: שָׁחַח (shâchach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sink** or **depress** (reflexive or causative)

===== Definitions =====

  - sink
  - depress

===== Usage =====

bend, bow (down), bring (cast) down, couch, humble self, be (bring) low, stoop.
