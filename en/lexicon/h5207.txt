====== H5207: נִיחוֹחַ (nîychôwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h5117|H5117]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **restful**, i.e. **pleasant**; abstractly, **delight**

===== Definitions =====

  - restful
  - pleasant
  - delight

===== Usage =====

sweet (odour).
