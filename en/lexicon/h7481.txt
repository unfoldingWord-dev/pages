====== H7481: רָעַם (râʻam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tumble**, i.e. **be violently agitated**; specifically, to **crash** (of thunder); figuratively, to **irritate** (with anger)

===== Definitions =====

  - tumble
  - be violently agitated
  - crash
  - irritate

===== Usage =====

make to fret, roar, thunder, trouble.
