====== G4789: συγκληρονόμος (synklēronómos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2818|G2818]];

===== Meaning =====

a co-heir, i.e. (by analogy) participant in common

===== Usage =====

fellow (joint)-heir, heir together, heir with.
