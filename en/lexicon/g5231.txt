====== G5231: ὑπεράνω (hyperánō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]] and [[en:lexicon:g507|G507]];

===== Meaning =====

above upward, i.e. greatly higher (in place or rank)

===== Usage =====

far above, over.
