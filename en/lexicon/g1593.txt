====== G1593: ἐκνεύω (ekneúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g3506|G3506]];

===== Meaning =====

(by analogy) to slip off, i.e. quietly withdraw

===== Usage =====

convey self away.
