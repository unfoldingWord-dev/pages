====== H2953: טְפַר (ṭᵉphar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h6852|H6852]], and meaning the same as [[en:lexicon:h6856|H6856]];

===== Meaning =====

a **finger-nail**; also a **hoof** or **claw**

===== Definitions =====

  - finger-nail
  - hoof
  - claw

===== Usage =====

nail.
