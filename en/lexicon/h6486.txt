====== H6486: פְּקֻדָּה (pᵉquddâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h6485|H6485]];

===== Meaning =====

**visitation** (in many senses, chiefly official)

===== Definitions =====

  - visitation

===== Usage =====

account, (that have the) charge, custody, that which...laid up, numbers, office(-r), ordering, oversight,   prison, reckoning, visitation.
