====== H6231: עָשַׁק (ʻâshaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h6229|H6229]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **press upon**, i.e. **oppress**, **defraud**, **violate**, **overflow**

===== Definitions =====

  - press upon
  - oppress
  - defraud
  - violate
  - overflow

===== Usage =====

get deceitfully, deceive, defraud, drink up, (use) oppress(-ion), -or), do violence (wrong).
