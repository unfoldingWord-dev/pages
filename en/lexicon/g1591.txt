====== G1591: ἐκμάσσω (ekmássō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g3145|G3145]];

===== Meaning =====

to knead out, i.e. (by analogy) to wipe dry

===== Usage =====

wipe.
