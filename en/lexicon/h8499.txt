====== H8499: תְּכוּנָה (tᵉkûwnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3559|H3559]]; or probably identical with [[en:lexicon:h8498|H8498]];

===== Meaning =====

**something arranged** or **fixed**, i.e. a place

===== Definitions =====

  - something arranged
  - fixed

===== Usage =====

seat.
