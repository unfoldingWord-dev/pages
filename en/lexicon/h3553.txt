====== H3553: כּוֹבַע (kôwbaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be **high** or **rounded**;

===== Meaning =====

a **helmet** (as **arched**)

===== Definitions =====

  - high
  - rounded
  - helmet
  - arched

===== Usage =====

helmet. Compare
