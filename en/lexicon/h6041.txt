====== H6041: עָנִי (ʻânîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6031|H6031]]; (practically the same as [[en:lexicon:h6035|H6035]], although the margin constantly disputes this, making [[en:lexicon:h6035|H6035]] subjective and objective);

===== Meaning =====

**depressed**, in mind or circumstances

===== Definitions =====

  - depressed

===== Usage =====

afflicted, humble, lowly, needy, poor.
