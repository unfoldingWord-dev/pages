====== H4208: מַזָּלָה (mazzâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h5140|H5140]] in the sense of raining;

===== Meaning =====

a **constellation**, i.e. **Zodiacal sign** (perhaps as affecting the weather)

===== Definitions =====

  - constellation
  - Zodiacal sign

===== Usage =====

planet. Compare
