====== H5237: נׇכְרִי (nokrîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5235|H5235]] (second form);

===== Meaning =====

**strange**, in a variety of degrees and applications (**foreign**, **non-relative**, **adulterous**, **different**, **wonderful**)

===== Definitions =====

  - strange
  - foreign
  - non-relative
  - adulterous
  - different
  - wonderful

===== Usage =====

alien, foreigner, outlandish, strange(-r, woman).
