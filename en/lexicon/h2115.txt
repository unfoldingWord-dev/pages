====== H2115: זוּר (zûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h6695|H6695]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **press together**, **tighten**

===== Definitions =====

  - press together
  - tighten

===== Usage =====

close, rush, thrust together.
