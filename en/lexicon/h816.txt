====== H816: אָשַׁם (ʼâsham) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be guilty**; by implication to **be punished** or **perish**

===== Definitions =====

  - be guilty
  - be punished
  - perish

===== Usage =====

× certainly, be(-come, made) desolate, destroy, × greatly, be(-come, found, hold) guilty, offend (acknowledge offence), trespass.
