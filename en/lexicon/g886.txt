====== G886: ἀχειροποίητος (acheiropoíētos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5499|G5499]];

===== Meaning =====

unmanufactured, i.e. inartificial

===== Usage =====

made without (not made with) hands.
