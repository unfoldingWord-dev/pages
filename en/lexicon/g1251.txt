====== G1251: διακούομαι (diakoúomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g191|G191]];

===== Meaning =====

to hear throughout, i.e. patiently listen (to a prisoner's plea)

===== Usage =====

hear.
