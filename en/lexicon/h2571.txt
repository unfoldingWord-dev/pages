====== H2571: חָמֻשׁ (châmush) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

passive participle of the same as [[en:lexicon:h2570|H2570]];

===== Meaning =====

**staunch**, i.e. able-bodied **soldiers**

===== Definitions =====

  - staunch
  - soldiers

===== Usage =====

armed (men), harnessed.
