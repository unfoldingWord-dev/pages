====== H8052: שְׁמוּעָה (shᵉmûwʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h8074|H8074]];

===== Meaning =====

something **heard**, i.e. an **announcement**

===== Definitions =====

  - heard
  - announcement

===== Usage =====

bruit, doctrine, fame, mentioned, news, report, rumor, tidings.
