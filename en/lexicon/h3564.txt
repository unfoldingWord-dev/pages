====== H3564: כּוּר (kûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning properly, to **dig** through;

===== Meaning =====

a **pot** or **furnace** (as if excavated)

===== Definitions =====

  - dig
  - pot
  - furnace

===== Usage =====

furnace. Compare
