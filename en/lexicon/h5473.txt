====== H5473: סוּג (çûwg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably rather identical with [[en:lexicon:h5472|H5472]] through the idea of shrinking from a hedge; compare [[en:lexicon:h7735|H7735]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **hem in**, i.e. **bind**

===== Definitions =====

  - hem in
  - bind

===== Usage =====

set about.
