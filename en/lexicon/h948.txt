====== H948: בּוּץ (bûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (of the same form) meaning to bleach, i.e. (intransitive) be white;

===== Meaning =====

probably **cotton** (of some sort)

===== Definitions =====

  - cotton

===== Usage =====

fine (white) linen.
