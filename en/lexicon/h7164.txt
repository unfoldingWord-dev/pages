====== H7164: קָרַס (qâraç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to **protrude**; used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7165|H7165]] (for alliteration with [[en:lexicon:h7167|H7167]]),

===== Meaning =====

to **hunch**, i.e. be hump-backed

===== Definitions =====

  - protrude
  - hunch

===== Usage =====

stoop.
