====== H3915: לַיִל (layil) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (Isaiah 21:11) [[en:lexicon:|]]; also [[en:lexicon:|]]; from the same as [[en:lexicon:h3883|H3883]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **twist** (away of the light), i.e. **night**; figuratively, **adversity**

===== Definitions =====

  - twist
  - night
  - adversity

===== Usage =====

(mid-)night (season).
