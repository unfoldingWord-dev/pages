====== G4952: συσπαράσσω (sysparássō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g4682|G4682]];

===== Meaning =====

to rend completely, i.e. (by analogy) to convulse violently

===== Usage =====

throw down.
