====== G318: ἀνάγκη (anánkē) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g43|G43]];

===== Meaning =====

constraint (literally or figuratively); by implication, distress

===== Usage =====

distress, must needs, (of) necessity(-sary), needeth, needful.
