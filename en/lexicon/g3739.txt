====== G3739: ὅς (hós) ======

===== Source =====

probably a primary word (or perhaps a form of the article [[en:lexicon:g3588|G3588]]);

===== Meaning =====

the relatively (sometimes demonstrative) pronoun, who, which, what, that

===== Usage =====

one, (an-, the) other, some, that, what, which, who(-m, -se), etc.
