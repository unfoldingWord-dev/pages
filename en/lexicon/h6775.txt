====== H6775: צָמַד (tsâmad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **link**, i.e. **gird**; figuratively, to **serve**, (mentally) **contrive**

===== Definitions =====

  - link
  - gird
  - serve
  - contrive

===== Usage =====

fasten, frame, join (self).
