====== G398: ἀναφαίνω (anaphaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]];

===== Meaning =====

to show, i.e. (reflexively) appear, or (passively) to have pointed out

===== Usage =====

(should) appear, discover.
