====== H5845: עֲטִין (ʻăṭîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning apparently to contain;

===== Meaning =====

a **receptacle** (for milk, i.e. pail; figuratively, breast)

===== Definitions =====

  - receptacle

===== Usage =====

breast.
