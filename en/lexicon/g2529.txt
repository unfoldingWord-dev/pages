====== G2529: καθοράω (kathoráō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g3708|G3708]];

===== Meaning =====

to behold fully, i.e. (figuratively) distinctly apprehend

===== Usage =====

clearly see.
