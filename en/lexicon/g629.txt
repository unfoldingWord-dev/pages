====== G629: ἀπολύτρωσις (apolýtrōsis) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g3083|G3083]];

===== Meaning =====

(the act) ransom in full, i.e. (figuratively) riddance, or (specially) Christian salvation

===== Usage =====

deliverance, redemption.
