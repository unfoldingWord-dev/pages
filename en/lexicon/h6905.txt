====== H6905: קָבָל (qâbâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6901|H6901]] in the sense of opposite (see [[en:lexicon:h6904|H6904]]);

===== Meaning =====

the **presence**, i.e. (adverbially) **in front of**

===== Definitions =====

  - presence
  - in front of

===== Usage =====

before.
