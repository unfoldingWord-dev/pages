====== H6320: פּוּךְ (pûwk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **paint**;

===== Meaning =====

**dye** (specifically, stibium for the eyes)

===== Definitions =====

  - paint
  - dye

===== Usage =====

fair colours, glistering, paint(-ed) (-ing).
