====== G824: ἄτοπος (átopos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5117|G5117]];

===== Meaning =====

out of place, i.e. (figuratively) improper, injurious, wicked

===== Usage =====

amiss, harm, unreasonable.
