====== H8565: תַּן (tan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to **elongate**;

===== Meaning =====

a **monster** (as preternaturally formed), i.e. a **sea-serpent** (or other huge marine animal); also a **jackal** (or other hideous land animal)

===== Definitions =====

  - elongate
  - monster
  - sea-serpent
  - jackal

===== Usage =====

dragon, whale. Compare
