====== H2707: חָקָה (châqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **carve**; by implication, to **delineate**; also to **entrench**

===== Definitions =====

  - carve
  - delineate
  - entrench

===== Usage =====

carved work, portrayed, set a print.
