====== G4226: ποῦ (poû) ======

===== Source =====

genitive case of an interrogative pronoun πός (pós) (what) otherwise
 obsolete (perhaps the same as [[en:lexicon:g4225|G4225]] used with the rising slide of
 inquiry);

===== Meaning =====

as adverb of place; at (by implication, to) what locality

===== Usage =====

where, whither.
