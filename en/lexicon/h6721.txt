====== H6721: צִידוֹן (Tsîydôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6679|H6679]] in the sense of catching fish; fishery;

===== Meaning =====

**Tsidon**, the name of a son of Canaan, and of a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Tsidon

===== Usage =====
