====== H525: אָמוֹן (ʼâmôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h539|H539]], probably in the sense of training; like [[en:lexicon:h542|H542]]

===== Meaning =====

**skilled**, i.e. **an architect**

===== Definitions =====

  - skilled
  - an architect

===== Usage =====

one brought up.
