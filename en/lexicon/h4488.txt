====== H4488: מָנֶה (mâneh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4487|H4487]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **fixed weight** or **measured amount**, i.e. (techn.) a **maneh** or **mina**

===== Definitions =====

  - fixed weight
  - measured amount
  - maneh
  - mina

===== Usage =====

maneh, pound.
