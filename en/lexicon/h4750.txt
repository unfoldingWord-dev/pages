====== H4750: מִקְשָׁה (miqshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7180|H7180]];

===== Meaning =====

literally a **cucumbered** field, i.e. a **cucumber** patch

===== Definitions =====

  - cucumbered
  - cucumber

===== Usage =====

garden of cucumbers.
