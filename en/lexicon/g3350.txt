====== G3350: μετοικεσία (metoikesía) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g3624|G3624]];

===== Meaning =====

a change of abode, i.e. (specially), expatriation

===== Usage =====

X brought, carried(-ying) away (in-)to.
