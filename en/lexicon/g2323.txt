====== G2323: θεραπεύω (therapeúō) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g2324|G2324]];

===== Meaning =====

to wait upon menially, i.e. (figuratively) to adore (God), or (specially) to relieve (of disease)

===== Usage =====

cure, heal, worship.
