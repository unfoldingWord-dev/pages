====== H1212: בְּצַלְאֵל (Bᵉtsalʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:h6738|H6738]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]] with a prepositional prefix; **in** (the) **shadow** (i.e. protection) **of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Betsalel**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - in
  - shadow
  - of God
  - Betsalel

===== Usage =====

Bezaleel.
