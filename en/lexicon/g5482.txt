====== G5482: χάραξ (chárax) ======

===== Source =====

from χαράσσω (charássō) (to sharpen to a point; akin to [[en:lexicon:g1125|G1125]] through the idea of
 scratching);

===== Meaning =====

a stake, i.e. (by implication) a palisade or rampart (military mound for circumvallation in a siege)

===== Usage =====

trench.
