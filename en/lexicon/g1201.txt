====== G1201: δεσμωτήριον (desmōtḗrion) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1199|G1199]] (equivalent to [[en:lexicon:g1196|G1196]]);

===== Meaning =====

a place of bondage, i.e. a dungeon

===== Usage =====

prison.
