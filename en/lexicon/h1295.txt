====== H1295: בְּרֵכָה (bᵉrêkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1288|H1288]];

===== Meaning =====

a **reservoir** (at which camels **kneel** as a resting-place)

===== Definitions =====

  - reservoir
  - kneel

===== Usage =====

(fish-) pool.
