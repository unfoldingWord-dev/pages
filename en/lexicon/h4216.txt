====== H4216: מַזָּרָה (mazzârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h5144|H5144]] in the sense of distinction;

===== Meaning =====

**some noted constellation** (only in the plural), perhaps collectively, the **zodiac**

===== Definitions =====

  - some noted constellation
  - zodiac

===== Usage =====

Mazzoroth. Compare
