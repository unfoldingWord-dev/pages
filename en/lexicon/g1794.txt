====== G1794: ἐντυλίσσω (entylíssō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and τυλίσσω (tylíssō) (to twist; probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g1507|G1507]]);

===== Meaning =====

to entwine, i.e. wind up in

===== Usage =====

wrap in (together).
