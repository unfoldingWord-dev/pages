====== H1817: דֶּלֶת (deleth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculinenoun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1802|H1802]];

===== Meaning =====

**something swinging**, i.e. **the valve of a door**

===== Definitions =====

  - something swinging
  - the valve of a door

===== Usage =====

door (two-leaved), gate, leaf, lid. (Psalm 141:3).
