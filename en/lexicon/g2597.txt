====== G2597: καταβαίνω (katabaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to descend (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

come (get, go, step) down, fall (down).
