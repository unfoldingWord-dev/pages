====== G4388: προτίθεμαι (protíthemai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

to place before, i.e. (for oneself) to exhibit; (to oneself) to propose (determine)

===== Usage =====

purpose, set forth.
