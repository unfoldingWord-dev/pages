====== G4963: συστροφή (systrophḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4962|G4962]];

===== Meaning =====

a twisting together, i.e. (figuratively) a secret coalition, riotous crowd

===== Usage =====

+ band together, concourse.
