====== H1784: דִּינַי (Dîynay) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) partial from uncertain primitive;

===== Meaning =====

a **Dinaite** or inhabitant of some unknown Assyria province

===== Definitions =====

  - Dinaite

===== Usage =====

Dinaite.
