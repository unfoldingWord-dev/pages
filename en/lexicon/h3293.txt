====== H3293: יַעַר (yaʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to thicken with verdure;

===== Meaning =====

a **copse of bushes**; hence, a **forest**; hence, **honey in the comb** (as hived in trees)

===== Definitions =====

  - copse of bushes
  - forest
  - honey in the comb

===== Usage =====

(honey-) comb, forest, wood.
