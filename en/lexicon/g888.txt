====== G888: ἀχρεῖος (achreîos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5534|G5534]] (compare

===== Meaning =====

===== Usage =====
