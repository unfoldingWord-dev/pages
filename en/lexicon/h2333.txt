====== H2333: חַוָּה (chavvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

properly, the same as [[en:lexicon:h2332|H2332]] (**life-giving**, i.e. **living-place**);

===== Meaning =====

by implication, an encampment or **village**

===== Definitions =====

  - life-giving
  - living-place
  - village

===== Usage =====

(small) town.
