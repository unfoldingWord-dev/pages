====== H8259: שָׁקַף (shâqaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **lean out** (of a window), i.e. (by implication) **peep** or **gaze** (passively, **be a spectacle**)

===== Definitions =====

  - lean out
  - peep
  - gaze
  - be a spectacle

===== Usage =====

appear, look (down, forth, out).
