====== H1430: גָּדִישׁ (gâdîysh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to heap up);

===== Meaning =====

a **stack** of sheaves; by analogy, a **tomb**

===== Definitions =====

  - stack
  - tomb

===== Usage =====

shock (stack) (of corn), tomb.
