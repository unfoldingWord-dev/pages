====== H5306: נֹפֶךְ (nôphek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **glisten**;

===== Meaning =====

**shining**; a gem, probably the **garnet**

===== Definitions =====

  - glisten
  - shining
  - garnet

===== Usage =====

emerald.
