====== H7185: קָשָׁה (qâshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be dense**, i.e. tough or **severe** (in various applications)

===== Definitions =====

  - be dense
  - severe

===== Usage =====

be cruel, be fiercer, make grievous, be ((ask a), be in, have, seem, would) hard(-en, (labour), -ly, thing), be sore, (be, make) stiff(-en, (-necked)).
