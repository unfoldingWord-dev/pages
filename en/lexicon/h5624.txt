====== H5624: סֶרֶד (Çered) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from a primitive root meaning to **tremble**; **trembling**;

===== Meaning =====

**Sered**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - tremble
  - trembling
  - Sered

===== Usage =====

Sered.
