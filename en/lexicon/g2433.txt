====== G2433: ἱλάσκομαι (hiláskomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from the same as [[en:lexicon:g2436|G2436]];

===== Meaning =====

to conciliate, i.e. (transitively) to atone for (sin), or (intransitively) be propitious

===== Usage =====

be merciful, make reconciliation for.
