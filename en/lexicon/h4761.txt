====== H4761: מַרְאָשָׁה (marʼâshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7218|H7218]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **headship**, i.e. (plural for collective) **dominion**

===== Definitions =====

  - headship
  - dominion

===== Usage =====

principality.
