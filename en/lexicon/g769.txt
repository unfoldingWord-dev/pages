====== G769: ἀσθένεια (asthéneia) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g772|G772]];

===== Meaning =====

feebleness (of mind or body); by implication, malady; morally, frailty

===== Usage =====

disease, infirmity, sickness, weakness.
