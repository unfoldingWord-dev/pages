====== H7237: רַבָּה (Rabbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h7227|H7227]]; **great**;

===== Meaning =====

**Rabbah**, the name of two places in Palestine, East and West

===== Definitions =====

  - great
  - Rabbah

===== Usage =====

Rabbah, Rabbath.
