====== H4745: מִקְרֶה (miqreh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7136|H7136]];

===== Meaning =====

something **met** with, i.e. an **accident** or **fortune**

===== Definitions =====

  - met
  - accident
  - fortune

===== Usage =====

something befallen, befalleth, chance, event, hap(-peneth).
