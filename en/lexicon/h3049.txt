====== H3049: יִדְּעֹנִי (yiddᵉʻônîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3045|H3045]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **knowing** one; specifically, a **conjurer**; (by impl) a **ghost**

===== Definitions =====

  - knowing
  - conjurer
  - ghost

===== Usage =====

wizard.
