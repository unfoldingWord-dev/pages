====== G3543: νομίζω (nomízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3551|G3551]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to do by law (usage), i.e. to accustom (passively, be usual); by extension, to deem or regard

===== Usage =====

 suppose, thing, be wont.
