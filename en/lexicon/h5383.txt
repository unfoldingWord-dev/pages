====== H5383: נָשָׁה (nâshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather identical with [[en:lexicon:h5382|H5382]], in the sense of [[en:lexicon:h5378|H5378]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **lend** or (by reciprocity) **borrow** on security or interest

===== Definitions =====

  - lend
  - borrow

===== Usage =====

creditor, exact, extortioner, lend, usurer, lend on (taker on) usury.
