====== H38: אֲבִיָּם (ʼĂbîyâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h3220|H3220]]; father of (the) sea (i.e. seaman);

===== Meaning =====

**Abijam** (or Abijah), a king of Judah

===== Definitions =====

  - Abijam

===== Usage =====

Abijam.
