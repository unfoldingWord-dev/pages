====== G4237: πρασιά (prasiá) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from πράσον (práson) (a leek, and so an onion-patch);

===== Meaning =====

a garden plot, i.e. (by implication, of regular beds) a row (repeated in plural by Hebraism, to indicate an arrangement)

===== Usage =====

in ranks.
