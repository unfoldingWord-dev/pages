====== H6520: פְּרָזוֹן (pᵉrâzôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6518|H6518]];

===== Meaning =====

**magistracy**, i.e. **leadership** (also concretely, chieftains)

===== Definitions =====

  - magistracy
  - leadership

===== Usage =====

village.
