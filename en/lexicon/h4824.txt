====== H4824: מֵרֹנֹתִי (Mêrônôthîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

patrial from an unused noun;

===== Meaning =====

a **Meronothite**, or inhabitant of some (otherwise unknown) Meronoth.

===== Definitions =====

  - Meronothite

===== Usage =====

Meronothite.
