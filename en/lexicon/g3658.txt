====== G3658: ὅμιλος (hómilos) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g3674|G3674]] and a derivative of the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g138|G138]]
 (meaning a crowd);

===== Meaning =====

association together, i.e. a multitude

===== Usage =====
