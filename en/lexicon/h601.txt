====== H601: אֲנָפָה (ʼănâphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h599|H599]];

===== Meaning =====

an **unclean bird**, perhaps the **parrot** (from its irascibility)

===== Definitions =====

  - unclean bird
  - parrot

===== Usage =====

heron.
