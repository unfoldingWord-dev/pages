====== H6440: פָּנִים (pânîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

plural (but always as singular) of an unused noun [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6437|H6437]]);

===== Meaning =====

the **face** (as the part that turns); used in a great variety of applications (literally and figuratively); also (with prepositional prefix) as a preposition (**before**, etc.)

===== Definitions =====

  - face
  - before

===== Usage =====
