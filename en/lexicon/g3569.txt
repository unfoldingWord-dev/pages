====== G3569: τανῦν (tanŷn) ======

===== Source =====

from neuter plural of [[en:lexicon:g3588|G3588]] and [[en:lexicon:g3568|G3568]];

===== Meaning =====

the things now, i.e. (adverbially) at present

===== Usage =====

(but) now.
