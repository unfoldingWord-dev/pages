====== G4873: συνανάκειμαι (synanákeimai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g345|G345]];

===== Meaning =====

to recline in company with (at a meal)

===== Usage =====

sit (down, at the table, together) with (at meat).
