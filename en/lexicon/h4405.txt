====== H4405: מִלָּה (millâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4448|H4448]] (plural masculine as if from [[en:lexicon:|]];

===== Meaning =====

a **word**; collectively, a **discourse**; figuratively, a **topic**

===== Definitions =====

  - word
  - discourse
  - topic

===== Usage =====

  answer, by-word, matter, any thing (what) to say, to speak(-ing), speak, talking, word.
