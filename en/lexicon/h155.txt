====== H155: אַדֶּרֶת (ʼaddereth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h117|H117]]; ; also the same as [[en:lexicon:h145|H145]]

===== Meaning =====

something **ample** (as a large vine, a wide dress)

===== Definitions =====

  - ample

===== Usage =====

garment, glory, goodly, mantle, robe.
