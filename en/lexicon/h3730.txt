====== H3730: כַּפְתֹּר (kaphtôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (Amos 9:1) [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably from an unused root meaning to **encircle**;

===== Meaning =====

a **chaplet**; but used only in an architectonic sense, i.e. the **capital** of a column, or a wreath-like **button** or **disk** on the candelabrum

===== Definitions =====

  - encircle
  - chaplet
  - capital
  - button
  - disk

===== Usage =====

knop, (upper) lintel.
