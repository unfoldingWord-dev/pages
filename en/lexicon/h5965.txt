====== H5965: עָלַס (ʻâlaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **leap for joy**, i.e. **exult**, **wave joyously**

===== Definitions =====

  - leap for joy
  - exult
  - wave joyously

===== Usage =====

× peacock, rejoice, solace self.
