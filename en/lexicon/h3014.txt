====== H3014: יָגָה (yâgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably rather the same as [[en:lexicon:h3013|H3013]] through the common idea of dissatisfaction);

===== Meaning =====

to **push away**

===== Definitions =====

  - push away

===== Usage =====

be removed.
