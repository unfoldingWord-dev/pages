====== G4784: συγκατατίθεμαι (synkatatíthemai) ======

===== Source =====

middle from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2698|G2698]];

===== Meaning =====

to deposit (one's vote or opinion) in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to accord with

===== Usage =====

consent.
