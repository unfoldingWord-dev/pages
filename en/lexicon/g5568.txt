====== G5568: ψαλμός (psalmós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5567|G5567]];

===== Meaning =====

a set piece of music, i.e. a sacred ode (accompanied with the voice, harp or other instrument; a "psalm"); collectively, the book of the Psalms

===== Usage =====

psalm.
