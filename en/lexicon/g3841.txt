====== G3841: παντοκράτωρ (pantokrátōr) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3956|G3956]] and [[en:lexicon:g2904|G2904]];

===== Meaning =====

the all-ruling, i.e. God (as absolute and universal sovereign)

===== Usage =====

Almighty, Omnipotent.
