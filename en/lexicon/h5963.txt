====== H5963: עַלְמֹן דִּבְלָתָיְמָה (ʻAlmôn Diblâthâyᵉmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5960|H5960]] and the dual of [[en:lexicon:h1960|H1960]] (compare [[en:lexicon:h1015|H1015]]) with enclitic of direction; Almon towards Diblathajim;

===== Meaning =====

**Almon-Diblathajemah**, a place in Moab

===== Definitions =====

  - Almon-Diblathajemah

===== Usage =====

Almondilathaim.
