====== H7641: שִׁבֹּל (shibbôl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from the same as [[en:lexicon:h7640|H7640]];

===== Meaning =====

a **stream** (as flowing); also an **ear of grain** (as growing out); by analogy, a **branch**

===== Definitions =====

  - stream
  - ear of grain
  - branch

===== Usage =====

branch, channel, ear (of corn), (water-)flood, Shibboleth. Compare
