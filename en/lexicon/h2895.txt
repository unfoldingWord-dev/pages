====== H2895: טוֹב (ṭôwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root,

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (transitively, **do** or **make**) **good** (or well) in the widest sense

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - do
  - make
  - good

===== Usage =====

be (do) better, cheer, be (do, seem) good, (make) goodly, × please, (be, do, go, play) well.
