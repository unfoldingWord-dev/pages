====== G4954: σύσσωμος (sýssōmos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g4983|G4983]];

===== Meaning =====

of a joint body, i.e. (figuratively) a fellow-member of the Christian community

===== Usage =====

of the same body.
