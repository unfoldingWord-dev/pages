====== H6640: צְבוּ (tsᵉbûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:lexicon:h6634|H6634]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **will**; concretely, an **affair** (as a matter of determination)

===== Definitions =====

  - will
  - affair

===== Usage =====

purpose.
