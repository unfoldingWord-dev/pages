====== H4482: מֵן (mên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **apportion**;

===== Meaning =====

a **part**; hence, a musical **chord** (as parted into strings)

===== Definitions =====

  - apportion
  - part
  - chord

===== Usage =====

in [the same] (Psalm 68:23), stringed instrument (Psalm 150:4), whereby (Psalm 45:8 [defective plural]).
