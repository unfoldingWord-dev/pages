====== H3256: יָסַר (yâçar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **chastise**, literally (with blows) or figuratively (with words); hence, to **instruct**

===== Definitions =====

  - chastise
  - instruct

===== Usage =====

bind, chasten, chastise, correct, instruct, punish, reform, reprove, sore, teach.
