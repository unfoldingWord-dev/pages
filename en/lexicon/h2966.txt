====== H2966: טְרֵפָה (ṭᵉrêphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine (collectively) of [[en:lexicon:h2964|H2964]];

===== Meaning =====

**prey**, i.e. flocks devoured by animals

===== Definitions =====

  - prey

===== Usage =====

ravin, (that which was) torn (of beasts, in pieces).
