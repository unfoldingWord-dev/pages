====== H454: אֶלְיְהוֹעֵינַי (ʼElyᵉhôwʻêynay) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h413|H413]] and [[en:lexicon:h3068|H3068]] and [[en:lexicon:h5869|H5869]]; towards Jehovah (are) my eyes;

===== Meaning =====

**Eljehoenai** or **Eljoenai**, the name of seven Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Eljehoenai
  - Eljoenai

===== Usage =====

Elihoenai, Elionai.
