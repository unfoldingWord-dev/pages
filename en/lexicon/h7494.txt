====== H7494: רַעַשׁ (raʻash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7493|H7493]];

===== Meaning =====

**vibration**, **bounding**, **uproar**

===== Definitions =====

  - vibration
  - bounding
  - uproar

===== Usage =====

commotion, confused noise, earthquake, fierceness, quaking, rattling, rushing, shaking.
