====== H6536: פָּרַס (pâraç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **break** in pieces, i.e. (usually without violence) to **split**, **distribute**

===== Definitions =====

  - break
  - split
  - distribute

===== Usage =====

deal, divide, have hoofs, part, tear.
