====== G5217: ὑπάγω (hypágō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to lead (oneself) under, i.e. withdraw or retire (as if sinking out of sight), literally or figuratively

===== Usage =====

depart, get hence, go (a-)way.
