====== H3815: לָאֵל (Lâʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the prepositional prefix and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; (belonging) **to God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Laël** an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - to God
  - Laël

===== Usage =====

Lael.
