====== H6963: קוֹל (qôwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to call aloud;

===== Meaning =====

a **voice** or **sound**

===== Definitions =====

  - voice
  - sound

===== Usage =====

  aloud, bleating, crackling, cry (  out), fame, lightness, lowing, noise,   hold peace, (pro-) claim, proclamation,   sing, sound,   spark, thunder(-ing), voice,   yell.
