====== H7292: רָהַב (râhab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **urge** severely, i.e. (figuratively) **importune**, **embolden**, **capture**, **act insolently**

===== Definitions =====

  - urge
  - importune
  - embolden
  - capture
  - act insolently

===== Usage =====

overcome, behave self proudly, make sure, strengthen.
