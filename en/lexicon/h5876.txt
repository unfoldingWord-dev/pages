====== H5876: עֵין חַדָּה (ʻÊyn Chaddâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5869|H5869]] and the feminine of a derivative from [[en:lexicon:h2300|H2300]]; **fountain of sharpness**;

===== Meaning =====

**En-Chaddah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - fountain of sharpness
  - En-Chaddah

===== Usage =====

En-haddah.
