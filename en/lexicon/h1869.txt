====== H1869: דָּרַךְ (dârak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tread**; by implication, to **walk**; also to **string** a bow (by treading on it in bending)

===== Definitions =====

  - tread
  - walk
  - string

===== Usage =====

archer, bend, come, draw, go (over), guide, lead (forth), thresh, tread (down), walk.
