====== H1420: גְּדוּלָה (gᵉdûwlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (shortened) or (less accurately) [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine of [[en:lexicon:h1419|H1419]];

===== Meaning =====

**greatness**; (concretely) **mighty acts**

===== Definitions =====

  - greatness
  - mighty acts

===== Usage =====

dignity, great things(-ness), majesty.
