====== H6205: עֲרָפֶל (ʻărâphel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:h6201|H6201]];

===== Meaning =====

**gloom** (as of a **lowering** sky)

===== Definitions =====

  - gloom
  - lowering

===== Usage =====

(gross, thick) dark (cloud, -ness).
