====== H3774: כְּרֵתִי (Kᵉrêthîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:h3772|H3772]] in the sense of executioner; (compare [[en:lexicon:h2876|H2876]]) (only collectively in the singular as plural)

===== Meaning =====

a **Kerethite** or **life-guardsman**

===== Definitions =====

  - Kerethite
  - life-guardsman

===== Usage =====

Cherethims, Cherethites.
