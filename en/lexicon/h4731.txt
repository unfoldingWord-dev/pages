====== H4731: מַקֵּל (maqqêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning apparently to germinate;

===== Meaning =====

a **shoot**, i.e. **stick** (with leaves on, or for walking, striking, guiding, divining)

===== Definitions =====

  - shoot
  - stick

===== Usage =====

rod, (hand-)staff.
