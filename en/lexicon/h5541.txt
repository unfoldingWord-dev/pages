====== H5541: סָלָה (çâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **hang** up, i.e. **weigh**, or (figuratively) **contemn**

===== Definitions =====

  - hang
  - weigh
  - contemn

===== Usage =====

tread down (under foot), value.
