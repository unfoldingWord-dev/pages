====== H4717: מַקָּבָה (maqqâbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5344|H5344]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **perforatrix**, i.e. a **hammer** (as **piercing**)

===== Definitions =====

  - perforatrix
  - hammer
  - piercing

===== Usage =====

hammer.
