====== G1685: ἐμβάλλω (embállō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g906|G906]];

===== Meaning =====

to throw on, i.e. (figuratively) subject to (eternal punishment)

===== Usage =====

cast into.
