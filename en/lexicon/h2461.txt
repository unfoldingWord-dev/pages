====== H2461: חָלָב (châlâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h2459|H2459]];

===== Meaning =====

**milk** (as the **richness** of kine)

===== Definitions =====

  - milk
  - richness

===== Usage =====

  cheese, milk, sucking.
