====== G1256: διαλέγομαι (dialégomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]];

===== Meaning =====

to say thoroughly, i.e. discuss (in argument or exhortation)

===== Usage =====

dispute, preach (unto), reason (with), speak.
