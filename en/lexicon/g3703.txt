====== G3703: ὀπώρα (opṓra) ======

===== Source =====

apparently from the base of [[en:lexicon:g3796|G3796]] and [[en:lexicon:g5610|G5610]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, even-tide of the (summer) season (dog-days), i.e. (by implication) ripe fruit

===== Usage =====

fruit.
