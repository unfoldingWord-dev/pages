====== H3573: כּוּשַׁן רִשְׁעָתַיִם (Kûwshan Rishʻâthayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h3572|H3572]] and the dual of [[en:lexicon:h7564|H7564]]; Cushan of double wickedness;

===== Meaning =====

**Cushan-Rishathajim**, a Mesopotamian king

===== Definitions =====

  - Cushan-Rishathajim

===== Usage =====

Chushan-rishathayim.
