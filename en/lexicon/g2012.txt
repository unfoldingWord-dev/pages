====== G2012: ἐπίτροπος (epítropos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g5158|G5158]] (in the sense of [[en:lexicon:g2011|G2011]]);

===== Meaning =====

a commissioner, i.e. domestic manager, guardian

===== Usage =====

steward, tutor.
