====== G4372: πρόσφατος (prósphatos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4969|G4969]];

===== Meaning =====

previously (recently) slain (fresh), i.e. (figuratively) lately made

===== Usage =====
