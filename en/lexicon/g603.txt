====== G603: ἀποκαραδοκία (apokaradokía) ======

===== Source =====

from a comparative of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and a compound of κάρα (kára) (the head) and [[en:lexicon:g1380|G1380]]
 (in the sense of watching);

===== Meaning =====

intense anticipation

===== Usage =====

earnest expectation.
