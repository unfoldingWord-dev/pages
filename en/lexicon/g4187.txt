====== G4187: πολυτρόπως (polytrópōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4183|G4183]] and [[en:lexicon:g5158|G5158]];

===== Meaning =====

in many ways, i.e. variously as to method or form

===== Usage =====

in divers manners.
