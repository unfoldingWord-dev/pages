====== G1345: δικαίωμα (dikaíōma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1344|G1344]];

===== Meaning =====

an equitable deed; by implication, a statute or decision

===== Usage =====

judgment, justification, ordinance, righteousness.
