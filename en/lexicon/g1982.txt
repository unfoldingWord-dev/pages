====== G1982: ἐπισκιάζω (episkiázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4639|G4639]];

===== Meaning =====

to cast a shade upon, i.e. (by analogy) to envelop in a haze of brilliancy; figuratively, to invest with preternatural influence

===== Usage =====

overshadow.
