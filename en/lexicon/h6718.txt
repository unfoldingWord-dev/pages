====== H6718: צַיִד (tsayid) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from a form of [[en:lexicon:h6679|H6679]] and meaning the same;

===== Meaning =====

the **chase**; also **game** (thus taken)

===== Definitions =====

  - chase
  - game

===== Usage =====

× catcheth, food, × hunter, (that which he took in) hunting, venison, victuals.
