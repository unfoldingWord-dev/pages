====== H6116: עֲצָרָה (ʻătsârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6113|H6113]];

===== Meaning =====

an **assembly**, especially on a **festival** or **holiday**

===== Definitions =====

  - assembly
  - festival
  - holiday

===== Usage =====

(solemn) assembly (meeting).
