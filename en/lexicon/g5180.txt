====== G5180: τύπτω (týptō) ======

===== Source =====

a primary verb (in a strengthened form);

===== Meaning =====

to "thump", i.e. cudgel or pummel (properly, with a stick or bastinado), but in any case by repeated blows; thus differing from

===== Usage =====

beat, smite, strike, wound.
