====== G2588: καρδία (kardía) ======

===== Source =====

prolonged from a primary κάρ (kár) (Latin cor, "heart");

===== Meaning =====

the heart, i.e. (figuratively) the thoughts or feelings (mind); also (by analogy) the middle

===== Usage =====

(+ broken-)heart(-ed).
