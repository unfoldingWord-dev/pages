====== G377: ἀναπίπτω (anapíptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g4098|G4098]];

===== Meaning =====

to fall back, i.e. lie down, lean back

===== Usage =====

lean, sit down (to meat).
