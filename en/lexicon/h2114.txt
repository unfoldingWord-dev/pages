====== H2114: זוּר (zûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **turn aside** (especially for lodging); hence to **be a foreigner**, **strange**, **profane**; specifically (active participle) to **commit adultery**

===== Definitions =====

  - turn aside
  - be a foreigner
  - strange
  - profane
  - commit adultery

===== Usage =====

(come from) another (man, place), fanner, go away, (e-) strange(-r, thing, woman).
