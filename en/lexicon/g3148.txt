====== G3148: μάστιξ (mástix) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the base of [[en:lexicon:g3145|G3145]] (through the idea of contact);

===== Meaning =====

a whip (literally, the Roman flagellum for criminals; figuratively, a disease)

===== Usage =====

plague, scourging.
