====== G96: ἀδόκιμος (adókimos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g1384|G1384]];

===== Meaning =====

unapproved, i.e. rejected; by implication, worthless (literally or morally)

===== Usage =====

castaway, rejected, reprobate.
