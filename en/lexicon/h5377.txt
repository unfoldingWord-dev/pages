====== H5377: נָשָׁא (nâshâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **lead astray**, i.e. (mentally) to **delude**, or (morally) to **seduce**

===== Definitions =====

  - lead astray
  - delude
  - seduce

===== Usage =====

beguile, deceive, × greatly, × utterly.
