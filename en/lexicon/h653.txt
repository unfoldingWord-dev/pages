====== H653: אֲפֵלָה (ʼăphêlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h651|H651]];

===== Meaning =====

**duskiness**, figuratively, **misfortune**; concrete, **concealment**

===== Definitions =====

  - duskiness
  - misfortune
  - concealment

===== Usage =====

dark, darkness, gloominess, × thick.
