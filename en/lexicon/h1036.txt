====== H1036: בֵּית לְעַפְרָה (Bêyth lᵉ-ʻAphrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1004|H1004]] and the feminine of [[en:lexicon:h6083|H6083]] (with preposition interposed); house to (i.e. of) dust;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-le-Aphrah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-le-Aphrah

===== Usage =====

house of Aphrah.
