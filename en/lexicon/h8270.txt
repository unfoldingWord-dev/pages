====== H8270: שֹׁר (shôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8324|H8324]]; (compare [[en:lexicon:h8306|H8306]])

===== Meaning =====

a **string** (as twisted ), i.e. (specifically) the **umbilical cord** (also figuratively, as the centre of strength)

===== Definitions =====

  - string
  - umbilical cord

===== Usage =====

navel.
