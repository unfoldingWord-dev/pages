====== G120: ἀθυμέω (athyméō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g2372|G2372]];

===== Meaning =====

to be spiritless, i.e. disheartened

===== Usage =====

be dismayed.
