====== G1370: διχοστασία (dichostasía) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1364|G1364]] and [[en:lexicon:g4714|G4714]];

===== Meaning =====

disunion, i.e. (figuratively) dissension

===== Usage =====

division, sedition.
