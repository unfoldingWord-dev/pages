====== G5299: ὑπωπιάζω (hypōpiázō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3700|G3700]];

===== Meaning =====

to hit under the eye (buffet or disable an antagonist as a pugilist), i.e. (figuratively) to tease or annoy (into compliance), subdue (one's passions)

===== Usage =====

keep under, weary.
