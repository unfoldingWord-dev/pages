====== H120: אָדָם (ʼâdâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h119|H119]];

===== Meaning =====

**ruddy** i.e. a **human being** (an individual or the species, **mankind**, etc.)

===== Definitions =====

  - ruddy
  - human being
  - mankind

===== Usage =====

× another,   hypocrite,   common sort, × low, man (mean, of low degree), person.
