====== H7275: רָגַם (râgam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h7263|H7263]], [[en:lexicon:h7321|H7321]], [[en:lexicon:h7551|H7551]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **cast together** (stones), i.e. to **lapidate**

===== Definitions =====

  - cast together
  - lapidate

===== Usage =====

× certainly, stone.
