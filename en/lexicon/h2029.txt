====== H2029: הָרָה (hârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (or **become**) **pregnant**, **conceive** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - become
  - pregnant
  - conceive

===== Usage =====

been, be with child, conceive, progenitor.
