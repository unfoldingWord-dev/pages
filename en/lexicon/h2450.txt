====== H2450: חָכָם (châkâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2449|H2449]];

===== Meaning =====

**wise**, (i.e. intelligent, skilful or artful)

===== Definitions =====

  - wise

===== Usage =====

cunning (man), subtil, (un-), wise((hearted), man).
