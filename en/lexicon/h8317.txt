====== H8317: שָׁרַץ (shârats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **wriggle**, i.e. (by implication) **swarm** or **abound**

===== Definitions =====

  - wriggle
  - swarm
  - abound

===== Usage =====

breed (bring forth, increase) abundantly (in abundance), creep, move.
