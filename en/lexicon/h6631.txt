====== H6631: צֶאֱצָא (tseʼĕtsâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3318|H3318]];

===== Meaning =====

**issue**, i.e. **produce**, **children**

===== Definitions =====

  - issue
  - produce
  - children

===== Usage =====

that which cometh forth (out), offspring.
