====== G5252: ὑπερφρονέω (hyperphronéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]] and [[en:lexicon:g5426|G5426]];

===== Meaning =====

to esteem oneself overmuch, i.e. be vain or arrogant

===== Usage =====

think more highly.
