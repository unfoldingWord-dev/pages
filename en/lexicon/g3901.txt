====== G3901: παραῤῥυέω (pararrhyéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g4482|G4482]];

===== Meaning =====

to flow by, i.e. (figuratively) carelessly pass (miss)

===== Usage =====

let slip.
