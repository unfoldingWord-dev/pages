====== H3591: כִּידוֹן (kîydôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h3589|H3589]]; (perhaps smaller than [[en:lexicon:h2595|H2595]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, something to **strike** with, i.e. a **dart**

===== Definitions =====

  - strike
  - dart

===== Usage =====

lance, shield, spear, target.
