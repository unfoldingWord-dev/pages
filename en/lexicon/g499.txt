====== G499: ἀντίτυπον (antítypon) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and [[en:lexicon:g5179|G5179]];

===== Meaning =====

corresponding ("antitype"), i.e. a representative, counterpart

===== Usage =====

(like) figure (whereunto).
