====== G1993: ἐπιστομίζω (epistomízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g4750|G4750]];

===== Meaning =====

to put something over the mouth, i.e. (figuratively) to silence

===== Usage =====

stop mouths.
