====== H4588: מָעוּף (mâʻûwph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5774|H5774]] in the sense of **covering** with shade (compare [[en:lexicon:h4155|H4155]]);

===== Meaning =====

**darkness**

===== Definitions =====

  - covering
  - darkness

===== Usage =====

dimness.
