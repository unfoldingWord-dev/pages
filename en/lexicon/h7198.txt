====== H7198: קֶשֶׁת (qesheth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7185|H7185]] in the original sense (of [[en:lexicon:h6983|H6983]]) of bending;

===== Meaning =====

a **bow**, for shooting (hence, figuratively, **strength**) or the **iris**

===== Definitions =====

  - bow
  - strength
  - iris

===== Usage =====

× arch(-er),   arrow, bow(-man, -shot).
