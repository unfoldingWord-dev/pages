====== H4341: מַכְאֹב (makʼôb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

sometimes [[en:lexicon:|]]; also (feminine Isaiah 53:3) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h3510|H3510]];

===== Meaning =====

**anguish** or (figuratively) **affliction**

===== Definitions =====

  - anguish
  - affliction

===== Usage =====

grief, pain, sorrow.
