====== H6630: צַאֲנָן (Tsaʼănân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6629|H6629]] used denominatively; **sheep pasture**;

===== Meaning =====

**Zaanan**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - sheep pasture
  - Zaanan

===== Usage =====

Zaanan.
