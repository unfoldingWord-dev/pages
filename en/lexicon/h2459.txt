====== H2459: חֶלֶב (cheleb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to be fat;

===== Meaning =====

**fat**, whether literally or figuratively; hence, the **richest** or **choice** part

===== Definitions =====

  - fat
  - richest
  - choice

===== Usage =====

× best, fat(-ness), × finest, grease, marrow.
