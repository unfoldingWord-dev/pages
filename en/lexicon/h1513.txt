====== H1513: גֶּחֶל (gechel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **glow** or **kindle**;

===== Meaning =====

an **ember**

===== Definitions =====

  - glow
  - kindle
  - ember

===== Usage =====

(burning) coal.
