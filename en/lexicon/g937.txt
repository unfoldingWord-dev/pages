====== G937: βασιλικός (basilikós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g935|G935]];

===== Meaning =====

regal (in relation), i.e. (literally) belonging to (or befitting) the sovereign (as land, dress, or a courtier), or (figuratively) preeminent

===== Usage =====

king's, nobleman, royal.
