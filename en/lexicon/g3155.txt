====== G3155: μάτην (mátēn) ======

===== Source =====

accusative case of a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3145|G3145]] (through the idea
 of tentative manipulation, i.e. unsuccessful search, or else of
 punishment);

===== Meaning =====

folly, i.e. (adverbially) to no purpose

===== Usage =====

in vain.
