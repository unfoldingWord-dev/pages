====== H249: אֶזְרָח (ʼezrâch) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2224|H2224]] (in the sense of **springing up**);

===== Meaning =====

a spontaneous **growth**, i.e. **native** (tree or persons)

===== Definitions =====

  - springing up
  - growth
  - native

===== Usage =====

bay tree, (home-) born (in the land), of the (one's own) country (nation).
