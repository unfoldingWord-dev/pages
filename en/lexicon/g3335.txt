====== G3335: μεταλαμβάνω (metalambánō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g2983|G2983]];

===== Meaning =====

to participate; genitive case, to accept (and use)

===== Usage =====

eat, have, be partaker, receive, take.
