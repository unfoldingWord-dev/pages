====== G5569: ψευδάδελφος (pseudádelphos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5571|G5571]] and [[en:lexicon:g80|G80]];

===== Meaning =====

a spurious brother, i.e. pretended associate

===== Usage =====

false brethren.
