====== G4920: συνίημι (syníēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and ἵημι (híēmi) (to send);

===== Meaning =====

to put together, i.e. (mentally) to comprehend; by implication, to act piously

===== Usage =====

 consider, understand, be wise.
