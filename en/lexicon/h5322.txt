====== H5322: נֵץ (nêts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5340|H5340]];

===== Meaning =====

a **flower** (from its **brilliancy**); also a **hawk** (from it **flashing** speed)

===== Definitions =====

  - flower
  - brilliancy
  - hawk
  - flashing

===== Usage =====

blossom, hawk.
