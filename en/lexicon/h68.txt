====== H68: אֶבֶן (ʼeben) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the root of [[en:lexicon:h1129|H1129]] through the meaning to **build**;

===== Meaning =====

a **stone**

===== Definitions =====

  - build
  - stone

===== Usage =====

  carbuncle,   mason,   plummet, (chalk-, hail-, head-, sling-) stone(-ny), (divers) weight(-s).
