====== H1239: בָּקַר (bâqar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **plough**, or (generally) **break** forth, i.e. (figuratively) to **inspect**, **admire**, **care for**, **consider**

===== Definitions =====

  - plough
  - break
  - inspect
  - admire
  - care for
  - consider

===== Usage =====

(make) inquire (-ry), (make) search, seek out.
