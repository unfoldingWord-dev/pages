====== H6264: עָתִיד (ʻâthîyd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6257|H6257]];

===== Meaning =====

**prepared**; by implication, **skilful**; feminine plural the **future**; also **treasure**

===== Definitions =====

  - prepared
  - skilful
  - future
  - treasure

===== Usage =====

things that shall come, ready, treasures.
