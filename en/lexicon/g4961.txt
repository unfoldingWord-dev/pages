====== G4961: συστρατιώτης (systratiṓtēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g4757|G4757]];

===== Meaning =====

a co-campaigner, i.e. (figuratively) an associate in Christian toil

===== Usage =====

fellowsoldier.
