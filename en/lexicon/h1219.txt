====== H1219: בָּצַר (bâtsar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to clip off; specifically (as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h1210|H1210]])

===== Meaning =====

to **gather grapes**; also to **be isolated** (i.e. inaccessible by height or fortification)

===== Definitions =====

  - gather grapes
  - be isolated

===== Usage =====

cut off, (de-) fenced, fortify, (grape) gather(-er), mighty things, restrain, strong, wall (up), withhold.
