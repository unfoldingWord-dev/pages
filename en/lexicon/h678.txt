====== H678: אָצִיל (ʼâtsîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h680|H680]] (in its secondary sense of **separation**);

===== Meaning =====

an **extremity** (Isaiah 41:9), also a **noble**

===== Definitions =====

  - separation
  - extremity
  - noble

===== Usage =====

chief man, noble.
