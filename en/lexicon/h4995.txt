====== H4995: נָא (nâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h5106|H5106]] in the sense of harshness from refusal;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **tough**, i.e. **uncooked** (flesh)

===== Definitions =====

  - tough
  - uncooked

===== Usage =====

raw.
