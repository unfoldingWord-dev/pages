====== H7467: רְעוּאֵל (Rᵉʻûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h7466|H7466]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; friend of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Reuel**, the name of Moses' father-in-law, also of an Edomite and an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Reuel

===== Usage =====

Raguel, Reuel.
