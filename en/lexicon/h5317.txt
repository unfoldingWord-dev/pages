====== H5317: נֹפֶת (nôpheth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5130|H5130]] in the sense of shaking to pieces;

===== Meaning =====

a **dripping** i.e. of honey (from the comb)

===== Definitions =====

  - dripping

===== Usage =====

honeycomb.
