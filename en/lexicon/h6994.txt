====== H6994: קָטֹן (qâṭôn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather denominative from [[en:lexicon:h6996|H6996]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **diminish**, i.e. **be** (causatively, **make**) **diminutive** or (figuratively) **of no account**

===== Definitions =====

  - diminish
  - be
  - make
  - diminutive
  - of no account

===== Usage =====

be a (make) small (thing), be not worthy.
