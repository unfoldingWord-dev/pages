====== H4928: מִשְׁמַעַת (mishmaʻath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4926|H4926]];

===== Meaning =====

**audience**, i.e. the royal **court**; also **obedience**, i.e. (concretely) a **subject**

===== Definitions =====

  - audience
  - court
  - obedience
  - subject

===== Usage =====

bidding, guard, obey.
