====== G2443: ἵνα (hína) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the same as the former part of [[en:lexicon:g1438|G1438]] (through the
 demonstrative idea; compare [[en:lexicon:g3588|G3588]]);

===== Meaning =====

in order that (denoting the purpose or the result)

===== Usage =====

albeit, because, to the intent (that), lest, so as, (so) that, (for) to.
