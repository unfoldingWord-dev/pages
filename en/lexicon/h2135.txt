====== H2135: זָכָה (zâkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2141|H2141]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **be translucent**; figuratively, to **be innocent**

===== Definitions =====

  - be translucent
  - be innocent

===== Usage =====

be (make) clean, cleanse, be clear, count pure.
