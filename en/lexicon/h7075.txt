====== H7075: קִנְיָן (qinyân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7069|H7069]];

===== Meaning =====

**creation**, i.e. (concretely) **creatures**; also **acquisition**, **purchase**, **wealth**

===== Definitions =====

  - creation
  - creatures
  - acquisition
  - purchase
  - wealth

===== Usage =====

getting, goods, × with money, riches, substance.
