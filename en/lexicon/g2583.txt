====== G2583: κανών (kanṓn) ======

===== Source =====

from κάνη (kánē) (a straight reed, i.e. rod);

===== Meaning =====

a rule ("canon"), i.e. (figuratively) a standard (of faith and practice); by implication, a boundary, i.e. (figuratively) a sphere (of activity)

===== Usage =====

line, rule.
