====== H5795: עֵז (ʻêz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5810|H5810]];

===== Meaning =====

a **she-goat** (as strong), but masculine in plural (which also is used elliptically for goat's hair)

===== Definitions =====

  - she-goat

===== Usage =====

(she) goat, kid.
