====== H1181: בַּעֲלֵי בָּמוֹת (Baʻălêy Bâmôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the plural of [[en:lexicon:h1168|H1168]] and the plural of [[en:lexicon:h1116|H1116]]; Baals of (the) heights;

===== Meaning =====

**Baale-Bamoth**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Baale-Bamoth

===== Usage =====

lords of the high places.
