====== H4686: מָצוּד (mâtsûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; for [[en:lexicon:h4685|H4685]];

===== Meaning =====

a **net**, or (abstractly) **capture**; also a **fastness**

===== Definitions =====

  - fastness

===== Usage =====

castle, defense, fort(-ress), (strong) hold, be hunted, net, snare, strong place.
