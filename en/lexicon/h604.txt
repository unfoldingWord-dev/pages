====== H604: אֲנָקָה (ʼănâqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h603|H603]];

===== Meaning =====

some kind of **lizard**, probably the **gecko** (from its wail)

===== Definitions =====

  - lizard
  - gecko

===== Usage =====

ferret.
