====== G3927: παρεπίδημος (parepídēmos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g1927|G1927]];

===== Meaning =====

an alien alongside, i.e. a resident foreigner

===== Usage =====

pilgrim, stranger.
