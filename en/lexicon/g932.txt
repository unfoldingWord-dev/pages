====== G932: βασιλεία (basileía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g935|G935]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, royalty, i.e. (abstractly) rule, or (concretely) a realm (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

kingdom, + reign.
