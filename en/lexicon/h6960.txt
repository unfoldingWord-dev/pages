====== H6960: קָוָה (qâvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **bind** together (perhaps by twisting), i.e. **collect**; (figuratively) to **expect**

===== Definitions =====

  - bind
  - collect
  - expect

===== Usage =====

gather (together), look, patiently, tarry, wait (for, on, upon).
