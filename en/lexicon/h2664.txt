====== H2664: חָפַשׂ (châphas) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **seek**; causatively, to **conceal oneself** (i.e. let be sought), or **mask**

===== Definitions =====

  - seek
  - conceal oneself
  - mask

===== Usage =====

change, (make) diligent (search), disquise self, hide, search (for, out).
