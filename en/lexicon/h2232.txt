====== H2232: זָרַע (zâraʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sow**; figuratively, to **disseminate**, **plant**, **fructify**

===== Definitions =====

  - sow
  - disseminate
  - plant
  - fructify

===== Usage =====

bear, conceive seed, set with sow(-er), yield.
