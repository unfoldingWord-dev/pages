====== G5570: ψευδαπόστολος (pseudapóstolos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5571|G5571]] and [[en:lexicon:g652|G652]];

===== Meaning =====

a spurious apostle, i.e. pretended pracher

===== Usage =====

false teacher.
