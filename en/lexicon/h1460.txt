====== H1460: גֵּו (gêv) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1342|H1342]] (corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h1354|H1354]]);

===== Meaning =====

the **back**; by analogy, the **middle**

===== Definitions =====

  - back
  - middle

===== Usage =====

  among, back, body.
