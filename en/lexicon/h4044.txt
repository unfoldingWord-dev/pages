====== H4044: מְגִנָּה (mᵉginnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4042|H4042]];

===== Meaning =====

a **covering** (in a bad sense), i.e. **blindness** or **obduracy**

===== Definitions =====

  - covering
  - blindness
  - obduracy

===== Usage =====

sorrow. See also
