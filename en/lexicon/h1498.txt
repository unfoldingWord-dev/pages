====== H1498: גָּזֵל (gâzêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1497|H1497]];

===== Meaning =====

**robbery**, or (concretely) **plunder**

===== Definitions =====

  - robbery
  - plunder

===== Usage =====

robbery, thing taken away by violence.
