====== H5619: סָקַל (çâqal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be weighty**; but used only in the sense of **lapidation** or its contrary (as if a **de**lapidation)

===== Definitions =====

  - be weighty
  - lapidation
  - de

===== Usage =====

(cast, gather out, throw) stone(-s), × surely.
