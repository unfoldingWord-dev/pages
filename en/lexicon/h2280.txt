====== H2280: חָבַשׁ (châbash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **wrap** firmly (especially a turban, compress, or **saddle**); figuratively, to **stop**, to **rule**

===== Definitions =====

  - wrap
  - saddle
  - stop
  - rule

===== Usage =====

bind (up), gird about, govern, healer, put, saddle, wrap about.
