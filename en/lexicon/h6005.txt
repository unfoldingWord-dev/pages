====== H6005: עִמָּנוּאֵל (ʻImmânûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5973|H5973]] and with a pronominal suffix inserted; **with us** (is) **God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Immanuel**, a type name of Isaiah's son

===== Definitions =====

  - with us
  - God
  - Immanuel

===== Usage =====

Immanuel.
