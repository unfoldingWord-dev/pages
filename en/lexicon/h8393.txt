====== H8393: תְּבוּאָה (tᵉbûwʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h935|H935]];

===== Meaning =====

**income**, i.e. **produce** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - income
  - produce

===== Usage =====

fruit, gain, increase, revenue.
