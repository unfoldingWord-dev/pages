====== H7827: שְׁחֵלֶת (shᵉchêleth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

apparently from the same as [[en:lexicon:h7826|H7826]] through some obscure idea, perhaps that of **peeling** off by concussion of sound;

===== Meaning =====

a **scale** or shell, i.e. the aromatic **mussel**.

===== Definitions =====

  - peeling
  - scale
  - mussel

===== Usage =====

onycha.
