====== H6679: צוּד (tsûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also denominative from [[en:lexicon:h6718|H6718]]

===== Meaning =====

to **lie** alongside (i.e. in wait); by implication, to **catch** an animal (figuratively, men); to **victual** (for a journey)

===== Definitions =====

  - lie
  - catch
  - victual

===== Usage =====

chase, hunt, sore, take (provision).
