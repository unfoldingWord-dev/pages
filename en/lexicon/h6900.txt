====== H6900: קְבוּרָה (qᵉbûwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h6912|H6912]];

===== Meaning =====

**sepulture**; (concretely) a **sepulchre**

===== Definitions =====

  - sepulture
  - sepulchre

===== Usage =====

burial, burying place, grave, sepulchre.
