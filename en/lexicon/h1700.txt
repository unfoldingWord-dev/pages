====== H1700: דִּבְרָה (dibrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h1697|H1697]];

===== Meaning =====

a **reason**, **suit** or **style**

===== Definitions =====

  - reason
  - suit
  - style

===== Usage =====

cause, end, estate, order, regard.
