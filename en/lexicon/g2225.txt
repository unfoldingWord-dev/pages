====== G2225: ζωογονέω (zōogonéō) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g2226|G2226]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1096|G1096]];

===== Meaning =====

to engender alive, i.e. (by analogy) to rescue (passively, be saved) from death

===== Usage =====

live, preserve.
