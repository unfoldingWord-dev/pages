====== H7146: קָרַחַת (qârachath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7139|H7139]];

===== Meaning =====

a **bald** spot (on the back of the head); figuratively, a **threadbare** spot (on the back side of the cloth)

===== Definitions =====

  - bald
  - threadbare

===== Usage =====

bald head, bare within.
