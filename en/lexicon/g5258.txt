====== G5258: ὕπνος (hýpnos) ======

===== Source =====

from an obsolete primary (perhaps akin to [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] through the idea of
 subsilience);

===== Meaning =====

sleep, i.e. (figuratively) spiritual torpor

===== Usage =====

sleep.
