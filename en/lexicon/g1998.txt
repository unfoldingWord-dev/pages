====== G1998: ἐπισυντρέχω (episyntréchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g4936|G4936]];

===== Meaning =====

to hasten together upon one place (or a particular occasion)

===== Usage =====

come running together.
