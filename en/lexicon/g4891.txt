====== G4891: συνεγείρω (synegeírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g1453|G1453]];

===== Meaning =====

to rouse (from death) in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to revivify (spirtually) in resemblance to

===== Usage =====

raise up together, rise with.
