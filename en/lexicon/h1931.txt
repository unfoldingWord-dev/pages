====== H1931: הוּא (hûwʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

personal pronoun

===== Source =====

of which the feminine (beyond the Pentateuch) is [[en:lexicon:|]]; **he** a primitive word, the third person pronoun singular;

===== Meaning =====

**he** (**she** or **it**); only expressed when emphatic or without a verb; also (intensively) **self**, or (especially with the article) the **same**; sometimes (as demonstrative) **this** or **that**; occasionally (instead of copula) **as** or **are**

===== Definitions =====

  - he
  - he
  - she
  - it
  - self
  - same
  - this
  - that
  - as
  - are

===== Usage =====

he, as for her, him(-self), it, the same, she (herself), such, that (...it), these, they, this, those, which (is), who.
