====== G5524: χορηγέω (chorēgéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5525|G5525]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a dance-leader, i.e. (generally) to furnish

===== Usage =====

give, minister.
