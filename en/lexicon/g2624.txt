====== G2624: κατακληροδοτέω (kataklērodotéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and a derivative of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2819|G2819]] and [[en:lexicon:g1325|G1325]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a giver of lots to each, i.e. (by implication) to apportion an estate

===== Usage =====

divide by lot.
