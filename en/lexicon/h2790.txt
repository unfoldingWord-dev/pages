====== H2790: חָרַשׁ (chârash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **scratch**, i.e. (by implication) to **engrave**, **plough**; hence (from the use of tools) to **fabricate** (of any material); figuratively, to **devise** (in a bad sense); hence (from the idea of secrecy) to **be silent**, to **let alone**; hence (by implication) to **be deaf** (as an accompaniment of dumbness)

===== Definitions =====

  - scratch
  - engrave
  - plough
  - fabricate
  - devise
  - be silent
  - let alone
  - be deaf

===== Usage =====

× altogether, cease, conceal, be deaf, devise, ear, graven, imagine, leave off speaking, hold peace, plow(-er, man), be quiet, rest, practise secretly, keep silence, be silent, speak not a word, be still, hold tongue, worker.
