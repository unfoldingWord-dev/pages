====== H5727: עָדַן (ʻâdan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be soft** or **pleasant**; figuratively and reflexively, to **live voluptuously**

===== Definitions =====

  - be soft
  - pleasant
  - live voluptuously

===== Usage =====

delight self.
