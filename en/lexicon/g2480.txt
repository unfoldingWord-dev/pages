====== G2480: ἰσχύω (ischýō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2479|G2479]];

===== Meaning =====

to have (or exercise) force (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

be able, avail, can do(-not), could, be good, might, prevail, be of strength, be whole, + much work.
