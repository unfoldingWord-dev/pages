====== G2607: καταγινώσκω (kataginṓskō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g1097|G1097]];

===== Meaning =====

to note against, i.e. find fault with

===== Usage =====

blame, condemn.
