====== H4546: מְסִלָּה (mᵉçillâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5549|H5549]];

===== Meaning =====

a **thoroughfare** (as **turnpiked**), literally or figuratively; specifically a **viaduct**, a **staircase**

===== Definitions =====

  - thoroughfare
  - turnpiked
  - viaduct
  - staircase

===== Usage =====

causeway, course, highway, path, terrace.
