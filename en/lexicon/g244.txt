====== G244: ἀλλοτριεπίσκοπος (allotriepískopos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g245|G245]] and [[en:lexicon:g1985|G1985]];

===== Meaning =====

overseeing others' affairs, i.e. a meddler (specially, in Gentile customs)

===== Usage =====

busybody in other men's matters.
