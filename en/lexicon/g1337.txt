====== G1337: διθάλασσος (dithálassos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1364|G1364]] and [[en:lexicon:g2281|G2281]];

===== Meaning =====

having two seas, i.e. a sound with a double outlet

===== Usage =====

where two seas meet.
