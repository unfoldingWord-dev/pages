====== H2588: חָנוּת (chânûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2583|H2583]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **vault** or **cell** (with an arch); by implication, a **prison**

===== Definitions =====

  - vault
  - cell
  - prison

===== Usage =====

cabin.
