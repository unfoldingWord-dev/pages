====== G4520: σαββατισμός (sabbatismós) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4521|G4521]];

===== Meaning =====

a "sabbatism", i.e. (figuratively) the repose of Christianity (as a type of heaven)

===== Usage =====

rest.
