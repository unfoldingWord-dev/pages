====== H2918: טִירָה (ṭîyrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of (an equivalent to) [[en:lexicon:h2905|H2905]];

===== Meaning =====

a **wall**; hence, a **fortress** or a **hamlet**

===== Definitions =====

  - wall
  - fortress
  - hamlet

===== Usage =====

(goodly) castle, habitation, palace, row.
