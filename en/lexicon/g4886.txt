====== G4886: σύνδεσμος (sýndesmos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g1199|G1199]];

===== Meaning =====

a joint tie, i.e. ligament, (figuratively) uniting principle, control

===== Usage =====

band, bond.
