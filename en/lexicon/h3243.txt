====== H3243: יָנַק (yânaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **suck**; causatively, to **give milk**

===== Definitions =====

  - suck
  - give milk

===== Usage =====

milch, nurse(-ing mother), (give, make to) suck(-ing child, -ling).
