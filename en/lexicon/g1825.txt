====== G1825: ἐξεγείρω (exegeírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g1453|G1453]];

===== Meaning =====

to rouse fully, i.e. (figuratively) to resuscitate (from death), release (from infliction)

===== Usage =====

raise up.
