====== G4773: συγγενής (syngenḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g1085|G1085]];

===== Meaning =====

a relative (by blood); by extension, a fellow countryman

===== Usage =====

cousin, kin(-sfolk, -sman).
