====== H6403: פָּלַט (pâlaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **slip** out, i.e. **escape**; causatively, to **deliver**

===== Definitions =====

  - slip
  - escape
  - deliver

===== Usage =====

calve, carry away safe, deliver, (cause to) escape.
