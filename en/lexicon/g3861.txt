====== G3861: παράδοξος (parádoxos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g1391|G1391]] (in the sense of seeming);

===== Meaning =====

contrary to expectation, i.e. extraordinary ("paradox")

===== Usage =====

strange.
