====== G86: ᾅδης (háidēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g1492|G1492]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, unseen, i.e. "Hades" or the place (state) of departed souls

===== Usage =====

grave, hell.
