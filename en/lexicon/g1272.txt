====== G1272: διανοίγω (dianoígō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g455|G455]];

===== Meaning =====

to open thoroughly, literally (as a first-born) or figuratively (to expound)

===== Usage =====

open.
