====== H4295: מַטָּה (maṭṭâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5786|H5786]] with directive enclitic appended;

===== Meaning =====

**downward**, **below** or **beneath**; often adverbially with or without prefixes

===== Definitions =====

  - downward
  - below
  - beneath

===== Usage =====

beneath, down(-ward), less, very low, under(-neath).
