====== H3925: לָמַד (lâmad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **goad**, i.e. (by implication) to **teach** (the rod being an Oriental **incentive**)

===== Definitions =====

  - goad
  - teach
  - incentive

===== Usage =====

(un-) accustomed, × diligently, expert, instruct, learn, skilful, teach(-er, -ing).
