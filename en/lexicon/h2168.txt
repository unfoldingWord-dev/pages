====== H2168: זָמַר (zâmar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2167|H2167]], [[en:lexicon:h5568|H5568]], [[en:lexicon:h6785|H6785]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **trim** (a vine)

===== Definitions =====

  - trim

===== Usage =====

prune.
