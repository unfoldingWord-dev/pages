====== H8050: שְׁמוּאֵל (Shᵉmûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h8085|H8085]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; heard of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Shemuël**, the name of three Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Shemuël

===== Usage =====

Samuel, Shemuel.
