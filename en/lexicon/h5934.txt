====== H5934: עָלוּם (ʻâlûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h5956|H5956]] in the denominative sense of [[en:lexicon:h5958|H5958]];

===== Meaning =====

(only in plural as abstract) **adolescence**; figuratively, **vigor**

===== Definitions =====

  - adolescence
  - vigor

===== Usage =====

youth.
