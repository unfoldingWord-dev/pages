====== H4347: מַכָּה (makkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (masculine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; (plural only) from [[en:lexicon:h5221|H5221]];

===== Meaning =====

a **blow** (in 2 Chronicles 2:10, of the flail); by implication, a **wound**; figuratively, **carnage**, also **pestilence**

===== Definitions =====

  - blow
  - wound
  - carnage
  - pestilence

===== Usage =====

beaten, blow, plague, slaughter, smote, × sore, stripe, stroke, wound(-ed).
