====== H7487: רַעֲנַן (raʻănan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h7488|H7488]];

===== Meaning =====

**green**, i.e. (figuratively) **prosperous**

===== Definitions =====

  - green
  - prosperous

===== Usage =====

flourishing.
