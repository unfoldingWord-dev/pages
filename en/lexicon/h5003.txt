====== H5003: נָאַף (nâʼaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **commit adultery**; figuratively, to **apostatize**

===== Definitions =====

  - commit adultery
  - apostatize

===== Usage =====

adulterer(-ess), commit(-ing) adultery, woman that breaketh wedlock.
