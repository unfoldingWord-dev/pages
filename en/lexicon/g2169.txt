====== G2169: εὐχαριστία (eucharistía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2170|G2170]];

===== Meaning =====

gratitude; actively, grateful language (to God, as an act of worship)

===== Usage =====

thankfulness, (giving of) thanks(-giving).
