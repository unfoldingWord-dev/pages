====== H1310: בָּשַׁל (bâshal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **boil** up; hence, to **be done** in cooking; figuratively to **ripen**

===== Definitions =====

  - boil
  - be done
  - ripen

===== Usage =====

bake, boil, bring forth, roast, seethe, sod (be sodden).
