====== G3969: Πατροβᾶς (Patrobâs) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps a contraction for Πατρόβιος (Patróbios) (a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3962|G3962]] and [[en:lexicon:g979|G979]]);

===== Meaning =====

father's life; Patrobas, a Christian

===== Usage =====

Patrobas.
