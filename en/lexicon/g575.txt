====== G575: ἀπό (apó) ======

===== Source =====

a primary particle;

===== Meaning =====

"off," i.e. away (from something near), in various senses (of place, time, or relation; literal or figurative)

===== Usage =====

(X here-)after, ago, at, because of, before, by (the space of), for(-th), from, in, (out) of, off, (up-)on(-ce), since, with.
