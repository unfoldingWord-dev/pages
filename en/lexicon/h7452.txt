====== H7452: רֵעַ (rêaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7321|H7321]];

===== Meaning =====

a **crash** (of thunder), **noise** (of war), **shout** (of joy)

===== Definitions =====

  - crash
  - noise
  - shout

===== Usage =====

× aloud, noise, shouted.
