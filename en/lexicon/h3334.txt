====== H3334: יָצַר (yâtsar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **press** (intransitive), i.e. **be narrow**; figuratively, **be in distress**

===== Definitions =====

  - press
  - be narrow
  - be in distress

===== Usage =====

be distressed, be narrow, be straitened (in straits), be vexed.
