====== H7322: רוּף (rûwph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **triturate** (in a mortar), i.e. (figuratively) to **agitate** (by concussion)

===== Definitions =====

  - triturate
  - agitate

===== Usage =====

tremble.
