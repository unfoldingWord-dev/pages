====== H7050: קֶלַע (qelaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7049|H7049]];

===== Meaning =====

a **sling**; also a (door) **screen** (as if slung across), or the **valve** (of the door) itself

===== Definitions =====

  - sling
  - screen
  - valve

===== Usage =====

hanging, leaf, sling.
