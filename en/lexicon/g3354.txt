====== G3354: μετρέω (metréō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3358|G3358]];

===== Meaning =====

to measure (i.e. ascertain in size by a fixed standard); by implication, to admeasure (i.e. allot by rule)

===== Usage =====

figuratively, to estimatemeasure, mete.
