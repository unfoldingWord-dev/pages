====== H5445: סָבַל (çâbal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **carry** (literally or figuratively), or (reflexively) **be burdensome**; specifically, to **be gravid**

===== Definitions =====

  - carry
  - be burdensome
  - be gravid

===== Usage =====

bear, be a burden, carry, strong to labour.
