====== G3752: ὅταν (hótan) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3753|G3753]] and [[en:lexicon:g302|G302]];

===== Meaning =====

whenever (implying hypothesis or more or less uncertainty); also causatively (conjunctionally) inasmuch as

===== Usage =====

as long (soon) as, that, + till, when(-soever), while.
