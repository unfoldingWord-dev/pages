====== H5646: עָב (ʻâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to cover; properly, equivalent to [[en:lexicon:h5645|H5645]]; but used only as an architectural term,

===== Meaning =====

an **architrave** (as shading the pillars)

===== Definitions =====

  - architrave

===== Usage =====

thick (beam, plant).
