====== G444: ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g435|G435]] and ὤψ (ṓps) (the countenance; from [[en:lexicon:g3700|G3700]]);

===== Meaning =====

man-faced, i.e. a human being

===== Usage =====
