====== H6734: צִיצִת (tsîytsith) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h6731|H6731]];

===== Meaning =====

a **floral** or **wing**-like projection, i.e. a **fore-lock** of hair, a **tassel**

===== Definitions =====

  - floral
  - wing
  - fore-lock
  - tassel

===== Usage =====

fringe, lock.
