====== G4079: πηδάλιον (pēdálion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a (presumed) derivative of πηδόν (pēdón) (the blade of an oar; from
 the same as [[en:lexicon:g3976|G3976]]);

===== Meaning =====

a "pedal", i.e. helm

===== Usage =====

rudder.
