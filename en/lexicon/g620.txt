====== G620: ἀπολείπω (apoleípō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g3007|G3007]];

===== Meaning =====

to leave behind (passively, remain); by implication, to forsake

===== Usage =====

leave, remain.
