====== H1891: הָבַל (hâbal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be vain** in act, word, or expectation; specifically to **lead astray**

===== Definitions =====

  - be vain
  - lead astray

===== Usage =====

be (become, make) vain.
