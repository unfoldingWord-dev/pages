====== H7626: שֵׁבֶט (shêbeṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to **branch** off;

===== Meaning =====

a **scion**, i.e. (literally) a **stick** (for punishing, writing, fighting, ruling, walking, etc.) or (figuratively) a **clan**

===== Definitions =====

  - branch
  - scion
  - stick
  - clan

===== Usage =====

× correction, dart, rod, sceptre, staff, tribe.
