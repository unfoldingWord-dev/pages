====== G313: ἀναγεννάω (anagennáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g1080|G1080]];

===== Meaning =====

to beget or (by extension) bear (again)

===== Usage =====

beget, (bear) X (again).
