====== G4205: πόρνος (pórnos) ======

===== Source =====

from πέρνημι (pérnēmi) (to sell; akin to the base of [[en:lexicon:g4097|G4097]]);

===== Meaning =====

a (male) prostitute (as venal), i.e. (by analogy) a debauchee (libertine)

===== Usage =====

fornicator, whoremonger.
