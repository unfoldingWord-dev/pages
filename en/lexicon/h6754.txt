====== H6754: צֶלֶם (tselem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to shade;

===== Meaning =====

a **phantom**, i.e. (figuratively) **illusion**, **resemblance**; hence, a **representative figure**, especially an **idol**

===== Definitions =====

  - phantom
  - illusion
  - resemblance
  - representative figure
  - idol

===== Usage =====

image, vain shew.
