====== G692: ἀργός (argós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g2041|G2041]];

===== Meaning =====

inactive, i.e. unemployed; (by implication) lazy, useless

===== Usage =====

barren, idle, slow.
