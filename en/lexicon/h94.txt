====== H94: אָגוּר (ʼÂgûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h103|H103]]; gathered (i.e. received among the sages);

===== Meaning =====

**Agur**, a fanciful name for Solomon

===== Definitions =====

  - Agur

===== Usage =====

Agur.
