====== G1422: δύσκολος (dýskolos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1418|G1418]] and κόλον (kólon) (food);

===== Meaning =====

properly, fastidious about eating (peevish), i.e. (genitive case) impracticable

===== Usage =====

hard.
