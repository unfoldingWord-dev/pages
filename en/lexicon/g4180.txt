====== G4180: πολυλογία (polylogía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4183|G4183]] and [[en:lexicon:g3056|G3056]];

===== Meaning =====

loquacity, i.e. prolixity

===== Usage =====
