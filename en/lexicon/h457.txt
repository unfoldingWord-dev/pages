====== H457: אֱלִיל (ʼĕlîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h408|H408]];

===== Meaning =====

good for **nothing**, by analogy **vain** or **vanity**; specifically an **idol**

===== Definitions =====

  - nothing
  - vain
  - vanity
  - idol

===== Usage =====

idol, no value, thing of nought.
