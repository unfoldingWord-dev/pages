====== G4949: Συροφοίνισσα (Syrophoínissa) ======

===== Source =====

feminine of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4948|G4948]] and the same as [[en:lexicon:g5403|G5403]];

===== Meaning =====

a Syro-phœnician woman, i.e. a female native of Phœnicia in Syria

===== Usage =====

Syrophenician.
