====== G3792: ὀχλοποιέω (ochlopoiéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3793|G3793]] and [[en:lexicon:g4160|G4160]];

===== Meaning =====

to make a crowd, i.e. raise a public disturbance

===== Usage =====

gather a company.
