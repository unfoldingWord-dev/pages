====== G1723: ἐναγκαλίζομαι (enankalízomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g43|G43]];

===== Meaning =====

to take in one's arms, i.e. embrace

===== Usage =====

take up in arms.
