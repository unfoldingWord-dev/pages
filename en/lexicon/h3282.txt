====== H3282: יַעַן (yaʻan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

conjunction

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to pay attention;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **heed**; by implication, **purpose** (sake or account); used adverbially to indicate the **reason** or **cause**

===== Definitions =====

  - heed
  - purpose
  - reason
  - cause

===== Usage =====

because (that), forasmuch (  as), seeing then,   that,   whereas,   why.
