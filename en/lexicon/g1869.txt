====== G1869: ἐπαίρω (epaírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g142|G142]];

===== Meaning =====

to raise up (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

exalt self, poise (lift, take) up.
