====== H2782: חָרַץ (chârats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **point** sharply, i.e. (literally) to **wound**; figuratively, to **be alert**, to **decide**

===== Definitions =====

  - point
  - wound
  - be alert
  - decide

===== Usage =====

bestir self, decide, decree, determine, maim, move.
