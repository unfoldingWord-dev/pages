====== H8641: תְּרוּמָה (tᵉrûwmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Deuteronomy 12:11), from [[en:lexicon:h7311|H7311]];

===== Meaning =====

a **present** (as offered **up**), especially in **sacrifice** or as **tribute**

===== Definitions =====

  - present
  - up
  - sacrifice
  - tribute

===== Usage =====

gift, heave offering (shoulder), oblation, offered(-ing).
