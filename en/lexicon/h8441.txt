====== H8441: תּוֹעֵבַה (tôwʻêbah) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine active participle of [[en:lexicon:h8581|H8581]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **disgusting** (morally), i.e. (as noun) an **abhorrence**; especially **idolatry** or (concretely) an **idol**

===== Definitions =====

  - disgusting
  - abhorrence
  - idolatry
  - idol

===== Usage =====

abominable (custom, thing), abomination.
