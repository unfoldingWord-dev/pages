====== G2991: λαξευτός (laxeutós) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of λᾶς (lâs) (a stone) and the base of [[en:lexicon:g3584|G3584]] (in its original
 sense of scraping);

===== Meaning =====

rock-quarried

===== Usage =====

hewn in stone.
