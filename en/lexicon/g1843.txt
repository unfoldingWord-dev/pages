====== G1843: ἐξομολογέω (exomologéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g3670|G3670]];

===== Meaning =====

to acknowledge or (by implication, of assent) agree fully

===== Usage =====

confess, profess, promise.
