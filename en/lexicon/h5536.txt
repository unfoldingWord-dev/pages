====== H5536: סַל (çal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5549|H5549]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **willow twig** (as pendulous), i.e. an **osier**; but only as **woven** into a basket

===== Definitions =====

  - willow twig
  - osier
  - woven

===== Usage =====

basket.
