====== H2308: חָדַל (châdal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be flabby**, i.e. (by implication) **desist**; (figuratively) **be lacking** or **idle**

===== Definitions =====

  - be flabby
  - desist
  - be lacking
  - idle

===== Usage =====

cease, end, fall, forbear, forsake, leave (off), let alone, rest, be unoccupied, want.
