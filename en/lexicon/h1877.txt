====== H1877: דֶּשֶׁא (desheʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1876|H1876]];

===== Meaning =====

a **sprout**; by analogy, **grass**

===== Definitions =====

  - sprout
  - grass

===== Usage =====

(tender) grass, green, (tender) herb.
