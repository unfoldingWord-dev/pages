====== H398: אָכַל (ʼâkal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **eat** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - eat

===== Usage =====

× at all, burn up, consume, devour(-er, up), dine, eat(-er, up), feed (with), food, × freely, × in...wise(-deed, plenty), (lay) meat, × quite.
