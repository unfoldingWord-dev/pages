====== G3903: παρασκευάζω (paraskeuázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4632|G4632]];

===== Meaning =====

to furnish aside, i.e. get ready

===== Usage =====

prepare self, be (make) ready.
