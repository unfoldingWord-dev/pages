====== G4028: περικαλύπτω (perikalýptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2572|G2572]];

===== Meaning =====

to cover all around, i.e. entirely (the face, a surface)

===== Usage =====

blindfold, cover, overlay.
