====== H5526: סָכַךְ (çâkak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Exodus 33:22), a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **entwine** as a screen; by implication, to **fence** in, **cover** over, (figuratively) **protect**

===== Definitions =====

  - entwine
  - fence
  - cover
  - protect

===== Usage =====

cover, defence, defend, hedge in, join together, set, shut up.
