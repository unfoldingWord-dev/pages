====== G333: ἀναθεωρέω (anatheōréō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g2334|G2334]];

===== Meaning =====

to look again (i.e. attentively) at (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

behold, consider.
