====== H1524: גִּיל (gîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1523|H1523]];

===== Meaning =====

a **revolution** (of time, i.e. an age); also **joy**

===== Definitions =====

  - revolution
  - joy

===== Usage =====

× exceedingly, gladness, × greatly, joy, rejoice(-ing), sort.
