====== H6927: קַדְמָה (qadmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6923|H6923]];

===== Meaning =====

**priority** (in time); also used adverbially (**before**)

===== Definitions =====

  - priority
  - before

===== Usage =====

afore, antiquity, former (old) estate.
