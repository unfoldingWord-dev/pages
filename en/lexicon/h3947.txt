====== H3947: לָקַח (lâqach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **take** (in the widest variety of applications)

===== Definitions =====

  - take

===== Usage =====

accept, bring, buy, carry away, drawn, fetch, get, infold, × many, mingle, place, receive(-ing), reserve, seize, send for, take (away, -ing, up), use, win.
