====== H8638: תִּרְגַּם (tirgam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7275|H7275]] in the sense of throwing over;

===== Meaning =====

to **transfer**, i.e. **translate**

===== Definitions =====

  - transfer
  - translate

===== Usage =====

interpret.
