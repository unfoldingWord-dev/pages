====== G5578: ψευδοπροφήτης (pseudoprophḗtēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5571|G5571]] and [[en:lexicon:g4396|G4396]];

===== Meaning =====

a spurious prophet, i.e. pretended foreteller or religious impostor

===== Usage =====

false prophet.
