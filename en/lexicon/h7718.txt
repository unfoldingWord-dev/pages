====== H7718: שֹׁהַם (shôham) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably mean to blanch;

===== Meaning =====

a **gem**, probably the **beryl** (from its pale green color)

===== Definitions =====

  - gem
  - beryl

===== Usage =====

onyx.
