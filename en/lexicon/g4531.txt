====== G4531: σαλεύω (saleúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4535|G4535]];

===== Meaning =====

to waver, i.e. agitate, rock, topple or (by implication) destroy; figuratively, to disturb, incite

===== Usage =====

move, shake (together), which can(-not) be shaken, stir up.
