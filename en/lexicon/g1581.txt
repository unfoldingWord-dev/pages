====== G1581: ἐκκόπτω (ekkóptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g2875|G2875]];

===== Meaning =====

to exscind; figuratively, to frustrate

===== Usage =====

cut down (off, out), hew down, hinder.
