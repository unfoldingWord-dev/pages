====== H3365: יָקַר (yâqar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, apparently, to **be heavy**, i.e. (figuratively) **valuable**; causatively, to **make rare** (figuratively, to **inhibit**)

===== Definitions =====

  - be heavy
  - valuable
  - make rare
  - inhibit

===== Usage =====

be (make) precious, be prized, be set by, withdraw.
