====== H7646: שָׂבַע (sâbaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sate**, i.e. **fill** to satisfaction (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - sate
  - fill

===== Usage =====

have enough, fill (full, self, with), be (to the) full (of), have plenty of, be satiate, satisfy (with), suffice, be weary of.
