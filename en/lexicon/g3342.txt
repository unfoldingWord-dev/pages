====== G3342: μεταξύ (metaxý) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and a form of [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]];

===== Meaning =====

betwixt (of place or person); (of time) as adjective, intervening, or (by implication) adjoining

===== Usage =====

between, mean while, next.
