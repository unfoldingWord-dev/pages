====== G4215: ποταμός (potamós) ======

===== Source =====

probably from a derivative of the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g4095|G4095]] (compare [[en:lexicon:g4224|G4224]]);

===== Meaning =====

a current, brook or freshet (as drinkable), i.e. running water

===== Usage =====

flood, river, stream, water.
