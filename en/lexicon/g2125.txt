====== G2125: εὐλαβέομαι (eulabéomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g2126|G2126]];

===== Meaning =====

to be circumspect, i.e. (by implication) to be apprehensive; religiously, to reverence

===== Usage =====

(moved with) fear.
