====== G5082: τηλικοῦτος (tēlikoûtos) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3588|G3588]] with [[en:lexicon:g2245|G2245]] and [[en:lexicon:g3778|G3778]];

===== Meaning =====

such as this, i.e. (in (figurative) magnitude) so vast

===== Usage =====

so great, so mighty.
