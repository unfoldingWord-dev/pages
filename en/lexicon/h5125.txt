====== H5125: נוּן (nûwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **resprout**, i.e. **propagate by shoots**; figuratively, to **be perpetual**

===== Definitions =====

  - resprout
  - propagate by shoots
  - be perpetual

===== Usage =====

be continued.
