====== G3716: ὀρθοποδέω (orthopodéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3717|G3717]] and [[en:lexicon:g4228|G4228]];

===== Meaning =====

to be straight-footed, i.e. (figuratively) to go directly forward

===== Usage =====

walk uprightly.
