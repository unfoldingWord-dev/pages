====== G3900: παράπτωμα (paráptōma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3895|G3895]];

===== Meaning =====

a side-slip (lapse or deviation), i.e. (unintentional) error or (wilful) transgression

===== Usage =====

fall, fault, offence, sin, trespass.
