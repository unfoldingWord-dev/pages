====== H775: אַרְפַּכְשַׁד (ʼArpakshad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably of foreign origin;

===== Meaning =====

**Arpakshad**, a son of Noah; also the region settled by him
