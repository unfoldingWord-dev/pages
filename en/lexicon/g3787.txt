====== G3787: ὀφθαλμοδουλεία (ophthalmodouleía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3788|G3788]] and [[en:lexicon:g1397|G1397]];

===== Meaning =====

sight-labor, i.e. that needs watching (remissness)

===== Usage =====
