====== H3819: לֹא רֻחָמָה (Lôʼ Ruchâmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3808|H3808]] and [[en:lexicon:h7355|H7355]]; **not pitied**;

===== Meaning =====

**Lo-Ruchamah**, the symbolic name of a son of Hosea

===== Definitions =====

  - not pitied
  - Lo-Ruchamah

===== Usage =====

Lo-ruhamah.
