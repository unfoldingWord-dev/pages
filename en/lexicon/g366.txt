====== G366: ἀνανήφω (ananḗphō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g3525|G3525]];

===== Meaning =====

to become sober again, i.e. (figuratively) regain (one's) senses

===== Usage =====

recover self.
