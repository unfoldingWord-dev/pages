====== H2504: חָלָץ (châlâts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2502|H2502]] (in the sense of **strength**); only in the dual;

===== Meaning =====

the **loins** (as the seat of vigor)

===== Definitions =====

  - strength
  - loins

===== Usage =====

loins, reins.
