====== H1690: דְּבֵלָה (dᵉbêlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (akin to [[en:lexicon:h2082|H2082]]) probably meaning to **press** together;

===== Meaning =====

a **cake** of pressed figs

===== Definitions =====

  - press
  - cake

===== Usage =====

cake (lump) of figs.
