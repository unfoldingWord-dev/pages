====== G1917: ἐπιβουλή (epiboulḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g1014|G1014]];

===== Meaning =====

a plan against someone, i.e. a plot

===== Usage =====

laying (lying) in wait.
