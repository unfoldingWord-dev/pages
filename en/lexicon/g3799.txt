====== G3799: ὄψις (ópsis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3700|G3700]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, sight (the act), i.e. (by implication) the visage, an external show

===== Usage =====

appearance, countenance, face.
