====== H2844: חַת (chath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2865|H2865]];

===== Meaning =====

concretely, **crushed**; also **afraid**; abstractly, **terror**

===== Definitions =====

  - crushed
  - afraid
  - terror

===== Usage =====

broken, dismayed, dread, fear.
