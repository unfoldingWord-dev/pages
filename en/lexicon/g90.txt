====== G90: ἀδιαφθορία (adiaphthoría) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a
 derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1311|G1311]];

===== Meaning =====

incorruptibleness, i.e. (figuratively) purity (of doctrine)

===== Usage =====

uncorruptness.
