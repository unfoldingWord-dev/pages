====== G4686: σπεῖρα (speîra) ======

===== Source =====

of immediate Latin origin, but ultimately a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g138|G138]] in the
 sense of its cognate [[en:lexicon:g1507|G1507]];

===== Meaning =====

a coil (spira, "spire"), i.e. (figuratively) a mass of men (a Roman military cohort; also (by analogy) a squad of Levitical janitors)

===== Usage =====

band.
