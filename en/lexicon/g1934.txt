====== G1934: ἐπιζητέω (epizētéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g2212|G2212]];

===== Meaning =====

to search (inquire) for; intensively, to demand, to crave

===== Usage =====

desire, enquire, seek (after, for).
