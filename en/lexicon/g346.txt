====== G346: ἀνακεφαλαίομαι (anakephalaíomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g2775|G2775]] (in its original sense);

===== Meaning =====

to sum up

===== Usage =====

briefly comprehend, gather together in one.
