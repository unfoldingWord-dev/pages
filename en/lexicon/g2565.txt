====== G2565: καλλιέλαιος (kalliélaios) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g2566|G2566]] and [[en:lexicon:g1636|G1636]];

===== Meaning =====

a cultivated olive tree, i.e. a domesticated or improved one

===== Usage =====

good olive tree.
