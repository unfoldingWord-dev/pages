====== H2689: חֲצֹצְרָה (chătsôtsᵉrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

by reduplication from [[en:lexicon:h2690|H2690]];

===== Meaning =====

a **trumpet** (from its **sundered** or quavering note)

===== Definitions =====

  - trumpet
  - sundered

===== Usage =====

trumpet(-er).
