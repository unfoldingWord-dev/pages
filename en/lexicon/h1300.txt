====== H1300: בָּרָק (bârâq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1299|H1299]];

===== Meaning =====

**lightning**; by analogy, a **gleam**; concretely, a **flashing sword**

===== Definitions =====

  - lightning
  - gleam
  - flashing sword

===== Usage =====

bright, glitter(-ing sword), lightning.
