====== G4020: περιεργάζομαι (periergázomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2038|G2038]];

===== Meaning =====

to work all around, i.e. bustle about (meddle)

===== Usage =====

be a busybody.
