====== H4560: מָסַר (mâçar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sunder**, i.e. (transitively) **set apart**, or (reflex.) **apostatize**

===== Definitions =====

  - sunder
  - set apart
  - apostatize

===== Usage =====

commit, deliver.
