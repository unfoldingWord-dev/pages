====== G1622: ἐκτός (ektós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]];

===== Meaning =====

the exterior; figuratively (as a preposition) aside from, besides

===== Usage =====

but, except(-ed), other than, out of, outside, unless, without.
