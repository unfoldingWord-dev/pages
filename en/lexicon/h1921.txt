====== H1921: הָדַר (hâdar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **swell** up (literally or figuratively, active or passive); by implication, to **favor** or **honour**, **be high** or **proud**

===== Definitions =====

  - swell
  - favor
  - honour
  - be high
  - proud

===== Usage =====

countenance, crooked place, glorious, honour, put forth.
