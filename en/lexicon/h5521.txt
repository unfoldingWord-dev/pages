====== H5521: סֻכָּה (çukkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h5520|H5520]];

===== Meaning =====

a **hut** or **lair**

===== Definitions =====

  - hut
  - lair

===== Usage =====

booth, cottage, covert, pavilion, tabernacle, tent.
