====== H8447: תּוֹר (tôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h8446|H8446]];

===== Meaning =====

a **succession**, i.e. a **string** or (abstractly) **order**

===== Definitions =====

  - succession
  - string
  - order

===== Usage =====

border, row, turn.
