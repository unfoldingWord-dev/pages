====== G1266: διαμερίζω (diamerízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g3307|G3307]];

===== Meaning =====

to partition thoroughly (literally in distribution, figuratively in dissension)

===== Usage =====

cloven, divide, part.
