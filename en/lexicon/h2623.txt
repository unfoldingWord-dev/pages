====== H2623: חָסִיד (châçîyd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2616|H2616]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **kind**, i.e. (religiously) **pious** (a saint)

===== Definitions =====

  - kind
  - pious

===== Usage =====

godly (man), good, holy (one), merciful, saint, (un-) godly.
