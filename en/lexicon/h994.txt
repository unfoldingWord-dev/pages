====== H994: בִּי (bîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

particle

===== Source =====

perhaps from [[en:lexicon:h1158|H1158]] (in the sense of asking); properly, a request; used only adverbially (always with 'my Lord');

===== Meaning =====

**oh that!**; **with leave**, or **if it please**

===== Definitions =====

  - oh that!
  - with leave
  - if it please

===== Usage =====

alas, O, oh.
