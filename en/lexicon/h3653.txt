====== H3653: כֵּן (kên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h3651|H3651]], used as a noun;

===== Meaning =====

a **stand**, i.e. **pedestal** or **station**

===== Definitions =====

  - stand
  - pedestal
  - station

===== Usage =====

base, estate, foot, office, place, well.
