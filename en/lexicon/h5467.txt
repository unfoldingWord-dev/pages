====== H5467: סְדֹם (Çᵉdôm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to scorch; burnt (i.e. volcanic or bituminous) district;

===== Meaning =====

**Sedom**, a place near the Dead Sea

===== Definitions =====

  - Sedom

===== Usage =====

Sodom.
