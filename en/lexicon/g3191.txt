====== G3191: μελετάω (meletáō) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3199|G3199]];

===== Meaning =====

to take care of, i.e. (by implication) revolve in the mind

===== Usage =====

imagine, (pre-)meditate.
