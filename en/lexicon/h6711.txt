====== H6711: צָחַק (tsâchaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **laugh outright** (in merriment or scorn); by implication, to **sport**

===== Definitions =====

  - laugh outright
  - sport

===== Usage =====

laugh, mock, play, make sport.
