====== H1226: בַּצֹּרֶת (batstsôreth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine intensive from [[en:lexicon:h1219|H1219]];

===== Meaning =====

**restraint** (of rain), i.e. **drought**

===== Definitions =====

  - restraint
  - drought

===== Usage =====

dearth, drought.
