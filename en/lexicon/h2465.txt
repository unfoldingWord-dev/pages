====== H2465: חֶלֶד (cheled) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to glide swiftly;

===== Meaning =====

**life** (as a fleeting portion of time); hence, the **world** (as transient)

===== Definitions =====

  - life
  - world

===== Usage =====

age, short time, world.
