====== H4429: מֶלֶךְ (Melek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h4428|H4428]]; **king**;

===== Meaning =====

**Melek**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - king
  - Melek

===== Usage =====

Melech, Hammelech (by including the article).
