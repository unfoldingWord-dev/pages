====== H646: אֵפוֹד (ʼêphôwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

rarely [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably of foreign derivation

===== Meaning =====

a **girdle**; specifically the **ephod** or highpriest's shoulder-piece; also generally, an **image**

===== Definitions =====

  - girdle
  - ephod
  - image

===== Usage =====

ephod.
