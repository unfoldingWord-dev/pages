====== H1234: בָּקַע (bâqaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cleave**; generally, to **rend**, **break**, **rip** or **open**

===== Definitions =====

  - cleave
  - rend
  - break
  - rip
  - open

===== Usage =====

make a breach, break forth (into, out, in pieces, through, up), be ready to burst, cleave (asunder), cut out, divide, hatch, rend (asunder), rip up, tear, win.
