====== G3650: ὅλος (hólos) ======

===== Source =====

a primary word;

===== Meaning =====

"whole" or "all", i.e. complete (in extent, amount, time or degree), especially (neuter) as noun or adverb

===== Usage =====

all, altogether, every whit, + throughout, whole.
