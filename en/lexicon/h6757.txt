====== H6757: צַלְמָוֶת (tsalmâveth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6738|H6738]] and [[en:lexicon:h4194|H4194]];

===== Meaning =====

**shade of death**, i.e. the **grave** (figuratively, **calamity**)

===== Definitions =====

  - shade of death
  - grave
  - calamity

===== Usage =====

shadow of death.
