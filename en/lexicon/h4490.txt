====== H4490: מָנָה (mânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4487|H4487]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **weighed** out, i.e. (generally) a **division**; specifically (of food) a **ration**; also a **lot**

===== Definitions =====

  - weighed
  - division
  - ration
  - lot

===== Usage =====

such things as belonged, part, portion.
