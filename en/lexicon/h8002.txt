====== H8002: שֶׁלֶם (shelem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7999|H7999]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **requital**, i.e. a (voluntary) sacrifice in **thanks**

===== Definitions =====

  - requital
  - thanks

===== Usage =====

peace offering.
