====== G1215: δημηγορέω (dēmēgoréō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1218|G1218]] and [[en:lexicon:g58|G58]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a people-gatherer, i.e. to address a public assembly

===== Usage =====

make an oration.
