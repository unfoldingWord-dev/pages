====== H7412: רְמָה (rᵉmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h7411|H7411]];

===== Meaning =====

to **throw**, **set**, (figuratively) **assess**

===== Definitions =====

  - throw
  - set
  - assess

===== Usage =====

cast (down), impose.
