====== H6302: פָּדוּי (pâdûwy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h6299|H6299]]. **ransomed** (and so occurring under [[en:lexicon:h6299|H6299]]);

===== Meaning =====

as abstractly (in plural masculine) a **ransom**

===== Definitions =====

  - ransomed
  - ransom

===== Usage =====

(that are) to be (that were) redeemed.
