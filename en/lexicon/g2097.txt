====== G2097: εὐαγγελίζω (euangelízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and [[en:lexicon:g32|G32]];

===== Meaning =====

to announce good news ("evangelize") especially the gospel

===== Usage =====

declare, bring (declare, show) glad (good) tidings, preach (the gospel).
