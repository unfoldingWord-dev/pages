====== H4760: מֻרְאָה (murʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

apparently feminine passive causative participle of [[en:lexicon:h7200|H7200]];

===== Meaning =====

something **conspicuous**, i.e. the **craw** of a bird (from its prominence)

===== Definitions =====

  - conspicuous
  - craw

===== Usage =====

crop.
