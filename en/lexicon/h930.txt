====== H930: בְּהֵמוֹת (bᵉhêmôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

in form a plural or [[en:lexicon:h929|H929]], but really a singular of Egyptian derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a **water-ox**, i.e. the **hippopotamus** or **Nile-horse**

===== Definitions =====

  - water-ox
  - hippopotamus
  - Nile-horse

===== Usage =====

Behemoth.
