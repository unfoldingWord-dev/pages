====== G94: ἄδικος (ádikos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g1349|G1349]];

===== Meaning =====

unjust; by extension wicked; by implication, treacherous; specially, heathen

===== Usage =====

unjust, unrighteous.
