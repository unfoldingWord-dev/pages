====== G4022: περιέρχομαι (periérchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2064|G2064]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to come all around, i.e. stroll, vacillate, veer

===== Usage =====

fetch a compass, vagabond, wandering about.
