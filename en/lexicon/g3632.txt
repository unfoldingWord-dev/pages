====== G3632: οἰνοφλυγία (oinophlygía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3631|G3631]] and a form of the base of [[en:lexicon:g5397|G5397]];

===== Meaning =====

an overflow (or surplus) of wine, i.e. vinolency (drunkenness)

===== Usage =====

excess of wine.
