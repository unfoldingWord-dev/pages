====== G169: ἀκάθαρτος (akáthartos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2508|G2508]]
 (meaning cleansed);

===== Meaning =====

impure (ceremonially, morally (lewd) or specially, (demonic))

===== Usage =====

foul, unclean.
