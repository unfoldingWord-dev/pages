====== H6038: עֲנָוָה (ʻănâvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6035|H6035]];

===== Meaning =====

**condescension**, human and subjective (modesty), or divine and objective (clemency)

===== Definitions =====

  - condescension

===== Usage =====

gentleness, humility, meekness.
