====== H182: אוֹדוֹת (ʼôwdôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]] (only thus in the plural); from the same as [[en:lexicon:h181|H181]];

===== Meaning =====

**turnings** (i.e. **occasions**); (adverb) on **account** of

===== Definitions =====

  - turnings
  - occasions
  - account

===== Usage =====

(be-) cause, concerning, sake.
