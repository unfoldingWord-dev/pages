====== G4030: περικεφαλαία (perikephalaía) ======

===== Source =====

feminine of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2776|G2776]];

===== Meaning =====

encirclement of the head, i.e. a helmet

===== Usage =====

helmet.
