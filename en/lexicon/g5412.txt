====== G5412: φορτίζω (phortízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5414|G5414]];

===== Meaning =====

to load up (properly, as a vessel or animal), i.e. (figuratively) to overburden with ceremony (or spiritual anxiety)

===== Usage =====

lade, by heavy laden.
