====== G1063: γάρ (gár) ======

===== Source =====

a primary particle;

===== Meaning =====

properly, assigning a reason (used in argument, explanation or intensification; often with other particles)

===== Usage =====

and, as, because (that), but, even, for, indeed, no doubt, seeing, then, therefore, verily, what, why, yet.
