====== G3806: πάθος (páthos) ======

===== Source =====

from the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g3958|G3958]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, suffering ("pathos"), i.e. (subjectively) a passion (especially concupiscence)

===== Usage =====

 (inordinate) affection, lust.
