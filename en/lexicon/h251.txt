====== H251: אָח (ʼâch) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

a primitive word;

===== Meaning =====

a **brother** (used in the widest sense of literal relationship and metaphorical affinity or resemblance [like [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]]])

===== Definitions =====

  - brother

===== Usage =====

another, brother(-ly); kindred, like, other. Compare also the proper names beginning with 'Ah-' or 'Ahi-'.
