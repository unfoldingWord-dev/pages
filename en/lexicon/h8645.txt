====== H8645: תִּרְזָה (tirzâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:h7329|H7329]];

===== Meaning =====

a species of tree (apparently from its **slenderness**), perhaps the **cypress**

===== Definitions =====

  - slenderness
  - cypress

===== Usage =====

cypress.
