====== H6289: פָּארוּר (pâʼrûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6286|H6286]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **illuminated**, i.e. a **glow**; as noun, a **flush** (of anxiety)

===== Definitions =====

  - illuminated
  - glow
  - flush

===== Usage =====

blackness.
