====== H5414: נָתַן (nâthan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **give**, used with greatest latitude of application (**put**, **make**, etc.)

===== Definitions =====

  - give
  - put
  - make

===== Usage =====

add, apply, appoint, ascribe, assign, × avenge, × be (healed), bestow, bring (forth, hither), cast, cause, charge, come, commit, consider, count,   cry, deliver (up), direct, distribute, do, × doubtless, × without fail, fasten, frame, × get, give (forth, over, up), grant, hang (up), × have, × indeed, lay (unto charge, up), (give) leave, lend, let (out),   lie, lift up, make,   O that, occupy, offer, ordain, pay, perform, place, pour, print, × pull, put (forth), recompense, render, requite, restore, send (out), set (forth), shew, shoot forth (up),   sing,   slander, strike, (sub-) mit, suffer, × surely, × take, thrust, trade, turn, utter,   weep,   willingly,   withdraw,   would (to) God, yield.
