====== H450: אֶלְיָדָע (ʼElyâdâʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]] and [[en:lexicon:h3045|H3045]]; God (is) knowing;

===== Meaning =====

**Eljada**, the name of two Israelites and of an Aramaean leader
