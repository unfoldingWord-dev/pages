====== H2291: חַגִּי (Chaggîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculineadjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2287|H2287]]; **festive**

===== Meaning =====

**Chaggi**, an Israelite; also (patronymically) a **Chaggite**, or descendant of the same

===== Definitions =====

  - festive
  - Chaggi
  - Chaggite

===== Usage =====

Haggi, Haggites.
