====== G1739: ἐνδόμησις (endómēsis) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g1218|G1218]];

===== Meaning =====

a housing in (residence), i.e. structure

===== Usage =====

building.
