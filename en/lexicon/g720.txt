====== G720: ἀρνέομαι (arnéomai) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g4483|G4483]];

===== Meaning =====

to contradict, i.e. disavow, reject, abnegate

===== Usage =====

deny, refuse.
