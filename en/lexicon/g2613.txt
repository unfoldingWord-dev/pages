====== G2613: καταδικάζω (katadikázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1349|G1349]];

===== Meaning =====

to adjudge against, i.e. pronounce guilty

===== Usage =====

condemn.
