====== H7896: שִׁית (shîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **place** (in a very wide application)

===== Definitions =====

  - place

===== Usage =====

apply, appoint, array, bring, consider, lay (up), let alone, × look, make, mark, put (on),   regard, set, shew, be stayed, × take.
