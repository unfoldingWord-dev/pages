====== G281: ἀμήν (amḗn) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin ([[en:lexicon:h0543|H0543]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, firm, i.e. (figuratively) trustworthy; adverbially, surely (often as interjection, so be it)

===== Usage =====

amen, verily.
