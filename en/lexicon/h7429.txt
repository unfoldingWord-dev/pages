====== H7429: רָמַס (râmaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tread** upon (as a potter, in walking or abusively)

===== Definitions =====

  - tread

===== Usage =====

oppressor, stamp upon, trample (under feet), tread (down, upon).
