====== H2796: חָרָשׁ (chârâsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2790|H2790]];

===== Meaning =====

a **fabricator** or any material

===== Definitions =====

  - fabricator

===== Usage =====

artificer, ( ) carpenter, craftsman, engraver, maker,   mason, skilful, ( ) smith, worker, workman, such as wrought.
