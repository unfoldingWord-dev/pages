====== H2606: חֲנַנְאֵל (Chănanʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2603|H2603]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; God has favored;

===== Meaning =====

**Chananel**, probably an Israelite, from whom a tower of Jerusalem was named

===== Definitions =====

  - Chananel

===== Usage =====

Hananeel.
