====== G4374: προσφέρω (prosphérō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to bear towards, i.e. lead to, tender (especially to God), treat

===== Usage =====

bring (to, unto), deal with, do, offer (unto, up), present unto, put to.
