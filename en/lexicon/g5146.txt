====== G5146: τρίβολος (tríbolos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5140|G5140]] and [[en:lexicon:g956|G956]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a crow-foot (three-pronged obstruction in war), i.e. (by analogy) a thorny plant (caltrop)

===== Usage =====

brier, thistle.
