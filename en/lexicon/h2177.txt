====== H2177: זַן (zan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2109|H2109]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **nourished** (or fully developed), i.e. a **form** or **sort**

===== Definitions =====

  - nourished
  - form
  - sort

===== Usage =====

divers kinds, × all manner of store.
