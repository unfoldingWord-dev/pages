====== H7945: שֶׁל (shel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

relative pronoun

===== Source =====

for the rel. [[en:lexicon:h834|H834]]; used with prepositional prefix, and often followed by some pronominal affix;

===== Meaning =====

**on account of**, **whatsoever**, **whichsoever**

===== Definitions =====

  - on account of
  - whatsoever
  - whichsoever

===== Usage =====

cause, sake.
