====== G801: ἀσύνετος (asýnetos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g4908|G4908]];

===== Meaning =====

unintelligent; by implication, wicked

===== Usage =====

foolish, without understanding.
