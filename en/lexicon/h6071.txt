====== H6071: עָסִיס (ʻâçîyç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6072|H6072]];

===== Meaning =====

**must** or fresh grape-juice (as just **trodden** out)

===== Definitions =====

  - must
  - trodden

===== Usage =====

juice, new (sweet) wine.
