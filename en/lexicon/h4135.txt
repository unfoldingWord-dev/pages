====== H4135: מוּל (mûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cut** short, i.e. **curtail** (specifically the prepuce, i.e. to **circumcise**); by implication, to **blunt**; figuratively, to **destroy**

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - curtail
  - circumcise
  - blunt
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

circumcise(-ing), selves), cut down (in pieces), destroy, × must needs.
