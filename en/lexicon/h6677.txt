====== H6677: צַוָּאר (tsavvâʼr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Nehemiah 3:5), or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Song of Solomon 4:9), or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Micah 2:3), intensively from [[en:lexicon:h6696|H6696]] in the sense of **binding**;

===== Meaning =====

the back of the **neck** (as that on which burdens are **bound**)

===== Definitions =====

  - binding
  - neck
  - bound

===== Usage =====

neck.
