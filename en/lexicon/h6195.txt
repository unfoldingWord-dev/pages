====== H6195: עׇרְמָה (ʻormâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h6193|H6193]];

===== Meaning =====

**trickery**; or (in a good sense) **discretion**

===== Definitions =====

  - trickery
  - discretion

===== Usage =====

guile, prudence, subtilty, wilily, wisdom.
