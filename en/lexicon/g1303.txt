====== G1303: διατίθεμαι (diatíthemai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

to put apart, i.e. (figuratively) dispose (by assignment, compact, or bequest)

===== Usage =====

appoint, make, testator.
