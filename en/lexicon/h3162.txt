====== H3162: יַחַד (yachad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3161|H3161]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **unit**, i.e. (adverb) **unitedly**

===== Definitions =====

  - unit
  - unitedly

===== Usage =====

alike, at all (once), both, likewise, only, (al-) together, withal.
