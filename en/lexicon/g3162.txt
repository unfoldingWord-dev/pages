====== G3162: μάχαιρα (máchaira) ======

===== Source =====

probably feminine of a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3163|G3163]];

===== Meaning =====

a knife, i.e. dirk; figuratively, war, judicial punishment

===== Usage =====

sword.
