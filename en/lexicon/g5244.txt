====== G5244: ὑπερήφανος (hyperḗphanos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]] and [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]];

===== Meaning =====

appearing above others (conspicuous), i.e. (figuratively) haughty

===== Usage =====

proud.
