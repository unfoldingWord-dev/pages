====== G1888: ἐπαυτοφώρῳ (epautophṓrōi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g846|G846]] and (the dative case singular of) a derivative of
 φώρ (phṓr) (a thief);

===== Meaning =====

in theft itself, i.e. (by analogy) in actual crime

===== Usage =====

in the very act.
