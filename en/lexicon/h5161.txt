====== H5161: נֶחֱלָמִי (Nechĕlâmîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

apparently a patronymic from an unused name (apparently passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h2492|H2492]]); **dreamed**;

===== Meaning =====

a **Nechelamite**, or descendant of Nechlam

===== Definitions =====

  - dreamed
  - Nechelamite

===== Usage =====

Nehelamite.
