====== G458: ἀνομία (anomía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g459|G459]];

===== Meaning =====

illegality, i.e. violation of law or (genitive case) wickedness

===== Usage =====

iniquity, X transgress(-ion of) the law, unrighteousness.
