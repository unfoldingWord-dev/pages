====== H2203: זֶפֶת (zepheth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to **liquify**);

===== Meaning =====

**asphalt** (from its tendency to **soften** in the sun)

===== Definitions =====

  - liquify
  - asphalt
  - soften

===== Usage =====

pitch.
