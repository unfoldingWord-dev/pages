====== G69: ἀγρυπνέω (agrypnéō) ======

===== Source =====

ultimately from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5258|G5258]];

===== Meaning =====

to be sleepless, i.e. keep awake

===== Usage =====

watch.
