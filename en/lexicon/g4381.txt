====== G4381: προσωπολήπτης (prosōpolḗptēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4383|G4383]] and [[en:lexicon:g2983|G2983]];

===== Meaning =====

an accepter of a face (individual), i.e. (specially), one exhibiting partiality

===== Usage =====

respecter of persons.
