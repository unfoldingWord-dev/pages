====== G1327: διέξοδος (diéxodos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g1841|G1841]];

===== Meaning =====

an outlet through, i.e. probably an open square (from which roads diverge)

===== Usage =====

highway.
