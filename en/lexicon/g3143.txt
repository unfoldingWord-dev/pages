====== G3143: μαρτύρομαι (martýromai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g3144|G3144]];

===== Meaning =====

to be adduced as a witness, i.e. (figuratively) to obtest (in affirmation or exhortation)

===== Usage =====

take to record, testify.
