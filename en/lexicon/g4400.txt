====== G4400: προχειρίζομαι (procheirízomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5495|G5495]];

===== Meaning =====

to handle for oneself in advance, i.e. (figuratively) to purpose

===== Usage =====

choose, make.
