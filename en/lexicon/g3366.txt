====== G3366: μηδέ (mēdé) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3361|G3361]] and [[en:lexicon:g1161|G1161]];

===== Meaning =====

but not, not even; in a continued negation, nor

===== Usage =====

neither, nor (yet), (no) not (once, so much as).
