====== H6438: פִּנָּה (pinnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h6434|H6434]];

===== Meaning =====

an **angle**; by implication, a **pinnacle**; figuratively, a **chieftain**

===== Definitions =====

  - angle
  - pinnacle
  - chieftain

===== Usage =====

bulwark, chief, corner, stay, tower.
