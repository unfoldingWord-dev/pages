====== G5434: φρύγανον (phrýganon) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative of φρύγω (phrýgō) (to roast or parch; akin to
 the base of [[en:lexicon:g5395|G5395]]);

===== Meaning =====

something desiccated, i.e. a dry twig

===== Usage =====

stick.
