====== G3809: παιδεία (paideía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3811|G3811]];

===== Meaning =====

tutorage, i.e. education or training; by implication, disciplinary correction

===== Usage =====

chastening, chastisement, instruction, nurture.
