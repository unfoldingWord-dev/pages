====== G3794: ὀχύρωμα (ochýrōma) ======

===== Source =====

from a remote derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]] (meaning to fortify, through the idea
 of holding safely);

===== Meaning =====

a castle (figuratively, argument)

===== Usage =====

stronghold.
