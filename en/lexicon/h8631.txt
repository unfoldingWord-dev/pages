====== H8631: תְּקֵף (tᵉqêph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h8630|H8630]];

===== Meaning =====

to **become** (causatively, make) **mighty** or (figuratively) **obstinate**

===== Definitions =====

  - become
  - mighty
  - obstinate

===== Usage =====

make firm, harden, be(-come) strong.
