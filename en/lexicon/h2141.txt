====== H2141: זָכַךְ (zâkak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2135|H2135]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **be transparent** or **clean** (phys. or morally)

===== Definitions =====

  - be transparent
  - clean

===== Usage =====

be (make) clean, be pure(-r).
