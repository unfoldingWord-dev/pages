====== G2767: κεράννυμι (keránnymi) ======

===== Source =====

prolonged form of a more primary κεράω (keráō)  (which is used in
 certain tenses);

===== Meaning =====

to mingle, i.e. (by implication) to pour out (for drinking)

===== Usage =====

fill, pour out.
