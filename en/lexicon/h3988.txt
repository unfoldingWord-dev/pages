====== H3988: מָאַס (mâʼaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **spurn**; also (intransitively) to **disappear**

===== Definitions =====

  - spurn
  - disappear

===== Usage =====

abhor, cast away (off), contemn, despise, disdain, (become) loathe(some), melt away, refuse, reject, reprobate, × utterly, vile person.
