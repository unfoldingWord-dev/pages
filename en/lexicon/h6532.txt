====== H6532: פֹּרֶכֶת (pôreketh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine active participle of the same as [[en:lexicon:h6531|H6531]];

===== Meaning =====

a **separatrix**, i.e. (the sacred) **screen**

===== Definitions =====

  - separatrix
  - screen

===== Usage =====

vail.
