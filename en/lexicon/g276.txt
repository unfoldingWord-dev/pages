====== G276: ἀμετάθετος (ametáthetos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3346|G3346]];

===== Meaning =====

unchangeable, or (neuter as abstract) unchangeability

===== Usage =====

immutable(-ility).
