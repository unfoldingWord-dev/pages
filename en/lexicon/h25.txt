====== H25: אֲבִי גִבְעוֹן (ʼĂbîy Gibʻôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h1391|H1391]]; father (i.e. founder) of Gibon;

===== Meaning =====

**Abi-Gibon**, perhaps an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Abi-Gibon

===== Usage =====

father of Gibeon.
