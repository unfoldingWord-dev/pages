====== H3992: מָאַר (mâʼar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be bitter** or (causatively) to **embitter**, i.e. **be painful**

===== Definitions =====

  - be bitter
  - embitter
  - be painful

===== Usage =====

fretting, picking.
