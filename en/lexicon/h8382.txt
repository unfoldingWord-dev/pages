====== H8382: תָּאַם (tâʼam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to be complete; but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h8380|H8380]],

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **make**) **twinned**, i.e. (figuratively) **duplicate** or (architectural) **jointed**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - twinned
  - duplicate
  - jointed

===== Usage =====

coupled (together), bear twins.
