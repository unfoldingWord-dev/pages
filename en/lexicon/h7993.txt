====== H7993: שָׁלַךְ (shâlak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **throw** out, down or away (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - throw

===== Usage =====

adventure, cast (away, down, forth, off, out), hurl, pluck, throw.
