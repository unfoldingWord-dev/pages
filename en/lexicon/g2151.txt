====== G2151: εὐσεβέω (eusebéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2152|G2152]];

===== Meaning =====

to be pious, i.e. (towards God) to worship, or (towards parents) to respect (support)

===== Usage =====

show piety, worship.
