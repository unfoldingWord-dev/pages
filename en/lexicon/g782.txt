====== G782: ἀσπάζομαι (aspázomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a particle of union) and a presumed form of [[en:lexicon:g4685|G4685]];

===== Meaning =====

to enfold in the arms, i.e. (by implication) to salute, (figuratively) to welcome

===== Usage =====

embrace, greet, salute, take leave.
