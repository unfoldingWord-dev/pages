====== H4046: מַגֵּפָה (maggêphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5062|H5062]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pestilence**; by analogy, **defeat**

===== Definitions =====

  - pestilence
  - defeat

===== Usage =====

(× be) plague(-d), slaughter, stroke.
