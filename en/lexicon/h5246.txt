====== H5246: נָמֵר (nâmêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning properly, to **filtrate**, i.e. **be limpid** (comp [[en:lexicon:h5247|H5247]] and [[en:lexicon:h5249|H5249]]); and thus to **spot** or **stain** as if by dripping;

===== Meaning =====

a **leopard** (from its stripes)

===== Definitions =====

  - filtrate
  - be limpid
  - spot
  - stain
  - leopard

===== Usage =====

leopard.
