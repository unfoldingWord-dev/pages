====== G1709: ἐμπνέω (empnéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g4154|G4154]];

===== Meaning =====

to inhale, i.e. (figuratively) to be animated by (bent upon)

===== Usage =====

breathe.
