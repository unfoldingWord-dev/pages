====== H6311: פֹּה (pôh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Job 38:11), or [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably from a primitive inseparable particle 'p' (of demonstrative force) and [[en:lexicon:h1931|H1931]];

===== Meaning =====

**this place** (French ici), i.e. **here** or hence

===== Definitions =====

  - this place
  - here

===== Usage =====

here, hither, the one (other, this, that) side.
