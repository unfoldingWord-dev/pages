====== G3748: ὅστις (hóstis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3739|G3739]] and [[en:lexicon:g5100|G5100]];

===== Meaning =====

which some, i.e. any that; also (definite) which same

===== Usage =====

X and (they), (such) as, (they) that, in that they, what(-soever), whereas ye, (they) which, who(-soever).
