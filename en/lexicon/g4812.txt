====== G4812: συλαγωγέω (sylagōgéō) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4813|G4813]] and (the reduplicated form of) [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to lead away as booty, i.e. (figuratively) seduce

===== Usage =====

spoil.
