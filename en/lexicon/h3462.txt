====== H3462: יָשֵׁן (yâshên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be slack** or **languid**, i.e. (by implication) **sleep** (figuratively, to **die**); also to **grow old**, **stale** or **inveterate**

===== Definitions =====

  - be slack
  - languid
  - sleep
  - die
  - grow old
  - stale
  - inveterate

===== Usage =====

old (store), remain long, (make to) sleep.
