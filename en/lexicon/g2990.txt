====== G2990: λανθάνω (lanthánō) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of a primary verb, which is used only as an alternate
 in certain tenses;

===== Meaning =====

to lie hid (literally or figuratively); often used adverbially, unwittingly

===== Usage =====

be hid, be ignorant of, unawares.
