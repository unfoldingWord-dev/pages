====== G2617: καταισχύνω (kataischýnō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g153|G153]];

===== Meaning =====

to shame down, i.e. disgrace or (by implication) put to the blush

===== Usage =====

confound, dishonour, (be a-, make a-)shame(-d).
