====== G4599: σθενόω (sthenóō) ======

===== Source =====

from σθένος (sthénos) (bodily vigor; probably akin to the base of [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]]);

===== Meaning =====

to strengthen, i.e. (figuratively) confirm (in spiritual knowledge and power)

===== Usage =====

strengthen.
