====== H6854: צְפַרְדֵּעַ (tsᵉphardêaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6852|H6852]] and a word elsewhere unused meaning a swamp;

===== Meaning =====

a **marsh-leaper**, i.e. **frog**

===== Definitions =====

  - marsh-leaper
  - frog

===== Usage =====

frog.
