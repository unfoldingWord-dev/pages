====== G3586: ξύλον (xýlon) ======

===== Source =====

from another form of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3582|G3582]];

===== Meaning =====

timber (as fuel or material); by implication, a stick, club or tree or other wooden article or substance

===== Usage =====

staff, stocks, tree, wood.
