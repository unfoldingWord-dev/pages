====== H6788: צַמֶּרֶת (tsammereth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6785|H6785]];

===== Meaning =====

**fleeciness**, i.e. **foliage**

===== Definitions =====

  - fleeciness
  - foliage

===== Usage =====

highest branch, top.
