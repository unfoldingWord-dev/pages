====== H4195: מוֹתָר (môwthâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3498|H3498]];

===== Meaning =====

literally **gain**; figuratively, **superiority**

===== Definitions =====

  - gain
  - superiority

===== Usage =====

plenteousness, preeminence, profit.
