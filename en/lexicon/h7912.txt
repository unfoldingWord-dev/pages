====== H7912: שְׁכַח (shᵉkach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h7911|H7911]] through the idea of disclosure of a covered or forgotten thing;

===== Meaning =====

to **discover** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - discover

===== Usage =====

find.
