====== H5102: נָהַר (nâhar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sparkle**, i.e. (figuratively) **be cheerful**; hence (from the **sheen** of a running stream) to **flow**, i.e. (figuratively) **assemble**

===== Definitions =====

  - sparkle
  - be cheerful
  - sheen
  - flow
  - assemble

===== Usage =====

flow (together), be lightened.
