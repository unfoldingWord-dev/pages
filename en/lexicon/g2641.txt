====== G2641: καταλείπω (kataleípō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g3007|G3007]];

===== Meaning =====

to leave down, i.e. behind; by implication, to abandon, have remaining

===== Usage =====

forsake, leave, reserve.
