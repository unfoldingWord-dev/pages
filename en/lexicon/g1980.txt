====== G1980: ἐπισκέπτομαι (episképtomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g4649|G4649]];

===== Meaning =====

to inspect, i.e. (by implication) to select; by extension, to go to see, relieve

===== Usage =====

look out, visit.
