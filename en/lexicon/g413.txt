====== G413: ἀνέκλειπτος (anékleiptos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1587|G1587]];

===== Meaning =====

not left out, i.e. (by implication) inexhaustible

===== Usage =====

that faileth not.
