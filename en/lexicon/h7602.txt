====== H7602: שָׁאַף (shâʼaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **inhale eagerly**; figuratively, to **cover**; by implication, to **be angry**; also to **hasten**

===== Definitions =====

  - inhale eagerly
  - cover
  - be angry
  - hasten

===== Usage =====

desire (earnestly), devour, haste, pant, snuff up, swallow up.
