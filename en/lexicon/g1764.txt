====== G1764: ἐνίστημι (enístēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]];

===== Meaning =====

to place on hand, i.e. (reflexively) impend, (participle) be instant

===== Usage =====

come, be at hand, present.
