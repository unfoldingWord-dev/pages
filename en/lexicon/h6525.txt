====== H6525: פֶּרַח (perach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6524|H6524]];

===== Meaning =====

a **calyx** (natural or artificial); generally, **bloom**

===== Definitions =====

  - calyx
  - bloom

===== Usage =====

blossom, bud, flower.
