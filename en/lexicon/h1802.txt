====== H1802: דָּלָה (dâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h1809|H1809]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **dangle**, i.e. to **let down a bucket** (for drawing out water); figuratively, to **deliver**

===== Definitions =====

  - dangle
  - let down a bucket
  - deliver

===== Usage =====

draw (out), × enough, lift up.
