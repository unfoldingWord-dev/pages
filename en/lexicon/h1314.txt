====== H1314: בֶּשֶׂם (besem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1313|H1313]];

===== Meaning =====

**fragrance**; by implication, **spicery**; also the **balsam** plant

===== Definitions =====

  - fragrance
  - spicery
  - balsam

===== Usage =====

smell, spice, sweet (odour).
