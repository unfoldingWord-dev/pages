====== H7420: רֹמַח (rômach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **hurl**;

===== Meaning =====

a **lance** (as **thrown**); especially the iron **point**

===== Definitions =====

  - hurl
  - lance
  - thrown
  - point

===== Usage =====

buckler, javelin, lancet, spear.
