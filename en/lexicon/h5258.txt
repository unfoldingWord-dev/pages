====== H5258: נָסַךְ (nâçak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pour** out, especially a libation, or to **cast** (metal); by analogy, to **anoint** a king

===== Definitions =====

  - pour
  - cast
  - anoint

===== Usage =====

cover, melt, offer, (cause to) pour (out), set (up).
