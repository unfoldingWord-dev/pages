====== H6920: קַדַּחַת (qaddachath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6919|H6919]];

===== Meaning =====

**inflammation**, i.e. **febrile disease**

===== Definitions =====

  - inflammation
  - febrile disease

===== Usage =====

burning ague, fever.
