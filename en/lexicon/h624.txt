====== H624: אָסֻף (ʼâçuph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h622|H622]];

===== Meaning =====

**collected** (only in the plural), i.e. a **collection of offerings**

===== Definitions =====

  - collected
  - collection of offerings

===== Usage =====

threshold, Asuppim.
