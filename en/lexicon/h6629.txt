====== H6629: צֹאן (tsôʼn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Psalm 144:13), from an unused root meaning to **migrate**;

===== Meaning =====

a collective name for a **flock** (of sheep or goats); also figuratively (of men)

===== Definitions =====

  - migrate
  - flock

===== Usage =====

(small) cattle, flock (  -s), lamb (  -s), sheep(-cote, -fold, -shearer, -herds).
