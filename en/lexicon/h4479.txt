====== H4479: מָן (mân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

interrogative pronoun

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:lexicon:h4101|H4101]];

===== Meaning =====

**who** or **what** (properly, interrogatively, hence, also indefinitely and relatively)

===== Definitions =====

  - who
  - what

===== Usage =====

what, who(-msoever,   -so).
