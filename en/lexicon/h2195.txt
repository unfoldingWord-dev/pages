====== H2195: זַעַם (zaʻam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2194|H2194]];

===== Meaning =====

strictly **froth** at the mouth, i.e. (figuratively) **fury** (especially of God's displeasure with sin)

===== Definitions =====

  - froth
  - fury

===== Usage =====

angry, indignation, rage.
