====== H7934: שָׁכֵן (shâkên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7931|H7931]];

===== Meaning =====

a **resident**; by extension, a **fellow-citizen**

===== Definitions =====

  - resident
  - fellow-citizen

===== Usage =====

inhabitant, neighbour, nigh.
