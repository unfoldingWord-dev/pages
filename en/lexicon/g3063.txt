====== G3063: λοιπόν (loipón) ======

===== Source =====

neuter singular of the same as [[en:lexicon:g3062|G3062]];

===== Meaning =====

something remaining (adverbially)

===== Usage =====

besides, finally, furthermore, (from) henceforth, moreover, now, + it remaineth, then.
