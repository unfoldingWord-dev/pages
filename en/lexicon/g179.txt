====== G179: ἀκατάλυτος (akatálytos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2647|G2647]];

===== Meaning =====

indissoluble, i.e. (figuratively) permanent

===== Usage =====

endless.
