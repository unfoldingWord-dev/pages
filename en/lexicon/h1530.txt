====== H1530: גַּל (gal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1556|H1556]];

===== Meaning =====

something **rolled**, i.e. a **heap** of stone or dung (plural **ruins**), by analogy, a **spring** of water (plural **waves**)

===== Definitions =====

  - rolled
  - heap
  - ruins
  - spring
  - waves

===== Usage =====

billow, heap, spring, wave.
