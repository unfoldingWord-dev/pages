====== G3857: παράδεισος (parádeisos) ======

===== Source =====

of Oriental origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h06508|H06508]]);

===== Meaning =====

a park, i.e. (specially), an Eden (place of future happiness, "paradise")

===== Usage =====

paradise.
