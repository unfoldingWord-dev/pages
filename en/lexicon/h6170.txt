====== H6170: עֲרוּגָה (ʻărûwgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h6165|H6165]];

===== Meaning =====

something **piled up** (as if (figuratively) **raised by mental aspiration)**, i.e. a **paterre**

===== Definitions =====

  - piled up
  - raised by mental aspiration)
  - paterre

===== Usage =====

bed, furrow.
