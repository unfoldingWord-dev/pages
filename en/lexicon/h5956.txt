====== H5956: עָלַם (ʻâlam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **veil** from sight, i.e. **conceal** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - veil
  - conceal

===== Usage =====

× any ways, blind, dissembler, hide (self), secret (thing).
