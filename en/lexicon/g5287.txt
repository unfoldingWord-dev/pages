====== G5287: ὑπόστασις (hypóstasis) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]];

===== Meaning =====

a setting under (support), i.e. (figuratively) concretely, essence, or abstractly, assurance (objectively or subjectively)

===== Usage =====

confidence, confident, person, substance.
