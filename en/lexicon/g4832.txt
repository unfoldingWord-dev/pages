====== G4832: σύμμορφος (sýmmorphos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3444|G3444]];

===== Meaning =====

jointly formed, i.e. (figuratively) similar

===== Usage =====

conformed to, fashioned like unto.
