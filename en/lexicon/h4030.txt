====== H4030: מִגְדָּנָה (migdânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h4022|H4022]];

===== Meaning =====

**preciousness**, i.e. a **gem**

===== Definitions =====

  - preciousness
  - gem

===== Usage =====

precious thing, present.
