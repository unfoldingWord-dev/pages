====== H7181: קָשַׁב (qâshab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **prick up** the ears, i.e. **hearken**

===== Definitions =====

  - prick up
  - hearken

===== Usage =====

attend, (cause to) hear(-ken), give heed, incline, mark (well), regard.
