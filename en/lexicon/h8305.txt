====== H8305: שְׂרִיקָה (sᵉrîyqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h8321|H8321]] in the original sense of **piercing**;

===== Meaning =====

**hetchelling** (or combing flax), i.e. (concretely) **tow** (by extension, **linen** cloth)

===== Definitions =====

  - piercing
  - hetchelling
  - tow
  - linen

===== Usage =====

fine.
