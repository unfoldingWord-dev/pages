====== H8269: שַׂר (sar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8323|H8323]];

===== Meaning =====

a **head** person (of any rank or class)

===== Definitions =====

  - head

===== Usage =====

captain (that had rule), chief (captain), general, governor, keeper, lord,(-task-)master, prince(-ipal), ruler, steward.
