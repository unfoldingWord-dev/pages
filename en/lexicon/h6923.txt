====== H6923: קָדַם (qâdam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **project** (one self), i.e. **precede**; hence, to **anticipate**, **hasten**, **meet** (usually for help)

===== Definitions =====

  - project
  - precede
  - anticipate
  - hasten
  - meet

===== Usage =====

come (go, (flee)) before,   disappoint, meet, prevent.
