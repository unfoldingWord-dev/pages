====== H4109: מַהֲלָךְ (mahălâk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1980|H1980]];

===== Meaning =====

a **walk**, i.e. a **passage** or a **distance**

===== Definitions =====

  - walk
  - passage
  - distance

===== Usage =====

journey, walk.
