====== G1826: ἔξειμι (éxeimi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and εἶμι (eîmi) (to go);

===== Meaning =====

to issue, i.e. leave (a place), escape (to the shore)

===== Usage =====

depart, get (to land), go out.
