====== H3734: כֹּר (kôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h3564|H3564]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a deep round **vessel**, i.e. (specifically) a **cor** or measure for things dry

===== Definitions =====

  - vessel
  - cor

===== Usage =====

cor, measure. Aramaic the same.
