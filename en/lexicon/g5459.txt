====== G5459: φωσφόρος (phōsphóros) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5457|G5457]] and [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]];

===== Meaning =====

light-bearing ("phosphorus"), i.e. (specially), the morning-star (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

day star.
