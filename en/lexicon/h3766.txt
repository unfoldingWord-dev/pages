====== H3766: כָּרַע (kâraʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **bend** the knee; by implication, to **sink**, to **prostrate**

===== Definitions =====

  - bend
  - sink
  - prostrate

===== Usage =====

bow (down, self), bring down (low), cast down, couch, fall, feeble, kneeling, sink, smite (stoop) down, subdue, × very.
