====== H2237: זָרַר (zârar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2114|H2114]]);

===== Meaning =====

perhaps to **diffuse**, i.e. (specifically) to **sneeze**

===== Definitions =====

  - diffuse
  - sneeze

===== Usage =====

sneeze.
