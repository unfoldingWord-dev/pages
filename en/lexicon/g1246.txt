====== G1246: διακατελέγχομαι (diakatelénchomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g1651|G1651]];

===== Meaning =====

to prove downright, i.e. confute

===== Usage =====

convince.
