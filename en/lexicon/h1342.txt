====== H1342: גָּאָה (gâʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **mount** up; hence, in general, to **rise**, (figuratively) be **majestic**

===== Definitions =====

  - mount
  - rise
  - majestic

===== Usage =====

gloriously, grow up, increase, be risen, triumph.
