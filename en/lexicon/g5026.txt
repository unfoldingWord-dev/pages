====== G5026: ταύτῃ (taútēi) ======

===== Source =====

dative case, accusative case and genitive case respectively of the
 feminine singular of [[en:lexicon:g3778|G3778]];

===== Meaning =====

(towards or of) this

===== Usage =====

her, + hereof, it, that, + thereby, the (same), this (same).
