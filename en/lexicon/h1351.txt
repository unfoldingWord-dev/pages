====== H1351: גָּאַל (gâʼal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root, (rather identified with [[en:lexicon:h1350|H1350]], through the idea of **freeing**, i.e. **repudiating**);

===== Meaning =====

to **soil** or (figuratively) **desecrate**

===== Definitions =====

  - freeing
  - repudiating
  - soil
  - desecrate

===== Usage =====

defile, pollute, stain.
