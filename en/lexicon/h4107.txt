====== H4107: מָהַל (mâhal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **cut down** or **reduce**, i.e. by implication, to **adulterate**

===== Definitions =====

  - cut down
  - reduce
  - adulterate

===== Usage =====

mixed.
