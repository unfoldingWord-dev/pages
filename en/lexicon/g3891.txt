====== G3891: παρανομέω (paranoméō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g3551|G3551]];

===== Meaning =====

to be opposed to law, i.e. to transgress

===== Usage =====

contrary to law.
