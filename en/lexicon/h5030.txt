====== H5030: נָבִיא (nâbîyʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5012|H5012]];

===== Meaning =====

a **prophet** or (generally) **inspired man**

===== Definitions =====

  - prophet
  - inspired man

===== Usage =====

prophecy, that prophesy, prophet.
