====== G3772: οὐρανός (ouranós) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from the same as [[en:lexicon:g3735|G3735]] (through the idea of elevation);

===== Meaning =====

the sky; by extension, heaven (as the abode of God); by implication, happiness, power, eternity; specially, the Gospel (Christianity)

===== Usage =====

air, heaven(-ly), sky.
