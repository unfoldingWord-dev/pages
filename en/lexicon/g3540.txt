====== G3540: νόημα (nóēma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3539|G3539]];

===== Meaning =====

a perception, i.e. purpose, or (by implication) the intellect, disposition, itself

===== Usage =====

device, mind, thought.
