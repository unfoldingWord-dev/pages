====== H5737: עֲדַר (ʻădar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **arrange**, as a battle, a vineyard (to **hoe**); hence, to **muster** and so to **miss** (or find **wanting**)

===== Definitions =====

  - arrange
  - hoe
  - muster
  - miss
  - wanting

===== Usage =====

dig, fail, keep (rank), lack.
