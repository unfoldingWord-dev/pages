====== G312: ἀναγγέλλω (anangéllō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g32|G32]];

===== Meaning =====

to announce (in detail)

===== Usage =====

declare, rehearse, report, show, speak, tell.
