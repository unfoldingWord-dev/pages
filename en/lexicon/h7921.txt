====== H7921: שָׁכֹל (shâkôl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **miscarry**, i.e. **suffer abortion**; by analogy, to **bereave** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - miscarry
  - suffer abortion
  - bereave

===== Usage =====

bereave (of children), barren, cast calf (fruit, young), be (make) childless, deprive, destroy, × expect, lose children, miscarry, rob of children, spoil.
