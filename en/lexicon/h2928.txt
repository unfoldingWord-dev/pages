====== H2928: טֶלֶם (Ṭelem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculinenoun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to break up or treat violently; oppression;

===== Meaning =====

**Telem**, the name of a place in Idumaea, also of a temple doorkeeper

===== Definitions =====

  - Telem

===== Usage =====

Telem.
