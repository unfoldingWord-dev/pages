====== G561: ἀπέναντι (apénanti) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g1725|G1725]];

===== Meaning =====

from in front, i.e. opposite, before or against

===== Usage =====

before, contrary, over against, in the presence of.
