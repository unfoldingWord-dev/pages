====== H2554: חָמַס (châmaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be violent**; by implication, to **maltreat**

===== Definitions =====

  - be violent
  - maltreat

===== Usage =====

make bare, shake off, violate, do violence, take away violently, wrong, imagine wrongfully.
