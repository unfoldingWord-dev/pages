====== H5045: נֶגֶב (negeb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be parched;

===== Meaning =====

the **south** (from its drought); specifically, the **Negeb** or southern district of Judah, occasionally, **Egypt** (as south to Palestine)

===== Definitions =====

  - south
  - Negeb
  - Egypt

===== Usage =====

south (country, side, -ward).
