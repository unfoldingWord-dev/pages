====== H8633: תֹּקֶף (tôqeph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8630|H8630]];

===== Meaning =====

**might** or (figuratively) **positiveness**

===== Definitions =====

  - might
  - positiveness

===== Usage =====

authority, power, strength.
