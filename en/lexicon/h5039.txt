====== H5039: נְבָלָה (nᵉbâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h5036|H5036]];

===== Meaning =====

**foolishness**, i.e. (morally) **wickedness**; concretely, a **crime**; by extension, **punishment**

===== Definitions =====

  - foolishness
  - wickedness
  - crime
  - punishment

===== Usage =====

folly, vile, villany.
