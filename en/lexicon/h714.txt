====== H714: אַרְדְּ (ʼArd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to wander; **fugitive**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ard**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - fugitive
  - Ard

===== Usage =====

Ard.
