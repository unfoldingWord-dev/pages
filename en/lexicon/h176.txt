====== H176: אוֹ (ʼôw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

conjunction

===== Source =====

presumed to be the 'constructive' or genitival form of [[en:lexicon:|]] ; short for [[en:lexicon:h185|H185]];

===== Meaning =====

**desire** (and so probably in Proverbs 31:4); hence (by way of alternative) **or**, also **if**

===== Definitions =====

  - desire
  - or
  - if

===== Usage =====

also, and, either, if, at the least, × nor, or, otherwise, then, whether.
