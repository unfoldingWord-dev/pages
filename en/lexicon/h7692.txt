====== H7692: שִׁגָּיוֹן (shiggâyôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h7686|H7686]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **aberration**, i.e. (technically) a **dithyramb** or **rambling poem**

===== Definitions =====

  - aberration
  - dithyramb
  - rambling poem

===== Usage =====

Shiggaion, Shigionoth.
