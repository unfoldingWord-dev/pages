====== G3334: μετακινέω (metakinéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g2795|G2795]];

===== Meaning =====

to stir to a place elsewhere, i.e. remove (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

move away.
