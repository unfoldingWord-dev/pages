====== G2662: καταπατέω (katapatéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g3961|G3961]];

===== Meaning =====

to trample down; figuratively, to reject with disdain

===== Usage =====

trample, tread (down, underfoot).
