====== G4964: συσχηματίζω (syschēmatízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4976|G4976]];

===== Meaning =====

to fashion alike, i.e. conform to the same pattern (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

conform to, fashion self according to.
