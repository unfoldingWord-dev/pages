====== G1553: ἐκδημέω (ekdēméō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g1218|G1218]];

===== Meaning =====

to emigrate, i.e. (figuratively) vacate or quit

===== Usage =====

be absent.
