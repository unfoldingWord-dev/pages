====== H3791: כָּתָב (kâthâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3789|H3789]];

===== Meaning =====

something **written**, i.e. a **writing**, **record** or **book**

===== Definitions =====

  - written
  - writing
  - record
  - book

===== Usage =====

register, scripture, writing.
