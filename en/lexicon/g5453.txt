====== G5453: φύω (phýō) ======

===== Source =====

a primary verb;

===== Meaning =====

probably originally, to "puff" or blow, i.e. to swell up; but only used in the implied sense, to germinate or grow (sprout, produce), literally or figuratively

===== Usage =====

spring (up).
