====== G480: ἀντίκειμαι (antíkeimai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and [[en:lexicon:g2749|G2749]];

===== Meaning =====

to lie opposite, i.e. be adverse (figuratively, repugnant) to

===== Usage =====

adversary, be contrary, oppose.
