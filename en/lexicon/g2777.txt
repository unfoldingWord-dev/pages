====== G2777: κεφαλίς (kephalís) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2776|G2776]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a knob, i.e. (by implication) a roll (by extension from the end of a stick on which the manuscript was rolled)

===== Usage =====

volume.
