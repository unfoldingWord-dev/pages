====== G1823: ἐξαστράπτω (exastráptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g797|G797]];

===== Meaning =====

to lighten forth, i.e. (figuratively) to be radiant (of very white garments)

===== Usage =====

glistening.
