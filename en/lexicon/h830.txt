====== H830: אַשְׁפֹּת (ʼashpôth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (contraction) plural of a noun of the same form as [[en:lexicon:h827|H827]], from [[en:lexicon:h8192|H8192]] (in the sense of **scraping**);

===== Meaning =====

a heap of **rubbish** or **filth**

===== Definitions =====

  - scraping
  - rubbish
  - filth

===== Usage =====

dung (hill).
