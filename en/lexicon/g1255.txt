====== G1255: διαλαλέω (dialaléō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g2980|G2980]];

===== Meaning =====

to talk throughout a company, i.e. converse or (genitive case) publish

===== Usage =====

commune, noise abroad.
