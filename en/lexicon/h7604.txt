====== H7604: שָׁאַר (shâʼar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **swell** up, i.e. **be** (causatively, **make**) **redundant**

===== Definitions =====

  - swell
  - be
  - make
  - redundant

===== Usage =====

leave, (be) left, let, remain, remnant, reserve, the rest.
