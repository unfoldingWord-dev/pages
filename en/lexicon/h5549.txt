====== H5549: סָלַל (çâlal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **mound** up (especially a turnpike); figurative, to **exalt**; reflexively, to **oppose** (as by a dam)

===== Definitions =====

  - mound
  - exalt
  - oppose

===== Usage =====

cast up, exalt (self), extol, make plain, raise up.
