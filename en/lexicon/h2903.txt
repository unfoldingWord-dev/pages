====== H2903: טוֹפָפָה (ṭôwphâphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **go around** or **bind**;

===== Meaning =====

a **fillet** for the forehead

===== Definitions =====

  - go around
  - bind
  - fillet

===== Usage =====

frontlet.
