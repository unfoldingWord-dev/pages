====== G4377: προσφωνέω (prosphōnéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g5455|G5455]];

===== Meaning =====

to sound towards, i.e. address, exclaim, summon

===== Usage =====

call unto, speak (un-)to.
