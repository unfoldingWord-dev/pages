====== H4159: מוֹפֵת (môwphêth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h3302|H3302]] in the sense of **conspicuousness**;

===== Meaning =====

a **miracle**; by implication, a **token** or **omen**

===== Definitions =====

  - conspicuousness
  - miracle
  - token
  - omen

===== Usage =====

miracle, sign, wonder(-ed at).
