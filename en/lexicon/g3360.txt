====== G3360: μέχρι (méchri) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3372|G3372]];

===== Meaning =====

as far as, i.e. up to a certain point (as a preposition, of extent (denoting the terminus, whereas

===== Usage =====

till, (un-)to, until.
