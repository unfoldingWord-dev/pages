====== G2368: θυμίαμα (thymíama) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2370|G2370]];

===== Meaning =====

an aroma, i.e. fragrant powder burnt in religious service; by implication, the burning itself

===== Usage =====

incense, odour.
