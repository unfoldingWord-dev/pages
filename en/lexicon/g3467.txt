====== G3467: μυωπάζω (myōpázō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3466|G3466]] and ὤψ (ṓps) (the face; from [[en:lexicon:g3700|G3700]]);

===== Meaning =====

to shut the eyes, i.e. blink (see indistinctly)

===== Usage =====

 cannot see far off.
