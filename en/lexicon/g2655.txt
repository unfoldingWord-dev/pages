====== G2655: καταναρκάω (katanarkáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and ναρκάω (narkáō) (to be numb);

===== Meaning =====

to grow utterly torpid, i.e. (by implication) slothful (figuratively, expensive)

===== Usage =====

be burdensome (chargeable).
