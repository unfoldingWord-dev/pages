====== G3523: νῆστις (nēstis) ======

===== Source =====

from the inseparable negative particle νή- (nḗ-) (not) and [[en:lexicon:g2068|G2068]];

===== Meaning =====

not eating, i.e. abstinent from food (religiously)

===== Usage =====

fasting.
