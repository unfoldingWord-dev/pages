====== H7613: שְׂאֵת (sᵉʼêth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5375|H5375]];

===== Meaning =====

an **elevation** or **leprous scab**; figuratively, **elation** or **cheerfulness**; **exaltation** in rank or character

===== Definitions =====

  - elevation
  - leprous scab
  - elation
  - cheerfulness
  - exaltation

===== Usage =====

be accepted, dignity, excellency, highness, raise up self, rising.
