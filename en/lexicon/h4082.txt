====== H4082: מְדִינָה (mᵉdîynâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1777|H1777]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **judgeship**, i.e. **jurisdiction**; by implication, a **district** (as ruled by a judge); generally, a **region**

===== Definitions =====

  - judgeship
  - jurisdiction
  - district
  - region

===== Usage =====

(× every) province.
