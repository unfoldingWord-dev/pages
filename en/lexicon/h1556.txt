====== H1556: גָּלַל (gâlal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **roll** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - roll

===== Usage =====

commit, remove, roll (away, down, together), run down, seek occasion, trust, wallow.
