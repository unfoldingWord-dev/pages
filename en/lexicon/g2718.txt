====== G2718: κατέρχομαι (katérchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2064|G2064]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to come (or go) down (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

come (down), depart, descend, go down, land.
