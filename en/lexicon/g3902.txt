====== G3902: παράσημος (parásēmos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g4591|G4591]];

===== Meaning =====

side-marked, i.e. labelled (with a badge (figure-head) of a ship)

===== Usage =====
