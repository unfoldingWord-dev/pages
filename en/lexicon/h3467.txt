====== H3467: יָשַׁע (yâshaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be open**, **wide** or **free**, i.e. (by implication) to **be safe**; causatively, to **free** or **succor**

===== Definitions =====

  - be open
  - wide
  - free
  - be safe
  - free
  - succor

===== Usage =====

× at all, avenging, defend, deliver(-er), help, preserve, rescue, be safe, bring (having) salvation, save(-iour), get victory.
