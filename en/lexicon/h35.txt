====== H35: אֲבִיּוֹנָה (ʼăbîyôwnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h14|H14]];

===== Meaning =====

**provocative of desire**; the **caper berry** (from its stimulative taste)

===== Definitions =====

  - provocative of desire
  - caper berry

===== Usage =====

desire.
