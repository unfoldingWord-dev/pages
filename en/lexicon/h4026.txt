====== H4026: מִגְדָּל (migdâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

also (in plural) feminine [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1431|H1431]];

===== Meaning =====

a **tower** (from its size or height); by analogy, a **rostrum**; figuratively, a (pyramidal) **bed of flowers**

===== Definitions =====

  - tower
  - rostrum
  - bed of flowers

===== Usage =====

castle, flower, tower. Compare the names following.
