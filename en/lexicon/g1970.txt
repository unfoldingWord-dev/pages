====== G1970: ἐπιπνίγω (epipnígō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g4155|G4155]];

===== Meaning =====

to throttle upon, i.e. (figuratively) overgrow
