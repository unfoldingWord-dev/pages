====== G1918: ἐπιγαμβρεύω (epigambreúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1062|G1062]];

===== Meaning =====

to form affinity with, i.e. (specially) in a levirate way

===== Usage =====

marry.
