====== H5458: סְגוֹר (çᵉgôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5462|H5462]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **shut up**, i.e. the **breast** (as enclosing the heart); also **gold** (as generally shut up safely)

===== Definitions =====

  - shut up
  - breast
  - gold

===== Usage =====

caul, gold.
