====== H6617: פָּתַל (pâthal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **twine**, i.e. (literally) to **struggle** or (figuratively) be (morally) **tortuous**

===== Definitions =====

  - twine
  - struggle
  - tortuous

===== Usage =====

(shew self) froward, shew self unsavoury, wrestle.
