====== G5114: τομώτερος (tomṓteros) ======

===== Source =====

comparative of a derivative of the primary τέμνω (témnō) (to cut; more
 comprehensive or decisive than [[en:lexicon:g2875|G2875]], as if by a single stroke; whereas
 that implies repeated blows, like hacking);

===== Meaning =====

more keen

===== Usage =====

sharper.
