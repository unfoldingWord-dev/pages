====== G809: ἀσχήμων (aschḗmōn) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]] (in
 the sense of its congener [[en:lexicon:g4976|G4976]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, shapeless, i.e. (figuratively) inelegant

===== Usage =====

uncomely.
