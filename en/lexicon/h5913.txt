====== H5913: עָכַס (ʻâkaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to tie, specifically, with fetters; but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h5914|H5914]];

===== Meaning =====

to **put on anklets**

===== Definitions =====

  - put on anklets

===== Usage =====

make a tinkling ornament.
