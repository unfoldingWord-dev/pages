====== G2634: κατακυριεύω (katakyrieúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2961|G2961]];

===== Meaning =====

to lord against, i.e. control, subjugate

===== Usage =====

exercise dominion over (lordship), be lord over, overcome.
