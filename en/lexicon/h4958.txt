====== H4958: מַשְׂרֵת (masrêth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from an unused root meaning to **perforate**, i.e. **hollow out**;

===== Meaning =====

a **pan**

===== Definitions =====

  - perforate
  - hollow out
  - pan

===== Usage =====

pan.
