====== H3680: כָּסָה (kâçâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **plump**, i.e. **fill up** hollows; by implication, to **cover** (for clothing or secrecy)

===== Definitions =====

  - plump
  - fill up
  - cover

===== Usage =====

clad self, close, clothe, conceal, cover (self), (flee to) hide, overwhelm. Compare
