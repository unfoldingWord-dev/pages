====== H8582: תָּעָה (tâʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **vacillate**, i.e. **reel** or **stray** (literally or figuratively); also causative of both

===== Definitions =====

  - vacillate
  - reel
  - stray

===== Usage =====

(cause to) go astray, deceive, dissemble, (cause to, make to) err, pant, seduce, (make to) stagger, (cause to) wander, be out of the way.
