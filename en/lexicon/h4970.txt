====== H4970: מָתַי (mâthay) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to extend;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **extent** (of time); but used only adverbially (especially with other particle prefixes), **when** (either relative or interrogative)

===== Definitions =====

  - extent
  - when

===== Usage =====

long, when.
