====== H1360: גֶּבֶא (gebeʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning probably to **collect**;

===== Meaning =====

a **reservoir**; by analogy, a **marsh**

===== Definitions =====

  - collect
  - reservoir
  - marsh

===== Usage =====

marish, pit.
