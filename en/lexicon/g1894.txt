====== G1894: ἐπειδή (epeidḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1893|G1893]] and [[en:lexicon:g1211|G1211]];

===== Meaning =====

since now, i.e. (of time) when, or (of cause) whereas

===== Usage =====

after that, because, for (that, -asmuch as), seeing, since.
