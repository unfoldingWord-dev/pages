====== H7054: קָמָה (qâmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of active participle of [[en:lexicon:h6965|H6965]];

===== Meaning =====

something that **rises**, i.e. a **stalk** of grain

===== Definitions =====

  - rises
  - stalk

===== Usage =====

(standing) corn, grown up, stalk.
