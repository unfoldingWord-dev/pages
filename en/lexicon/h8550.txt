====== H8550: תֻּמִּים (Tummîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

plural of [[en:lexicon:h8537|H8537]];

===== Meaning =====

**perfections**, i.e. (techn.) one of the epithets of the objects in the high-priest's breastplate as an emblem of complete Truth

===== Definitions =====

  - perfections

===== Usage =====

Thummim.
