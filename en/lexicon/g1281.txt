====== G1281: διαπραγματεύομαι (diapragmateúomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g4231|G4231]];

===== Meaning =====

to thoroughly occupy oneself, i.e. (transitively and by implication) to earn in business

===== Usage =====

gain by trading.
