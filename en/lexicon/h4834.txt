====== H4834: מָרַץ (mârats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **press**, i.e. (figuratively) to **be pungent** or **vehement**; to **irritate**

===== Definitions =====

  - press
  - be pungent
  - vehement
  - irritate

===== Usage =====

embolden, be forcible, grievous, sore.
