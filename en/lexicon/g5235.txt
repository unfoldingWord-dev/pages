====== G5235: ὑπερβάλλω (hyperbállō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]] and [[en:lexicon:g906|G906]];

===== Meaning =====

to throw beyod the usual mark, i.e. (figuratively) to surpass (only active participle supereminent)

===== Usage =====

exceeding, excel, pass.
