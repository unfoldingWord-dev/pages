====== G3670: ὁμολογέω (homologéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3674|G3674]] and [[en:lexicon:g3056|G3056]];

===== Meaning =====

to assent, i.e. covenant, acknowledge

===== Usage =====

con- (pro-)fess, confession is made, give thanks, promise.
