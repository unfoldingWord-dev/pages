====== H8081: שֶׁמֶן (shemen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8080|H8080]];

===== Meaning =====

**grease**, especially liquid (as from the olive, often perfumed); figuratively, **richness**

===== Definitions =====

  - grease
  - richness

===== Usage =====

anointing, × fat (things), × fruitful, oil(-ed), ointment, olive,   pine.
