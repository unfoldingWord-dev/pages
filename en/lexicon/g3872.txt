====== G3872: παρακαταθήκη (parakatathḗkē) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g2698|G2698]];

===== Meaning =====

something put down alongside, i.e. a deposit (sacred trust)

===== Usage =====

that (thing) which is committed (un-)to (trust).
