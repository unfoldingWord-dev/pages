====== G3504: νεόφυτος (neóphytos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3501|G3501]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5453|G5453]];

===== Meaning =====

newly planted, i.e. (figuratively) a young convert ("neophyte")

===== Usage =====

novice.
