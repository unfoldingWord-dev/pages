====== G4483: ῥέω (rhéō) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps akin (or identical) with [[en:lexicon:g4482|G4482]] (through the idea of pouring
 forth);

===== Meaning =====

to utter, i.e. speak or say

===== Usage =====

command, make, say, speak (of).
