====== H3835: לָבַן (lâban) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h3843|H3843]]

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (or **become**) **white**; to **make bricks**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - become
  - white
  - make bricks

===== Usage =====

make brick, be (made, make) white(-r).
