====== H3700: כָּסַף (kâçaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **become pale**, i.e. (by implication) to **pine after**; also to **fear**

===== Definitions =====

  - become pale
  - pine after
  - fear

===== Usage =====

(have) desire, be greedy, long, sore.
