====== G1294: διαστρέφω (diastréphō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g4762|G4762]];

===== Meaning =====

to distort, i.e. (figuratively) misinterpret, or (morally) corrupt

===== Usage =====

perverse(-rt), turn away.
