====== H3496: יַתְנִיאֵל (Yathnîyʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to endure, and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; **continued of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jathniel**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - continued of God
  - Jathniel

===== Usage =====

Jathniel.
