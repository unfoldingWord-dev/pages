====== H3032: יָדַד (yâdad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare [[en:lexicon:h3034|H3034]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **handle**, i.e. to **throw**, e.g. **lots**

===== Definitions =====

  - handle
  - throw
  - lots

===== Usage =====

cast.
