====== G3846: παραβάλλω (parabállō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g906|G906]];

===== Meaning =====

to throw alongside, i.e. (reflexively) to reach a place, or (figuratively) to liken

===== Usage =====

arrive, compare.
