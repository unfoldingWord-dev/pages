====== G3344: μεταστρέφω (metastréphō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g4762|G4762]];

===== Meaning =====

to turn across, i.e. transmute or (figuratively) corrupt

===== Usage =====

pervert, turn.
