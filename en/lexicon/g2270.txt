====== G2270: ἡσυχάζω (hēsycházō) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g2272|G2272]];

===== Meaning =====

to keep still (intransitively), i.e. refrain from labor, meddlesomeness or speech

===== Usage =====

cease, hold peace, be quiet, rest.
