====== H1839: דָּנִי (Dânîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

patronymically from [[en:lexicon:h1835|H1835]];

===== Meaning =====

a **Danite** (often collectively) or descendants (or inhabitants) of Dan

===== Definitions =====

  - Danite

===== Usage =====

Danites, of Daniel
