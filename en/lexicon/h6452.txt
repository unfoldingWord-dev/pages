====== H6452: פָּסַח (pâçach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **hop**, i.e. (figuratively) **skip over** (or spare); by implication, to **hesitate**; also (literally) to **limp**, to **dance**

===== Definitions =====

  - hop
  - skip over
  - hesitate
  - limp
  - dance

===== Usage =====

halt, become lame, leap, pass over.
