====== H759: אַרְמוֹן (ʼarmôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to **be elevated**);

===== Meaning =====

a **citadel** (from its **height**)

===== Definitions =====

  - be elevated
  - citadel
  - height

===== Usage =====

castle, palace. Compare
