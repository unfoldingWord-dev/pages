====== H5435: סֹבֶא (çôbeʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5433|H5433]];

===== Meaning =====

**potation**, concretely (**wine**), or abstractly (**carousal**)

===== Definitions =====

  - potation
  - wine
  - carousal

===== Usage =====

drink, drunken, wine.
