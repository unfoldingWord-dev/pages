====== H2111: זוּעַ (zûwaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **shake off**, i.e. (figuratively) to **agitate** (as with fear)

===== Definitions =====

  - shake off
  - agitate

===== Usage =====

move, tremble, vex.
