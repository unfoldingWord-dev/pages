====== H637: אַף (ʼaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

particle

===== Source =====

a primitive particle;

===== Meaning =====

meaning **accession** (used as an adverb or conjunction); also or **yea**; adversatively **though**

===== Definitions =====

  - accession
  - yea
  - though

===== Usage =====

also, + although, and (furthermore, yet), but, even, + how much less (more, rather than), moreover, with, yea.
