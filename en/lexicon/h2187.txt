====== H2187: זָנַק (zânaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **draw together the feet** (as an animal about to dart upon its prey), i.e. to **spring forward**

===== Definitions =====

  - draw together the feet
  - spring forward

===== Usage =====

leap.
