====== H3399: יָרַט (yâraṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **precipitate** or **hurl** (**rush**) headlong; (intransitively) to **be rash**

===== Definitions =====

  - precipitate
  - hurl
  - rush
  - be rash

===== Usage =====

be perverse, turn over.
