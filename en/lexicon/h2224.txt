====== H2224: זָרַח (zârach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **irradiate** (or shoot forth beams), i.e. to **rise** (as the sun); specifically, to **appear** (as a symptom of leprosy)

===== Definitions =====

  - irradiate
  - rise
  - appear

===== Usage =====

arise, rise (up), as soon as it is up.
