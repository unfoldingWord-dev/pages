====== H6982: קוֹרָה (qôwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6979|H6979]];

===== Meaning =====

a **rafter** (forming trenches as it were); by implication, a **roof**

===== Definitions =====

  - rafter
  - roof

===== Usage =====

beam, roof.
