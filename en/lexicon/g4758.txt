====== G4758: στρατολογέω (stratologéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of the base of [[en:lexicon:g4756|G4756]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]] (in its original sense);

===== Meaning =====

to gather (or select) as a warrior, i.e. enlist in the army

===== Usage =====

choose to be a soldier.
