====== H4962: מַת (math) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h4970|H4970]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, an **adult** (as of full length); by implication, a **man** (only in the plural)

===== Definitions =====

  - adult
  - man

===== Usage =====

  few, × friends, men, persons, × small.
