====== G5230: ὑπέρακμος (hypérakmos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g188|G188]];

===== Meaning =====

beyond the "acme", i.e. figuratively (of a daughter) past the bloom (prime) of youth

===== Usage =====

+ pass the flower of (her) age.
