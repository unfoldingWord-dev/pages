====== H2530: חָמַד (châmad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **delight in**

===== Definitions =====

  - delight in

===== Usage =====

beauty, greatly beloved, covet, delectable thing, (× great) delight, desire, goodly, lust, (be) pleasant (thing), precious (thing).
