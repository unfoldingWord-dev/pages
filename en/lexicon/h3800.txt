====== H3800: כֶּתֶם (kethem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3799|H3799]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **carved** out, i.e. **ore**; hence, **gold** (pure as originally mined)

===== Definitions =====

  - carved
  - ore
  - gold

===== Usage =====

((most) fine, pure) gold(-en wedge).
