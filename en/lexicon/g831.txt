====== G831: αὐθεντέω (authentéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g846|G846]] and an obsolete ἕντης (héntēs) (a worker);

===== Meaning =====

to act of oneself, i.e. (figuratively) dominate

===== Usage =====

usurp authority over.
