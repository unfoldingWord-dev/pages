====== H7595: שְׁאֵלָא (shᵉʼêlâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:lexicon:h7593|H7593]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **question** (at law), i.e. **judicial decision** or **mandate**

===== Definitions =====

  - question
  - judicial decision
  - mandate

===== Usage =====

demand.
