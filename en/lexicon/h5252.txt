====== H5252: נְסִבָּה (nᵉçibbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine participle passive of [[en:lexicon:h5437|H5437]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, an **environment**, i.e. **circumstance** or **turn of affairs**

===== Definitions =====

  - environment
  - circumstance
  - turn of affairs

===== Usage =====

cause.
