====== H4730: מִקְטֶרֶת (miqṭereth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4729|H4729]];

===== Meaning =====

something **to fume** (incense) **in**, i.e. a **coal-pan**

===== Definitions =====

  - to fume
  - in
  - coal-pan

===== Usage =====

censer.
