====== G3879: παρακύπτω (parakýptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g2955|G2955]];

===== Meaning =====

to bend beside, i.e. lean over (so as to peer within)

===== Usage =====

look (into), stoop down.
