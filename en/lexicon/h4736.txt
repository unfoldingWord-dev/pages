====== H4736: מִקְנָה (miqnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4735|H4735]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **buying**, i.e. **acquisition**; concretely, a piece of **property** (land or living); also the **sum** paid

===== Definitions =====

  - buying
  - acquisition
  - property
  - sum

===== Usage =====

(he that is) bought, possession, piece, purchase.
