====== G411: ἀνεκδιήγητος (anekdiḗgētos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1555|G1555]];

===== Meaning =====

not expounded in full, i.e. indescribable

===== Usage =====

unspeakable.
