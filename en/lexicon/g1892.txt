====== G1892: ἐπεγείρω (epegeírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g1453|G1453]];

===== Meaning =====

to rouse upon, i.e. (figuratively) to excite against

===== Usage =====

raise, stir up.
