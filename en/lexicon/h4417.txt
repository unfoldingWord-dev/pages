====== H4417: מֶלַח (melach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4414|H4414]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **powder**, i.e. (specifically) **salt** (as easily pulverized and dissolved)

===== Definitions =====

  - powder
  - salt

===== Usage =====

salt(-pit).
