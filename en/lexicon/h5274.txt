====== H5274: נָעַל (nâʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also denominative from [[en:lexicon:h5275|H5275]]

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **fasten** up, i.e. with a bar or cord; hence, to **sandal**, i.e. furnish with slippers

===== Definitions =====

  - fasten
  - sandal

===== Usage =====

bolt, inclose, lock, shoe, shut up.
