====== G3038: λιθόστρωτος (lithóstrōtos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3037|G3037]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4766|G4766]];

===== Meaning =====

stone-strewed, i.e. a tessellated mosaic on which the Roman tribunal was placed

===== Usage =====

Pavement.
