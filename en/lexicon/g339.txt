====== G339: ἀνακαθίζω (anakathízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g2523|G2523]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to set up, i.e. (reflexively) to sit up

===== Usage =====

sit up.
