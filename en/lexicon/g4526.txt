====== G4526: σάκκος (sákkos) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin ([[en:lexicon:h08242|H08242]]);

===== Meaning =====

"sack"-cloth, i.e. mohair (the material or garments made of it, worn as a sign of grief)

===== Usage =====

 sackcloth.
