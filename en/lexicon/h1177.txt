====== H1177: בַּעַל חָנָן (Baʻal Chânân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1167|H1167]] and [[en:lexicon:h2603|H2603]]; **possessor of grace**;

===== Meaning =====

**Baal-Chanan**, the name of an Edomite, also of an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - possessor of grace
  - Baal-Chanan

===== Usage =====

Baal-hanan.
