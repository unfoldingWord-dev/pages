====== H3920: לָכַד (lâkad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **catch** (in a net, trap or pit); generally, to **capture** or occupy; also to **choose** (by lot); figuratively, to **cohere**

===== Definitions =====

  - catch
  - capture
  - choose
  - cohere

===== Usage =====

× at all, catch (self), be frozen, be holden, stick together, take.
