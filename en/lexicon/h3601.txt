====== H3601: כִּישׁוֹר (kîyshôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3787|H3787]]; compare [[en:lexicon:h6418|H6418]]

===== Meaning =====

literally a **director**, i.e. the **spindle** or **shank of a distaff**, by which it is twirled

===== Definitions =====

  - director
  - spindle
  - shank of a distaff

===== Usage =====

spindle.
