====== H3551: כַּו (kav) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h3854|H3854]] in the sense of piercing;

===== Meaning =====

a **window** (as a perforation)

===== Definitions =====

  - window

===== Usage =====

window.
