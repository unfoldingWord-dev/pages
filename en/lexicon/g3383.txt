====== G3383: μήτε (mḗte) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3361|G3361]] and [[en:lexicon:g5037|G5037]];

===== Meaning =====

not too, i.e. (in continued negation) neither or nor; also, not even

===== Usage =====

neither, (n-)or, so as much.
