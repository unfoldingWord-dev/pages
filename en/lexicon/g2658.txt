====== G2658: καταντάω (katantáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]];

===== Meaning =====

to meet against, i.e. arrive at (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

attain, come.
