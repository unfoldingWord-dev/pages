====== G3356: μετριοπαθέω (metriopathéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3357|G3357]] and [[en:lexicon:g3806|G3806]];

===== Meaning =====

to be moderate in passion, i.e. gentle (to treat indulgently)

===== Usage =====

have compassion.
