====== H8285: שֵׁרָה (shêrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8324|H8324]] in its original sense of **pressing**;

===== Meaning =====

a wrist-**band** (as **compact** or **clasping**)

===== Definitions =====

  - pressing
  - band
  - compact
  - clasping

===== Usage =====

bracelet.
