====== H5309: נֶפֶל (nephel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h5307|H5307]];

===== Meaning =====

something **fallen**, i.e. an **abortion**

===== Definitions =====

  - fallen
  - abortion

===== Usage =====

untimely birth.
