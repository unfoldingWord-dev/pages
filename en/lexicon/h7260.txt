====== H7260: רַבְרַב (rabrab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:lexicon:h7229|H7229]];

===== Meaning =====

**huge** (in size); **domineering** (in character)

===== Definitions =====

  - huge
  - domineering

===== Usage =====

(very) great (things).
