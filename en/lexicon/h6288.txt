====== H6288: פְּאֹרָה (pᵉʼôrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6286|H6286]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **ornamentation**, i.e. (plural) **foliage** (including the limbs) as **bright green**

===== Definitions =====

  - ornamentation
  - foliage
  - bright green

===== Usage =====

bough, branch, sprig.
