===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

properly, feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h3245|H3245]];

**something founded**, i.e. **a foundation**

===== Definitions =====

  - something founded
  - a foundation

===== Usage =====

foundation.
