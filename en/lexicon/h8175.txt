====== H8175: שָׂעַר (sâʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **storm**; by implication, to **shiver**, i.e. **fear**

===== Definitions =====

  - storm
  - shiver
  - fear

===== Usage =====

be (horribly) afraid, fear, hurl as a storm, be tempestuous, come like (take away as with) a whirlwind.
