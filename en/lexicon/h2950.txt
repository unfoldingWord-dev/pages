====== H2950: טָפַל (ṭâphal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **stick on as a patch**; figuratively, to **impute falsely**

===== Definitions =====

  - stick on as a patch
  - impute falsely

===== Usage =====

forge(-r), sew up.
