====== G3975: παχύνω (pachýnō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4078|G4078]] (meaning thick);

===== Meaning =====

to thicken, i.e. (by implication) to fatten (figuratively, stupefy or render callous)

===== Usage =====

wax gross.
