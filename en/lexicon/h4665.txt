====== H4665: מִפְרֶקֶת (miphreqeth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6561|H6561]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **fracture**, i.e. **joint** (vertebrae) of the neck

===== Definitions =====

  - fracture
  - joint

===== Usage =====

neck.
