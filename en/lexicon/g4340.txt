====== G4340: πρόσκαιρος (próskairos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g2540|G2540]];

===== Meaning =====

for the occasion only, i.e. temporary

===== Usage =====

dur-(eth) for awhile, endure for a time, for a season, temporal.
