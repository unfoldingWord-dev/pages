====== H8398: תֵּבֵל (têbêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2986|H2986]];

===== Meaning =====

the **earth** (as **moist** and therefore inhabited); by extension, the **globe**; by implication, its **inhabitants**; specifically, a particular **land**, as Babylonia, Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - earth
  - moist
  - globe
  - inhabitants
  - land

===== Usage =====

habitable part, world.
