====== H6945: קָדֵשׁ (qâdêsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6942|H6942]];

===== Meaning =====

a (quasi) **sacred person**, i.e. (technically) a (male) **devotee** (by prostitution) to **licentious idolatry**

===== Definitions =====

  - sacred person
  - devotee
  - licentious idolatry

===== Usage =====

sodomite, unclean.
