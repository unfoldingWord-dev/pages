====== H5716: עֲדִי (ʻădîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5710|H5710]] in the sense of **trappings**;

===== Meaning =====

**finery**; generally an **outfit**; specifically, a **headstall**

===== Definitions =====

  - trappings
  - finery
  - outfit
  - headstall

===== Usage =====

× excellent, mouth, ornament.
