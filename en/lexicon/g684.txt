====== G684: ἀπώλεια (apṓleia) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g622|G622]];

===== Meaning =====

ruin or loss (physical, spiritual or eternal)

===== Usage =====

damnable(-nation), destruction, die, perdition, X perish, pernicious ways, waste.
