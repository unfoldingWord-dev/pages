====== H1356: גֵּב (gêb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1461|H1461]];

===== Meaning =====

a **log** (as cut out); also **well** or **cistern** (as dug)

===== Definitions =====

  - log
  - well
  - cistern

===== Usage =====

beam, ditch, pit.
