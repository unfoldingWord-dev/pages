====== H8672: תֵּשַׁע (têshaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

or (masculine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; perhaps from [[en:lexicon:h8159|H8159]] through the idea of a turn to the next or full number ten;

===== Meaning =====

**nine** or (ordinal) **ninth**

===== Definitions =====

  - nine
  - ninth

===== Usage =====

nine (  -teen,   -teenth, -th).
