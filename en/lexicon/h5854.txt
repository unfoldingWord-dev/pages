====== H5854: עַטְרוֹת בֵּית יוֹאָב (ʻAṭrôwth bêyth Yôwʼâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5852|H5852]] and [[en:lexicon:h1004|H1004]] and [[en:lexicon:h3097|H3097]]; crowns of the house of Joab;

===== Meaning =====

**Atroth-beth-Joab**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Atroth-beth-Joab

===== Usage =====

Ataroth the house of Joab.
