====== H1434: גְּדִל (gᵉdil) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1431|H1431]] (in the sense of twisting);

===== Meaning =====

**thread**, i.e. a **tassel** or **festoon**

===== Definitions =====

  - thread
  - tassel
  - festoon

===== Usage =====

fringe, wreath.
