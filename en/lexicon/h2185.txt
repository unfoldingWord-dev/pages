====== H2185: זֹנוֹת (zônôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

regarded by some as if from [[en:lexicon:h2109|H2109]] or an unused root, and applied to military equipments; but evidently the feminine plural active participle of [[en:lexicon:h2181|H2181]];

===== Meaning =====

**harlots**

===== Definitions =====

  - harlots

===== Usage =====

armour.
