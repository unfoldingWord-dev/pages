====== G4620: σιτόμετρον (sitómetron) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4621|G4621]] and [[en:lexicon:g3358|G3358]];

===== Meaning =====

a grain-measure, i.e. (by implication) ration (allowance of food)

===== Usage =====

portion of meat.
