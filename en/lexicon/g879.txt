====== G879: ἀφυπνόω (aphypnóō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g5258|G5258]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to become awake, i.e. (by implication) to drop (off) in slumber

===== Usage =====

fall asleep.
