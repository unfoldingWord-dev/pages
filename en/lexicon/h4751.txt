====== H4751: מַר (mar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective - masculine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h4843|H4843]];

===== Meaning =====

**bitter** (literally or figuratively); also (as noun) **bitterness**, or (adverbially) **bitterly**

===== Definitions =====

  - bitter
  - bitterness
  - bitterly

===== Usage =====

  angry, bitter(-ly, -ness), chafed, discontented, × great, heavy.
