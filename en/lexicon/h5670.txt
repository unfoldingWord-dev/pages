====== H5670: עָבַט (ʻâbaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pawn**; causatively, to **lend** (on security); figuratively, to **entangle**

===== Definitions =====

  - pawn
  - lend
  - entangle

===== Usage =====

borrow, break (ranks), fetch (a pledge), lend, × surely.
