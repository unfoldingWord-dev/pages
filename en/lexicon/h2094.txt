====== H2094: זָהַר (zâhar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **gleam**; figuratively, to **enlighten** (by caution)

===== Definitions =====

  - gleam
  - enlighten

===== Usage =====

admonish, shine, teach, (give) warn(-ing).
