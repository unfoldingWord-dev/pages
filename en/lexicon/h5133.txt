====== H5133: נוֹצָה (nôwtsâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine active participle of [[en:lexicon:h5327|H5327]] in the sense of **flying**;

===== Meaning =====

a **pinion** (or wing feather); often (collectively) **plumage**

===== Definitions =====

  - flying
  - pinion
  - plumage

===== Usage =====

feather(-s), ostrich.
