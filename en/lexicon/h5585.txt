====== H5585: סָעִיף (çâʻîyph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5586|H5586]];

===== Meaning =====

a **fissure** (of rocks); also a **bough** (as **subdivided**)

===== Definitions =====

  - fissure
  - bough
  - subdivided

===== Usage =====

(outmost) branch, clift, top.
