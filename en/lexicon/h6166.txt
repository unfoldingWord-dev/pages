====== H6166: עֲרָד (ʻĂrâd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to sequester itself; **fugitive**;

===== Meaning =====

**Arad**, the name of a place near Palestine, also of a Canaanite and an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - fugitive
  - Arad

===== Usage =====

Arad.
