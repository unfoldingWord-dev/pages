====== H7952: שָׁלָה (shâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably identical with [[en:lexicon:h7953|H7953]] through the idea of educing);

===== Meaning =====

**to mislead**

===== Definitions =====

  - to mislead

===== Usage =====

deceive, be negligent.
