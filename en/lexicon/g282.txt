====== G282: ἀμήτωρ (amḗtōr) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g3384|G3384]];

===== Meaning =====

motherless, i.e. of unknown maternity

===== Usage =====

without mother.
