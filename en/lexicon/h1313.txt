====== H1313: בָּשָׂם (bâsâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be fragrant; (compare [[en:lexicon:h5561|H5561]])

===== Meaning =====

**the balsam plant**

===== Definitions =====

  - the balsam plant

===== Usage =====

spice.
