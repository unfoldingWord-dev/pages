====== G1837: ἐξηχέομαι (exēchéomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g2278|G2278]];

===== Meaning =====

to "echo" forth, i.e. resound (be generally reported)

===== Usage =====

sound forth.
