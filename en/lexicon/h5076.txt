====== H5076: נָדֻד (nâdud) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h5074|H5074]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **tossed**; abstractly, a **rolling** (on the bed)

===== Definitions =====

  - tossed
  - rolling

===== Usage =====

tossing to and fro.
