====== G5498: χειρόγραφον (cheirógraphon) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5495|G5495]] and [[en:lexicon:g1125|G1125]];

===== Meaning =====

something hand-written ("chirograph"), i.e. a manuscript (specially, a legal document or bond (figuratively))

===== Usage =====

handwriting.
