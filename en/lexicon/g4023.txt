====== G4023: περιέχω (periéchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

to hold all around, i.e. include, clasp (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

+ astonished, contain, after (this manner).
