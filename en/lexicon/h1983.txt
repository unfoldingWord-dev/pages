====== H1983: הֲלָךְ (hălâk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:lexicon:h1981|H1981]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **journey**, i.e. (by implication) **toll on goods at a road**

===== Definitions =====

  - journey
  - toll on goods at a road

===== Usage =====

custom.
