====== H6285: פֵּאָה (pêʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h6311|H6311]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **mouth** in a figurative sense, i.e. **direction**, **region**, **extremity**

===== Definitions =====

  - mouth
  - direction
  - region
  - extremity

===== Usage =====

corner, end, quarter, side.
