====== H330: אֵטוּן (ʼêṭûwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (probably meaning to bind);

===== Meaning =====

properly, **twisted** (yarn), i.e. **tapestry**

===== Definitions =====

  - twisted
  - tapestry

===== Usage =====

fine linen.
