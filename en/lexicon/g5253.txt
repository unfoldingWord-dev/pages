====== G5253: ὑπερῷον (hyperōion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]];

===== Meaning =====

a higher part of the house, i.e. apartment in the third story

===== Usage =====

upper chamber (room).
