====== G2550: κακοήθεια (kakoḗtheia) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2556|G2556]] and [[en:lexicon:g2239|G2239]];

===== Meaning =====

bad character, i.e. (specially) mischievousness

===== Usage =====

malignity.
