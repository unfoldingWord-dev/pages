====== H70: אֹבֶן (ʼôben) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h68|H68]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pair of stones** (only dual); a **potter's wheel** or a **midwife's stool** (consisting alike of two horizontal disks with a support between)

===== Definitions =====

  - pair of stones
  - potter's wheel
  - midwife's stool

===== Usage =====

wheel, stool.
