====== G5484: χάριν (chárin) ======

===== Source =====

accusative case of [[en:lexicon:g5485|G5485]] as preposition;

===== Meaning =====

through favor of, i.e. on account of

===== Usage =====

be-(for) cause of, for sake of, +…fore, X reproachfully.
