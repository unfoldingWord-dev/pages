====== G2873: κόπος (kópos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2875|G2875]];

===== Meaning =====

a cut, i.e. (by analogy) toil (as reducing the strength), literally or figuratively; by implication, pains

===== Usage =====

labour, + trouble, weariness.
