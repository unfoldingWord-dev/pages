====== H3746: כָּרִי (kârîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

perhaps an abridged plural of [[en:lexicon:h3733|H3733]] in the sense of leader (of the flock);

===== Meaning =====

a **life-guardsman**

===== Definitions =====

  - life-guardsman

===== Usage =====

captains, Cherethites (from the margin).
