====== H7140: קֶרַח (qerach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h7139|H7139]];

===== Meaning =====

**ice** (as if bald, i.e. smooth); hence, **hail**; by resemblance, **rock crystal**

===== Definitions =====

  - ice
  - hail
  - rock crystal

===== Usage =====

crystal, frost, ice.
