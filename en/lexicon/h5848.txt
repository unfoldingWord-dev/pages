====== H5848: עָטַף (ʻâṭaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **shroud**, i.e. **clothe** (whether transitive or reflex.); hence (from the idea of **darkness**) to **languish**

===== Definitions =====

  - shroud
  - clothe
  - darkness
  - languish

===== Usage =====

cover (over), fail, faint, feebler, hide self, be overwhelmed, swoon.
