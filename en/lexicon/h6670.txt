====== H6670: צָהַל (tsâhal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **gleam**, i.e. (figuratively) **be cheerful**; by transference to **sound** clear (of various animal or human expressions)

===== Definitions =====

  - gleam
  - be cheerful
  - sound

===== Usage =====

bellow, cry aloud (out), lift up, neigh, rejoice, make to shine, shout.
