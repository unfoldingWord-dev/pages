====== H5214: נִיר (nîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a root probably identical with that of [[en:lexicon:h5216|H5216]], through the idea of the gleam of a fresh furrow;

===== Meaning =====

to **till the soil**

===== Definitions =====

  - till the soil

===== Usage =====

break up.
