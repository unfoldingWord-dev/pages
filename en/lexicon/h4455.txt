====== H4455: מַלְקוֹחַ (malqôwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3947|H3947]];

===== Meaning =====

transitively (in dual) the **jaws** (as taking food); intransitively, **spoil** [and captives] (as taken)

===== Definitions =====

  - jaws
  - spoil

===== Usage =====

booty, jaws, prey.
