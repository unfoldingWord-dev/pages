====== G3931: παρηγορία (parēgoría) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g58|G58]] (meaning to harangue an
 assembly);

===== Meaning =====

an address alongside, i.e. (specially), consolation

===== Usage =====

comfort.
