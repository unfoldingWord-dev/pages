====== G2255: ἥμισυ (hḗmisy) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a derivative from an inseparable prefix akin to [[en:lexicon:g260|G260]] (through
 the idea of partition involved in connection) and meaning semi-;

===== Meaning =====

(as noun) half
