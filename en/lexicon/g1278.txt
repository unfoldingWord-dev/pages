====== G1278: διαπονέω (diaponéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4192|G4192]];

===== Meaning =====

to toil through, i.e. (passively) be worried

===== Usage =====

be grieved.
