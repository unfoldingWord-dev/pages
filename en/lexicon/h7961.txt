====== H7961: שָׁלֵו (shâlêv) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h7951|H7951]];

===== Meaning =====

**tranquil**; (in a bad sense) **careless**; abstractly, **security**

===== Definitions =====

  - tranquil
  - careless
  - security

===== Usage =====

(being) at ease, peaceable, (in) prosper(-ity), quiet(-ness), wealthy.
