====== G1494: εἰδωλόθυτον (eidōlóthyton) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1497|G1497]] and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2380|G2380]];

===== Meaning =====

an image-sacrifice, i.e. part of an idolatrous offering

===== Usage =====

(meat, thing that is) offered (in sacrifice, sacrificed) to (unto) idols.
