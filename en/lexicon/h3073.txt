====== H3073: יְהֹוָה שָׁלוֹם (Yᵉhôvâh shâlôwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3068|H3068]] and [[en:lexicon:h7965|H7965]]; Jehovah (is) peace;

===== Meaning =====

**Jehovah-Shalom**, a symbolical name of an altar in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Jehovah-Shalom

===== Usage =====

Jehovah-shalom.
