====== G4730: στενοχωρία (stenochōría) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4728|G4728]] and [[en:lexicon:g5561|G5561]];

===== Meaning =====

narrowness of room, i.e. (figuratively) calamity

===== Usage =====

anguish, distress.
