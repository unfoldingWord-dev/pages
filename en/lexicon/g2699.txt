====== G2699: κατατομή (katatomḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and τέμνω (témnō) (to cut);

===== Meaning =====

a cutting down (off), i.e. mutilation (ironically)

===== Usage =====

concision.
