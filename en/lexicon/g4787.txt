====== G4787: συγκινέω (synkinéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4682|G4682]] and [[en:lexicon:g2795|G2795]];

===== Meaning =====

to move together, i.e. (specially), to excite as a mass (to sedition)

===== Usage =====

stir up.
