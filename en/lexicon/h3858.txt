====== H3858: לַהַט (lahaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3857|H3857]];

===== Meaning =====

a **blaze**; also (from the idea of enwrapping) **magic** (as covert)

===== Definitions =====

  - blaze
  - magic

===== Usage =====

flaming, enchantment.
