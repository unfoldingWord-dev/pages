====== G106: ἄζυμος (ázymos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g2219|G2219]];

===== Meaning =====

unleavened, i.e. (figuratively) uncorrupted; (in the neutral plural) specially (by implication) the Passover week

===== Usage =====

unleavened (bread).
