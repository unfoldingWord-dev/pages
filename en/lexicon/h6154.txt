====== H6154: עֵרֶב (ʻêreb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (1 Kings 10:15), (with the article prefix), from [[en:lexicon:h6148|H6148]];

===== Meaning =====

the **web** (or transverse threads of cloth); also a **mixture**, (or **mongrel** race)

===== Definitions =====

  - web
  - mixture
  - mongrel

===== Usage =====

Arabia, mingled people, mixed (multitude), woof.
