====== G509: ἄνωθεν (ánōthen) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g507|G507]];

===== Meaning =====

from above; by analogy, from the first; by implication, anew

===== Usage =====

from above, again, from the beginning (very first), the top.
