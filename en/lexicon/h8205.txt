====== H8205: שְׁפִי (shᵉphîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8192|H8192]];

===== Meaning =====

**bareness**; concretely, a **bare** hill or plain

===== Definitions =====

  - bareness
  - bare

===== Usage =====

high place, stick out.
