====== G4755: στρατηγός (stratēgós) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4756|G4756]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]] or [[en:lexicon:g2233|G2233]];

===== Meaning =====

a general, i.e. (by implication or analogy) a (military) governor (prætor), the chief (præfect) of the (Levitical) temple-wardens

===== Usage =====

captain, magistrate.
