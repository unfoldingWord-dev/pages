====== H5712: עֵדָה (ʻêdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h5707|H5707]] in the original sense of **fixture**;

===== Meaning =====

a stated **assemblage** (specifically, a **concourse**, or generally, a **family** or **crowd**)

===== Definitions =====

  - fixture
  - assemblage
  - concourse
  - family
  - crowd

===== Usage =====

assembly, company, congregation, multitude, people, swarm. Compare
