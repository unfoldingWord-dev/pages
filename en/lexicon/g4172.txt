====== G4172: πόλις (pólis) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:lexicon:g4171|G4171]], or perhaps from [[en:lexicon:g4183|G4183]];

===== Meaning =====

a town (properly, with walls, of greater or less size)

===== Usage =====

city.
