====== H5586: סָעַף (çâʻaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to divide up; but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h5585|H5585]],

===== Meaning =====

to **disbranch** (a tree)

===== Definitions =====

  - disbranch

===== Usage =====

top.
