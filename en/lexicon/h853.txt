====== H853: אֵת (ʼêth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

particle

===== Source =====

apparent contracted from [[en:lexicon:h226|H226]] in the demonstrative sense of **entity**;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **self** (but generally used to point out more definitely the object of a verb or preposition, **even** or **namely**)

===== Definitions =====

  - entity
  - self
  - even
  - namely

===== Usage =====

[as such unrepresented in English].
