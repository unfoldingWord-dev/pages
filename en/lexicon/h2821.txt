====== H2821: חָשַׁךְ (châshak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be dark** (as **withholding** light); transitively, to **darken**

===== Definitions =====

  - be dark
  - withholding
  - darken

===== Usage =====

be black, be (make) dark, darken, cause darkness, be dim, hide.
