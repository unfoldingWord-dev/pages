====== G729: ἄῤῥαφος (árrhaphos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of the same
 as [[en:lexicon:g4476|G4476]];

===== Meaning =====

unsewed, i.e. of a single piece

===== Usage =====

without seam.
