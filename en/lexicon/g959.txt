====== G959: Βερνίκη (Berníkē) ======

===== Source =====

from a provincial form of [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]] and [[en:lexicon:g3529|G3529]];

===== Meaning =====

victorious; Bernice, a member of the Herodian family
