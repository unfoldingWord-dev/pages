====== H6131: עָקַר (ʻâqar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pluck** up (especially by the roots); specifically, to **hamstring**; figuratively, to **exterminate**

===== Definitions =====

  - pluck
  - hamstring
  - exterminate

===== Usage =====

dig down, hough, pluck up, root up.
