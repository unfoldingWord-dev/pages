====== G5358: φιλάγαθος (philágathos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5384|G5384]] and [[en:lexicon:g18|G18]];

===== Meaning =====

fond to good, i.e. a promoter of virtue

===== Usage =====

love of good men.
