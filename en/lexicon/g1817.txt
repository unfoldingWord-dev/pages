====== G1817: ἐξανίστημι (exanístēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g450|G450]];

===== Meaning =====

objectively, to produce, i.e. (figuratively) beget; subjectively, to arise, i.e. (figuratively) object

===== Usage =====

raise (rise) up.
