====== H2840: חִשֻּׁר (chishshur) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to bind together;

===== Meaning =====

**combined**, i.e. the **nave** or **hub** of a wheel (as holding the spokes together)

===== Definitions =====

  - combined
  - nave
  - hub

===== Usage =====

spoke.
