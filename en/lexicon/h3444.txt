====== H3444: יְשׁוּעָה (yᵉshûwʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h3467|H3467]];

===== Meaning =====

something **saved**, i.e. (abstractly) **deliverance**; hence, **aid**, **victory**, **prosperity**

===== Definitions =====

  - saved
  - deliverance
  - aid
  - victory
  - prosperity

===== Usage =====

deliverance, health, help(-ing), salvation, save, saving (health), welfare.
