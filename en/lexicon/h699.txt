====== H699: אֲרֻבָּה (ʼărubbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine participle passive of [[en:lexicon:h693|H693]] (as if for lurking);

===== Meaning =====

a **lattice**; (by implication) a **window**, **dovecot** (because of the pigeon-holes), **chimney** (with its apertures for smoke), **sluice** (with openings for water)

===== Definitions =====

  - lattice
  - window
  - dovecot
  - chimney
  - sluice

===== Usage =====

chimney, window.
