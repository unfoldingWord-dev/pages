====== G5389: φιλοτιμέομαι (philotiméomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5384|G5384]] and [[en:lexicon:g5092|G5092]];

===== Meaning =====

to be fond of honor, i.e. emulous (eager or earnest to do something)

===== Usage =====

labour, strive, study.
