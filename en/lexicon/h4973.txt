====== H4973: מְתַלְּעָה (mᵉthallᵉʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

contr. from [[en:lexicon:h3216|H3216]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **biter**, i.e. a **tooth**

===== Definitions =====

  - biter
  - tooth

===== Usage =====

cheek (jaw) tooth, jaw.
