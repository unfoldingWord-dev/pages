====== H7281: רֶגַע (regaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7280|H7280]].

===== Meaning =====

a **wink** (of the eyes), i.e. a very **short space** of time

===== Definitions =====

  - wink
  - short space

===== Usage =====

instant, moment, space, suddenly.
