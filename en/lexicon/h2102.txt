====== H2102: זוּד (zûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or (by permutation) [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **seethe**; figuratively, to **be insolent**

===== Definitions =====

  - seethe
  - be insolent

===== Usage =====

be proud, deal proudly, presume, (come) presumptuously, sod.
