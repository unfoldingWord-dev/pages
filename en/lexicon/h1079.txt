====== H1079: בָּל (bâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:lexicon:h1080|H1080]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **anxiety**, i.e. (by implication) the **heart** (as its seat)

===== Definitions =====

  - anxiety
  - heart

===== Usage =====

heart.
