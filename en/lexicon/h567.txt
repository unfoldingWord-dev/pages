====== H567: אֱמֹרִי (ʼĔmôrîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably a patronymic from an unused name derived from [[en:lexicon:h559|H559]] in the sense of **publicity**, i.e. prominence; thus, a **mountaineer**;

===== Meaning =====

an **Emorite**, one of the Canaanitish tribes

===== Definitions =====

  - publicity
  - mountaineer
  - Emorite

===== Usage =====

Amorite.
