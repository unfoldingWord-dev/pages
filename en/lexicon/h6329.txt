====== H6329: פּוּק (pûwq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root [rather identical with [[en:lexicon:h6328|H6328]] through the idea of **dropping** out; compare [[en:lexicon:h5312|H5312]]];

===== Meaning =====

to **issue**, i.e. **furnish**; causatively, to **secure**; figuratively, to **succeed**

===== Definitions =====

  - dropping
  - issue
  - furnish
  - secure
  - succeed

===== Usage =====

afford, draw out, further, get, obtain.
