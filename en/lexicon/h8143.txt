====== H8143: שֶׁנְהַבִּים (shenhabbîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8127|H8127]] and the plural apparently of a foreign word;

===== Meaning =====

probably, **tooth of elephants**, i.e. **ivory tusk**

===== Definitions =====

  - tooth of elephants
  - ivory tusk

===== Usage =====

ivory.
