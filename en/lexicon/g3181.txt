====== G3181: μεθόριος (methórios) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g3725|G3725]];

===== Meaning =====

bounded alongside, i.e. contiguous (neuter plural as noun, frontier)

===== Usage =====

border.
