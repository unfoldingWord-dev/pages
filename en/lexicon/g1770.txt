====== G1770: ἐννεύω (enneúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g3506|G3506]];

===== Meaning =====

to nod at, i.e. beckon or communicate by gesture

===== Usage =====

make signs.
