====== H2555: חָמָס (châmâç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2554|H2554]];

===== Meaning =====

**violence**; by implication, **wrong**; by metonymy **unjust gain**

===== Definitions =====

  - violence
  - wrong
  - unjust gain

===== Usage =====

cruel(-ty), damage, false, injustice, × oppressor, unrighteous, violence (against, done), violent (dealing), wrong.
