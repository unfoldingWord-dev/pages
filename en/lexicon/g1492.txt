====== G1492: εἴδω (eídō) ======

===== Source =====

a primary verb;

===== Meaning =====

used only in certain past tenses, the others being borrowed from the equivalent

===== Usage =====

be aware, behold, X can (+ not tell), consider, (have) know(-ledge), look (on), perceive, see, be sure, tell, understand, wish, wot.
