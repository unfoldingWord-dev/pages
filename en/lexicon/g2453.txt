====== G2453: Ἰουδαῖος (Ioudaîos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2448|G2448]] (in the sense of [[en:lexicon:g2455|G2455]] as a country);

===== Meaning =====

Judæan, i.e. belonging to Jehudah

===== Usage =====

Jew(-ess), of Judæa.
