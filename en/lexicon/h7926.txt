====== H7926: שְׁכֶם (shᵉkem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7925|H7925]];

===== Meaning =====

the **neck** (between the shoulders) as the place of burdens; figuratively, the **spur** of a hill

===== Definitions =====

  - neck
  - spur

===== Usage =====

back, × consent, portion, shoulder.
