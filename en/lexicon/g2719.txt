====== G2719: κατεσθίω (katesthíō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2068|G2068]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to eat down, i.e. devour (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

devour.
