====== G1988: ἐπιστάτης (epistátēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]];

===== Meaning =====

an appointee over, i.e. commander (teacher)

===== Usage =====

master.
