====== G4259: προαύλιον (proaúlion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g833|G833]];

===== Meaning =====

a forecourt, i.e. vestibule (alley-way)

===== Usage =====

porch.
