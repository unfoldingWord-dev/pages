====== H8443: תּוֹעָפָה (tôwʻâphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3286|H3286]];

===== Meaning =====

(only in plural collective) **weariness**, i.e. (by implication) **toil** (**treasure** so obtained) or **speed**

===== Definitions =====

  - weariness
  - toil
  - treasure
  - speed

===== Usage =====

plenty, strength.
