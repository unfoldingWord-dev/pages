====== G622: ἀπόλλυμι (apóllymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g3639|G3639]];

===== Meaning =====

to destroy fully (reflexively, to perish, or lose), literally or figuratively

===== Usage =====

destroy, die, lose, mar, perish.
