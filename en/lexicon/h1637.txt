====== H1637: גֹּרֶן (gôren) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to smooth;

===== Meaning =====

a **threshing-floor** (as made even); by analogy, any **open area**

===== Definitions =====

  - threshing-floor
  - open area

===== Usage =====

(barn, corn, threshing-) floor, (threshing-, void) place.
