====== G4352: προσκυνέω (proskynéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and a probable derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2965|G2965]] (meaning to kiss, like a
 dog licking his master's hand);

===== Meaning =====

to fawn or crouch to, i.e. (literally or figuratively) prostrate oneself in homage (do reverence to, adore)

===== Usage =====

worship.
