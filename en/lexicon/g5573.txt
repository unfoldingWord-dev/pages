====== G5573: ψευδολόγος (pseudológos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5571|G5571]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]];

===== Meaning =====

mendacious, i.e. promulgating erroneous Christian doctrine

===== Usage =====

speaking lies.
