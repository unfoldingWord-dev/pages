====== H1019: בֵּית הַגִּלְגָּל (Bêyth hag-Gilgâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1004|H1004]] and [[en:lexicon:h1537|H1537]] with the article interposed; house of Gilgal (or rolling);

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-hag-Gilgal**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-hag-Gilgal

===== Usage =====

Beth-gilgal.
