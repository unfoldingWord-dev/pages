====== H6064: עָנַשׁ (ʻânash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **urge**; by implication, to **inflict a penalty**, specifically, to **fine**

===== Definitions =====

  - urge
  - inflict a penalty
  - fine

===== Usage =====

amerce, condemn, punish, × surely.
