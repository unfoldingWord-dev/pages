====== H6418: פֶּלֶךְ (pelek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be round;

===== Meaning =====

a **circuit** (i.e. district); also a **spindle** (as whirled); hence, a **crutch**

===== Definitions =====

  - circuit
  - spindle
  - crutch

===== Usage =====

(di-) staff, participle
