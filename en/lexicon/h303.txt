====== H303: אַחְלָב (ʼAchlâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the same root as [[en:lexicon:h2459|H2459]]; fatness (i.e. fertile);

===== Meaning =====

**Achlab**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Achlab

===== Usage =====

Ahlab.
