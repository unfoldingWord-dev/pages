====== G2734: κατοπτρίζομαι (katoptrízomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3700|G3700]] (compare
 [[en:lexicon:g2072|G2072]]);

===== Meaning =====

to mirror oneself, i.e. to see reflected (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

behold as in a glass.
