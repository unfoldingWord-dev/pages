====== G4390: προτρέχω (protréchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g5143|G5143]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to run forward, i.e. outstrip, precede

===== Usage =====

outrun, run before.
