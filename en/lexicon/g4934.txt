====== G4934: συντίθεμαι (syntíthemai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

to place jointly, i.e. (figuratively) to consent (bargain, stipulate), concur

===== Usage =====

agree, assent, covenant.
