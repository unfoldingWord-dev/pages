====== G3424: μογιλάλος (mogilálos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3425|G3425]] and [[en:lexicon:g2980|G2980]];

===== Meaning =====

hardly talking, i.e. dumb (tongue-tied)

===== Usage =====

having an impediment in his speech.
