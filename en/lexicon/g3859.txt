====== G3859: παραδιατριβή (paradiatribḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g1304|G1304]];

===== Meaning =====

misemployment, i.e. meddlesomeness

===== Usage =====

perverse disputing.
