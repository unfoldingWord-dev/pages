====== G2371: θυμομαχέω (thymomachéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g2372|G2372]] and [[en:lexicon:g3164|G3164]];

===== Meaning =====

to be in a furious fight, i.e. (figuratively) to be exasperated

===== Usage =====

be highly displeased.
