====== H1548: גָּלַח (gâlach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be bald**, i.e. (causatively) to **shave**; figuratively to **lay waste**

===== Definitions =====

  - be bald
  - shave
  - lay waste

===== Usage =====

poll, shave (off).
