====== G2438: ἱμάς (himás) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from the same as [[en:lexicon:g260|G260]];

===== Meaning =====

a strap, i.e. (specially) the tie (of a sandal) or the lash (of a scourge)

===== Usage =====

latchet, thong.
