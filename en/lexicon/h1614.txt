====== H1614: גׇּפְרִית (gophrîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

probably feminine of [[en:lexicon:h1613|H1613]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **cypress-resin**; by analogy, **sulphur** (as equally inflammable)

===== Definitions =====

  - cypress-resin
  - sulphur

===== Usage =====

brimstone.
