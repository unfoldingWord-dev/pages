====== H5782: עוּר (ʻûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather identical with through the idea of **opening** the eyes);

===== Meaning =====

to **wake** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - opening
  - wake

===== Usage =====

(a-) wake(-n, up), lift up (self), × master, raise (up), stir up (self).
