====== H4863: מִשְׁאֶרֶת (mishʼereth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7604|H7604]] in the original sense of **swelling**;

===== Meaning =====

a **kneading-trough** (in which the dough rises)

===== Definitions =====

  - swelling
  - kneading-trough

===== Usage =====

kneading trough, store.
