====== G4634: σκηνοπηγία (skēnopēgía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4636|G4636]] and [[en:lexicon:g4078|G4078]];

===== Meaning =====

the Festival of Tabernacles (so called from the custom of erecting booths for temporary homes)

===== Usage =====

tabernacles.
