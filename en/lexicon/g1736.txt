====== G1736: ἐνδημέω (endēméō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g1218|G1218]];

===== Meaning =====

to be in one's own country, i.e. home (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

be at home (present).
