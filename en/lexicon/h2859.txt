====== H2859: חָתַן (châthan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **give** (a daughter) **away in marriage**; hence (generally) to **contract affinity by marriage**

===== Definitions =====

  - give
  - away in marriage
  - contract affinity by marriage

===== Usage =====

join in affinity, father in law, make marriages, mother in law, son in law.
