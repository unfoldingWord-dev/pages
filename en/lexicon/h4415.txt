====== H4415: מְלַח (mᵉlach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h4414|H4414]];

===== Meaning =====

to **eat** salt, i.e. (generally) **subsist**

===== Definitions =====

  - eat
  - subsist

===== Usage =====

  have maintenance.
