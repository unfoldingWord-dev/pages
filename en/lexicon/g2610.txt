====== G2610: καταγωνίζομαι (katagōnízomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g75|G75]];

===== Meaning =====

to struggle against, i.e. (by implication) to overcome

===== Usage =====

subdue.
