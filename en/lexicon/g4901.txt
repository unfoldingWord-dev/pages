====== G4901: συνεπιμαρτυρέω (synepimartyréō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g1957|G1957]];

===== Meaning =====

to testify further jointly, i.e. unite in adding evidence

===== Usage =====

also bear witness.
