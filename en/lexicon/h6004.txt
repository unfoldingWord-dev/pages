====== H6004: עָמַם (ʻâmam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **associate**; by implication, to **overshadow** (by **huddling** together)

===== Definitions =====

  - associate
  - overshadow
  - huddling

===== Usage =====

become dim, hide.
