====== G3781: ὀφειλέτης (opheilétēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3784|G3784]];

===== Meaning =====

an ower, i.e. person indebted; figuratively, a delinquent; morally, a transgressor (against God)

===== Usage =====

debtor, which owed, sinner.
