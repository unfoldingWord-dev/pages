====== H7973: שֶׁלַח (shelach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7971|H7971]];

===== Meaning =====

a **missile** of attack, i.e. **spear**; also (figuratively) a **shoot** of growth; i.e. **branch**

===== Definitions =====

  - missile
  - spear
  - shoot
  - branch

===== Usage =====

dart, plant, × put off, sword, weapon.
