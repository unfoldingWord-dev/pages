====== G4216: ποταμοφόρητος (potamophórētos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4215|G4215]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5409|G5409]];

===== Meaning =====

river-borne, i.e. overwhelmed by a stream

===== Usage =====

carried away of the flood.
