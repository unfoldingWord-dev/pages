====== H1729: דּוּגָה (dûwgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1728|H1728]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **fishery**, i.e. a **hook for fishing**

===== Definitions =====

  - fishery
  - hook for fishing

===== Usage =====

fish (hook).
