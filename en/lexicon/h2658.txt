====== H2658: חָפַר (châphar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **pry into**; by implication, to **delve**, to **explore**

===== Definitions =====

  - pry into
  - delve
  - explore

===== Usage =====

dig, paw, search out, seek.
