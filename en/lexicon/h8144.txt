====== H8144: שָׁנִי (shânîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

of uncertain derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**crimson**, properly, the insect or its color, also stuff dyed with it

===== Definitions =====

  - crimson

===== Usage =====

crimson, scarlet (thread).
