====== H3946: לַקּוּם (Laqqûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from an unused root thought to mean to stop up by a barricade; perhaps fortification;

===== Meaning =====

**Lakkum**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Lakkum

===== Usage =====

Lakum.
