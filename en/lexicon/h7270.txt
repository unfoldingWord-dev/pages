====== H7270: רָגַל (râgal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;	also as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7272|H7272]]

===== Meaning =====

to **walk along**; but only in specifically, applications, to **reconnoiter**, to **be a tale-bearer** (i.e. slander); to **lead about**

===== Definitions =====

  - walk along
  - reconnoiter
  - be a tale-bearer
  - lead about

===== Usage =====

backbite, search, slander, (e-) spy (out), teach to go, view.
