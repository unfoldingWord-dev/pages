====== G4027: περικάθαρμα (perikátharma) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2508|G2508]];

===== Meaning =====

something cleaned off all around, i.e. refuse (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

filth.
