====== H1061: בִּכּוּר (bikkûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1069|H1069]];

===== Meaning =====

the **first-fruits** of the crop

===== Definitions =====

  - first-fruits

===== Usage =====

first fruit (-ripe (figuratively)), hasty fruit.
