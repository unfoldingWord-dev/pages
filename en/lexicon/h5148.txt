====== H5148: נָחָה (nâchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **guide**; by implication, to **transport** (into exile, or as colonists)

===== Definitions =====

  - guide
  - transport

===== Usage =====

bestow, bring, govern, guide, lead (forth), put, straiten.
