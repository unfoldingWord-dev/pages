====== H5645: עָב (ʻâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(masculine and feminine); from [[en:lexicon:h5743|H5743]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, an **envelope**, i.e. **darkness** (or **density**, 2 Chronicles 4:17); specifically, a (scud) **cloud**; also a **copse**

===== Definitions =====

  - envelope
  - darkness
  - density
  - cloud
  - copse

===== Usage =====

clay, (thick) cloud, × thick, thicket. Compare
