====== H1644: גָּרַשׁ (gârash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **drive out** from a possession; especially to **expatriate** or **divorce**

===== Definitions =====

  - drive out
  - expatriate
  - divorce

===== Usage =====

cast up (out), divorced (woman), drive away (forth, out), expel, × surely put away, trouble, thrust out.
