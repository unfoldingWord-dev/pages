====== G1693: ἐμμαίνομαι (emmaínomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g3105|G3105]];

===== Meaning =====

to rave on, i.e. rage at

===== Usage =====

be mad against.
