====== G3072: Λυκαονιστί (Lykaonistí) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3071|G3071]];

===== Meaning =====

Lycaonistically, i.e. in the language of the Lycaonians

===== Usage =====

in the speech of Lycaonia.
