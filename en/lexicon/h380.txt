====== H380: אִישׁוֹן (ʼîyshôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

diminutive from [[en:lexicon:h376|H376]];

===== Meaning =====

the **little man** of the eye; the **pupil** or **ball**; hence, the **middle** (of night)

===== Definitions =====

  - little man
  - pupil
  - ball
  - middle

===== Usage =====

apple (of the eye), black, obscure.
