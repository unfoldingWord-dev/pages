====== H3665: כָּנַע (kânaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **bend** the knee; hence, to **humiliate**, **vanquish**

===== Definitions =====

  - bend
  - humiliate
  - vanquish

===== Usage =====

bring down (low), into subjection, under, humble (self), subdue.
