====== G679: ἄπταιστος (áptaistos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4417|G4417]];

===== Meaning =====

not stumbling, i.e. (figuratively) without sin

===== Usage =====

from falling.
