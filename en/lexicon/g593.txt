====== G593: ἀποδοκιμάζω (apodokimázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g1381|G1381]];

===== Meaning =====

to disapprove, i.e. (by implication) to repudiate

===== Usage =====

disallow, reject.
