====== H8654: תִּרְעָתִי (Tirʻâthîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

patrial from an unused name meaning gate;

===== Meaning =====

a **Tirathite** or inhabitant of an unknown Tirah

===== Definitions =====

  - Tirathite

===== Usage =====

Tirathite.
