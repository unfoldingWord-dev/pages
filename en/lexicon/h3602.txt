====== H3602: כָּכָה (kâkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3541|H3541]];

===== Meaning =====

**just so**, referring to the previous or following context

===== Definitions =====

  - just so

===== Usage =====

after that (this) manner, this matter, (even) so, in such a case, thus.
