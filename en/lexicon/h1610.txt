====== H1610: גַּף (gaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **arch**;

===== Meaning =====

the **back**; by extension the **body** or self

===== Definitions =====

  - arch
  - back
  - body

===== Usage =====

  highest places, himself.
