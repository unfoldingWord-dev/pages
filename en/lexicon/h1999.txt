====== H1999: הֲמֻלָּה (hămullâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (too fully) [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Jeremiah 11:16), feminine passive participle of an unused root meaning to rush (as rain with a windy roar);

===== Meaning =====

a **sound**

===== Definitions =====

  - sound

===== Usage =====

speech, tumult.
