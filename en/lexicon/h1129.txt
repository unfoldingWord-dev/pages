====== H1129: בָּנָה (bânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **build** (literally and figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - build

===== Usage =====

(begin to) build(-er), obtain children, make, repair, set (up), × surely.
