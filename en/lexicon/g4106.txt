====== G4106: πλάνη (plánē) ======

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:g4108|G4108]] (as abstractly);

===== Meaning =====

objectively, fraudulence; subjectively, a straying from orthodoxy or piety

===== Usage =====

deceit, to deceive, delusion, error.
