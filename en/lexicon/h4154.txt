====== H4154: מוּעֶדֶת (mûwʻedeth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h4571|H4571]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **made to slip**, i.e. **dislocated**

===== Definitions =====

  - made to slip
  - dislocated

===== Usage =====

out of joint.
