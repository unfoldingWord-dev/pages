====== H2685: חֲצַף (chătsaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **shear** or **cut close**; figuratively, to **be severe**

===== Definitions =====

  - shear
  - cut close
  - be severe

===== Usage =====

hasty, be urgent.
