====== H6875: צְרִי (tsᵉrîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to crack (as by pressure), hence, to leak;

===== Meaning =====

**distillation**, i.e. **balsam**

===== Definitions =====

  - distillation
  - balsam

===== Usage =====

balm.
