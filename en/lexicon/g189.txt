====== G189: ἀκοή (akoḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g191|G191]];

===== Meaning =====

hearing (the act, the sense or the thing heard)

===== Usage =====

audience, ear, fame, which ye heard, hearing, preached, report, rumor.
