====== G4391: προϋπάρχω (proÿpárchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g5225|G5225]];

===== Meaning =====

to exist before, i.e. (adverbially) to be or do something previously

===== Usage =====

+ be before(-time).
