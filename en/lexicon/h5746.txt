====== H5746: עוּג (ʻûwg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to gyrate; but used only as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h5692|H5692]],

===== Meaning =====

to **bake** (round cakes on the hearth)

===== Definitions =====

  - bake

===== Usage =====

bake.
