====== H471: אֶלְיָקִים (ʼElyâqîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]] and [[en:lexicon:h6965|H6965]]; **God of raising**;

===== Meaning =====

**Eljakim**, the name of four Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - God of raising
  - Eljakim

===== Usage =====

Eliakim.
