====== G1927: ἐπιδημέω (epidēméō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g1218|G1218]];

===== Meaning =====

to make oneself at home, i.e. (by extension) to reside (in a foreign country)

===== Usage =====

(be) dwelling (which were) there, stranger.
