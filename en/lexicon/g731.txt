====== G731: ἄῤῥητος (árrhētos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and the same as [[en:lexicon:g4490|G4490]];

===== Meaning =====

unsaid, i.e. (by implication) inexpressible

===== Usage =====

unspeakable.
