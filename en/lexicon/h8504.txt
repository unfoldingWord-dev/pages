====== H8504: תְּכֵלֶת (tᵉkêleth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

probably for [[en:lexicon:h7827|H7827]];

===== Meaning =====

the cerulean **mussel**, i.e. the color (**violet**) obtained therefrom or stuff dyed therewith

===== Definitions =====

  - mussel
  - violet

===== Usage =====

blue.
