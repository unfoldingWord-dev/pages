====== H3895: לְחִי (lᵉchîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **be soft**;

===== Meaning =====

the **cheek** (from its **fleshiness**); hence, the **jaw**-bone

===== Definitions =====

  - be soft
  - cheek
  - fleshiness
  - jaw

===== Usage =====

cheek (bone), jaw (bone).
