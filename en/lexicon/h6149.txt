====== H6149: עָרֵב (ʻârêb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather identical with [[en:lexicon:h6148|H6148]] through the idea of close association);

===== Meaning =====

to be **agreeable**

===== Definitions =====

  - agreeable

===== Usage =====

be pleasant(-ing), take pleasure in, be sweet.
