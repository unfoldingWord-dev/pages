====== G1562: ἐκδύω (ekdýō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g1416|G1416]];

===== Meaning =====

to cause to sink out of, i.e. (specially as of clothing) to divest

===== Usage =====

strip, take off from, unclothe.
