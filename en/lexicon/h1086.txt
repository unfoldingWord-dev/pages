====== H1086: בָּלָה (bâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **fail**; by implication to **wear out**, **decay** (causatively, **consume**, **spend**)

===== Definitions =====

  - fail
  - wear out
  - decay
  - consume
  - spend

===== Usage =====

consume, enjoy long, become (make, wax) old, spend, waste.
