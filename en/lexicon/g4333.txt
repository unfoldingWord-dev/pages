====== G4333: προσεργάζομαι (prosergázomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g2038|G2038]];

===== Meaning =====

to work additionally, i.e. (by implication) acquire besides

===== Usage =====

gain.
