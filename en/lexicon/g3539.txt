====== G3539: νοιέω (noiéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3563|G3563]];

===== Meaning =====

to exercise the mind (observe), i.e. (figuratively) to comprehend, heed

===== Usage =====

consider, perceive, think, understand.
