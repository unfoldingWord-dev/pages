====== H4551: מַסָּע (maççâʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5265|H5265]] in the sense of **projecting**;

===== Meaning =====

a **missile** (spear or arrow); also a **quarry** (whence stones are, as it were, **ejected**)

===== Definitions =====

  - projecting
  - missile
  - quarry
  - ejected

===== Usage =====

before it was brought, dart.
