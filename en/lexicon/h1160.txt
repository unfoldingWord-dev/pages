====== H1160: בְּעוֹר (Bᵉʻôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1197|H1197]] (in the sense of burning); a **lamp**;

===== Meaning =====

**Beor**, the name of the father of an Edomitish king; also of that of Balaam

===== Definitions =====

  - lamp
  - Beor

===== Usage =====

Beor.
