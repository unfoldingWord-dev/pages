====== H1854: דָּקַק (dâqaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h1915|H1915]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **crush** (or intransitively) **crumble**

===== Definitions =====

  - crush
  - crumble

===== Usage =====

beat in pieces (small), bruise, make dust, (into) × powder, (be, very) small, stamp (small).
