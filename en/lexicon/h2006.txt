====== H2006: הֵן (hên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

interjection

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h2005|H2005]];

===== Meaning =====

**lo!** also **there**(-fore), (un-) **less**, **whether**, **but**, **if**

===== Definitions =====

  - lo!
  - there
  - less
  - whether
  - but
  - if

===== Usage =====

(that) if, or, whether.
