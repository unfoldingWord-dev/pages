====== H1486: גּוֹרָל (gôwrâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to be rough (as stone);

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **pebble**, i.e. a **lot** (small stones being used for that purpose); figuratively, a **portion** or **destiny** (as if determined by lot)

===== Definitions =====

  - pebble
  - lot
  - portion
  - destiny

===== Usage =====

lot.
