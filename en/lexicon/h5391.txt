====== H5391: נָשַׁךְ (nâshak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **strike** with a sting (as a serpent); figuratively, to **oppress** with interest on a loan

===== Definitions =====

  - strike
  - oppress

===== Usage =====

bite, lend upon usury.
