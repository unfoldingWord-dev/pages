====== H7468: רְעוּת (rᵉʻûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7462|H7462]] in the sense of [[en:lexicon:h7453|H7453]];

===== Meaning =====

a female **associate**; generally an **additional** one

===== Definitions =====

  - associate
  - additional

===== Usage =====

  another, mate, neighbour.
