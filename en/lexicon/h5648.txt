====== H5648: עֲבַד (ʻăbad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h5647|H5647]];

===== Meaning =====

to **do**, **make**, **prepare**, **keep**, etc.

===== Definitions =====

  - do
  - make
  - prepare
  - keep

===== Usage =====

× cut, do, execute, go on, make, move, work.
