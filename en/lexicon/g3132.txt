====== G3132: μαντεύομαι (manteúomai) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3105|G3105]] (meaning a prophet, as supposed to rave
 through inspiration);

===== Meaning =====

to divine, i.e. utter spells (under pretense of foretelling

===== Usage =====

by soothsaying.
