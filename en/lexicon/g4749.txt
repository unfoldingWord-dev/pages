====== G4749: στολή (stolḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4724|G4724]];

===== Meaning =====

equipment, i.e. (specially), a "stole" or long-fitting gown (as a mark of dignity)

===== Usage =====

long clothing (garment), (long) robe.
