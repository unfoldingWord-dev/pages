====== G4522: σαγήνη (sagḗnē) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of σάττω (sáttō) (to equip) meaning furniture, especially a
 pack-saddle (which in the East is merely a bag of netted rope);

===== Meaning =====

a "seine" for fishing

===== Usage =====

net.
