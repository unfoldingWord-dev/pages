====== G4764: στρῆνος (strēnos) ======

===== Source =====

akin to [[en:lexicon:g4731|G4731]];

===== Meaning =====

a "straining", "strenuousness" or "strength", i.e. (figuratively) luxury (voluptuousness)

===== Usage =====

delicacy.
