====== H6817: צָעַק (tsâʻaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **shriek**; (by implication) to **proclaim** (an assembly)

===== Definitions =====

  - shriek
  - proclaim

===== Usage =====

× at all, call together, cry (out), gather (selves) (together).
