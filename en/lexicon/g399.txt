====== G399: ἀναφέρω (anaphérō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]];

===== Meaning =====

to take up (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

bear, bring (carry, lead) up, offer (up).
