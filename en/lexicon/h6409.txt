====== H6409: פַּלְטִיאֵל (Palṭîyʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6404|H6404]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; **deliverance of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Paltiel**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - deliverance of God
  - Paltiel

===== Usage =====

Paltiel, Phaltiel.
