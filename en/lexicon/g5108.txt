====== G5108: τοιοῦτος (toioûtos) ======

===== Source =====

(including the other inflections); from [[en:lexicon:g5104|G5104]] and [[en:lexicon:g3778|G3778]];

===== Meaning =====

truly this, i.e. of this sort (to denote character or individuality)

===== Usage =====

like, such (an one).
