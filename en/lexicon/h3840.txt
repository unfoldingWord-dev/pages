====== H3840: לִבְנָה (libnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3835|H3835]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **whiteness**, i.e. (by implication) **transparency**

===== Definitions =====

  - whiteness
  - transparency

===== Usage =====

paved.
