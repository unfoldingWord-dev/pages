====== H8257: שָׁקַע (shâqaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(abbreviated Amos 8:8); a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **subside**; by implication, to **be overflowed**, **cease**; causatively, to **abate**, **subdue**

===== Definitions =====

  - subside
  - be overflowed
  - cease
  - abate
  - subdue

===== Usage =====

make deep, let down, drown, quench, sink.
