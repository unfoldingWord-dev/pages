====== G4650: σκορπίζω (skorpízō) ======

===== Source =====

apparently from the same as [[en:lexicon:g4651|G4651]] (through the idea of penetrating);

===== Meaning =====

to dissipate, i.e. (figuratively) put to flight, waste, be liberal

===== Usage =====

disperse abroad, scatter (abroad).
