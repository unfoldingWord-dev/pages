====== H1157: בְּעַד (bᵉʻad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

preposition

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5704|H5704]] with prepositional prefix;

===== Meaning =====

**in up to** or **over against**; generally **at**, **beside**, **among**, **behind**, **for**, etc.

===== Definitions =====

  - in up to
  - over against
  - at
  - beside
  - among
  - behind
  - for

===== Usage =====

about, at by (means of), for, over, through, up (-on), within.
