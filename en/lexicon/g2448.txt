====== G2448: Ἰουδά (Ioudá) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin ([[en:lexicon:h03063|H03063]] or perhaps [[en:lexicon:h03194|H03194]]);

===== Meaning =====

Judah (i.e. Jehudah or Juttah), a part of (or place in) Palestine

===== Usage =====

Judah.
