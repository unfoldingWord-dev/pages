====== G4312: προπετής (propetḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g4098|G4098]];

===== Meaning =====

falling forward, i.e. headlong (figuratively, precipitate)

===== Usage =====

heady, rash(-ly).
