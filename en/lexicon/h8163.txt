====== H8163: שָׂעִיר (sâʻîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjectivenoun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h8175|H8175]];

===== Meaning =====

**shaggy**; as noun, a **he-goat**; by analogy, a **faun**

===== Definitions =====

  - shaggy
  - he-goat
  - faun

===== Usage =====

devil, goat, hairy, kid, rough, satyr.
