====== G761: ἀσάλευτος (asáleutos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4531|G4531]];

===== Meaning =====

unshaken, i.e. (by implication) immovable (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

which cannot be moved, unmovable.
