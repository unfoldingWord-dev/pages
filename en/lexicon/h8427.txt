====== H8427: תָּוָה (tâvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **mark out**, i.e. (primitive) **scratch** or (definite) **imprint**

===== Definitions =====

  - mark out
  - scratch
  - imprint

===== Usage =====

scrabble, set (a mark).
