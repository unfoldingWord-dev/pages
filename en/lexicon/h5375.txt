====== H5375: נָשָׂא (nâsâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Psalm 4:6 [7]), a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **lift**, in a great variety of applications, literal and figurative, absolute and relative

===== Definitions =====

  - lift

===== Usage =====

accept, advance, arise, (able to, (armor), suffer to) bear(-er, up), bring (forth), burn, carry (away), cast, contain, desire, ease, exact, exalt (self), extol, fetch, forgive, furnish, further, give, go on, help, high, hold up, honorable (  man), lade, lay, lift (self) up, lofty, marry, magnify, × needs, obtain, pardon, raise (up), receive, regard, respect, set (up), spare, stir up,   swear, take (away, up), × utterly, wear, yield.
