====== H3598: כִּימָה (Kîymâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h3558|H3558]];

===== Meaning =====

a **cluster of stars**, i.e. the **Pleiades**

===== Definitions =====

  - cluster of stars
  - Pleiades

===== Usage =====

Pleiades, seven stars.
