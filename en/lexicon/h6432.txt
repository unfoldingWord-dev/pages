====== H6432: פְּלֵתִי (Pᵉlêthîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from the same form as [[en:lexicon:h6431|H6431]];

===== Meaning =====

a **courier** (collectively) or **official messenger**

===== Definitions =====

  - courier
  - official messenger

===== Usage =====

Pelethites.
