====== G818: ἀτιμάζω (atimázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g820|G820]];

===== Meaning =====

to render infamous, i.e. (by implication) contemn or maltreat

===== Usage =====

despise, dishonour, suffer shame, entreat shamefully.
