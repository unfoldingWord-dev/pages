====== G1654: ἐλεημοσύνη (eleēmosýnē) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1656|G1656]];

===== Meaning =====

compassionateness, i.e. (as exercised towards the poor) beneficence, or (concretely) a benefaction

===== Usage =====

alms(-deeds).
