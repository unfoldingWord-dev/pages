====== G3618: οἰκοδομέω (oikodoméō) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g3619|G3619]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a house-builder, i.e. construct or (figuratively) confirm

===== Usage =====

(be in) build(-er, -ing, up), edify, embolden.
