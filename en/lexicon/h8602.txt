====== H8602: תָּפֵל (tâphêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **smear**;

===== Meaning =====

**plaster** (as **gummy**) or **slime**; (figuratively) **frivolity**

===== Definitions =====

  - smear
  - plaster
  - gummy
  - slime
  - frivolity

===== Usage =====

foolish things, unsavoury, untempered.
