====== H7660: שָׁבַץ (shâbats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **interweave** (colored) threads in squares; by implication (**of reticulation**) to **inchase** gems in gold

===== Definitions =====

  - interweave
  - of reticulation
  - inchase

===== Usage =====

embroider, set.
