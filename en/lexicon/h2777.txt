====== H2777: חַרְסוּת (charçûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2775|H2775]] (apparently in the sense of a red tile used for scraping);

===== Meaning =====

**a potsherd**, i.e. (by implication) **a pottery**; the name of a gate at Jerusalem

===== Definitions =====

  - a potsherd
  - a pottery

===== Usage =====

east.
