====== G1074: γενεά (geneá) ======

===== Source =====

from (a presumed derivative of) [[en:lexicon:g1085|G1085]];

===== Meaning =====

a generation; by implication, an age (the period or the persons)

===== Usage =====

age, generation, nation, time.
