====== G793: ἀστήρικτος (astḗriktos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4741|G4741]];

===== Meaning =====

unfixed, i.e. (figuratively) vacillating

===== Usage =====

unstable.
