====== G394: ἀνατίθεμαι (anatíthemai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

to set forth (for oneself), i.e propound

===== Usage =====

communicate, declare.
