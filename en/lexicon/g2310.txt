====== G2310: θεμέλιος (themélios) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

something put down, i.e. a substruction (of a building, etc.), (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

foundation.
