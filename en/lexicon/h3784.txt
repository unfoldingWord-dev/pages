====== H3784: כָּשַׁף (kâshaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **whisper** a spell, i.e. to **inchant** or **practise magic**

===== Definitions =====

  - whisper
  - inchant
  - practise magic

===== Usage =====

sorcerer, (use) witch(-craft).
