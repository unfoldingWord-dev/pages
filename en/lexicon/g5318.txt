====== G5318: φανερός (phanerós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]];

===== Meaning =====

shining, i.e. apparent (literally or figuratively); neuter (as adverb) publicly, externally

===== Usage =====

abroad, + appear, known, manifest, open (+ -ly), outward (+ -ly).
