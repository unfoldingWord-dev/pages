====== H4118: מַהֵר (mahêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjectiveadverb

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4116|H4116]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **hurrying**; hence (adverbially) **in a hurry**

===== Definitions =====

  - hurrying
  - in a hurry

===== Usage =====

hasteth, hastily, at once, quickly, soon, speedily, suddenly.
