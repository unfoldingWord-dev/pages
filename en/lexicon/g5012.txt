====== G5012: ταπεινοφροσύνη (tapeinophrosýnē) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5011|G5011]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5424|G5424]];

===== Meaning =====

humiliation of mind, i.e. modesty

===== Usage =====

humbleness of mind, humility (of mind, loneliness (of mind).
