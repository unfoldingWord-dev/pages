====== H7880: שִׂיחַ (sîyach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7878|H7878]];

===== Meaning =====

a **shoot** (as if uttered or put forth), i.e. (generally) **shrubbery**

===== Definitions =====

  - shoot
  - shrubbery

===== Usage =====

bush, plant, shrub.
