====== G3648: ὁλόκληρος (holóklēros) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3650|G3650]] and [[en:lexicon:g2819|G2819]];

===== Meaning =====

complete in every part, i.e. perfectly sound (in body)

===== Usage =====

entire, whole.
