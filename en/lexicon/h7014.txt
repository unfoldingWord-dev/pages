====== H7014: קַיִן (Qayin) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h7013|H7013]] (with a play upon the affinity to [[en:lexicon:h7069|H7069]]);

===== Meaning =====

**Kajin**, the name of the first child, also of a place in Palestine, and of an Oriental tribe

===== Definitions =====

  - Kajin

===== Usage =====

Cain, Kenite(-s).
