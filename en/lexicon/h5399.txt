====== H5399: נֶשֶׁף (nesheph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5398|H5398]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **breeze**, i.e. (by implication) **dusk** (when the evening breeze prevails)

===== Definitions =====

  - breeze
  - dusk

===== Usage =====

dark, dawning of the day (morning), night, twilight.
