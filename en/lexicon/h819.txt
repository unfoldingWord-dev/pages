====== H819: אַשְׁמָה (ʼashmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h817|H817]];

===== Meaning =====

**guiltiness**, a **fault**, the **presentation of a sin-offering**

===== Definitions =====

  - guiltiness
  - fault
  - presentation of a sin-offering

===== Usage =====

offend, sin, (cause of) trespass(-ing, offering).
