====== H4678: מַצֶּבֶת (matstsebeth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5324|H5324]];

===== Meaning =====

something **stationary**, i.e. a monumental **stone**; also the **stock** of a tree

===== Definitions =====

  - stationary
  - stone
  - stock

===== Usage =====

pillar, substance.
