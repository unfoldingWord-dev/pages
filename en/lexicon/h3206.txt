====== H3206: יֶלֶד (yeled) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3205|H3205]];

===== Meaning =====

**something born**, i.e. a **lad** or **offspring**

===== Definitions =====

  - something born
  - lad
  - offspring

===== Usage =====

boy, child, fruit, son, young man (one).
