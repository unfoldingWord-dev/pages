====== H1711: דָּגָה (dâgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to move rapidly; used only as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h1709|H1709]];

===== Meaning =====

to **spawn**, i.e. **become numerous**

===== Definitions =====

  - spawn
  - become numerous

===== Usage =====

grow.
