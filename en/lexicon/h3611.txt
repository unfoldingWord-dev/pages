====== H3611: כֶּלֶב (keleb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root means. to **yelp**, or else to **attack**;

===== Meaning =====

a **dog**; hence (by euphemism) a male **prostitute**

===== Definitions =====

  - yelp
  - attack
  - dog
  - prostitute

===== Usage =====

dog.
