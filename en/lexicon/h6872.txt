====== H6872: צְרוֹר (tsᵉrôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shorter) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6887|H6887]];

===== Meaning =====

a **parcel** (as **packed** up); also a **kernel** or **particle** (as if a **package**)

===== Definitions =====

  - parcel
  - packed
  - kernel
  - particle
  - package

===== Usage =====

bag, × bendeth, bundle, least grain, small stone.
