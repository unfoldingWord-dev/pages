====== H1396: גָּבַר (gâbar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be strong**; by implication, to **prevail**, **act insolently**

===== Definitions =====

  - be strong
  - prevail
  - act insolently

===== Usage =====

exceed, confirm, be great, be mighty, prevail, put to more (strength), strengthen, be stronger, be valiant.
