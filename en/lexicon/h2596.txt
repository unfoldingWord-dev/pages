====== H2596: חָנַךְ (chânak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare [[en:lexicon:h2614|H2614]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **narrow**; figuratively, to **initiate** or **discipline**

===== Definitions =====

  - narrow
  - initiate
  - discipline

===== Usage =====

dedicate, train up.
