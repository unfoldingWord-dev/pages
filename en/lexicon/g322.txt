====== G322: ἀναδείκνυμι (anadeíknymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g1166|G1166]];

===== Meaning =====

to exhibit, i.e. (by implication) to indicate, appoint

===== Usage =====

appoint, shew.
