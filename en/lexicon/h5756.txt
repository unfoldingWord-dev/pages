====== H5756: עוּז (ʻûwz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be strong**; causatively, to **strengthen**, i.e. (figuratively) to **save** (by flight)

===== Definitions =====

  - be strong
  - strengthen
  - save

===== Usage =====

gather (self, self to flee), retire.
