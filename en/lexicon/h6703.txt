====== H6703: צַח (tsach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6705|H6705]];

===== Meaning =====

**dazzling**, i.e. **sunny**, **bright**, (figuratively) **evident**

===== Definitions =====

  - dazzling
  - sunny
  - bright
  - evident

===== Usage =====

clear, dry, plainly, white.
