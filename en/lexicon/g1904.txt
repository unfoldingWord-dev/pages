====== G1904: ἐπέρχομαι (epérchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g2064|G2064]];

===== Meaning =====

to supervene, i.e. arrive, occur, impend, attack, (figuratively) influence

===== Usage =====

come (in, upon).
