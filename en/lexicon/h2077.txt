====== H2077: זֶבַח (zebach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2076|H2076]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **slaughter**, i.e. the **flesh** of an animal; by implication, a **sacrifice** (the victim or the act)

===== Definitions =====

  - slaughter
  - flesh
  - sacrifice

===== Usage =====

offer(-ing), sacrifice.
