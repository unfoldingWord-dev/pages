====== G1121: γράμμα (grámma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1125|G1125]];

===== Meaning =====

a writing, i.e. a letter, note, epistle, book, etc.; plural learning

===== Usage =====

bill, learning, letter, scripture, writing, written.
