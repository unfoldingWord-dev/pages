====== H4687: מִצְוָה (mitsvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6680|H6680]];

===== Meaning =====

a **command**, whether human or divine (collectively, the **Law**)

===== Definitions =====

  - command
  - Law

===== Usage =====

(which was) commanded(-ment), law, ordinance, precept.
