====== H6275: עָתַק (ʻâthaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **remove** (intransitive or transitive) figuratively, to **grow old**; specifically, to **transcribe**

===== Definitions =====

  - remove
  - grow old
  - transcribe

===== Usage =====

copy out, leave off, become (wax) old, remove.
