====== H2677: חֵצִי (chêtsîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2673|H2673]];

===== Meaning =====

the **half** or **middle**

===== Definitions =====

  - half
  - middle

===== Usage =====

half, middle, mid(-night), midst, part, two parts.
