====== H1761: דַּחֲוָה (dachăvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from the equivalent of [[en:lexicon:h1760|H1760]];

===== Meaning =====

probably a **musical instrument** (as being struck)

===== Definitions =====

  - musical instrument

===== Usage =====

instrument of music.
