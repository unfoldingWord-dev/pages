====== G4828: συμμαρτυρέω (symmartyréō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3140|G3140]];

===== Meaning =====

to testify jointly, i.e. corroborate by (concurrent) evidence

===== Usage =====

testify unto, (also) bear witness (with).
