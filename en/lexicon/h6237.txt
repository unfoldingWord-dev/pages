====== H6237: עָשַׂר (ʻâsar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with [[en:lexicon:h6238|H6238]]); to accumulate; but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h6235|H6235]];

===== Meaning =====

to **tithe**, i.e. to **take** or **give a tenth**

===== Definitions =====

  - tithe
  - take
  - give a tenth

===== Usage =====

× surely, give (take) the tenth, (have, take) tithe(-ing, -s), × truly.
