====== H6803: צִנְצֶנֶת (tsintseneth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6791|H6791]];

===== Meaning =====

a **vase** (probably a vial **tapering** at the top)

===== Definitions =====

  - vase
  - tapering

===== Usage =====

pot.
