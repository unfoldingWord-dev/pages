====== H4487: מָנָה (mânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **weigh out**; by implication, to **allot** or **constitute** officially; also to **enumerate** or **enroll**

===== Definitions =====

  - weigh out
  - allot
  - constitute
  - enumerate
  - enroll

===== Usage =====

appoint, count, number, prepare, set, tell.
