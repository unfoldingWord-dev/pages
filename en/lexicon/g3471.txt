====== G3471: μωραίνω (mōraínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3474|G3474]];

===== Meaning =====

to become insipid; figuratively, to make (passively, act) as a simpleton

===== Usage =====

become fool, make foolish, lose savour.
