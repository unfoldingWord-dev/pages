====== G3011: λειτουργός (leitourgós) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2992|G2992]] and [[en:lexicon:g2041|G2041]];

===== Meaning =====

a public servant, i.e. a functionary in the Temple or Gospel, or (genitive case) a worshipper (of God) or benefactor (of man)

===== Usage =====

minister(-ed).
