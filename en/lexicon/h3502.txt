====== H3502: יִתְרָה (yithrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h3499|H3499]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **excellence**, i.e. (by implication) **wealth**

===== Definitions =====

  - excellence
  - wealth

===== Usage =====

abundance, riches.
