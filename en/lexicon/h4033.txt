====== H4033: מָגוּר (mâgûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1481|H1481]] in the sense of lodging;

===== Meaning =====

a **temporary abode**; by extension, a **permanent residence**

===== Definitions =====

  - temporary abode
  - permanent residence

===== Usage =====

dwelling, pilgrimage, where sojourn, be a stranger. Compare
