====== H5472: סוּג (çûwg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **flinch**, i.e. (by implication) to **go back**, literally (to **retreat**) or figuratively (to **apostatize**)

===== Definitions =====

  - flinch
  - go back
  - retreat
  - apostatize

===== Usage =====

backslider, drive, go back, turn (away, back).
