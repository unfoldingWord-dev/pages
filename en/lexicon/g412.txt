====== G412: ἀνεκλάλητος (aneklálētos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1583|G1583]];

===== Meaning =====

not spoken out, i.e. (by implication) unutterable

===== Usage =====

unspeakable.
