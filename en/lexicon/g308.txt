====== G308: ἀναβλέπω (anablépō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g991|G991]];

===== Meaning =====

to look up; by implication, to recover sight

===== Usage =====

look (up), see, receive sight.
