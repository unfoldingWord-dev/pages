====== H4800: מֶרְחָב (merchâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7337|H7337]];

===== Meaning =====

**enlargement**, either literally (an **open space**, usually in a good sense), or figuratively (**liberty**)

===== Definitions =====

  - enlargement
  - open space
  - liberty

===== Usage =====

breadth, large place (room).
