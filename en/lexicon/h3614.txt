====== H3614: כָּלִבּוֹ (Kâlibbôw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

probably by erroneous transcription for [[en:lexicon:|]]; patronymically from [[en:lexicon:h3612|H3612]];

===== Meaning =====

a **Calebite** or descendant of Caleb

===== Definitions =====

  - Calebite

===== Usage =====

of the house of Caleb.
