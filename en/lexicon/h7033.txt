====== H7033: קָלָה (qâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather identical with [[en:lexicon:h7034|H7034]] through the idea of **shrinkage** by heat);

===== Meaning =====

to **toast**, i.e. **scorch** partially or slowly

===== Definitions =====

  - shrinkage
  - toast
  - scorch

===== Usage =====

dried, loathsome, parch, roast.
