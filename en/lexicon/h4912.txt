====== H4912: מָשָׁל (mâshâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h4910|H4910]] in some original sense of **superiority** in mental action;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **pithy** maxim, usually of metaphorical nature; hence, a **simile** (as an adage, poem, discourse)

===== Definitions =====

  - superiority
  - pithy
  - simile

===== Usage =====

byword, like, parable, proverb.
