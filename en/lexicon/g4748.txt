====== G4748: στοιχέω (stoichéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of στείχω (steíchō) (to range in regular line);

===== Meaning =====

to march in (military) rank (keep step), i.e. (figuratively) to conform to virtue and piety

===== Usage =====

walk (orderly).
