====== G2743: καυτηριάζω (kautēriázō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2545|G2545]];

===== Meaning =====

to brand ("cauterize"), i.e. (by implication) to render unsensitive (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

sear with a hot iron.
