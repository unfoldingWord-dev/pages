====== H1: אָב (ʼâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

a primitive word;

===== Meaning =====

**father**, in a literal and immediate, or figurative and remote application

===== Definitions =====

  - father

===== Usage =====

chief, (fore-) father(-less), × patrimony, principal. Compare names in 'Abi-'.
