====== G4727: στενάζω (stenázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4728|G4728]];

===== Meaning =====

to make (intransitively, be) in straits, i.e. (by implication) to sigh, murmur, pray inaudibly

===== Usage =====

with grief, groan, grudge, sigh.
