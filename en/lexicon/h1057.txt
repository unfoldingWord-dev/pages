====== H1057: בָּכָא (bâkâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h1056|H1056]];

===== Meaning =====

the **weeping tree** (some gum-distilling tree, perhaps the balsam)

===== Definitions =====

  - weeping tree

===== Usage =====

mulberry tree.
