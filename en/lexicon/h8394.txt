====== H8394: תָּבוּן (tâbûwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

and (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h995|H995]];

===== Meaning =====

**intelligence**; by implication, an **argument**; by extension, **caprice**

===== Definitions =====

  - intelligence
  - argument
  - caprice

===== Usage =====

discretion, reason, skilfulness, understanding, wisdom.
