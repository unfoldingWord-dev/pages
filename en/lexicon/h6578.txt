====== H6578: פְּרָת (Pᵉrâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to break forth; rushing;

===== Meaning =====

**Perath** (i.e. Euphrates), a river of the East

===== Definitions =====

  - Perath

===== Usage =====

Euphrates.
