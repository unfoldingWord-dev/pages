====== G4436: Πύθων (Pýthōn) ======

===== Source =====

from Πυθώ (Pythṓ) (the name of the region where Delphi, the seat of the
 famous oracle, was located);

===== Meaning =====

a Python, i.e. (by analogy, with the supposed diviner there) inspiration (soothsaying)

===== Usage =====

 divination.
