====== G1231: διαγινώσκω (diaginṓskō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g1097|G1097]];

===== Meaning =====

to know thoroughly, i.e. ascertain exactly

===== Usage =====

(would) enquire, know the uttermost.
