====== H3547: כָּהַן (kâhan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root, apparently meaning to mediate in religious services; but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h3548|H3548]];

===== Meaning =====

to **officiate** as a priest; figuratively, to **put on regalia**

===== Definitions =====

  - officiate
  - put on regalia

===== Usage =====

deck, be (do the office of a, execute the, minister in the) priest('s office).
