====== H4057: מִדְבָּר (midbâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1696|H1696]] in the sense of driving;

===== Meaning =====

a **pasture** (i.e. open field, whither cattle are driven); by implication, a **desert**; also **speech** (including its organs)

===== Definitions =====

  - pasture
  - desert
  - speech

===== Usage =====

desert, south, speech, wilderness.
