====== G5456: φωνή (phōnḗ) ======

===== Source =====

probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]] through the idea of disclosure;

===== Meaning =====

a tone (articulate, bestial or artificial); by implication, an address (for any purpose), saying or language

===== Usage =====

noise, sound, voice.
