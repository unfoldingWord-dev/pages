====== G2518: καθεύδω (katheúdō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and εὕδω (heúdō) (to sleep);

===== Meaning =====

to lie down to rest, i.e. (by implication) to fall asleep (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

(be a-)sleep.
