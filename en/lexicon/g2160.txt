====== G2160: εὐτραπελία (eutrapelía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g5157|G5157]] (meaning
 well-turned, i.e. ready at repartee, jocose);

===== Meaning =====

witticism, i.e. (in a vulgar sense) ribaldry

===== Usage =====

jesting.
