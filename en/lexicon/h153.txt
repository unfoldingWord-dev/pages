====== H153: אֶדְרָע (ʼedrâʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) an orthographical variation for [[en:lexicon:h1872|H1872]];

===== Meaning =====

an **arm**, i.e. (figuratively) **power**

===== Definitions =====

  - arm
  - power

===== Usage =====

force.
