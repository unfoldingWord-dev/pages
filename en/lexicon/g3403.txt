====== G3403: μιμνήσκω (mimnḗskō) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of [[en:lexicon:g3415|G3415]] (from which some of the tenses are borrowed);

===== Meaning =====

to remind, i.e. (middle voice) to recall to mind

===== Usage =====

be mindful, remember.
