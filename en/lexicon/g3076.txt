====== G3076: λυπέω (lypéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3077|G3077]];

===== Meaning =====

to distress; reflexively or passively, to be sad

===== Usage =====

cause grief, grieve, be in heaviness, (be) sorrow(-ful), be (make) sorry.
