====== H148: אֲדַרְגָּזֵר (ʼădargâzêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from the same as [[en:lexicon:h147|H147]], and [[en:lexicon:h1505|H1505]];

===== Meaning =====

a **chief diviner**, or **astrologer**

===== Definitions =====

  - chief diviner
  - astrologer

===== Usage =====

judge.
