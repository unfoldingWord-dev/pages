====== H4526: מִסְגֶּרֶת (miçgereth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5462|H5462]];

===== Meaning =====

something **enclosing**, i.e. a **margin** (of a region, of a panel); concretely, a **stronghold**

===== Definitions =====

  - enclosing
  - margin
  - stronghold

===== Usage =====

border, close place, hole.
