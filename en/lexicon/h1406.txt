====== H1406: גָּג (gâg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably by reduplication from [[en:lexicon:h1342|H1342]];

===== Meaning =====

a **roof**; by analogy, the **top** of an altar

===== Definitions =====

  - roof
  - top

===== Usage =====

roof (of the house), (house) top (of the house).
