====== G3592: ὅδε (hóde) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3588|G3588]] and [[en:lexicon:g1161|G1161]];

===== Meaning =====

the same, i.e. this or that one (plural these or those); often used as a personal pronoun

===== Usage =====

he, she, such, these, thus.
