====== H1819: דָּמָה (dâmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **compare**; by implication, to **resemble**, **liken**, **consider**

===== Definitions =====

  - compare
  - resemble
  - liken
  - consider

===== Usage =====

compare, devise, (be) like(-n), mean, think, use similitudes.
