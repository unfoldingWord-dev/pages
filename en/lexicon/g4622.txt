====== G4622: Σιών (Siṓn) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin ([[en:lexicon:h06726|H06726]]);

===== Meaning =====

Sion (i.e. Tsijon), a hill of Jerusalem; figuratively, the Church (militant or triumphant)

===== Usage =====

Sion.
