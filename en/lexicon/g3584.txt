====== G3584: ξηρός (xērós) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g3582|G3582]] (through the idea of scorching);

===== Meaning =====

arid; by implication, shrunken, earth (as opposed to water)

===== Usage =====

dry land, withered.
