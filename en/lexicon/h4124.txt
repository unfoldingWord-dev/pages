====== H4124: מוֹאָב (Môwʼâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculinenoun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from a prolonged form of the prepositional prefix m- and [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]]; **from** (her [the mother's]) **father**;

===== Meaning =====

**Moab**, an incestuous son of Lot; also his territory and descendants

===== Definitions =====

  - from
  - father
  - Moab

===== Usage =====

Moab.
