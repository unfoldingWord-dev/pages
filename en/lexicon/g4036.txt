====== G4036: περίλυπος (perílypos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g3077|G3077]];

===== Meaning =====

grieved all around, i.e. intensely sad

===== Usage =====

exceeding (very) sorry(-owful).
