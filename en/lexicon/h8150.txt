====== H8150: שָׁנַן (shânan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **point** (transitive or intransitive); intensively, to **pierce**; figuratively, to **inculcate**

===== Definitions =====

  - point
  - pierce
  - inculcate

===== Usage =====

prick, sharp(-en), teach diligently, whet.
