====== G3733: ὄρνις (órnis) ======

===== Source =====

probably from a prolonged form of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3735|G3735]];

===== Meaning =====

a bird (as rising in the air), i.e. (specially), a hen (or female domestic fowl)

===== Usage =====

hen.
