====== H8326: שֹׁרֶר (shôrer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8324|H8324]] in the sense of twisting (compare [[en:lexicon:h8270|H8270]]);

===== Meaning =====

**the umbilical cord**, i.e. (by extension) **a bodice**

===== Definitions =====

  - the umbilical cord
  - a bodice

===== Usage =====

navel.
