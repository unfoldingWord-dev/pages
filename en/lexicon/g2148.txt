====== G2148: Εὐροκλύδων (Euroklýdōn) ======

===== Source =====

from Εὖρος (Eûros) (the east wind) and [[en:lexicon:g2830|G2830]];

===== Meaning =====

a storm from the East (or southeast), i.e. (in modern phrase) a Levanter

===== Usage =====

Euroklydon.
