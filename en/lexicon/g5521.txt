====== G5521: χολή (cholḗ) ======

===== Source =====

feminine of an equivalent perhaps akin to the same as 5514 (from the
 greenish hue);

===== Meaning =====

"gall" or bile, i.e. (by analogy) poison or an anodyne (wormwood, poppy, etc.)

===== Usage =====

gall.
