====== G472: ἀντέχομαι (antéchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

to hold oneself opposite to, i.e. (by implication) adhere to; by extension to care for

===== Usage =====

hold fast, hold to, support.
