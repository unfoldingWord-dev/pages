====== G4950: σύρτις (sýrtis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4951|G4951]];

===== Meaning =====

a shoal (from the sand drawn thither by the waves), i.e. the Syrtis Major or great bay on the north coast of Africa

===== Usage =====

quicksands.
