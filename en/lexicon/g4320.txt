====== G4320: προσαναβαίνω (prosanabaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g305|G305]];

===== Meaning =====

to ascend farther, i.e. be promoted (take an upper (more honorable) seat)

===== Usage =====

go up.
