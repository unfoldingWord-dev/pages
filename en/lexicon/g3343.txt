====== G3343: μεταπέμπω (metapémpō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g3992|G3992]];

===== Meaning =====

to send from elsewhere, i.e. (middle voice) to summon or invite

===== Usage =====

call (send) for.
