====== H4603: מָעַל (mâʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **cover up**; used only figuratively, to **act covertly**, i.e. **treacherously**

===== Definitions =====

  - cover up
  - act covertly
  - treacherously

===== Usage =====

transgress, (commit, do a) trespass(-ing).
