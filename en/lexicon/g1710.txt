====== G1710: ἐμπορεύομαι (emporeúomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g4198|G4198]];

===== Meaning =====

to travel in (a country as a pedlar), i.e. (by implication) to trade

===== Usage =====

buy and sell, make merchandise.
