====== H1846: דָּעַךְ (dâʻak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be extinguished**; figuratively, to **expire** or **be dried up**

===== Definitions =====

  - be extinguished
  - expire
  - be dried up

===== Usage =====

be extinct, consumed, put out, quenched.
