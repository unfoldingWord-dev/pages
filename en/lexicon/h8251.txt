====== H8251: שִׁקּוּץ (shiqqûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h8262|H8262]];

===== Meaning =====

**disgusting**, i.e. **filthy**; especially **idolatrous** or (concretely) an **idol**

===== Definitions =====

  - disgusting
  - filthy
  - idolatrous
  - idol

===== Usage =====

abominable filth (idol, -ation), detestable (thing).
