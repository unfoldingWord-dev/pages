====== H7757: שׁוּל (shûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **hang** down;

===== Meaning =====

a **skirt**; by implication, a bottom **edge**

===== Definitions =====

  - hang
  - skirt
  - edge

===== Usage =====

hem, skirt, train.
