====== G393: ἀνατέλλω (anatéllō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5056|G5056]];

===== Meaning =====

to (cause to) arise

===== Usage =====

(a-, make to) rise, at the rising of, spring (up), be up.
