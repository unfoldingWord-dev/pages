====== G5363: φιλανθρωπία (philanthrōpía) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g5364|G5364]];

===== Meaning =====

fondness of mankind, i.e. benevolence ("philanthropy")

===== Usage =====

kindness, love towards man.
