====== H7001: קְטַר (qᵉṭar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h7000|H7000]];

===== Meaning =====

a **knot** (as tied up), i.e. (figuratively) a **riddle**; also a **vertebra** (as if a knot)

===== Definitions =====

  - knot
  - riddle
  - vertebra

===== Usage =====

doubt, joint.
