====== H2022: הַר (har) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

a shortened form of [[en:lexicon:h2042|H2042]];

===== Meaning =====

a **mountain** or **range** of hills (sometimes used figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - mountain
  - range

===== Usage =====

hill (country), mount(-ain), × promotion.
