====== H5006: נָאַץ (nâʼats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **scorn**; or (in Ecclesiastes 12:5), by interchange for [[en:lexicon:h5132|H5132]], to **bloom**

===== Definitions =====

  - scorn
  - bloom

===== Usage =====

abhor, (give occasion to) blaspheme, contemn, despise, flourish, × great, provoke.
