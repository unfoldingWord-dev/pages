====== G3763: οὐδέποτε (oudépote) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3761|G3761]] and [[en:lexicon:g4218|G4218]];

===== Meaning =====

not even at any time, i.e. never at all

===== Usage =====

neither at any time, never, nothing at any time.
