====== G1807: ἐξαιρέω (exairéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g138|G138]];

===== Meaning =====

actively, to tear out; middle voice, to select; figuratively, to release

===== Usage =====

deliver, pluck out, rescue.
