====== H6106: עֶצֶם (ʻetsem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6105|H6105]];

===== Meaning =====

a **bone** (as **strong**); by extension, the **body**; figuratively, the **substance**, i.e. (as pron.) **selfsame**

===== Definitions =====

  - bone
  - strong
  - body
  - substance
  - selfsame

===== Usage =====

body, bone, × life, (self-) same, strength, × very.
