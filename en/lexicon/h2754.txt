====== H2754: חָרִיט (chârîyṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from the same as [[en:lexicon:h2747|H2747]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **cut** out (or **hollow**), i.e. (by implication) a **pocket**

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - hollow
  - pocket

===== Usage =====

bag, crisping pin.
