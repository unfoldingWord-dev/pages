====== G2762: κεραία (keraía) ======

===== Source =====

feminine of a presumed derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g2768|G2768]];

===== Meaning =====

something horn-like, i.e. (specially) the apex of a Hebrew letter (figuratively, the least particle)

===== Usage =====

tittle.
