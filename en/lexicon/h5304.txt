====== H5304: נְפִיסִים (Nᵉphîyçîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun

===== Source =====

plural from an unused root meaning to scatter; expansions;

===== Meaning =====

**Nephisim**, a Temple-servant

===== Definitions =====

  - Nephisim

===== Usage =====

Nephusim (from the margin).
