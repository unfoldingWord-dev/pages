====== H6984: קוּשָׁיָהוּ (qûwshâyâhûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h6983|H6983]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; **entrapped of Jah**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kushajah**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - entrapped of Jah
  - Kushajah

===== Usage =====

Kushaiah.
