====== H3739: כָּרָה (kârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

usually assigned as a primitive root, but probably only a special application of [[en:lexicon:h3738|H3738]] (through the common idea of planning implied in a bargain);

===== Meaning =====

to **purchase**

===== Definitions =====

  - purchase

===== Usage =====

buy, prepare.
