====== H5863: עִיֵּי הָעֲבָרִים (ʻÎyêy hâ-ʻĂbârîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the plural of [[en:lexicon:h5856|H5856]] and the plural of the active participle of [[en:lexicon:h5674|H5674]] with the article interposed; **ruins of the passers**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ije-ha-Abarim**, a place near Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - ruins of the passers
  - Ije-ha-Abarim

===== Usage =====

Ije-abarim.
