====== G1659: ἐλευθερόω (eleutheróō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1658|G1658]];

===== Meaning =====

to liberate, i.e. (figuratively) to exempt (from moral, ceremonial or mortal liability)

===== Usage =====

deliver, make free.
