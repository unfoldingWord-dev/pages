====== G3873: παράκειμαι (parákeimai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g2749|G2749]];

===== Meaning =====

to lie near, i.e. be at hand (figuratively, be prompt or easy)

===== Usage =====

be present.
