====== G3930: παρέχω (paréchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

to hold near, i.e. present, afford, exhibit, furnish occasion

===== Usage =====

bring, do, give, keep, minister, offer, shew, + trouble.
