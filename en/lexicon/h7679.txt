====== H7679: שָׂגָא (sâgâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

**to grow**, i.e. (causatively) **to enlarge**, (figuratively) **laud**

===== Definitions =====

  - to grow
  - to enlarge
  - laud

===== Usage =====

increase, magnify.
