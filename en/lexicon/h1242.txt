====== H1242: בֹּקֶר (bôqer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1239|H1239]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **dawn** (as the break of day); generally, **morning**

===== Definitions =====

  - dawn
  - morning

===== Usage =====

( ) day, early, morning, morrow.
