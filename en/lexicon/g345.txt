====== G345: ἀνακεῖμαι (anakeîmai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g2749|G2749]];

===== Meaning =====

to recline (as a corpse or at a meal)

===== Usage =====

guest, lean, lie, sit (down, at meat), at the table.
