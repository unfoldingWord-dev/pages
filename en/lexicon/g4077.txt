====== G4077: πηγή (pēgḗ) ======

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:g4078|G4078]] (through the idea of gushing plumply);

===== Meaning =====

a fount (literally or figuratively), i.e. source or supply (of water, blood, enjoyment) (not necessarily the original spring)

===== Usage =====

fountain, well.
