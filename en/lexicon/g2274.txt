====== G2274: ἡττάω (hēttáō) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g2276|G2276]];

===== Meaning =====

to make worse, i.e. vanquish (literally or figuratively); by implication, to rate lower

===== Usage =====

be inferior, overcome.
