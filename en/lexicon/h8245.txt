====== H8245: שָׁקַד (shâqad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be alert**, i.e. **sleepless**; hence to **be on the lookout** (whether for good or ill)

===== Definitions =====

  - be alert
  - sleepless
  - be on the lookout

===== Usage =====

hasten, remain, wake, watch (for).
