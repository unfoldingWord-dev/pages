====== G5411: φόρος (phóros) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]];

===== Meaning =====

a load (as borne), i.e. (figuratively) a tax (properly, an individual assessment on persons or property; whereas

===== Usage =====

tribute.
