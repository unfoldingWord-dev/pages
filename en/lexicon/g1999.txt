====== G1999: ἐπισύστασις (episýstasis) ======

===== Source =====

from the middle voice of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g4921|G4921]];

===== Meaning =====

a conspiracy, i.e. concourse (riotous or friendly)

===== Usage =====

that which cometh upon, + raising up.
