====== H40: אֲבִימֶלֶךְ (ʼĂbîymelek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h4428|H4428]]; father of (the) king;

===== Meaning =====

**Abimelek**, the name of two Philistine kings and of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Abimelek

===== Usage =====

Abimelech.
