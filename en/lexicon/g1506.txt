====== G1506: εἰλικρινής (eilikrinḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from εἵλη (heílē) (the sun's ray) and [[en:lexicon:g2919|G2919]];

===== Meaning =====

judged by sunlight, i.e. tested as genuine (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

pure, sincere.
