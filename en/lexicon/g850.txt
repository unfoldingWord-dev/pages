====== G850: αὐχμηρός (auchmērós) ======

===== Source =====

from αὐχμός (auchmós) (probably from a base akin to that of [[en:lexicon:g109|G109]]) (dust, as
 dried by wind);

===== Meaning =====

properly, dirty, i.e. (by implication) obscure

===== Usage =====

dark.
