====== H8334: שָׁרַת (shârath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **attend** as a menial or worshipper; figuratively, to **contribute** to

===== Definitions =====

  - attend
  - contribute

===== Usage =====

minister (unto), (do) serve(-ant, -ice, -itor), wait on.
