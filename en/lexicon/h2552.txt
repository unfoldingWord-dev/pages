====== H2552: חָמַם (châmam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be hot** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - be hot

===== Usage =====

enflame self, get (have) heat, be (wax) hot, (be, wax) warm (self, at).
