====== G5282: ὑπονοέω (hyponoéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g3539|G3539]];

===== Meaning =====

to think under (privately), i.e. to surmise or conjecture

===== Usage =====

think, suppose, deem.
