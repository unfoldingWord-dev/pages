====== H2154: זִמָּה (zimmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h2161|H2161]];

===== Meaning =====

a **plan**, especially a **bad one**

===== Definitions =====

  - plan
  - bad one

===== Usage =====

heinous crime, lewd(-ly, -ness), mischief, purpose, thought, wicked (device, mind, -ness).
