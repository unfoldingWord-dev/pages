====== H5170: נַחַר (nachar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

and (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **snort** or **snore**;

===== Meaning =====

a **snorting**

===== Definitions =====

  - snort
  - snore
  - snorting

===== Usage =====

nostrils, snorting.
