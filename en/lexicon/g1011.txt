====== G1011: βουλεύω (bouleúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1012|G1012]];

===== Meaning =====

to advise, i.e. (reflexively) deliberate, or (by implication) resolve

===== Usage =====

consult, take counsel, determine, be minded, purpose.
