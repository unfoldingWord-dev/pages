====== H4131: מוֹט (môwṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **waver**; by implication, to **slip**, **shake**, **fall**

===== Definitions =====

  - waver
  - slip
  - shake
  - fall

===== Usage =====

be carried, cast, be out of course, be fallen in decay, × exceedingly, fall(-ing down), be (re-) moved, be ready, shake, slide, slip.
