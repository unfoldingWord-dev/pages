====== G1082: Γεννησαρέτ (Gennēsarét) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h03672|H03672]]);

===== Meaning =====

Gennesaret (i.e. Kinnereth), a lake and plain in Palestine
