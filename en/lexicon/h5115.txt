====== H5115: נָוָה (nâvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare [[en:lexicon:h5116|H5116]])

===== Meaning =====

to **rest** (as at home); causatively (through the implied idea of **beauty**), to **celebrate** (with praises)

===== Definitions =====

  - rest
  - beauty
  - celebrate

===== Usage =====

keep at home, prepare an habitation.
