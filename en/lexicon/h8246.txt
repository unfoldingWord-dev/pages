====== H8246: שָׁקַד (shâqad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h8247|H8247]];

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (intensively, **make**) **almond-shaped**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - almond-shaped

===== Usage =====

make like (unto, after the fashion of) almonds.
