====== G3676: ὅμως (hómōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from the base of [[en:lexicon:g3674|G3674]];

===== Meaning =====

at the same time, i.e. (conjunctionally) notwithstanding, yet still

===== Usage =====

and even, nevertheless, though but.
