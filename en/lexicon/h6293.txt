====== H6293: פָּגַע (pâgaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **impinge**, by accident or violence, or (figuratively) by importunity

===== Definitions =====

  - impinge

===== Usage =====

come (betwixt), cause to entreat, fall (upon), make intercession, intercessor, intreat, lay, light (upon), meet (together), pray, reach, run.
