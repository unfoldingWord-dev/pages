====== G508: ἀνώγεον (anṓgeon) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g507|G507]] and [[en:lexicon:g1093|G1093]];

===== Meaning =====

above the ground, i.e. (properly) the second floor of a building; used for a dome or a balcony on the upper story

===== Usage =====

upper room.
