====== H3882: לִוְיָתָן (livyâthân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3867|H3867]];

===== Meaning =====

a **wreathed** animal, i.e. a **serpent** (especially the **crocodile** or some other large sea-monster); figuratively, the constellation of the **dragon**; also as a symbol of **Babylon**

===== Definitions =====

  - wreathed
  - serpent
  - crocodile
  - dragon
  - Babylon

===== Usage =====

leviathan, mourning.
