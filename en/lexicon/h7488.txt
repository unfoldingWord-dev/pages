====== H7488: רַעֲנָן (raʻănân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verbadjective

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be green;

===== Meaning =====

**verdant**; by analogy, **new**; figuratively, **prosperous**

===== Definitions =====

  - verdant
  - new
  - prosperous

===== Usage =====

green, flourishing.
