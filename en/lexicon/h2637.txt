====== H2637: חָסֵר (châçêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **lack**; by implication, to **fail**, **want**, **lessen**

===== Definitions =====

  - lack
  - fail
  - want
  - lessen

===== Usage =====

be abated, bereave, decrease, (cause to) fail, (have) lack, make lower, want.
