====== H8432: תָּוֶךְ (tâvek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **sever**;

===== Meaning =====

a **bisection**, i.e. (by implication) the **centre**

===== Definitions =====

  - sever
  - bisection
  - centre

===== Usage =====

among(-st), × between, half, × (there-, where-), in(-to), middle, mid(-night), midst (among), × out (of), × through, × with(-in).
