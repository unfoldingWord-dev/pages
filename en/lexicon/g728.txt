====== G728: ἀῤῥαβών (arrhabṓn) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin ([[en:lexicon:h06162|H06162]]);

===== Meaning =====

a pledge, i.e. part of the purchase-money or property given in advance as security for the rest

===== Usage =====

earnest.
