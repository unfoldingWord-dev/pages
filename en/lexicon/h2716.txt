====== H2716: חֶרֶא (chereʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused (and vulgar) root probably meaning to **evacuate** the bowels;

===== Meaning =====

**excrement**

===== Definitions =====

  - evacuate
  - excrement

===== Usage =====

dung. Also
