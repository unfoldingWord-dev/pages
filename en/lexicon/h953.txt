====== H953: בּוֹר (bôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h952|H952]] (in the sense of [[en:lexicon:h877|H877]]);

===== Meaning =====

a pit **hole** (especially one used as a cistern or a prison)

===== Definitions =====

  - hole

===== Usage =====

cistern, dungeon, fountain, pit, well.
