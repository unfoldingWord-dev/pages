====== H7225: רֵאשִׁית (rêʼshîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h7218|H7218]];

===== Meaning =====

the **first**, in place, time, order or rank (specifically, a **firstfruit**)

===== Definitions =====

  - first
  - firstfruit

===== Usage =====

beginning, chief(-est), first(-fruits, part, time), principal thing.
