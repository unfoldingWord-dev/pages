====== G1573: ἐκκακέω (ekkakéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g2556|G2556]];

===== Meaning =====

to be (bad or) weak, i.e. (by implication) to fail (in heart)

===== Usage =====

faint, be weary.
