====== H4842: מִרְקַחַת (mirqachath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7543|H7543]];

===== Meaning =====

an aromatic **unguent**; also an **unguent-pot**

===== Definitions =====

  - unguent
  - unguent-pot

===== Usage =====

prepared by the apothecaries' art, compound, ointment.
