====== H5477: סוּחַ (Çûwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **wipe away**; **sweeping**;

===== Meaning =====

**Suach**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - wipe away
  - sweeping
  - Suach

===== Usage =====

Suah.
