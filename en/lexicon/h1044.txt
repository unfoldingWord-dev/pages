====== H1044: בֵּית עֵקֶד (Bêyth ʻÊqed) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1004|H1004]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:h6123|H6123]]; house of (the) binding (for sheep-shearing);

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-Eked**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-Eked

===== Usage =====

shearing house.
