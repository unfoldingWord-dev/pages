====== G3474: μωρός (mōrós) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the base of [[en:lexicon:g3466|G3466]];

===== Meaning =====

dull or stupid (as if shut up), i.e. heedless, (morally) blockhead, (apparently) absurd

===== Usage =====

fool(-ish, X -ishness).
