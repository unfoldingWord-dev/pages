====== H5332: נֵצַח (Nêtsach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably identical with [[en:lexicon:h5331|H5331]], through the idea of **brilliancy** of color;

===== Meaning =====

**juice** of the grape (as blood red)

===== Definitions =====

  - brilliancy
  - juice

===== Usage =====

blood, strength.
