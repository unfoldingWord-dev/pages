====== H8083: שְׁמֹנֶה (shᵉmôneh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; apparently from [[en:lexicon:h8082|H8082]] through the idea of plumpness;

===== Meaning =====

a cardinal number, **eight** (as if a surplus above the 'perfect' seven); also (as ordinal) **eighth**

===== Definitions =====

  - eight
  - eighth

===== Usage =====

eight(-een, -eenth), eighth.
