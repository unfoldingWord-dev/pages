====== G3893: παραπικραίνω (parapikraínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g4087|G4087]];

===== Meaning =====

to embitter alongside, i.e. (figuratively) to exasperate

===== Usage =====
