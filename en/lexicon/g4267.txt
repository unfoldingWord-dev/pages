====== G4267: προγινώσκω (proginṓskō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g1097|G1097]];

===== Meaning =====

to know beforehand, i.e. foresee

===== Usage =====

foreknow (ordain), know (before).
