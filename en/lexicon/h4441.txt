====== H4441: מַלְכִּיָּה (Malkîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Jeremiah 38:6), from [[en:lexicon:h4428|H4428]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; **king of** (i.e. appointed by) **Jah**;

===== Meaning =====

**Malkijah**, the name of ten Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - king of
  - Jah
  - Malkijah

===== Usage =====

Malchiah, Malchijah.
