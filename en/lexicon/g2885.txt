====== G2885: κοσμέω (kosméō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2889|G2889]];

===== Meaning =====

to put in proper order, i.e. decorate (literally or figuratively); specially, to snuff (a wick)

===== Usage =====

adorn, garnish, trim.
