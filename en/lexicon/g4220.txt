====== G4220: πότερον (póteron) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a comparative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g4226|G4226]];

===== Meaning =====

interrogative as adverb, which (of two), i.e. is it this or that

===== Usage =====

whether.
