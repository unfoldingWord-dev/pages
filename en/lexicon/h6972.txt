====== H6972: קוּץ (qûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to **clip** off; used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7019|H7019]];

===== Meaning =====

to **spend the harvest** season

===== Definitions =====

  - clip
  - spend the harvest

===== Usage =====

summer.
