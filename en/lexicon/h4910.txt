====== H4910: מָשַׁל (mâshal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **rule**

===== Definitions =====

  - rule

===== Usage =====

(have, make to have) dominion, governor, × indeed, reign, (bear, cause to, have) rule(-ing, -r), have power.
