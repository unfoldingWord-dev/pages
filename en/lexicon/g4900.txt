====== G4900: συνελαύνω (synelaúnō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g1643|G1643]];

===== Meaning =====

to drive together, i.e. (figuratively) exhort (to reconciliation)

===== Usage =====

+ set at one again.
