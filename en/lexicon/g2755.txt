====== G2755: κενόδοξος (kenódoxos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2756|G2756]] and [[en:lexicon:g1391|G1391]];

===== Meaning =====

vainly glorifying, i.e. self-conceited

===== Usage =====

desirous of vain-glory.
