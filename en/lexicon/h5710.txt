====== H5710: עָדָה (ʻâdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **advance**, i.e. **pass** on or **continue**; causatively, to **remove**; specifically, to **bedeck** (i.e. bring an ornament upon)

===== Definitions =====

  - advance
  - pass
  - continue
  - remove
  - bedeck

===== Usage =====

adorn, deck (self), pass by, take away.
