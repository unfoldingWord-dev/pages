====== G987: βλασφημέω (blasphēméō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g989|G989]];

===== Meaning =====

to vilify; specially, to speak impiously

===== Usage =====

(speak) blaspheme(-er, -mously, -my), defame, rail on, revile, speak evil.
