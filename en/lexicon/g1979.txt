====== G1979: ἐπισιτισμός (episitismós) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4621|G4621]];

===== Meaning =====

a provisioning, i.e. (concretely) food

===== Usage =====
