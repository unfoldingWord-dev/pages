====== H2143: זֵכֶר (zêker) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h2142|H2142]];

===== Meaning =====

a **memento**, abstractly **recollection** (rarely if ever); by implication, **commemoration**

===== Definitions =====

  - memento
  - recollection
  - commemoration

===== Usage =====

memorial, memory, remembrance, scent.
