====== G113: ἄθεσμος (áthesmos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]] (in the sense
 of enacting);

===== Meaning =====

lawless, i.e. (by implication) criminal

===== Usage =====

wicked.
