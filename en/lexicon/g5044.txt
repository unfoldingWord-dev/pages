====== G5044: τεκνοτροφέω (teknotrophéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5043|G5043]] and [[en:lexicon:g5142|G5142]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a childrearer, i.e. fulfil the duties of a female parent

===== Usage =====

bring up children.
