====== H3072: יְהֹוָה צִדְקֵנוּ (Yᵉhôvâh tsidqênûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3068|H3068]] and [[en:lexicon:h6664|H6664]] with pronominal suffix; Jehovah (is) our right;

===== Meaning =====

**Jehovah-Tsidkenu**, a symbolical epithet of the Messiah and of Jerusalem

===== Definitions =====

  - Jehovah-Tsidkenu

===== Usage =====

the Lord our righteousness.
