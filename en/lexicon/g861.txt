====== G861: ἀφθαρσία (aphtharsía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g862|G862]];

===== Meaning =====

incorruptibility; genitive, unending existence; (figuratively) genuineness

===== Usage =====

immortality, incorruption, sincerity.
