====== H3779: כַּשְׂדַּי (Kasday) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h3778|H3778]];

===== Meaning =====

a **Chaldaean** or inhabitant of Chaldaea; by implication, a Magian or professional astrologer

===== Definitions =====

  - Chaldaean

===== Usage =====

Chaldean.
