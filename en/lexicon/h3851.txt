====== H3851: לַהַב (lahab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an usused root meaning to **gleam**;

===== Meaning =====

a **flash**; figuratively, a sharply polished **blade** or point of a weapon

===== Definitions =====

  - gleam
  - flash
  - blade

===== Usage =====

blade, bright, flame, glittering.
