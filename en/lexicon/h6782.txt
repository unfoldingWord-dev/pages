====== H6782: צַמִּים (tsammîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6777|H6777]];

===== Meaning =====

a **noose** (as fastening); figuratively, **destruction**

===== Definitions =====

  - noose
  - destruction

===== Usage =====

robber.
