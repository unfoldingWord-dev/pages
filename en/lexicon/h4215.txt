====== H4215: מְזָרֶה (mᵉzâreh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h2219|H2219]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **scatterer**, i.e. the **north wind** (as dispersing clouds; only in plural)

===== Definitions =====

  - scatterer
  - north wind

===== Usage =====

north.
