====== G5005: ταλαίπωρος (talaípōros) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g5007|G5007]] and a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3984|G3984]];

===== Meaning =====

enduring trial, i.e. miserable

===== Usage =====

wretched.
