====== G4289: πρόθυμος (próthymos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2372|G2372]];

===== Meaning =====

forward in spirit, i.e. predisposed; neuter (as noun) alacrity

===== Usage =====

ready, willing.
