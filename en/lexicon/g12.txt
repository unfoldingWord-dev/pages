====== G12: ἄβυσσος (ábyssos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a variation of [[en:lexicon:g1037|G1037]];

===== Meaning =====

depthless, i.e. (specially) (infernal) "abyss"

===== Usage =====

deep, (bottomless) pit.
