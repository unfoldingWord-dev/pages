====== H2779: חֹרֶף (chôreph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2778|H2778]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, the **crop gathered**, i.e. (by implication) the **autumn** (and winter) **season**; figuratively, **ripeness of age**

===== Definitions =====

  - crop gathered
  - autumn
  - season
  - ripeness of age

===== Usage =====

cold, winter (-house), youth.
