====== G1288: διασπάω (diaspáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g4685|G4685]];

===== Meaning =====

to draw apart, i.e. sever or dismember

===== Usage =====

pluck asunder, pull in pieces.
