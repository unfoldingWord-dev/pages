====== H3584: כָּחַשׁ (kâchash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be untrue**, in word (to **lie**, **feign**, **disown**) or deed (to **disappoint**, **fail**, **cringe**)

===== Definitions =====

  - be untrue
  - lie
  - feign
  - disown
  - disappoint
  - fail
  - cringe

===== Usage =====

deceive, deny, dissemble, fail, deal falsely, be found liars, (be-) lie, lying, submit selves.
