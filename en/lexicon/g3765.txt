====== G3765: οὐκέτι (oukéti) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3756|G3756]] and [[en:lexicon:g2089|G2089]];

===== Meaning =====

not yet, no longer

===== Usage =====

after that (not), (not) any more, henceforth (hereafter) not, no longer (more), not as yet (now), now no more (not), yet (not).
