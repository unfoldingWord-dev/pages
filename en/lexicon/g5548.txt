====== G5548: χρίω (chríō) ======

===== Source =====

probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g5530|G5530]] through the idea of contact;

===== Meaning =====

to smear or rub with oil, i.e. (by implication) to consecrate to an office or religious service

===== Usage =====

anoint.
