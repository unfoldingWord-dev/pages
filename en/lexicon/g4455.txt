====== G4455: πώποτε (pṓpote) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4452|G4452]] and [[en:lexicon:g4218|G4218]];

===== Meaning =====

at any time, i.e. (with negative particle) at no time

===== Usage =====

at any time, + never (…to any man), + yet, never man.
