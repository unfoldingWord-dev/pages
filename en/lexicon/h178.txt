====== H178: אוֹב (ʼôwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] (apparently through the idea of **prattling** a father's name);

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **mumble**, i.e. a water-**skin** (from its hollow sound); hence a **necromancer** (ventriloquist, as from a jar)

===== Definitions =====

  - prattling
  - mumble
  - skin
  - necromancer

===== Usage =====

bottle, familiar spirit.
