====== G3699: ὅπου (hópou) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3739|G3739]] and [[en:lexicon:g4225|G4225]];

===== Meaning =====

what(-ever) where, i.e. at whichever spot

===== Usage =====

in what place, where(-as, -soever), whither (+ soever).
