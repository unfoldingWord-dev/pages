====== H4370: מִכְנָס (miknâç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3647|H3647]] in the sense of **hiding**;

===== Meaning =====

(only in dual) **drawers** (from **concealing** the private parts)

===== Definitions =====

  - hiding
  - drawers
  - concealing

===== Usage =====

breeches.
