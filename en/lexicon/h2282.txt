====== H2282: חַג (chag) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h2287|H2287]];

===== Meaning =====

a **festival**, or a **victim** therefor

===== Definitions =====

  - festival
  - victim

===== Usage =====
