====== G3941: πάροικος (pároikos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g3624|G3624]];

===== Meaning =====

having a home near, i.e. (as noun) a by-dweller (alien resident)

===== Usage =====

foreigner, sojourn, stranger.
