====== H4743: מָקַק (mâqaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

' a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **melt**; figuratively, to **flow**, **dwindle**, **vanish**

===== Definitions =====

  - melt
  - flow
  - dwindle
  - vanish

===== Usage =====

consume away, be corrupt, dissolve, pine away.
