====== H2248: חֲבוּלָה (chăbûwlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:lexicon:h2255|H2255]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **overthrown**, i.e. (morally) **crime**

===== Definitions =====

  - overthrown
  - crime

===== Usage =====

hurt.
