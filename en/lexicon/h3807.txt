====== H3807: כָּתַת (kâthath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **bruise** or violently **strike**

===== Definitions =====

  - bruise
  - strike

===== Usage =====

beat (down, to pieces), break in pieces, crushed, destroy, discomfit, smite, stamp. l
