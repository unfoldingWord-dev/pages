====== H6641: צָבוּעַ (tsâbûwaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

passive participle of the same as [[en:lexicon:h6648|H6648]];

===== Meaning =====

**dyed** (in stripes), i.e. the **hyena**

===== Definitions =====

  - dyed
  - hyena

===== Usage =====

speckled.
