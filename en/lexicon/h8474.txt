====== H8474: תַּחָרָה (tachârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a facitious root from [[en:lexicon:h2734|H2734]] through the idea of the heat of jealousy;

===== Meaning =====

to **vie with a rival**

===== Definitions =====

  - vie with a rival

===== Usage =====

close, contend.
