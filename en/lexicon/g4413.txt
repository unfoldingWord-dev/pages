====== G4413: πρῶτος (prōtos) ======

===== Source =====

contracted superlative of [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]];

===== Meaning =====

foremost (in time, place, order or importance)

===== Usage =====

before, beginning, best, chief(-est), first (of all), former.
