====== G5178: τυμπανίζω (tympanízō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5180|G5180]] (meaning a drum, "tympanum");

===== Meaning =====

to stretch on an instrument of torture resembling a drum, and thus beat to death

===== Usage =====

torture.
