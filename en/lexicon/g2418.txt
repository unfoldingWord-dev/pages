====== G2418: ἱερουργέω (hierourgéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2411|G2411]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g2041|G2041]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a temple-worker, i.e. officiate as a priest (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

minister.
