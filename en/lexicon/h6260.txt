====== H6260: עַתּוּד (ʻattûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6257|H6257]];

===== Meaning =====

**prepared**, i.e. **full grown**; spoken only (in plural) of **he-goats**, or (figuratively) **leaders** of the people

===== Definitions =====

  - prepared
  - full grown
  - he-goats
  - leaders

===== Usage =====

chief one, (he) goat, ram.
