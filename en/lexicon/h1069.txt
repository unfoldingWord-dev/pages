====== H1069: בָּכַר (bâkar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to burst the womb, i.e. (causatively) bear or make early fruit (of woman or tree); also (as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h1061|H1061]])

===== Meaning =====

to **give the birthright**

===== Definitions =====

  - give the birthright

===== Usage =====

make firstborn, be firstling, bring forth first child (new fruit).
