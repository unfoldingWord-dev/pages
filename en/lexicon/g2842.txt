====== G2842: κοινωνία (koinōnía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2844|G2844]];

===== Meaning =====

partnership, i.e. (literally) participation, or (social) intercourse, or (pecuniary) benefaction

===== Usage =====

(to) communicate(-ation), communion, (contri-)distribution, fellowship.
