====== H4174: מוֹרָד (môwrâd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3381|H3381]];

===== Meaning =====

a **descent**; as architecture, an **ornamental appendage**, perhaps a **festoon**

===== Definitions =====

  - descent
  - ornamental appendage
  - festoon

===== Usage =====

going down, steep place, thin work.
