====== H7753: שׂוּךְ (sûwk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **entwine**, i.e. **shut in** (for formation, protection or restraint)

===== Definitions =====

  - entwine
  - shut in

===== Usage =====

fence, (make an) hedge (up).
