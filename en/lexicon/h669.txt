====== H669: אֶפְרַיִם (ʼEphrayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

dual of masculine form of [[en:lexicon:h672|H672]]; **double fruit**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ephrajim**, a son of Joseph; also the tribe descended from him, and its territory

===== Definitions =====

  - double fruit
  - Ephrajim

===== Usage =====

Ephraim, Ephraimites.
