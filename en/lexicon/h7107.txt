====== H7107: קָצַף (qâtsaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **crack** off, i.e. (figuratively) **burst** out in rage

===== Definitions =====

  - crack
  - burst

===== Usage =====

(be) anger(-ry), displease, fret self, (provoke to) wrath (come), be wroth.
