====== G3883: παραλλαγή (parallagḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g236|G236]];

===== Meaning =====

transmutation (of phase or orbit), i.e. (figuratively) fickleness

===== Usage =====

variableness.
