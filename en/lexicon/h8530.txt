====== H8530: תַּלְפִּיָּה (talpîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine from an unused root meaning to tower;

===== Meaning =====

something **tall**, i.e. (plural collective) **slenderness**

===== Definitions =====

  - tall
  - slenderness

===== Usage =====

armoury.
