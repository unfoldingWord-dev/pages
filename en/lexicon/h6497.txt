====== H6497: פֶּקַע (peqaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to burst; only used as an architectural term of an ornament similar to [[en:lexicon:h6498|H6498]],

===== Meaning =====

a **semi-globe**

===== Definitions =====

  - semi-globe

===== Usage =====

knop.
