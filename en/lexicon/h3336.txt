====== H3336: יֵצֶר (yêtser) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3335|H3335]];

===== Meaning =====

a **form**; figuratively, **conception** (i.e. purpose)

===== Definitions =====

  - form
  - conception

===== Usage =====

frame, thing framed, imagination, mind, work.
