====== H3619: כְּלוּב (kᵉlûwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h3611|H3611]];

===== Meaning =====

a **bird-trap** (as furnished with a clap-stick or treadle to spring it); hence, a **basket** (as resembling a wicker cage)

===== Definitions =====

  - bird-trap
  - basket

===== Usage =====

basket, cage.
