====== H4410: מְלוּכָה (mᵉlûwkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h4427|H4427]];

===== Meaning =====

something **ruled**, i.e. a **realm**

===== Definitions =====

  - ruled
  - realm

===== Usage =====

kingsom, king's, × royal.
