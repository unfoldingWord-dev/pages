====== G3717: ὀρθός (orthós) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the base of [[en:lexicon:g3735|G3735]];

===== Meaning =====

right (as rising), i.e. (perpendicularly) erect (figuratively, honest), or (horizontally) level or direct

===== Usage =====

straight, upright.
