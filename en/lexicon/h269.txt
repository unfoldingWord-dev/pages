====== H269: אָחוֹת (ʼâchôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

irregular feminine of [[en:lexicon:h251|H251]];

===== Meaning =====

a **sister** (used very widely [like [[en:lexicon:h251|H251]]], literally and figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - sister

===== Usage =====

(an-) other, sister, together.
