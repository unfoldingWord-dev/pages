====== H370: אַיִן (ʼayin) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

probably identical with [[en:lexicon:h369|H369]] in the sense of query (compare [[en:lexicon:h336|H336]])

===== Meaning =====

**where?** (only in connection with prepositional prefix, whence)

===== Definitions =====

  - where?

===== Usage =====

whence, where.
