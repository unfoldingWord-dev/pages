====== G4834: συμπαθέω (sympathéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4835|G4835]];

===== Meaning =====

to feel "sympathy" with, i.e. (by implication) to commiserate

===== Usage =====

have compassion, be touched with a feeling of.
