====== G5168: τρυμαλιά (trymaliá) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of τρύω (trýō) (to wear away; akin to the base of [[en:lexicon:g5134|G5134]],
 [[en:lexicon:g5147|G5147]] and [[en:lexicon:g5176|G5176]]);

===== Meaning =====

an orifice, i.e. needle's eye

===== Usage =====

eye.
