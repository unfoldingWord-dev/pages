====== G5097: τιμωρέω (timōréō) ======

===== Source =====

from a comparative of [[en:lexicon:g5092|G5092]] and οὖρος (oûros) ouros (a guard);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to protect one's honor, i.e. to avenge (inflict a penalty)

===== Usage =====

punish.
