====== G1908: ἐπηρεάζω (epēreázō) ======

===== Source =====

from a comparative of [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and (probably) ἀρειά (areiá) (threats);

===== Meaning =====

to insult, slander

===== Usage =====

use despitefully, falsely accuse.
