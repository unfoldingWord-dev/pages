====== H5832: עֲזַרְאֵל (ʻĂzarʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5826|H5826]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; **God has helped**;

===== Meaning =====

**Azarel**, the name of five Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - God has helped
  - Azarel

===== Usage =====

Azarael, Azareel.
