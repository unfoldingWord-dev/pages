====== H1503: גֶּזַע (gezaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **cut** down (trees);

===== Meaning =====

the **trunk** or **stump** of a tree (as felled or as planted)

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - trunk
  - stump

===== Usage =====

stem, stock.
