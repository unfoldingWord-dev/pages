====== H501: אֶלְעָשָׂה (ʼElʻâsâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]] and [[en:lexicon:h6213|H6213]]; God has made;

===== Meaning =====

**Elasah**, the name of four Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Elasah

===== Usage =====

Elasah, Eleasah.
