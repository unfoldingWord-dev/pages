====== G1704: ἐμπεριπατέω (emperipatéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g4043|G4043]];

===== Meaning =====

to perambulate on a place, i.e. (figuratively) to be occupied among persons

===== Usage =====

walk in.
