====== H5042: נָבַע (nâbaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **gush forth**; figuratively, to **utter** (good or bad words); specifically, to **emit** (a foul odor)

===== Definitions =====

  - gush forth
  - utter
  - emit

===== Usage =====

belch out, flowing, pour out, send forth, utter (abundantly).
