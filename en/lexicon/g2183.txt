====== G2183: ἐφημερία (ephēmería) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2184|G2184]];

===== Meaning =====

diurnality, i.e. (specially) the quotidian rotation or class of the Jewish priests' service at the Temple, as distributed by families

===== Usage =====

course.
