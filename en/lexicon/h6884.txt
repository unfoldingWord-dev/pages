====== H6884: צָרַף (tsâraph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **fuse** (metal), i.e. **refine** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - fuse
  - refine

===== Usage =====

cast, (re-) fine(-er), founder, goldsmith, melt, pure, purge away, try.
