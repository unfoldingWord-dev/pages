====== H6774: צִמָּאוֹן (tsimmâʼôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6771|H6771]];

===== Meaning =====

a **thirsty place**, i.e. **desert**

===== Definitions =====

  - thirsty place
  - desert

===== Usage =====

drought, dry ground, thirsty land.
