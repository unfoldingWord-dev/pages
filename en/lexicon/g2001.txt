====== G2001: ἐπισχύω (epischýō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g2480|G2480]];

===== Meaning =====

to avail further, i.e. (figuratively) insist stoutly

===== Usage =====

be the more fierce.
