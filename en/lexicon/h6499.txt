====== H6499: פַּר (par) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6565|H6565]];

===== Meaning =====

a **bullock** (apparently as **breaking** forth in wild strength, or perhaps as **dividing** the hoof)

===== Definitions =====

  - bullock
  - breaking
  - dividing

===== Usage =====

(  young) bull(-ock), calf, ox.
