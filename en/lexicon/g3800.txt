====== G3800: ὀψώνιον (opsṓnion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative of the same as [[en:lexicon:g3795|G3795]];

===== Meaning =====

rations for a soldier, i.e. (by extension) his stipend or pay

===== Usage =====

 wages.
