====== G4887: συνδέω (syndéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g1210|G1210]];

===== Meaning =====

to bind with, i.e. (passively) be a fellow-prisoner (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

be bound with.
