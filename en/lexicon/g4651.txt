====== G4651: σκορπίος (skorpíos) ======

===== Source =====

probably from an obsolete σκέρπω (skérpō) (perhaps strengthened from the base
 of [[en:lexicon:g4649|G4649]] and meaning to pierce);

===== Meaning =====

a "scorpion" (from its sting)

===== Usage =====

scorpion.
