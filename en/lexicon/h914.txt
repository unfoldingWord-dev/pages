====== H914: בָּדַל (bâdal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **divide** (in variation senses literally or figuratively, separate, distinguish, differ, select, etc.)

===== Definitions =====

  - divide

===== Usage =====

(make, put) difference, divide (asunder), (make) separate (self, -ation), sever (out), × utterly.
