====== G5035: ταχύ (tachý) ======

===== Source =====

neuter singular of [[en:lexicon:g5036|G5036]] (as adverb);

===== Meaning =====

shortly, i.e. without delay, soon, or (by surprise) suddenly, or (by implication, of ease) readily

===== Usage =====

lightly, quickly.
