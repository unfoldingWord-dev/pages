====== H484: אַלְמֻגִּים (ʼalmuggîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably of foreign derivation (used thus only in the plural);

===== Meaning =====

**almug** (i.e. probably sandle-wood) **sticks**

===== Definitions =====

  - almug
  - sticks

===== Usage =====

almug trees. Compare
