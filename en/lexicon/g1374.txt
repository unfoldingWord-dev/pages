====== G1374: δίψυχος (dípsychos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1364|G1364]] and [[en:lexicon:g5590|G5590]];

===== Meaning =====

two-spirited, i.e. vacillating (in opinion or purpose)

===== Usage =====

double minded.
