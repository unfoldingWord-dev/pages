====== H659: אֵפַע (ʼêphaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to **breathe**;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **breath**, i.e. **nothing**

===== Definitions =====

  - breathe
  - breath
  - nothing

===== Usage =====

of nought.
