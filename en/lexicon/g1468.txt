====== G1468: ἐγκρατής (enkratḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g2904|G2904]];

===== Meaning =====

strong in a thing (masterful), i.e. (figuratively and reflexively) self-controlled (in appetite, etc.)

===== Usage =====

temperate.
