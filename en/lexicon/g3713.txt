====== G3713: ὀρέγομαι (orégomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice of apparently a prolonged form of an obsolete primary
 (compare [[en:lexicon:g3735|G3735]]);

===== Meaning =====

to stretch oneself, i.e. reach out after (long for)

===== Usage =====

covet after, desire.
