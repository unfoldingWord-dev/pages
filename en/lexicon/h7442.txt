====== H7442: רָנַן (rânan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **creak** (or emit a stridulous sound), i.e. to **shout** (usually for joy)

===== Definitions =====

  - creak
  - shout

===== Usage =====

aloud for joy, cry out, be joyful (greatly, make to) rejoice, (cause to) shout (for joy), (cause to) sing (aloud, for joy, out), triumph.
