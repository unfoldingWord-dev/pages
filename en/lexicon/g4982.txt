====== G4982: σώζω (sṓzō) ======

===== Source =====

from a primary σῶς (sōs) (contraction for obsolete σάος (sáos), "safe");

===== Meaning =====

to save, i.e. deliver or protect (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

heal, preserve, save (self), do well, be (make) whole.
