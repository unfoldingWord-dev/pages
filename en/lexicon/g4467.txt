====== G4467: ῥᾳδιούργημα (rhaidioúrgēma) ======

===== Source =====

from a comparative of ῥᾴδιος (rháidios) (easy, i.e. reckless) and [[en:lexicon:g2041|G2041]];

===== Meaning =====

easy-going behavior, i.e. (by extension) a crime

===== Usage =====

lewdness.
