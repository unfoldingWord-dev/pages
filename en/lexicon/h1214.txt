====== H1214: בָּצַע (bâtsaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root

===== Meaning =====

to **break** off, i.e. (usually) **plunder**; figuratively, to **finish**, or (intransitively) **stop**

===== Definitions =====

  - break
  - plunder
  - finish
  - stop

===== Usage =====

(be) covet(-ous), cut (off), finish, fulfill, gain (greedily), get, be given to (covetousness), greedy, perform, be wounded.
