====== H7797: שׂוּשׂ (sûws) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be bright**, i.e. **cheerful**

===== Definitions =====

  - be bright
  - cheerful

===== Usage =====

be glad, × greatly, joy, make mirth, rejoice.
