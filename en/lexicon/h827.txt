====== H827: אַשְׁפָּה (ʼashpâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

perhaps (feminine) from the same as [[en:lexicon:h825|H825]] (in the sense of **covering**);

===== Meaning =====

a **quiver** or arrow-case

===== Definitions =====

  - covering
  - quiver

===== Usage =====

quiver.
