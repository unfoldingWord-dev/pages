====== H5265: נָסַע (nâçaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **pull** up, especially the tent-pins, i.e. **start** on a journey

===== Definitions =====

  - pull
  - start

===== Usage =====

cause to blow, bring, get, (make to) go (away, forth, forward, onward, out), (take) journey, march, remove, set aside (forward), × still, be on his (go their) way.
