====== G2603: καταβραβεύω (katabrabeúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g1018|G1018]] (in its original sense);

===== Meaning =====

to award the price against, i.e. (figuratively) to defraud (of salvation)

===== Usage =====

beguile of reward.
