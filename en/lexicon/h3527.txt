====== H3527: כָּבַר (kâbar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **plait together**, i.e. (figuratively) to **augment** (especially in number or quantity, to accumulate)

===== Definitions =====

  - plait together
  - augment

===== Usage =====

in abundance, multiply.
