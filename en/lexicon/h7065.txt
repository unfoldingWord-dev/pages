====== H7065: קָנָא (qânâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **make**) **zealous**, i.e. (in a bad sense) **jealous** or **envious**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - zealous
  - jealous
  - envious

===== Usage =====

(be) envy(-ious), be (move to, provoke to) jealous(-y), × very, (be) zeal(-ous).
