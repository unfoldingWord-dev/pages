====== H8213: שָׁפֵל (shâphêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **depress** or **sink** (especially figuratively, to **humiliate**, intransitive or transitive)

===== Definitions =====

  - depress
  - sink
  - humiliate

===== Usage =====

abase, bring (cast, put) down, debase, humble (self), be (bring, lay, make, put) low(-er).
