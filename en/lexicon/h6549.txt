====== H6549: פַּרְעֹה נְכֹה (Parʻôh Nᵉkôh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; of Egyptian derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**Paroh-Nekoh** (or -Neko), an Egyptian king

===== Definitions =====

  - Paroh-Nekoh

===== Usage =====

Pharaoh-necho, Pharaohnechoh.
