====== G2455: Ἰούδας (Ioúdas) ======

===== Source =====

of Hebrew origin ([[en:lexicon:h03063|H03063]]);

===== Meaning =====

Judas (i.e. Jehudah), the name of ten Israelites; also of the posterity of one of them and its region

===== Usage =====

Juda(-h, -s); Jude.
