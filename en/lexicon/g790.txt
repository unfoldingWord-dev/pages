====== G790: ἀστατέω (astatéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]];

===== Meaning =====

to be non-stationary, i.e. (figuratively) homeless

===== Usage =====

have no certain dwelling-place.
