====== H6016: עֹמֶר (ʻômer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6014|H6014]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **heap**, i.e. a **sheaf**; also an **omer**, as a dry measure

===== Definitions =====

  - heap
  - sheaf
  - omer

===== Usage =====

omer, sheaf.
