====== G606: ἀπόκειμαι (apókeimai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g2749|G2749]];

===== Meaning =====

to be reserved; figuratively, to await

===== Usage =====

be appointed, (be) laid up.
