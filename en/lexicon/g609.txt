====== G609: ἀποκόπτω (apokóptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g2875|G2875]];

===== Meaning =====

to amputate; reflexively (by irony) to mutilate (the privy parts)

===== Usage =====

cut off.
