====== H4366: מִכְמְתָת (Mikmᵉthâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

apparently from an unused root meaning to hide; concealment;

===== Meaning =====

**Mikmethath**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Mikmethath

===== Usage =====

Michmethath.
