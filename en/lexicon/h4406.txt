====== H4406: מִלָּה (millâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h4405|H4405]];

===== Meaning =====

a **word**, **command**, **discourse**, or **subject**

===== Definitions =====

  - word
  - command
  - discourse
  - subject

===== Usage =====

commandment, matter, thing. word.
