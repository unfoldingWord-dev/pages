====== H8321: שֹׂרֵק (sôrêq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; and (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h8319|H8319]] in the sense of **redness** (compare [[en:lexicon:h8320|H8320]]);

===== Meaning =====

a **vine** stock (properly, one yielding **purple** grapes, the richest variety)

===== Definitions =====

  - redness
  - vine
  - purple
