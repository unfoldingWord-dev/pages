====== H4199: מִזָּה (Mizzâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from an unused root meaning to faint with fear; **terror**;

===== Meaning =====

**Mizzah**, an Edomite

===== Definitions =====

  - terror
  - Mizzah

===== Usage =====

Mizzah.
