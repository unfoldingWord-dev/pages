====== H1297: בְּרַם (bᵉram) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) perhaps from [[en:lexicon:h7313|H7313]] with a prepositional prefix;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **highly**, i.e. **surely**; but used adversatively, **however**

===== Definitions =====

  - highly
  - surely
  - however

===== Usage =====

but, nevertheless, yet.
