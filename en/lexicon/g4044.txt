====== G4044: περιπείρω (peripeírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g4008|G4008]];

===== Meaning =====

to penetrate entirely, i.e. transfix (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

pierce through.
