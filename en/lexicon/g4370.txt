====== G4370: προστρέχω (prostréchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g5143|G5143]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to run towards, i.e. hasten to meet or join

===== Usage =====

run (thither to, to).
