====== G563: ἀπερισπάστως (aperispástōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative participle) and a presumed
 derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4049|G4049]];

===== Meaning =====

undistractedly, i.e. free from (domestic) solicitude

===== Usage =====

without distraction.
