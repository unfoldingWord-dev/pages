====== H5378: נָשָׁא (nâshâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (perhaps identical with [[en:lexicon:h5377|H5377]], through the idea of **imposition**);

===== Meaning =====

to **lend** on interest; by implication, to **dun** for debt

===== Definitions =====

  - imposition
  - lend
  - dun

===== Usage =====

× debt, exact, giver of usury.
