====== H5732: עִדָּן (ʻiddân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to that of [[en:lexicon:h5708|H5708]];

===== Meaning =====

a **set time**; technically, a **year**

===== Definitions =====

  - set time
  - year

===== Usage =====

time.
