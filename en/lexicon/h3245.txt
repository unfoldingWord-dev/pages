====== H3245: יָסַד (yâçad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **set** (literally or figuratively); intensively, to **found**; reflexively, to **sit** down together, i.e. **settle**, **consult**

===== Definitions =====

  - set
  - found
  - sit
  - settle
  - consult

===== Usage =====

appoint, take counsel, establish, (lay the, lay for a) found(-ation), instruct, lay, ordain, set, × sure.
