====== G3965: πατριά (patriá) ======

===== Source =====

as if feminine of a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3962|G3962]];

===== Meaning =====

paternal descent, i.e. (concretely) a group of families or a whole race (nation)

===== Usage =====

family, kindred, lineage.
