====== G2702: καταφέρω (kataphérō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to bear down, i.e. (figuratively) overcome (with drowsiness); specially, to cast a vote

===== Usage =====

fall, give, sink down.
