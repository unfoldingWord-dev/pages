====== G211: ἀλάβαστρον (alábastron) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of ἀλάβαστρος (alábastros) (of uncertain derivation), the name of a stone;

===== Meaning =====

properly, an "alabaster" box, i.e. (by extension) a perfume vase (of any material)

===== Usage =====

(alabaster) box.
