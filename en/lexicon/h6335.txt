====== H6335: פּוּשׁ (pûwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **spread**; figuratively, **act proudly**

===== Definitions =====

  - spread
  - act proudly

===== Usage =====

grow up, be grown fat, spread selves, be scattered.
