====== H737: אֲרֻחָה (ʼăruchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h732|H732]] (in the sense of appointing);

===== Meaning =====

a **ration of food**

===== Definitions =====

  - ration of food

===== Usage =====

allowance, diet, dinner, victuals.
