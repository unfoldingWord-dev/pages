====== G4067: περίψωμα (perípsōma) ======

===== Source =====

from a comparative of [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and ψάω (psáō) (to rub);

===== Meaning =====

something brushed all around, i.e. off-scrapings (figuratively, scum)

===== Usage =====

offscouring.
