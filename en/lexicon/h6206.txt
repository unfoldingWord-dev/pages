====== H6206: עָרַץ (ʻârats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **awe** or (intransitive) to **dread**; hence, to **harass**

===== Definitions =====

  - awe
  - dread
  - harass

===== Usage =====

be affrighted (afraid, dread, feared, terrified), break, dread, fear, oppress, prevail, shake terribly.
