====== H982: בָּטַח (bâṭach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **hie** for refuge (but not so **precipitately** as [[en:lexicon:h2620|H2620]]); figuratively, to **trust**, be **confident** or **sure**

===== Definitions =====

  - hie
  - precipitately
  - trust
  - confident
  - sure

===== Usage =====

be bold (confident, secure, sure), careless (one, woman), put confidence, (make to) hope, (put, make to) trust.
