====== G1467: ἐγκρατεύομαι (enkrateúomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1468|G1468]];

===== Meaning =====

to exercise self-restraint (in diet and chastity)

===== Usage =====

can(-not) contain, be temperate.
