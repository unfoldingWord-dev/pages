====== H5043: נֶבְרְשָׁא (nebrᵉshâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from an unused root meaning to shine;

===== Meaning =====

a **light**; plural (collectively) a **chandelier**

===== Definitions =====

  - light
  - chandelier

===== Usage =====

candlestick.
