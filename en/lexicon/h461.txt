====== H461: אֱלִיעֶזֶר (ʼĔlîyʻezer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]] and [[en:lexicon:h5828|H5828]]; **God of help**;

===== Meaning =====

**Eliezer**, the name of a Damascene and of ten Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - God of help
  - Eliezer

===== Usage =====

Eliezer.
