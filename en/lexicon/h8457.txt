====== H8457: תַּזְנוּת (taznûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h2181|H2181]];

===== Meaning =====

**harlotry**, i.e. (figuratively) **idolatry**

===== Definitions =====

  - harlotry
  - idolatry

===== Usage =====

fornication, whoredom.
