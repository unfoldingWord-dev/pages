====== H4340: מֵיתָר (mêythâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3498|H3498]]; (compare [[en:lexicon:h3499|H3499]])

===== Meaning =====

a **cord** (of a tent); or the **string** (of a bow)

===== Definitions =====

  - cord
  - string

===== Usage =====

cord, string.
