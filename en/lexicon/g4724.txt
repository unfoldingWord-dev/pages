====== G4724: στέλλω (stéllō) ======

===== Source =====

probably strengthened from the base of [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to set fast ("stall"), i.e. (figuratively) to repress (reflexively, abstain from associating with)

===== Usage =====

avoid, withdraw self.
