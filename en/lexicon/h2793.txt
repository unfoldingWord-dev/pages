====== H2793: חֹרֶשׁ (chôresh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2790|H2790]];

===== Meaning =====

a **forest** (perhaps as furnishing the material for fabric)

===== Definitions =====

  - forest

===== Usage =====

bough, forest, shroud, wood.
