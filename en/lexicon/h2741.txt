====== H2741: חֲרוּפִי (Chărûwphîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

a patrial from (probably) a collateral form of [[en:lexicon:h2756|H2756]];

===== Meaning =====

**a Charuphite** or inhabitant of Charuph (or Chariph)

===== Definitions =====

  - a Charuphite

===== Usage =====

Haruphite.
