====== H6926: קִדְמָה (qidmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h6924|H6924]];

===== Meaning =====

the **forward** part (or relatively) **East** (often adverbially, on the east or in front)

===== Definitions =====

  - forward
  - East

===== Usage =====

east(-ward).
