====== H2370: חֲזָא (chăzâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Aramaic), corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h2372|H2372]];

===== Meaning =====

to **gaze upon**; mentally to **dream**, **be usual** (i.e. seem)

===== Definitions =====

  - gaze upon
  - dream
  - be usual

===== Usage =====

behold, have (a dream), see, be wont.
