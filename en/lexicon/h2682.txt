====== H2682: חָצִיר (châtsîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

perhaps originally the same as [[en:lexicon:h2681|H2681]], from the **greenness** of a courtyard;

===== Meaning =====

**grass**; also a **leek** (collectively)

===== Definitions =====

  - greenness
  - grass
  - leek

===== Usage =====

grass, hay, herb, leek.
