====== H4464: מַמְזֵר (mamzêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **alienate**;

===== Meaning =====

a **mongrel**, i.e. born of a Jewish father and a heathen mother

===== Definitions =====

  - alienate
  - mongrel

===== Usage =====

bastard.
