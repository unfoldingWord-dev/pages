====== G3887: παραμένω (paraménō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g3306|G3306]];

===== Meaning =====

to stay near, i.e. remain (literally, tarry; or figuratively, be permanent, persevere)

===== Usage =====

abide, continue.
