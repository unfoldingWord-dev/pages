====== H4564: מַסְתֵּר (maçtêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5641|H5641]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **hider**, i.e. (abstractly) a hiding, i.e. **aversion**

===== Definitions =====

  - hider
  - aversion

===== Usage =====

hid.
