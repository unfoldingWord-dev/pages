====== H3173: יָחִיד (yâchîyd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3161|H3161]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **united**, i.e. **sole**; by implication, **beloved**; also **lonely**; (feminine) the **life** (as not to be replaced)

===== Definitions =====

  - united
  - sole
  - beloved
  - lonely
  - life

===== Usage =====

darling, desolate, only (child, son), solitary.
