====== G5048: τελειόω (teleióō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5046|G5046]];

===== Meaning =====

to complete, i.e. (literally) accomplish, or (figuratively) consummate (in character)

===== Usage =====

consecrate, finish, fulfil, make) perfect.
