====== H7091: קִפּוֹז (qippôwz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **contract**, i.e.

===== Meaning =====

**spring** forward; an **arrow-snake** (as **darting** on its prey)

===== Definitions =====

  - contract
  - spring
  - arrow-snake
  - darting

===== Usage =====

great owl.
