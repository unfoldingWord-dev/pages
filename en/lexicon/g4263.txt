====== G4263: πρόβατον (próbaton) ======

===== Source =====

probably neuter of a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4260|G4260]];

===== Meaning =====

something that walks forward (a quadruped), i.e. (specially), a sheep (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

sheep(-fold).
