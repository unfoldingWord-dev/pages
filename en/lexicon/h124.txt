====== H124: אֹדֶם (ʼôdem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h119|H119]];

===== Meaning =====

**redness**, i.e. the **ruby**, **garnet**, or some other red gem

===== Definitions =====

  - redness
  - ruby
  - garnet

===== Usage =====

sardius.
