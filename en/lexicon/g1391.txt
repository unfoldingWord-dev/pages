====== G1391: δόξα (dóxa) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g1380|G1380]];

===== Meaning =====

glory (as very apparent), in a wide application (literal or figurative, objective or subjective)

===== Usage =====

dignity, glory(-ious), honour, praise, worship.
