====== H594: אֲנָךְ (ʼănâk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from an unused root meaning to be narrow;

===== Meaning =====

according to most a **plumb-line**, and to others a **hook**

===== Definitions =====

  - plumb-line
  - hook

===== Usage =====

plumb-line
