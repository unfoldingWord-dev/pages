====== H1973: הָלְאָה (hâlᵉʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from the primitive form of the article (hal);

===== Meaning =====

to the **distance**, i.e. **far away**; also (of time) **thus far**

===== Definitions =====

  - distance
  - far away
  - thus far

===== Usage =====

back, beyond, (hence,-) forward, hitherto, thence, forth, yonder.
