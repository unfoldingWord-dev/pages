====== H2673: חָצָה (châtsâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2086|H2086]]));

===== Meaning =====

to **cut** or **split** in two; to **halve**

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - split
  - halve

===== Usage =====

divide, × live out half, reach to the midst, participle
