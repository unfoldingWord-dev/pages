====== H3154: יֶזַע (yezaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root mean to ooze;

===== Meaning =====

**sweat**, i.e. (by implication) a **sweating dress**

===== Definitions =====

  - sweat
  - sweating dress

===== Usage =====

any thing that causeth sweat.
