====== H3363: יָקַע (yâqaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **sever** oneself, i.e. (by implication) to **be dislocated**; figuratively, to **abandon**; causatively, to **impale** (and thus allow to drop to pieces by rotting)

===== Definitions =====

  - sever
  - be dislocated
  - abandon
  - impale

===== Usage =====

be alienated, depart, hang (up), be out of joint.
