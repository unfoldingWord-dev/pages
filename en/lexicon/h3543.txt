====== H3543: כָּהָה (kâhâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be weak**, i.e. (figuratively) to **despond** (causatively, **rebuke**), or (of light, the eye) to **grow dull**

===== Definitions =====

  - be weak
  - despond
  - rebuke
  - grow dull

===== Usage =====

darken, be dim, fail, faint, restrain, × utterly.
