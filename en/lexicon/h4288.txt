====== H4288: מְחִתָּה (mᵉchittâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2846|H2846]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **dissolution**; concretely, a **ruin**, or (abstractly) **consternation**

===== Definitions =====

  - dissolution
  - ruin
  - consternation

===== Usage =====

destruction, dismaying, ruin, terror.
