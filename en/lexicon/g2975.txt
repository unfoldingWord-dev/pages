====== G2975: λαγχάνω (lanchánō) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of a primary verb, which is only used as an alternate
 in certain tenses;

===== Meaning =====

to lot, i.e. determine (by implication, receive) especially by lot

===== Usage =====

his lot be, cast lots, obtain.
