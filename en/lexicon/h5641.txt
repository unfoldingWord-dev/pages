====== H5641: סָתַר (çâthar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **hide** (by covering), literally or figuratively

===== Definitions =====

  - hide

===== Usage =====

be absent, keep close, conceal, hide (self), (keep) secret, × surely.
