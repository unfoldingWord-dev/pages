====== H1589: גָּנַב (gânab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **thieve** (literally or figuratively); by implication, to **deceive**

===== Definitions =====

  - thieve
  - deceive

===== Usage =====

carry away, × indeed, secretly bring, steal (away), get by stealth.
