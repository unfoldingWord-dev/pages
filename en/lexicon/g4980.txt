====== G4980: σχολάζω (scholázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4981|G4981]];

===== Meaning =====

to take a holiday, i.e. be at leisure for (by implication, devote oneself wholly to); figuratively, to be vacant (of a house)

===== Usage =====

empty, give self.
