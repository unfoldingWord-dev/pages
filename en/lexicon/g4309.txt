====== G4309: προορίζω (proorízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g3724|G3724]];

===== Meaning =====

to limit in advance, i.e. (figuratively) predetermine

===== Usage =====

determine before, ordain, predestinate.
