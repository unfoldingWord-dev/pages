====== H2810: חִשָּׁבוֹן (chishshâbôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2803|H2803]];

===== Meaning =====

a **contrivance**, i.e. **actual** (a warlike machine) or **mental** (a machination)

===== Definitions =====

  - contrivance
  - actual
  - mental

===== Usage =====

engine, invention.
