====== H3754: כֶּרֶם (kerem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root of uncertain meaning;

===== Meaning =====

a **garden** or **vineyard**

===== Definitions =====

  - garden
  - vineyard

===== Usage =====

vines, (increase of the) vineyard(-s), vintage. See also
