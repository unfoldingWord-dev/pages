====== G2639: καταλέγω (katalégō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]] (in its original meaning);

===== Meaning =====

to lay down, i.e. (figuratively) to enrol

===== Usage =====
