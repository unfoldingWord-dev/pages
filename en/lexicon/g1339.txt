====== G1339: διΐστημι (diḯstēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]];

===== Meaning =====

to stand apart, i.e. (reflexively) to remove, intervene

===== Usage =====

go further, be parted, after the space of.
