====== G4785: συγκαταψηφίζω (synkatapsēphízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g5585|G5585]];

===== Meaning =====

to count down in company with, i.e. enroll among

===== Usage =====

number with.
