====== G2158: εὐσχήμων (euschḗmōn) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and [[en:lexicon:g4976|G4976]];

===== Meaning =====

well-formed, i.e. (figuratively) decorous, noble (in rank)

===== Usage =====

comely, honourable.
