====== H1158: בָּעָה (bâʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **gush** over, i.e. to **swell**; (figuratively) to **desire** earnestly; by implication to **ask**

===== Definitions =====

  - gush
  - swell
  - desire
  - ask

===== Usage =====

cause, inquire, seek up, swell out.
