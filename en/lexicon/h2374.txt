====== H2374: חֹזֶה (chôzeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

active participle of [[en:lexicon:h2372|H2372]];

===== Meaning =====

a **beholder** in vision; also a **compact** (as **looked upon** with approval)

===== Definitions =====

  - beholder
  - compact
  - looked upon

===== Usage =====

agreement, prophet, see that, seer, (star-) gazer.
