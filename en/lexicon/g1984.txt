====== G1984: ἐπισκοπή (episkopḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1980|G1980]];

===== Meaning =====

inspection (for relief); by implication, superintendence; specially, the Christian "episcopate"

===== Usage =====

the office of a "bishop", bishoprick, visitation.
