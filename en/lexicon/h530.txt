====== H530: אֱמוּנָה (ʼĕmûwnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine of [[en:lexicon:h529|H529]];

===== Meaning =====

literally **firmness**; figuratively **security**; morally **fidelity**

===== Definitions =====

  - firmness
  - security
  - fidelity

===== Usage =====

faith(-ful, -ly, -ness, (man)), set office, stability, steady, truly, truth, verily.
