====== H4829: מִרְעֶה (mirʻeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7462|H7462]] in the sense of feeding;

===== Meaning =====

**pasture** (the place or the act); also the **haunt** of wild animals

===== Definitions =====

  - pasture
  - haunt

===== Usage =====

feeding place, pasture.
