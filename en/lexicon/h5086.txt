====== H5086: נָדַף (nâdaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **shove** asunder, i.e. **disperse**

===== Definitions =====

  - shove
  - disperse

===== Usage =====

drive (away, to and fro), thrust down, shaken, tossed to and fro.
