====== H6815: צַעֲנַנִּים (Tsaʻănannîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

or (dual) [[en:lexicon:|]]; plural from [[en:lexicon:h6813|H6813]]; **removals**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tsaanannim** or **Tsaanajim**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - removals
  - Tsaanannim
  - Tsaanajim

===== Usage =====

Zaannannim, Zaanaim.
