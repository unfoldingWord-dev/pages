====== G1950: ἐπιλανθάνομαι (epilanthánomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g2990|G2990]];

===== Meaning =====

to lose out of mind; by implication, to neglect

===== Usage =====

(be) forget(-ful of).
