====== G676: ἀπρόσιτος (aprósitos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of a comparative of
 [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and εἶμι (eîmi) (to go);

===== Meaning =====

inaccessible

===== Usage =====

which no man can approach.
