====== H8058: שָׁמַט (shâmaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **fling down**; incipiently to **jostle**; figuratively, to **let alone**, **desist**, **remit**

===== Definitions =====

  - fling down
  - jostle
  - let alone
  - desist
  - remit

===== Usage =====

discontinue, overthrow, release, let rest, shake, stumble, throw down.
