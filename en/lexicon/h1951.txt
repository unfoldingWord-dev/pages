====== H1951: הוּן (hûwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be naught**, i.e. (figuratively) to **be** (causatively, **act**) **light**

===== Definitions =====

  - be naught
  - be
  - act
  - light

===== Usage =====

be ready.
