====== G272: ἀμελέω (ameléō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g3199|G3199]];

===== Meaning =====

to be careless of

===== Usage =====

make light of, neglect, be negligent, no regard.
