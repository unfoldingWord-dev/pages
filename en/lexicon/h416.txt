====== H416: אֵל בֵּית־אֵל (ʼÊl Bêyth-ʼÊl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]] and [[en:lexicon:h1008|H1008]]; the God of Bethel;

===== Meaning =====

**El-Bethel**, the title given to a consecrated spot by Jacob

===== Definitions =====

  - El-Bethel

===== Usage =====

El-beth-el.
