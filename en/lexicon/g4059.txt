====== G4059: περιτέμνω (peritémnō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5114|G5114]];

===== Meaning =====

to cut around, i.e. (specially) to circumcise

===== Usage =====
