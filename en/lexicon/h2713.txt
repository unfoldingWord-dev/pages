====== H2713: חָקַר (châqar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **penetrate**; hence, to **examine intimately**

===== Definitions =====

  - penetrate
  - examine intimately

===== Usage =====

find out, (make) search (out), seek (out), sound, try.
