====== H1494: גָּזַז (gazâz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (akin to [[en:lexicon:h1468|H1468]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **cut off**; specifically to **shear** a flock or **shave** the hair; figuratively to **destroy** an enemy

===== Definitions =====

  - cut off
  - shear
  - shave
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

cut off (down), poll, shave, (sheep-) shear(-er).
