====== G5268: ὑποζύγιον (hypozýgion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g2218|G2218]];

===== Meaning =====

an animal under the yoke (draught-beast), i.e. (specially), a donkey

===== Usage =====

ass.
