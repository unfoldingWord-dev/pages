====== H1697: דָּבָר (dâbâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1696|H1696]];

===== Meaning =====

a **word**; by implication, a **matter** (as **spoken** of) or **thing**; adverbially, a **cause**

===== Definitions =====

  - word
  - matter
  - spoken
  - thing
  - cause

===== Usage =====
