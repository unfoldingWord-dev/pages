====== H3238: יָנָה (yânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **rage** or **be violent**; by implication, to **suppress**, to **maltreat**

===== Definitions =====

  - rage
  - be violent
  - suppress
  - maltreat

===== Usage =====

destroy, (thrust out by) oppress(-ing, -ion, -or), proud, vex, do violence.
