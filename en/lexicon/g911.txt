====== G911: βάπτω (báptō) ======

===== Source =====

a primary verb;

===== Meaning =====

to whelm, i.e. cover wholly with a fluid; in the New Testament only in a qualified or special sense, i.e. (literally) to moisten (a part of one's person), or (by implication) to stain (as with dye)

===== Usage =====

dip.
