====== H408: אַל (ʼal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

a negative particle (akin to [[en:lexicon:h3808|H3808]]);

===== Meaning =====

**not** (the qualified negation, used as a deprecative); once (Job 24:25) as a noun, **nothing**

===== Definitions =====

  - not
  - nothing

===== Usage =====

nay, neither, + never, no, nor, not, nothing (worth), rather than.
