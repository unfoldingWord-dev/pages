====== G1624: ἐκτρέπω (ektrépō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5157|G5157]];

===== Meaning =====

to deflect, i.e. turn away (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

avoid, turn (aside, out of the way).
