====== G4336: προσεύχομαι (proseúchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g2172|G2172]];

===== Meaning =====

to pray to God, i.e. supplicate, worship

===== Usage =====

pray (X earnestly, for), make prayer.
