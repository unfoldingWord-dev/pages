====== H4217: מִזְרָח (mizrâch) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2224|H2224]];

===== Meaning =====

**sunrise**, i.e. the **east**

===== Definitions =====

  - sunrise
  - east

===== Usage =====

east (side, -ward), (sun-) rising (of the sun).
