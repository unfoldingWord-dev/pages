====== H1120: בָּמוֹת (Bâmôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

plural of [[en:lexicon:h1116|H1116]]; heights; or (fully) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from the same and [[en:lexicon:h1168|H1168]]; heights of Baal;

===== Meaning =====

**Bamoth** or **Bamoth-Baal**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Bamoth
  - Bamoth-Baal

===== Usage =====

Bamoth, Bamoth-baal.
