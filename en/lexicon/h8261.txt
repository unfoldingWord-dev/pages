====== H8261: שָׁקֻף (shâquph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h8259|H8259]]; (compare [[en:lexicon:h8260|H8260]])

===== Meaning =====

an **embrasure** or **opening**	with bevelled jam

===== Definitions =====

  - embrasure
  - opening

===== Usage =====

light, window.
