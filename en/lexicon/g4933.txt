====== G4933: συντηρέω (syntēréō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g5083|G5083]];

===== Meaning =====

to keep closely together, i.e. (by implication) to conserve (from ruin); mentally, to remember (and obey)

===== Usage =====

keep, observe, preserve.
