====== G2698: κατατίθημι (katatíthēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

to place down, i.e. deposit (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

do, lay, shew.
