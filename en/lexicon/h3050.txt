====== H3050: יָהּ (Yâhh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun

===== Source =====

contraction for [[en:lexicon:h3068|H3068]], and meaning the same;

===== Meaning =====

**Jah**, the sacred name

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah

===== Usage =====

Jah, the Lord, most vehement. Compare names in '-iah,' '-jah.'
