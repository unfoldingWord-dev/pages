====== H1305: בָּרַר (bârar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **clarify** (i.e. **brighten**), **examine**, **select**

===== Definitions =====

  - clarify
  - brighten
  - examine
  - select

===== Usage =====

make bright, choice, chosen, cleanse (be clean), clearly, polished, (shew self) pure(-ify), purge (out).
