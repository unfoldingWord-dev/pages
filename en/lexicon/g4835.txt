====== G4835: συμπαθής (sympathḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4841|G4841]];

===== Meaning =====

having a fellow-feeling ("sympathetic"), i.e. (by implication) mutually commiserative

===== Usage =====

having compassion one of another.
