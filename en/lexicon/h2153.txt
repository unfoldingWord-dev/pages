====== H2153: זִלְפָּה (Zilpâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

; from an unused root apparently meaning to trickle, as myrrh; fragrant dropping;

===== Meaning =====

**Zilpah**, Leah's maid

===== Definitions =====

  - Zilpah

===== Usage =====

Zilpah.
