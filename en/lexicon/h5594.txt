====== H5594: סָפַד (çâphad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to tear the hair and beat the breasts (as Orientals do in grief); generally to **lament**; by implication, to **wail**

===== Definitions =====

  - lament
  - wail

===== Usage =====

lament, mourn(-er), wail.
