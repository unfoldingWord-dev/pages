====== G3473: μωρολογία (mōrología) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3474|G3474]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]];

===== Meaning =====

silly talk, i.e. buffoonery

===== Usage =====

foolish talking.
