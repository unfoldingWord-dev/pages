====== H3218: יֶלֶק (yeleq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to lick up;

===== Meaning =====

a **devourer**; specifically, the **young locust**

===== Definitions =====

  - devourer
  - young locust

===== Usage =====

cankerworm, caterpillar.
