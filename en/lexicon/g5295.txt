====== G5295: ὑποτρέχω (hypotréchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g5143|G5143]] (including its alternate);

===== Meaning =====

to run under, i.e. (specially), to sail past

===== Usage =====
