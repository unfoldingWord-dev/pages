====== G4214: πόσος (pósos) ======

===== Source =====

from an absolute πός (pós) (who, what) and [[en:lexicon:g3739|G3739]];

===== Meaning =====

interrogative pronoun (of amount) how much (large, long or (plural) many)

===== Usage =====

how great (long, many), what.
