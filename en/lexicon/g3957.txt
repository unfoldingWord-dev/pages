====== G3957: πάσχα (páscha) ======

===== Source =====

of Chaldee origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h06453|H06453]]);

===== Meaning =====

the Passover (the meal, the day, the festival or the special sacrifices connected with it)

===== Usage =====

Easter, Passover.
