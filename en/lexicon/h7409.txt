====== H7409: רֶכֶשׁ (rekesh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7408|H7408]];

===== Meaning =====

a **relay of animals on a post-route** (as stored up for that purpose); by implication, a **courser**

===== Definitions =====

  - relay of animals on a post-route
  - courser

===== Usage =====

dromedary, mule, swift beast.
