====== G5030: ταχέως (tachéōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from [[en:lexicon:g5036|G5036]];

===== Meaning =====

briefly, i.e. (in time) speedily, or (in manner) rapidly

===== Usage =====

hastily, quickly, shortly, soon, suddenly.
