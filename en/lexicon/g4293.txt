====== G4293: προκαταγγέλλω (prokatangéllō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2605|G2605]];

===== Meaning =====

to anounce beforehand, i.e. predict, promise

===== Usage =====

foretell, have notice, (shew) before.
