====== G3789: ὄφις (óphis) ======

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:g3700|G3700]] (through the idea of sharpness of vision);

===== Meaning =====

a snake, figuratively, (as a type of sly cunning) an artful malicious person, especially Satan

===== Usage =====

serpent.
