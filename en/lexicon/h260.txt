====== H260: אָחוּ (ʼâchûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

of uncertain (perhaps Egyptian) derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a **bulrush** or any marshy grass (particularly that along the Nile)

===== Definitions =====

  - bulrush

===== Usage =====

flag, meadow.
