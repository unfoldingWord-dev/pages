====== H5314: נָפַשׁ (nâphash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **breathe**; passively, to be breathed upon , i.e. (figuratively) **refreshed** (as if by a current of air)

===== Definitions =====

  - breathe
  - refreshed

===== Usage =====

(be) refresh selves (-ed).
