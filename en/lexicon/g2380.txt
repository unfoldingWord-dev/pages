====== G2380: θύω (thýō) ======

===== Source =====

a primary verb;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to rush (breathe hard, blow, smoke), i.e. (by implication) to sacrifice (properly, by fire, but genitive case); by extension to immolate (slaughter for any purpose)

===== Usage =====

kill, (do) sacrifice, slay.
