====== H7795: שׂוֹרָה (sôwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7786|H7786]] in the primitive sense of [[en:lexicon:h5493|H5493]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **ring**, i.e. (by analogy) a **row** (adverbially)

===== Definitions =====

  - ring
  - row

===== Usage =====

principal.
