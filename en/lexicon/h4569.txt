====== H4569: מַעֲבָר (maʻăbâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or feminine [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h5674|H5674]];

===== Meaning =====

a **crossing**-place (of a river, a **ford**; of a mountain, a **pass**); abstractly, a **transit**, i.e. (figuratively) **overwhelming**

===== Definitions =====

  - crossing
  - ford
  - pass
  - transit
  - overwhelming

===== Usage =====

ford, place where...pass, passage.
