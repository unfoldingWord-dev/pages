====== H8433: תּוֹכֵחָה (tôwkêchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

and [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h3198|H3198]];

===== Meaning =====

**chastisement**; figuratively (by words) **correction**, **refutation**, **proof** (even in defence)

===== Definitions =====

  - chastisement
  - correction
  - refutation
  - proof

===== Usage =====

argument, × chastened, correction, reasoning, rebuke, reproof, × be (often) reproved.
