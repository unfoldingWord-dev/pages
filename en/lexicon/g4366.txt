====== G4366: προσρήγνυμι (prosrḗgnymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g4486|G4486]];

===== Meaning =====

to tear towards, i.e. burst upon (as a tempest or flood)

===== Usage =====

beat vehemently against (upon).
