====== G3826: παμπληθεί (pamplētheí) ======

===== Source =====

dative case (adverb) of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3956|G3956]] and [[en:lexicon:g4128|G4128]];

===== Meaning =====

in full multitude, i.e. concertedly or simultaneously

===== Usage =====
