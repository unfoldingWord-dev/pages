====== G418: ἀνένδεκτος (anéndektos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of the same as [[en:lexicon:g1735|G1735]];

===== Meaning =====

unadmitted, i.e. (by implication) not supposable

===== Usage =====

impossible.
