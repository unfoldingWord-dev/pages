====== G335: ἀναίδεια (anaídeia) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle (compare [[en:lexicon:g427|G427]])) and [[en:lexicon:g127|G127]];

===== Meaning =====

impudence, i.e. (by implication) importunity
