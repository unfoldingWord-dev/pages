====== H6367: פִּי הַחִירֹת (Pîy ha-Chîyrôth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6310|H6310]] and the feminine plural of a noun (from the same root as [[en:lexicon:h2356|H2356]]), with the article interpolated; **mouth of the gorges**;

===== Meaning =====

**Pi-ha-Chiroth**, a place in Egypt

===== Definitions =====

  - mouth of the gorges
  - Pi-ha-Chiroth

===== Usage =====

Pi-hahiroth. (In Numbers 14:19 without Pi-.)
