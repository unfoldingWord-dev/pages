====== H1438: גָּדַע (gâdaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **fell** a tree; generally, to **destroy** anything

===== Definitions =====

  - fell
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

cut (asunder, in sunder, down, off), hew down.
