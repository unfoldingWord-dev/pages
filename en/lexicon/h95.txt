====== H95: אֲגוֹרָה (ʼăgôwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h94|H94]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **gathered**, i.e. perhaps a **grain** or **berry**; used only of a **small** (silver) **coin**

===== Definitions =====

  - gathered
  - grain
  - berry
  - small
  - coin

===== Usage =====

piece (of) silver.
