====== G3552: νοσέω (noséō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3554|G3554]];

===== Meaning =====

to be sick, i.e. (by implication, of a diseased appetite) to hanker after (figuratively, to harp upon)

===== Usage =====

dote.
