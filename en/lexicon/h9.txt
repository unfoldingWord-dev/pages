====== H9: אֲבֵדָה (ʼăbêdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6|H6]]; Compare [[en:lexicon:h10|H10]].

===== Meaning =====

concrete, something **lost**; abstract, **destruction**, i.e. Hades

===== Definitions =====

  - lost
  - destruction

===== Usage =====

lost.
