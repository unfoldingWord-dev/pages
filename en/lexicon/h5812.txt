====== H5812: עֲזַזְיָהוּ (ʻĂzazyâhûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5810|H5810]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; **Jah has strengthened**;

===== Meaning =====

**Azazjah**, the name of three Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has strengthened
  - Azazjah

===== Usage =====

Azaziah.
