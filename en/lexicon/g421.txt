====== G421: ἀνεξιχνίαστος (anexichníastos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of a
 compound of [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2487|G2487]];

===== Meaning =====

not tracked out, i.e. (by implication) untraceable

===== Usage =====

past finding out; unsearchable.
