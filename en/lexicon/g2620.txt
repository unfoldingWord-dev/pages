====== G2620: κατακαυχάομαι (katakaucháomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2744|G2744]];

===== Meaning =====

to exult against (i.e. over)

===== Usage =====

boast (against), glory, rejoice against.
