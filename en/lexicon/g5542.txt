====== G5542: χρηστολογία (chrēstología) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5543|G5543]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]];

===== Meaning =====

fair speech, i.e. plausibility

===== Usage =====
