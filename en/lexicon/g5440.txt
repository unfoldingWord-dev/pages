====== G5440: φυλακτήριον (phylaktḗrion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5442|G5442]];

===== Meaning =====

a guard-case, i.e. "phylactery" for wearing slips of Scripture texts

===== Usage =====

 phylactery.
