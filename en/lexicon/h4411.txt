====== H4411: מָלוֹן (mâlôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3885|H3885]];

===== Meaning =====

a **lodgment**, i.e. **caravanserai** or **encampment**

===== Definitions =====

  - lodgment
  - caravanserai
  - encampment

===== Usage =====

inn, place where...lodge, lodging (place).
