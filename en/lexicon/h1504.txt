====== H1504: גָּזַר (gâzar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cut** down or off; (figuratively) to **destroy**, **divide**, **exclude**, or **decide**

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - destroy
  - divide
  - exclude
  - decide

===== Usage =====

cut down (off), decree, divide, snatch.
