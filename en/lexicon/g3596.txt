====== G3596: ὁδοιπορέω (hodoiporéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3598|G3598]] and [[en:lexicon:g4198|G4198]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a wayfarer, i.e. travel

===== Usage =====
