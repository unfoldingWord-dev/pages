====== H6150: עָרַב (ʻârab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather identical with [[en:lexicon:h6148|H6148]] through the idea of covering with a texture);

===== Meaning =====

to **grow dusky** at sundown

===== Definitions =====

  - grow dusky

===== Usage =====

be darkened, (toward) evening.
