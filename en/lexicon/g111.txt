====== G111: ἀθέμιτος (athémitos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of θέμις (thémis) (statute;
 from the base of [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]]);

===== Meaning =====

illegal; by implication, flagitious

===== Usage =====

abominable, unlawful thing.
