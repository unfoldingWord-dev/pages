====== H6780: צֶמַח (tsemach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6779|H6779]];

===== Meaning =====

a **sprout** (usually concrete), literal or figurative

===== Definitions =====

  - sprout

===== Usage =====

branch, bud, that which (where) grew (upon), spring(-ing).
