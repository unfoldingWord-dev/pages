====== H3372: יָרֵא (yârêʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **fear**; morally to **revere**; causatively to **frighten**

===== Definitions =====

  - fear
  - revere
  - frighten

===== Usage =====

affright, be (make) afraid, dread(-ful), (put in) fear(-ful, -fully, -ing), (be had in) reverence(-end), × see, terrible (act, -ness, thing).
