====== H5259: נָסַךְ (nâçak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably identical with [[en:lexicon:h5258|H5258]] through the idea of fusion);

===== Meaning =====

to **interweave**, i.e. (figuratively) to **overspread**

===== Definitions =====

  - interweave
  - overspread

===== Usage =====

that is spread.
