====== G504: ἄνυδρος (ánydros) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5204|G5204]];

===== Meaning =====

waterless, i.e. dry

===== Usage =====
