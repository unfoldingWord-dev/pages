====== H6918: קָדוֹשׁ (qâdôwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6942|H6942]];

===== Meaning =====

**sacred** (ceremonially or morally); (as noun) **God** (by eminence), an **angel**, a **saint**, a **sanctuary**

===== Definitions =====

  - sacred
  - God
  - angel
  - saint
  - sanctuary

===== Usage =====

holy (One), saint.
