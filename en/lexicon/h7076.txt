====== H7076: קִנָּמוֹן (qinnâmôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to **erect**);

===== Meaning =====

**cinnamon** bark (as in **upright** rolls)

===== Definitions =====

  - erect
  - cinnamon
  - upright

===== Usage =====

cinnamon.
