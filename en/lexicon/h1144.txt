====== H1144: בִּנְיָמִין (Binyâmîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1121|H1121]] and [[en:lexicon:h3225|H3225]]; son of (the) right hand;

===== Meaning =====

**Binjamin**, youngest son of Jacob; also the tribe descended from him, and its territory

===== Definitions =====

  - Binjamin

===== Usage =====

Benjamin.
