====== H4628: מַעֲרָב (maʻărâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6150|H6150]], in the sense of **shading**;

===== Meaning =====

the **west** (as a region of the **evening** sun)

===== Definitions =====

  - shading
  - west
  - evening

===== Usage =====

west.
