====== H2910: טֻחָה (ṭuchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2909|H2909]] (or [[en:lexicon:h2902|H2902]]) in the sense of overlaying;

===== Meaning =====

(in the plural only) the **kidneys** (as being covered); hence (figuratively) the **inmost thought**

===== Definitions =====

  - kidneys
  - inmost thought

===== Usage =====

inward parts.
