====== G4899: συνεκλεκτός (syneklektós) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g1586|G1586]];

===== Meaning =====

chosen in company with, i.e. co-elect (fellow Christian)

===== Usage =====

elected together with.
