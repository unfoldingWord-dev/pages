====== H4605: מַעַל (maʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5927|H5927]];

===== Meaning =====

properly,the **upper part**, used only adverbially with prefix **upward**, **above**, **overhead**, **from the top**, etc.

===== Definitions =====

  - upper part
  - upward
  - above
  - overhead
  - from the top

===== Usage =====

above, exceeding(-ly), forward, on (× very) high, over, up(-on, -ward), very.
