====== H3051: יָהַב (yâhab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **give** (whether literal or figurative); generally, to **put**; imperatively (reflexive) **come**

===== Definitions =====

  - give
  - put
  - come

===== Usage =====

ascribe, bring, come on, give, go, set, take.
