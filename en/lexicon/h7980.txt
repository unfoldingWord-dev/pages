====== H7980: שָׁלַט (shâlaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **dominate**, i.e. **govern**; by implication, to **permit**

===== Definitions =====

  - dominate
  - govern
  - permit

===== Usage =====

(bear, have) rule, have dominion, give (have) power.
