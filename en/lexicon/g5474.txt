====== G5474: χαλκολίβανον (chalkolíbanon) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5475|G5475]] and [[en:lexicon:g3030|G3030]] (in the implied mean of
 whiteness or brilliancy);

===== Meaning =====

burnished copper, an alloy of copper (or gold) and silver having a brilliant lustre

===== Usage =====

fine brass.
