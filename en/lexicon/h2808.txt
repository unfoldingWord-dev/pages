====== H2808: חֶשְׁבּוֹן (cheshbôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2803|H2803]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **contrivance**; by implication, **intelligence**

===== Definitions =====

  - contrivance
  - intelligence

===== Usage =====

account, device, reason.
