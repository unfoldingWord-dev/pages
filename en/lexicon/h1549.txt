====== H1549: גִּלָּיוֹן (gillâyôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1540|H1540]];

===== Meaning =====

a **tablet** for writing (as **bare**); by analogy, a **mirror** (as a **plate**)

===== Definitions =====

  - tablet
  - bare
  - mirror
  - plate

===== Usage =====

glass, roll.
