====== H5548: סַלְכָה (Çalkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from an unused root. meaning to walk; **walking**;

===== Meaning =====

**Salcah**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - walking
  - Salcah

===== Usage =====

Salcah, Salchah.
