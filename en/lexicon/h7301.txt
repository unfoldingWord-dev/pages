====== H7301: רָוָה (râvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **slake** the thirst (occasionally of other appetites)

===== Definitions =====

  - slake

===== Usage =====

bathe, make drunk, (take the) fill, satiate, (abundantly) satisfy, soak, water (abundantly).
