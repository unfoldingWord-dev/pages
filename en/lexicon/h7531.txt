====== H7531: רִצְפָּה (ritspâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h7529|H7529]];

===== Meaning =====

a hot **stone**; also a tessellated **pavement**

===== Definitions =====

  - stone
  - pavement

===== Usage =====

live coal, pavement.
