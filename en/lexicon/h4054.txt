====== H4054: מִגְרָשׁ (migrâsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

also (in plural) feminine (Ezekiel 27:28) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1644|H1644]];

===== Meaning =====

a **suburb** (i.e. open country whither flocks are driven from pasture); hence, the **area around a building**, or the **margin of the sea**

===== Definitions =====

  - suburb
  - area around a building
  - margin of the sea

===== Usage =====

cast out, suburb.
