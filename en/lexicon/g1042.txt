====== G1042: γαββαθά (gabbathá) ======

===== Source =====

of Chaldee origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h01355|H01355]]);

===== Meaning =====

the knoll; gabbatha, a vernacular term for the Roman tribunal in Jerusalem

===== Usage =====

 Gabbatha.
