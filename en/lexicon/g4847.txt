====== G4847: συμπολίτης (sympolítēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g4177|G4177]];

===== Meaning =====

a native of the same town, i.e. (figuratively) co-religionist (fellow-Christian)

===== Usage =====

fellow- citizen.
