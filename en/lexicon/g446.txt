====== G446: ἀνθύπατος (anthýpatos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and a superlative of [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]];

===== Meaning =====

instead of the highest officer, i.e. (specially) a Roman proconsul

===== Usage =====

deputy.
