====== H1265: בְּרוֹשׁ (bᵉrôwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

of uncertain derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a **cypress** (?) tree; hence, a **lance** or a **musical** instrument (as made of that wood)

===== Definitions =====

  - cypress
  - lance
  - musical

===== Usage =====

fir (tree).
