====== G3720: ὀρθρινός (orthrinós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3722|G3722]];

===== Meaning =====

relating to the dawn, i.e. matutinal (as an epithet of Venus, especially brilliant in the early day)

===== Usage =====

morning.
