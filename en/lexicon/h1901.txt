====== H1901: הָגִיג (hâgîyg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root akin to [[en:lexicon:h1897|H1897]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **murmur**, i.e. **complaint**

===== Definitions =====

  - murmur
  - complaint

===== Usage =====

meditation, musing.
