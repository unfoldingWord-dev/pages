====== H2964: טֶרֶף (ṭereph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2963|H2963]];

===== Meaning =====

something **torn**, i.e. a fragment, e.g. a **fresh** leaf, **prey**, **food**

===== Definitions =====

  - torn
  - fresh
  - prey
  - food

===== Usage =====

leaf, meat, prey, spoil.
