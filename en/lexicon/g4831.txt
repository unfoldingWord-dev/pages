====== G4831: συμμιμητής (symmimētḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3401|G3401]];

===== Meaning =====

a co-imitator, i.e. fellow votary

===== Usage =====

follower together.
