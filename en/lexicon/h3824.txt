====== H3824: לֵבָב (lêbâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3823|H3823]]; used also like [[en:lexicon:h3820|H3820]]

===== Meaning =====

the **heart** (as the most interior organ);

===== Definitions =====

  - heart

===== Usage =====

  bethink themselves, breast, comfortably, courage, ((faint), (tender-) heart(-ed), midst, mind, × unawares, understanding.
