====== G160: αἰφνίδιος (aiphnídios) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]] (compare [[en:lexicon:g1810|G1810]])
 (meaning non-apparent);

===== Meaning =====

unexpected, i.e. (adverbially) suddenly

===== Usage =====

sudden, unawares.
