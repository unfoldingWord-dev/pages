====== H4549: מָסַס (mâçaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **liquefy**; figuratively, to **waste** (with disease), to **faint** (with fatigue, fear or grief)

===== Definitions =====

  - liquefy
  - waste
  - faint

===== Usage =====

discourage, faint, be loosed, melt (away), refuse, × utterly.
