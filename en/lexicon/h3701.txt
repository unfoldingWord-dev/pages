====== H3701: כֶּסֶף (keçeph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3700|H3700]];

===== Meaning =====

**silver** (from its **pale** color); by implication, **money**

===== Definitions =====

  - silver
  - pale
  - money

===== Usage =====

money, price, silver(-ling).
