====== H1627: גָּרוֹן (gârôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1641|H1641]]; (compare [[en:lexicon:h1621|H1621]])

===== Meaning =====

the **throat** (as **roughened** by swallowing)

===== Definitions =====

  - throat
  - roughened

===== Usage =====

× aloud, mouth, neck, throat.
