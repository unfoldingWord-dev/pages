====== G2679: κατασκάπτω (kataskáptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g4626|G4626]];

===== Meaning =====

to undermine, i.e. (by implication) destroy

===== Usage =====

dig down, ruin.
