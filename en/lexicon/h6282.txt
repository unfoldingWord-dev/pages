====== H6282: עָתָר (ʻâthâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6280|H6280]]; also compare [[en:lexicon:h6279|H6279]]

===== Meaning =====

**incense** (as increasing to a **volume** of smoke); hence	a **worshipper**

===== Definitions =====

  - incense
  - volume
  - worshipper

===== Usage =====

suppliant, thick.
