====== G1879: ἐπαναπαύομαι (epanapaúomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g373|G373]];

===== Meaning =====

to settle on; literally (remain) or figuratively (rely)

===== Usage =====

rest in (upon).
