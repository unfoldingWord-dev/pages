====== H1128: בֶּן־דֶּקֶר (Ben-Deqer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1121|H1121]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:h1856|H1856]]; son of piercing (or of a lance);

===== Meaning =====

**Ben-Deker**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Ben-Deker

===== Usage =====

the son of Dekar.
