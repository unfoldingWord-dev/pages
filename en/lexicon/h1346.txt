====== H1346: גַּאֲוָה (gaʼăvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1342|H1342]];

===== Meaning =====

**arrogance** or **majesty**; by implication, (concretely) **ornament**

===== Definitions =====

  - arrogance
  - majesty
  - ornament

===== Usage =====

excellency, haughtiness, highness, pride, proudly, swelling.
