====== H6843: צְפִירָה (tsᵉphîyrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine formed like [[en:lexicon:h6842|H6842]];

===== Meaning =====

a **crown** (as **encircling** the head); also a **turn** of affairs (i.e. **mishap**)

===== Definitions =====

  - crown
  - encircling
  - turn
  - mishap

===== Usage =====

diadem, morning.
