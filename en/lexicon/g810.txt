====== G810: ἀσωτία (asōtía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed
 derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4982|G4982]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, unsavedness, i.e. (by implication) profligacy

===== Usage =====

excess, riot.
