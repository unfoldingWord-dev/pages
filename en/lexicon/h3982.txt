====== H3982: מַאֲמַר (maʼămar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h559|H559]];

===== Meaning =====

something (authoritatively) **said**, i.e. an **edict**

===== Definitions =====

  - said
  - edict

===== Usage =====

commandment, decree.
