====== H1731: דּוּד (dûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1730|H1730]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pot** (for **boiling**); also (by resemblance of shape) a **basket**

===== Definitions =====

  - pot
  - boiling
  - basket

===== Usage =====

basket, caldron, kettle, (seething) pot.
