====== H5517: סִיעָא (Çîyʻâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **converse**; **congregation**;

===== Meaning =====

**Sia** or **Siaha**, one of the Nethinim

===== Definitions =====

  - converse
  - congregation
  - Sia
  - Siaha

===== Usage =====

Sia, Siaha.
