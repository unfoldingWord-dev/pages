====== H7137: קָרֶה (qâreh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7136|H7136]];

===== Meaning =====

an (unfortunate) **occurrence**, i.e. some **accidental** (ceremonial) **disqualification**

===== Definitions =====

  - occurrence
  - accidental
  - disqualification

===== Usage =====

uncleanness that chanceth.
