====== G2721: κατεφίστημι (katephístēmi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2186|G2186]];

===== Meaning =====

to stand over against, i.e. rush upon (assault)

===== Usage =====

make insurrection against.
