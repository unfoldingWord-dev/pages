====== H7588: שָׁאוֹן (shâʼôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7582|H7582]];

===== Meaning =====

**uproar** (as of **rushing**); by implication, **destruction**

===== Definitions =====

  - uproar
  - rushing
  - destruction

===== Usage =====

× horrible, noise, pomp, rushing, tumult (× -uous).
