====== H5181: נָחַת (nâchath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sink**, i.e. **descend**; causatively, to **press** or **lead** down

===== Definitions =====

  - sink
  - descend
  - press
  - lead

===== Usage =====

be broken, (cause to) come down, enter, go down, press sore, settle, stick fast.
