====== H8498: תְּכוּנָה (tᵉkûwnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h8505|H8505]];

===== Meaning =====

**adjustment**, i.e. **structure**; by implication, **equipage**

===== Definitions =====

  - adjustment
  - structure
  - equipage

===== Usage =====

fashion, store.
