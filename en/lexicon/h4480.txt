====== H4480: מִן (min) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

preposition

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (constructive plural) (Isaiah 30:11); for [[en:lexicon:h4482|H4482]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **part** of; hence (prepositionally), **from** or **out of** in many senses

===== Definitions =====

  - part
  - from
  - out of

===== Usage =====

above, after, among, at, because of, by (reason of), from (among), in, × neither, × nor, (out) of, over, since, × then, through, × whether, with.
