====== G4781: συγκάμπτω (synkámptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2578|G2578]];

===== Meaning =====

to bend together, i.e. (figuratively) to afflict

===== Usage =====

bow down.
