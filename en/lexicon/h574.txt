====== H574: אֵמְתָּנִי (ʼêmᵉtânîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to that of [[en:lexicon:h4975|H4975]];

===== Meaning =====

**well-loined** (i.e. burly) or **mighty**

===== Definitions =====

  - well-loined
  - mighty

===== Usage =====

terrible.
