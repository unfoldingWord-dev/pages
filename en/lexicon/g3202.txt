====== G3202: μεμψίμοιρος (mempsímoiros) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3201|G3201]] and μοῖρα (moîra) (fate; akin to the base
 of [[en:lexicon:g3313|G3313]]);

===== Meaning =====

blaming fate, i.e. querulous (discontented)

===== Usage =====

complainer.
