====== H4351: מְכוּרָה (mᵉkûwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from the same as [[en:lexicon:h3564|H3564]] in the sense of **dipping**;

===== Definitions =====

  - dipping
  - origin

===== Usage =====

birth, habitation, nativity.
