====== G2665: καταπέτασμα (katapétasma) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and a congener of [[en:lexicon:g4072|G4072]];

===== Meaning =====

something spread thoroughly, i.e. (specially) the door screen (to the Most Holy Place) in the Jewish Temple

===== Usage =====

vail.
