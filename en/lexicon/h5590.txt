====== H5590: סָעַר (çâʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **rush upon**; by implication, to **toss** (transitive or intransitive, literal or figurative)

===== Definitions =====

  - rush upon
  - toss

===== Usage =====

be (toss with) tempest(-uous), be sore, troubled, come out as a (drive with the, scatter with a) whirlwind.
