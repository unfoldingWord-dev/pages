====== H5180: נְחֻשְׁתָּן (Nᵉchushtân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5178|H5178]];

===== Meaning =====

something **made of copper**, i.e. the **copper serpent of the Desert**

===== Definitions =====

  - made of copper
  - copper serpent of the Desert

===== Usage =====

Nehushtan.
