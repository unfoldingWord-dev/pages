====== G5279: ὑπομιμνήσκω (hypomimnḗskō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g3403|G3403]];

===== Meaning =====

to remind quietly, i.e. suggest to the (middle voice, one's own) memory

===== Usage =====

put in mind, remember, bring to (put in) remembrance.
