====== G993: Βοανεργές (Boanergés) ======

===== Source =====

of Chaldee origin ([[en:lexicon:h01123|H01123]] and [[en:lexicon:h07266|H07266]]);

===== Meaning =====

sons of commotion; Boanerges, an epithet of two of the apostles

===== Usage =====

Boanerges.
