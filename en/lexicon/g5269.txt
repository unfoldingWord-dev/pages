====== G5269: ὑποζώννυμι (hypozṓnnymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g2224|G2224]];

===== Meaning =====

to gird under, i.e. frap (a vessel with cables across the keel, sides and deck)

===== Usage =====

undergirt.
