====== G58: ἀγορά (agorá) ======

===== Source =====

from ἀγείρω (ageírō) (to gather; probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g1453|G1453]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, the town-square (as a place of public resort); by implication, a market or thoroughfare

===== Usage =====

market(-place), street.
