====== H339: אִי (ʼîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h183|H183]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **habitable spot** (as desirable); **dry land**, a **coast**, an **island**

===== Definitions =====

  - habitable spot
  - dry land
  - coast
  - island

===== Usage =====

country, isle, island.
