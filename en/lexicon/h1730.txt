====== H1730: דּוֹד (dôwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning properly, to **boil**, i.e.

===== Meaning =====

(figuratively) to **love**; by implication, a **love-token**, **lover**, **friend**; specifically an **uncle**

===== Definitions =====

  - boil
  - love
  - love-token
  - lover
  - friend
  - uncle

===== Usage =====

(well-) beloved, father's brother, love, uncle.
