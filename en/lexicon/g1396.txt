====== G1396: δουλαγωγέω (doulagōgéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g1401|G1401]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a slave-driver, i.e. to enslave (figuratively, subdue)

===== Usage =====

bring into subjection.
