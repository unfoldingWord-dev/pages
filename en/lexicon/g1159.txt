====== G1159: δαπανάω (dapanáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1160|G1160]];

===== Meaning =====

to expend, i.e. (in a good sense) to incur cost, or (in a bad one) to waste

===== Usage =====

be at charges, consume, spend.
