====== H913: בְּדִיל (bᵉdîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h914|H914]];

===== Meaning =====

**alloy** (because **removed** by smelting); by analogy, **tin**

===== Definitions =====

  - alloy
  - removed
  - tin

===== Usage =====

  plummet, tin.
