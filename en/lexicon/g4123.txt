====== G4123: πλεονέκτης (pleonéktēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4119|G4119]] and [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

holding (desiring) more, i.e. eager for gain (avaricious, hence a defrauder)

===== Usage =====

covetous.
