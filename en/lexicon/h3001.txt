====== H3001: יָבֵשׁ (yâbêsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be ashamed**, **confused** or **disappointed**; also (as failing) to **dry up** (as water) or **wither** (as herbage)

===== Definitions =====

  - be ashamed
  - confused
  - disappointed
  - dry up
  - wither

===== Usage =====

be ashamed, clean, be confounded, (make) dry (up), (do) shame(-fully), × utterly, wither (away).
