====== H7878: שִׂיחַ (sîyach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **ponder**, i.e. (by implication) **converse** (with oneself, and hence, aloud) or (transitively) **utter**

===== Definitions =====

  - ponder
  - converse
  - utter

===== Usage =====

commune, complain, declare, meditate, muse, pray, speak, talk (with).
