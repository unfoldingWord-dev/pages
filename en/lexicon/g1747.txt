====== G1747: ἐνέδρα (enédra) ======

===== Source =====

feminine from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g1476|G1476]];

===== Meaning =====

an ambuscade, i.e. (figuratively) murderous purpose

===== Usage =====

lay wait.
