====== H2926: טָלַל (ṭâlal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **strew over**, i.e. (by implication) to **cover in** or **plate** (with beams)

===== Definitions =====

  - strew over
  - cover in
  - plate

===== Usage =====

cover.
