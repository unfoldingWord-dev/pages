====== H8417: תׇּהֳלָה (tohŏlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of an unused noun (apparently from [[en:lexicon:h1984|H1984]]) meaning **bluster**;

===== Meaning =====

**braggadocio**, i.e. (by implication) **fatuity**

===== Definitions =====

  - bluster
  - braggadocio
  - fatuity

===== Usage =====

folly.
