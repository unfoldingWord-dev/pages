====== H2833: חֹשֶׁן (chôshen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to **contain** or **sparkle**;

===== Meaning =====

perhaps a **pocket** (as holding the Urim and Thummim), or **rich** (as containing gems), used only of the **gorget** of the highpriest

===== Definitions =====

  - contain
  - sparkle
  - pocket
  - rich
  - gorget

===== Usage =====

breastplate.
