====== H7966: שִׁלּוּם (shillûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h7999|H7999]];

===== Meaning =====

a **requital**, i.e. (secure) **retribution**, (venal) a **fee**

===== Definitions =====

  - requital
  - retribution
  - fee

===== Usage =====

recompense, reward.
