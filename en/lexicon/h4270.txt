====== H4270: מַחְסוֹר (machçôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h2637|H2637]];

===== Meaning =====

**deficiency**; hence, **impoverishment**

===== Definitions =====

  - deficiency
  - impoverishment

===== Usage =====

lack, need, penury, poor, poverty, want.
