====== H5069: נְדַב (nᵉdab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h5068|H5068]];

===== Meaning =====

**be** (or give) **liberal**(-ly)

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - liberal

===== Usage =====

(be minded of...own) freewill (offering), offer freely (willingly).
