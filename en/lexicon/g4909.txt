====== G4909: συνευδοκέω (syneudokéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2106|G2106]];

===== Meaning =====

to think well of in common, i.e. assent to, feel gratified with

===== Usage =====

allow, assent, be pleased, have pleasure.
