====== H6736: צִיר (tsîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h6735|H6735]];

===== Meaning =====

a **form** (of beauty; as if **pressed** out, i.e. carved); hence, an (idolatrous) **image**

===== Definitions =====

  - form
  - pressed
  - image

===== Usage =====

beauty, idol.
