====== H215: אוֹר (ʼôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causative, **make**) **luminous** (literally and metaphorically)

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - luminous

===== Usage =====

× break of day, glorious, kindle, (be, en-, give, show) light (-en, -ened), set on fire, shine.
