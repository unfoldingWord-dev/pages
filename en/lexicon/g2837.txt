====== G2837: κοιμάω (koimáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2749|G2749]];

===== Meaning =====

to put to sleep, i.e. (passively or reflexively) to slumber; figuratively, to decease

===== Usage =====

(be a-, fall a-, fall on) sleep, be dead.
