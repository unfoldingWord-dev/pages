====== G1758: ἐνέχω (enéchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

to hold in or upon, i.e. ensnare; by implication, to keep a grudge

===== Usage =====

entangle with, have a quarrel against, urge.
