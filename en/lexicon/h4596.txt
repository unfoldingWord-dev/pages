====== H4596: מְעִי (mᵉʻîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5753|H5753]]; (compare [[en:lexicon:h5856|H5856]])

===== Meaning =====

a **pile** of rubbish (as **contorted**), i.e. a **ruin**

===== Definitions =====

  - pile
  - contorted
  - ruin

===== Usage =====

heap.
