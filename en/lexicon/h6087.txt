====== H6087: עָצַב (ʻâtsab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **carve**, i.e. **fabricate** or **fashion**; hence (in a bad sense) to **worry**, **pain** or **anger**

===== Definitions =====

  - carve
  - fabricate
  - fashion
  - worry
  - pain
  - anger

===== Usage =====

displease, grieve, hurt, make, be sorry, vex, worship, wrest.
