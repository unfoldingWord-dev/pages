====== G3378: μὴ οὐκ (mḕ ouk) ======

===== Source =====

i.e. [[en:lexicon:g3361|G3361]] and [[en:lexicon:g3756|G3756]];

===== Meaning =====

as interrogative and negative, is it not that?

===== Usage =====

neither (followed by no), + never, not.
