====== G185: ἀκέραιος (akéraios) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[en:lexicon:g2767|G2767]];

===== Meaning =====

unmixed, i.e. (figuratively) innocent

===== Usage =====

harmless, simple.
