====== H8577: תַּנִּין (tannîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Ezekiel 29:3), intensive from the same as [[en:lexicon:h8565|H8565]];

===== Meaning =====

a marine or land **monster**, i.e. **sea-serpent** or **jackal**

===== Definitions =====

  - monster
  - sea-serpent
  - jackal

===== Usage =====

dragon, sea-monster, serpent, whale.
