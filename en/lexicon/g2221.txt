====== G2221: ζωγρέω (zōgréō) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g2226|G2226]] and [[en:lexicon:g64|G64]];

===== Meaning =====

to take alive (make a prisoner of war), i.e. (figuratively) to capture or ensnare

===== Usage =====

take captive, catch.
