====== H8088: שֵׁמַע (shêmaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8085|H8085]];

===== Meaning =====

something **heard**, i.e. a **sound**, **rumor**, **announcement**; abstractly, **audience**

===== Definitions =====

  - heard
  - sound
  - rumor
  - announcement
  - audience

===== Usage =====

bruit, fame, hear(-ing), loud, report, speech, tidings.
