====== H8511: תָּלָא (tâlâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **suspend**; figuratively (through hesitation) to **be uncertain**; by implication (of mental dependence) to **habituate**

===== Definitions =====

  - suspend
  - be uncertain
  - habituate

===== Usage =====

be bent, hang (in doubt).
