====== G3849: παραβιάζομαι (parabiázomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and the middle voice of [[en:lexicon:g971|G971]];

===== Meaning =====

to force contrary to (nature), i.e. compel (by entreaty)

===== Usage =====

constrain.
