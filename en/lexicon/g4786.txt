====== G4786: συγκεράννυμι (synkeránnymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2767|G2767]];

===== Meaning =====

to commingle, i.e. (figuratively) to combine or assimilate

===== Usage =====

mix with, temper together.
