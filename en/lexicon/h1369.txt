====== H1369: גְּבוּרָה (gᵉbûwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1368|H1368]];

===== Meaning =====

**force** (literally or figuratively); by implication, **valor**, **victory**

===== Definitions =====

  - force
  - valor
  - victory

===== Usage =====

force, mastery, might, mighty (act, power), power, strength.
