====== H628: אֲסְפְּסֻף (ʼăçpᵉçuph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

by reduplication from [[en:lexicon:h624|H624]];

===== Meaning =====

**gathered up together**, i.e. a **promiscuous assemblage** (of people)

===== Definitions =====

  - gathered up together
  - promiscuous assemblage

===== Usage =====

mixt multitude.
