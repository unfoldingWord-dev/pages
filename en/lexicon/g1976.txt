====== G1976: ἐπιῤῥάπτω (epirrháptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g4476|G4476]];

===== Meaning =====

to stitch upon, i.e. fasten with the needle

===== Usage =====

sew on.
