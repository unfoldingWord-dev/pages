====== G2115: εὔθυμος (eúthymos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and [[en:lexicon:g2372|G2372]];

===== Meaning =====

in fine spirits, i.e. cheerful

===== Usage =====

of good cheer, the more cheerfully.
