====== H6117: עָקַב (ʻâqab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to swell out or up; used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h6119|H6119]],

===== Meaning =====

to **seize by the heel**; figuratively, to **circumvent** (as if tripping up the heels); also to **restrain** (as if holding by the heel)

===== Definitions =====

  - seize by the heel
  - circumvent
  - restrain

===== Usage =====

take by the heel, stay, supplant, × utterly.
