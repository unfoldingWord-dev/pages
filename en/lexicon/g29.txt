====== G29: ἀγγαρεύω (angareúō) ======

===== Source =====

of foreign origin (compare [[en:lexicon:h0104|H0104]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to be a courier, i.e. (by implication) to press into public service

===== Usage =====

compel (to go).
