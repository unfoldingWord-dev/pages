====== G3853: παραγγέλλω (parangéllō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g32|G32]];

===== Meaning =====

to transmit a message, i.e. (by implication) to enjoin

===== Usage =====

(give in) charge, (give) command(-ment), declare.
