====== H1139: בְּנֵי־בְּרַק (Bᵉnêy-Bᵉraq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the plural construction of [[en:lexicon:h1121|H1121]] and [[en:lexicon:h1300|H1300]];

===== Meaning =====

**sons of lightning**, **Bene-berak**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - sons of lightning
  - Bene-berak

===== Usage =====

Bene-barak.
