====== G5420: φράσσω (phrássō) ======

===== Source =====

apparently a strengthening form of the base of [[en:lexicon:g5424|G5424]];

===== Meaning =====

to fence or inclose, i.e. (specially), to block up (figuratively, to silence)

===== Usage =====

stop.
