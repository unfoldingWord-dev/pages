====== H8252: שָׁקַט (shâqaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **repose** (usually figurative)

===== Definitions =====

  - repose

===== Usage =====

appease, idleness, (at, be at, be in, give) quiet(-ness), (be at, be in, give, have, take) rest, settle, be still.
