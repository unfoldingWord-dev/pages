====== G4080: πηλίκος (pēlíkos) ======

===== Source =====

a quantitative form (the feminine) of the base of [[en:lexicon:g4225|G4225]];

===== Meaning =====

how much (as an indefinite), i.e. in size or (figuratively) dignity

===== Usage =====

how great (large).
