====== G3825: πάλιν (pálin) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:lexicon:g3823|G3823]] (through the idea of oscillatory
 repetition);

===== Meaning =====

(adverbially) anew, i.e. (of place) back, (of time) once more, or (conjunctionally) furthermore or on the other hand

===== Usage =====

again.
