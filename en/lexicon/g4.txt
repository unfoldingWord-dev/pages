====== G4: ἀβαρής (abarḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g922|G922]];

===== Meaning =====

weightless, i.e. (figuratively) not burdensome

===== Usage =====

from being burdensome.
