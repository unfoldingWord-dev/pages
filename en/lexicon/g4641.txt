====== G4641: σκληροκαρδία (sklērokardía) ======

===== Source =====

feminine of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4642|G4642]] and [[en:lexicon:g2588|G2588]];

===== Meaning =====

hard-heartedness, i.e. (specially), destitution of (spiritual) perception

===== Usage =====

hardness of heart.
