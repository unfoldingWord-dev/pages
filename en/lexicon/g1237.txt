====== G1237: διαδέχομαι (diadéchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g1209|G1209]];

===== Meaning =====

to receive in turn, i.e. (figuratively) succeed to

===== Usage =====

come after.
