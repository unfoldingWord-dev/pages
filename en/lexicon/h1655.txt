====== H1655: גֶּשֶׁם (geshem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) apparently the same as [[en:lexicon:h1653|H1653]];

===== Meaning =====

used in a peculiar sense, the **body** (probably for the (figuratively) idea of a **hard rain)**

===== Definitions =====

  - body
  - hard rain)

===== Usage =====

body.
