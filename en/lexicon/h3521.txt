====== H3521: כָּבוּל (Kâbûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h3525|H3525]] in the sense of limitation; sterile;

===== Meaning =====

**Cabul**, the name of two places in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Cabul

===== Usage =====

Cabul.
