====== H392: אַכְזִיב (ʼAkzîyb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h391|H391]]; **deceitful** (in the sense of a winter-torrent which fails in summer);

===== Meaning =====

**Akzib**, the name of two places in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - deceitful
  - Akzib

===== Usage =====

Achzib.
