====== G1315: διαχειρίζομαι (diacheirízomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5495|G5495]];

===== Meaning =====

to handle thoroughly, i.e. lay violent hands upon

===== Usage =====

kill, slay.
