====== H4945: מַשְׁקֶה (mashqeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8248|H8248]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **causing to drink**, i.e. a **butler**; by implication (intransitively), **drink** (itself); figuratively, a **well-watered** region

===== Definitions =====

  - causing to drink
  - butler
  - drink
  - well-watered

===== Usage =====

butler(-ship), cupbearer, drink(-ing), fat pasture, watered.
