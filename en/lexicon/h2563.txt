====== H2563: חֹמֶר (chômer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2560|H2560]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **bubbling** up, i.e. of water, a **wave**; of earth, **mire** or **clay** (cement); also a **heap**; hence, a **chomer** or dry measure

===== Definitions =====

  - bubbling
  - wave
  - mire
  - clay
  - heap
  - chomer

===== Usage =====

clay, heap, homer, mire, motion.
