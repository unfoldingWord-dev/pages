====== G562: ἀπέραντος (apérantos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a secondary derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4008|G4008]];

===== Meaning =====

unfinished, i.e. (by implication) interminable

===== Usage =====

endless.
