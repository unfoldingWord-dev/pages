====== H874: בָּאַר (bâʼar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **dig**; by analogy, to **engrave**; figuratively, to **explain**

===== Definitions =====

  - dig
  - engrave
  - explain

===== Usage =====

declare, (make) plain(-ly).
