====== G1545: ἔκβασις (ékbasis) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]] (meaning to go out);

===== Meaning =====

an exit (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

end, way to escape.
