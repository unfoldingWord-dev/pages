====== G5206: υἱοθεσία (huiothesía) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g5207|G5207]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

the placing as a son, i.e. adoption (figuratively, Christian sonship in respect to God)

===== Usage =====

adoption (of children, of sons).
