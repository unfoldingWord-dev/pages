====== H6213: עָשָׂה (ʻâsâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **do** or **make**, in the broadest sense and widest application

===== Definitions =====

  - do
  - make

===== Usage =====
