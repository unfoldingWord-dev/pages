====== H6453: פֶּסַח (peçach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6452|H6452]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pretermission**, i.e. **exemption**; used only techically of the Jewish **Passover** (the festival or the victim)

===== Definitions =====

  - pretermission
  - exemption
  - Passover

===== Usage =====

passover (offering).
