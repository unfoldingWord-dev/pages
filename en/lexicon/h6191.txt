====== H6191: עָרַם (ʻâram) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be** (or **make**) **bare**; but used only in the derivative sense (through the idea perhaps of smoothness) to **be cunning** (usually in a bad sense)

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - bare
  - be cunning

===== Usage =====

× very, beware, take crafty (counsel), be prudent, deal subtilly.
