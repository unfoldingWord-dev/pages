====== H6797: צָנִיף (tsânîyph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h6801|H6801]];

===== Meaning =====

a **head-dress** (i.e. piece of cloth **wrapped** around)

===== Definitions =====

  - head-dress
  - wrapped

===== Usage =====

diadem, hood, mitre.
