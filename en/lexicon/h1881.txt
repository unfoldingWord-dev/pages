====== H1881: דָּת (dâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

of uncertain (perhaps foreign) derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a **royal edict** or **statute**

===== Definitions =====

  - royal edict
  - statute

===== Usage =====

commandment, commission, decree, law, manner.
