====== G1371: διχοτομέω (dichotoméō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1364|G1364]] and a derivative of
 τέμνω (témnō) (to cut);

===== Meaning =====

to bisect, i.e. (by extension) to flog severely

===== Usage =====

cut asunder (in sunder).
