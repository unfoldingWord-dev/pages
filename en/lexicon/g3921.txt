====== G3921: παρεισδύνω (pareisdýnō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1519|G1519]] and [[en:lexicon:g1416|G1416]];

===== Meaning =====

to settle in alongside, i.e. lodge stealthily

===== Usage =====

creep in unawares.
