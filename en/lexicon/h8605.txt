====== H8605: תְּפִלָּה (tᵉphillâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6419|H6419]];

===== Meaning =====

**intercession**, **supplication**; by implication, a **hymn**

===== Definitions =====

  - intercession
  - supplication
  - hymn

===== Usage =====

prayer.
