====== G2766: κέραμος (kéramos) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the base of [[en:lexicon:g2767|G2767]] (through the idea of mixing clay and
 water);

===== Meaning =====

earthenware, i.e. a tile (by analogy, a thin roof or awning)

===== Usage =====

tiling.
