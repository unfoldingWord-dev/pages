====== G4347: προσκολλάω (proskolláō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g2853|G2853]];

===== Meaning =====

to glue to, i.e. (figuratively) to adhere

===== Usage =====

cleave, join (self).
