====== G5227: ὑπεναντίος (hypenantíos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g1727|G1727]];

===== Meaning =====

under (covertly) contrary to, i.e. opposed or (as noun) an opponent

===== Usage =====

adversary, against.
