====== G2854: κολλούριον (kolloúrion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed derivative of κολλύρα (kollýra) (a cake; prob akin to the
 base of [[en:lexicon:g2853|G2853]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, a poultice (as made of or in the form of crackers), i.e. (by analogy) a plaster

===== Usage =====

 eyesalve.
