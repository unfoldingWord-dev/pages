====== H809: אֲשִׁישָׁה (ʼăshîyshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h808|H808]];

===== Meaning =====

something **closely pressed together**, i.e. a **cake of raisins** or **other comfits**

===== Definitions =====

  - closely pressed together
  - cake of raisins
  - other comfits

===== Usage =====

flagon.
