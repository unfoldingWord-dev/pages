====== H5197: נָטַף (nâṭaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **ooze**, i.e. **distil gradually**; by implication, to **fall in drops**; figuratively, to **speak by inspiration**

===== Definitions =====

  - ooze
  - distil gradually
  - fall in drops
  - speak by inspiration

===== Usage =====

drop(-ping), prophesy(-et).
