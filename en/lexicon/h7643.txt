====== H7643: שְׂבָם (Sᵉbâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably from [[en:lexicon:h1313|H1313]]; **spice**;

===== Meaning =====

**Sebam** or **Sibmah**, a place in Moab

===== Definitions =====

  - spice
  - Sebam
  - Sibmah

===== Usage =====

Shebam, Shibmah, Sibmah.
