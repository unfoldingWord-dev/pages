====== H5279: נַעֲמָה (Naʻămâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - femininenoun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h5277|H5277]]; **pleasantness**;

===== Meaning =====

**Naamah**, the name of an antediluvian woman, of an Ammonitess, and of a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - pleasantness
  - Naamah

===== Usage =====

Naamah.
