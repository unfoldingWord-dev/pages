====== G1983: ἐπισκοπέω (episkopéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g4648|G4648]];

===== Meaning =====

to oversee; by implication, to beware

===== Usage =====

look diligently, take the oversight.
