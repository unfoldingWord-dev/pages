====== H3811: לָאָה (lâʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tire**; (figuratively) to **be** (or **make**) **disgusted**

===== Definitions =====

  - tire
  - be
  - make
  - disgusted

===== Usage =====

faint, grieve, lothe, (be, make) weary (selves).
