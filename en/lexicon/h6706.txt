====== H6706: צְחִיחַ (tsᵉchîyach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6705|H6705]];

===== Meaning =====

**glaring**, i.e. **exposed** to the bright sun

===== Definitions =====

  - glaring
  - exposed

===== Usage =====

higher place, top.
