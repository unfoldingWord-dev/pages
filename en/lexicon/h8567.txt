====== H8567: תָּנָה (tânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of attributing honor);

===== Meaning =====

to **ascribe** (praise), i.e. **celebrate**, **commemorate**

===== Definitions =====

  - ascribe
  - celebrate
  - commemorate

===== Usage =====

lament, rehearse.
