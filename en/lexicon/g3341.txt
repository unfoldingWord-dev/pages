====== G3341: μετάνοια (metánoia) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3340|G3340]];

===== Meaning =====

(subjectively) compunction (for guilt, including reformation); by implication, reversal (of (another's) decision)

===== Usage =====

repentance.
