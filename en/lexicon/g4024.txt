====== G4024: περιζώννυμι (perizṓnnymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2224|G2224]];

===== Meaning =====

to gird all around, i.e. (middle voice or passive) to fasten on one's belt (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

gird (about, self).
