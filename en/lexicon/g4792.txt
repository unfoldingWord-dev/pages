====== G4792: συγκομίζω (synkomízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2865|G2865]];

===== Meaning =====

to convey together, i.e. collect or bear away in company with others

===== Usage =====

carry.
