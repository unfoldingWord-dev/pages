====== H7925: שָׁכַם (shâkam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to incline (the shoulder to a burden); but used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7926|H7926]];

===== Meaning =====

literally, to **load up** (on the back of man or beast), i.e. to **start early in the morning**

===== Definitions =====

  - load up
  - start early in the morning

===== Usage =====

(arise, be up, get (oneself) up, rise up) early (betimes), morning.
