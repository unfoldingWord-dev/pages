====== G4358: προσορμίζω (prosormízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and a derivative of the same as [[en:lexicon:g3730|G3730]] (meaning to tie
 (anchor) or lull);

===== Meaning =====

to moor to, i.e. (by implication) land at

===== Usage =====

draw to the shore.
