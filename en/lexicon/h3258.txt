====== H3258: יַעְבֵּץ (Yaʻbêts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculinenoun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to **grieve**; **sorrowful**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jabets**, the name of an Israelite, and also of a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - grieve
  - sorrowful
  - Jabets

===== Usage =====

Jabez.
