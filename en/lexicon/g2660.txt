====== G2660: κατανύσσω (katanýssō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g3572|G3572]];

===== Meaning =====

to pierce thoroughly, i.e. (figuratively) to agitate violently ("sting to the quick")

===== Usage =====

prick.
