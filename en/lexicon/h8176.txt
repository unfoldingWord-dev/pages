====== H8176: שָׁעַר (shâʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to **split** or **open**, i.e. (literally, but only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h8179|H8179]]); compare see [[en:lexicon:h7778|H7778]]

===== Meaning =====

to **act as gatekeeper**; (figuratively) to **estimate**

===== Definitions =====

  - split
  - open
  - act as gatekeeper
  - estimate

===== Usage =====

think.
