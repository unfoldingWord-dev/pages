====== H8262: שָׁקַץ (shâqats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be filthy**, i.e. (intensively) to **loathe**, **pollute**

===== Definitions =====

  - be filthy
  - loathe
  - pollute

===== Usage =====

abhor, make abominable, have in abomination, detest, × utterly.
