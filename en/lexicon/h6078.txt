====== H6078: עׇפְנִי (ʻOphnîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from an unused noun (denoting a place in Palestine; from an unused root of uncertain meaning);

===== Meaning =====

an **Ophnite** (collectively) or inhabitants of Ophen

===== Definitions =====

  - Ophnite

===== Usage =====

Ophni.
