====== G46: ἄγναφος (ágnaphos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and the same as [[en:lexicon:g1102|G1102]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, unfulled, i.e. (by implication) new (cloth)

===== Usage =====

new.
