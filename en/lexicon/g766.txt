====== G766: ἀσέλγεια (asélgeia) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed
 σελγής (selgḗs) (of uncertain derivation, but apparently meaning
 continent);

===== Meaning =====

licentiousness (sometimes including other vices)

===== Usage =====

filthy, lasciviousness, wantonness.
