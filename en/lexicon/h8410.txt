====== H8410: תִּדְהָר (tidhâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:h1725|H1725]];

===== Meaning =====

**enduring**; a species of hard-wood or **lasting** tree (perhaps **oak**)

===== Definitions =====

  - enduring
  - lasting
  - oak

===== Usage =====

pine (tree).
