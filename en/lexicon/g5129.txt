====== G5129: τούτῳ (toútōi) ======

===== Source =====

dative case singular masculine or neuter of [[en:lexicon:g3778|G3778]];

===== Meaning =====

to (in, with or by) this (person or thing)

===== Usage =====

here(-by, -in), him, one, the same, there(-in), this.
