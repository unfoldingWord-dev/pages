====== H3607: כָּלָא (kâlâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **restrict**, by act (**hold back** or **in**) or word (**prohibit**)

===== Definitions =====

  - restrict
  - hold back
  - in
  - prohibit

===== Usage =====

finish, forbid, keep (back), refrain, restrain, retain, shut up, be stayed, withhold.
