====== G1877: ἐπανάγω (epanágō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g321|G321]];

===== Meaning =====

to lead up on, i.e. (technical) to put out (to sea); (intransitively) to return

===== Usage =====

launch (thrust) out, return.
