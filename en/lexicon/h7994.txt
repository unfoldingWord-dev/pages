====== H7994: שָׁלָךְ (shâlâk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7993|H7993]];

===== Meaning =====

**bird of prey**, usually thought to be the **pelican** (from casting itself into the sea)

===== Definitions =====

  - bird of prey
  - pelican

===== Usage =====

cormorant.
