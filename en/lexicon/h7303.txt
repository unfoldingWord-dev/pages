====== H7303: רוֹהֲגָה (Rôwhăgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to cry out; **outcry**;

===== Meaning =====

**Rohagah**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - outcry
  - Rohagah

===== Usage =====

Rohgah.
