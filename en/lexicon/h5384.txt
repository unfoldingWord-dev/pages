====== H5384: נָשֶׁה (nâsheh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5382|H5382]], in the sense of failure;

===== Meaning =====

**rheumatic** or **crippled** (from the incident to Jacob)

===== Definitions =====

  - rheumatic
  - crippled

===== Usage =====

which shrank.
