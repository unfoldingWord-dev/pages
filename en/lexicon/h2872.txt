====== H2872: טַבּוּר (ṭabbûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to pile up;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **accumulated**; i.e. (by implication) a **summit**

===== Definitions =====

  - accumulated
  - summit

===== Usage =====

middle, midst.
