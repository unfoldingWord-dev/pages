====== H5063: נֶגֶף (negeph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5062|H5062]];

===== Meaning =====

a **trip** (of the foot); figuratively, an **infliction** (of disease)

===== Definitions =====

  - trip
  - infliction

===== Usage =====

plague, stumbling.
