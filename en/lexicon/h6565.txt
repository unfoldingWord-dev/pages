====== H6565: פָּרַר (pârar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **break up** (usually figuratively), i.e. to **violate**, **frustrate**

===== Definitions =====

  - break up
  - violate
  - frustrate

===== Usage =====

× any ways, break (asunder), cast off, cause to cease, × clean, defeat, disannul, disappoint, dissolve, divide, make of none effect, fail, frustrate, bring (come) to nought, × utterly, make void.
