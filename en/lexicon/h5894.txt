====== H5894: עִיר (ʻîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h5782|H5782]];

===== Meaning =====

a **watcher**, i.e. an **angel** (as guardian)

===== Definitions =====

  - watcher
  - angel

===== Usage =====

watcher.
