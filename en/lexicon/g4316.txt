====== G4316: προσαγορεύω (prosagoreúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g58|G58]] (mean to harangue);

===== Meaning =====

to address, i.e. salute by name

===== Usage =====

call.
