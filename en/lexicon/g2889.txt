====== G2889: κόσμος (kósmos) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the base of [[en:lexicon:g2865|G2865]];

===== Meaning =====

orderly arrangement, i.e. decoration; by implication, the world (in a wide or narrow sense, including its inhabitants, literally or figuratively (morally))

===== Usage =====

adorning, world.
