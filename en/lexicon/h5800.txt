====== H5800: עָזַב (ʻâzab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **loosen**, i.e. **relinquish**, **permit**, etc.

===== Definitions =====

  - loosen
  - relinquish
  - permit

===== Usage =====

commit self, fail, forsake, fortify, help, leave (destitute, off), refuse, × surely.
