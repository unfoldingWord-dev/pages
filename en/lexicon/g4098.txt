====== G4098: πίπτω (píptō) ======

===== Source =====

probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g4072|G4072]] through the idea of alighting;

===== Meaning =====

to fall (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

fail, fall (down), light on.
