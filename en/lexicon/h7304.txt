====== H7304: רָוַח (râvach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with [[en:lexicon:h7306|H7306]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **breathe** freely, i.e. **revive**; by implication, to **have ample room**

===== Definitions =====

  - breathe
  - revive
  - have ample room

===== Usage =====

be refreshed, large.
