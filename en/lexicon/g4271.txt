====== G4271: πρόδηλος (pródēlos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g1212|G1212]];

===== Meaning =====

plain before all men, i.e. obvious

===== Usage =====

evident, manifest (open) beforehand.
