====== H7585: שְׁאוֹל (shᵉʼôwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h7592|H7592]];

===== Meaning =====

**hades** or the world of the dead (as if a subterranean **retreat**), including its accessories and inmates

===== Definitions =====

  - hades
  - retreat

===== Usage =====

grave, hell, pit.
