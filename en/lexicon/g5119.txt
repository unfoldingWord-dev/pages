====== G5119: τότε (tóte) ======

===== Source =====

from (the neuter of) [[en:lexicon:g3588|G3588]] and [[en:lexicon:g3753|G3753]];

===== Meaning =====

the when, i.e. at the time that (of the past or future, also in consecution)

===== Usage =====

that time, then.
