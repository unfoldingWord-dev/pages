====== H7750: שׂוּט (sûwṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or (by permutation) [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **detrude**, i.e. (intransitively and figuratively) **become derelict** (wrongly practise; namely, idolatry)

===== Definitions =====

  - detrude
  - become derelict

===== Usage =====

turn aside to.
