====== H2644: חָפָא (châphâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

an orthographical variation of [[en:lexicon:h2645|H2645]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **cover**, i.e. (in a sinister sense) to **act covertly**

===== Definitions =====

  - cover
  - act covertly

===== Usage =====

do secretly.
