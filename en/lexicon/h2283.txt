====== H2283: חָגָא (châgâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **revolve** (compare [[en:lexicon:h2287|H2287]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, **vertigo**, i.e. (figuratively) **fear**

===== Definitions =====

  - revolve
  - vertigo
  - fear

===== Usage =====

terror.
