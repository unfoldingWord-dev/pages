====== H6621: פֶּתַע (pethaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **open** (the eyes); (compare [[en:lexicon:h6597|H6597]])

===== Meaning =====

a **wink**, i.e. **moment** (used only [with or without preposition] adverbially, **quickly** or **unexpectedly**)

===== Definitions =====

  - open
  - wink
  - moment
  - quickly
  - unexpectedly

===== Usage =====

at an instant, suddenly, × very.
