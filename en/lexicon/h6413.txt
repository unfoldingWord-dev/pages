===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine of [[en:lexicon:h6412|H6412]];

===== Meaning =====

**deliverance**; concretely, an **escaped** portion

===== Definitions =====

===== Usage =====

deliverance, (that is) escape(-d), remnant.
