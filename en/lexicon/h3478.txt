====== H3478: יִשְׂרָאֵל (Yisrâʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8280|H8280]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]];

===== Meaning =====

**he will rule as God**; **Jisraël**, a symbolical name of Jacob; also (typically) of his posterity

===== Definitions =====

  - he will rule as God
  - Jisraël

===== Usage =====

Israel.
