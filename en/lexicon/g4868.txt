====== G4868: συναίρω (synaírō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g142|G142]];

===== Meaning =====

to make up together, i.e. (figuratively) to compute (an account)

===== Usage =====

reckon, take.
