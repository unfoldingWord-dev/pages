====== G235: ἀλλά (allá) ======

===== Source =====

neuter plural of [[en:lexicon:g243|G243]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, other things, i.e. (adverbially) contrariwise (in many relations)

===== Usage =====

and, but (even), howbeit, indeed, nay, nevertheless, no, notwithstanding, save, therefore, yea, yet.
