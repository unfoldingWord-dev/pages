====== G4181: πολυμερῶς (polymerōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4183|G4183]] and [[en:lexicon:g3313|G3313]];

===== Meaning =====

in many portions, i.e. variously as to time and agency (piecemeal)

===== Usage =====

at sundry times.
