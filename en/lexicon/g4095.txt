====== G4095: πίνω (pínō) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of
      πίω (píō)  ;  which (together with another form)
      πόω (póō)  ; occurs only as an alternate in certain tenses;

===== Meaning =====

to imbibe (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

drink.
