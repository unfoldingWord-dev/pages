====== H1416: גְּדוּד (gᵉdûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1413|H1413]];

===== Meaning =====

a **crowd** (especially of soldiers)

===== Definitions =====

  - crowd

===== Usage =====

army, band (of men), company, troop (of robbers).
