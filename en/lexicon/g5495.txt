====== G5495: χείρ (cheír) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from the base of [[en:lexicon:g5494|G5494]] in the sense of its congener the base of
 [[en:lexicon:g5490|G5490]] (through the idea of hollowness for grasping);

===== Meaning =====

the hand (literally or figuratively (power); especially (by Hebraism) a means or instrument)

===== Usage =====

hand.
