====== G4013: περιάγω (periágō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to take around (as a companion); reflexively, to walk around

===== Usage =====

compass, go (round) about, lead about.
