====== H8237: שַׁפְרוּר (shaphrûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8231|H8231]];

===== Meaning =====

**splendid**, i.e. **a tapestry** or **canopy**

===== Definitions =====

  - splendid
  - a tapestry
  - canopy

===== Usage =====

royal pavilion.
