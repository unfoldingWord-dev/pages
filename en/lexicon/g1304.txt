====== G1304: διατρίβω (diatríbō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5147|G5147]];

===== Meaning =====

to wear through (time), i.e. remain

===== Usage =====

abide, be, continue, tarry.
