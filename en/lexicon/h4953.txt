====== H4953: מַשְׁרוֹקִי (mashrôwqîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h8319|H8319]];

===== Meaning =====

a (musical) **pipe** (from its whistling sound)

===== Definitions =====

  - pipe

===== Usage =====

flute.
