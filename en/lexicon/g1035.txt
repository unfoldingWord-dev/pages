====== G1035: βρῶσις (brōsis) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g977|G977]];

===== Meaning =====

(abstractly) eating (literally or figuratively); by extension (concretely) food (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

eating, food, meat.
