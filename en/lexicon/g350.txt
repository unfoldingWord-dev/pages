====== G350: ἀνακρίνω (anakrínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g2919|G2919]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to scrutinize, i.e. (by implication) investigate, interrogate, determine

===== Usage =====

ask, question, discern, examine, judge, search.
