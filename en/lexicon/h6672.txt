====== H6672: צֹהַר (tsôhar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6671|H6671]];

===== Meaning =====

a **light** (i.e. **window**); dual **double light**, i.e. **noon**

===== Definitions =====

  - light
  - window
  - double light
  - noon

===== Usage =====

midday, noon(-day, -tide), window.
