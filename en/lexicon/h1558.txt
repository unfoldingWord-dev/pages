====== H1558: גָּלָל (gâlâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1556|H1556]];

===== Meaning =====

a **circumstance** (as rolled around); only used adverbially, **on account of**

===== Definitions =====

  - circumstance
  - on account of

===== Usage =====

because of, for (sake).
