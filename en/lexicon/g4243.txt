====== G4243: πρεσβεύω (presbeúō) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4245|G4245]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a senior, i.e. (by implication) act as a representative (figuratively, preacher)

===== Usage =====

be an ambassador.
