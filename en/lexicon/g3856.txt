====== G3856: παραδειγματίζω (paradeigmatízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g1165|G1165]];

===== Meaning =====

to show alongside (the public), i.e. expose to infamy

===== Usage =====

make a public example, put to an open shame.
