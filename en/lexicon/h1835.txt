====== H1835: דָּן (Dân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1777|H1777]]; **judge**;

===== Meaning =====

**Dan**, one of the sons of Jacob; also the tribe descended from him, and its territory; likewise a place in Palestine colonized by them

===== Definitions =====

  - judge
  - Dan

===== Usage =====

Daniel
