====== H4960: מִשְׁתֶּה (mishteh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8354|H8354]];

===== Meaning =====

**drink**, by implication, **drinking** (the act); also (by implication) a **banquet** or (generally) **feast**

===== Definitions =====

  - drink
  - drinking
  - banquet
  - feast

===== Usage =====

banquet, drank, drink, feast((-ed), -ing).
