====== H6498: פַּקֻּעָה (paqquʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6497|H6497]];

===== Meaning =====

the **wild cucumber** (from splitting open to shed its seeds)

===== Definitions =====

  - wild cucumber

===== Usage =====

gourd.
