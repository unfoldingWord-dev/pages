====== H4373: מִכְסָה (mikçâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4371|H4371]];

===== Meaning =====

an **enumeration**; by implication, a **valuation**

===== Definitions =====

  - enumeration
  - valuation

===== Usage =====

number, worth.
