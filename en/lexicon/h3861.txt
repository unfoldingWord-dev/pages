====== H3861: לָהֵן (lâhên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

conjunction

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h3860|H3860]];

===== Meaning =====

**therefore**; also **except**

===== Definitions =====

  - therefore
  - except

===== Usage =====

but, except, save, therefore, wherefore.
