====== G5297: ὑποφέρω (hypophérō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]];

===== Meaning =====

to bear from underneath, i.e. (figuratively) to undergo hardship

===== Usage =====

bear, endure.
