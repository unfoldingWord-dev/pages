====== H6728: צִיִּי (tsîyîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h6723|H6723]];

===== Meaning =====

a **desert-dweller**, i.e. **nomad** or wild **beast**

===== Definitions =====

  - desert-dweller
  - nomad
  - beast

===== Usage =====

wild beast of the desert, that dwell in (inhabiting) the wilderness.
