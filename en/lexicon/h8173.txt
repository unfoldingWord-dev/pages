====== H8173: שָׁעַע (shâʻaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

(in a good acceptation) to **look upon** (with complacency), i.e. **fondle**, **please** or **amuse** (self); (in a bad one) to **look about** (in dismay), i.e. **stare**

===== Definitions =====

  - look upon
  - fondle
  - please
  - amuse
  - look about
  - stare
