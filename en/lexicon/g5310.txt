====== G5310: ὕψιστος (hýpsistos) ======

===== Source =====

superlative from the base of [[en:lexicon:g5311|G5311]];

===== Meaning =====

highest, i.e. (masculine singular) the Supreme (God), or (neuter plural) the heavens

===== Usage =====

most high, highest.
