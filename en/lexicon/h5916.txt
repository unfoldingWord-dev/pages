====== H5916: עָכַר (ʻâkar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **roil water**; figuratively, to **disturb** or **affict**

===== Definitions =====

  - roil water
  - disturb
  - affict

===== Usage =====

trouble, stir.
