====== G1611: ἔκστασις (ékstasis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1839|G1839]];

===== Meaning =====

a displacement of the mind, i.e. bewilderment, "ecstasy"

===== Usage =====

+ be amazed, amazement, astonishment, trance.
