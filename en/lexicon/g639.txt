====== G639: ἀπορέω (aporéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and the base of [[en:lexicon:g4198|G4198]];

===== Meaning =====

to have no way out, i.e. be at a loss (mentally)

===== Usage =====

 (stand in) doubt, be perplexed.
