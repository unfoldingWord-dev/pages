====== H8415: תְּהוֹם (tᵉhôwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (usually feminine) from [[en:lexicon:h1949|H1949]];

===== Meaning =====

an **abyss** (as a surging mass of water), especially the **deep** (the main sea or the subterranean watersupply)

===== Definitions =====

  - abyss
  - deep

===== Usage =====

deep (place), depth.
