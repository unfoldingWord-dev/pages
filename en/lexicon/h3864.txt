====== H3864: לוּבִי (Lûwbîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Daniel 11:43), partrial from a name probably derived from an unused root meaning to thirst, i.e. a dry region; apparently

===== Meaning =====

**a Libyan** or inhabitant of interior Africa (only in plural)

===== Definitions =====

  - a Libyan

===== Usage =====

Lubim(-s), Libyans.
