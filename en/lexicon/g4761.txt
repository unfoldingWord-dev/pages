====== G4761: στρεβλόω (streblóō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4762|G4762]];

===== Meaning =====

to wrench, i.e. (specially), to torture (by the rack), but only figuratively, to pervert

===== Usage =====

 wrest.
