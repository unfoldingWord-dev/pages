====== H1330: בְּתוּלָה (bᵉthûwlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of an unused root meaning to separate;

===== Meaning =====

a **virgin** (from her privacy); sometimes (by continuation) a **bride**; also (figuratively) a city or state

===== Definitions =====

  - virgin
  - bride

===== Usage =====

maid, virgin.
