====== G2626: κατακλύζω (kataklýzō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g2830|G2830]];

===== Meaning =====

to dash (wash) down, i.e. (by implication) to deluge

===== Usage =====

overflow.
