====== G2744: καυχάομαι (kaucháomai) ======

===== Source =====

from some (obsolete) base akin to that of αὐχέω (auchéō) (to boast) and [[en:lexicon:g2172|G2172]];

===== Meaning =====

to vaunt (in a good or a bad sense)

===== Usage =====

(make) boast, glory, joy, rejoice.
