====== H4902: מֶשֶׁךְ (Meshek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

the same in form as [[en:lexicon:h4901|H4901]], but probably of foreign derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**Meshek**, a son of Japheth, and the people descended from him

===== Definitions =====

  - Meshek

===== Usage =====

Mesech, Meshech.
