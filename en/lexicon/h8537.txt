====== H8537: תֹּם (tôm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8552|H8552]];

===== Meaning =====

**completeness**; figuratively, **prosperity**; usually (morally) **innocence**

===== Definitions =====

  - completeness
  - prosperity
  - innocence
