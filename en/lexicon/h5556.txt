====== H5556: סׇלְעָם (çolʻâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5553|H5553]] in the sense of crushing as with a rock, i.e. consuming;

===== Meaning =====

a **kind of locust** (from its destructiveness)

===== Definitions =====

  - kind of locust

===== Usage =====

bald locust.
