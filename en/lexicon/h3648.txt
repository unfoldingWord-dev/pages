====== H3648: כָּמַר (kâmar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **intertwine** or **contract**, i.e. (by implication) to **shrivel** (as with heat); figuratively, to **be deeply affected with passion** (love or pity)

===== Definitions =====

  - intertwine
  - contract
  - shrivel
  - be deeply affected with passion

===== Usage =====

be black, be kindled, yearn.
