====== H5289: נַעַר (naʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5287|H5287]] in its derivative sense of **tossing about**;

===== Meaning =====

a **wanderer**

===== Definitions =====

  - tossing about
  - wanderer

===== Usage =====

young one.
