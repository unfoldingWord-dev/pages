====== H7857: שָׁטַף (shâṭaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **gush**; by implication, to **inundate**, **cleanse**; by analogy, to **gallop**, **conquer**

===== Definitions =====

  - gush
  - inundate
  - cleanse
  - gallop
  - conquer

===== Usage =====

drown, (over-) flow(-whelm, rinse, run, rush, (throughly) wash (away).
