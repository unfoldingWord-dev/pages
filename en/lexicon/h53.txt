====== H53: אֲבִישָׁלוֹם (ʼĂbîyshâlôwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:lexicon:|]] ; from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h7965|H7965]]; father of peace (i.e. friendly);

===== Meaning =====

**Abshalom**, a son of David; also (the fuller form) a later Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Abshalom

===== Usage =====

Abishalom, Absalom.
