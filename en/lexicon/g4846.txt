====== G4846: συμπνίγω (sympnígō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g4155|G4155]];

===== Meaning =====

to strangle completely, i.e. (literally) to drown, or (figuratively) to crowd

===== Usage =====

choke, throng.
