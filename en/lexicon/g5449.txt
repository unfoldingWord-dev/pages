====== G5449: φύσις (phýsis) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5453|G5453]];

===== Meaning =====

growth (by germination or expansion), i.e. (by implication) natural production (lineal descent); by extension, a genus or sort; figuratively, native disposition, constitution or usage

===== Usage =====

(man-)kind, nature(-al).
