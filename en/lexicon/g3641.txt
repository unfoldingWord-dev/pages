====== G3641: ὀλίγος (olígos) ======

===== Source =====

of uncertain affinity;

===== Meaning =====

puny (in extent, degree, number, duration or value); especially neuter (adverbially) somewhat

===== Usage =====

+ almost, brief(-ly), few, (a) little, + long, a season, short, small, a while.
