====== G941: βαστάζω (bastázō) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps remotely derived from the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]] (through the idea of
 removal);

===== Meaning =====

to lift, literally or figuratively (endure, declare, sustain, receive, etc.)

===== Usage =====

bear, carry, take up.
