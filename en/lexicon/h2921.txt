====== H2921: טָלָא (ṭâlâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **cover** with pieces; i.e. (by implication) to **spot** or **variegate** (as tapestry)

===== Definitions =====

  - cover
  - spot
  - variegate

===== Usage =====

clouted, with divers colours, spotted.
