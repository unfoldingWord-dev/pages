====== G3697: ὁποῖος (hopoîos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3739|G3739]] and [[en:lexicon:g4169|G4169]];

===== Meaning =====

of what kind that, i.e. how (as) great (excellent) (specially, as an indefinite correlative to the definite antecedent

===== Usage =====

what manner (sort) of, such as whatsoever.
