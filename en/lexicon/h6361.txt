====== H6361: פַּטִּישׁ (paṭṭîysh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to that of [[en:lexicon:h6360|H6360]];

===== Meaning =====

a **gown** (as if hammered out wide)

===== Definitions =====

  - gown

===== Usage =====

hose.
