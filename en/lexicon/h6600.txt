====== H6600: פִּתְגָּם (pithgâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h6599|H6599]];

===== Meaning =====

a **word**, **answer**, **letter** or **decree**

===== Definitions =====

  - word
  - answer
  - letter
  - decree

===== Usage =====

answer, letter, matter, word.
