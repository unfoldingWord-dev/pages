====== G1463: ἐγκομβόομαι (enkombóomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and κομβόω (kombóō) (to gird);

===== Meaning =====

to engirdle oneself (for labor), i.e. figuratively (the apron as being a badge of servitude) to wear (in token of mutual deference)

===== Usage =====

be clothed with.
