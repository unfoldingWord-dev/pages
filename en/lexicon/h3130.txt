====== H3130: יוֹסֵף (Yôwçêph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

future of [[en:lexicon:h3254|H3254]]; **let him add** (or perhaps simply active participle **adding**);

===== Meaning =====

**Joseph**, the name of seven Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - let him add
  - adding
  - Joseph

===== Usage =====

Joseph. Compare
