====== H670: אֲפָרְסַי (ʼĂphârᵉçay) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) of foreign origin (only in the plural);

===== Meaning =====

an **Apherasite** or inhabitant of an unknown region of Assyria

===== Definitions =====

  - Apherasite

===== Usage =====

Apharsite.
