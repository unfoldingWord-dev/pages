====== G5052: τελεσφορέω (telesphoréō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5056|G5056]] and [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a bearer to completion (maturity), i.e. to ripen fruit (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

bring fruit to perfection.
