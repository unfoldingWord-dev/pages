====== H29: אֲבִיָּה (ʼĂbîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or prolonged [[en:lexicon:|]] ; from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; father (i.e. worshipper) of Jah;

===== Meaning =====

**Abijah**, the name of several Israelite men and two Israelitesses

===== Definitions =====

  - Abijah

===== Usage =====

Abiah, Abijah.
