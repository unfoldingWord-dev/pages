====== G4819: συμβαίνω (symbaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to walk (figuratively, transpire) together, i.e. concur (take place)

===== Usage =====

be(-fall), happen (unto).
