====== H381: אִישׁ־חַיִל (ʼÎysh-Chayil) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h376|H376]] and [[en:lexicon:h2428|H2428]]; man of might; by defective transcription (2 Samuel 23:20) [[en:lexicon:|]]; as if from [[en:lexicon:h376|H376]] and [[en:lexicon:h2416|H2416]]; living man;

===== Meaning =====

**Ishchail** (or Ish-chai), an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Ishchail

===== Usage =====
