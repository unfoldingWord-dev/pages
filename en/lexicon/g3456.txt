====== G3456: μυκτηρίζω (myktērízō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g3455|G3455]] (meaning snout, as that whence
 lowing proceeds);

===== Meaning =====

to make mouths at, i.e. ridicule

===== Usage =====

mock.
