====== H4042: מָגַן (mâgan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h4043|H4043]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **shield**; **encompass with**; figuratively, to **rescue**, to **hand safely over** (i.e. surrender)

===== Definitions =====

  - shield
  - encompass with
  - rescue
  - hand safely over

===== Usage =====

deliver.
