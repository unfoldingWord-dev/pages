====== G2078: ἔσχατος (éschatos) ======

===== Source =====

a superlative probably from [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]] (in the sense of contiguity);

===== Meaning =====

farthest, final (of place or time)

===== Usage =====

ends of, last, latter end, lowest, uttermost.
