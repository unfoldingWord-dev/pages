====== H4754: מָרָא (mârâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **rebel**; hence (through the idea of maltreating) to **whip**, i.e. **lash** (self with wings, as the ostrich in running)

===== Definitions =====

  - rebel
  - whip
  - lash

===== Usage =====

be filthy, lift up self.
