====== H6887: צָרַר (tsârar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cramp**, literally or figuratively, transitive or intransitive

===== Definitions =====

  - cramp

===== Usage =====

adversary, (be in) afflict(-ion), beseige, bind (up), (be in, bring) distress, enemy, narrower, oppress, pangs, shut up, be in a strait (trouble), vex.
