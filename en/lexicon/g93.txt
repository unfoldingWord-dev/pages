====== G93: ἀδικία (adikía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g94|G94]];

===== Meaning =====

(legal) injustice (properly, the quality, by implication, the act); morally, wrongfulness (of character, life or act)

===== Usage =====

iniquity, unjust, unrighteousness, wrong.
