====== G1567: ἐκζητέω (ekzētéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g2212|G2212]];

===== Meaning =====

to search out, i.e. (figuratively)investigate, crave, demand, (by Hebraism) worship

===== Usage =====

en- (re-)quire, seek after (carefully, diligently).
