====== H8551: תָּמַךְ (tâmak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **sustain**; by implication, to **obtain**, **keep fast**; figuratively, to **help**, **follow close**

===== Definitions =====

  - sustain
  - obtain
  - keep fast
  - help
  - follow close

===== Usage =====

(take, up-) hold (up), maintain, retain, stay (up).
