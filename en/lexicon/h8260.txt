====== H8260: שֶׁקֶף (sheqeph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8259|H8259]];

===== Meaning =====

a **loophole** (for looking out), to **admit light and air**

===== Definitions =====

  - loophole
  - admit light and air

===== Usage =====

window.
