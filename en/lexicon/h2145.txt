====== H2145: זָכָר (zâkâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2142|H2142]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **remembered**, i.e. a **male** (of man or animals, as being the most noteworthy sex)

===== Definitions =====

  - remembered
  - male

===== Usage =====

× him, male, man(child, -kind).
