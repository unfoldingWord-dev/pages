====== H219: אוֹרָה (ʼôwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h216|H216]];

===== Meaning =====

**luminousness**, i.e. (figuratively) **prosperity**; also **a plant** (as being bright)

===== Definitions =====

  - luminousness
  - prosperity
  - a plant

===== Usage =====

herb, light.
