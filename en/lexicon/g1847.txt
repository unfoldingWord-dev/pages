====== G1847: ἐξουδενόω (exoudenóō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and a derivative of the neuter of [[en:lexicon:g3762|G3762]];

===== Meaning =====

to make utterly nothing of, i.e. despise

===== Usage =====

set at nought.
