====== H7496: רָפָא (râphâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7495|H7495]] in the sense of [[en:lexicon:h7503|H7503]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **lax**, i.e. (figuratively) a **ghost** (as **dead**; in plural only)

===== Definitions =====

  - lax
  - ghost
  - dead

===== Usage =====

dead, deceased.
