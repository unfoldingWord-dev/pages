====== H7787: שׂוּר (sûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with [[en:lexicon:h7786|H7786]] through the idea of reducing to pieces; compare [[en:lexicon:h4883|H4883]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **saw**

===== Definitions =====

  - saw

===== Usage =====

cut.
