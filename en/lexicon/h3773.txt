====== H3773: כָּרֻתָה (kâruthâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

passive participle feminine of [[en:lexicon:h3772|H3772]];

===== Meaning =====

something **cut**, i.e. a **hewn timber**

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - hewn timber

===== Usage =====

beam.
