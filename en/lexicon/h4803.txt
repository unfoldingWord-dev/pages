====== H4803: מָרַט (mâraṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **polish**; by implication, to **make bald** (the head), to **gall** (the shoulder); also, to **sharpen**

===== Definitions =====

  - polish
  - make bald
  - gall
  - sharpen

===== Usage =====

bright, furbish, (have his) hair (be) fallen off, peeled, pluck off (hair).
