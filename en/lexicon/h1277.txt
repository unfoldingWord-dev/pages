====== H1277: בָּרִיא (bârîyʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1254|H1254]] (in the sense of [[en:lexicon:h1262|H1262]]);

===== Meaning =====

**fatted** or **plump**

===== Definitions =====

  - fatted
  - plump

===== Usage =====

fat ((fleshed), -ter), fed, firm, plenteous, rank.
