====== H4421: מִלְחָמָה (milchâmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3898|H3898]] (in the sense of **fighting**);

===== Meaning =====

a **battle** (i.e. the **engagement**); generally, **war** (i.e. **war-fare**)

===== Definitions =====

  - fighting
  - battle
  - engagement
  - war
  - war-fare

===== Usage =====

battle, fight(-ing), war(-rior).
