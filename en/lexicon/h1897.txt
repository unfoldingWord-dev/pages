====== H1897: הָגָה (hâgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h1901|H1901]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **murmur** (in pleasure or anger); by implication, to **ponder**

===== Definitions =====

  - murmur
  - ponder

===== Usage =====

imagine, meditate, mourn, mutter, roar, × sore, speak, study, talk, utter.
