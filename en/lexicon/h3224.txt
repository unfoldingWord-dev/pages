====== H3224: יְמִימָה (Yᵉmîymâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

perhaps from the same as [[en:lexicon:h3117|H3117]]; properly, warm, i.e. affectionate; hence, dove (compare [[en:lexicon:h3123|H3123]]);

===== Meaning =====

**Jemimah**, one of Job's daughters

===== Definitions =====

  - Jemimah

===== Usage =====

Jemimah.
