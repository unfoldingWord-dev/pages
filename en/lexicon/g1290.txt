====== G1290: διασπορά (diasporá) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1289|G1289]];

===== Meaning =====

dispersion, i.e. (specially and concretely) the (converted) Israelite resident in Gentile countries

===== Usage =====

(which are) scattered (abroad).
