====== H1820: דָּמָה (dâmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be dumb** or **silent**; hence, to **fail** or **perish**; **trans**. to **destroy**

===== Definitions =====

  - be dumb
  - silent
  - fail
  - perish
  - trans
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

cease, be cut down (off), destroy, be brought to silence, be undone, × utterly.
