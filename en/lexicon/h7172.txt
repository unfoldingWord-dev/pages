====== H7172: קַרְקַע (qarqaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7167|H7167]];

===== Meaning =====

**floor** (as if a pavement of pieces or **tesseræ**), of a building or the sea

===== Definitions =====

  - floor
  - tesseræ

===== Usage =====

bottom, (× one side of the) floor.
