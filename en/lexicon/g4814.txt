====== G4814: συλλαλέω (syllaléō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2980|G2980]];

===== Meaning =====

to talk together, i.e. converse

===== Usage =====

commune (confer, talk) with, speak among.
