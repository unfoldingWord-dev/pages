====== G2065: ἐρωτάω (erōtáō) ======

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:lexicon:g2046|G2046]] (compare [[en:lexicon:g2045|G2045]]);

===== Meaning =====

to interrogate; by implication, to request

===== Usage =====

ask, beseech, desire, intreat, pray.
