====== H8491: תִּיצִי (Tîytsîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

patrial or patronymically from an unused noun of uncertain meaning;

===== Meaning =====

a **Titsite** or descendant or inhabitant of an unknown Tits

===== Definitions =====

  - Titsite

===== Usage =====

Tizite.
