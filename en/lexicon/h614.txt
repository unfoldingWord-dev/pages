====== H614: אָסִיף (ʼâçîyph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h622|H622]];

===== Meaning =====

**gathered**, i.e. (abstractly) a **gathering in of crops**

===== Definitions =====

  - gathered
  - gathering in of crops

===== Usage =====

ingathering.
