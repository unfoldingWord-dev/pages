====== G3955: παῤῥησιάζομαι (parrhēsiázomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g3954|G3954]];

===== Meaning =====

to be frank in utterance, or confident in spirit and demeanor

===== Usage =====

be (wax) bold, (preach, speak) boldly.
