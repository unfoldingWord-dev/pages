====== H2945: טַף (ṭaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2952|H2952]] (perhaps referring to the tripping gait of children);

===== Meaning =====

a **family** (mostly used collectively in the singular)

===== Definitions =====

  - family

===== Usage =====

(little) children (ones), families.
