====== H929: בְּהֵמָה (bᵉhêmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root (probably meaning to be **mute**);

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **dumb** beast; especially any large quadruped or **animal** (often collective)

===== Definitions =====

  - mute
  - dumb
  - animal

===== Usage =====

beast, cattle.
