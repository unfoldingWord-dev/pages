====== G2523: καθίζω (kathízō) ======

===== Source =====

another (active) form for [[en:lexicon:g2516|G2516]];

===== Meaning =====

to seat down, i.e. set (figuratively, appoint); intransitively, to sit (down); figuratively, to settle (hover, dwell)

===== Usage =====

continue, set, sit (down), tarry.
