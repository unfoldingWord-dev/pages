====== H5929: עָלֶה (ʻâleh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5927|H5927]];

===== Meaning =====

a **leaf** (as coming up on a tree); collectively, **foliage**

===== Definitions =====

  - leaf
  - foliage

===== Usage =====

branch, leaf.
