====== G2026: ἐποικοδομέω (epoikodoméō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g3618|G3618]];

===== Meaning =====

to build upon, i.e. (figuratively) to rear up

===== Usage =====

build thereon (thereupon, on, upon).
