====== G4149: πλοῦτος (ploûtos) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4130|G4130]];

===== Meaning =====

wealth (as fulness), i.e. (literally) money, possessions, or (figuratively) abundance, richness, (specially), valuable bestowment

===== Usage =====

riches.
