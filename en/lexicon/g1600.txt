====== G1600: ἐκπετάννυμι (ekpetánnymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and a form of [[en:lexicon:g4072|G4072]];

===== Meaning =====

to fly out, i.e. (by analogy) to extend

===== Usage =====

stretch forth.
