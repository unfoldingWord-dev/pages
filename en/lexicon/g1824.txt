====== G1824: ἐξαυτῆς (exautēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and the genitive case singular feminine of [[en:lexicon:g846|G846]] ([[en:lexicon:g5610|G5610]] being
 understood);

===== Meaning =====

from that hour, i.e. instantly

===== Usage =====

by and by, immediately, presently, straightway.
