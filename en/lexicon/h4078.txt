====== H4078: מַדַּי (madday) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4100|H4100]] and [[en:lexicon:h1767|H1767]];

===== Meaning =====

**what** (is) **enough**, i.e. **sufficiently**

===== Definitions =====

  - what
  - enough
  - sufficiently

===== Usage =====

sufficiently.
