====== H6279: עָתַר (ʻâthar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather denominative from [[en:lexicon:h6281|H6281]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **burn incense** in worship, i.e. **intercede** (reciprocally, listen to prayer)

===== Definitions =====

  - burn incense
  - intercede

===== Usage =====

intreat, (make) pray(-er).
