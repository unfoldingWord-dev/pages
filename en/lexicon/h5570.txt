====== H5570: סְנָאָה (Çᵉnâʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to prick; **thorny**;

===== Meaning =====

**Senaah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - thorny
  - Senaah

===== Usage =====

Senaah, Hassenaah (with the article).
