====== H1856: דָּקַר (dâqar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **stab**; by analogy, to **starve**; figuratively, to **revile**

===== Definitions =====

  - stab
  - starve
  - revile

===== Usage =====

pierce, strike (thrust) through, wound.
