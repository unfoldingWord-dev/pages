====== H2961: טָרִי (ṭârîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to be **moist**;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **dripping**; hence, **fresh** (i.e. recently made such)

===== Definitions =====

  - moist
  - dripping
  - fresh

===== Usage =====

new, putrefying.
