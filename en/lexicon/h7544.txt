====== H7544: רֶקַח (reqach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7543|H7543]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **perfumery**, i.e. (by implication) **spicery** (for flavor)

===== Definitions =====

  - perfumery
  - spicery

===== Usage =====

spiced.
