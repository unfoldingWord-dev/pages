====== G2601: καταβιβάζω (katabibázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to cause to go down, i.e. precipitate

===== Usage =====

bring (thrust) down.
