====== H4952: מִשְׁרָה (mishrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8281|H8281]] in the sense of **loosening**;

===== Meaning =====

**maceration**, i.e. steeped **juice**

===== Definitions =====

  - loosening
  - maceration
  - juice

===== Usage =====

liquor.
