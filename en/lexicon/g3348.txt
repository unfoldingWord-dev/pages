====== G3348: μετέχω (metéchō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

to share or participate; by implication, belong to, eat (or drink)

===== Usage =====

be partaker, pertain, take part, use.
