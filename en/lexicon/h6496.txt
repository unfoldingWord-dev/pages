====== H6496: פָּקִיד (pâqîyd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6485|H6485]];

===== Meaning =====

a **superintendent** (civil, military or religious)

===== Definitions =====

  - superintendent

===== Usage =====

which had the charge, governor, office, overseer, (that) was set.
