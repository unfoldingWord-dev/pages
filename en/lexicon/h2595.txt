====== H2595: חֲנִית (chănîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2583|H2583]];

===== Meaning =====

a **lance** (for **thrusting**, like **pitching** a tent)

===== Definitions =====

  - lance
  - thrusting
  - pitching

===== Usage =====

javelin, spear.
