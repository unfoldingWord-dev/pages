====== H1353: גְּאֻלָּה (gᵉʼullâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h1350|H1350]];

===== Meaning =====

**redemption** (including the right and the object); by implication, **relationship**

===== Definitions =====

  - redemption
  - relationship

===== Usage =====

kindred, redeem, redemption, right.
