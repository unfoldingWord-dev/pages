====== H1917: הַדָּם (haddâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to that of [[en:lexicon:h1916|H1916]];

===== Meaning =====

something **stamped to pieces**, i.e. a **bit**

===== Definitions =====

  - stamped to pieces
  - bit

===== Usage =====

piece.
