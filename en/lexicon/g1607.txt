====== G1607: ἐκπορεύομαι (ekporeúomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g4198|G4198]];

===== Meaning =====

to depart, be discharged, proceed, project

===== Usage =====

come (forth, out of), depart, go (forth, out), issue, proceed (out of).
