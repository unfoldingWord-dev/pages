====== G68: ἀγρός (agrós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

a field (as a drive for cattle); genitive case, the country; specially, a farm, i.e. hamlet

===== Usage =====

country, farm, piece of ground, land.
