====== H5459: סְגֻלָּה (çᵉgullâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of an unused root meaning to **shut** up;

===== Meaning =====

**wealth** (as closely **shut** up)

===== Definitions =====

  - shut
  - wealth
  - shut

===== Usage =====

jewel, peculiar (treasure), proper good, special.
