====== H1468: גּוּז (gûwz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h1494|H1494]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **shear off**; but used only in the (figuratively) sense of **passing rapidly**

===== Definitions =====

  - shear off
  - passing rapidly

===== Usage =====

bring, cut off.
