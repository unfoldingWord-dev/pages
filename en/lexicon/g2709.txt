====== G2709: καταχθόνιος (katachthónios) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and χθών (chthṓn) (the ground);

===== Meaning =====

subterranean, i.e. infernal (belonging to the world of departed spirits)

===== Usage =====

under the earth.
