====== G3339: μεταμορφόω (metamorphóō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and [[en:lexicon:g3445|G3445]];

===== Meaning =====

to transform (literally or figuratively, "metamorphose")

===== Usage =====

change, transfigure, transform.
