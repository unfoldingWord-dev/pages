====== H6133: עֵקֶר (ʻêqer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6131|H6131]];

===== Meaning =====

figuratively, a **transplanted person**, i.e. **naturalized citizen**

===== Definitions =====

  - transplanted person
  - naturalized citizen

===== Usage =====

stock.
