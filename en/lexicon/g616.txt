====== G616: ἀποκυέω (apokyéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g2949|G2949]];

===== Meaning =====

to breed forth, i.e. (by transference) to generate (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

beget, produce.
