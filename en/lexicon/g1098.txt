====== G1098: γλεῦκος (gleûkos) ======

===== Source =====

akin to [[en:lexicon:g1099|G1099]];

===== Meaning =====

sweet wine, i.e. (properly) must (fresh juice), but used of the more saccharine (and therefore highly inebriating) fermented wine

===== Usage =====

new wine.
