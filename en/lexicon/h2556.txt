====== H2556: חָמֵץ (châmêts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be pungent**; i.e. in taste (**sour**, i.e. literally **fermented**, or figuratively, **harsh**), in color (**dazzling**)

===== Definitions =====

  - be pungent
  - sour
  - fermented
  - harsh
  - dazzling

===== Usage =====

cruel (man), dyed, be grieved, leavened.
