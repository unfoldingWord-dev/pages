====== H7329: רָזָה (râzâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **emaciate**, i.e. **make** (**become**) **thin** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - emaciate
  - make
  - become
  - thin

===== Usage =====

famish, wax lean.
