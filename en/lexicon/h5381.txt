====== H5381: נָשַׂג (nâsag) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **reach** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - reach

===== Usage =====

ability, be able, attain (unto), (be able to, can) get, lay at, put, reach, remove, wax rich, × surely, (over-) take (hold of, on, upon).
