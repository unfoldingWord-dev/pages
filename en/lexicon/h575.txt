====== H575: אָן (ʼân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; contracted from [[en:lexicon:h370|H370]];

===== Meaning =====

**where?**; hence, **whither?**, **when?**; also **hither** and **thither**

===== Definitions =====

  - where?
  - whither?
  - when?
  - hither
  - thither

===== Usage =====

  any (no) whither, now, where, whither(-soever).
