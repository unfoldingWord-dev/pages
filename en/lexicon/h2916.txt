====== H2916: טִיט (ṭîyṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning apparently to **be sticky** (rather perhaps a demonstrative); from [[en:lexicon:h2894|H2894]], through the idea of dirt to be **swept** away);

===== Meaning =====

**mud** or **clay**; figuratively, **calamity**

===== Definitions =====

  - be sticky
  - swept
  - mud
  - clay
  - calamity

===== Usage =====

clay, dirt, mire.
