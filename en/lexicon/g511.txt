====== G511: ἀνώτερος (anṓteros) ======

===== Source =====

comparative degree of [[en:lexicon:g507|G507]];

===== Meaning =====

upper, i.e. (neuter as adverb) to a more conspicuous place, in a former part of the book

===== Usage =====

above, higher.
