====== H804: אַשּׁוּר (ʼAshshûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculinenoun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; apparently from [[en:lexicon:h833|H833]] (in the sense of successful);

===== Meaning =====

**Ashshur**, the second son of Shem; also his descendants and the country occupied by them (i.e. Assyria), its region and its empire
