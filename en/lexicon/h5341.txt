====== H5341: נָצַר (nâtsar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **guard**, in a good sense (to **protect**, **maintain**, **obey**, etc.) or a bad one (to **conceal**, etc.)

===== Definitions =====

  - guard
  - protect
  - maintain
  - obey
  - conceal

===== Usage =====

besieged, hidden thing, keep(-er, -ing), monument, observe, preserve(-r), subtil, watcher(-man).
