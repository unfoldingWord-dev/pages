====== H2604: חֲנַן (chănan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h2603|H2603]];

===== Meaning =====

to **favor** or (causatively) to **entreat**

===== Definitions =====

  - favor
  - entreat

===== Usage =====

shew mercy, make supplication.
