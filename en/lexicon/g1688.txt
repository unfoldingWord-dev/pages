====== G1688: ἐμβιβάζω (embibázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and βιβάζω (bibázō) (to mount; causative of [[en:lexicon:g1684|G1684]]);

===== Meaning =====

to place on, i.e. transfer (aboard a vessel)

===== Usage =====

put in.
