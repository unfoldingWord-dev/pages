====== G5319: φανερόω (phaneróō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5318|G5318]];

===== Meaning =====

to render apparent (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

appear, manifestly declare, (make) manifest (forth), shew (self).
