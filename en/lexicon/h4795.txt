====== H4795: מָרוּק (mârûwq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4838|H4838]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **rubbed**; but used abstractly, a **rubbing** (with perfumery)

===== Definitions =====

  - rubbed
  - rubbing

===== Usage =====

purification.
