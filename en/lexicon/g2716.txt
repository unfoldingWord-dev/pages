====== G2716: κατεργάζομαι (katergázomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2038|G2038]];

===== Meaning =====

to work fully, i.e. accomplish; by implication, to finish, fashion

===== Usage =====

cause, to (deed), perform, work (out).
