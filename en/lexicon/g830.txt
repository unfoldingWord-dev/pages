====== G830: αὐθαίρετος (authaíretos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g846|G846]] and the same as [[en:lexicon:g140|G140]];

===== Meaning =====

self-chosen, i.e. (by implication) voluntary

===== Usage =====

of own accord, willing of self.
