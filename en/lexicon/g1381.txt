====== G1381: δοκιμάζω (dokimázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1384|G1384]];

===== Meaning =====

to test (literally or figuratively); by implication, to approve

===== Usage =====

allow, discern, examine, X like, (ap-)prove, try.
