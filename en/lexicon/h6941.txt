====== H6941: קְדֹרַנִּית (qᵉdôrannîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

adverb from [[en:lexicon:h6937|H6937]];

===== Meaning =====

**blackish** ones (i.e. in sackcloth); used adverbially, in **mourning weeds**

===== Definitions =====

  - blackish
  - mourning weeds

===== Usage =====

mournfully.
