====== H5271: נָעוּר (nâʻûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculinenoun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; and (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; properly, passive participle from [[en:lexicon:h5288|H5288]] as denominative;

===== Meaning =====

(only in plural collective or emphatic form) **youth**, the state (**juvenility**) or the persons (**young** people)

===== Definitions =====

  - youth
  - juvenility
  - young

===== Usage =====

childhood, youth.
