====== G4302: προλέγω (prolégō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g3004|G3004]];

===== Meaning =====

to say beforehand, i.e. predict, forewarn

===== Usage =====

foretell, tell before.
