====== H8099: שִׁמְעֹנִי (Shimʻônîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

patronymically from [[en:lexicon:h8095|H8095]];

===== Meaning =====

a **Shimonite** (collectively) or descendants of Shimon

===== Definitions =====

  - Shimonite

===== Usage =====

tribe of Simeon, Simeonites.
