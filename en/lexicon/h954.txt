====== H954: בּוּשׁ (bûwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **pale**, i.e. by implication to **be ashamed**; also (by implication) to **be disappointed** or **delayed**

===== Definitions =====

  - pale
  - be ashamed
  - be disappointed
  - delayed

===== Usage =====

(be, make, bring to, cause, put to, with, a-) shamed(-d), be (put to) confounded(-fusion), become dry, delay, be long.
