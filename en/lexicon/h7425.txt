====== H7425: רְמַלְיָהוּ (Rᵉmalyâhûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]] (perhaps meaning to **deck**); **Jah has bedecked**;

===== Meaning =====

**Remaljah**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - deck
  - Jah has bedecked
  - Remaljah

===== Usage =====

Remaliah.
