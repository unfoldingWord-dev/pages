====== G4254: προάγω (proágō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to lead forward (magisterially); intransitively, to precede (in place or time (participle, previous))

===== Usage =====

bring (forth, out), go before.
