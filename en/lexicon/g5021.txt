====== G5021: τάσσω (tássō) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of a primary verb (which latter appears only in
 certain tenses);

===== Meaning =====

to arrange in an orderly manner, i.e. assign or dispose (to a certain position or lot)

===== Usage =====

addict, appoint, determine, ordain, set.
