====== H7306: רוּחַ (rûwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **blow**, i.e. **breathe**; only (literally) to **smell** or (by implication, perceive (figuratively, to anticipate, enjoy)

===== Definitions =====

  - blow
  - breathe
  - smell

===== Usage =====

accept, smell, × touch, make of quick understanding.
