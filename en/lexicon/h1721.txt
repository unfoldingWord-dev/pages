====== H1721: דֹּדָנִים (Dôdânîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (by orthographical error) [[en:lexicon:|]] (1 Chronicles 1:7); a plural of uncertain derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**Dodanites**, or descendants of a son of Javan

===== Definitions =====

  - Dodanites

===== Usage =====

Dodanim.
