====== G3843: πάντως (pántōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from [[en:lexicon:g3956|G3956]];

===== Meaning =====

entirely; specially, at all events, (with negative, following) in no event

===== Usage =====

by all means, altogether, at all, needs, no doubt, in (no) wise, surely.
