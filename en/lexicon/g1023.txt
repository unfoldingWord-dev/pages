====== G1023: βραχίων (brachíōn) ======

===== Source =====

properly, comparative of [[en:lexicon:g1024|G1024]], but apparently in the sense of
 βράσσω (brássō) (to wield);

===== Meaning =====

the arm, i.e. (figuratively) strength

===== Usage =====

arm.
