====== H4013: מִבְצָר (mibtsâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

also (in plural) feminine (Daniel 11:l5) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1219|H1219]];

===== Meaning =====

a **fortification**, **castle**, or **fortified** city; figuratively, a **defender**

===== Definitions =====

  - fortification
  - castle
  - fortified
  - defender

===== Usage =====

(de-, most) fenced, fortress, (most) strong (hold).
