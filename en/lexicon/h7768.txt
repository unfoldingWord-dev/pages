====== H7768: שָׁוַע (shâvaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be free**; but used only causatively and reflexively, to **halloo** (for help, i.e. **freedom** from some trouble)

===== Definitions =====

  - be free
  - halloo
  - freedom

===== Usage =====

cry (aloud, out), shout.
