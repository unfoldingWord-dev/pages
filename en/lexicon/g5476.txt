====== G5476: χαμαί (chamaí) ======

===== Source =====

adverb perhaps from the base of [[en:lexicon:g5490|G5490]] through the idea of a fissure in
 the soil;

===== Meaning =====

earthward, i.e. prostrate

===== Usage =====

on (to) the ground.
