====== H8271: שְׁרֵא (shᵉrêʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) a root corresponding to that of [[en:lexicon:h8293|H8293]];

===== Meaning =====

to **free**, **separate**; figuratively, to **unravel**, **commence**; by implication (of unloading beasts) to **reside**

===== Definitions =====

  - free
  - separate
  - unravel
  - commence
  - reside

===== Usage =====

begin, dissolve, dwell, loose.
