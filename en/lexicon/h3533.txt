====== H3533: כָּבַשׁ (kâbash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **tread down**; hence, negatively, to **disregard**; positively, to **conquer**, **subjugate**, **violate**

===== Definitions =====

  - tread down
  - disregard
  - conquer
  - subjugate
  - violate

===== Usage =====

bring into bondage, force, keep under, subdue, bring into subjection.
