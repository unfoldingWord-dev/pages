====== G23: ἀγανακτέω (aganaktéō) ======

===== Source =====

from ἄγαν (ágan) (much) and ἄχθος (áchthos) (grief; akin to the base of [[en:lexicon:g43|G43]]);

===== Meaning =====

to be greatly afflicted, i.e. (figuratively) indignant

===== Usage =====

be much (sore) displeased, have (be moved with, with) indignation.
