====== H5908: עַכָּבִישׁ (ʻakkâbîysh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from an unused root in the literal sense of **entangling**;

===== Meaning =====

a **spider** (as **weaving** a network)

===== Definitions =====

  - entangling
  - spider
  - weaving

===== Usage =====

spider.
