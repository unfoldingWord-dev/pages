====== H2407: חַטּוּשׁ (Chaṭṭûwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root of uncertain signification;

===== Meaning =====

**Chattush**, the name of four or five Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Chattush

===== Usage =====

Hattush.
