====== G3878: παρακούω (parakoúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g191|G191]];

===== Meaning =====

to mishear, i.e. (by implication) to disobey

===== Usage =====

neglect to hear.
