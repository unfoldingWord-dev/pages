====== G749: ἀρχιερεύς (archiereús) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g746|G746]] and [[en:lexicon:g2409|G2409]];

===== Meaning =====

the high-priest (literally, of the Jews, typically, Christ); by extension a chief priest

===== Usage =====

chief (high) priest, chief of the priests.
