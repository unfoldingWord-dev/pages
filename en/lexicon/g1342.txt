====== G1342: δίκαιος (díkaios) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1349|G1349]];

===== Meaning =====

equitable (in character or act); by implication, innocent, holy (absolutely or relatively)

===== Usage =====

just, meet, right(-eous).
