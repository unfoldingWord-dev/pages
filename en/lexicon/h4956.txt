====== H4956: מִשְׂרְפוֹת מַיִם (Misrᵉphôwth mayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the plural of [[en:lexicon:h4955|H4955]] and [[en:lexicon:h4325|H4325]]; **burnings of water**;

===== Meaning =====

**Misrephoth-Majim**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - burnings of water
  - Misrephoth-Majim

===== Usage =====

Misrephoth-mayim.
