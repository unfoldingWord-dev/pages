====== H7217: רֵאשׁ (rêʼsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h7218|H7218]];

===== Meaning =====

the **head**; figuratively, the **sum**

===== Definitions =====

  - head
  - sum

===== Usage =====

chief, head, sum.
