====== H5032: נְבָיוֹת (Nᵉbâyôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine plural from [[en:lexicon:h5107|H5107]]; **fruitfulnesses**;

===== Meaning =====

**Nebajoth**, a son of Ismael, and the country settled by him

===== Definitions =====

  - fruitfulnesses
  - Nebajoth

===== Usage =====

Nebaioth, Nebajoth.
