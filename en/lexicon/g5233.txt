====== G5233: ὑπερβαίνω (hyperbaínō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5228|G5228]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g939|G939]];

===== Meaning =====

to transcend, i.e. (figuratively) to overreach
