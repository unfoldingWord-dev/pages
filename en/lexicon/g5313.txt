====== G5313: ὕψωμα (hýpsōma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5312|G5312]];

===== Meaning =====

an elevated place or thing, i.e. (abstractly) altitude, or (by implication) a barrier (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

 height, high thing.
