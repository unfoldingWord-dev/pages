====== G91: ἀδικέω (adikéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g94|G94]];

===== Meaning =====

to be unjust, i.e. (actively) do wrong (morally, socially or physically)

===== Usage =====

hurt, injure, be an offender, be unjust, (do, suffer, take) wrong.
