====== H5640: סָתַם (çâtham) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Numbers 24:15), a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **stop** up; by implication, to **repair**; figuratively, to **keep secret**

===== Definitions =====

  - stop
  - repair
  - keep secret

===== Usage =====

closed up, hidden, secret, shut out (up), stop.
