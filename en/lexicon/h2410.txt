====== H2410: חֲטִיטָא (Chăṭîyṭâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to dig out; explorer;

===== Meaning =====

**Chatita**, a temple porter

===== Definitions =====

  - Chatita

===== Usage =====

Hatita.
