====== H3429: יֹשֵׁב בַּשֶּׁבֶת (Yôshêb bash-Shebeth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the active participle of [[en:lexicon:h3427|H3427]] and [[en:lexicon:h7674|H7674]], with a preposition and the article interposed; sitting in the seat;

===== Meaning =====

**Joshebbash-Shebeth**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Joshebbash-Shebeth

===== Usage =====

that sat in the seat.
