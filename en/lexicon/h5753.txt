====== H5753: עָוָה (ʻâvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **crook**, literally or figuratively

===== Definitions =====

  - crook

===== Usage =====

do amiss, bow down, make crooked, commit iniquity, pervert, (do) perverse(-ly), trouble, × turn, do wickedly, do wrong.
