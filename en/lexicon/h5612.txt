====== H5612: סֵפֶר (çêpher) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Psalm 56:8 ([[en:lexicon:h9|H9]])), from [[en:lexicon:h5608|H5608]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **writing** (the art or a document); by implication, a **book**

===== Definitions =====

  - writing
  - book

===== Usage =====

bill, book, evidence, × learn(-ed) (-ing), letter, register, scroll.
