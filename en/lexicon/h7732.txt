====== H7732: שׁוֹבָל (Shôwbâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h7640|H7640]]; **overflowing**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shobal**, the name of an Edomite and two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - overflowing
  - Shobal

===== Usage =====

Shobal.
