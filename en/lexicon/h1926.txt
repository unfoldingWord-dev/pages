====== H1926: הָדָר (hâdâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1921|H1921]];

===== Meaning =====

**magnificence**, i.e. ornament or splendor

===== Definitions =====

  - magnificence

===== Usage =====

beauty, comeliness, excellency, glorious, glory, goodly, honour, majesty.
