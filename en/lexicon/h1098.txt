====== H1098: בְּלִיל (bᵉlîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1101|H1101]];

===== Meaning =====

**mixed**, i.e. (specifically) **feed** (for cattle)

===== Definitions =====

  - mixed
  - feed

===== Usage =====

corn, fodder, provender.
