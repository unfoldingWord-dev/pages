====== H2519: חֲלַקְלַקָּה (chălaqlaqqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

by reduplication from [[en:lexicon:h2505|H2505]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **very smooth**; i.e. a **treacherous** spot; figuratively, **blandishment**

===== Definitions =====

  - very smooth
  - treacherous
  - blandishment

===== Usage =====

flattery, slippery.
