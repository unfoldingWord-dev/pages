====== H6348: פָּחַז (pâchaz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **bubble up** or **froth** (as boiling water), i.e. (figuratively) to **be unimportant**

===== Definitions =====

  - bubble up
  - froth
  - be unimportant

===== Usage =====

light.
