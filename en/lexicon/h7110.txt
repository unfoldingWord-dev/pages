====== H7110: קֶצֶף (qetseph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7107|H7107]];

===== Meaning =====

a **splinter** (as chipped off); figuratively, **rage** or **strife**

===== Definitions =====

  - splinter
  - rage
  - strife

===== Usage =====

foam, indignation, × sore, wrath.
