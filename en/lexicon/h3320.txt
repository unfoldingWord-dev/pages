====== H3320: יָצַב (yâtsab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **place** (any thing so as to stay); reflexively, to **station**, **offer**, **continue**

===== Definitions =====

  - place
  - station
  - offer
  - continue

===== Usage =====

present selves, remaining, resort, set (selves), (be able to, can, with-) stand (fast, forth, -ing, still, up).
