====== H801: אִשָּׁה (ʼishshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h800|H800]], but used in a liturgical sense;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **burnt-offering**; but occasionally of any **sacrifice**

===== Definitions =====

  - burnt-offering
  - sacrifice

===== Usage =====

(offering, sacrifice), (made) by fire.
