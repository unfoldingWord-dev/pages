====== H4261: מַחְמָד (machmâd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2530|H2530]];

===== Meaning =====

**delightful**; hence, **a delight**, i.e. object of affection or desire

===== Definitions =====

  - delightful
  - a delight

===== Usage =====

beloved, desire, goodly, lovely, pleasant (thing).
