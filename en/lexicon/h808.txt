====== H808: אָשִׁישׁ (ʼâshîysh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h784|H784]] (in the sense of **pressing** down firmly; compare [[en:lexicon:h803|H803]]);

===== Meaning =====

a (ruined) **foundation**

===== Definitions =====

  - pressing
  - foundation

===== Usage =====

foundation.
