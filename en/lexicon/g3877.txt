====== G3877: παρακολουθέω (parakolouthéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g190|G190]];

===== Meaning =====

to follow near, i.e. (figuratively) attend (as a result), trace out, conform to

===== Usage =====

attain, follow, fully know, have understanding.
