====== H7610: שְׁאָר יָשׁוּב (Shᵉʼâr Yâshûwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7605|H7605]] and [[en:lexicon:h7725|H7725]]; a **remnant will return**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shear-Jashub**, the symbolic name of one of Isaiah's sons

===== Definitions =====

  - remnant will return
  - Shear-Jashub

===== Usage =====

Shear-jashub.
