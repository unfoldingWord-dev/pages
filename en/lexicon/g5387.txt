====== G5387: φιλόστοργος (philóstorgos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5384|G5384]] and στοργή (storgḗ) (cherishing one's kindred, especially parents or
 children);

===== Meaning =====

fond of natural relatives, i.e. fraternal towards fellow Christian

===== Usage =====

kindly affectioned.
