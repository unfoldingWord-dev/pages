====== G1798: ἐνύπνιον (enýpnion) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g5258|G5258]];

===== Meaning =====

something seen in sleep, i.e. a dream (vision in a dream)

===== Usage =====

dream.
