====== G4410: πρωτοκαθεδρία (prōtokathedría) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4413|G4413]] and [[en:lexicon:g2515|G2515]];

===== Meaning =====

a sitting first (in the front row), i.e. preeminence in council

===== Usage =====

chief (highest, uppermost) seat.
