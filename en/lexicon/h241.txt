====== H241: אֹזֶן (ʼôzen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h238|H238]];

===== Meaning =====

**broadness**. i.e. (concrete) the **ear** (from its form in man)

===== Definitions =====

  - broadness
  - ear

===== Usage =====

  advertise, audience,   displease, ear, hearing,   show.
