====== H3064: יְהוּדִי (Yᵉhûwdîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

patronymically from [[en:lexicon:h3063|H3063]];

===== Meaning =====

a **Jehudite** (i.e. Judaite or Jew), or descendant of Jehudah (i.e. Judah)

===== Definitions =====

  - Jehudite

===== Usage =====

Jew.
