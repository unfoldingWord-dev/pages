====== G3100: μαθητεύω (mathēteúō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3101|G3101]];

===== Meaning =====

intransitively, to become a pupil; transitively, to disciple, i.e. enrol as scholar

===== Usage =====

be disciple, instruct, teach.
