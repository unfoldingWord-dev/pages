====== H6690: צוֹפַח (Tsôwphach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to expand, **breadth**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tsophach**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - breadth
  - Tsophach

===== Usage =====

Zophah.
