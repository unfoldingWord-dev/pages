====== H2068: זַבְדִּיאֵל (Zabdîyʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2065|H2065]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]];

===== Meaning =====

**gift of God**; **Zabdiel**, the name of two Israelites
