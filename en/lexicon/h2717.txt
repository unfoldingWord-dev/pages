====== H2717: חָרַב (chârab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **parch** (through drought) i.e. (by analogy,) to **desolate**, **destroy**, **kill**

===== Definitions =====

  - parch
  - desolate
  - destroy
  - kill

===== Usage =====

decay, (be) desolate, destroy(-er), (be) dry (up), slay, × surely, (lay, lie, make) waste.
