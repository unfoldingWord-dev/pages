====== H2100: זוּב (zûwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **flow** freely (as water), i.e. (specifically) to **have** a (sexual) **flux**; figuratively, to **waste** away; also to **overflow**

===== Definitions =====

  - flow
  - have
  - flux
  - waste
  - overflow

===== Usage =====

flow, gush out, have a (running) issue, pine away, run.
