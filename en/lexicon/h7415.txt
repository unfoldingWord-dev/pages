====== H7415: רִמָּה (rimmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7426|H7426]] in the sense of **breeding** (compare [[en:lexicon:h7311|H7311]]);

===== Meaning =====

a **maggot** (as rapidly **bred**), literally or figuratively

===== Definitions =====

  - breeding
  - maggot
  - bred

===== Usage =====

worm.
