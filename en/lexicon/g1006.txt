====== G1006: βόσκω (bóskō) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of a primary verb (compare [[en:lexicon:g977|G977]], [[en:lexicon:g1016|G1016]]);

===== Meaning =====

to pasture; by extension to, fodder; reflexively, to graze

===== Usage =====

feed, keep.
