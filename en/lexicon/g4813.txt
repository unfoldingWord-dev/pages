====== G4813: συλάω (syláō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of σύλλω (sýllō) (to strip; probably akin to [[en:lexicon:g138|G138]]; compare
 [[en:lexicon:g4661|G4661]]);

===== Meaning =====
