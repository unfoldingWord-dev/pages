====== H680: אָצַל (ʼâtsal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **join**; used only as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h681|H681]]; to **separate**; hence, to **select**, **refuse**, **contract**

===== Definitions =====

  - join
  - separate
  - select
  - refuse
  - contract

===== Usage =====

keep, reserve, straiten, take.
