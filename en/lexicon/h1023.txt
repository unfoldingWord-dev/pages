====== H1023: בֵּית הַמֶּרְחָק (Bêyth ham-Merchâq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1004|H1004]] and [[en:lexicon:h4801|H4801]] with the article interposed; house of the breadth;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-ham-Merchak**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-ham-Merchak

===== Usage =====

place that was far off.
