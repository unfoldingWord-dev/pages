====== G384: ἀνασκευάζω (anaskeuázō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] (in the sense of reversal) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4632|G4632]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to pack up (baggage), i.e. (by implication, and figuratively) to upset

===== Usage =====

subvert.
