====== H2645: חָפָה (châphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2644|H2644]], [[en:lexicon:h2653|H2653]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **cover**; by implication, to **veil**, to **encase**, **protect**

===== Definitions =====

  - cover
  - veil
  - encase
  - protect

===== Usage =====

ceil, cover, overlay.
