====== G4778: συγκακουχέω (synkakouchéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g2558|G2558]];

===== Meaning =====

to maltreat in company with, i.e. (passively) endure persecution together

===== Usage =====

suffer affliction with.
