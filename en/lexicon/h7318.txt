====== H7318: רוֹמָם (rôwmâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7426|H7426]];

===== Meaning =====

**exaltation**, i.e. (figuratively and specifically) **praise**

===== Definitions =====

  - exaltation
  - praise

===== Usage =====

be extolled.
