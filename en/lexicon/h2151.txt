====== H2151: זָלַל (zâlal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2107|H2107]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **shake** (as in the wind), i.e. to **quake**; figuratively, to **be loose** morally, **worthless** or **prodigal**

===== Definitions =====

  - shake
  - quake
  - be loose
  - worthless
  - prodigal

===== Usage =====

blow down, glutton, riotous (eater), vile.
