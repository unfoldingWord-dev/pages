====== H5849: עָטַר (ʻâṭar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **encircle** (for attack or protection); especially to **crown** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - encircle
  - crown

===== Usage =====

compass, crown.
