====== G1200: δεσμοφύλαξ (desmophýlax) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1199|G1199]] and [[en:lexicon:g5441|G5441]];

===== Meaning =====

a jailer (as guarding the prisoners)

===== Usage =====

jailor, keeper of the prison.
