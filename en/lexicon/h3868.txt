====== H3868: לוּז (lûwz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare [[en:lexicon:h3867|H3867]], [[en:lexicon:h3874|H3874]] and [[en:lexicon:h3885|H3885]])

===== Meaning =====

to **turn** aside , i.e. (literally) to **depart**, (figuratively) **be perverse**

===== Definitions =====

  - turn
  - depart
  - be perverse

===== Usage =====

depart, froward, perverse(-ness).
