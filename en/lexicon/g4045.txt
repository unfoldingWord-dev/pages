====== G4045: περιπίπτω (peripíptō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g4098|G4098]];

===== Meaning =====

to fall into something that is all around, i.e. light among or upon, be surrounded with

===== Usage =====

fall among (into).
