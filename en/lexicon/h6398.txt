====== H6398: פָּלַח (pâlach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **slice**, i.e. **break open** or **pierce**

===== Definitions =====

  - slice
  - break open
  - pierce

===== Usage =====

bring forth, cleave, cut, shred, strike through.
