====== H8462: תְּחִלָּה (tᵉchillâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2490|H2490]] in the sense of opening;

===== Meaning =====

a **commencement**; relatively **original** (adverb, -ly)

===== Definitions =====

  - commencement
  - original

===== Usage =====

begin(-ning), first (time).
