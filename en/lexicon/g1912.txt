====== G1912: ἐπιβαρέω (epibaréō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g916|G916]];

===== Meaning =====

to be heavy upon, i.e. (pecuniarily) to be expensive to; figuratively, to be severe towards

===== Usage =====

be chargeable to, overcharge.
