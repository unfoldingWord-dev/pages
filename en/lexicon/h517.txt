====== H517: אֵם (ʼêm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

a primitive word;

===== Meaning =====

a **mother** (as the bond of the family); in a wide sense (both literally and figuratively [like father])

===== Definitions =====

  - mother

===== Usage =====

dam, mother, × parting.
