====== G2725: κατήγορος (katḗgoros) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g58|G58]];

===== Meaning =====

against one in the assembly, i.e. a complainant at law; specially, Satan

===== Usage =====

accuser.
