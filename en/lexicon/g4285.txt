====== G4285: προηγέομαι (proēgéomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2233|G2233]];

===== Meaning =====

to lead the way for others, i.e. show deference

===== Usage =====
