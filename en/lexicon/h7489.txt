====== H7489: רָעַע (râʻaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **spoil** (literally, by breaking to pieces); figuratively, to **make** (or **be**) **good for nothing**, i.e. **bad** (physically, socially or morally)

===== Definitions =====

  - spoil
  - make
  - be
  - good for nothing
  - bad
