====== H3341: יָצַת (yâtsath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **burn** or **set on fire**; figuratively, to **desolate**

===== Definitions =====

  - burn
  - set on fire
  - desolate

===== Usage =====

burn (up), be desolate, set (on) fire (fire), kindle.
