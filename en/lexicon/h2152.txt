====== H2152: זַלְעָפָה (zalʻâphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h2196|H2196]];

===== Meaning =====

a **glow** (of wind or anger); also a **famine** (as consuming)

===== Definitions =====

  - glow
  - famine

===== Usage =====

horrible, horror, terrible.
