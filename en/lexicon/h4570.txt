====== H4570: מַעְגָּל (maʻgâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or feminine [[en:lexicon:|]]; from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5696|H5696]];

===== Meaning =====

a **track** (literally or figuratively); also a **rampart** (as **circular**)

===== Definitions =====

  - track
  - rampart
  - circular

===== Usage =====

going, path, trench, way(-side).
