====== G3513: νή (nḗ) ======

===== Source =====

probably an intensive form of [[en:lexicon:g3483|G3483]];

===== Meaning =====

a particle of attestation (accompanied by the object invoked or appealed to in confirmation); as sure as

===== Usage =====

I protest by.
