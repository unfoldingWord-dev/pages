====== G3568: νῦν (nŷn) ======

===== Source =====

a primary particle of present time;

===== Meaning =====

"now" (as adverb of date, a transition or emphasis); also as noun or adjective present or immediate

===== Usage =====

henceforth, + hereafter, of late, soon, present, this (time).
