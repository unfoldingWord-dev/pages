====== G683: ἀπωθέομαι (apōthéomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and the middle voice of ὠθέω (ōthéō) or ὤθω (ṓthō) (to shove);

===== Meaning =====

to push off, figuratively, to reject

===== Usage =====

cast away, put away (from), thrust away (from).
