====== H3715: כְּפִיר (kᵉphîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3722|H3722]];

===== Meaning =====

a **village** (as covered in by walls); also a **young lion** (perhaps as covered with a mane)

===== Definitions =====

  - village
  - young lion

===== Usage =====

(young) lion, village. Compare
