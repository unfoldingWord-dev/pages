====== H1760: דָּחָה (dâchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Jeremiah 23:12), a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **push down**

===== Definitions =====

  - push down

===== Usage =====

chase, drive away (on), overthrow, outcast, × sore, thrust, totter.
