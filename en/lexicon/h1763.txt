====== H1763: דְּחַל (dᵉchal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h2119|H2119]];

===== Meaning =====

to **slink**, i.e. (by implication) to **fear**, or (causatively) **be formidable**

===== Definitions =====

  - slink
  - fear
  - be formidable

===== Usage =====

make afraid, dreadful, fear, terrible.
