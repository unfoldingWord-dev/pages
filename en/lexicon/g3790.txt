====== G3790: ὀφρῦς (ophrŷs) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from [[en:lexicon:g3700|G3700]] (through the idea of the shading or proximity to the
 organ of vision);

===== Meaning =====

the eye-"brow" or forehead, i.e. (figuratively) the brink of a precipice

===== Usage =====

brow.
