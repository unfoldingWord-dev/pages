====== H4297: מֻטֶּה (muṭṭeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5186|H5186]];

===== Meaning =====

a **stretching**, i.e. **distortion** (figuratively, iniquity)

===== Definitions =====

  - stretching
  - distortion

===== Usage =====

perverseness.
