====== G1735: ἐνδέχεται (endéchetai) ======

===== Source =====

third person singular present of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g1209|G1209]];

===== Meaning =====

(impersonally) it is accepted in, i.e. admitted (possible)

===== Usage =====

can (+ not) be.
