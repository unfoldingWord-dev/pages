====== H6694: צוּק (tsûwq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of **narrowness** (of orifice));

===== Meaning =====

to **pour** out, i.e. (figuratively) **smelt**, **utter**

===== Definitions =====

  - narrowness
  - pour
  - smelt
  - utter

===== Usage =====

be molten, pour.
