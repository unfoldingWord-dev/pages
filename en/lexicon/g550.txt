====== G550: ἀπειπόμην (apeipómēn) ======

===== Source =====

reflexive past of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g2036|G2036]];

===== Meaning =====

to say off for oneself, i.e. disown

===== Usage =====
