====== H5327: נָצָה (nâtsâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **go forth**, i.e. (by implication) to **be expelled**, and (consequently) **desolate**; causatively, to **lay waste**; also (specifically), to **quarrel**

===== Definitions =====

  - go forth
  - be expelled
  - desolate
  - lay waste
  - quarrel

===== Usage =====

be laid waste, runinous, strive (together).
