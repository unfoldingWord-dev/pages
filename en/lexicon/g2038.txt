====== G2038: ἐργάζομαι (ergázomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g2041|G2041]];

===== Meaning =====

to toil (as a task, occupation, etc.), (by implication) effect, be engaged in or with, etc.

===== Usage =====

commit, do, labor for, minister about, trade (by), work.
