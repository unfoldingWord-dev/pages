====== G2104: εὐγενής (eugenḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and [[en:lexicon:g1096|G1096]];

===== Meaning =====

well born, i.e. (literally) high in rank, or (figuratively) generous

===== Usage =====

more noble, nobleman.
