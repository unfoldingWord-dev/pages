====== G4255: προαιρέομαι (proairéomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g138|G138]];

===== Meaning =====

to choose for oneself before another thing (prefer), i.e. (by implication) to propose (intend)

===== Usage =====

purpose.
