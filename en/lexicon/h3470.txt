====== H3470: יְשַׁעְיָה (Yᵉshaʻyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h3467|H3467]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; **Jah has saved**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jeshajah**, the name of seven Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has saved
  - Jeshajah

===== Usage =====

Isaiah, Jesaiah, Jeshaiah.
