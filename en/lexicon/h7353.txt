====== H7353: רָחֵל (râchêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **journey**;

===== Meaning =====

a **ewe** [the **females** being the predominant element of a flock] (as a good **traveller**)

===== Definitions =====

  - journey
  - ewe
  - females
  - traveller

===== Usage =====

ewe, sheep.
