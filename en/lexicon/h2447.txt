====== H2447: חַכְלִיל (chaklîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective - masculine

===== Source =====

by reduplication from an unused root apparently meaning to be dark;

===== Meaning =====

**darkly flashing** (only of the eyes); in a good sense, **brilliant** (as stimulated by wine)

===== Definitions =====

  - darkly flashing
  - brilliant

===== Usage =====

red.
