====== H8664: תִּשְׁבִּי (Tishbîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun

===== Source =====

patrial from an unused name meaning recourse;

===== Meaning =====

a **Tishbite** or inhabitant of Tishbeh (in Gilead)

===== Definitions =====

  - Tishbite

===== Usage =====

Tishbite.
