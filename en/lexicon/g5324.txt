====== G5324: φαντάζω (phantázō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]];

===== Meaning =====

to make apparent, i.e. (passively) to appear (neuter participle as noun, a spectacle)

===== Usage =====

sight.
