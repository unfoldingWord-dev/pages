====== H6895: קָבַב (qâbab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **scoop** out, i.e. (figuratively) to **malign** or **execrate** (i.e. **stab** with words)

===== Definitions =====

  - scoop
  - malign
  - execrate
  - stab

===== Usage =====

× at all, curse.
