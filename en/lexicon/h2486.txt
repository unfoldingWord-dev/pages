====== H2486: חָלִילָה (châlîylâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

interjection

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; a directive from [[en:lexicon:h2490|H2490]];

===== Meaning =====

literal for a **profaned** thing; used (interj.) **far be it!**

===== Definitions =====

  - profaned
  - far be it!

===== Usage =====

be far, (× God) forbid.
