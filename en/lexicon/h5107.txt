====== H5107: נוּב (nûwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **germinate**, i.e. (figuratively) to (causatively, **make**) **flourish**; also (of words), to **utter**

===== Definitions =====

  - germinate
  - make
  - flourish
  - utter

===== Usage =====

bring forth (fruit), make cheerful, increase.
