====== H217: אוּר (ʼûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h215|H215]];

===== Meaning =====

**flame**; hence (in the plural) **the East** (as being the region of light)

===== Definitions =====

  - flame
  - the East

===== Usage =====

fire, light. See also
