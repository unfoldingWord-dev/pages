====== G2623: κατακλείω (katakleíō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g2808|G2808]];

===== Meaning =====

to shut down (in a dungeon), i.e. incarcerate

===== Usage =====
