====== H7751: שׁוּט (shûwṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **push forth**; (but used only figuratively) to **lash**, i.e. (the sea with oars) to **row**; by implication, to **travel**

===== Definitions =====

  - push forth
  - lash
  - row
  - travel

===== Usage =====

go (about, through, to and fro), mariner, rower, run to and fro.
