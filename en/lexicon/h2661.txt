====== H2661: חֲפֹר (chăphôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2658|H2658]]; a **hole**; only in connection with [[en:lexicon:h6512|H6512]], which ought rather to be joined as one word, thus [[en:lexicon:|]]; by reduplication from [[en:lexicon:h2658|H2658]];

===== Meaning =====

a **burrower**, i.e. probably a **rat**

===== Definitions =====

  - hole
  - burrower
  - rat

===== Usage =====

  mole.
