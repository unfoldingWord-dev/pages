====== H8401: תֶּבֶן (teben) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:h1129|H1129]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **material**, i.e. (specifically) **refuse haum** or **stalks of grain** (as chopped in threshing and used for fodder)

===== Definitions =====

  - material
  - refuse haum
  - stalks of grain

===== Usage =====

chaff, straw, stubble.
