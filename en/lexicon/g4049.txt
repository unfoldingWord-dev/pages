====== G4049: περισπάω (perispáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g4685|G4685]];

===== Meaning =====

to drag all around, i.e. (figuratively) to distract (with care)

===== Usage =====

cumber.
