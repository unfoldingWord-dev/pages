====== H3644: כְּמוֹ (kᵉmôw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

particle

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; a form of the prefix **k**, but used separately [compare [[en:lexicon:h3651|H3651]]];

===== Meaning =====

**as**, **thus**, **so**

===== Definitions =====

  - k
  - as
  - thus
  - so

===== Usage =====

according to, (such) as (it were, well as), in comparison of, like (as, to, unto), thus, when, worth.
