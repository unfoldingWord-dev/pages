====== H2781: חֶרְפָּה (cherpâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2778|H2778]];

===== Meaning =====

**contumely**, **disgrace**, the **pudenda**

===== Definitions =====

  - contumely
  - disgrace
  - pudenda

===== Usage =====

rebuke, reproach(-fully), shame.
