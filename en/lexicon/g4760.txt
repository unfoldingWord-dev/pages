====== G4760: στρατόπεδον (stratópedon) ======

===== Source =====

from the base of [[en:lexicon:g4756|G4756]] and the same as [[en:lexicon:g3977|G3977]];

===== Meaning =====

a camping-ground, i.e. (by implication) a body of troops

===== Usage =====

army.
