====== H8458: תַּחְבֻּלָה (tachbulâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h2254|H2254]] as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h2256|H2256]];

===== Meaning =====

(only in plural) properly, **steerage** (as a management of ropes), i.e. (figuratively) **guidance** or (by implication) a **plan**

===== Definitions =====

  - steerage
  - guidance
  - plan

===== Usage =====

good advice, (wise) counsels.
