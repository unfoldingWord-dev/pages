====== H1244: בִּקֹּרֶת (biqqôreth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1239|H1239]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **examination**, i.e. (by implication) **punishment**

===== Definitions =====

  - examination
  - punishment

===== Usage =====

scourged.
