====== G3961: πατέω (patéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a derivative probably of [[en:lexicon:g3817|G3817]] (meaning a "path");

===== Meaning =====

to trample (literally or figuratively)

===== Usage =====

tread (down, under foot).
