====== G5561: χώρα (chṓra) ======

===== Source =====

feminine of a derivative of the base of [[en:lexicon:g5490|G5490]] through the idea of empty
 expanse;

===== Meaning =====

room, i.e. a space of territory (more or less extensive; often including its inhabitants)

===== Usage =====

coast, county, fields, ground, land, region.
