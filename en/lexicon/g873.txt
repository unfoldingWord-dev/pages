====== G873: ἀφορίζω (aphorízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g3724|G3724]];

===== Meaning =====

to set off by boundary, i.e. (figuratively) limit, exclude, appoint, etc.

===== Usage =====

divide, separate, sever.
