====== G4518: σαβαχθάνι (sabachtháni) ======

===== Source =====

of Chaldee or ([[en:lexicon:h07662|H07662]] with pronominal suffix);

===== Meaning =====

thou hast left me; sabachthani (i.e. shebakthani), a cry of distress

===== Usage =====

sabachthani.
