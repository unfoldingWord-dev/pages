====== G5147: τρίβος (tríbos) ======

===== Source =====

from τρίβω (tríbō) (to "rub"; akin to τείρω (teírō), τρύω (trýō), and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5131|G5131]],
 [[en:lexicon:g5134|G5134]]);

===== Meaning =====

a rut or worn track
