====== H5983: עַמּוֹן (ʻAmmôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5971|H5971]]; tribal, i.e. inbred;

===== Meaning =====

**Ammon**, a son of Lot; also his posterity and their country

===== Definitions =====

  - Ammon

===== Usage =====

Ammon, Ammonites.
