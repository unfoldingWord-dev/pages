====== H870: אֲתַר (ʼăthar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to that of [[en:lexicon:h871|H871]];

===== Meaning =====

a **place**; (adverb) **after**

===== Definitions =====

  - place
  - after

===== Usage =====

after, place.
