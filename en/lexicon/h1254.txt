====== H1254: בָּרָא (bârâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

(absolutely) to **create**; (qualified) to **cut down** (a wood), **select**, **feed** (as formative processes)

===== Definitions =====

  - create
  - cut down
  - select
  - feed

===== Usage =====

choose, create (creator), cut down, dispatch, do, make (fat).
