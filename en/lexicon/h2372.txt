====== H2372: חָזָה (châzâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **gaze** at; mentally to **perceive**, **contemplate** (with pleasure); specifically, to **have a vision of**

===== Definitions =====

  - gaze
  - perceive
  - contemplate
  - have a vision of

===== Usage =====

behold, look, prophesy, provide, see.
