====== G4033: περικυκλόω (perikyklóō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2944|G2944]];

===== Meaning =====

to encircle all around, i.e. blockade completely

===== Usage =====

compass round.
