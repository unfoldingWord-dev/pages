====== G823: ἄτομος (átomos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5114|G5114]];

===== Meaning =====

uncut, i.e. (by implication) indivisible (an "atom" of time)

===== Usage =====

 moment.
