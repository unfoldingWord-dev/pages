====== G430: ἀνέχομαι (anéchomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g303|G303]] and [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

to hold oneself up against, i.e. (figuratively) put up with

===== Usage =====

bear with, endure, forbear, suffer.
