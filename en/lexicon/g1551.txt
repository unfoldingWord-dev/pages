====== G1551: ἐκδέχομαι (ekdéchomai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g1209|G1209]];

===== Meaning =====

to accept from some source, i.e. (by implication) to await

===== Usage =====

expect, look (tarry) for, wait (for).
