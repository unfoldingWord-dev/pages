====== H7554: רָקַע (râqaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pound** the earth (as a sign of passion); by analogy to **expand** (by hammering); by implication, to **overlay** (with thin sheets of metal)

===== Definitions =====

  - pound
  - expand
  - overlay

===== Usage =====

beat, make broad, spread abroad (forth, over, out, into plates), stamp, stretch.
