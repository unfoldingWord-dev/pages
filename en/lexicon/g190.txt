====== G190: ἀκολουθέω (akolouthéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a particle of union) and κέλευθος (kéleuthos) (a road);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to be in the same way with, i.e. to accompany (specially, as a disciple)

===== Usage =====

follow, reach.
