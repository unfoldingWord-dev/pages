====== G1863: ἐπάγω (epágō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g71|G71]];

===== Meaning =====

to superinduce, i.e. inflict (an evil), charge (a crime)

===== Usage =====

bring upon.
