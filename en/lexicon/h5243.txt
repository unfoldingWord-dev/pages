====== H5243: נָמַל (nâmal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **become clipped** or (specifically) **circumcised**

===== Definitions =====

  - become clipped
  - circumcised

===== Usage =====

(branch to) be cut down (off), circumcise.
