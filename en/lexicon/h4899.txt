====== H4899: מָשִׁיחַ (mâshîyach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4886|H4886]];

===== Meaning =====

**anointed**; usually a **consecrated** person (as a king, priest, or saint); specifically, the **Messiah**

===== Definitions =====

  - anointed
  - consecrated
  - Messiah

===== Usage =====

anointed, Messiah.
