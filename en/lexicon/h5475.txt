====== H5475: סוֹד (çôwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3245|H3245]];

===== Meaning =====

a **session**, i.e. **company** of persons (in close deliberation); by implication, **intimacy**, **consultation**, a **secret**

===== Definitions =====

  - session
  - company
  - intimacy
  - consultation
  - secret

===== Usage =====

assembly, consel, inward, secret (counsel).
