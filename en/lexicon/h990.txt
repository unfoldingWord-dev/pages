====== H990: בֶּטֶן (beṭen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to be hollow;

===== Meaning =====

the **belly**, especially the **womb**; also the **bosom** or **body** of anything

===== Definitions =====

  - belly
  - womb
  - bosom
  - body

===== Usage =====

belly, body,   as they be born,   within, womb.
