====== G4889: σύνδουλος (sýndoulos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g1401|G1401]];

===== Meaning =====

a co-slave, i.e. servitor or ministrant of the same master (human or divine)

===== Usage =====

fellowservant.
