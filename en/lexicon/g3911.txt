====== G3911: παραφέρω (paraphérō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g5342|G5342]] (including its alternate forms);

===== Meaning =====

to bear along or aside, i.e. carry off (literally or figuratively); by implication, to avert

===== Usage =====

remove, take away.
