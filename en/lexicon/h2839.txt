====== H2839: חִשֻּׁק (chishshuq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2836|H2836]];

===== Meaning =====

**conjoined**, i.e. a **wheel-spoke** or rod connecting the hub with the rim

===== Definitions =====

  - conjoined
  - wheel-spoke

===== Usage =====

felloe.
