====== H8534: תַּלְתַּל (taltal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

by reduplication, from [[en:lexicon:h8524|H8524]] through the idea of vibration;

===== Meaning =====

a **trailing bough** (as pendulous)

===== Definitions =====

  - trailing bough

===== Usage =====

bushy.
