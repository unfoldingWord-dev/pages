====== G5346: φημί (phēmí) ======

===== Source =====

properly, the same as the base of [[en:lexicon:g5457|G5457]] and [[en:lexicon:g5316|G5316]];

===== Meaning =====

to show or make known one's thoughts, i.e. speak or say

===== Usage =====

affirm, say.
