====== H123: אֱדֹם (ʼĔdôm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (fully) [[en:lexicon:|]] ; from [[en:lexicon:h122|H122]]; **red** (see Genesis 25:25);

===== Meaning =====

**Edom**, the elder twin-brother of Jacob; hence the region (Idumaea) occupied by him

===== Definitions =====

  - red
  - Edom

===== Usage =====

Edom, Edomites, Idumea.
