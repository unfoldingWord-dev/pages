====== H1534: גַּלְגַּל (galgal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

by reduplication from [[en:lexicon:h1556|H1556]];

===== Meaning =====

a **wheel**; by analogy, a **whirlwind**; also **dust** (as **whirled**)

===== Definitions =====

  - wheel
  - whirlwind
  - dust
  - whirled

===== Usage =====

heaven, rolling thing, wheel.
