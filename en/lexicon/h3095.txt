====== H3095: יַהֲלֹם (yahălôm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1986|H1986]] (in the sense of **hardness**);

===== Meaning =====

a precious stone, probably **onyx**

===== Definitions =====

  - hardness
  - onyx

===== Usage =====

diamond.
