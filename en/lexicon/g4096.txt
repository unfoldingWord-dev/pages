====== G4096: πιότης (piótēs) ======

===== Source =====

from πίων (píōn) (fat; perhaps akin to the alternate of [[en:lexicon:g4095|G4095]] through the idea
 of repletion);

===== Meaning =====

plumpness, i.e. (by implication) richness (oiliness)

===== Usage =====

fatness.
