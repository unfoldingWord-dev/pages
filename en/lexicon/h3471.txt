====== H3471: יָשְׁפֵה (yâshᵉphêh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **polish**;

===== Meaning =====

a gem supposed to be **jasper** (from the resemblance in name)

===== Definitions =====

  - polish
  - jasper

===== Usage =====

jasper.
