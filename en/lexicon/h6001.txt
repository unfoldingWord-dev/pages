====== H6001: עָמֵל (ʻâmêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculineadjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5998|H5998]];

===== Meaning =====

**toiling**; concretely, a **laborer**; figuratively, **sorrowful**

===== Definitions =====

  - toiling
  - laborer
  - sorrowful

===== Usage =====

that laboureth, that is a misery, had taken (labour), wicked, workman.
