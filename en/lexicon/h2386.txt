====== H2386: חֲזִיר (chăzîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to **inclose**;

===== Meaning =====

a **hog** (perhaps as **penned**)

===== Definitions =====

  - inclose
  - hog
  - penned

===== Usage =====

boar, swine.
