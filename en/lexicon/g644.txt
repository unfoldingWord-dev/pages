====== G644: ἀποσκίασμα (aposkíasma) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4639|G4639]];

===== Meaning =====

a shading off, i.e. obscuration

===== Usage =====
