====== G5516: χξϛ (chx stigma) ======

===== Source =====

the 22nd, 14th and an obsolete letter ([[en:lexicon:g4742|G4742]] as a cross) of the Greek
 alphabet (intermediate between the 5th and 6th), used as numbers;
 denoting respectively 600, 60 and 6;

===== Meaning =====

666 as a numeral

===== Usage =====

six hundred threescore and six.
