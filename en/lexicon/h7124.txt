====== H7124: קֹרֵא (qôrêʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

properly, active participle of [[en:lexicon:h7121|H7121]];

===== Meaning =====

a **caller**, i.e. **partridge** (from its cry)

===== Definitions =====

  - caller
  - partridge

===== Usage =====

partridge. See also
