====== H6026: עָנַג (ʻânag) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to be **soft** or pliable, i.e. (figuratively) **effeminate** or luxurious

===== Definitions =====

  - soft
  - effeminate

===== Usage =====

delicate(-ness), (have) delight (self), sport self.
