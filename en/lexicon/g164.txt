====== G164: αἰχμάλωτος (aichmálōtos) ======

===== Source =====

from αἰχμή (aichmḗ) (a spear) and a derivative of the same as [[en:lexicon:g259|G259]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a prisoner of war, i.e. (genitive case) a captive

===== Usage =====

captive.
