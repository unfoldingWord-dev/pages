====== H1241: בָּקָר (bâqâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1239|H1239]];

===== Meaning =====

a **beeve** or an animal of the ox family of either gender (as used for **plowing**); collectively, a **herd**

===== Definitions =====

  - beeve
  - plowing
  - herd

===== Usage =====

beeve, bull (  -ock),   calf,   cow, great (cattle),   heifer, herd, kine, ox.
