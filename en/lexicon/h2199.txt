====== H2199: זָעַק (zâʻaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **shriek** (from anguish or danger); by analogy, (as a herald) to **announce** or **convene** publicly

===== Definitions =====

  - shriek
  - announce
  - convene

===== Usage =====

assemble, call (together), (make a) cry (out), come with such a company, gather (together), cause to be proclaimed.
