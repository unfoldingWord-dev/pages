====== H4022: מֶגֶד (meged) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to be eminent;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **distinguished thing**; hence something **valuable**, as a product or fruit

===== Definitions =====

  - distinguished thing
  - valuable

===== Usage =====

pleasant, precious fruit (thing).
