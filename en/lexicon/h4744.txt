====== H4744: מִקְרָא (miqrâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7121|H7121]];

===== Meaning =====

something **called** out, i.e. a public **meeting** (the act, the persons, or the place); also a **rehearsal**

===== Definitions =====

  - called
  - meeting
  - rehearsal

===== Usage =====

assembly, calling, convocation, reading.
