====== G3796: ὀψέ (opsé) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g3694|G3694]] (through the idea of backwardness);

===== Meaning =====

(adverbially) late in the day; by extension, after the close of the day

===== Usage =====

(at) even, in the end.
