====== H1986: הָלַם (hâlam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **strike** down; by implication, to **hammer**, **stamp**, **conquer**, **disband**

===== Definitions =====

  - strike
  - hammer
  - stamp
  - conquer
  - disband

===== Usage =====

beat (down), break (down), overcome, smite (with the hammer).
