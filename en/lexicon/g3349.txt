====== G3349: μετεωρίζω (meteōrízō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3326|G3326]] and a collateral form of [[en:lexicon:g142|G142]] or perhaps rather
 [[en:lexicon:g109|G109]] (compare "meteor");

===== Meaning =====

to raise in mid-air, i.e. (figuratively) suspend (passively, fluctuate or be anxious)

===== Usage =====

be of doubtful mind.
