====== G1271: διάνοια (diánoia) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and [[en:lexicon:g3563|G3563]];

===== Meaning =====

deep thought, properly, the faculty (mind or its disposition), by implication, its exercise

===== Usage =====

 imagination, mind, understanding.
