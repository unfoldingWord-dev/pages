====== G3619: οἰκοδομή (oikodomḗ) ======

===== Source =====

feminine (abstract) of a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3624|G3624]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g1430|G1430]];

===== Meaning =====

architecture, i.e. (concretely) a structure; figuratively, confirmation

===== Usage =====

building, edify(-ication, -ing).
