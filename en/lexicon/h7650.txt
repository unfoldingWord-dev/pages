====== H7650: שָׁבַע (shâbaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly to **be complete**, but used only as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7651|H7651]];

===== Meaning =====

to **seven** oneself, i.e. **swear** (as if by repeating a declaration seven times)

===== Definitions =====

  - be complete
  - seven
  - swear
