====== H1550: גָּלִיל (gâlîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1556|H1556]];

===== Meaning =====

a **valve of a folding door** (as turning); also a **ring** (as round)

===== Definitions =====

  - valve of a folding door
  - ring

===== Usage =====

folding, ring.
