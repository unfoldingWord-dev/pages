====== H7553: רִקְמָה (riqmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7551|H7551]];

===== Meaning =====

**variegation of color**; specifically, **embroidery**

===== Definitions =====

  - variegation of color
  - embroidery

===== Usage =====

broidered (work), divers colours, (raiment of) needlework (on both sides).
