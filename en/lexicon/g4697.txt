====== G4697: σπλαγχνίζομαι (splanchnízomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4698|G4698]];

===== Meaning =====

to have the bowels yearn, i.e. (figuratively) feel sympathy, to pity

===== Usage =====

have (be moved with) compassion.
