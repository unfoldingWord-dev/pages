====== G1208: δεύτερος (deúteros) ======

===== Source =====

as the comparative of [[en:lexicon:g1417|G1417]];

===== Meaning =====

(ordinal) second (in time, place, or rank; also adverb)

===== Usage =====

afterward, again, second(-arily, time).
