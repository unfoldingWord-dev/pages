====== H3548: כֹּהֵן (kôhên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

active participle of [[en:lexicon:h3547|H3547]];

===== Meaning =====

literally one **officiating**, a **priest**; also (by courtesy) an **acting priest** (although a layman)

===== Definitions =====

  - officiating
  - priest
  - acting priest

===== Usage =====

chief ruler, × own, priest, prince, principal officer.
