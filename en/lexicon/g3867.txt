====== G3867: παραινέω (parainéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g134|G134]];

===== Meaning =====

to mispraise, i.e. recommend or advise (a different course)

===== Usage =====

admonish, exhort.
