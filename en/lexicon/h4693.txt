====== H4693: מָצוֹר (mâtsôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h4692|H4692]] in the sense of a **limit**;

===== Meaning =====

**Egypt** (as the **border** of Palestine)

===== Definitions =====

  - limit
  - Egypt
  - border

===== Usage =====

besieged places, defense, fortified.
