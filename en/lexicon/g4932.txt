====== G4932: συντέμνω (syntémnō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5114|G5114]];

===== Meaning =====

to contract by cutting, i.e. (figuratively) do concisely (speedily)

===== Usage =====

(cut) short.
