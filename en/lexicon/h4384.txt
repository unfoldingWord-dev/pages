====== H4384: מַכְשֵׁלָה (makshêlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine from [[en:lexicon:h3782|H3782]];

===== Meaning =====

a **stumbling-block**, but only figuratively (**fall**, **enticement** [idol])

===== Definitions =====

  - stumbling-block
  - fall
  - enticement

===== Usage =====

ruin, stumbling-block.
