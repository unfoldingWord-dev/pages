====== H1270: בַּרְזֶל (barzel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

perhaps from the root of [[en:lexicon:h1269|H1269]];

===== Meaning =====

**iron** (as cutting); by extension, an **iron implement**

===== Definitions =====

  - iron
  - iron implement

===== Usage =====

(ax) head, iron.
