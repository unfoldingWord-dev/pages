====== H4599: מַעְיָן (maʻyân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; (Psalm 114:8), or (feminine) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h5869|H5869]] (as a denominative in the sense of a spring);

===== Meaning =====

a **fountain** (also collectively), figuratively, a **source** (of satisfaction)

===== Definitions =====

  - fountain
  - source

===== Usage =====

fountain, spring, well.
