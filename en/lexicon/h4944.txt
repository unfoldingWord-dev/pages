====== H4944: מַשָּׁק (mashshâq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8264|H8264]];

===== Meaning =====

a **traversing**, i.e. **rapid motion**

===== Definitions =====

  - traversing
  - rapid motion

===== Usage =====

running to and fro.
