====== G40: ἅγιος (hágios) ======

===== Source =====

from ἅγος (hágos) (an awful thing) (compare [[en:lexicon:g53|G53]], [[en:lexicon:g2282|G2282]]);

===== Meaning =====

sacred (physically, pure, morally blameless or religious, ceremonially, consecrated)

===== Usage =====

(most) holy (one, thing), saint.
