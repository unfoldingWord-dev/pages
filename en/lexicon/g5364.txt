====== G5364: φιλανθρώπως (philanthrṓpōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5384|G5384]] and [[en:lexicon:g444|G444]];

===== Meaning =====

fondly to man ("philanthropically"), i.e. humanely

===== Usage =====

courteously.
