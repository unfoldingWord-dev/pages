====== H724: אֲרוּכָה (ʼărûwkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; feminine passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h748|H748]] (in the sense of **restoring** to soundness);

===== Meaning =====

**wholeness** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - restoring
  - wholeness

===== Usage =====

health, made up, perfected.
