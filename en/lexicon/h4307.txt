====== H4307: מַטָּרָא (maṭṭârâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h5201|H5201]];

===== Meaning =====

a **jail** (as a guard-house); also an **aim** (as being closely watched)

===== Definitions =====

  - jail
  - aim

===== Usage =====

mark, prison.
