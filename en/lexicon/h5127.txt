====== H5127: נוּס (nûwç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **flit**, i.e. **vanish** away (subside, escape; causatively, chase, impel, deliver)

===== Definitions =====

  - flit
  - vanish

===== Usage =====

× abate, away, be displayed, (make to) flee (away, -ing), put to flight, × hide, lift up a standard.
