====== H4133: מוֹטָה (môwṭâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h4132|H4132]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pole**; by implication, an ox-**bow**; hence, a **yoke** (either literal or figurative)

===== Definitions =====

  - pole
  - bow
  - yoke

===== Usage =====

bands, heavy, staves, yoke.
