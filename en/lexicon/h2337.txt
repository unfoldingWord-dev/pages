====== H2337: חָוָח (châvâch) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

perhaps the same as [[en:lexicon:h2336|H2336]];

===== Meaning =====

a **dell** or **crevice** (as if pierced in the earth)

===== Definitions =====

  - dell
  - crevice

===== Usage =====

thicket.
