====== H6544: פָּרַע (pâraʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **loosen**; by implication, to **expose**, **dismiss**; figuratively, **absolve**, **begin**

===== Definitions =====

  - loosen
  - expose
  - dismiss
  - absolve
  - begin

===== Usage =====

avenge, avoid, bare, go back, let, (make) naked, set at nought, perish, refuse, uncover.
