====== G857: ἀφειδία (apheidía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5339|G5339]];

===== Meaning =====

unsparingness, i.e. austerity (asceticism)

===== Usage =====

neglecting.
