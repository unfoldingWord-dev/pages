====== G1310: διαφημίζω (diaphēmízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1223|G1223]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5345|G5345]];

===== Meaning =====

to report thoroughly, i.e. divulgate

===== Usage =====

blaze abroad, commonly report, spread abroad, fame.
