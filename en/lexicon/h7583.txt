====== H7583: שָׁאָה (shâʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of whirling to giddiness);

===== Meaning =====

to **stun**, i.e. (intransitively) **be astonished**

===== Definitions =====

  - stun
  - be astonished

===== Usage =====

wonder.
