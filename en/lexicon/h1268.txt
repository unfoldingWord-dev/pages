====== H1268: בֵּרוֹתָה (Bêrôwthâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; probably from [[en:lexicon:h1266|H1266]]; cypress or cypresslike;

===== Meaning =====

**Berothah** or **Berothai**, a place north of Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Berothah
  - Berothai

===== Usage =====

Berothah, Berothai.
