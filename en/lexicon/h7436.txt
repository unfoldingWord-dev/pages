====== H7436: רָמָתַיִם צוֹפִים (Râmâthayim Tsôwphîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the dual of [[en:lexicon:h7413|H7413]] and the plural of the active participle of [[en:lexicon:h6822|H6822]]; double height of watchers;

===== Meaning =====

**Ramathajim-Tsophim**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Ramathajim-Tsophim

===== Usage =====

Ramathaimzophim.
