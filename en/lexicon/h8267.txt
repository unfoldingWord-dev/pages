====== H8267: שֶׁקֶר (sheqer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8266|H8266]];

===== Meaning =====

an **untruth**; by implication, a **sham** (often adverbial)

===== Definitions =====

  - untruth
  - sham

===== Usage =====

without a cause, deceit(-ful), false(-hood, -ly), feignedly, liar,   lie, lying, vain (thing), wrongfully.
