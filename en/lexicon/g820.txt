====== G820: ἄτιμος (átimos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[en:lexicon:g5092|G5092]];

===== Meaning =====

(negatively) unhonoured or (positively) dishonoured

===== Usage =====

despised, without honour, less honourable (comparative degree).
