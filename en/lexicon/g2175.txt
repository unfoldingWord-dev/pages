====== G2175: εὐωδία (euōdía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3605|G3605]];

===== Meaning =====

good-scentedness, i.e. fragrance

===== Usage =====
