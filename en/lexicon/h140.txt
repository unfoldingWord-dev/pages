====== H140: אֲדֹנִיקָם (ʼĂdônîyqâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h113|H113]] and [[en:lexicon:h6965|H6965]]; lord of rising (i.e. high);

===== Meaning =====

**Adonikam**, the name of one or two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Adonikam

===== Usage =====

Adonikam.
