====== G2008: ἐπιτιμάω (epitimáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g5091|G5091]];

===== Meaning =====

to tax upon, i.e. censure or admonish; by implication, forbid

===== Usage =====

(straitly) charge, rebuke.
