====== H5669: עָבוּר (ʻâbûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h5668|H5668]];

===== Meaning =====

**passed**, i.e. **kept over**; used only of **stored grain**

===== Definitions =====

  - passed
  - kept over
  - stored grain

===== Usage =====

old corn.
