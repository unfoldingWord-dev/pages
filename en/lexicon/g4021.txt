====== G4021: περίεργος (períergos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g2041|G2041]];

===== Meaning =====

working all around, i.e. officious (meddlesome, neuter plural magic)

===== Usage =====

busybody, curious arts.
