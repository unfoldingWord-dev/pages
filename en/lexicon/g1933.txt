====== G1933: ἐπιεικής (epieikḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g1503|G1503]];

===== Meaning =====

appropriate, i.e. (by implication) mild

===== Usage =====

gentle, moderation, patient.
