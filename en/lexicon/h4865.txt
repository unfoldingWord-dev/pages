====== H4865: מִשְׁבְּצָה (mishbᵉtsâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7660|H7660]];

===== Meaning =====

a **brocade**; by analogy, a (reticulated) **setting** of a gem

===== Definitions =====

  - brocade
  - setting

===== Usage =====

ouch, wrought.
