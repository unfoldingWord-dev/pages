====== H49: אֲבִישַׁג (ʼĂbîyshag) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h7686|H7686]]; father of error (i.e. blundering);

===== Meaning =====

**Abishag**, a concubine of David

===== Definitions =====

  - Abishag

===== Usage =====

Abishag.
