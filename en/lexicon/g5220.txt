====== G5220: ὕπανδρος (hýpandros) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g435|G435]];

===== Meaning =====

in subjection under a man, i.e. a married woman

===== Usage =====

which hath an husband.
