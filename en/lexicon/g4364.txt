====== G4364: προσποιέομαι (prospoiéomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g4160|G4160]];

===== Meaning =====

to do forward for oneself, i.e. pretend (as if about to do a thing)

===== Usage =====

make as though.
