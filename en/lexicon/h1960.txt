====== H1960: הֻיְּדָה (huyᵉdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h1959|H1959]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, an **acclaim**, i.e. a **choir of singers**

===== Definitions =====

  - acclaim
  - choir of singers

===== Usage =====

thanksgiving.
