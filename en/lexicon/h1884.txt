====== H1884: דְּתָבָר (dᵉthâbâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) of Persian origin;

===== Meaning =====

**meaning one skilled in law**; a **judge**

===== Definitions =====

  - meaning one skilled in law
  - judge

===== Usage =====

counsellor.
