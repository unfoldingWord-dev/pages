====== G2745: καύχημα (kaúchēma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2744|G2744]];

===== Meaning =====

a boast (properly, the object; by implication, the act) in a good or a bad sense

===== Usage =====

boasting, (whereof) to glory (of), glorying, rejoice(-ing).
