====== G3081: λυσιτελεῖ (lysiteleî) ======

===== Source =====

third person singular present indicative active of a derivative of a
 compound of [[en:lexicon:g3080|G3080]] and [[en:lexicon:g5056|G5056]];

===== Meaning =====

impersonally, it answers the purpose, i.e. is advantageous

===== Usage =====

it is better.
