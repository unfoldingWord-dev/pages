====== H428: אֵלֶּה (ʼêl-leh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

demonstrative pronoun

===== Source =====

prolonged from [[en:lexicon:h411|H411]];

===== Meaning =====

**these** or **those**

===== Definitions =====

  - these
  - those

===== Usage =====

an-(the) other; one sort, so, some, such, them, these (same), they, this, those, thus, which, who(-m).
