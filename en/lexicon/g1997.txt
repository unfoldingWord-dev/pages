====== G1997: ἐπισυναγωγή (episynagōgḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1996|G1996]];

===== Meaning =====

a complete collection; especially a Christian meeting (for worship)

===== Usage =====

assembling (gathering) together.
