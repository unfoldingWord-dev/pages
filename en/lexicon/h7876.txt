====== H7876: שָׁיָה (shâyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **keep in memory**

===== Definitions =====

  - keep in memory

===== Usage =====

be unmindful. (Render Deuteronomy 32:18, 'A Rock bore thee, thou must recollect; and (yet) thou hast forgotten,' etc.)
