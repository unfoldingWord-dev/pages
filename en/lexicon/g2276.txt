====== G2276: ἥττον (hḗtton) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of comparative of ἧκα (hēka) (slightly) used for that of [[en:lexicon:g2556|G2556]];

===== Meaning =====

worse (as noun); by implication, less (as adverb)

===== Usage =====

less, worse.
