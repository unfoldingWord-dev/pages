====== H134: אֶדֶן (ʼeden) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h113|H113]] (in the sense of **strength**);

===== Meaning =====

a **basis** (of a building, a column, etc.)

===== Definitions =====

  - strength
  - basis

===== Usage =====

foundation, socket.
