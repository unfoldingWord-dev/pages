====== G891: ἄχρι (áchri) ======

===== Source =====

akin to [[en:lexicon:g206|G206]] (through the idea of a terminus);

===== Meaning =====

(of time) until or (of place) up to

===== Usage =====

as far as, for, in(-to), till, (even, un-)to, until, while.
