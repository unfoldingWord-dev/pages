====== H3326: יָצוּעַ (yâtsûwaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h3331|H3331]];

===== Meaning =====

**spread**, i.e. a **bed**; (architecture) an **extension**, i.e. **wing** or **lean-to** (a single story or collectively)

===== Definitions =====

  - spread
  - bed
  - extension
  - wing
  - lean-to

===== Usage =====

bed, chamber, couch.
