====== G5257: ὑπηρέτης (hypērétēs) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and a derivative of ἐρέσσω (eréssō) (to row);

===== Meaning =====

an under-oarsman, i.e. (generally) subordinate (assistant, sexton, constable)

===== Usage =====

minister, officer, servant.
