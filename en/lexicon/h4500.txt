====== H4500: מָנוֹר (mânôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5214|H5214]];

===== Meaning =====

a **yoke** (properly, for plowing), i.e. the **frame of a loom**

===== Definitions =====

  - yoke
  - frame of a loom

===== Usage =====

beam.
