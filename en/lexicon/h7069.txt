====== H7069: קָנָה (qânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **erect**, i.e. **create**; by extension, to **procure**, especially by purchase (causatively, **sell**); by implication to **own**

===== Definitions =====

  - erect
  - create
  - procure
  - sell
  - own

===== Usage =====

attain, buy(-er), teach to keep cattle, get, provoke to jealousy, possess(-or), purchase, recover, redeem, × surely, × verily.
