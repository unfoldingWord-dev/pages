====== H8156: שָׁסַע (shâçaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **split** or **tear**; figuratively, to **upbraid**

===== Definitions =====

  - split
  - tear
  - upbraid

===== Usage =====

cleave, (be) cloven (footed), rend, stay.
