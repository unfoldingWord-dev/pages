====== H2624: חֲסִידָה (chăçîydâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h2623|H2623]];

===== Meaning =====

the **kind** (maternal) **bird**, i.e. a **stork**

===== Definitions =====

  - kind
  - bird
  - stork

===== Usage =====

× feather, stork.
