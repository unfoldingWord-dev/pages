====== G2986: λαμπρός (lamprós) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g2985|G2985]];

===== Meaning =====

radiant; by analogy, limpid; figuratively, magnificent or sumptuous (in appearance)

===== Usage =====

bright, clear, gay, goodly, gorgeous, white.
