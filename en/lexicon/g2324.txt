====== G2324: θεράπων (therápōn) ======

===== Source =====

apparently a participle from an otherwise obsolete derivative of the
 base of [[en:lexicon:g2330|G2330]];

===== Meaning =====

a menial attendant (as if cherishing)

===== Usage =====

servant.
