====== G3625: οἰκουμένη (oikouménē) ======

===== Source =====

feminine participle present passive of [[en:lexicon:g3611|G3611]] (as noun, by implication,
 of [[en:lexicon:g1093|G1093]]);

===== Meaning =====

land, i.e. the (terrene part of the) globe; specially, the Roman empire

===== Usage =====

earth, world.
