====== G1956: ἐπιλύω (epilýō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g3089|G3089]];

===== Meaning =====

to solve further, i.e. (figuratively) to explain, decide

===== Usage =====

determine, expound.
