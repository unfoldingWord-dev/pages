====== H4009: מִבְטָח (mibṭâch) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h982|H982]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **refuge**, i.e. (objective) **security**, or (subjective) **assurance**

===== Definitions =====

  - refuge
  - security
  - assurance

===== Usage =====

confidence, hope, sure, trust.
