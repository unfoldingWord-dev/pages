====== H7431: רֶמֶשׂ (remes) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7430|H7430]];

===== Meaning =====

a **reptile** or any other rapidly moving animal

===== Definitions =====

  - reptile

===== Usage =====

that creepeth, creeping (moving) thing.
