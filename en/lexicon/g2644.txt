====== G2644: καταλλάσσω (katallássō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and [[en:lexicon:g236|G236]];

===== Meaning =====

to change mutually, i.e. (figuratively) to compound a difference

===== Usage =====

reconcile.
