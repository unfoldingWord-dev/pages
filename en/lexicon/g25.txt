====== G25: ἀγαπάω (agapáō) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from ἄγαν (ágan) (much) (or compare [[en:lexicon:h5689|H5689]]);

===== Meaning =====

to love (in a social or moral sense)

===== Usage =====

(be-)love(-ed).
