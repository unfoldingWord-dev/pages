====== H5676: עֵבֶר (ʻêber) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5674|H5674]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a region **across**; but used only adverbially (with or without a preposition) on the **opposite** side (especially of the Jordan; ususally meaning the **east**)

===== Definitions =====

  - across
  - opposite
  - east

===== Usage =====

× against, beyond, by, × from, over, passage, quarter, (other, this) side, straight.
