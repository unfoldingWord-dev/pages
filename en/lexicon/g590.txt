====== G590: ἀπόδημος (apódēmos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g1218|G1218]];

===== Meaning =====

absent from one's own people, i.e. a foreign traveller

===== Usage =====

taking a far journey.
