====== H6938: קֵדָר (Qêdâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6937|H6937]]; **dusky** (of the skin or the tent);

===== Meaning =====

**Kedar**, a son of Ishmael; also (collectively) Bedouin (as his descendants or representatives)

===== Definitions =====

  - dusky
  - Kedar

===== Usage =====

Kedar.
