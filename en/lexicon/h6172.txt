====== H6172: עֶרְוָה (ʻervâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6168|H6168]];

===== Meaning =====

**nudity**, literally (especially the pudenda) or figuratively (disgrace, blemish)

===== Definitions =====

  - nudity

===== Usage =====

nakedness, shame, unclean(-ness).
