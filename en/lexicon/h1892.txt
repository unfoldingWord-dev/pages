====== H1892: הֶבֶל (hebel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (rarely in the abs.) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1891|H1891]];

===== Meaning =====

**emptiness** or **vanity**; figuratively, something **transitory** and **unsatisfactory**; often used as an adverb

===== Definitions =====

  - emptiness
  - vanity
  - transitory
  - unsatisfactory

===== Usage =====

× altogether, vain, vanity.
