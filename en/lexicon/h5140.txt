====== H5140: נָזַל (nâzal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **drip**, or **shed** by trickling

===== Definitions =====

  - drip
  - shed

===== Usage =====

distil, drop, flood, (cause to) flow(-ing), gush out, melt, pour (down), running water, stream.
