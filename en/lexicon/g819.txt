====== G819: ἀτιμία (atimía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g820|G820]];

===== Meaning =====

infamy, i.e. (subjectively) comparative indignity, (objectively) disgrace

===== Usage =====

dishonour, reproach, shame, vile.
