====== H5031: נְבִיאָה (nᵉbîyʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h5030|H5030]];

===== Meaning =====

a **prophetess** or (generally) **inspired** woman; by implication, a **poetess**; by association a **prophet's wife**

===== Definitions =====

  - prophetess
  - inspired
  - poetess
  - prophet's wife

===== Usage =====

prophetess.
