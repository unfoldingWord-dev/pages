====== H3637: כָּלַם (kâlam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **wound**; but only figuratively, to **taunt** or **insult**

===== Definitions =====

  - wound
  - taunt
  - insult

===== Usage =====

be (make) ashamed, blush, be confounded, be put to confusion, hurt, reproach, (do, put to) shame.
