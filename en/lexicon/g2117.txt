====== G2117: εὐθύς (euthýs) ======

===== Source =====

perhaps from [[en:lexicon:g2095|G2095]] and [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

straight, i.e. (literally) level, or (figuratively) true; adverbially (of time) at once

===== Usage =====

anon, by and by, forthwith, immediately, straightway.
