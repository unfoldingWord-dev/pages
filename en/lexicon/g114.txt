====== G114: ἀθετέω (athetéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of
 [[en:lexicon:g5087|G5087]];

===== Meaning =====

to set aside, i.e. (by implication) to disesteem, neutralize or violate

===== Usage =====

cast off, despise, disannul, frustrate, bring to nought, reject.
