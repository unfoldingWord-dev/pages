====== H4719: מַקֵּדָה (Maqqêdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5348|H5348]] in the denominative sense of herding (compare [[en:lexicon:h5349|H5349]]); **fold**;

===== Meaning =====

**Makkedah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - fold
  - Makkedah

===== Usage =====

Makkedah.
