====== G1994: ἐπιστρέφω (epistréphō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g4762|G4762]];

===== Meaning =====

to revert (literally, figuratively or morally)

===== Usage =====

come (go) again, convert, (re-)turn (about, again).
