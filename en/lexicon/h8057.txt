====== H8057: שִׂמְחָה (simchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8056|H8056]];

===== Meaning =====

**blithesomeness** or **glee**, (religious or festival)

===== Definitions =====

  - blithesomeness
  - glee

===== Usage =====

× exceeding(-ly), gladness, joy(-fulness), mirth, pleasure, rejoice(-ing).
