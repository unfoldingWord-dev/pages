====== H4155: מוּעָף (mûwʻâph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5774|H5774]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **covered**, i.e. **dark**; abstractly, **obscurity**, i.e. **distress**

===== Definitions =====

  - covered
  - dark
  - obscurity
  - distress

===== Usage =====

dimness.
