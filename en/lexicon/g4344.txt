====== G4344: προσκεφάλαιον (proskephálaion) ======

===== Source =====

neuter of a presumed compound of [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g2776|G2776]];

===== Meaning =====

something for the head, i.e. a cushion

===== Usage =====
