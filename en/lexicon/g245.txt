====== G245: ἀλλότριος (allótrios) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g243|G243]];

===== Meaning =====

another's, i.e. not one's own; by extension foreign, not akin, hostile

===== Usage =====

alien, (an-)other (man's, men's), strange(-r).
