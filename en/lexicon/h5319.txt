====== H5319: נַפְתּוּל (naphtûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6617|H6617]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **wrestled**; but used (in the plural) transitively, a **struggle**

===== Definitions =====

  - wrestled
  - struggle

===== Usage =====

wrestling.
