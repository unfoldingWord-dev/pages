====== G4396: προφήτης (prophḗtēs) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g5346|G5346]];

===== Meaning =====

a foreteller ("prophet"); by analogy, an inspired speaker; by extension, a poet

===== Usage =====

prophet.
