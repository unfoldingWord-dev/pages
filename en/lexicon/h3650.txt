====== H3650: כִּמְרִיר (kimrîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

redupl. from [[en:lexicon:h3648|H3648]];

===== Meaning =====

**obscuration** (as if from shrinkage of light), i.e. an eclipse (only in plural)

===== Definitions =====

  - obscuration

===== Usage =====

blackness.
