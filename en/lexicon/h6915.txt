====== H6915: קָדַד (qâdad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **shrivel up**, i.e. **contract** or **bend the body** (or neck) **in deference**

===== Definitions =====

  - shrivel up
  - contract
  - bend the body
  - in deference

===== Usage =====

bow (down) (the) head, stoop.
