====== H3307: יָפֵחַ (yâphêach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3306|H3306]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **puffing**, i.e. (figuratively) **meditating**

===== Definitions =====

  - puffing
  - meditating

===== Usage =====

such as breathe out.
