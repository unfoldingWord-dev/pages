====== G4769: στῦλος (stŷlos) ======

===== Source =====

from στύω (stýō) (to stiffen; properly akin to the base of [[en:lexicon:g2476|G2476]]);

===== Meaning =====

a post ("style"), i.e. (figuratively) support

===== Usage =====

pillar.
