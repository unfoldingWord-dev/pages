====== G475: ἀντιδιατίθεμαι (antidiatíthemai) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g473|G473]] and [[en:lexicon:g1303|G1303]];

===== Meaning =====

to set oneself opposite, i.e. be  disputatious

===== Usage =====

that oppose themselves.
