====== H1250: בָּר (bâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h1305|H1305]] (in the sense of winnowing);

===== Meaning =====

**grain** of any kind (even while standing in the field); by extension **the open country**

===== Definitions =====

  - grain
  - the open country

===== Usage =====

corn, wheat.
