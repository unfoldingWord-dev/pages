====== H3562: כּוֹנַנְיָהוּ (Kôwnanyâhûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3559|H3559]] and [[en:lexicon:h3050|H3050]]; **Jah has sustained**;

===== Meaning =====

**Conanjah**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has sustained
  - Conanjah

===== Usage =====
