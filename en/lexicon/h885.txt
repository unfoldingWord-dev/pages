====== H885: בְּאֵרֹת בְּנֵי־יַעֲקַן (Bᵉʼêrôth Bᵉnêy-Yaʻăqan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from the feminine plural of [[en:lexicon:h875|H875]], and the plural contraction of [[en:lexicon:h1121|H1121]], and [[en:lexicon:h3292|H3292]]; wells of (the) sons of Jaakan;

===== Meaning =====

**Beeroth-Bene-Jaakan**, a place in the Desert

===== Definitions =====

  - Beeroth-Bene-Jaakan

===== Usage =====

Beeroth of the children of Jaakan.
