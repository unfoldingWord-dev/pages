===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

patronymically from [[en:lexicon:h1648|H1648]];

===== Meaning =====

a **Gereshonite** or descendant of Gereshon

===== Definitions =====

  - Gereshonite

===== Usage =====

Gershonite, sons of Gershon.
