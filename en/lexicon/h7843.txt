====== H7843: שָׁחַת (shâchath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **decay**, i.e. (causatively) **ruin** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - decay
  - ruin

===== Usage =====

batter, cast off, corrupt(-er, thing), destroy(-er, -uction), lose, mar, perish, spill, spoiler, × utterly, waste(-r).
