====== H8212: שׇׁפְכָה (shophkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of a derivative from [[en:lexicon:h8210|H8210]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pipe** (for pouring forth, e.g. wine), i.e. the **penis**

===== Definitions =====

  - pipe
  - penis

===== Usage =====

privy member.
