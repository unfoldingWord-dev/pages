====== H8505: תָּכַן (tâkan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **balance**, i.e. **measure** out (by weight or dimension); figuratively to **arrange**, **equalize**, through the idea of **levelling** (ment, **estimate**, **test**)

===== Definitions =====

  - balance
  - measure
  - arrange
  - equalize
  - levelling
  - estimate
  - test

===== Usage =====

bear up, direct, be (un-)equal, mete, ponder, tell, weigh.
