====== H3738: כָּרָה (kârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **dig**; figuratively, to **plot**; generally, to **bore** or **open**

===== Definitions =====

  - dig
  - plot
  - bore
  - open

===== Usage =====

dig, × make (a banquet), open.
