====== H4600: מָעַךְ (mâʻak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **press**, i.e. to **pierce**, **emasculate**, **handle**

===== Definitions =====

  - press
  - pierce
  - emasculate
  - handle

===== Usage =====

bruised, stuck, be pressed.
