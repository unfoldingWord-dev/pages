====== G3824: παλιγγενεσία (palingenesía) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3825|G3825]] and [[en:lexicon:g1078|G1078]];

===== Meaning =====

(spiritual) rebirth (the state or the act), i.e. (figuratively) spiritual renovation; specially, Messianic restoration

===== Usage =====

regeneration.
