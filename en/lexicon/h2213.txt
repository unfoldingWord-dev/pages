====== H2213: זֵר (zêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2237|H2237]] (in the sense of **scattering**);

===== Meaning =====

a **chaplet** (as **spread** around the top), i.e. (specifically) a border **moulding**

===== Definitions =====

  - scattering
  - chaplet
  - spread
  - moulding

===== Usage =====

crown.
