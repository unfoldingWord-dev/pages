====== H2962: טֶרֶם (ṭerem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

preposition

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to interrupt or suspend;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **non-occurrence**; used adverbially, **not yet** or **before**

===== Definitions =====

  - non-occurrence
  - not yet
  - before

===== Usage =====

before, ere, not yet.
