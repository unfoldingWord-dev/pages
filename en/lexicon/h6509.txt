====== H6509: פָּרָה (pârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **bear fruit** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - bear fruit

===== Usage =====

bear, bring forth (fruit), (be, cause to be, make) fruitful, grow, increase.
