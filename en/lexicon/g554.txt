====== G554: ἀπεκδύομαι (apekdýomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g1562|G1562]];

===== Meaning =====

to divest wholly oneself, or (for oneself) despoil

===== Usage =====

put off, spoil.
