====== H48: אֲבִירָם (ʼĂbîyrâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h7311|H7311]]; father of height (i.e. lofty);

===== Meaning =====

**Abiram**, the name of two Israelites

  - Abiram

===== Usage =====

Abiram.
