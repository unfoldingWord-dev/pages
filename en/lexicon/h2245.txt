====== H2245: חָבַב (châbab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:lexicon:h2244|H2244]], [[en:lexicon:h2247|H2247]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **hide** (as in the bosom), i.e. to **cherish** (with affection)

===== Definitions =====

  - hide
  - cherish

===== Usage =====

love.
