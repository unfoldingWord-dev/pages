====== H5774: עוּף (ʻûwph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verbnoun - feminine

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to **cover** (with wings or obscurity); hence (as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h5775|H5775]])

===== Meaning =====

to **fly**; also (by implication of dimness) to **faint** (from the darkness of swooning)

===== Definitions =====

  - cover
  - fly
  - faint

===== Usage =====

brandish, be (wax) faint, flee away, fly (away), × set, shine forth, weary.
