====== H1633: גָּרַם (gâram) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to be spare or skeleton-like; used only as a denominative from [[en:lexicon:h1634|H1634]];

===== Meaning =====

(causative) to **bone**, i.e. **denude** (by extensive, craunch) the **bones**

===== Definitions =====

  - bone
  - denude
  - bones

===== Usage =====

gnaw the bones, break.
