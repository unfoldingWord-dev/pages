====== H6655: צַד (tsad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h6654|H6654]];

===== Meaning =====

used adverbially (with preposition) **at** or **upon the side of**

===== Definitions =====

  - at
  - upon the side of

===== Usage =====

against, concerning.
