====== H6324: פּוּנִי (Pûwnîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

patronymically from an unused name meaning a **turn**;

===== Meaning =====

a **Punite** (collectively) or descendants of an unknown Pun

===== Definitions =====

  - turn
  - Punite

===== Usage =====

Punites.
