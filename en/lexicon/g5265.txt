====== G5265: ὑποδέω (hypodéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g1210|G1210]];

===== Meaning =====

to bind under one's feet, i.e. put on shoes or sandals

===== Usage =====

bind on, (be) shod.
