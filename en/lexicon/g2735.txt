====== G2735: κατόρθωμα (katórthōma) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g2596|G2596]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g3717|G3717]] (compare [[en:lexicon:g1357|G1357]]);

===== Meaning =====

something made fully upright, i.e. (figuratively) rectification (specially, good public administration)

===== Usage =====

very worthy deed.
