====== H6368: פִּיחַ (pîyach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6315|H6315]];

===== Meaning =====

a **powder** (as easily puffed away), i.e. **ashes** or **dust**

===== Definitions =====

  - powder
  - ashes
  - dust

===== Usage =====

ashes.
