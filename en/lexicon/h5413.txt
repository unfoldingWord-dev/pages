====== H5413: נָתַךְ (nâthak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **flow forth** (literally or figuratively); by implication, to **liquify**

===== Definitions =====

  - flow forth
  - liquify

===== Usage =====

drop, gather (together), melt, pour (forth, out).
