====== G2272: ἡσύχιος (hēsýchios) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of a compound probably of a derivative of the base of
 [[en:lexicon:g1476|G1476]] and perhaps [[en:lexicon:g2192|G2192]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, keeping one's seat (sedentary), i.e. (by implication) still (undisturbed, undisturbing)

===== Usage =====

peaceable, quiet.
