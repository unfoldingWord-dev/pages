====== H4185: מוּשׁ (mûwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (perhaps rather the same as [[en:lexicon:h4184|H4184]] through the idea of receding by **contact**);

===== Meaning =====

to **withdraw** (both literally and figuratively, whether intransitive or transitive)

===== Definitions =====

  - contact
  - withdraw

===== Usage =====

cease, depart, go back, remove, take away.
