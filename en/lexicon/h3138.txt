====== H3138: יוֹרֶה (yôwreh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

active participle of [[en:lexicon:h3384|H3384]];

===== Meaning =====

**sprinkling**; hence, a **sprinkling** (or autumnal showers)

===== Definitions =====

  - sprinkling
  - sprinkling

===== Usage =====

first rain, former (rain).
