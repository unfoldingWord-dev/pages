====== G3724: ὁρίζω (horízō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3725|G3725]];

===== Meaning =====

to mark out or bound ("horizon"), i.e. (figuratively) to appoint, decree, specify

===== Usage =====

declare, determine, limit, ordain.
