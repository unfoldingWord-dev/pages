====== H8655: תְּרָפִים (tᵉrâphîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

plural from [[en:lexicon:h7495|H7495]]; a **healer**;

===== Meaning =====

**Teraphim** (singular or plural) a family idol

===== Definitions =====

  - healer
  - Teraphim

===== Usage =====

idols(-atry), images, teraphim.
