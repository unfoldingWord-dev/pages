====== H4469: מַמְסָךְ (mamçâk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4537|H4537]];

===== Meaning =====

**mixture**, i.e. (specifically) wine **mixed** (with water or spices)

===== Definitions =====

  - mixture
  - mixed

===== Usage =====

drink-offering, mixed wine.
