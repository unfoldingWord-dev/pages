====== H7081: קֶסֶם (qeçem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7080|H7080]];

===== Meaning =====

a **lot**; also **divination** (including its fee), **oracle**

===== Definitions =====

  - lot
  - divination
  - oracle

===== Usage =====

(reward of) divination, divine sentence, witchcraft.
