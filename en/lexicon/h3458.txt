====== H3458: יִשְׁמָעֵאל (Yishmâʻêʼl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h8085|H8085]] and [[en:lexicon:h410|H410]]; God will hear;

===== Meaning =====

**Jishmael**, the name of Abraham's oldest son, and of five Israelites

===== Usage =====
