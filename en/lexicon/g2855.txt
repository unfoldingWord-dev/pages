====== G2855: κολλυβιστής (kollybistḗs) ======

===== Source =====

from a presumed derivative of κόλλυβος (kóllybos) (a small coin; probably akin to
 [[en:lexicon:g2854|G2854]]);

===== Meaning =====

===== Usage =====
