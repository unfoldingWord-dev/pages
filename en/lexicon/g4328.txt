====== G4328: προσδοκάω (prosdokáō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and dokeuo (to watch);

===== Meaning =====

to anticipate (in thought, hope or fear); by implication, to await

===== Usage =====

(be in) expect(-ation), look (for), when looked, tarry, wait for.
