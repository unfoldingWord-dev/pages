====== H1692: דָּבַק (dâbaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **impinge**, i.e. **cling** or **adhere**; figuratively, to **catch** by pursuit

===== Definitions =====

  - impinge
  - cling
  - adhere
  - catch

===== Usage =====

abide fast, cleave (fast together), follow close (hard after), be joined (together), keep (fast), overtake, pursue hard, stick, take.
