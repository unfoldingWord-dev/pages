====== H3594: כִּיּוּן (Kîyûwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3559|H3559]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **statue**, i.e. **idol**; but used (by euphemism) for some **heathen deity** (perhaps corresponding to Priapus or Baal-peor)

===== Definitions =====

  - statue
  - idol
  - heathen deity

===== Usage =====

Chiun.
