====== H4985: מָתַק (mâthaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **suck**, by implication, to **relish**, or (intransitively) **be sweet**

===== Definitions =====

  - suck
  - relish
  - be sweet

===== Usage =====

be (made, × take) sweet.
