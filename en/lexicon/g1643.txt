====== G1643: ἐλαύνω (elaúnō) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of a primary verb (obsolete except in certain tenses
 as an alternative of this) of uncertain affinity;

===== Meaning =====

to push (as wind, oars or dæmonical power)

===== Usage =====

carry, drive, row.
