====== G5260: ὑποβάλλω (hypobállō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g5259|G5259]] and [[en:lexicon:g906|G906]];

===== Meaning =====

to throw in stealthily, i.e. introduce by collusion

===== Usage =====

suborn.
