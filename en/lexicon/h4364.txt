====== H4364: מַכְמָר (makmâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h3648|H3648]] in the sense of **blackening** by heat;

===== Meaning =====

a (hunter's) **net** (as **dark** from concealment)

===== Definitions =====

  - blackening
  - net
  - dark

===== Usage =====

net.
