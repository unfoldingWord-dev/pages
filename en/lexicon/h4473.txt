====== H4473: מִמְשַׁח (mimshach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h4886|H4886]], in the sense of expansion;

===== Meaning =====

**outspread** (i.e. with outstretched wings)

===== Definitions =====

  - outspread

===== Usage =====

anointed.
