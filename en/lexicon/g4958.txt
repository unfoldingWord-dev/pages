====== G4958: συστέλλω (systéllō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g4724|G4724]];

===== Meaning =====

to send (draw) together, i.e. enwrap (enshroud a corpse for burial), contract (an interval)

===== Usage =====

 short, wind up.
