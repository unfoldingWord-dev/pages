====== H2380: חָזוּת (châzûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2372|H2372]];

===== Meaning =====

a **look**; hence (figuratively) striking **appearance**, **revelation**, or (by implication) **compact**

===== Definitions =====

  - look
  - appearance
  - revelation
  - compact

===== Usage =====

agreement, notable (one), vision.
