====== H64: אָבֵל כְּרָמִים (ʼÂbêl Kᵉrâmîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h58|H58]] and the plural of [[en:lexicon:h3754|H3754]]; meadow of vineyards;

===== Meaning =====

**Abel-Keramim**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Abel-Keramim

===== Usage =====

plain of the vineyards.
