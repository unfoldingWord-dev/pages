====== H6795: צָנַח (tsânach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **alight**; (transitive) to **cause to descend**, i.e. **drive down**

===== Definitions =====

  - alight
  - cause to descend
  - drive down

===== Usage =====

fasten, light (from off).
