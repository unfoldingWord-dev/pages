====== G3850: παραβολή (parabolḗ) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3846|G3846]];

===== Meaning =====

a similitude ("parable"), i.e. (symbolic) fictitious narrative (of common life conveying a moral), apothegm or adage

===== Usage =====

comparison, figure, parable, proverb.
