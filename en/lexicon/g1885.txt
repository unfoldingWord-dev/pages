====== G1885: ἐπαρχία (eparchía) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g757|G757]] (meaning a governor of a district,
 "eparch");

===== Meaning =====

a special region of government, i.e. a Roman præfecture

===== Usage =====

province.
