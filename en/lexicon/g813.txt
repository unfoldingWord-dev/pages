====== G813: ἄτακτος (átaktos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1|G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g5021|G5021]];

===== Meaning =====

unarranged, i.e. (by implication) insubordinate (religiously)

===== Usage =====

unruly.
