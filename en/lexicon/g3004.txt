====== G3004: λέγω (légō) ======

===== Source =====

a primary verb;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to "lay" forth, i.e. (figuratively) relate (in words (usually of systematic or set discourse; whereas

===== Usage =====

ask, bid, boast, call, describe, give out, name, put forth, say(-ing, on), shew, speak, tell, utter.
