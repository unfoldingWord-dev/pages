====== H4494: מָנוֹחַ (mânôwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5117|H5117]];

===== Meaning =====

**quiet**, i.e. (concretely) a **settled spot**, or (figuratively) a **home**

===== Definitions =====

  - quiet
  - settled spot
  - home

===== Usage =====

(place of) rest.
