====== H32: אֲבִיהַיִל (ʼĂbîyhayil) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (more correctly) [[en:lexicon:|]] ; from [[en:lexicon:h1|H1]] and [[en:lexicon:h2428|H2428]]; father (i.e. possessor) of might;

===== Meaning =====

**Abihail** or **Abichail**, the name of three Israelites and two Israelitesses

===== Definitions =====

  - Abihail
  - Abichail

===== Usage =====

Abihail.
