====== G584: ἀποδείκνυμι (apodeíknymi) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g1166|G1166]];

===== Meaning =====

to show off, i.e. exhibit; figuratively, to demonstrate, i.e. accredit

===== Usage =====

(ap-)prove, set forth, shew.
