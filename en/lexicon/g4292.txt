====== G4292: προκαλέομαι (prokaléomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2564|G2564]];

===== Meaning =====

to call forth to oneself (challenge), i.e. (by implication) to irritate

===== Usage =====

provoke.
