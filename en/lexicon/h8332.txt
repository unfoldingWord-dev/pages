====== H8332: שְׁרֹשׁוּ (shᵉrôshûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h8327|H8327]];

===== Meaning =====

**eradication**, i.e. (figuratively) **exile**

===== Definitions =====

  - eradication
  - exile

===== Usage =====

banishment.
