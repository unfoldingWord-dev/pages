====== G626: ἀπολογέομαι (apologéomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and [[en:lexicon:g3056|G3056]];

===== Meaning =====

to give an account (legal plea) of oneself, i.e. exculpate (self)

===== Usage =====

answer (for self), make defence, excuse (self), speak for self.
