====== H7949: שָׁלַג (shâlag) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, meaning to be white; used only as denominative from [[en:lexicon:h7950|H7950]];

===== Meaning =====

to **be snow-white** (with the linen clothing of the slain)

===== Definitions =====

  - be snow-white

===== Usage =====

be as snow.
