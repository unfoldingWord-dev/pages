====== G2302: θέατρον (théatron) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g2300|G2300]];

===== Meaning =====

a place for public show ("theatre"), i.e. general audience-room; by implication, a show itself (figuratively)

===== Usage =====

spectacle, theatre.
