====== G1199: δεσμόν (desmón) ======

===== Source =====

neuter and masculine respectively from [[en:lexicon:g1210|G1210]];

===== Meaning =====

a band, i.e. ligament (of the body) or shackle (of a prisoner); figuratively, an impediment or disability

===== Usage =====

band, bond, chain, string.
