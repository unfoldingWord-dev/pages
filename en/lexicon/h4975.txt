====== H4975: מֹתֶן (môthen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **be slender**;

===== Meaning =====

properly, the **waist** or small of the back; only in plural the **loins**

===== Definitions =====

  - be slender
  - waist
  - loins

===== Usage =====

  greyhound, loins, side.
