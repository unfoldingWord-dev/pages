====== H2336: חוֹחַ (chôwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to **pierce**;

===== Meaning =====

a **thorn**; by analogy, a **ring** for the nose

===== Definitions =====

  - pierce
  - thorn
  - ring

===== Usage =====

bramble, thistle, thorn.
