====== G4040: περίοικος (períoikos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4012|G4012]] and [[en:lexicon:g3624|G3624]];

===== Meaning =====

housed around, i.e. neighboring (used elliptically as a noun)

===== Usage =====
