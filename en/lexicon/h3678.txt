====== H3678: כִּסֵּא (kiççêʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from [[en:lexicon:h3680|H3680]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **covered**, i.e. a **throne** (as **canopied**)

===== Definitions =====

  - covered
  - throne
  - canopied

===== Usage =====

seat, stool, throne.
