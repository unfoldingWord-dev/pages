====== H7455: רֹעַ (rôaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7489|H7489]];

===== Meaning =====

**badness** (as **marring**), physically or morally

===== Definitions =====

  - badness
  - marring

===== Usage =====

× be so bad, badness, (× be so) evil, naughtiness, sadness, sorrow, wickedness.
