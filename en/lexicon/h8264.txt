====== H8264: שָׁקַק (shâqaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **course** (like a beast of prey); by implication, to **seek** greedily

===== Definitions =====

  - course
  - seek

===== Usage =====

have appetite, justle one against another, long, range, run (to and fro).
