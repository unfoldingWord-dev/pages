====== G4818: συλλυπέω (syllypéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4862|G4862]] and [[en:lexicon:g3076|G3076]];

===== Meaning =====

to afflict jointly, i.e. (passive) sorrow at (on account of) someone

===== Usage =====

be grieved.
