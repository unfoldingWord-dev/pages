====== G1195: δεσμεύω (desmeúō) ======

===== Source =====

from a (presumed) derivative of [[en:lexicon:g1196|G1196]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a binder (captor), i.e. to enchain (a prisoner), to tie on (a load)

===== Usage =====

 bind.
