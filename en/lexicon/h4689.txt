====== H4689: מָצוֹק (mâtsôwq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6693|H6693]];

===== Meaning =====

a **narrow place**, i.e. (abstractly and figuratively) **confinement** or **disability**

===== Definitions =====

  - narrow place
  - confinement
  - disability

===== Usage =====

anguish, distress, straitness.
