====== H6566: פָּרַשׂ (pâras) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **break** apart, **disperse**, etc.

===== Definitions =====

  - break
  - disperse

===== Usage =====

break, chop in pieces, lay open, scatter, spread (abroad, forth, selves, out), stretch (forth, out).
