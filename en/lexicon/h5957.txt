====== H5957: עָלַם (ʻâlam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h5769|H5769]];

===== Meaning =====

**remote time**, i.e. the **future** or **past** indefinitely; often adverb, **forever**

===== Definitions =====

  - remote time
  - future
  - past
  - forever

===== Usage =====

for (n-)ever (lasting), old.
