====== G1582: ἐκκρέμαμαι (ekkrémamai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g2910|G2910]];

===== Meaning =====

to hang upon the lips of a speaker, i.e. listen closely

===== Usage =====

be very attentive.
