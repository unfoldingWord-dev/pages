====== H5844: עָטָה (ʻâṭâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **wrap**, i.e. **cover**, **veil**, **clothe**, or **roll**

===== Definitions =====

  - wrap
  - cover
  - veil
  - clothe
  - roll

===== Usage =====

array self, be clad, (put a) cover (-ing, self), fill, put on, × surely, turn aside.
