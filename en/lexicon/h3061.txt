====== H3061: יְהוּד (Yᵉhûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - location

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) contracted from a form corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h3063|H3063]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **Judah**, hence, **Judaea**

===== Definitions =====

  - Judah
  - Judaea

===== Usage =====

Jewry, Judah, Judea.
