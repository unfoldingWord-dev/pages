====== H5657: עֲבֻדָּה (ʻăbuddâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h5647|H5647]];

===== Meaning =====

something **wrought**, i.e. (concretely) **service**

===== Definitions =====

  - wrought
  - service

===== Usage =====

household, store of servants.
