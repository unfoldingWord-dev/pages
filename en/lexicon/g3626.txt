====== G3626: οἰκουρός (oikourós) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3624|G3624]] and οὖρος (oûros) (a guard; be "ware");

===== Meaning =====

a stayer at home, i.e. domestically inclined (a "good housekeeper")

===== Usage =====

keeper at home.
