====== H1739: דָּוֶה (dâveh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h1738|H1738]];

===== Meaning =====

**sick** (especially in menstruation)

===== Definitions =====

  - sick

===== Usage =====

faint, menstruous cloth, she that is sick, having sickness.
