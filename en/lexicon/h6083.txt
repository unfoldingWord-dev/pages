====== H6083: עָפָר (ʻâphâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6080|H6080]];

===== Meaning =====

**dust** (as powdered or gray); hence, **clay**, **earth**, **mud**

===== Definitions =====

  - dust
  - clay
  - earth
  - mud

===== Usage =====

ashes, dust, earth, ground, morter, powder, rubbish.
