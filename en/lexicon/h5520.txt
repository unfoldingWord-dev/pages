====== H5520: סֹךְ (çôk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5526|H5526]];

===== Meaning =====

a **hut** (as of **entwined** boughs); also a **lair**

===== Definitions =====

  - hut
  - entwined
  - lair

===== Usage =====

covert, den, pavilion, tabernacle.
