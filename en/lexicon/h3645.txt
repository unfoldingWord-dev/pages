====== H3645: כְּמוֹשׁ (Kᵉmôwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun

===== Source =====

or (Jeremiah 48:7) [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **subdue**; the **powerful**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kemosh**, the god of the Moabites

===== Definitions =====

  - subdue
  - powerful
  - Kemosh

===== Usage =====

Chemosh.
