====== G3943: πάροινος (pároinos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g3631|G3631]];

===== Meaning =====

staying near wine, i.e. tippling (a toper)

===== Usage =====

given to wine.
