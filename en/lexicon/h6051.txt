====== H6051: עָנָן (ʻânân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6049|H6049]];

===== Meaning =====

a **cloud** (as **covering** the sky), i.e. the **nimbus** or thunder-cloud

===== Definitions =====

  - cloud
  - covering
  - nimbus

===== Usage =====

cloud(-y).
