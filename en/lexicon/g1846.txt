====== G1846: ἐξορύσσω (exorýssō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1537|G1537]] and [[en:lexicon:g3736|G3736]];

===== Meaning =====

to dig out, i.e. (by extension) to extract (an eye), remove (roofing)

===== Usage =====

break up, pluck out.
