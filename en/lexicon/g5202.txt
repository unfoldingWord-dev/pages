====== G5202: ὑδροποτέω (hydropotéō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g5204|G5204]] and a derivative of [[en:lexicon:g4095|G4095]];

===== Meaning =====

to be a water-drinker, i.e. to abstain from vinous beverages

===== Usage =====

drink water.
