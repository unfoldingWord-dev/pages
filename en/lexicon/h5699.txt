====== H5699: עֲגָלָה (ʻăgâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5696|H5696]];

===== Meaning =====

something **revolving**, i.e. a wheeled **vehicle**

===== Definitions =====

  - revolving
  - vehicle

===== Usage =====

cart, chariot, wagon
