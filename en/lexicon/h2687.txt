====== H2687: חָצָץ (châtsâts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2687|H2687]];	also like [[en:lexicon:h2671|H2671]]

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **cutting**; hence, **gravel** (as grit); an **arrow**

===== Definitions =====

  - cutting
  - gravel
  - arrow

===== Usage =====

arrow, gravel (stone).
