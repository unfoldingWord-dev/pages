====== H6215: עֵשָׂו (ʻÊsâv) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently a form of the passive participle of [[en:lexicon:h6213|H6213]] in the original sense of handling; **rough** (i.e. sensibly felt);

===== Meaning =====

**Esav**, a son of Isaac, including his posterity

===== Definitions =====

  - rough
  - Esav

===== Usage =====

Esau.
