====== G3660: ὀμνύω (omnýō) ======

===== Source =====

a prolonged form of a primary,
                         but obsolete ὄμω (ómō), for which
                         another prolonged form
      ὀμόω (omóō)      is used in certain tenses;

===== Meaning =====

to swear, i.e. take (or declare on) oath

===== Usage =====

swear.
