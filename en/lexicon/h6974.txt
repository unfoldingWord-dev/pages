====== H6974: קוּץ (qûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of abruptness in starting up from sleep (compare [[en:lexicon:h3364|H3364]]));

===== Meaning =====

to **awake** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - awake

===== Usage =====

arise, (be) (a-) wake, watch.
