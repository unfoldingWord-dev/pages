====== H4766: מַרְבֶה (marbeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h7235|H7235]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **increasing**; as noun, **greatness**, or (adverbially) **greatly**

===== Definitions =====

  - increasing
  - greatness
  - greatly

===== Usage =====

great, increase.
