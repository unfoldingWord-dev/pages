====== H5931: עִלָּה (ʻillâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) feminine from a root corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h5927|H5927]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pretext** (as arising artificially)

===== Definitions =====

  - pretext

===== Usage =====

occasion.
