====== H1058: בָּכָה (bâkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **weep**; generally to **bemoan**

===== Definitions =====

  - weep
  - bemoan

===== Usage =====

× at all, bewail, complain, make lamentation, × more, mourn, × sore, × with tears, weep.
