====== H931: בֹּהֶן (bôhen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to be **thick**;

===== Meaning =====

the **thumb** of the hand or **great toe** of the foot

===== Definitions =====

  - thick
  - thumb
  - great toe

===== Usage =====

thumb, great toe.
