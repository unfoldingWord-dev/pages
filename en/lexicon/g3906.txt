====== G3906: παρατηρέω (paratēréō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g5083|G5083]];

===== Meaning =====

to inspect alongside, i.e. note insidiously or scrupulously

===== Usage =====

observe, watch.
