====== H7616: שָׁבָב (shâbâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **break up**;

===== Meaning =====

a **fragment**, i.e. **ruin**

===== Definitions =====

  - break up
  - fragment
  - ruin

===== Usage =====

broken in pieces.
