====== H329: אָטָד (ʼâṭâd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to pierce or make fast;

===== Meaning =====

a **thorn-tree** (especially the buckthorn)

===== Definitions =====

  - thorn-tree

===== Usage =====

Atad, bramble, thorn.
