====== G1484: ἔθνος (éthnos) ======

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:lexicon:g1486|G1486]];

===== Meaning =====

a race (as of the same habit), i.e. a tribe; specially, a foreign (non-Jewish) one (usually, by implication, pagan)

===== Usage =====

Gentile, heathen, nation, people.
