====== G4296: προκηρύσσω (prokērýssō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2784|G2784]];

===== Meaning =====

to herald (i.e. proclaim) in advance

===== Usage =====

before (first) preach.
