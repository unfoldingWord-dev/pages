====== G4345: προσκληρόω (prosklēróō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4314|G4314]] and [[en:lexicon:g2820|G2820]];

===== Meaning =====

to give a common lot to, i.e. (figuratively) to associate with

===== Usage =====

consort with.
