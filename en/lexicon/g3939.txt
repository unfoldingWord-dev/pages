====== G3939: παροικέω (paroikéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g3844|G3844]] and [[en:lexicon:g3611|G3611]];

===== Meaning =====

to dwell near, i.e. reside as a foreigner

===== Usage =====

sojourn in, be a stranger.
