====== H1269: בִּרְזוֹת (Birzôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

probably feminine plural from an unused root (apparently mean to pierce); holes;

===== Meaning =====

**Birzoth**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Birzoth

===== Usage =====

Birzavith (from the marg.).
