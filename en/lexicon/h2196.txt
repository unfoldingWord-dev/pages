====== H2196: זָעַף (zâʻaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **boil up**, i.e. (figuratively) to **be peevish** or **angry**

===== Definitions =====

  - boil up
  - be peevish
  - angry

===== Usage =====

fret, sad, worse liking, be wroth.
