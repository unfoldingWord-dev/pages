====== H4388: מַכְתֵּשׁ (maktêsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3806|H3806]];

===== Meaning =====

a **mortar**; by analogy, a **socket** (of a tooth)

===== Definitions =====

  - mortar
  - socket

===== Usage =====

hollow place, mortar.
