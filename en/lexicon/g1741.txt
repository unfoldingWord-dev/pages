====== G1741: ἔνδοξος (éndoxos) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1722|G1722]] and [[en:lexicon:g1391|G1391]];

===== Meaning =====

in glory, i.e. splendid, (figuratively) noble

===== Usage =====

glorious, gorgeous(-ly), honourable.
