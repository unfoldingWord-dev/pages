====== G4690: σπέρμα (spérma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4687|G4687]];

===== Meaning =====

something sown, i.e. seed (including the male "sperm"); by implication, offspring; specially, a remnant (figuratively, as if kept over for planting)

===== Usage =====

issue, seed.
