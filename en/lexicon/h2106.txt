====== H2106: זָוִית (zâvîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

apparently from the same root as [[en:lexicon:h2099|H2099]] (in the sense of prominence);

===== Meaning =====

an **angle** (as projecting), i.e. (by implication) a **corner-column** (or anta)

===== Definitions =====

  - angle
  - corner-column

===== Usage =====

corner(stone).
