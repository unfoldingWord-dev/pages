====== H5128: נוּעַ (nûwaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **waver**, in a great variety of applications, literally and figuratively (as subjoined)

===== Definitions =====

  - waver

===== Usage =====

continually, fugitive, × make, to (go) up and down, be gone away, (be) move(-able, -d), be promoted, reel, remove, scatter, set, shake, sift, stagger, to and fro, be vagabond, wag, (make) wander (up and down).
