====== H5091: נָהָה (nâhâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **groan**, i.e. **bewail**; hence (through the idea of crying aloud), to **assemble** (as if on proclamation)

===== Definitions =====

  - groan
  - bewail
  - assemble

===== Usage =====

lament, wail.
