====== H2363: חוּשׁ (chûwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **hurry**; figuratively, to **be eager with excitement** or **enjoyment**

===== Definitions =====

  - hurry
  - be eager with excitement
  - enjoyment

===== Usage =====

(make) haste(-n), ready.
