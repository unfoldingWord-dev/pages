====== H7122: קָרָא (qârâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **encounter**, whether accidentally or in a hostile manner

===== Definitions =====

  - encounter

===== Usage =====

befall, (by) chance, (cause to) come (upon), fall out, happen, meet.
