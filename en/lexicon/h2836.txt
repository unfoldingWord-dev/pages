====== H2836: חָשַׁק (châshaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also interchangeable for [[en:lexicon:h2820|H2820]]

===== Meaning =====

to **cling**, i.e. **join**, (figuratively) to **love**, **delight in**; elliptically; to **deliver**

===== Definitions =====

  - cling
  - join
  - love
  - delight in
  - deliver

===== Usage =====

have a delight, (have a) desire, fillet, long, set (in) love.
