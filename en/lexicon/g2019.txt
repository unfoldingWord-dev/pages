====== G2019: ἐπιφωνέω (epiphōnéō) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g1909|G1909]] and [[en:lexicon:g5455|G5455]];

===== Meaning =====

to call at something, i.e. exclaim

===== Usage =====

cry (against), give a shout.
