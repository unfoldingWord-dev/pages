====== H4233: מַחֲוִים (Machăvîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

apparently a patrial, but from an unknown place (in the plural only for a singular);

===== Meaning =====

a **Machavite** or inhabitant of some place named Machaveh

===== Definitions =====

  - Machavite

===== Usage =====

Mahavite.
