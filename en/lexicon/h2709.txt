====== H2709: חֲקוּפָא (Chăqûwphâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to bend; crooked;

===== Meaning =====

**Chakupha**, one of the Nethinim

===== Definitions =====

  - Chakupha

===== Usage =====

Hakupha.
