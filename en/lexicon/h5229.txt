====== H5229: נְכֹחָה (nᵉkôchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:lexicon:h5228|H5228]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **straightforwardness**, i.e. (figuratively) **integrity**, or (concretely) a **truth**

===== Definitions =====

  - straightforwardness
  - integrity
  - truth

===== Usage =====

equity, right (thing), uprightness.
