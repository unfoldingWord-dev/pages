====== H3493: יַתִּיר (yattîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:lexicon:h3492|H3492]];

===== Meaning =====

**preeminent**; as an adverb, **very**

===== Definitions =====

  - preeminent
  - very

===== Usage =====

exceeding(-ly), excellent.
