====== G4283: προευαγγελίζομαι (proeuangelízomai) ======

===== Source =====

middle voice from [[en:lexicon:g4253|G4253]] and [[en:lexicon:g2097|G2097]];

===== Meaning =====

to announce glad news in advance

===== Usage =====

preach before the gospel.
