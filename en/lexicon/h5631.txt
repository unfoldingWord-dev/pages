====== H5631: סָרִיס (çârîyç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:lexicon:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **castrate**;

===== Meaning =====

a **eunuch**; by implication, **valet** (especially of the female apartments), and thus, a **minister** of state

===== Definitions =====

  - castrate
  - eunuch
  - valet
  - minister

===== Usage =====

chamberlain, eunuch, officer. Compare
