====== H3748: כְּרִיתוּת (kᵉrîythûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h3772|H3772]];

===== Meaning =====

a **cutting** (of the matrimonial bond), i.e. **divorce**

===== Definitions =====

  - cutting
  - divorce

===== Usage =====

divorce(-ment).
