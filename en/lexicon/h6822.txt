====== H6822: צָפָה (tsâphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **lean** forward, i.e. to **peer** into the distance; by implication, to **observe**, **await**

===== Definitions =====

  - lean
  - peer
  - observe
  - await

===== Usage =====

behold, espy, look up (well), wait for, (keep the) watch(-man).
