====== H6089: עֶצֶב (ʻetseb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h6087|H6087]];

===== Meaning =====

an **earthen vessel**; usually (painful) **toil**; also a **pang** (whether of body or mind)

===== Definitions =====

  - earthen vessel
  - toil
  - pang

===== Usage =====

grievous, idol, labor, sorrow.
