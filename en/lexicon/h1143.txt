====== H1143: בֵּנַיִם (bênayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

dual of [[en:lexicon:h996|H996]];

===== Meaning =====

**a double interval**, i.e. **the space between two armies**

===== Definitions =====

  - a double interval
  - the space between two armies

===== Usage =====

+ champion.
