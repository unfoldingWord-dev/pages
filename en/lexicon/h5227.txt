====== H5227: נֹכַח (nôkach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:h5226|H5226]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, the **front** part; used adverbially (especially with preposition), **opposite**, **in front of**, **forward**, **in behalf of**

===== Definitions =====

  - front
  - opposite
  - in front of
  - forward
  - in behalf of

===== Usage =====

(over) against, before, direct(-ly), for, right (on).
