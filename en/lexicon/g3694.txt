====== G3694: ὀπίσω (opísō) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:lexicon:g3693|G3693]] with enclitic of direction;

===== Meaning =====

to the back, i.e. aback (as adverb or preposition of time or place; or as noun)

===== Usage =====

after, back(-ward), (+ get) behind, + follow.
