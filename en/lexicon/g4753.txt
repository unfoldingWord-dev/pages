====== G4753: στράτευμα (stráteuma) ======

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:g4754|G4754]];

===== Meaning =====

an armament, i.e. (by implication) a body of troops (more or less extensive or systematic)

===== Usage =====

army, soldier, man of war.
