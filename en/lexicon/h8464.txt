====== H8464: תַּחְמָס (tachmâç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h2554|H2554]];

===== Meaning =====

a **species of unclean bird** (from its violence), perhaps an **owl**

===== Definitions =====

  - species of unclean bird
  - owl

===== Usage =====

night hawk.
