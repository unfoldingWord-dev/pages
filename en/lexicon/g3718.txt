====== G3718: ὀρθοτομέω (orthotoméō) ======

===== Source =====

from a compound of [[en:lexicon:g3717|G3717]] and the base of [[en:lexicon:g5114|G5114]], to make a straight cut,
 i.e. (figuratively) to dissect (expound) correctly (the divine
 message)

===== Meaning =====



===== Usage =====

rightly divide.
