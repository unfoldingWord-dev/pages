====== H3302: יָפָה (yâphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **be bright**, i.e. (by implication) **beautiful**

===== Definitions =====

  - be bright
  - beautiful

===== Usage =====

be beautiful, be (make self) fair(-r), deck.
