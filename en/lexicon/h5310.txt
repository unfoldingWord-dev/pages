====== H5310: נָפַץ (nâphats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **dash** to pieces, or **scatter**

===== Definitions =====

  - dash
  - scatter

===== Usage =====

be beaten in sunder, break (in pieces), broken, dash (in pieces), cause to be discharged, dispersed, be overspread, scatter.
