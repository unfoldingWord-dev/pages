====== G664: ἀποτόμως (apotómōs) ======

===== Source =====

adverb from a derivative of a comparative of [[en:lexicon:g575|G575]] and τέμνω (témnō) (to cut);

===== Meaning =====

abruptly, i.e. peremptorily

===== Usage =====

sharply(-ness).
