====== H8569: תְּנוּאָה (tᵉnûwʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:lexicon:h5106|H5106]];

===== Meaning =====

**alienation**; by implication, **enmity**

===== Definitions =====

  - alienation
  - enmity

===== Usage =====

breach of promise, occasion.
