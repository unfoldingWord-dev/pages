====== Rebekah ======


===== Facts: =====


Rebekah was a daughter of one of Abraham's nephews. Her grandfather was Abraham's brother.

    * God chose Rebekah to be the wife of Abraham's son Isaac. 
    * Rebekah left her country and went with Abraham's servant to go to Isaac's country, marry Isaac, and live there with his family.
    * For a long time Rebekah did not have any children, but finally God blessed her with twin boys, Esau and Jacob.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:en:uwb:notes:frames:06-02| [06-02]]]** After a very long journey to the land where Abraham's relatives lived, God led the servant to **Rebekah**. She was the granddaughter of Abraham's brother.
  ***[[:en:uwb:notes:frames:06-06| [06-06]]]** God told **Rebekah**, "There are two nations inside of you."
  ***[[:en:uwb:notes:frames:07-01| [07-01]]]** As the boys grew up, **Rebekah** loved Jacob, but Isaac loved Esau.
  ***[[:en:uwb:notes:frames:07-03| [07-03]]]** Isaac wanted to give his blessing to Esau. But before he did, **Rebekah** and Jacob tricked him by having Jacob pretend to be Esau.
  ***[[:en:uwb:notes:frames:07-06| [07-06]]]** But **Rebekah** heard of Esau's plan. So she sent Jacob far away to live with her relatives.
